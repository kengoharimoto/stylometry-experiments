prapīḍitāḥ tatas mayaḥ svakam sainyam māyā śaraiḥ prapīḍitam vavarṣa ca aśma varṣāṇi deva sainya upari dhruvam bhūyas vavarṣa śṛṅgāni samādāya ca māyayā gaṇḍaśailān drumān ca eva yugapad ca mayaḥ aasuraḥ hilā druma śara āghātaiḥ vyathitāḥ ca bhaya āturāḥ tatas yuyudhatuḥ vīrau nānā māyā viśāradau dyotayan sādṛśyam ca tatas tvaṣṭā kṛtvā manaḥ hi māyayā sākam tadā mahat yuddham kaṭhoram medinī tale gatvā ca ārūḍhavan agni vṛṣṭim vavarṣa ājau śilā varṣam tatas param śara varṣam vavarṣa āśu śastra varṣam tathāvidham druma varṣam punar vipraiḥ pāṃśu varṣam ca viśvasṛj tatas petuḥ ca daiteyāḥ vaman asṛj mukhāt vraṇāt samantataḥ tatas mayaḥ samājñāya māyā udbhavam hi puruṣam mayaḥ ca tadā vipra saṃsṛjya māyayā ātmanaḥ sādṛśyam puruṣam tena yodhāpayat mithas samam utpapāta antarikṣam ca mayaḥ māyā mayaḥ tanuḥ tatra dadarśa yāna stham tvaṣṭāram ca nabhastale ghātayantam śilā varṣaiḥ daitya sainyān samantataḥ vavarṣa śara varṣāṇi durbhikṣe śalabhān iva mahā sāram tathā tīvram pāṃsu varṣam samantataḥ tatas mayaḥ ca māyāvī māyayā mohayat raṇe layaiḥ gītaiḥ mṛdaṅgaiḥ ca tvaṣṭāram ca api anartayat nṛtyantam gītavantam tam tvaṣṭāram prekṣya candramāḥ saṃdarśya māyayā āviṣṭam tvaṣṭāram vavarṣa amṛtam tatas tvaṣṭā amṛta āsāraiḥ pariplutaḥ ghaṭodbhava iti śrī skandapurāṇe himavat khaṇḍe devāsura saṃgrāme viśvakarmāpayānam nāma ūnatriṃśaḥ adhyāyaḥ
