---
title: 021

---
।। sūta uvāca ।।  
ṛṣīṇāmagnikalpānāṃ naimiṣāraṇyavāsinām ।  
ṛṣiḥ śrutidharaḥ prājñaḥ sāvarṇirnnāma nāmataḥ ।। 21.1 ।।  
teṣāṃ sopyagrato bhūtvā vāyuṃ vākyaviśāradaḥ।  
sātatyaṃ tatra kurvantaṃ priyārthe satrayājinām।  
vinayenopasaṃgamya papraccha sa mahādyutim ।। 21.2 ।।  
।।sāvarṇiruvāca ।।  
vibho purāṇasaṃ baddhāṃ kathāṃ vai vedasaṃmitām।  
śrotumicchāmahe samyak prasādātsarvadarśinaḥ ।। 21.3 ।।  
hiraṇyagarbho bhagavān lalāṭānnīlalohitam।  
kathaṃ tattejasaṃ devaṃ labdhavān putramātmanaḥ ।। 21.4 ।।  
kathaṃ ca bhagavān jajñe brahmā kamalasaṃbhavaḥ।  
rudratvaṃ caiva sarvasya svātmajasya kathaṃ punaḥ ।। 21.5 ।।  
kathaṃ ca viṣṇo rudreṇa sārddhaṃ prītiranuttamā।  
sarve viṣṇumayā devā sarve viṣṇumayā gaṇāḥ ।। 21.6 ।।  
na ca viṣṇusamā kācidgatiranyā vidhīyate।  
ityevaṃ satataṃ devā gāyante nātra saṃśayaḥ।  
bhavasya sakathaṃ nityaṃ praṇāmaṃ kurute hariḥ ।। 21.7 ।।  
।।sūta uvāca ।।  
evamukte tu bhagavān vāyuḥ sāvarṇimabravīt।  
aho sādhu tvayā sādho pṛṣṭaḥ praśno hyanuttamaḥ ।। 21.8 ।।  
bhavasya putrajanmatvaṃ brahmaṇaḥ so'bhavadyathā।  
brahmaṇaḥ padma yonitvaṃ rudratvaṃ śaṃkarasya ca ।। 21.9 ।।  
dvābhyāmapi ca samprītir  
viṣṇoścaiva bhavasya ca ।  
yaccāpi kurute nityaṃ praṇāmaṃ śaṃkarasya ca।  
vistareṇānupūrvyācca śrṛṇuta bruvato mama ।। 21.10 ।।  
manvantarasya saṃhāre paścimasya mahātmanaḥ।  
āsīttu saptamaḥ kalpaḥ padmo nāma dvijottama।  
vārāhaḥ sāmpratasteṣāṃ tasya vakṣyāmi vistaram ।। 21.11 ।।  
।।sāvarṇiruvāca।।  
kiyatā caiva kālena kalpaḥ sambhavate katham।  
kiṃ ca pramāṇaṃ kalpasya tatra prabrūhi pṛcchatām ।। 21.12 ।।  
।।vāyuruvāca।।  
manvantarāṇāṃ saptānāṃ kālasaṃkhyā yathākramam।  
pravakṣyāmi samāsena bruvato me nibodhata ।। 21.13 ।।  
koṭīnāṃ dve sahasre vai aṣṭau koṭiśatāni ca ।  
dviṣaṣṭiśca tathā koṭyo niyutāni ca saptatiḥ।  
kalpārddhasya tu saṃkhyāyāmetat sarvamudāhṛtam ।। 21.14 ।।  
pūrvoktau ca guṇacchedau varṣāgraṃ labdamādiśet ।  
śataṃ caiva tu koṭīnāṃ koṭīnāmaṣṭasaptatiḥ।  
dve ca śatasahasre tu navatirniyutāni ca ।। 21.15 ।।  
mānuṣeṇa pramāṇena yāvadvaivasvatāntaram।  
eṣa kalpastu vijñeyaḥ kalpārddhadviguṇīkṛtaḥ ।। 21.16 ।।  
anāgatānāṃ saptānāmetadeva yathākramam।  
pramāṇaṃ kālasaṃkhyāyā vijñeyaṃ matamaiśvaram ।। 21.17 ।।  
niyutānyaṣṭapañcāśattathā'śītiśatāni ca।  
caturaśītiścavānyāni prayutāni pramāṇataḥ ।। 21.18 ।।  
saptarṣayo manuścaiva devāścendrapurogamāḥ।  
etat kālasya vijñeyaṃ varṣāgrantu pramāṇataḥ ।। 21.19 ।।  
evaṃ manvantaraṃ teṣāṃ mānuṣāntaḥ prakīrttitaḥ।  
praṇavāntāśacava ye devāḥ sādhyā devagaṇāśca ye।  
viśvedevāśacava ye nityāḥ kalpaṃ jīvanti te gaṇāḥ ।। 21.20 ।।  
ayaṃ yo varttate kalpo vārāhaḥ sa tu kīrttyate।  
yasmin svāyambhuvādyāśca manavaśca caturddaśa ।। 21.21 ।।  
।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।  
kasmādvārāhakalpoyaṃ nāmataḥ parikīrtitaḥ।  
kasmācca kāraṇāddevo varāha iti kīrttyate ।। 21.22 ।।  
ko vā varāho bhagavān kasya yoniḥ kimātmakaḥ।  
varāhaḥ kathamutpanna etadicchāma veditum ।। 21.23 ।।  
।।vāyuruvāca।।  
varāhastu yathotpanno yasminnarthe ca kalpitaḥ।  
vārāhaśca yathā kalpaḥ kalpatvaṃ kalpanā ca yā ।। 21.24 ।।  
kalpayorantaraṃ yacca tasya cāsya ca kalpitam।  
tatsarvaṃ sampravakṣyāmi yathādṛṣṭaṃ yathāśrutam ।। 21.25 ।।  
bhavastu prathamaḥ kalpo lokādau prathitaḥ purā।  
jñātavyo bhagavānatra hyānandaḥ sāmprataḥ svayam ।। 21.26 ।।  
brahmasthānamidaṃ divyaṃ prāptaṃ vā divyasambhavam।  
dvitīyastu bhuvaḥ kalpastṛtīyastapa ucyate ।। 21.27 ।।  
bhavaścaturtho vijñeyaḥ paṃcamo rambha eva ca।  
ṛtukalpastathā ṣaṣṭhaḥ saptamastu kratuḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 21.28 ।।  
aṣṭamastu bhavedvahnirnavamo havyavahanaḥ।  
sāvitro daśamaḥ kalpo bhuvastvekādaśaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 21.29 ।।  
uśiko dvādaśastatra kuśikastu trayodaśaḥ।  
caturddaśastu gandharvo gandharvo yatra vai svaraḥ।  
utpannastu yathā nādo gandharvā yatra cotthitāḥ ।। 21.30 ।।  
ṛṣabhastu tataḥ kalpo jñeyaḥ paṃcadaśo dvijāḥ।  
ṛṣayo yatra sambhūtāḥ svaro lokamanoharaḥ ।। 21.31 ।।  
ṣaḍjastu ṣoḍaśaḥ kalpaḥ ṣaḍjanā yatra carṣayaḥ।  
śiśiraśca vasantaśca nidāgho varṣa eva ca ।। 21.32 ।।  
śaraddhemanta ityete mānasā brahmaṇaḥ sutāḥ।  
utpannāḥ ṣaḍja saṃsiddhāḥ putrāḥ kalpe tu ṣoḍaśe ।। 21.33 ।।  
yasmājjātaiśca taiḥ ṣaḍabhiḥ sadyo jāto maheśvaraḥ।  
tasmāt samutthitaḥ ṣaḍjaḥ svarastūdadhisannibhaḥ ।। 21.34 ।।  
tataḥ saptadaśaḥ kalpo mārjālīya iti smṛtaḥ।  
mārjjālīyaṃ tu tat karma yasmādbrāhmāmakalpayat ।। 21.35 ।।  
tatastu madhyamo nāma kalpo'ṣṭādaśa ucyate।  
yasmistu madhyamo nāma svaro dhaivatapūjitaḥ ।  
utpannaḥ sarvabhūteṣu madhyamo vai svayaṃbhuvaḥ ।। 21.36 ।।  
tatastvekonaviṃśastu kalpo vairājakaḥ smṛtaḥ।  
vairājo yatra bhagavān munurvai brahmaṇaḥ sutaḥ ।। 21.37 ।।  
tasya putrastu dharmātmā dadīcirnnāma dhārmikaḥ।  
prajāpatirmahātejā babhūva tridaśeśvaraḥ ।। 21.38 ।।  
akāmayata gāyatrī yajamānaṃ prajāpatim।  
tasmājjajñe svaraḥ srigdhaḥ putrastasya dadhīcinaḥ ।। 21.39 ।।  
tato viṃśatimaḥ kalpo niṣādaḥ parikīrttitaḥ।  
prajāpatistu taṃ dṛṣṭvā svayambhūprabhavaṃ tadā।  
virarāma prajāḥ sraṣṭuṃ niṣādastu tapo'tapat ।। 21.40 ।।  
divyaṃ varṣasahasrantu nirāhāro jītendriyaḥ।  
tamuvāca mahāṃtejā brahmā lokapitāmahaḥ ।। 21.41 ।।  
ūrddhvabāhuṃ tapoglānaṃ duḥkhitaṃ kṣutpipāsitam।  
niṣīdetyabravīdenaṃ putraṃ śāntaṃ pitāmahaḥ।  
tasmānniṣādaḥ sambhūtaḥ svarastu sa niṣādavān ।। 21.42 ।।  
ekaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo vijñeyaḥ pañcamo dvijāḥ।  
prāṇo'pānaḥ samānaśca udāno vyāna eva ca ।। 21.43 ।।  
brahmaṇo mānasāḥ putrāḥ pañcaite brahmaṇaḥ samāḥ।  
taistvarthavādibhiryuktairvāgbhiriṣṭo maheśvaraḥ ।। 21.44 ।।  
yasmātparigatairgītaḥ pañcabhistairmahātmabhiḥ।  
svarastu pañcamaḥ snigdhaḥ tasmātkalpastu pañcamaḥ ।। 21.45 ।।  
dvāviṃśastu tathā kalpo vijñeyo meghavāhanaḥ।  
yatra viṣṇurmahābāhurmegho bhūtvā maheśvaram।  
divyaṃ varṣasahasrantu avahat kṛttivāsasam ।। 21.46 ।।  
tasya niḥśvasamānasya bhārākāntasya vai mukhāt।  
nirjagāma mahākāyaḥ kālo lokaprakāśanaḥ।  
yastvayaṃ paṭyate viprairviṣṇurvai kaśyapātmajaḥ ।। 21.47 ।।  
trayoviṃśatimaḥ kalpo vijñeyaścintakastathā।  
prajāpatisutaḥ śrīmān citiśca mithunañca tau ।। 21.48 ।।  
dhyāyato brahmaṇaścaiva yasmāccintā samutthitā।  
tasmāttu cintakaḥ so vai kalpaḥ proktaḥ svayambhuvā ।। 21.49 ।।  
caturviṃśatimaścāpi hyākūtiḥ kalpa ucyate।  
ākūtiśca tathā devī mithunaṃ sambabhūva ha ।। 21.50 ।।  
prajāḥ sraṣṭuṃ tathākūtiṃ yasmādāha prajāpatiḥ।  
tasmāt sa puruṣo jñeya ākūtiḥ kalpasaṃjñitaḥ ।। 21.51 ।।  
pañcaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo vijñātiḥ parikīrttitaḥ।  
vijñātiśacava tathā devī mithunaṃ samprasūyate ।। 21.52 ।।  
dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya manasyadhyātmasaṃjñitam।  
vijñātaṃ vai samāsena vijñātistu tataḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 21.53 ।।  
ṣaḍ‌viṃśastu tataḥ kalpo mana ityabhidhīyate।  
devī ca śāṅkarī nāma mithunaṃ samprasūyate ।। 21.54 ।।  
prajā vai cintamā nasya sraṣṭukāmasya vai tadā।  
yasmāt prajāsambhavanādutpannastu svayambhuvā ।  
tasmāt prajāsambhavanādbhāvanāsambhavaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 21.55 ।।  
saptaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo bhāvo vai kalpasaṃjñitaḥ।  
paurṇamāsī tathā devī mithunaṃ samapadyata ।। 21.56 ।।  
prajā vai sraṣṭukāmasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ।  
dhyāyatastu paraṃ dhyānaṃ paramātmānamīśvaram ।। 21.57 ।।  
agnistu maṇḍalībhūtvā raśmijālasamāvṛtaḥ ।  
bhuvandivañcaviṣṭabhya dīpyate sa mahāvapuḥ ।। 21.58 ।।  
tato varṣasahasrānte sampūrṇe jyotimaṇḍale।  
aviṣṭayā sahotpannamapasyat sūryamaṇḍalam ।। 21.59 ।।  
yasmādadṛśyo bhūtānāṃ brahmaṇā parameṣṭinā।  
dṛṣṭastu bhagavān devaḥ sūryyaḥ sampūrṇamaṇḍalaḥ ।। 21.60 ।।  
sarvve yogāśca mantrāśca maṇḍalena sahotthitāḥ।  
yasmāt kalpo hyayaṃ dṛṣṭastasmāttaṃ darśa mucyate ।। 21.61 ।।  
yasmānmanasi sampūrṇo brahamaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ।  
purā vai bhagavān somaḥ paurṇamāsī tataḥ smṛtā ।। 21.62 ।।  
tasmāttu parvadarśe vai paurṇamāsañca yogibhiḥ।  
ubhayoḥ pakṣayorjyeṣṭhamātmano hitakāmyayā ।। 21.63 ।।  
darśañca paurṇamāsañca ye yajanti dvijātayaḥ।  
na teṣāṃ punarāvṛttirbrahmalokāt kadācana ।। 21.64 ।।  
yo'nāhitāgniḥ prayato vīrādhvānaṃ gatopi vā ।  
samādhāya manastīvraṃ mantramuccārayecchanaiḥ ।। 21.65 ।।  
tvamagne rudro asuro mahī divastvaṃ śarvvo mārutaṃ pṛṣṭa īśiṣe।  
tvaṃ pāśagandharvvaśiṣaṃ pūṣā vidhattapāsinā।  
ityeva mantraṃ manasā samyaguccārayed‌dvijaḥ।  
agniṃ praviśate yastu rudralokaṃ sa gacchati ।। 21.66 ।।  
somaścāgnistu bhagavān kālo rudra iti śrutiḥ।  
tasmādyaḥ praviśedagniṃ sa rudrānna nivarttate ।। 21.67 ।।  
aṣṭāviṃśatimaḥ kalpo bṛhadityabhisaṃjñitaḥ।  
brahmaṇaḥ putrakāmasya sraṣṭukāmasya vai prajāḥ।  
dhyāyamānasya manasā bṛhatsāma rathantaram ।। 21.68 ।।  
yasmāttatra samutpanno bṛhataḥ sarvvatomukhaḥ।  
tasmāttu bṛhataḥ kalpo vijñeyastattvacintakaiḥ ।। 21.69 ।।  
aṣṭāśītisahasrāṇāṃ yojanānāṃ pramāṇataḥ ।  
rathantarantuvijñeyaṃ paramaṃ sūryyamaṇḍalam।  
tasmādaṇḍantu vijñeyamabhedyaṃ sūryamaṇḍalam ।। 21.70 ।।  
yutsūryyamaṇḍalañcāpi bṛhatsāma tu bhidyate।  
bhitvā caivaṃ dvijā yānti yogātmāno dṛḍhavratāḥ।  
saṅghātamupanītāśca anye kalpā rathantare ।। 21.71 ।।  
ityetattu mayā proktaṃ citramadhyātmadarśanam।  
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi kalpānāṃ vistaraṃ śubham ।। 21.72 ।।  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte kalpanirūpaṇaṃ nāmaikaviṃśodhyāyaḥ ।। 21 ।

title: 022

---
।। ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ।।  
atyadbhutamidaṃ sarvvaṃ kalpānānte mahāmune।  
rahasyaṃ vai samākhyātaṃ mantrāṇāñca prakalpanam ।। 22. 1 ।।  
  
na tavāviditaṃ kiñcit triṣu lokeṣu vidyate।  
tasmādvistarataḥ sarvvāḥ kalpasaṃkhyā bravīhi naḥ ।। 22.2 ।।  
  
।।vāyuruvāca ।।  
atra vaḥ kathayiṣyāmi kalpasaṃkhyāṃ yathā tathā।  
yugāgraṃ ca varṣāgrantu brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ ।। 22.3 ।।  
  
ekaṃ kalpasahasrantu brahmaṇo'bdaḥ prakīrttitaḥ।  
etadaṣṭasahasrantu brahmaṇastadyugaṃ smṛtam ।। 22.4 ।।  
  
ekaṃ yugasahasrantu savanaṃ tat prajāpateḥ।  
savanānāṃ sahasrantu dviguṇaṃ trivṛtaṃ tathā ।। 22.5 ।।  
  
brahmaṇaḥ sthitikālasya caitat sarvaṃ prakīrtitam।  
tasya saṃkhyāṃ pravakṣyāmi purastādvai yathākramam ।। 22.6 ।।  
  
aṣṭāviṃśatirye kalpā nāmataḥ parikīrtitāḥ।  
taiṣāṃ purastādvakṣyāmi kalpasaṃjñāṃ, yathākramam ।। 22.7 ।।  
  
rathantarasya sāmnastu upariṣṭānnibodhata।  
kalpānte nāma dheyāni mantrotpattiśca yasya yā ।। 22.8 ।।  
  
ekonaviṃśakaḥ kalpo vijñeyaḥ śrṛtalohitaḥ।  
yasmiṃstat paramadhyānaṃ dhyāyato brahmaṇastathā ।। 22.9 ।।  
  
śvetoṣṇīṣaḥ śvetamālyaḥ śvetāmbaradharaḥ śikhī।  
utpannastu mahātejāḥ kumāraḥ pāvakopamaḥ ।। 22.10 ।।  
  
bhīmaṃ mukhaṃ mahāraudraṃ sughoraṃ śvetalohitam।  
dīptaṃ dīptena vapuṣā mahāsyaṃ śvetavarccasam ।। 22.11 ।।  
  
taṃ dṛṣṭvā puruṣaḥ śrīmān brahmā vai viśvatomukhaḥ।  
kumāraṃ lokadhātāraṃ viśvarūpaṃ maheśvaram ।। 22.12 ।।  
  
purāṇapuruṣaṃ devaṃ viśvātmā yogināṃ ciram।  
vavande devadeveśaṃ brahmā lokapitāmahaḥ ।। 22.13 ।।  
  
hṛdi kṛtvā mahādevaṃ paramātmānamīśvaram।  
sadyojātaṃ tato brahma brahmā vai sama cintayat।  
jñātvā mumoca deveśo hṛṣṭo hāsaṃ jagatpatiḥ ।। 22.14 ।।  
  
tato'sya pārśvataḥ śvetā ṛṣayo brahmavarccasaḥ।  
prādurbhūtā mahātmānaḥ śvetamālyānulepanāḥ ।। 22.15 ।।  
  
sunando nandakaśacavaiva viśvanando'tha nandanaḥ।  
śiṣyāste vai mahātmāno yaistu brahma tato vṛtam ।। 22.16 ।।  
  
tasyāgre śveta varṇābhaḥ śvetanāmā mahāmuniḥ।  
vijajñe'tha mahātejā yasmājjajñe narastvasau ।। 22.17 ।।  
  
tatra te ṛṣayaḥ sarvve sadyojātaṃ maheśvaram।  
tasmādviśveśvaraṃ devaṃ ye prapaśyanti vai dvijāḥ।  
prāṇāyāmaparā yuktā brahmaṇi vyavasāyinaḥ ।। 22.18 ।।  
  
te sarve pāpanirmmuktā vimalā brahmavarccasaḥ।  
brahmalo kamatikramya brahmalokaṃ vrajanti ca ।। 22.19 ।।  
  
।।vāyuruvāca।।  
tatastriṃśattamaḥ kalpo rakto nāma prakīrttitaḥ।  
rakto yatra mahātejā raktavarṇa madhārayat ।। 22.20 ।।  
  
dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ।  
prādurbhūto mahātejāḥ kumāro raktavigrahaḥ।  
raktamālyāmbaradharo raktanetraḥ pratāpavān ।। 22.21 ।।  
  
sa taṃ dṛṣṭvā mahādevaṃ kumāraṃ raktavāsasam।  
dhyānayogaṃ paraṅgatvā bubudhe viśvamīśvaram ।। 22.22 ।।  
  
sa taṃ praṇamya bhagavān brahmā paramayantritaḥ।  
vāmadevaṃ tato brahmā brahmātmakaṃ vyacintayat ।। 22.23 ।।  
  
evaṃ dhyāto mahādevo brahmaṇā parameṣṭinā।  
manasā prītiyuktena pitāmahamathābravīt ।। 22.24 ।।  
  
dhyāyatā putrakāmena yasmāttehaṃ pitāmahaḥ।

sthānābhimānino hyete tiṣṭhantīha prasaṃyamāt ।। 30.26 ।।  
agniṣvāttā barhiṣadaḥ pitaro dvividhāḥ smṛtāḥ।  
jajñāte ca pitṛbhyastu dve kanye lokaviśrute ।। 30.27 ।।  
menā ca dhāriṇī caiva yābhyāṃ viśvamidaṃ dhṛtam।  
pitaraste nije kanye dharmārthaṃ pradaduḥ śubhe।  
ta ubhe vrahmavādinyau yoginyau caiva te ubhe ।। 30.28 ।।  
agniṣvāttāstu ye proktāsteṣāṃ menā tu mānasī।  
dhāraṇī mānasī caiva kanyā barhiṣadāṃ smṛtā ।। 30.29 ।।  
merostu dhāraṇīṃ nāma patnyarthaṃ vyasṛjan śubhām।  
pitaraste barhiṣadaḥ smṛtā ye somapīthinaḥ ।। 30.30 ।।  
agniṣvāttāstu tāṃ menāṃ patnīṃ himavate daduḥ।  
smṛtāste vai tu dauhitrāstaddauhitrān nibodhata ।। 30.31 ।।  
menā himavataḥ patnī mainākaṃ sānvasūyata।  
gaṅgā saridvarā caiva patnī yā lavaṇo dadheḥ।  
mainākasyānujaḥ krauñcaḥ krauñcadvīpo yataḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 30.32 ।।  
merostu dhāraṇī patnī divyauṣadhisamanvitam।  
mandaraṃ suṣuve putraṃ tisraḥ kanyāśca viśrutāḥ ।। 30.33 ।।  
velā ca niyatiścaiva tṛtīyā cāyatiḥ punaḥ।  
dhātuścaivāyatiḥ patnī vidhāturniyatiḥ smṛtā ।। 30.34 ।।  
svāyambhuve'ntare pūrvantayorvai kīrtitāḥ prajāḥ।  
suṣuve sāgarādvelā kanyāmekāmaninditām ।। 30.35 ।।  
sāvarṇinā ca sāmudrī patnī prācīnabarhiṣaḥ।  
savarṇā sātha sāmudrī daśaprācīnabarhiṣaḥ।  
sarve pracetaso nāma dhanurvedasya pāragāḥ ।। 30.36 ।।  
teṣāṃ svāyambhuvau dakṣaḥ putratve jajñivān prabhuḥ।  
tryambakasyābhiśāpena cākṣuṣasyāntare manoḥ ।। 30.37 ।।  
etacchutvā tataḥ sūtamapṛcchacchāṃśapāyanaḥ।  
utpannaḥ sa kathaṃ dakṣo hyabhiśāpādbhavasya tu।  
cākṣuṣasyānvaye pūrvaṃ tannaḥ prabrūhi pṛcchatām ।। 30.38 ।।  
ityuktaḥ kathayāmāsa sūto dakṣāśritāṃ kathām।  
śāṃśapāyanamāmantrya tryambakācchāpakāraṇam ।। 30.39 ।।  
dakṣasyāsan sutā hyaṣṭau kanyā yāḥ kīrtitā mayā।  
svebyo gṛhebhyo hyānāyya tāḥ pitābhyarccayadgṛhe।  
tatastvabhyarcitāḥ sarvā nyavasaṃstāḥ piturgṛhe ।। 30.40 ।।  
tāsāṃ jyeṣṭā satī nāma patnī yā tryambakasya vai।  
nājuhāvātmajāṃ tāṃ vai dakṣo rudramabhidviṣan ।। 30.41 ।।  
akarotsa natiṃ dakṣe na kadācinmaheśvaraḥ।  
jāmātā śvaśure tasmin svabhāvāt tejasi sthitaḥ ।। 30.42 ।।  
tato jñātvā satī sarvāḥ svasraḥ prāptāḥ pitṝgṛham।  
jagāma sāpyanāhūtā satī tat svaṃ piturgṛham ।। 30.43 ।।  
tato'bravīt sā pitaraṃ devī krodhādamarṣitā।  
yavīyasībyo jyāyasīṃ kintu pūjāmimāṃ prabho।  
asammatāmavajñāya kṛtavānasi garhitām ।। 30.44 ।।  
ahaṃ jyeṣṭā variṣṭā hi na tvasat karttumarhasi।  
evamukto'bravīdenāṃ dakṣaḥ saṃraktalocanaḥ ।। 30.45 ।।  
tvantu śreṣṭha variṣṭā ca pūjyā bālā sadā mama।  
tāsāṃ ye caiva bharttāraste me bahumatāḥ sadā ।। 30.46 ।।  
brahnamaṣṭāśca tapiṣṭāśca mahāyogāḥ sudhārmikāḥ।  
guṇaiścaivādhikāḥ ślāghyāḥ sarve te tryambakātsati ।। 30.47 ।।  
vasiṣṭo'triḥ pulastyaśca aṅgirāḥ pulahaḥ kratuḥ।  
bhṛgurmarīciśca tathā śreṣṭā jāmātaro mama ।। 30.48 ।।  
tasyātmā sa ca te śarvo bhaktā cāsi hi taṃ sadā।  
tena tvāṃ na bubhūṣāmi pratikūlo hi me bhavaḥ ।। 30.49 ।।  
ityuvāca tadā dakṣaḥ saṃpramūḍhena cetasā।  
śāpārthamātmanaścaiva ye coktāḥ paramarṣayaḥ ।। 30.50 ।।  
tathoktā pitaraṃ sā vai kruddhā devīdamabravīt ।  
vāṅmanaḥkarmabhiryasmādaduṣṭāṃ māṃ vigarhase।  
tasmāttyajāmyahandehamidantāta tavātmajam ।। 30.51 ।।  
tatastenāvamānena satī duḥkhādamarṣitā।  
abravīdvacanaṃ tevī namaskṛtya maheśvaram ।। 30.52 ।।  
yatrāhamupapatsyehaṃ punardehena bhāsvatā।  
tatrāpyahamasaṃmūḍhā sambhūtā dhārmikī punaḥ।  
gaccheyaṃ dharmapatnītvaṃ tryambakasyaiva dharmataḥ ।। 30.53 ।।  
tatraivātha samāsīnā yuktātmānaṃ samādadhe।  
dhārayāmāsa cāgneyīṃ dhāraṇāṃ manasātmanaḥ ।। 30.54 ।।  
tata āgneyīsamutthena vāyunā samudīritaḥ।  
sarvāṅgebhyo viniḥ sṛtya vahnirbhasma cakāra tām ।। 30.55 ।।  
tadupaśrutya nidhanaṃ satyā devo'tha śūladhṛk।  
saṃvādañca tayorbuddhvā yāthātathyena śaṅkaraḥ।  
dakṣasyātha ṛṣīṇāñca cukopa bhagavān prabhuḥ ।। 30.56 ।।  
yasmādavamatā dakṣa matkṛte māna sā satī।  
praśastāścetarāḥ sarvāḥ svasutā bhartṛbhiḥ saha ।। 30.57 ।।  
tasmādvaivasvataṃ prāpya punareva maharṣayaḥ।  
utpatsyante dvitīye vai mama yajñe hyayonijāḥ ।। 30.58 ।।  
hute vai brahmaṇā śukte cākṣuṣasyāntare manoḥ।  
abhivyāhṛtya ca ṛṣīn dakṣamabhyagamat punaḥ ।। 30.59 ।।  
bhavitā cākṣuṣo rājā cāśruṣasya samanvaye।  
prācīnabarhiṣaḥ pautraḥ putraścaiva pracetasaḥ ।। 30.60 ।।  
dakṣa ityeva nāmnā tvaṃ mārṣāyāṃ janayiṣyasi।  
kanyāyāṃ śākhināñcaiva prāpte vai cākṣuṣe'ntare ।। 30.61 ।।  
।।dakṣa uvāca।।  
ahaṃ tatrāpi te vighnamācariṣyāmi durmate।  
dharmārthakāmayukteṣu karmasviha punaḥ punaḥ ।। 30.62 ।।  
yasmāt tvaṃ matkṛte krūramṛṣīn vyāhṛtavānasi।  
tasmātsārddhaṃ surairyajñe na tvāṃ yakṣyanti vai dvijāḥ ।। 30.63 ।।  
hutvāhutiṃ tataḥ krūraḥ apastyakṣyanti karmasu।  
ihaiva vatsyasi tathā divaṃ hitvā yugakṣayāt ।। 30.64 ।।  
।।rudra uvāca।।  
sarveṣāmeva lokānāṃ bhūrlokastvādirucyate।  
tamahaṃ dhārayiṣyāmi nideśāt parameṣṭinaḥ ।। 30.65 ।।  
asyāṃ kṣitau vṛtā lokāḥ sarve tiṣṭhanti bhāskarāḥ।  
tānahaṃ dhārayāmīha satataṃ na tavājñayā ।। 30.66 ।।  
cāturvarṇyaṃ hi devānāṃ te cāpyekatra bhuñjate।  
nāhaṃ taiḥ saha bhokṣyāmi tato dāsyanti te pṛthak।  
tato devaiḥ sa taiḥ sārddhaṃ nejyate pṛthagijyate ।। 30.67 ।।  
tato'bhivyāhṛto dakṣo rudreṇāmitatejasā।  
svāyambhuve'ntare tyaktvā utpanno mānuṣeṣviha ।। 30.68 ।।  
jñātvā gṛhapatiṃ dakṣaṃ jñānānāmīśvaraṃ prabhum।  
dakṣo nāma mahāyajñaiḥ so'yajaddaivataiḥ saha ।। 30.69 ।।  
atha devī satī yā tu prāpte vaivasvate'ntare।  
maināyāṃ tāmumāṃ devīṃ janayāmāsa śailarāṭ ।। 30.70 ।।  
sā tu devī satī pūrvaṃ tataḥ paścādumā'bhavat।  
saha vratā bhavasyaiṣā na tayā mucyate bhavaḥ।  
yāvadicchati saṃsthātuṃ prabhurmanvantareṣviha ।। 30.71 ।।  
mārīcaṃ kaśyapaṃ devī yathāhitiranuvratā।  
sādhyaṃ nārāyaṇaṃ śrīstu maghavantaṃ śacī yathā ।। 30.72 ।।  
viṣṇuṃ kīrtī ruciḥ sūryaṃ vasiṣṭhañcāpyarūndhatī।  
naitāstu vijahatyetān bhartṝn devyaḥ kathaṃcana।  
āvarttamānakalpeṣu punarjāyanti taiḥ saha ।। 30.73 ।।  
evaṃ prācetaso dakṣo jajñe vai cākṣuṣe'ntare।  
prācīnabarhiṣaḥ pautraḥ putraścaiva pracetasaḥ ।। 30.74 ।।  
daśabhyastu pracetobhyo mārṣāyāñca punarnṛpaḥ।  
jajñe rudrābhiśāpena dvitīye'sminniti śrutiḥ ।। 30.75 ।।  
bhṛgvādayastu te sarve jajñire vai maharṣayaḥ।  
ādye tretāyuge pūrvaṃ manorvaivasvatasya ha।  
devasya mahato yajñe rudrābhiśāpena dvitīye'sminniti śrutiḥ ।। 30.76 ।।  
iti sānuśayo'hyāsīttayorjātyantarāgataḥ।  
prajāpatestu dakṣasya tryambakasya ca dhīmataḥ ।। 30.77 ।।  
tasmānnānuśayaḥ kāryo vairiṣviha kadācana।  
jātyantaragatasyāpi bhāvitasya śubhāśubhaiḥ।  
jantuṃ na muñcati khyātistatra kāryaṃ vijānatā ।। 30.78 ।।  
।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।  
prācetasasya dakṣasya kathaṃ vaivasvate'ntare।  
vināśāmagamat sūta hayamedhaḥ prajāpateḥ ।। 30.79 ।।  
devyā mṛtyuṃ kṛtaṃ matvā kruddhaṃ sarvātmakaṃ prabhum।  
kathaṃ prasādayaddakṣaḥ sa yajñaḥ sādhitaḥ katham।  
etadveditumicchāmastanno brūhi yathātatham ।। 30.80 ।।  
।।sūta uvāca।।  
pūrā merordvijaśreṣṭāḥ śrṛṅgaṃ trailokyaviśrutam।  
jyotiṣkaṃ nāma sāvitraṃ sarvaratnavibhūṣitam ।। 30.81 ।।  
aprameyamanādhṛṣyaṃ sarvalokanamaskṛtam।  
tasmin devo giriśreṣṭe sarvadhātu vibhūṣite।  
paryaṅka iva vibhrājannupaviṣṭo babhūva ha ।। 30.82 ।।  
śailarājasutā cāsya nityaṃ pārśvasthitā'bhavat।  
ādhityāśca mahātmāno vasavaścāmitaujasaḥ ।। 30.83 ।।  
tathaiva ca mahātmānāvaśvinau bhiṣajāṃ varau।  
tathā vaiśravaṇo rājā guhyakaiḥ parivāritaḥ ।। 30.84 ।।  
yakṣāṇāmīśvaraḥ śrīmān kailāsanilayaḥ prabhuḥ।  
upāsate mahātmānaṃ uśanā ca mahāmuniḥ.  
sanatkumārapramukhāste caiva paramarṣayaḥ ।। 30.85 ।।  
aṅgiraḥpramukhāścaiva tathā devarṣayo'pare।  
viśvāvasuśca gandharvastathā nāradaparvatau ।। 30.86 ।।  
apsarogaṇasaṅghāśca samājagmuranekaśaḥ।  
vavau śivaḥ sukho vāyurnānāgandhavahaḥ śuciḥ ।। 30.87 ।।  
sarvartukusumopetāḥ puṣpavanto drumā stathā।  
tathā vidyādharāścaiva siddhāścaiva tapodhanāḥ ।। 30.88 ।।  
mahādevaṃ paśupatiṃ paryupāsanti tatra vai।  
bhūtāni ca tathānyāni nānārūpadharāṇyatha ।। 30.89 ।।  
rākṣasāśca mahāraudrāḥ piśācāśca mahābalāḥ।  
bahurūpadharā hṛṣṭā nānāpraharaṇodyatāḥ ।। 30.90 ।।  
devasyānucarāstatra tasthurvaiśvānaropamāḥ।  
nandīśvaraśca bhagavān devasyānuma te sthitaḥ ।। 30.91 ।।  
pragṛhya jvalitaṃ śūlaṃ dīpyamānaṃ svatejasā।  
gaṅgā ca saritāṃ śveṣṭā sarvatīrthajalodbhavā।  
paryupāsata tandevarūpiṇī dvijasattamāḥ ।। 30.92 ।।  
evaṃ sa bhagavāṃstatra dīpyamānaḥ surarṣibhiḥ।  
devaiśca sumahābāgairmahādevo vyavasthitaḥ ।। 30.93 ।।  
purā himavataḥ pṛṣṭe dakṣo vai yajñamārabhat।  
gaṅgādvāre śubhe deśe ṛṣisiddhaniṣevite ।। 30.94 ।।  
tatastasya makhe devāḥ śatakratupurogamāḥ।  
gamanāya samāgamya buddhimāpedire tadā ।। 30.95 ।।  
svairvimānairmahātmāno jvaladbhirjvalanaprabhāḥ।  
devasyānumane'gacchan gaṅgādvāra iti śrutiḥ ।। 30.96 ।।  
gandharvāpsarasākīrṇaṃ nānādrumalatāvṛtam।  
ṛṣisaṅghaiḥ parivṛtaṃ dakṣaṃ dharmabhṛtāṃ varam ।। 30.97 ।।  
pṛthivyāmantarikṣe vā ye ca svarlokavāsinaḥ।  
sarve prāñjalayo bhūtvā upatasthuḥ prajāpatim ।। 30.98 ।।  
ādityā vasavo rudrāḥ sādhyāḥ saha marudgaṇaiḥ।  
jiṣṇunā sahitāḥ sarve āgatā yajñabhāginaḥ ।। 30.99 ।।  
ūṣmapāḥ somapāścaiva ājyapā dhūmapāstathā।  
aśvinau pitaraścaiva āgatā brahmaṇā saha ।। 30.100 ।।  
ete cānye ca bahavo bhūtagrāmāstathaiva ca।  
jarāyujāṇḍajāścaiva svedajodbhijjakāstathā ।। 30.101 ।।  
āhūtā mantrataḥ sarve devāśca saha patnibhiḥ।  
virājante vimānasthā dīpyamānā ivāgnayaḥ ।। 30.102 ।।

naraḥ pāpamavāpnoti mahadvai nātra saṃśayaḥ ।। 30.103 ।।  
evamuktvā tu viprarṣiḥ punardakṣamabhāṣata।

।।dakṣa uvāca।।  
santi me bahavo rudrāḥ śūlahastāḥ kaparddinaḥ।  
ekādaśāvasthagatā nānyaṃ vejhi maheśvaram ।। 30.105 ।।  
।।dadhīca uvāca।।

yathāhaṃ śaṅkarādūrddhvaṃ nānyaṃ paśyāmi daivatam।  
tathā dakṣasya vipulo yajño'yaṃ na bhaviṣyati ।। 30.106 ।।  
।।dakṣa uvāca।।  
etanmakhe śūra suvarṇapātre haviḥ samastaṃ vidhimantrapūtam।

gatāstu devatā jñātvā śailarājasutā tadā।

।।umovāca।।

।।maheśvara uvāca।।

hayamedhena yajate tatra yānti divaukasaḥ ।। 30.110 ।।  
।।devyuvāca।।

।।maheśvara uvāca ।।

yajñeṣu mama sarveṣu na bhāga upakalpitaḥ ।। 30.112 ।।  
pūrvopāyopapannena mārgeṇa varavarṇini।

।।devyuvāca।।  
bhagavan sarvadeveṣu prabhāvānadhiko guṇaiḥ ।  
ajeyaścāpyadhṛṣyaśca tejasā yaśasā śriyā ।। 30.114 ।।  
anena tu mahābhāga pratiṣedhena nāmataḥ ।  
atīva duḥkhamāpannā vepathuśca mamānagha ।। 30.115 ।।  
kiṃ nāma dānaṃ niyamantapo vā kuryyāmahaṃ yena patirmamādya।

।।vāyu ruvāca।।  
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi praṇavasya viniśvayam।  
oṅkāramakṣaraṃ brahma trivarṇañcāditaḥ smṛtam ।। 32.1 ।।  
  
yo yo yasya yathā varṇo vihito devatāstathā।  
ṛco yajūṃṣi sāmāni vāyuragnistathā jalam ।। 32.2 ।।  
  
tasmāttu akṣarādeva punaranye prajajñire।  
caturddaśa mahātmāno devānāṃ ye tu daivatāḥ ।। 32.3 ।।

parivarttamānaistaireva bhramamāṇeṣu cakravat ।। 32.7 ।।

tasmāccatuḥśataṃ sandhyā sandhyāṃśaśca yathāvidhaḥ ।। 32.56 ।।  
  
tretādīni sahasrāṇi saṃkhyayā munibhiḥ saha।  
tasyāpi triśatī sandhyā sandhyāṃśastriśataḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 32.57 ।।  
  
anuṣaṅgapādastretāyāstrisāhasrastu saṅkhyayā।  
dvāpare dve sahasre tu varṣāṇāṃ samprakīrtitam ।। 32.58 ।।  
  
tasyāpi dviśatī sandhyā sandhyāṃśo dviśatastathā।  
upodvātastṛtīyastu dvāpare pāda ucyate ।। 32.59 ।।  
  
kaliṃ varṣasahasrantu prāhuḥ saṅkhyāvido janāḥ।  
tasyāpi śatikā sandhyā sandhyāṃśaḥ śatameva ca ।। 32.60 ।।  
  
saṃhārapādaḥ saṃkhyātaśvaturtho vai kalau yuge।  
sasandhyāni sahāṃśāni catvāri tu yugāni vai ।। 32.61 ।।  
  
etad dvādaśasāhasraṃ caturyugamiti smṛtam।  
evaṃ pādaiḥ sahasrāṇi ślokānāṃ pañca pañca ca ।। 32.62 ।।  
  
sandhyāsandhyāṃśakaireva dve sahasre tathā'pare।  
evaṃ dvādaśasāhasraṃ purāṇaṃ kavayo viduḥ ।। 32.63 ।।  
  
yathā vedaścatuṣpādaścatuṣpādaṃ tathā yugam।  
yathā yugaṃ catuṣpādaṃ vidhātrā vihitaṃ svayam।  
chacatuṣpādaṃ surāṇāntu brahmaṇā vihitaṃ purā ।। 32.64 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte yugadharmanirūpaṇaṃ nāma dvātriṃśo 'dhyāyaḥ ।। 32 ।

।।sūta uvāca।।  
manvantareṣu sarveṣu atītānāgateṣviha।  
tulyābhimāninaḥ sarve jāyante nāmarūpataḥ ।। 33.1 ।।  
  
devāśca vividhā ye ca tasmin manvantare'dhipāḥ।  
ṛṣayo mānavāścaiva sarve tulyābhimāninaḥ ।। 33.2 ।।  
  
maharṣisargaḥ prokto vai vaṃśaṃ svāyambhuvasya tu।  
vistareṇānupūrvyā ca kīrttyamānaṃ nibodhata ।। 33.3 ।।  
  
manoḥ svāyambhuvasyāsan daśa pautrāstu tatsamāḥ.  
yairiyaṃ pṛthivī sarvā saptadvīpasamanvitā ।। 33.4 ।।  
  
sasamudrākaravatī prativarṣanniveśitā।  
svāyambhuve'ntare pūrvamādye tretāyuge tadā ।। 33.5 ।।  
  
priyavratasya putraistaiḥ pautraiḥ svāyambhuvasya tu।  
prajāsargatapoyogaistairiyaṃ viniveśitā ।। 33.6 ।।  
  
priyavratāt prajāvantaḥ vīrāt kanyā vyajāyata।  
kanyā sā tu mahābhāgā karddamasya prajāpateḥ ।। 33.7 ।।  
  
kanye dve śataputrāṃśca samrāṭ kukṣiśca te ubhe।  
taryorvai bhrātaraḥ śūrāḥ prajāpatisamā daśa ।। 33.8 ।।  
  
agnīdhraścavapuṣmāṃśca medhā medhātithirvibhuḥ।  
jyotiṣmān dyutimān havyaḥ savanaḥ sarva eva ca ।। 33.9 ।।  
  
priyavrato'bhiṣicyaitān sapta saptasu pārthivān।  
dvīpeṣu teṣu dharmeṇa dvīpāṃstāṃśca nibodhata ।। 33.10 ।।  
  
jambūdvīpeśvaraṃ cakre agnīdhrantu mahābalam।  
plakṣadvīpeśvaraścāpi tena medhātithiḥ kṛtaḥ ।। 33.11 ।।  
  
śālmalau tu vapuṣmantaṃ rājānamabhiṣiktavān।  
jyotiṣmantaṃ kuśadvīpe rājānaṃ kṛtavān prabhuḥ ।। 33.12 ।।  
  
dyutimantañca rājānaṃ kraujcadvīpe samādiśat।  
śākadvīpeśvarañcāpi havyañcakre priyavrataḥ ।। 33.13 ।।  
  
puṣkarādhipatiñcāpi savanaṃ kṛtavān prabhuḥ।  
puṣkare savanasyāpi mahāvītaḥ suto'bhavat।  
dhātakiścaiva dvāvetau putrau putravatāṃ varo ।। 33.14 ।।  
  
mahāvītaṃ smṛtaṃ varṣaṃ tasya nāmnā mahātmanaḥ।  
nāmnā tu dhātakeścāpi dhātakīkhaṇḍa ucyate ।। 33.15 ।।  
  
havyo vyajanayat putrān śākadvīpeśvarān prabhuḥ।  
jaladañca kumārañca sukumāraṃ maṇīcakam।  
vasumodaṃ sumodākaṃ saptamañca mahādrumam ।। 33.16 ।।  
  
jaladaṃ jaladasyātha varṣaṃ prathamamucyate।  
kumārasya ca kaumāraṃ dvitīyaṃ parikīrtitam ।। 33.17 ।।  
  
sukumāraṃ tṛtīyantu sukumārasya kīrtitam।  
maṇīcakasya caturthaṃ maṇīcakamihocyate ।। 33.18 ।।  
  
vasumodasya vai varṣaṃ pañcamaṃ vasumodakam।  
modākasya tu modākaṃ varṣaṃ ṣaṣṭhaṃ prakīrtitam ।। 33.19 ।।  
  
mahādrumasya nāmnā tu saptamantu mahādrumam।  
eṣāntu mānabhistāni saptavarṣāṇi tatra vai ।। 33.20 ।।  
  
krauñcadvīpeśvarasyāpi putrā dyutimatastu vai।  
kuśalo manugaścoṣṇaḥ pīvaraścāndhakārakaḥ।  
muniśca dundubhiścaiva sutā dyutimatastu vai ।। 33.21 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ svanāmabhirddeśāḥ krauñcadvīpāśrayāḥ śubhāḥ।  
uṣṇasyoṣṇaḥ smṛto deśaḥ pīvarasyāpi pīvaraḥ ।। 33.22 ।।  
  
andhakārakadeśastu andhakāraśca kīrttyate।  
munestu munideśo vai dundubherdundubhiḥ smṛtaḥ।  
ete janapadāḥ sapta krauñcadvīpe tu bhāsvarāḥ ।। 33.23 ।।  
  
jyotiṣmataḥ kuśadvīpe saptaite sumahaujasaḥ।  
udbhido veṇumāṃścaiva svairatho lavaṇo dhṛtiḥ।  
ṣaṣṭhaḥ prabhākaraścaiva saptamaḥ kapilaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 33.24 ।।  
  
udbhidaṃ prathamaṃ varṣaṃ dvitīyaṃ veṇumaṇḍalam।  
tṛtīyaṃ svairathākāraṃ caturthaṃ lavaṇaṃ smṛtam ।। 33.25 ।।  
  
pañcamaṃ dhṛtimadvarṣaṃ ṣaṣṭhaṃ varṣaṃ prabhākaram।  
saptamaṃ kapilaṃ nāma kapilasya prakīrtitam ।। 33.26 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ dvīpāḥ kuśadvīpe tatsanāmāna eva tu।  
āśramācārayuktābhiḥ prajābhiḥ samalaṃkṛtāḥ ।। 33.27 ।।  
  
śālmalasyeśvarāḥ sapta putrāste tu vapuṣmataḥ।  
śvetaśca haritaścaiva jīmūto rohitastathā।  
vaidyuto mānasaścaiva suprabhaḥ saptamastathā ।। 33.28 ।।  
  
śvetasya śvetadeśastu rohitasya ca rohitaḥ।  
jīmūtasya ca jīmūto haritasya ca hāritaḥ ।। 33.29 ।।  
  
vaidyuto vaidyutasyāpi mānasasyāpi mānasaḥ।  
suprabhaḥ suprabhasyāpi saptaite deśapālakāḥ ।। 33.30 ।।  
  
saptadvīpe tu vakṣyāmi jambūdvīpādanantaram।  
sapta medhātitheḥ putrāḥ plakṣadvīpeśvarā nṛpāḥ ।। 33.31 ।।  
  
jyeṣṭhaḥ śāntabhayasteṣāṃ saptavarṣāṇi tāni vai।  
tasmācchāntabhayāccaiva śiśirastu sukhodayaḥ।  
ānandaśca dhruvaścaiva kṣemakaśca śivastathā ।। 33.32 ।।  
  
tāni teṣāṃ sanāmāni saptavarṣāṇi bhāgaśaḥ।  
niveśitāni taistāni pūrve svāyambhuve'ntare ।। 33.33 ।।  
  
medhātithestu putraistaiḥ saptadvīpanivāsibhiḥ।  
varṇāśramācārayuktāḥ plakṣadvīpe prajāḥ kṛtāḥ ।। 33.34 ।।  
  
plakṣadvīpādikeṣveva śākadvīpāntareṣu vai।  
jñeyaḥ pañcasu dharmo vai varṇāśramavibhāgaśaḥ ।। 33.35 ।।  
  
sukhamāyuśca rūpañca balaṃ dharmaśca nityaśaḥ।  
pañcasveteṣu dvīpeṣu sarvaṃ sādhāraṇaṃ smṛtam ।। 33.36 ।।  
  
saptadvīpaparikrāntaṃ jambūdvīpaṃ nibodhata।  
āgnīdhraṃ jyeṣṭhadāyādaṃ kanyāputraṃ mahābalam।  
priyavrato'bhyaṣiñcattaṃ jambūdvīpeśvaraṃ nṛpam ।। 33.37 ।।  
  
tasya putrā babhūvurhi prajāpatisamaujasaḥ।  
jyeṣṭo nābhi riti khyātastasya kimpuruṣo'nujaḥ ।। 33.38 ।।  
  
harivarṣastṛtīyastu caturtho'bhūdilāvṛtaḥ।  
ramyaḥ syātpañcamaḥ putro harinmān ṣaṣṭha ucyate ।। 33.39 ।।  
  
kurustu saptamasteṣāṃ bhadrāśvo hyaṣṭamaḥ smṛtaḥ।  
navamaḥ ketumālastu teṣāṃ deśānnibodhata ।। 33.40 ।।  
  
nābhestu dakṣiṇaṃ varṣaṃ himādvantu pitā dadau ।  
hema kūṭaṃ tu yadvarṣaṃ dadau kimpuruṣāya tat ।। 33.41 ।।  
  
naiṣadhaṃ yat smṛtaṃ varṣaṃ harivarṣāya taddadau।  
madhyamaṃ yatsumerostu sa dadau tadilāvṛte ।। 33.42 ।।  
  
nīlantu yat smṛtaṃ varṣaṃ ramyāyaitat pitā dadau ।  
śvetaṃ yaduttaraṃ tasmāt pitrā dattaṃ harinmate ।। 33.43 ।।  
  
yaduttaraṃ śrṛṅgavato varṣa tat kurave dadau ।  
varṣaṃ mālyavata ścāpi bhadrāśvāya nyavedayat ।। 33.44 ।।  
  
gandhamādanavarṣantu ketumāle nyavedayat।  
ityetāni mahāntīha navavarṣāṇi bhāgaśaḥ ।। 33.45 ।।  
  
āgnīdhrasteṣu sarveṣu putrāṃstānabhyaṣiñcata।  
yathākramaṃ sa dharmātmā tatastu tapasi sthitaḥ ।। 33.46 ।।  
  
ityetaiḥ sapbhiḥ kṛtsnāḥ saptadvīpā niveśitāḥ ।  
priyavratasya putraistaiḥ pautraiḥ svāyambhuvasya tu ।। 33.47 ।।  
  
yāni kimpuruṣādyāni varṣāṇyaṣṭau śubhāni tu।  
teṣāṃ svabhāvataḥ siddhiḥ sukhaprāyā hyayatnataḥ ।। 33.48 ।।  
  
viparyayo na teṣvasti jarāmṛtyubhayaṃ na ca ।  
dharmādharmau na teṣvāstāṃ nottamādhamamadhyamāḥ ।  
na teṣvasti yugāvasthā kṣetreṣveva tu sarvaśaḥ ।। 33.49 ।।  
  
nābherhi sargaṃ vakṣyāmi himāhve tannibodhata।  
nābhistvajanayat putraṃ meऱudevyāṃ mahādyutiḥ।  
ṛṣabhaṃ pārthivaśreṣṭhaṃ sarvakṣatrasya pūrvajam ।। 33.50 ।।  
  
ṛṣabhādbharato jajñe vīraḥ putraśatāgrajaḥ।  
so'bhiṣicyātha bharataḥ putraṃ prāvrājyamāsthitaḥ ।। 33.51 ।।  
  
himāhvaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ varṣaṃ bharatāya nyavedayat।  
tasmāttadbhārataṃ varṣaṃ tasya nāmnā vidurbudhāḥ ।। 33.52 ।।  
  
bharatasyātmajo vidvān sumatirnāma dhārmikaḥ।  
babhūva tasmiṃstadrājyaṃ bharataḥ saṃnyayojayat।  
putra saṃkrāmitaśrīko vanaṃ rājā viveśa saḥ ।। 33.53 ।।  
  
tejasastu sutaścāpi prajāpatiramitrajit।  
taijasasyātmajo vidvānindradyumna iti śrutaḥ ।। 33.54 ।।  
  
parameṣṭī sutaścātha nidhane tasya śobhanaḥ ।  
pratīhārakule tasya nāmnā jajñe tadanvayāt।  
pratihartteti vikhyāto jajñe tasyāpi dhīmataḥ ।। 33.55 ।।  
  
unnetā pratiharttustu bhuvastasya sutaḥ smṛtaḥ ।  
udgīthastasya putro'bhūtpratāviścāpi tatsutaḥ ।। 33.56 ।।  
  
pratāvestu vibhuḥ putraḥ pṛthustasya suto mataḥ ।  
pṛthoścāpi suto nakto naktasyāpi gayaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 33.57 ।।  
  
gayasya tu naraḥ putro narasyāpi suto virāṭ।  
virāṭ‌suto mahāvīryo dīmāṃstasya suto'bhavat ।। 33.58 ।।  
  
dhīmataśca mahān putro mahataścāpi bhauvanaḥ।  
bhauvanasya sutastvaṣṭā arijastasya cātmajaḥ ।। 33.59 ।।  
  
arijasya rajaḥ putraḥ śatajidrajaso mataḥ।  
tasya putraśataṃ tvāsīdrājānaḥ sarva eva te ।। 33.60 ।।  
  
viśvajyotiḥ pradhānāyaistairimā varddhitāḥ prajāḥ।  
tairidaṃ bhārataṃ varṣaṃ saptakhaṇḍaṃ kṛtaṃ purā ।। 33.61 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ vaṃśaprakūtaistu bhukteyaṃ bhāratī dharā।  
kṛtatretādiyuktāni yugākhyānyekasaptatiḥ ।। 33.62 ।।  
  
ye'tītāstairyugaiḥ sārddhaṃ rājānaste tadanvayāḥ।  
svāyambhuve'ntare pūrvaṃ śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ ।। 33.63 ।।  
  
eṣa svāyambhuvaḥ sargo yenedaṃ pūritaṃ jagat।  
ṛṣibhirdaivataiścāpi pitṛgandharvarākṣasaiḥ ।। 33.64 ।।  
  
yakṣabhūtapiśācaiśca manuṣyamṛgapakṣibhiḥ ।  
taiṣāṃ sṛṣṭiriyaṃ loke yugaiḥ saha vivarttate ।। 33.65 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte svāyambhuva vaṃśānukīrttanaṃ nāma trayastriśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 33 ।।
