carantyatithirūpeṇa devā yogī ca bhūtale
tasmādatithimāyāntam anugacchet kṛtāñjaliḥ
svāgatāsanapādyārghasnānānna śayanādibhiḥ
rūpānvitaṃ virūpaṃ vā malinaṃ malināmbaram
yogīndrāśaṅkayā nityam atithinna vicārayet
śivayogiśarīre tu nityaṃ sannihitaḥ śivaḥ
yogīndraṃ pūjayet tasmāt sa sākṣāt pūjitaḥ śivaḥ
yoginaṃ ye dviṣantyajñāḥ sarvapāpavyavasthitāḥ

ye śivajñānaniratān pradviṣanti manāgapi

ye cāpavādaṃ śṛṇvanti vimūḍhāḥ śivayoginām

dānaṃ tadvad pātreṣu vinyastaṃ niṣphalaṃ bhavet

mānavasya tathā janma jñānahīnasya niṣphalam
lohoḍupena prataran nimajjatyaudake yathā
tathā dātā grahītā ca patatyajñastathālpadhīḥ

adatvā jñāninaḥ pātraṃ nispṛhatvānna te'rthinaḥ

yasmāddātā prayātyūrdhvam adhastiṣṭhet pratigrahāta
dehītyeva vadannarthī janaṃ bodhayatīva saḥ
yadidaṃ kaṣṭam arthitvaṃ prāgadānaphalaṃ hi tat
bodhayanti na yāvatte dehīti kṛpaṇāñjanāḥ
avastheyam adānasya mā bhūdevaṃ bhavānapi
kalyāṇamitram āyāntam arthinaṃ ko na pūjayet
dānāt svargam avāpnoti ko'nya bandhurihārthinaḥ
ihāmutra phalenāpi dātāram anuyojayan
āyātyarthī gṛhandātuṃ kastanna pratipūjayet

ahanyahani mārgantaṃ ko na manyadguruṃ yathā
mārjanaṃ darpaṇasyeva yaḥ karoti dine dine
karamuttānakaṃ kṛtvā dānaṃ gṛhṇāti yo dvijaḥ
dānādūrdhvagatistena dātu darśayatīva saḥ
ekena tiṣṭhatyadhastadanyenopiri tiṣṭhati
dātṛyācakayorbhedaḥ karābhyāmeva sūcitaḥ

sa vaṇigdharmayuktatvānna dātā paramārthataḥ
yadyarthino narā na syur dānakarma kathaṃ bhavet

kva nu dānaṃ dayā kṣāntirbhāvyate sādhubhistadā
yadi nāma na deyaṃ syāt samprāptāyārthine gṛhe

pratyutthānābhigamanaṃ svāgataṃ svāsanaṃ priyam
pādodakam anuvrajyā svargasopānapustakam
vittaṃ cittānusāreṇa kadā kasya bhaviṣyati
grāsādardhamapi grāsaṃ yuktaṃ dātuṃ sadārthinām
dānaṃ priyavinirmuktaṃ naṣṭam āhur manīṣiṇaḥ
tasmāt sakṛtpradātavyam anantaphalamicchatā
pratyākhyānam api śreyaḥ priyānunayapeśalam
na tu dānam asatkāra pāruṣyamalinīkṛtam
varaṃ na dattamarthibhyaḥ sakrodhenāntarātmanā
na taddhanaṃ na ca prītirna dharmaḥ priyavarjitaḥ
dānaṃ vratāni niyamo yajñā dhyanaṃ hutaṃ tapaḥ
yatnenāpi kṛtaṃ sarvaṃ krodhanasya vṛthā bhaveta
yaḥ śraddhayārcitaṃ dadyāt pratigṛhṇāti cārcitaḥ
tāvubhau gacchataḥ svargaṃ narakaṃ tadviparyayāt
audāryaṃ svāgataṃ maitrīm anukampāmamatsaram
guṇānāvahate pañca dāturdānaṃ mahāphalam
vārāṇasī kurukṣetraṃ prayāgaḥ puṣkarāṇi ca
gaṅgā samudratīrañca naimiṣāmarakaṇṭakāḥ
śrīparvato mānasañca gokarṇo vedaparvataḥ
ityādyāḥ kīrtitā deśāḥ surasiddhaniṣevitāḥ
sarve śivāśramāḥ puṇyāḥ sarvā nadyaḥ saparvatāḥ
gosiddharṣinivāsāśca deśāḥ puṇyāḥ prakīrtitāḥ
śivāyatanasaṃsthānāṃ yad alpam api dīyate
tadanantaphalaṃ jñeyaṃ śivakṣetrānubhāvataḥ
grahaṇañ candrasūryābhyāṃ dakṣiṇāyanam uttaram
viṣuvatsavyatīpātaṃ ṣaḍaśītimukhaṃ tathā
dinakṣayāṇi saṃkrāntirjñeyaṃ viṣṇupadaṃ punaḥ
iti kālaḥ samākhyātaḥ puṃsāṃ puṇyavivardhanaḥ
bhaktirbhāvaḥ parā prītiḥ śivadharmaikatānatā
pratipattir iti jñeyaṃ śraddhāparyāyapañcakam
śraddhāmātraṃ ca jananī jñānasya sukṛtasya ca
tasmācchraddhāṃ samutpādya deyamakṣayamicchatā
yaddānaṃ śraddhayā pātre vidhivat pratipādanam
tadanantaphalaṃ jñeyam api bālāgramātrakam
ārteṣu dīneṣu guṇānviteṣu yacchaddhayāsvalpamapi pradattam
tatsarvakāmān samupaiti loke śraddhaiva dānaṃ pravadanti tajjñāḥ
śraddhāpradhānaṃ vijñeyaṃ satpātrādicatuṣṭayam
śraddhātaḥ kīrtitā tasmānnāśraddhātaḥ phalaṃ bhavet
iti śivadharmottare satpātrapradānaścaturtho'dhyāyaḥ
atha paśupatimate śivadharme mahāśāstre
śivadharmottare śivapuradharmagatirnāma pañcamo'dhyāyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāyaḥ
atha dharmāḥ śivenoktāḥ śivadharmāgamottare
jñeyā bahuvidhās te ca karmayogopabhedataḥ
hiṃsādoṣavinirmuktāḥ kleśāyāsavivarjitāḥ
dharmā bhūtahitāḥ śuddhāḥ susūkṣmāḥ sumahāphalāḥ
anantaśāvāvalitāḥ śivamūle ca saṃsthitāḥ
sarve sarvaguṇopetāḥ śivadharmāḥ sanātanāḥ
tārayanty aśivādyamād dhāryante śivabhāvitaiḥ
śivadharmāḥ smṛtās tasmāt saṃsārārṇavatāraṇāḥ
athāhiṃsā kṣamā satyaṃ hrīḥ śraddhendriyasaṃyamaḥ
dānam ijyā japo dhyānaṃ daśakaṃ dharmasādhanam
asamastaiḥ samastair vā śivadharmair anuṣṭhitaiḥ
śivaikasthānasamprāptair gatiścaikaiva kirtitā
yathā bhūḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ sthānaṃ sādhāraṇaṃ smṛtā
tathā tacchivabhaktānāṃ tulyaṃ śivapuraṃ smṛtam
yatheha sarvabhūtānāṃ bhogāḥ sādhāraṇāḥ smṛtāḥ
nānāpuṇyaviśeṣeṇa bhogāḥ śivapure tathā
śubhāśubhaphalañ cātra bhujyate karma dehibhiḥ
śivadharmasya caikasya phalan tatropabhujyate
yāpādṛgbhavetpuṇyaṃ śraddhāmātraviśeṣaṇam
bhogās tādṛgvidhāstasya jñeyāḥ sātiśayāḥ pure
sthānaprāptiphale tulye bhogāḥ sātiśayāḥ sthitāḥ
kuryāt puṇyaṃ mahat tasmān mahābhogajigīṣayā
sarvādhipatyamevaikaṃ tatra nāsti purottame
svātmabhogādhipatyaṃ syācchivaḥ sarvajagatpatiḥ
kecit tatraiva mucyante jñānayogaratā narāḥ
āvartante punaś cānye saṃsāre bhogatatparāḥ
tasmād vimokṣam anvicched bhogāsaktiṃ vivarjayet
viraktaḥ śāntacittātmā śivajñānam avāpnuyāt

teṣām api dadātīśa sthānaṃ bhāvānurūpataḥ

ye gītanṛtyaṃ kurvanti triścaturvā yadṛcchayā
śivāgratas tu te yānti gandharvabhavanan narāḥ

pāpīyān piśunaḥ kṣudraḥ sarvai brahmahaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ
devadvijagatāṃ bhūmiṃ parvabhuktāṃ harettu yaḥ
praṇaṣṭāmapi kālena tamāhurbrahmaghātakam
dvijavṛtyāpaharaṇe nyāyataḥ samupārjatam
brahmahatyāsamaṃ jñeyaṃ pātakaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ
adhīsa yaḥ śivajñānaṃ parityajati mūḍhadhīḥ
surāpānasamaṃ jñeyaṃ tasya pātakamulvaṇam
agnihotraparityāgī pañcayajñīyakarmaṇām
mātāpitṛparityāgī kūṭasākṣī sukṛdvadhaḥ
apriyaḥ śivabhaktānām abhakṣyasya ca bhakṣaṇaḥ
vane niraparādhānāṃ prāṇināñca nipātanam
gavālaye vane cāgniṃ pure grāme ca dīpayet
iti pāpāni ghorāṇi surāpānasamāni ca
dīnasarvasvaharaṇaṃ narastrīgajavājinām
gobhūratnatratānāmauṣadhīnāṃ rasasya ca
candanāgurukarpūrakastūrīpaṭavāsasām
hastanyāsāpaharaṇaṃ rakmasteyasamaṃ smṛtam
kanyānāṃ varayogyānāmadānaṃ sadṛśe vare
putramitrakalatreṣu gamanaṃ bhaginīṣu ca
kumārīsāhasaṃ ghoram antyajastrīniṣevanam
svavarṇāyāñca gamanaṃ gurutalpasamaṃ smṛtam

tāni pātakasaṃjñāni tadbhūtānyupapātakam
dvijāyārthaṃ pratijñāya na prayacchati yaḥ punaḥ

atimāno'tikopaśca dambhikatvaṃ kṛtaghnatā

tayordānañca kanyāyāstayoreva ca yācanam
putramitrakalatrāṇām adoṣe svāminastathā

gavāṃ kṣatriyavaiśyānāṃ strīśūdrāṇāṃ ca ghātane
śivāśramatarūpāñ ca puṣpāśamavināśanam

ityetaiḥ strīnarāḥ pāpair upapātakinaḥ smṛtāḥ

ye gobrāhmaṇakanyānāṃ svāmimitratapasvinām
antaraṃ yāntyakāryeṣu smṛtā nārakā narāḥ

paravyāpāraniratāḥ parastrī paradūṣakāḥ
dvijāya duḥkhaṃ kuryāt prahāramugdiracca yaḥ

ye pāpābhiratāḥ krūrāḥ ye ca hiṃsāpriyā narāḥ
pravyarthāṃ ye ca kurvanti dānayajñādikāṃ kriyām
goṣṭhāgnijalarathyāsu tarucchāyānageṣu ca
tyajanti ye purīṣādīn ārāmāyataneṣu ca
madyamāṃsaratā nityaṃ gītavādyaratā narāḥ
kelikīlābhujaṅgāśca randhrānveṣaṇatatparāḥ
vaṃśeṣṭakāśilākāṣṭhai svabhraiḥ śaṅkubhireva vā
ye mārgamuparundhanti parasminno haranti ca
kūṭaśāsanakartāraḥ kuṭakarmakriyāratāḥ
kūṭayuddhāḥ kuśāstrāṇi kūṭasaṃvyavahāriṇaḥ
dhanuṣaḥ śalyaśastrāṇāṃ yaḥ kartā yaśca vikrayī
nirdayo'tīva nṛtyeṣu paśūnāṃ damakāśca ye
mithyā pravadato vācamākarṇayati yaḥ śaniaḥ
svāmimitragurudrohī māyāvī capalaḥ śathaḥ
ye bhāryāputramitrāṇi bālavṛddhakṛśāturān
bhṛtyān atithibandhūśca tyaktvā'śnanti bubhukṣitān
yaḥ svayaṃ mṛṣṭamaśnāti viprāyānyat prayacchati
vṛthāpākaḥ sa vijñeyo brahmavādiṣu garhitaḥ
niyamān svayamadāya ye tyajanti jitendriyāḥ
pravakṣyā vasitā ye ca rahasyānāñca bhedakāḥ
ye tāḍayanti gāścaiva trāsayatni muhurmuhuḥ
durbalān na ca puṣṇanti praṇaṣṭān nānviṣanti ca
pīḍayantyatibhāreṇa sakṣatān vāhayanti ca
sārddhayāmācca parata abhukte ye ca bhuñjate
ye bhagnakṣatarogārtān gorūpāṃśca kṣudhāturān
na pālayanti te goghnā nārakāḥ pāpinaḥ smṛtāḥ
vṛṣaṇāṃ vṛṣaṇau ye ca pāpiṣṭhā gālayanti ca
vāhayanti ca gāṃ vandhyāṃ mahārārakiṇo narāḥ
āśāvāsamanuprāptān kṣuttṛṣṇāśramapīḍitān
ye'tirthāścāvamanyante te vai nirayagāminaḥ
anāthaṃ vikalaṃ dīnaṃ bālavṛddhākṛśāturān
nānukampanti ye mūḍhāste yānti nirayārṇavam
ajāviko māhiṣakaḥ sāmudrī vṛṣalīpatiḥ
śūdaviṭkṣatravṛttiśca nārakī syād dvijādhamaḥ
śilpinaḥ kārukā vaśyā hemakārā nṛpādhvajā
bhṛtakāḥ śāṭhyasaṃyuktāstathāntyā nārakāḥ smṛtāḥ
yaścocitamatikramya svacchāyā bāhyektaram
narakeṣu sa pacyeta yaśca daṇḍarucirnṛpaḥ
utkocikairadhikṛtaistaskaraiśca prapīḍyate
yasya rājñaḥ prajā rāṣṭre pacyate narakeṣu saḥ
ye dvijāḥ pratigṛhṇānti nṛpasyānyāyavartinaḥ
prayānti te'pi ghoreṣu narakeṣu na saṃśayaḥ
pāradārikacaurāṇāṃ yatpāpaṃ pārthivasya tat
bhavedarakṣataḥ sarvaṃ ghoraṃ tasya pratigrahāt
acauraṃ cauravat paśyeccaurān vā'caurarūpiṇaḥ
avicarya nṛpastasmād ghātayannarakaṃ vrajet
ghṛtatailānnapānāni madhumāṃsasurāsavam
guḍekṣukṣīraśākāni dadhimūlaphalāni ca
tṛṇakāṣṭhapatrapuṣpamauṣadhaṃ kāṃsyabhājanam
upānachatraśakaṭā yetayetmāsanaṃ gṛham
tāmrasīsatrapuktāṃsya śaṅkhādyañca jalodbhavam
vārkṣyaṃ vā vaiṇavādyaṃ vā gṛheṣu puṣkarāṇi ca
ūrṇākarpāsakauśeyabhaṅgapaṭṭodbhavāni ca
sthūlasūkṣmāṇi vastrāṇi ye lobhena haranti ca
evamādīni cānyāni dravyāṇi vividhāni ca
narakeṣu dhruvaṃ gacched apahṛtyālpakāny api
yadvā tadvā paradravyamapi sarṣapamātrakam
apahṛtya naro yāti naraknnātra saṃśayaḥ
evamādyair naraḥ pāpair vyākrāntaiḥ samanantaram
śrarīraṃ yātanārthīyaṃ pūrvākāramavāpnuyāt
yamalokaṃ vrajaṃstena śarīreṇa yamājñayā
yamadūtair mahāghorair nīyamānaḥ suduḥkhitaḥ
devamānuṣatiryāṇām adharmaniratātmanām
dharmarājaḥ smṛtaḥ śāstā sughorair vividhair vadhaiḥ
vinayācārayuktānāṃ pramodaskhalitātmanām
prāyaścittair guruḥ śāstā na vadhair iṣyate yamaḥ
pāradārikacaurāṇām anyāyavyavahāriṇām
nṛpatiḥ śāsakaḥ proktaḥ pracchannānāñca dharmarāṭ
tasmāt kṛtasya pāpasya prāyaścittaṃ samācaret
nābhuktasyānyathā nāśaḥ kalpakoṭiśatairapi

kāyena manasā vācā tasyordhā gotate phalam

pāpagatir nāma saptamo'dhyāyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāya

divyavastraparīdhānā yānti vastrapradāyinaḥ

devāgniguruviprāṇāṃ mātu pituśca pūjakāḥ

sarvakāmasamṛddhātmā bhūmidānena gacchati

ṛtukāle sadā śukraṃ nirdoṣaṃ yonisaṃsthitaṃ
tadā tadvāyunā spṛṣṭaṃ strīraktenaikatāṃ vrajet
visargakāle śukrasya jīvaḥ kāraṇasaṃyutaḥ
vṛttyā praviśale yoniṃ karmabhiḥ svairnniyojitaḥ
tac chukraṃ raktam ekastham ekānhāt kalkalam bhavet
pañcarātreṇa kalalama
budbudaṃ saptarātreṇa māṃsapeśī bhavet tataḥ
dvisaptāhād bhavet peśā raktamāṃsācitādṛḍhā
bījasyevāṅkuraḥ peśyāṃ pañcaviṃśatirātritaḥ
bhavanti māsamātreṇa pañcadhā jāyate punaḥ
grīvā śiraś ca skandhaś ca pṛṣṭhavaṃśastathodaraṃ
māsadvayena sarvāṇi kramaśaḥ sambhavanti ca
tribhir māsaiḥ prajāyante saddravyāṅkerasandhayaḥ
māsaiś caturbhir aṅgulyaḥ prajāyante yathākramaṃ
mukhaṃ nāsā ca karṇau ca māsair jāyanti pañcabhiḥ
dantapaṅktis tathā guhyaṃ jāyante ca nakhā punaḥ
vibhaktāvayavaḥ spaṣṭaḥ punar māsāṣṭakena ca
pañcātmakasamāyuktaḥ paripakva satiṣṭhati
māturāhāravīryeṇa ṣaḍvidhena rasenaca
nābhisūtranibandhena varddhate sa dine dine
tataḥ smṛtiṃ labhej jīvaḥ sampūrṇe'smin śarīrake
sukhaduḥkhaṃ vijānāti nidrāsvapnaṃ purā kṛtaṃ
mṛtaś cāhaṃ punarjāto jātaś cāhaṃ punarmṛtaḥ
nānāyonisahasrāṇi mayā dṛṣṭāni jāyate
adhunā jātamātro'haṃ prāpte saṃskāra eva vā
tat tac chreyaḥ kariṣyāmi yena gacche na sambhave
garbhasthaś cintayaty evam ahaṃ garbhād viniḥsṛtaḥ
adhyeṣyāmi śivajñānaṃ saṃsāravinivartakaṃ
evaṃ sa garbhaduḥkhena mahatā paripīḍitaḥ
jīvaḥ karmavaśād āste mokṣopāyaṃ vicintayan
yathā girivarākrāntaḥ kaś cid duḥkhena tiṣṭhati
tathā jarāyuṇā dehī duḥkhan tiṣṭhati veṣṭitaḥ
patitaḥ sāgare yadvad duḥkham āste samākulaḥ
garbhodakena siktāṅgas tathāste vyākulaḥ pumān
lohakumbhe yathā nyastaḥ pacyate kaścid agninā
garbhakumbhe tathā kṣiptaḥ pacyate jaṭhar āgninā
sūcibhir agnivarṇābhir vibhinnasya nirantaraṃ
yad duḥkhan tu prajāyeta tadgarbhe'ṣṭaguṇambhavet
garbhavāsāt paro vāsaḥ kaṣṭenaivātra kutracit
dehinā duḥkhabahulaṃ sughoram atisaṅkaṭaṃ
ityetad garbhaduḥkhaṃ hi prāṇināṃ parikīrtitaṃ
calāsthirāṇāṃ sarveṣām ātmagarbhānurūpataḥ
garbhāt koṭiguṇaṃ duḥkhaṃ yoniyantraprapīḍanāt
sammūrcchitasya jāyeta jāyamānasya dehinaḥ
śarīraṃ pīḍyamānasya yantreṇaiva samantataḥ
śirasā tāḍyamānasya pāpam udgarakeṇa ca
garbhāt koṭiguṇaṃ duḥkhaṃ paritrāṇam avindataḥ
yantreṇa pīḍitā yadvan niḥsārāḥ syustilekṣavaḥ
tathā śarīraṃ niḥsāraṃ yoniyantraprapīḍitaṃ
asthipaṭatulāstambhasnāyubandhena yantritaṃ
raktamāṃsamṛdāliptaṃ viṇmūtradravyabhājanaṃ
keśaromatṛṇācchannaṃ rogāyatanamāturaṃ
badanaikamahādvāraṃ gavākṣāṣṭaka bhūṣitaṃ
oṣṭhadvayakapāṭaṃ ca dantajihvārggalānvitaṃ
nāḍisvedapravāhaṃ ca kaphapittapariplutaṃ
jarāśokasamāviṣṭakālavaktrānalombhitaṃ
kāmakrodhendhanākrāntaṃ vyasanaiś copamarditam
bhogatṛṣṇāturaṃ mūḍhaṃ rāgadveṣavaśānugaṃ
saṃvartitāṅgapratyaṅgajarāyupariveṣṭitaṃ
saṅkaṭena viviktena yonimārgeṇa nirgataṃ
viṇmūtraraktasiktāṅgaṃ ṣaṭkośāṃśasamudbhavaṃ
asthipañjarasaṃkhyāntaṃ jñeyam asmin kalevare
śatatrayaṃ ṣaṣṭyadhikaṃ pañca peśīśatāni ca
sārddhābhis tisṛbhiś channaṃ samantād romakoṭibhiḥ
śarīraṃ sthūlasūkṣmābhir dṛśāś cayāvatāḥ sthitāḥ
tāvat ībhiś ca nāḍīnāṃ koṭibhis tatsamanvitaṃ
prasvedam aśucir yābhir antasthaṃ sravate vahiḥ
dvātriṃśad daśamāḥ proktāḥ viṃśatiś ca nakhāḥ smṛtāṃ
pittasya kuḍavaṃ jñeyaṃ kaphasyārddhāḍhakaṃ smṛtaṃ
vasāḥpalāni viṃśac ca tad arddhe kalalasya ca
pañcābrade palājñeyāḥ palāni daśa medasaḥ
palatrayaṃ mahāraktamajjāyās tu caturguṇaṃ
śukrārddhaṃkuḍavaṃ jñeyaṃ tadvīryan dehināṃ varaṃ
māṃsāsthnām ekapiṇḍena palasāhasratram ucyate
raktaṃ palaśataṃ jñeyaṃ viṇmūtrañ cāpramāṇataḥ
iti dehagṛhaṃ hy etan nityasyānityam ātmanaḥ
aviśuddhaṃ viśuddhasya karmabandhavinirmitaṃ
śukraśoṇitasaṃyogād dehaḥ sañjāyate yataḥ
nityaṃ viṇmūtrapūrṇaś ca tenāśuddhaḥ prakīrtitaḥ
yathāntarviṣṭhayā pūrṇaḥ śuciḥ syān na bahirghaṭaḥ
yatnataḥ śodhyamāno' pi deho'yamaśucis tathā
yaṃ prāpyāti pavitrāṇi pañcagavyaṃ havīṃṣi ca
aśucitvaṃ kṣaṇādyānti kim anyad aśucis tataḥ
hṛdy ānyapy annapānāni yaṃ prāpya surabhīṇi ca
aśucitvaṃ prayāntyāśu kim anyad aśucis tataḥ
hejanāḥ kin na paśyadhvaṃ yan niryāti dine dine
svadehāt kaśmalaṃ pūtis tad ādhāraḥ kathaṃ śuciḥ
dehaḥ saṃśodhyamāno'pi pañcagavyakuśāmbubhiḥ
ghṛṣyamāṇam ivāṅgāraṃ nirmalatvaṃ na gacchati
srotāṃsi yasya satataṃ pravahanti girer iva
kaphamūtrād yamaśuci sa dehaḥ śuddhyate kathaṃ
sarvāśucinidhānasya śarīrasya na vidyate
śucir ekaḥ pradeśo' pi viṇmūtrasya dṛter iva
smṛtvātmadehasrotāṃsi mṛttoyair dhāvataḥ karau
tathāpyaśuci bhāṇḍasya na virajyanti kiñjanāḥ
kāyaḥ sraggandhapuṣpādyair yatnenāpi susaṃskṛtaḥ
na jahāti svabhāvaṃ hi śvapuccham iva nāmitaṃ
yathā jātyaiva kṛṣṇārṇī na śuklāsyādupāyataḥ
tathā saṃśodhyamānāpi bhaven mūrtir anirmalā
jighrann api svadurgandhaṃ paśyann api svakalmalaṃ
na virajyati loko'yaṃ pīḍayann api nāsikāṃ
aho mohasya māhātmyaṃ yena vyāmohitaṃ jagat
jighrana paśyan svadoṣāṃś ca kāvyasya na virajyate
svadehāśucigandhena na virajyeta yo naraḥ
virāgakāraṇantasya kim anyad upadiśyate
sarvam etaj jagatpūtaṃ deha evāśuciḥ paraḥ
tatpalāvayavasparśāc chucir apyaśucir bhavet
gandhalepāpanodārthaṃ śaucaṃ dehasya kīrtitaṃ
dvayasyāpagamāt paścād bhāvaśuddhyā viśuddhyati
gaṇgātoyena sarveṇa mṛdbhāraiś ca na gopamaiḥ
āmṛtyorācaracchaicaṃ bhāvaduṣṭo na śuddhyati
tīrthasnānais tapobhir vā bhāvaduṣṭo na śuddhyati
adṛtiḥ kṣālitastīrthe kiṃ śuddhim adhigacchati
antarbhāvapraduṣṭasya viśato'pi hutāśane
na svargo nāpavargaś ca dehanirdahanam paraṃ
bhāvaśuddhiḥ paraṃ śaucaṃ pramāṇaṃ sarvakarmasu
anyathāliṅgyate kāntā bhāvena duhitānyathā
manasābhidyate bṛttir abhinneṣv api bastuṣu
anyathaiva stanaṃ putraś cintayatyanyathā patiḥ
paśya paśya ca bhāvasya mahābhāgyam aśoṣataḥ
pariṣvakto'pi yannāryā bhāvahīno na kāmayet
bhāvanā dvividhānasyambhakṣyāṇi surabhīṇi ca
abhāvena narastasmād bhāvaḥ sarvatra kāraṇaṃ
cittaṃ śodhaya yatnena kimanyairbāhyaśodhanaiḥ
bhāvataḥ suviśuddhātmā svargaṃ mokṣaṃ ca vindati
jñanām alāmbhasā puṃsaḥ sadvairāgyamṛdā punaḥ
avidyā rāgaviṇmūtralepagandhaviśodhanaṃ
evam etac charīraṃ hi nisargād aśuci smṛtaṃ
tvaṅmātrasāraṃ nissāraṃ kadalīsārasannibhaṃ
jñātvaivaṃ doṣavad dehaṃ yaḥ prājñaḥ śithilībhavet
so'tikrāmati saṃsāraṃ dṛḍhagrāhī ca tiṣṭhati
evam etan mahākaṣṭaṃ janmaduḥkhaṃ prarkīrtitaṃ
puṃsām ajñānadoṣeṇa nānākarmavaśena ca
garbhasthasya smṛtir yāsīt sā jātasya praṇaśyati
sammūrcchitasya duḥkhena yoniyantraprapīḍanāt
bāhyena vāyunā tasya mohājñānena dehinaḥ
spṛṣṭamātrasya ghoreṇa jvaraḥ samupajāyate
tena jvareṇa mahatā mahāmohaḥ prajāyate
sammūḍhasya smṛtibhraṃśaḥ śīghraṃ sañjāyate punaḥ
smṛtibhraṃśāt tatas tasya pūrvakarmavaśena ca
rasaḥ sañjāyate tūrṇaṃ jantos tatraiva janmani
rakto mūḍhaś caloko'yam akārye sampravartate
nacātmānaṃ vijānāti na paraṃ naca daivataṃ
na śṛṇoti paraṃ bhūyaḥ sati cakṣuṣi nekṣate
same pathi śanair gacchan skhalatīva pade pade
buddhau satyāṃ na jānāti bodhyamāno budhair api
saṃsāre kṣipyate tena rāgalobhavaśānugaḥ
gaṃgāsmṛter abhāvena śāstramuktaṃ śivena tu
tad dukhaḥ kathanārthāya svargamokṣaprasādhakaṃ
ye satyasmin śivajñāne sarvakāmārthasādhake
na kurvanty ātmanaḥ śreyas tadatraṃ mahad adbhutaṃ
avyaktendriyavṛtvitvād āste duḥkhaṃ mahatpunaḥ
icchann api na śaknoti vaktuṃ kartuṃ ca satkriyāṃ
dantotthāne mahad duḥkhaṃ maulena vyādhinā tathā
bālarogaiś ca vividhaiḥ pīḍābālagrahair api
tṛḍbubhukṣāparītāṅgaḥ kvacit tiṣṭhati cāraṭan
viṇmūtrabhakṣaṇādyaṃ ca mohād bālaḥ karoti vai
kaumāre karṇavedhe ca tanmātā pitṛtāḍanaṃ
akṣarādhyayanādyaiś ca duḥkhaṃ syād guruśāsanāt
pramattendriya bṛtteś ca kāmarāgaprapīḍanāt
rāgābṛtasya satataṃ kutaḥ saukhyaṃ hi yauvane
īrṣyayā ca mahad duḥkhaṃ mohādraktasya jāyate
netrasyākupitasyeva rāgo duḥkhāya kevalaṃ
na rātrau vindate nidrāṃ kāmāgniparikheditaḥ
divā cāsya kutaḥ saukhyam arthopārjanacintayā
striyāyāsitadehasya ye puṃsaḥ śukravindavaḥ
na te sukhāya mantavyaḥ khedajā iva bindavaḥ
kṛmibhiḥ khādyamānasya kuṣṭhinaḥ pāmitasya ca
kaṇḍūyānāgnitāpena yad bhavet strīṣu tad vidhaḥ
yādṛśaṃ manyate saukhyaṃ gaṇḍapūjya vinirgamāt
tādṛśaṃ strīṣu mantavyaṃ nādhikaṃ tāsu vidyate
gaṇḍasya vedanā yadvat sphuṭitasya nivartate
tadvat strīṣv api tad viddhi na saukhyaṃ paramārthataḥ
barccomūtrasamutsargāt sukhaṃ bhavati yādṛśaṃ
tādṛśaṃ strīṣu vijñeyaṃ mūḍhaiḥ kalpitam anyathā
nārīṣvavastubhūtāsu sarvadoṣāśrayāsu ca
nāṇumātrakamapy evaṃ sukham asti vicārataḥ
sammānam apamānena viyogena ca saṅgamaḥ
yauvanaṃ jarayā grastaṃ ka saukhyam anupadravaṃ
valīpalitakhālityaiḥ śithilīkṛtavigrahaḥ
sarvakriyāsvaśaktaś ca jarayā jarjarīkṛtaḥ
strīpuṃsor yauvanaṃ rupaṃ yad anyonyaṃ priyaṃ purā
tad eva jarayā grastam ubhayor api na priyaṃ
apūrvavat svamātmānaṃ jarayā parivartitaṃ
yaḥ paśyan na virajyeta ko'nyastas mādacetanaḥ
jarābhibhūtaḥ puruṣaḥ patnīputrādibāndhavaiḥ
aśaktatvād durācārair bhṛtyaiś ca paribhūyate
dharmam arthaṃ cakāmaṃ ca mokṣaṃ ca na jarīyataḥ
śaktaḥ sādhayituṃ tasmād yuvā dharmaṃ samācaret
vātapittakaphādīnāṃ vaiṣamyaṃ vyādhiriṣyate
vātādīnāṃ samūhaś ca deho'yaṃ parikīrtitaḥ
tasmād vyādhimayaṃ jñeyaṃ śarīram idam ātmane
vātādyavyatiriktatvād vyādhīnāṃ pañjarasya ca
rogair nānāvidhair yāni dehe duḥkhāny anekadhā
tāni naivātmavedyāni kim anyatkathayāmy ahaṃ
ekottaraṃ mṛtyuśatam asmin dehe pratiṣṭhitaṃ
tatraikaḥ kālasaṃjñaś ca śeṣāstvāg antavaḥ smṛtāḥ
ye tv ihāgantavaḥ proktās te praśāmyanti bheṣajaiḥ
japahomapradānaiś ca kālamṛtyur na śāmyati
yadi vā'pamṛtyur nna syād viṣayaḥ syād aśaṅkitaḥ
na cāsti puruṣas tasmād apamṛtyor vibheti yaḥ
vividhā vyādhayaḥ śastraṃ sarpādyāḥ prāṇinas tathā
viṣāṇi cābhicārāś ca mṛtyor dvārāṇi dūhinī
pīḍitaṃ sarvarogādyair api dhanvantariḥ svayaṃ
svasthī kartu nna śaknoti kālaprāptaṃ hi dehinaṃ
nauṣadhan na tapodānaṃ na mantrā na ca bāndhavāḥ
śaknuvanti paritrātuṃ naraṅkālaprapīḍitaṃ
rasāyanatapojapyair yogasiddhair mahātmabhiḥ
kālamṛtyur api prājñair jīyate nālasair naraiḥ
nāsti mṛtyusamaṃ duḥkhaṃ nāsti mṛtyusamaṃ bhayaṃ
nāsti mṛtyusamas trāsaḥ sarveṣām eva dehināṃ
sadbhāryāputramitrāṇi rājaiśvyaryasukhāni ca
abadhyāni ca bairāṇi mṛtryuḥ sarvāṇi kṛntati
henarāḥ kin na paśyadhvaṃ sahasrasyāpi madhyataḥ
janāḥ śatāyuṣaḥ pañca bhavanti na bhavanti vā
aśītikā vipacyante kecit saptatikā narāḥ
paramāyuḥ sthitaṃ ṣaṣṭis tad apy aniścitaṃ punaḥ
yasya yāvad bhaved āyur dehinaḥ pūrvakarmabhiḥ
tasyārddhaṃ cāyuṣo rārtri harate'rddhaṃ smṛto yamaḥ
bālabhāve ca mohena vārddhakye jarayā punaḥ
varṣāṇāṃ viṃśatiryāti dharmakāmārthavarjitāḥ
āgantukair bhayaiḥ puṃsāṃ vyādhiśokair anekadhā
hriyate'rddhaṃ ca tatrāpi yacchreyas tad vivarjitaṃ
jīvitānte ca maraṇaṃ mahāghoram avāpnuyāt
jāyate yonikoṭīṣu mṛtaḥ karmavaśāt punaḥ
dehabhedena yaḥ puṃsāṃ viyogaḥ karma saṃkṣayāt
maraṇaṃ tad vinirdiṣṭaṃ na nāśaḥ paramārthataḥ
mahātamaḥ praviṣṭasyac cidyamāneṣu marmasu
yad duḥkhaṃ maraṇe jantor nna tasyehopamā kvacit
hātāta mātarhākānte krandantyevaṃ suduḥkhitāḥ
maṇḍūkā iva sarpeṇa gīryante mṛtyunā janā
bāndhavaiḥ sa pariṣvaktaḥ priyaiś ca parivāritaḥ
niśvasandīrghomuṣṇaṃ ca mukhena pariśuṣyatā
caturantena khaṭvāyāḥ parivartan muhur muhuḥ
sa mūḍhaḥ kṣipate'ty arthaṃ hastapādānitas tataḥ
khaṭvāto vāñchate bhūmiṃ bhūmeḥ khaṭvāṃ punar mahīṃ
vivastras tyaktalajjaś ca mūtraviṣṭhānulepanaḥ
yācamānaś ca salilaṃ śuṣkakaṇṭhoṣṭhatālukaḥ
cintamānaś ca vittāni kasyaitāni mṛte mayi
khe nayaṃ ca balāt bhūyaḥ kālapāśena karṣitaḥ
mriyate paśyatām eva gale ghuraghurāyate
jīvas tṛṇajalaukeva dehād dehaṃ viśet kramāt
samprāpyottaram aṅgena dehaṃ tyajati pūrvikaṃ
narāṇāṃ prārthanāduḥkham adhikaṃ hi vivekinaḥ
kṣaṇikaṃ maraṇe duḥkham anantaṃ prārthanākṛtaṃ
jagatām patir arthitvād viṣṇur vāmanatāṅ gataḥ
adhikaḥ ko'paras tasmād yo na yāsyati lāghavaṃ
jñātaṃ mayedam adhunā mṛto bhavati yadguruḥ
na paraṃ prārthayed bhūyas tṛṣṇālāghavakāraṇaṃ
ādau duḥkhaṃ madhyaduḥkham ante duḥkhaṃ ca dāruṇaṃ
nisargād eva bhūtānām iti duḥkhaparamparā
vartamānāny atītāni duḥkhāny etāni yāni tu
narā na bhāvayanty ajñā na virajyanti tena te
aty āhārān mahāduḥkham anāhārāt tato'dhikaṃ
turitaṃ jīvitaṃ kaṣṭam anne'py evaṃ kutaḥ sukhaṃ
kṣudhā hi sarvarogāṇāṃ vyādhiḥ śreṣṭhatamaḥ smṛtaḥ
sā cānnauṣadhimātreṇa kṣaṇamātraṃ praśāmyati
kṣudvyādhivedanā tīvrā niḥ śeṣabalakṛntanī
te yānti bhūto mriyate yathānyair vyādhitor naraḥ
tad rasaṃ mihikā gandhāj jivhāgraparivartinī
tatkṣaṇārdhārdhakālena kaṇṭhaṃ prāpya nivartate
iti kṣudvyādhitaptānām annauṣadhabalaṃ smṛtaṃ
na tat sukhāya mantavyaṃ paramārthena paṇḍitaiḥ
mṛtopamaś ca yacchete sarvakāryavivarjitaḥ
tatrāpi tu kutaḥ saukhyaṃ tamasāc chāditātmanaḥ
prabodhe'pi kutaḥ saukhyaṃ kāryair upahatātmanaḥ
kṛṣisevādhvagorakṣāvāṇijyādipariśramaiḥ
prātaḥ purīṣamūtrābhyāṃ madhyānhe tṛḍbubhukṣayā
tṛptāhaṃ yena vādhyante nidrayā niśi jantavaḥ
arthasyopārjane duḥkhamarjitasya ca rakṣaṇe
nāśe duḥkhaṃ vyaye duḥkham arthe'py evaṃ kutaḥ sukhaṃ
caurebhyaḥ svajanād agneḥ salilāt pārthivād api
bhayam arthavatān nityaṃ mṛtror dehabhṛtām iva
khe yathā pakṣibhir māṃsaṃ bhakṣyante śvāpadair bhuvi
jale ca bhakṣyate matsyais tathā sarvatra vittavān
vimohayanti sampattau tāḍayanti vipattiṣu
khedayantyar janākāle kadā bhogāḥ sukhāvahāḥ
yaś cārthapatir udvigno yaś ca sarvārthanispṛhaḥ
