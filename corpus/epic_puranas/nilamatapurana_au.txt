śrīnivāsaṃ hariṃ devaṃ varadaṃ parameśvaram|
trailokyanāthaṃ govindaṃ praṇamyākṣaram avyayam|| 1 ||

parikṣidvaṃśabhṛcchrīmān nṛpatirjanamejayaḥ|
papraccha śiṣyaṃ vyāsasya vaiśampāyanam antikāt|| 2 ||

janamejaya uvāca |

mahābhāratasaṃgrāme nānādeśyā narādhipāḥ|
mahāśūrāḥ samāyātāḥ pitṛṇāṃ me mahātmanām|| 3 ||

kathaṃ kāśmīrako rājā nāyatastatra kīrtaya|
pāṇḍavairdhārtarāṣṭraiśca na vṛtaḥ sa kathaṃ nṛpaḥ|| 4 ||

kāśmīrāmaṇḍalaṃ caiva pradhānaṃ jagati sthitam|
 || 5 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

 |
tatrāgataṃ niśamyātha vāsudevaṃ svayaṃvare|
jagāma mādhavaṃ yoddhuṃ caturaṅgabalānvitaḥ|| 6 ||

tatra tasyābhavadyuddhaṃ vāsudevena dhīmatā|
yādṛśaṃ vāsudevasya narakeṇa sahābhavat|| 7 ||

tataḥ sa vāsudevena suyuddhe vinipātitaḥ|| 8 ||

antarvatnīṃ tasya patnīṃ vāsudevo 'abhyaṣecayat|
bhaviṣyatputrarājyārthaṃ tasya deśasya gauravāt|| 9 ||

tataḥ sā suṣuve putraṃ bālagonandasaṃjñitam|
bālabhāvāt pāṇḍusutairnānītaḥ kauravairna vā|| 10 ||

janamejaya uvāca |

deśasya gauravaṃ cakre kimarthaṃ dvijasattama|
vāsudevo mahātmā yadabhyaṣiñcat svayaṃ striyam|| 11 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

yaiva devī umā saiva kaśmīrā nṛpapuṃgava|
āsīt saraḥ pūrṇajalaṃ suramyaṃ sumanoharam|| 12 ||

kalpārambhaprabhṛti yat purā manvantarāṇi ṣaṭ|
asmin manvantare jātaṃ viṣayaṃ sumanoharam|| 13 ||

śālimālākulaṃ sphītaṃ satphalādyaiḥ samanvitam|
svādhyāyadhyānaniratairyajñaśīlairjanairyutam|| 14 ||

tapasvibhirdharmaparairvedavedāṅgapāragaiḥ|
kṣatriyaiḥ sumahābhāgaiḥ sarvaśastrāstrapāragaiḥ|| 15 ||

vaiśyairvṛttirataiḥ śūdrairdvijātiparicārakaiḥ|
devatāyatanopetaṃ sarvatīrthamyaṃ śubham|| 16 ||

pṛthivyāṃ yāni tīrthāni tāni tatra narādhipa|
ṛṣyāśramasusambādhaṃ śītāmalajalaṃ śubham|| 17 ||

adhṛṣyaṃ pararāṣṭrāṇāṃ tadbhayānām akovidam|
gośvanāgādibahulaṃ durbhikṣātaṅkavarjitam|| 18 ||

adevamātṛkaṃ ramyaṃ puṇyaṃ prāṇabhṛtāṃ hitam|
sarvasasyaguṇopetam anātaṅkaṃ bahuprajam|| 19 ||

strībhiśca sukumārābhirdevālayasamaśriyam|
duṣṭairbhujaṃgaśārdūlairmahiṣarkṣairvivarjitam|| 20 ||

brahmaghoṣadhanurghoṣa- nityotsavasamākulam|
keliprāyajanākīrṇaṃ nityahṛṣṭairjanairvṛtam|| 21 ||

udyānārāmasambādhaṃ vīṇāpaṭahanāditam|
nityaśauṇḍajanopetaṃ satāṃ hṛdayavallabham|| 22 ||

nānāpuṣpaphalopetaṃ nānādrumalatauṣadham|
nānāmṛgagaṇākīrṇaṃ siddhacāraṇasevitam|| 23 ||

kaśmīrāmaṇḍalaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvatīrtham ariṃdama|
tatra nāgahradāḥ puṇyāstatra puṇyāḥ śiloccayāḥ|| 24 ||

tatra nadyastathā puṇyāḥ puṇyāni ca sarāṃsyapi|
devālayā mahāpuṇyāḥ {} teṣāṃ caiva tathāśramāḥ|| 25 ||

tasya madhyena niryātā sīmantam iva kurvatī|
vitastā paramā devī sākṣāddhimanagodbhavā|| 26 ||

janamejaya uvāca |

manvantareṣu pūrveṣu yadāsīdvimalaṃ saraḥ|
kathaṃ vaivasvate jātaṃ tanmaṇḍalam iti prabho|| 27 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

imam arthaṃ purā jātu gonandākhyo nṛpottamaḥ|
tīrthayātrāprasaṅgena bṛhadaśvam upāgatam|
pūjayitvā sa nṛpatiḥ papraccha nṛpasattama|| 28 ||

gonanda uvāca |

manvantareṣu pūrveṣu nāsīddeśam idaṃ kila|
kaśmīrākhyaṃ babhūvāsmin kathaṃ vaivasvate 'antare|| 29 ||

iti nīlamate bṛhadaśvasamāgamaḥ|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

rāśibhogo ravermāsaḥ saura ityabhidhīyate|
ṛtustu māsau dvau jñeyāvayanaṃ ca ṛtutrayam|| 30 ||

ayane dve tathaivābdam abdānāṃ nṛpa saṃkhyayā|
dvātriṃśacca sahasrāṇi lakṣāṇāṃ ca catuṣṭayam|
proktaṃ kaliyugaṃ rājan dviguṇaṃ dvāparaṃ smṛtam|| 31 ||

triguṇaṃ tu tathā tretā kṛtaṃ jñeyaṃ caturguṇam|
caturyugaikasaptatyā manvantaram ihocyate|| 32 ||

tasmin manvantare 'atīte prajāḥ sasthāṇujaṅgamāḥ|
bhūrlokam āśritāḥ sarvā nāśam āyānti sarvaśaḥ|| 33 ||

ekārṇavaṃ jagat sarvaṃ tadā bhavati bhūpate|
himavān hemakūṭaśca niṣadho nīlaparvataḥ|| 34 ||

śvetaśca śṛṅgavān merurmālyavān gandhamādanaḥ|
mahendro malayaḥ sahyaḥ śuktimānṛkṣavānapi|| 35 ||

vindhyaśca pāriyātraśca na vinaśyanti parvatāḥ|
śeṣaṃ vinaśyate sarvaṃ jambudvīpam aśeṣataḥ|| 36 ||

tadā vinaṣṭe loke 'asmin mahādevaḥ svayaṃ prabhuḥ|
āpo bhūtvā svayaṃ loke tiṣṭhatyasmin samantataḥ|| 37 ||

satī devī tathā kāle tasmin nautvaṃ karoti vai|
manurbhaviṣyaṃstasmiṃstu sarvabījāni māyayā|| 38 ||

tadā sthāpayate rājaṃstāṃ ca nāvaṃ jagadguruḥ|
matsyarūpadharo viṣṇuḥ śṛṅgī bhūtvāpakarṣati|| 39 ||

ākṛṣya nāvaṃ tāṃ devastasmin parvatamastake|
baddhvā vrajati bhūpāla hyavijñātāṃ tadā gatim|| 40 ||

idaṃ ca śikharaṃ paśya deśe 'asmin nṛpa paścime|
naubandhanam iti khyātaṃ puṇyaṃ pāpabhayāpaham|| 41 ||

kṛtatulye tadā kāle vyatīte tu manustadā|
vidadhāti prajāsargaṃ yathāpūrvam ariṃdama|| 42 ||

naudehena satī devī bhūmirbhavati pārthiva|
tasyāṃ tu bhūmau bhavati sarastu vimalodakam|| 43 ||

ṣaḍyojanāyataṃ ramyaṃ tadardhena ca vistṛtam|
satīdeśam iti khyātaṃ devākrīḍaṃ manoharam|| 44 ||

ākāśam iva gambhīraṃ jalajaiśca vivarjitam|
śītalāmalapāṇīyaṃ sarvabhūmimanoharam|| 45 ||

iti nīlamate satīsaraḥprādurbhāvam|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

asmin vaivasvate prāpte rājan manvantare kila|
mārīcāya dadau dakṣaḥ kaśyapāya trayodaśa|| 46 ||

svasutāḥ pārthivaśreṣṭha tāsāṃ nāmāni me śṛṇu|
aditirditirdanāyuḥ surabhiḥ khaśā irā pravā|| 47 ||

muniḥ kālā danuḥ krodhā kadrūśca vinatā tathā|
aditestanayā devā diterdaityāstathaiva ca|| 48 ||

danāyuṣāyā vṛtrastu rudrāstu surabheḥ sutāḥ|
yakṣāśca rākṣasāścaiva khaśāyāstanayāḥ smṛtāḥ|| 49 ||

airāvaṇastvirāputraḥ pravāyā daśa gāyanāḥ|
muneḥ prasavam uktaṃ hi divyam apsarasāṃ gaṇam|| 50 ||

kālāyāḥ kālakalpāśca kālakeyāḥ sutā matāḥ|
dānavāśca danoḥ putrāḥ krodhāyāḥ kanyakā daśa|| 51 ||

kadrośca tanayā nāgā vinatāyāstathā sutau|
garuḍāraṇau vijñeyau pakṣiṇāṃ pravarau nṛpa|| 52 ||

kadrūśca vinatā caiva spardhamāne parasparam|
vidhānayogāt satataṃ cakraturvairam uttamam|| 53 ||

kadācidamṛtodbhūtaṃ dṛṣṭvoccaiḥśravasaṃ hayam|
śvetaṃ jagāda vinatā kadrūḥ śāṭhye tathā sthitā|
kṛṣṇavālam ahaṃ manye tam aśvaṃ vinate sadā|| 54 ||

 |
iti āha kadrūṃ vinatā paṇam āsīt tu tayostadā|
aśvaṃ prati mahīnātha dāsyabhāvo 'atha sarvathā|| 55 ||

 |
preritāstu tataḥ putrāḥ kadrvā gatvā tathā vyadhuḥ|| 56 ||

tataste kṛṣṇavālaṃ taṃ dṛṣṭvā turagam uttamam|
kadrūrjitāsyavocat tu vinatāṃ cārudarśanām|| 57 ||

dāsye sthitāṃ tu vinatāṃ garuḍaḥ sumahāyaśāḥ|
mokṣayāmāsa cāhṛtya somaṃ śakrānmahābalaḥ|| 58 ||

śakrāccaiva varaṃ lebhe pannagānāṃ ca bhakṣaṇam|
māturvairānubandhena bhakṣayāmāsa pannagān|| 59 ||

bhakṣyamāṇeṣu nāgeṣu garuḍena mahātmanā|
vāsukiḥ śaraṇaṃ prāyāddevadevaṃ janārdanam|| 60 ||

vāsukiruvāca |

namo 'astu te devavarāprameya namo 'astu te śārṅgagadāsipāṇe|
namo 'astu te dānavanāśanāya namo 'astu te padmajasaṃstutāya|| 61 ||

namo 'astu te lokahite ratāya namo 'astu te vāsavanandanāya|
namo 'astu te bhaktavarapradāya namo 'astu te satpathadarśanāya|| 62 ||

unnidranīlanalinadyuticāruvarṇaṃ saṃtaptahāṭakanibhe vasane vasānam|
sadratnacumbitakirīṭavirājamānaṃ dāmodaraṃ suraguruṃ praṇato 'asmi nityam|| 63 ||

namo 'astu te sevakārtibhañjanāya namo 'astu te devavara prasīda|
kṣīrodakanyārpitapādapadmaṃ devaṃ prapanno 'asmyanaghaṃ vareṇyam|| 64 ||

paraṃ purāṇaṃ paramaṃ sanātanaṃ tam ādidevaṃ praṇato 'asmi bhaktyā|
phaṇāvalīratnasahasracitre śeṣasya bhoge vimale viśeṣe|| 65 ||

lokasya sarvasya tu cintayānaḥ śubhāśubhaṃ rakṣa mām ādideva|
namo 'astu deveśa jagannivāsa tvam ādidevaḥ paripāhi viśvam|| 66 ||

khagapatiraticaṇḍabhīmavego mama kulam āśu vināśayatyananta|
kuru munivarasaṃstutādya rakṣāṃ pavanabalaṃ vinivārayasva tārkṣyam|| 67 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

tam āha vāsukiṃ devo bhagavān bhayavihvalam|
satīdeśe 'atra puṇyode sarasyambarasaṃnibhe|
dharmiṣṭhaiḥ sahito nāgairvasasvāmitavikrama|| 68 ||

tasmin sarasi ye sthānaṃ kariṣyanti bhujaṃgamāḥ|
tasya tasyāhiśatrurvai na haniṣyati jīvitam|| 69 ||

satīdeśakṛtasthānaṃ tiṣṭhantam akutobhayam|
na haniṣyati nāgendra nāgārirmama vāhanaḥ|| 70 ||

satīdeśe ca ye nāgā vasiṣyanti mahābalāḥ|
teṣāṃ rājye mahābhāga tvaṃ nīlam abhiṣecaya|| 71 ||

vāsukiśca tathā cakre devadevasya bhāṣitam|
tatrāsthānāṃ ca nāgānāṃ nāsīdgaruḍato bhayam|| 72 ||

iti nīlamate vāsukivaralābhaḥ|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

kadācit sarasastasya tīre 'amburuhalocanaḥ|
śakraścikrīḍa sahitaḥ paulomyā pārthivottama|| 73 ||

krīḍamānasya śakrasya taṃ deśaṃ kālacoditaḥ|
saṃgraho nāma daityendraḥ prāptaḥ paramadurjayaḥ|| 74 ||

tasya dṛṣṭvā śacīṃ retaḥ praskannaṃ salilāśaye|
 |
sa ca kāmavaśonmattaḥ śacīharaṇalālasaḥ|| 75 ||

 |
tataḥ śakreṇa saṃgrāme pūrṇaṃ saṃvatsaraṃ gatam|
varṣam āsīt tayoryuddhaṃ śakrasaṃgrahayoḥ purā|| 76 ||

saṃvatsarānte taṃ hatvā śakrastridaśapūjitaḥ|
jagāma tridivaṃ devaiḥ pūjyamānastadālayaiḥ|| 77 ||

tasmin sarasi yat tasya saṃgrahasya durātmanaḥ|
praskannaṃ patitaṃ retastasmājjāto jale śiśuḥ|| 78 ||

kṛpayā sa śiśurnāgairjale tasmin vivardhitaḥ|
yasmādayaṃ jale jātastasmādeṣa jalodbhavaḥ|| 79 ||

ārādhya tapasā lebhe varaṃ devāt pitāmahāt|
jale 'amaratvaṃ māyāśca vikramaṃ cātulaṃ tathā|| 80 ||

labdhamāyastu daityendro bhakṣayāmāsa mānavān|
samīpe sarasastasya nānādeśeṣvavasthitān|| 81 ||

dārvābhisāragāndhāra- juhuṇḍaraśakān khaśān|
taṅgaṇān māṇḍavān madrānantargiribahirgirīn|| 82 ||

te hanyamānāḥ pāpena diśaḥ samprādravan bhayāt|
śūnyeṣu teṣu deśeṣu vicacāra sa nirbhayaḥ|| 83 ||

iti nīlamate jalodbhavodbhavaḥ|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

etasminneva kāle tu kaśyapo bhagavānṛṣiḥ|
tīrthayātrāprasaṅgena cacāra sakalāṃ mahīm|| 84 ||

varṣe 'asmin bhārate puṇye śubhāśubhaphalaprade|
puṣkaraṃ duṣkaragamaṃ brahmalokapradaṃ śivam|| 85 ||

prayāgaṃ yāgabahulaṃ sarvakilbiṣanāśanam|
dharmakṣetraṃ kurukṣetraṃ naimiṣaṃ pāpanāśanam|| 86 ||

pitṛṇām ālayaṃ puṇyaṃ gayāśīrṣaṃ mahātmanām|
sarvapāpaharaṃ divyaṃ tathā cāmarakaṇṭakam|| 87 ||

varāhaparvataṃ puṇyaṃ puṇyaṃ pañcanadaṃ tathā|
kālañjaraṃ sagokarṇaṃ ca kedāraṃ samahālayam|| 88 ||

nārāyaṇasya ca sthānaṃ supuṇyaṃ badaryāśramam|
sugandhāṃ śatakumbhāṃ ca kālikāśramam eva ca|| 89 ||

śākambharīṃ lalitikaṃ śāligrāmaṃ pṛthūdakam|
suvarṇākṣaṃ rudrakoṭiṃ prabhāsaṃ sāgarodakam|| 90 ||

indramārgaṃ mataṅgasya vāpīṃ pāpaniṣūdanīm|
agastyasyāśramaṃ puṇyaṃ tathā taṇḍulikāśramam|| 91 ||

jambumārgaṃ tathā puṇyaṃ puṇyāṃ vārāṇasīṃ tathā|
tathaiva jāhnavīṃ devīṃ gaṅgāṃ gaganamekhalām|| 92 ||

yamunāṃ yamapāśaghnīṃ śatadruṃ drutagāminīm|
sarayūṃ yūpasampannāṃ tathā devīṃ sarasvatīm|| 93 ||

godāvarīṃ vaitaraṇīṃ gomatīṃ bāhudām api|
vedasmṛtiṃ savarṇāśāṃ tāmravarṇotpalāvatīm|| 94 ||

siprāṃ sanarmadāṃ śoṇaṃ paroṣṇīṃ ca mahānadīm|
ikṣumatīṃ saraṭṭāṃ ca durgāṃ mataśilām api|
kāverīṃ brāhmaṇīṃ gaurīṃ kampanāṃ tamasāṃ tathā|| 95 ||

gaṅgāsāgarasaṃdhiṃ ca sindhusāgarasaṃgamam|
bhṛgutuṅgaṃ viśālāṃ ca kubjāmraṃ raivataṃ tathā|| 96 ||

gaṅgādvāraṃ kuśāvartaṃ bilvakaṃ nīlaparvatam|
tathā kanakhalaṃ tīrthaṃ tīrthānyanyāni pārthiva|| 97 ||

tīrthayātrāgataṃ śrutvā kaśyapaṃ pannagādhipaḥ|
nīlo jagāma taṃ draṣṭuṃ tīrthe kanakhale tadā|| 98 ||

sa gatvā pitaraṃ dṛṣṭvā pituḥ pādau nipīḍya ca|
nivedya nāmadheyaṃ svaṃ vavande bhujagādhipaḥ|| 99 ||

pitrā mūrdhanyupāghrāya pūjitaḥ sa yathāvidhi|
nyaṣīdata tadā bṛṣyāṃ kauśyāṃ sa tadanujñayā|| 100 ||

upaviṣṭastadā nāgo vijñāpayata kaśyapam|
pitaraṃ tapasāṃ sthānaṃ yat tacchṛṇu narādhipa|| 101 ||

tīrthānyanusarantaṃ hi śrutvāhaṃ dharmavatsalam|
bhavantaṃ sahasā prāptaḥ śuśruṣārthī dvijottama|| 102 ||

pūrvadeśe tvayā brahman dakṣiṇe paścime tathā|
dṛṣṭāni sarvatīrthāni yāsyāmastūttarāṃ diśam|| 103 ||

tatra madreṣu tīrthāni santi puṇyāni mānada|
tathā ca parvataśreṣṭhe himavatyacalottame|| 104 ||

vipāśā pāpaśamanī śaśvacchreyaḥpradā śivā|
devalokapradā snāne nadī devahradā tathā|| 105 ||

tathā pāpaharo devo haraśca harirīśvaraḥ|
tathā ca saṃgamaḥ puṇyaḥ karavīrapuraṃ prati|| 106 ||

tatra devahradā yāti vipāśāṃ nimnagottamām|
vipāśāyāṃ tathā puṇyaṃ satataṃ kālikāśramam|| 107 ||

irāvatī tathā puṇyā sarvakalmaṣanāśinī|
revatyāṃ ca viśeṣeṇa tathāṣṭamyāṃ viśeṣataḥ|| 108 ||

ṣaṣṭitīrthasahasrāṇi vasantyekām irāvatīm|| 109 ||

kumbhāvasundaḥ puṇyodaḥ puṇyodā devikā nadī|
nityam eva tathā puṇyo viśvāmitro mahānadaḥ|| 110 ||

uddākhyastu mahāpuṇyaḥ saṃgamāśca pṛthak pṛthak|
irāvatyāṃ yathā puṇyaṃ devikāyāṃ tathaiva ca|| 111 ||

yaiva devī umā saiva devikā prathitā bhuvi|
madrāṇām anukampārthaṃ bhavadbhiravatāritā|| 112 ||

yāṃ dṛṣṭvā mānavaḥ pūto bhavatīha na saṃśayaḥ|
indramārgaṃ somatīrthaṃ puṇyam ambujanastathā|| 113 ||

suvarṇabinduṃ tatraiva harasyāyatanaṃ śubham|
skandasyāyatanaṃ tatra sarvapāpaniṣūdanam|| 114 ||

umāpatirmahāpuṇyo rudratīrthaṃ tathaiva ca|
durgādvāraṃ tu puṇyodaṃ koṭitīrthaṃ tathaiva ca|| 115 ||

rudrasya tīrthaṃ kāmākhyaṃ puṣpanyāsaṃ tathaiva ca|
puṇyaṃ haṃsapadaṃ proktam ṛṣirūpaṃ ca mānada|| 116 ||

sarvatra devikā tīrthaṃ kṣetraṃ krośacatuṣṭayam|
yatra kūpataḍāgākhyaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvam aśeṣataḥ|| 117 ||

āpagā ca nadī puṇyā tauṣī toṣitabhāskarā|
candrāṃśuśītalajalā candrabhāgā saridvarā|| 118 ||

puṇyaṃ ca candrabhāgāyāstīrthaṃ vaivaṭṭilāmukham|
śaṅkhamardalanāmā ca tathā pāpaniṣūdanaḥ|| 119 ||

guheśvaraḥ śatamukha iṣṭikāpatha eva ca|
kadambeśastathā puṇyaḥ kṣetraṃ caiva samantataḥ|| 120 ||

yāvacchatamukhaṃ tīrthaṃ yāvat tīrthaṃ guheśvaram|
tāvat kṣetraṃ samaṃ puṇyaṃ vārāṇasyātha vādhikam|| 121 ||

sarvatraiva sadā puṇyā candrabhāgā mahānadī|
māghaśuklatrayodaśyāṃ puṣyayoge viśeṣataḥ|| 122 ||

pṛthivyāṃ yāni tīrthāni hi āsamudrasarāṃsi ca|
candrabhāgāṃ gamiṣyanti māghaśuklatrayodaśīm|| 123 ||

puṇyaṃ vastrāpadaṃ proktaṃ devaṃ ca chāgaleśvaram|
dvitīyā yā tathā bhaumī tasyāḥ prabhāva eva ca|| 124 ||

satīdeśasya sarasastīrthaṃ viṣṇupadaṃ saraḥ|
kramasāreti vikhyātaṃ sarvakalmaṣanāśanam|| 125 ||

etānyanyāni ca mune tīrthānyanusarāśu vā|
yeṣāṃ snānena mucyante pāpātmāno 'api mānavāḥ|| 126 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

ityuktaḥ sa tathetyuktvā nīlena sahayāyinā|
jagāma tāni tīrthāni jātehaḥ pūrvam eva tu|| 127 ||

uttīrya yamunāṃ devīṃ tathā devīṃ sarasvatīm|
kurukṣetraṃ tathā dṛṣṭvā saṃnītiryatra viśrutā|| 128 ||

tīrthasaṃnayanaścaiva saṃnītirbhuvi kathyate|
prṭhivyāṃ yāni tīrthāni āsamudrasarāṃsi ca|
kṛṣṇapakṣāvasāne vai yatra yānti sadānagha|| 129 ||

śrāddhaṃ yaḥ kurute tatra rāhugraste divākare|
aśvamedhasahasrasya phalaṃ prāpnotyanuttamam|| 130 ||

saṃnītiṃ tāṃ tathā dṛṣṭvā cakratīrthaṃ tathaiva ca|
yadarthaṃ nāradodgītā gāthā carati bhūtale|| 131 ||

aho lokasya nirbandham ādityagrahaṇaṃ prati|
cakratīrthena paryāptaṃ grahāddaśaguṇaṃ phalam|| 132 ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā cakratīrthākhyaṃ tathā tīrthaṃ pṛthūdakam|
dṛṣṭvā viṣṇupadaṃ puṇyaṃ tathā cāmaraparpaṭam|| 133 ||

śatadruṃ ca tatottīrya ṛṣirgaṅgāṃ ca nimnagām|
arjunāśramam āsādya devasundaṃ tathaiva ca|| 134 ||

uttīrya ca mahābhāgāṃ vipāśāṃ pāpanāśinīm|
dṛṣṭavān sakalaṃ deśaṃ tadā śūnyaṃ sa kaśyapaḥ|| 135 ||

dṛṣṭvā sa madraviṣayaṃ śūnyaṃ provāca pannagam|
kimarthaṃ nīla deśo 'ayaṃ madrāṇāṃ śūnyatāṃ gataḥ|| 136 ||

ramaṇīyaḥ sadaivaiṣa durbhikṣāpāyavarjitaḥ|
nityaṃ dhānyadhanopetastanmamācakṣva pṛcchataḥ|| 137 ||

nīla uvāca |

bhagavan viditaṃ sarvaṃ yathā pūrvaṃ mayā śiśuḥ|
pālitaḥ saṃgrahasuto daityo nāma jalodbhavaḥ|| 138 ||

so 'adya labdhvā varān pāpo brahmaṇo 'avyaktayoninaḥ|
na māṃ gaṇayate dhṛṣṭo na cāhaṃ tasya nigrahe|
samartho varadānena trailokyādhipateḥ prabhoḥ|| 139 ||

tenedaṃ sakalaṃ śūnyaṃ madradeśaṃ kṛtaṃ prabho|
khādatā naramāṃsāni duṣṭenākṛtabuddhinā|| 140 ||

dārvābhisāragāndhāra- juhuṇḍaraśakāḥ khaśāḥ|
taṅgaṇā maṇḍavāścaiva antargiribahirgirīn|| 141 ||

ete vai mukhyatastena deśāḥ śūnyīkṛtāḥ prabho|
nigrahe bhagavaṃstasya kuru buddhiṃ jagaddhitām|| 142 ||

iti nīlamate kaśyapatīrthayātrā|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

evam uktastathetyuktvā snātvā tīrtheṣu kṛtsnataḥ|
ājagāma satīdeśaṃ vimalaṃ tat sarottamam|| 143 ||

tatra snātvā jagāmāśu brahmalokaṃ sanātanam|
padbhyāṃ caṃkramaṇaṃ tyaktvā svaṛddhyaiva narottama|| 144 ||

nīlena sahitaḥ prāyānnāgarājñā mahātmanā|
tau gatvā brahmasadanaṃ vavandaturariṃdama|| 145 ||

devaṃ kamalayoniṃ ca saṃgatyā tatra ca sthitau|
vāsudeveśvarau devāvanantaṃ ca mahāmatim|| 146 ||

taistu sampūjitau tatra jalodbhavaviceṣṭitam|
kathayāmāsaturubhau tato devaḥ pitāmahaḥ|| 147 ||

avocadevaṃ nāgaṃ tam ṛṣiṃ cāmitavikramam|
naubandhanaṃ prayāsyāmo vayaṃ ca tasya nigrahe|| 148 ||

tatastaṃ keśavo devo ghatayiṣyatyasaṃśayam|
etacchrutvā hariḥ prāyāt tārkṣyeṇa paravīrahā|| 149 ||

tam anvayādvṛṣārūḍho haro devyā sahānagha|
haṃsayāno gato brahmā nāgastu meghavāhanaḥ|| 150 ||

ṛddhyaiva kaśyapaḥ prāyācchrutavāṃstat puraṃdaraḥ|
tato devagaṇaiḥ sārdhaṃ sa yāto yatra keśavaḥ|| 151 ||

yamo 'agnirvaruṇo vāyuḥ kubero nṛpatistathā|
ādityā vasavo rudrā viśvedevā marudgaṇāḥ|| 152 ||

aśvinau bhṛgavaḥ sādhyāstathaivāṅgirasaḥ sutāḥ|
ṛṣayaśca mahābhāgā gandharvāpsarasāṃ gaṇāḥ|| 153 ||

devapatnyastathā sarvā devānāṃ yāśca mātaraḥ|
vidyādharagaṇā yakṣāḥ sāgarāḥ saritastathā|| 154 ||

makareṇa yayau gaṅgā kūrmeṇa yamunā nadī|
vṛṣārūḍhaḥ śatadruśca mahiṣeṇa sarasvatī|| 155 ||

aśvārūḍhā vipāśā ca gajārūḍhā irāvatī|
siṃhena candrabhāgā ca sindhurvyāghreṇa pārthiva|| 156 ||

devikā gavayārūḍhā mṛgeṇa sarayūrnadī|
mandākinī manuṣyeṇa paroṣṇī cāpyajena tu|| 157 ||

narmadā ca mayūreṇa sāraṅgena ca gomatī|
godāvarī ca meṣeṇa tathā haṃsena kampanā|| 158 ||

bakena gaṇḍakī rājan kāveryuṣṭragatā tathā|
nakreṇekṣumatī puṇyā sītā puṇyā balākayā|| 159 ||

camareṇa ca lauhityo vaṅkṣuḥ kroḍena satvaraḥ|
hlādinī jīvajīvena hrādinī kukkuṭena tu|| 160 ||

pāvanyapi kuliṅgena śonaḥ sarpagatastathā|
meghena kṛṣṇaveṇā ca bhuveṇā śaśakena ca|| 161 ||

etāścānyāśca yā nadyaḥ prayayurvāhanaiḥ svakaiḥ|
anujagmurjagannāthaṃ hariyuddhadidṛkṣayā|| 162 ||

naubandhanam athāsādya keśavo vai vyavasthitaḥ|| 163 ||

devānuyātraninadaṃ śrutvā daityo 'api durmatiḥ|
jale tvavadhyam ātmānaṃ viditvā na vinirgataḥ|| 164 ||

anirgataṃ taṃ tu tadā vijñāya madhusūdanaḥ|
naubandha eva mudito viveśātha suraiḥ saha|| 165 ||

naubandhaśikhare rudro dakṣiṇe śikhare hariḥ|
uttare śikhare brahmā teṣām anu surāsurāḥ|| 166 ||

evaṃ te viviśuḥ śaile tato devo janārdanaḥ|
anantam āha dharmātmā vadhārthaṃ dānavasya tu|| 167 ||

kuruṣva lāṅgalena tvaṃ vidāryādya himālayam|
idaṃ sarovaraṃ divyaṃ nistoyaṃ śīghram eva tu|| 168 ||

iti nīlamate devāgamanaṃ nāma|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

tatastvananto girisaṃnikāśaḥ samagracandrasya samānakāntiḥ|
vyavardhatāvṛtya mahīṃ divaṃ ca saṃtrāsayan daityagaṇān samantāt|| 169 ||

nīlāmbaraḥ kāñcanabaddhamauliḥ sampūjyamānastridaśaiḥ samastaiḥ|
vidārayāmāsa sa lāṅgalena himācalaṃ śailavaraṃ pṛthivyām|| 170 ||

vidārite parvatarājarāje viniryayau tajjalam āśu vegāt|
vegena śabdena ca sarvabhūtān saṃtrāsayānaṃ kuṭilaistaraṅgaiḥ|| 171 ||

himācalābhairgaganaṃ spṛśadbhiḥ samplāvayānaṃ girimastakāni|
 || 172 ||

saṃkṣīyamāṇe sarasastu toye cakāra māyāṃ sa jalodbhavākhyaḥ|

śambhustadā candradivākarau dvau jagrāha devo 'atha karadvayena|

yuddhaṃ ca te devagaṇāḥ samastāḥ|| 176 ||

 cakreṇa devapravaraḥ samānte|

brahmā viṣṇuśca śambhuśca yeṣu śṛṅgeṣvavasthitāḥ|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

tānetāñchikharān paśya brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarān|
naubandhaśikharo yastu sa eva nṛpa śaṃkaraḥ|| 181 ||

dakṣiṇo 'asya hariḥ pārśvo vāmo brahmā prakīrtitaḥ|

yo 'asau viṣṇupado nāma kramasāreti kīrtitaḥ|
tasyodagāśramaṃ cakre brahmā devavaraḥ svayam|
paścārdhe cāśramaṃ cakre kaśyapo bhagavānṛṣiḥ|| 183 ||

yasmin deśe sthito viṣṇurvijayaṃ prāptavāṃstadā|
tatrāśramapadaṃ cakre mahādevaḥ svayaṃ prabhuḥ|| 184 ||

tasyaiva cāpare bhāge hyanantastu āśramaṃ mahat|
cakāra halabhṛcchrīmān vāsudevamate sthitaḥ|| 185 ||

mahādevāśramādbhāge paścime 'arkaniśākarau|

pādonaṃ yojanaṃ gatvā mahādevāśramāddhariḥ|
ātmanastu āśramaṃ cakre narasiṃheti viśrutam|| 187 ||

anye tu devāḥ sarasi vitoye 'atha pṛthak pṛthak|
cakrurāśramaṃ svaṃ tatra ṛṣayaśca tapodhanāḥ|| 188 ||

āśramāṇi tathā nadyaścakrustīrthānyanekaśaḥ|
gandharvāpsaraso yakṣāḥ śailendrāśca saguhyakāḥ|| 189 ||

kṛtālayau tatra jagatpradhānāvupendrarudrau saha padmajena|
kṛtālayaṃ tatra jagat samagraṃ deśaṃ supuṇyaṃ paramaṃ pavitram|| 190 ||

iti nīlamate jalodbhavavadhaḥ|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

jalodbhavāsṛjā mattastadā cakraḥ sudarśanaḥ|
babhrāma deśaṃ śūnyaṃ taṃ taṃ ca jagrāha śaṃkaraḥ|| 191 ||

cakrahasto jagāmātha yatra devo janārdanaḥ|
tam uvāca harirdevaṃ prahasañchaṃkaraṃ tadā|| 192 ||

cakram arpaya me deva daityasaṃghavināśanam|
prahasantam uvācātha hariṃ hāsena śaṃkaraḥ|| 193 ||

svacchando 'ayaṃ mayā prāpto bhramamāṇo yadṛcchayā|
pratigraheṇa dāsyāmi tava cakraṃ janārdana|| 194 ||

evam astviti jagrāha taṃ cakraṃ madhusūdanaḥ|
asmin pradeśe rājendra yatra vartasi sāmpratam|| 195 ||

tādṛśaṃ parihāsaṃ tu kṛtvā devavaro hariḥ|
tādṛśīṃ kārayāmāsa pratimām ātmanastathā|| 196 ||

śambhordevyāśca rājendra yathāvṛttam ariṃdama|
tādṛśaṃ saṃvidhānaṃ tu kārayitvā janārdanaḥ|| 197 ||

jalodbhavaśirasyasmin pratiṣṭhāṃ kṛtavān hariḥ|
imāṃ manujaśārdūla yasyāṃ saṃnihitāvubhau|| 198 ||

keśavaśca śivaścaiva sarvakalmaṣanāśanau|
kṛtadevapratiṣṭhānaṃ devadevaṃ janārdanam|| 199 ||

ṛṣayo devatā nāgā gandharvāpsarasāṃ gaṇāḥ|
draṣṭuṃ sarve samājagmurjalodbhavaśirasyatha|| 200 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

devarṣināgamukhyeṣvadhiṣṭhiteṣvatha kaśyapaḥ|
uvāca varadaṃ viṣṇuṃ deśo 'ayaṃ deva mānuṣaiḥ|| 201 ||

vasatāṃ ramaṇīyaśca puṇyaśca bhavitā tathā|
kaśyape bruvati tvevaṃ nāgā vacanam abruvan|| 202 ||

na vayaṃ mānuṣaiḥ sārdhaṃ vasāmo munipuṃgava|
tānuvāca tataḥ kruddhaḥ kaśyapo vai prajāpatiḥ|| 203 ||

mama vākyam anādṛtya yasmāddhṛṣṭaṃ prabhāṣatha|
tasmāt piśācaiḥ sahitā vatsyadhvaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ|| 204 ||

evam ukte kaśyapena nīlaḥ prāñjalirabravīt|
ete krodhavaśādbrahman na vijānanti kiṃcana|| 205 ||

kaśyapastam uvācātha ṛṣiḥ paramadhārmikaḥ|
vālukārṇavamadhye tu dvīpaḥ ṣaḍyojanāyataḥ|| 206 ||

tatra santi piśācā ye daityapakṣāḥ sudāruṇāḥ|
teṣāṃ tu nigrahārthāya piśācādhipatirbalī|| 207 ||

nikumbho nāma dharmātmā kubereṇa tu yojitaḥ|
caitryāṃ yāti sadā yoddhuṃ piśācairbahubhiḥ saha|| 208 ||

pañca koṭyaḥ piśācānāṃ nikumbhasyānuyāyinām|
gatvā nikumbhastaiḥ sārdhaṃ ṣaṇmāsān yuddhyate sadā|| 209 ||

tatrāpi koṭyaḥ pañcaiva piśācānāṃ durātmanām|| 210 ||

ye 'adhikāḥ koṭidaśakānnāśam āyānti te sadā|
pakṣayorubhayornīla ṣaḍbhirmāsaiḥ sadaiva tu|| 211 ||

nikumbhaḥ punarāyāti pañcakoṭisamo balī|
śuklāśvayukpañcadaśyāṃ nityaṃ devaprasādataḥ|| 212 ||

himācale tu ṣaṇmāsān sa sadā vasate sukhī|
adyaprabhṛti ṣaṇmāsāṃstasyeha vasatirmayā|| 213 ||

dattā tu sahitāstena sasainyeneha vatsyatha|
ṣaṇmāsān mānavaiḥ sārdhaṃ nikumbhe nirgate sadā|| 214 ||

evam uktastadā nīlaḥ pitaraṃ prāha dhārmikaḥ|
nityam eva hi vatsyāmo mānuṣaiḥ sahitā vayam|| 215 ||

na piśācaistu vatsyāmo dāruṇairdāruṇapriyaiḥ|
evaṃ bruvati nāgendre nīlaṃ viṣṇurabhāṣata|| 216 ||

munivākyaṃ tu bhavitā nīla ekaṃ caturyugam|
tataḥ paraṃ tu sahitā manuṣyaiḥ saha vatsyatha|| 217 ||

alpavīryāḥ piśācāśca bhaviṣyantīha sarvadā|
vīryopetā gamiṣyanti ṣaṇmāsān vālukārṇavam|| 218 ||

nāgasya yasya ye sthāne nivasiṣyanti mānavāḥ|

naivedyairvividhairdīpaiḥ prekṣādānaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|| 219 ||

tvayoktaṃ ca sadācāraṃ pālayiṣyanti ye janāḥ|

kaḥ prajāpatiruddiṣṭaḥ kaśyapaśca prajāpatiḥ|

yaivomā saiva kaśmīrā yasmāt tasmādbhujaṃgama|
viśoketyabhivikhyātā bṛmhitā ca tathā mayā|
strīrūpadhāriṇī bhūtvā vṛddhatīrthe nivatsyati|| 223 ||

vāsukernāgarājasya tasmiṃstīrthavare sadā|
vasatirbhavitā nāga tatrasthaṃ taṃ ca pūjaya|| 224 ||

mamāṃśaḥ sa tu nāgendra nāgānām īśvareśvaraḥ|

nāgānām ālayaṃ nāga nāmnā bhogavatī purī|

evam uktvā tadā viṣṇuḥ prayayau īpsitaṃ gatim|
devarṣināgagandharvāḥ prayayuste yathāgatam|| 228 ||

nānādeśasamutthaistu tataḥprabhṛti mānavaiḥ|
ṣaṇmāsān vasate deśaḥ ṣaṇmāsān piśitāśanaiḥ|| 229 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

evaṃ niviṣṭāṃ kaśmīrāṃ dṛṣṭvā hṛṣṭastu kaśyapaḥ|

tasmādvitasteti kṛtaṃ nāmaitasyāḥ svayambhuvā|| 255 ||

tatastu sarvadeśeṣu janaḥ śuśrāva pārthiva|
satī devī nadī bhūtvā kaśmīrāyā vinirgatā|| 256 ||

mahāpātakasaṃyuktastasyāṃ snātuṃ tadā janaḥ|
ājagāma bhayāt teṣāṃ śūlaghātaniyojanāt|| 257 ||

rasātalaṃ jagāmāśu punastām eva kaśyapaḥ|
prasādyonmajjayāmāsa pañcahastasamīpataḥ|| 258 ||

pañcahastasya nāgasya bhavanāt tāṃ vinirgatām|
gavyūtimātram āyātāṃ kṛtaghnastāṃ dadarśa vai|| 259 ||

sā ca dṛṣṭā kṛtaghnena hyantardhānaṃ gatā punaḥ|
bhūyaḥ kaśyapavākyena coditā nimnagottamā|| 260 ||

taccakrāt krośamātre tu pradadau darśanaṃ tadā|
mitrastrīgāminā dṛṣṭā tato 'adarśanam āgatā|| 261 ||

bhūyaḥ kaśyapavākyena narasiṃhāśrame śubhā|
unmamajja nadī vipraiḥ stūyamānā sahasraśaḥ|| 262 ||

krośamātre tato dṛṣṭā brahmaghnena mahānadī|
antardhānaṃ jagāmāśu tatastām āha kaśyapaḥ|| 263 ||

namo 'astu te parvatarājakanye namo 'astu tubhyam ṛṣivaryajuṣṭe|
namo 'astu tubhyaṃ harasaṅgalabdha- pavitrabhāve varade vareṇye|| 264 ||

supuṇyatoye surayoṣitābhiścikrīḍamānābhirupetatīre|
devadvijādyairupagūhyamāne suśītatoye vimale viśoke|| 265 ||

yeṣāṃ hi bhītyā prapalāyasi tvaṃ tvaddarśanāddevi vimuktapāpāḥ|
vṛttā narāste gatasarvapāpā dyāṃ bhāsayantaḥ prabhayā mahatyā|| 266 ||

saptārcidagdhasya yathā śucitvaṃ tvaddarśanāddevi tathā śucitvam|
sarvaṃ pavitraṃ bhavatīha tadvat pāpaṃ maheśāni kuru prasādam|| 267 ||

pāpānāṃ pāvanārthāya prārthitā tvaṃ mahānadi|
tasmāt pāvaya pāpāni ma praṇāśaṃ vrajāśuge|| 268 ||

evaṃ prasāditā bhaktyā kaśyapena mahātmanā|
uvāca kaśyapaṃ devī taṃ tathāvādinaṃ tadā|| 269 ||

atyantapāpasaṃyuktān nāhaṃ pāvitum utsahe|
tadatra preraya vibho lakṣmīṃ śārṅgadharapriyām|| 270 ||

śaktā hi pāvane bhramaṃstrailokyasyāpi sā bhavet|
aditiśca ditiścaiva yā ca gaṅgā mahānadī|| 271 ||

anyāśca sarvāḥ saritastasyāḥ sāmyaṃ na bibhrati|
kevalaṃ prārthayasvādya lakṣmīṃ keśavavallabhām|| 272 ||

tasyāḥ sa vacanaṃ śrutvā prayayau bhagavān kila|
govindam ārādhayituṃ śvetadvīpaṃ vihāyasā|| 273 ||

govindastvabravīllakṣmīṃ gaccha tvaṃ devi nāciram|
keśavenaivam uktā hi lakṣmīḥ śokasamanvitā|| 274 ||

uvāca vākyaṃ prathamaṃ gatā tatra satī vibho|
paścānmama gatāyāḥ sā dhruvaṃ nāma hariṣyati|| 275 ||

evaṃ saśokāṃ vijñāya kaśyapastvabravīt punaḥ|
tvam eva paramā śaktirbahubhirmūrtibhiḥ sthitā|
kṣīrodakanye viraje pavitre maṅgalāspade|| 276 ||

tvam eva devī kaśmīrā tvam evomā prakīrtitā|
tvam eva sarvadevīnāṃ mūrtibhirdevi saṃsthitā|| 277 ||

vaitastam ambhastava toyamiśraṃ madhvamṛtādyaṃ tu yathā tathāstu|
snātāstvadambhasyapi pāpamagnāḥ sadyo vimuktā vimalībhavanti|| 278 ||

evaṃ stutā kaśyapena viśokā samapadyata|
cintayāmāsa varadā bhartṛvākyaṃ dhruvaṃ mayā|| 279 ||

kartavyam ṛṣivākyaṃ ca kiṃ vicāreṇa vai mama|
nadī bhūtvā jagāmāśu kaśmīrā vākyam abravīt|| 280 ||

vraja śīghraṃ yāvadiha tvatpratīkṣā satī sthitā|
yāvat sā prathamaṃ deśaṃ na pāvayati sundari|| 281 ||

tāvat pāvaya toyena tava nāma bhaviṣyati|
tasyāstadvacanaṃ śrutvā viśokā samapadyata|| 282 ||

tasmādviśoketi nadī satataṃ kathyate janaiḥ|
manojavāpi kaśmīrā satyai devyai nyavedayat|| 283 ||

lakṣmyā viceṣṭitaṃ śrutvā satī ca śrutavistarā|
halamārgeṇa cottasthau dhaumyāśramasamīpataḥ|| 284 ||

ākhorbilena śūnyatvādviśokā cāpyanantaram|
ājagāma vitastāṃ ca dadarśa purataḥ sthitām|| 285 ||

dṛṣṭvā vitastāṃ gatamatsarā sā jagāma yogaṃ ca tayā viśokā|
pūrvāgatā tatra tadā vitastā jagrāha nāma kṣitipapradhāna|| 286 ||

kaśmīrāṃ tu tadā kruddhā viśokā tvaśapat prabho|
upacīrṇānṛtenādya tvayā yasmādahaṃ khale|
satī ca śrāvitā yasmāt tvayā mama viceṣṭitam|| 287 ||

tasmāt te prāyaśo bhāvī janaḥ khalvanṛte rataḥ|
apūjanīyo lokeṣu malavān karmakṛt tathā|| 288 ||

yanme nāma hṛtaṃ satyā na me vrīḍātra jāyate|
yaivāhaṃ saiva kaśmīre satī devī na saṃśayaḥ|| 289 ||

satī lakṣmīsametā tu pāvayantrī tato janam|
jagāma halamārgeṇa mandaṃ mandaṃ saridvarā|| 290 ||

aditirdevamātā ca trikoṭītyabhiviśrutā|
saṃyogaṃ sahitā devyā tataḥ prāptā vitastayā|| 291 ||

tataḥ śacī śakrapatnī nāmnā harṣapathā nadī|
tataścandravatī nāma ditirdaityāraṇirnṛpa|| 292 ||

evaṃ krameṇa sā devī gṛhṇantyatha saridvarāḥ|
jagāma gaṅgayā sārdhaṃ saṃyogaṃ sindhunā saha|| 293 ||

vaitastam ambhaḥ saha saindhavena yuktaṃ yathā kṣīram ivāmṛtena|
lāvaṇyayuktaṃ ca yathaiva rūpaṃ śīlena yuktaṃ ca yathā śrutaṃ syāt|| 294 ||

śauryaṃ yathā syādvinayena yuktaṃ dharmaṃ yathā syāddraviṇena yuktam|
mṛdā yathā syānmṛjayaiva yuktā kāmaṃ yathā syānmanasopapannam|| 295 ||

ratnaṃ yathā syāt kanakena yuktam āyuryathā svāsthyayutaṃ nṛvīra|
saṃmānayuktaṃ ca yathaiva lābhaṃ tathā tu sā tatra tadā babhūva|| 296 ||

tapanasya sutā devī gaṅgāsnehena yantritā|
bahumānānmunerbhaktyā svenāṃśena vyavardhayat|| 297 ||

vitastāṃ tu saricchreṣṭhāṃ sarvakalmaṣanāśinīm|
gaṅgā sindhustu vijñeyā vitastā yamunā tathā|| 298 ||

sa prayāgasamo deśastayoryatra tu saṃgamaḥ|
gaṅgātoyam athādāya gaṅgāṃ tu yamunābravīt|| 299 ||

prayāge 'apahṛtaṃ nāma tvayā me varavarṇini|
kaśmīrāyāṃ tathā nāma mayā cāpahṛtaṃ tava|| 300 ||

tām abravīt tato gaṅgā bhūya eva mayā tava|
hartavyaṃ nāma subhage yadāhaṃ sindhusaṃjñitā|| 301 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

iti tathyaṃ satī jñātvā halamārgāt tu sindhugā|
himālayānna prayayau pāṭitātmā paraṃ nadī|| 302 ||

punastāṃ tu mahābhāgām ṛṣiḥ provāca kaśyapaḥ|
avaśyaṃ halamārgeṇa gantavyaṃ subhage tvayā|| 303 ||

anyathā deśa evāyaṃ sarastvam upayāsyati|
bhūyo bhūyaścodyamānā kaśyapena saridvarā|| 304 ||

krodhāt tato viniṣkrāntā halamārgeṇa tena sā|
tasmin deśe prasannāpi dṛśyate kaluṣā nadī|| 305 ||

kaśyapa uvāca |

vitastākhyā saridrūpā devi tvaṃ parvatātmaje|
tapasvinī parā śarvāccharvapatnyasi no nadī|| 306 ||

adrivatsāsi bhadraṃ te taddehācchṛṅgiṇī nadī|
śambhunoḍhāsi rudrāṇī sravantyasi mayārthitā|| 307 ||

tvayyarpitaśrīrā ye pāpiṣṭhāḥ svargatā api|
dṛṣṭvā ramante svaṃ deham uhyamānaṃ tavormibhiḥ|| 308 ||

vismayaṃ te narā jagmurdṛṣṭvā taṃ deham ātmanaḥ|
svargatāḥ smo 'atha comāyāṃ krīḍāmo jalamadhyagāḥ|| 309 ||

tavodgāraistaraṅgākhyairdevi marutacoditaiḥ|
śītaiḥ śīkarajairnṛṇāṃ nārako 'agniḥ praśāmyati|| 310 ||

pradīpadīpibhirdevi tathā vartibhirūrmibhiḥ|
triḥsaptanārakiṃ vahniṃ nṛṇāṃ śamayase 'adrije|| 311 ||

snānaṃ ye tu kariṣyanti tava puṇye narā jale|
brahmalokaṃ gamiṣyanti mahāpātakino 'api te|| 312 ||

yāmīṃ tu yātanāṃ ghorāṃ bhrūkuṭībhaṅgacoditām|
paśyanti te na svapne 'api ye snātāpsu sakṛt tava|| 313 ||

tava bhaktasya viprasya nityaṃ karmānutiṣṭhataḥ|
mokṣadaṃ munayaḥ snānaṃ gaṅgāyāṃ svargadaṃ viduḥ|| 314 ||

atiprabhāvayuktāsi trailokyasyāpi pāvanī|
janitrī sarvadevānām umādevyasi no nadī|| 315 ||

devānāṃ tvaṃ dhṛtirdevi devānāṃ bhāratī tathā|
tṛptiśca sarvabhūtānāṃ nimnage tvaṃ sadā bhuvi|| 316 ||

prasādaṃ kuru me devi nirgaccha bhavanāditaḥ|
avikṣubdhena manasā deśasyāsya hite ratā|| 317 ||

ardhaṃ dehāddharasya tvaṃ devapatnyasi no nadī|
sindhusaṃgamanaṃ yāvaddhāvatī yāhi me 'arthitā|| 318 ||

patiste śaṃkarastveko nāparo 'abdhiritastataḥ|
sindhusaṃgamanenāśu vrajasva svapatiṃ śivam|| 319 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

niśamyaivaṃ punardevī sasmāroktaṃ yathā svayam|
smṛtvā sotkaṇṭhitā bharturgamane matim ādadhe|| 320 ||

tato vegena mahatā sīmantam iva kurvatī|
himācalasya prayayau toyaśailopamā nadī|| 321 ||

tatastu saṃgatā devyā kṛṣṇayā saha pārthiva|
tathā saridvarābhiśca śataśo 'atha sahasraśaḥ|| 322 ||

svairājakānāṃ madhyena mātrāṇāṃ caiva bhāgaśaḥ|
bhogaprastham atikramya gaṅgayā saha saṃgatā|| 323 ||

eṣā hi pāpaśamanī vitastā nimnagottamā|
kaśyasya tu vākyena lakṣmyā saha gatā kṣitim|| 324 ||

aditiśca ditiścaiva śacī ca manujeśvara|
tapanasya sutā yā ca yā ca gaṅgā saridvarā|| 325 ||

iti nīlamate vitastājanma|

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

evaṃ vasantyāṃ prayayau kaśmīrāyāṃ caturyugam|| 326 ||

pūrṇe caturyuge tasmin kṛtvā dhānyādisaṃgraham|
āśvayujyām atītāyāṃ niryayurmānavā bahiḥ|| 327 ||

kaśyapaścandradevākhyo vṛddho brāhmaṇapuṃgavaḥ|
na nirjagāma nirvedāccodito 'arthena bhāvinā|| 328 ||

krīḍānimittaṃ ca bhayānnikumbhasya na ghātitaḥ|
brāhmaṇena piśācāstu cikrīḍustena te tadā|| 329 ||

rajjubandhena tu yathā pakṣiṇā nṛpa dārakāḥ|
kālyamānaḥ piśācaistu nirvedaṃ paramaṃ yayau|| 330 ||

himena śītena tathā piśācaiḥ sampīḍyamāno dvijavṛddhavaryaḥ|
babhrāma tatraiva vimūḍhacetā bhraman yayau yatra sa nāgarājaḥ|| 331 ||

yasmin deśe tvanantena halaṃ pūrvaṃ niveśitam|
tatra nīlasya vasatiḥ pūrvam eva subhāvitā|| 332 ||

etasminneva kāle tu nīlo nāgapatirvibhuḥ|
sevyamāno nikumbhena piśācena mahātmanā|| 333 ||

nāgaiścātyulbaṇairbhīmaiḥ paryaṅkavaram āśritaḥ|
āste girivarasyādho dhanadasya mahātmanaḥ|| 334 ||

nāgāstaṃ nāgarājānaṃ nāgakanyāśca bhūriśaḥ|
upāsanta mahātmānaṃ kaśmīrāyāṃ kṛtālayaḥ|| 335 ||

kecit stuvanti rājānaṃ kecidvījanti pannagāḥ|
kecidvarāsanagatāḥ paryupāsanti dhārmikam|| 336 ||

teṣāṃ madhyagataṃ nīlaṃ nīlāñjanacayopamam|
mukuṭenārkavarṇena kuṇḍalaiśca virājitam|| 337 ||

vidyududdyotavarṇena vāsasaikena śobhitam|
candraraśminikāśena tathā cīnāṃśukena ca|| 338 ||

vitānena vicitreṇa kiṅkiṇījālamālinā|
tathā phaṇaśatairbhīmaiḥ saptabhiḥ saṃvirājitam|| 339 ||

ratnojjvalairdīparatnairjvālāmālāsamākulam|
taṃ dṛṣṭvā candradevasya nīlo 'ayam abhavanmatiḥ|| 340 ||

nūnaṃ prasādāt tasyaiva bhujagādhipateḥ prabhoḥ|
 || 341 ||

sopasarpya dvijo nāgaṃ kṛtvā prādhyayanaṃ puraḥ|
jānubhyām avaniṃ gatvā tadā stotram udīrayat|| 342 ||

iti nīlamate nīladarśanam|

candradeva uvāca |

namaste nāgarājendra nīla nīlotpaladyute|
nīlameghacayaprakhya nīlatoyakṛtālaya|| 343 ||

phaṇānāṃ tvaṃ śatairnāga śobhase saptabhiḥ sadā|
saptasaptirivārciṣmān rājase tvaṃ gabhastibhiḥ|| 344 ||

tvaṃ nīla nīlārtha vinītapāpairdeveśa devairapi dṛśyase svaiḥ|
nāgendra bhogīndram ivāmbarasthaṃ dhyānena vidvadbhirivāmṛtākhyam|| 345 ||

tvaṃ nīla yajñeśam ivāsanasthaṃ vedārthavidbhirvividhairvidhānaiḥ|
saṃsārakāryeṣu suyāgakṛdbhirārādhyase mokṣaphalāya vipraiḥ|| 346 ||

nāgendra nīlārcim ivāmarendrairvijñāyase sūryam ivāmbarastham|
tvaṃ nīla nīlārcim iva jvalantaṃ bhaktasya kāryāṇi ca sādhayantam|| 347 ||

dṛṣṭaṃ mayā hetubhirāpadantaṃ sarvasya jantorvasase yato 'adya|
smṛtastato mokṣayasīti duḥkhāt trāyasva viprasya mano narendra|| 348 ||

tvaṃ nīla niraughacayaprakāśaṃ virājase viṣṇurivāmareśaḥ|
vidhervidhātā ramase yamīśaṃ tvaṃ vāsudevapraṇataḥ sadaiva|| 349 ||

tvāṃ nīla nīlāmbara nīlanetra ākāśavat sarvagataṃ sureśam|
dhyātvā naro yo 'apyajitendriyo vā nāgendra mucyeta tava prasādāt|| 350 ||

nīla tvām eva vedārthe jagurvedāḥ sanātanam|
dheyaṃ vahnau mumukṣūṇāṃ kāmināṃ cārthasādhanam|| 351 ||

tvatprakāśaṃ yato brahma niṣkalaṃ nirmalaṃ param|
sūkṣmato vyoma nirdiṣṭaṃ sarvagātrairakṛtrimam|| 352 ||

ākiṃcanyādavasutvam atisūkṣmasya no pṛthoḥ|
arthāśrayānmahārthatvaṃ tava tasyākṣarasya ca|| 353 ||

kadrūḥ putrasahasreṇa nāgarājendra śobhitā|
tvayā tu rājate 'atyarthaṃ viṣṇunaivāditiryathā|| 354 ||

tvam eva tapasātyarthaṃ tathā vidyotase prabho|
toyaṃ himaṃ śīkaraṃ ca tathā muñcasi dhārmika|| 355 ||

prajāpatiḥ kaśyapo hi sarvabhūtapitā prabho|
tvayā tu śobhate 'atyarthaṃ putreṇātyantadhārmika|| 356 ||

tvayi dharmaśca satyaṃ ca kṣamā ca satataṃ prabho|
devāsuravimardeṣu śataśo 'atha sahasraśaḥ|| 357 ||

tvayā vinihatā daityā devabrāhmaṇakaṇṭakāḥ|
varadastvaṃ vareṇyaśca surāribalahā vibho|| 358 ||

bahktānukampī bhaktaśca devadeve janārdane|
tasyātidayitaścāsi yathā nāgaḥ sa vāsukiḥ|| 359 ||

dhanadaste sakhā nāga yathā śarvasya nityadā|
dhanadaścāsi bhaktānāṃ dhaneśa iti viśrutaḥ|| 360 ||

nāgānāṃ tvaṃ gatirnityaṃ devānām iva vāsavaḥ|
bhaktimānasmi te nityaṃ tacca jānāsi dhārmika|| 361 ||

iti nīlamate nīlastotram|

nīla uvāca |

svāgataṃ te dvijaśreṣṭha diṣṭyā prāpto 'asi me 'antikam|
arcanīyo 'asi viprendra hyatithistvaṃ mato mama|| 362 ||

varaṃ varaya bhadraṃ te yatheṣṭaṃ manasi priyam|
gṛhaṃ ca me tathā paśya tatrāssva ca yathāsukham|| 363 ||

candradeva uvāca |

avaśyaṃ me varo deyastvayā nāgendrasattama|
varayāmi varaṃ deva taṃ me tvaṃ dātum arhasi|| 364 ||

kaśmīrāyāṃ jano nityaṃ vasatāṃ bhīmavikrama|
kliśyate hi sadā loko niṣkraman praviśan punaḥ|| 365 ||

gṛhāṇīha narāḥ kṛtvā purāṇi vividhāni ca|
vasantu tvatprasādena varam etadvṛtaṃ mayā|| 366 ||

nīla uvāca |

evam astu dvijaśreṣṭha vasantviha narāḥ sadā|
pālayantastu madvākyaṃ keśavādyanmayā śrutam|| 367 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

evam uktvā tadā nīlo brāhmaṇaṃ svaṃ niveśanam|
nītvā sampūjya sambhojya brāhmaṇasya yathāvidhi|| 368 ||

kaśmīrāyāṃ vasatyartham ācārāṇi jagāda vai|
dvijaścovāsa ṣaṇmāsān sukhī nīlaniveśane|| 369 ||

caitryāṃ tato vyatītāyāṃ prāviśat sarvato janaḥ|
rājā vīryodayākhyaśca hastyaśvairbahubhirvṛtaḥ|| 370 ||

praviṣṭe tu jane tasmin dvijo nīlena yojitaḥ|
yuvājanaughasahito yayau vīryodayaṃ nṛpam|| 371 ||

tasya sarvaṃ yathāvṛttaṃ kathayāmāsa sa dvijaḥ|
rājāpi sarvalokeṣu kathayāmāsa pārthiva|| 372 ||

nīloktaṃ vacanaṃ kurvaṃstataḥprabhṛti vai janaḥ|
uvāsa satataṃ hṛṣṭaḥ kaśmīrāyāṃ kṛtālayaḥ|| 373 ||

kṛtvā purāṇi grāmāṇi tīrthāni āyatanāni ca|
gṛhāṇi ca vicitrāṇi hyuvāsa vasatiṃ janaḥ|| 374 ||

tataḥprabhṛti deśe 'asmin svalpaṃ hi patate himam|
janastu nīlavacanaṃ pālayatyeva nityadā|| 375 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

evam uktaḥ sa gonando bṛhadaśvena bhūbhujā|
papraccha bhūyastam ṛṣiṃ jātakautūhalastadā|| 376 ||

kāni ācārāṇi nīlena candradevāya bhārgava|
purā proktāni caitāni kathayasva mahādyute|| 377 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

raukmapīṭhasthitaṃ vipraṃ nāgaḥ paryaṅkam āśritaḥ|
uvāca yat tadā rājaṃstacchṛṇuṣva samāhitaḥ|| 378 ||

nīla uvāca |

praṇipatya hṛṣīkeśaṃ parāśaraguruṃ harim|
kaśmīrāyāṃ vasatyarthaṃ vakṣyāmi ācaraṇaṃ tava|| 379 ||

āśvayujyāṃ nikumbhastu nityam āyāti kaśyapa|
hatvā piśācān saṃgrāme vālukārṇavagān bahūn|| 380 ||

pūjārthaṃ tasya kartavyā kaumudī tāṃ nibodha me|| 381 ||

sudhāvadātāḥ kartavyāḥ pūjitāśca tathā gṛhāḥ|
pumbhiḥ snātānuliptaiśca bhāvyaṃ bālairviśeṣataḥ|| 382 ||

na bhoktavyaṃ divā cāpi taddinaṃ puruṣaiḥ sadā|
kevalaṃ bhojanaṃ deyaṃ bālāturajanasya ca|| 383 ||

saphalaiḥ pattrasaṃghātaiḥ pūjanīyāstadā gṛhāḥ|
candrodaye tataḥ prāpte samprajvālya hutāśanam|| 384 ||

rudraṃ candram umāṃ skandaṃ nāsatyau nandinaṃ tathā|
pūjayitvārghamālyādi- naivedyaiśca pṛthak pṛthak|| 385 ||

tataḥ pūjā nikumbhasya kartavyā kṛsareṇa tu|
ādityaputro revantaḥ sāśvaiḥ pūjyaśca mānavaiḥ|| 386 ||

pūjanīyā ca surabhirgomadbhiḥ puruṣaistadā|
yeṣāṃ ca chāgalāḥ santi taiśca pūjyo hutāśanaḥ|| 387 ||

aurabhrikaistathā devaḥ pūjanīyo jalādhipaḥ|
yeṣāṃ santi karīndrāśca taiśca pūjyo gaṇādhipaḥ|| 388 ||

kṛtvāgnihavanaṃ paścāt pūjayitvā dvijottamān|
prayujya cātmanaḥ pūjāṃ bhoktavyaṃ māṃsavarjitam|
sārdhaṃ mitraistathā bhṛtyairdārāpatyādibhistathā|| 389 ||

vastavyā ca niśā saiva vahneḥ pārśvagatairnaraiḥ|
śaṅkhavādaravonmiśrairgītavādyaiśca sarvaśaḥ|| 390 ||

neyā bhavati rājendra tathā prekṣaṇakaiḥ śubhaiḥ|
tathā prabhātasamaye svanuliptaiḥ svalaṃkṛtaiḥ|| 391 ||

vahnipūjā ca kartavyā maṅgalālambhanaṃ tathā|
bhoktavyaṃ saha mitraiśca krīḍitavyaṃ yathāsukham|| 392 ||

suptavyaṃ tāṃ tathā rātrīṃ dvitīyāyām anantaram|
kardamenānuliptāṅgaiḥ krīḍitavyaṃ tathā naraiḥ|| 393 ||

suhṛdaḥ kardamenāpi lepayadbhiritastataḥ|
kāmārthavādibhiḥ sarvaistalliṅgārthaprabodhakaiḥ|| 394 ||

gantṛgamyaviśeṣaiśca vividhaiśca subhāṣitaiḥ|
aślīlaṃ vadamānaiśca hi ākrośadbhistathā dvija|| 395 ||

tasminnahani pūrvāhṇe nikumbhasyānuyāyinaḥ|
āviśanti narān sarvān piśācā ghoradarśanāḥ|| 396 ||

yaścaivaṃ kurute tasya hyaparāhṇe tadā tanum|
tyaktvā snātasya gacchanti śapante cāpyakāraṇam|| 397 ||

tataḥ snātaiśca kartavyaṃ keśavasyārcanaṃ naraiḥ|
sampūjya viprān bhoktavyaṃ svanuliptaiḥ svalaṃkṛtaiḥ|| 398 ||

mitrānujīvibhiḥ sārdhaṃ dārāpatyādibhistathā|
tataḥprabhṛti ṣaṇmāsān sveṣu veśmasu mānavaiḥ|| 399 ||

agniḥ saṃnihitaḥ kāryo rātrau viprairviśeṣataḥ|
rātrau dīpaśca dātavyo māsam ekaṃ bahirgṛhāt|| 400 ||

yāvat kārtikamāsasya paurṇamāsīṃ dvijottama|
eṣā tu kaumudī nāma tithiḥ kāryā śivapradā|| 401 ||

iti nīlamate kaumudī|

nīla uvāca |

tataḥ pakṣe vyatīte tu kartavyā sukhasuptikā|
pañcadaśyāṃ yathā vipra tathā me gadataḥ śṛṇu|| 402 ||

tasyāṃ divā na bhoktavyaṃ bālāturajanaṃ vinā|
sūrye tvastam anuprāpte pūjayitvā karīṣiṇīm|| 403 ||

dīpavṛkṣāstato deyā devatāyataneṣu ca|
catuṣpathaśmaśāneṣu nadīparvataveśmasu|| 404 ||

vṛkṣamūleṣu goṣṭheṣu catvareṣu āpaṇeṣu ca|
vastraiścaivāpaṇāḥ sarve kartavyā dvija śobhitāḥ|| 405 ||

dīpamālāparikṣipte pradeśe tadanantaram|
svalaṃkṛtena bhoktavyaṃ dvijendra navavāsasā|
suhṛdbhirbandhubhiḥ sārdhaṃ brāhmaṇaiścānuyāyibhiḥ|| 406 ||

tataḥ prāpte dvitīye 'ahni svanuliptaiḥ svalaṃkṛtaiḥ|
krīḍitavyaṃ tadā dyūtaiḥ śrotavyaṃ gītavāditam|| 407 ||

viśeṣavacca bhoktavyaṃ pūrvoktaistairjanaiḥ saha|
tasmin dyūte jayo yasya tasya saṃvatsaraḥ śubhaḥ|| 408 ||

tasyāṃ rātrau tu kartavyaṃ śayyāsthānaṃ suśobhitam|
gandhairvastraistathā puṣpai ratnaiścaivābhyalaṃkṛtam|| 409 ||

dīpamālāparikṣiptaṃ tathā dhūpena dhūpitam|
dayitābhiśca sahitairneyā sā ca niśā bhavet|| 410 ||

navaiśca vastraiḥ pūjyāśca suhṛtsambandhibāndhavāḥ|
brāhmaṇā bhṛtyavargāśca candradeva yathāvidhi|| 411 ||

iti nīlamate dīpamālāvidhiḥ|

nīla uvāca |

ekādaśyāṃ tato rātrau śuklapakṣasya mānavaḥ|
sopavāso hariṃ devaṃ nṛttagītairvibodhayet|| 412 ||

āṣāḍhamāsi pratimāṃ keśavasya tu kārayet|
suptāṃ tu śeṣaparyaṅke śilāmṛddhemadārubhiḥ|| 413 ||

tāmrārakūṭarajataiścitre vāpi niveśayet|
lakṣmyutsaṅgagatau pādau tadā tasya tu kārayet|| 414 ||

kārttikasya tu śuklānte kāryaṃ tasya vibodhanam|
yathā tathā me gadataḥ śṛṇu tvaṃ munipuṃgava|| 415 ||

ekādaśyāṃ tu kartavyaṃ rātrau jāgaraṇaṃ tathā|
gītairnṛttaistathā vādyairbrahmaghoṣaistathaiva ca|| 416 ||

vīṇāpaṭahaśabdaiśca purāṇānāṃ ca vācanaiḥ|
tatkathāśravaṇaiścānyaistathā stotraprakīrtanaiḥ|| 417 ||

prekṣaṇīyapradānaiśca bhūmiśobhābhireva ca|
puṣpadhūpapradānaiśca naivedyairvividhaistathā|| 418 ||

dīpavṛkṣaiśca vividhairvahnipūjābhireva ca|
bhakṣyairapūpaiḥ śākaiśca paramānnaistathā phalaiḥ|| 419 ||

ikṣorvikārairmadhunā mṛdvīkābhavyadāḍimaiḥ|
kuṭherakasya mañjaryā mālatyā lavaṇena ca|| 420 ||

raktasūtreṇa raktena candanena sitena ca|
alaktakena bījaiśca kuṅkumena sugandhinā|| 421 ||

sampūjya pratimāṃ rātrau dvitīye 'ahani paṇḍitaḥ|
snātvā nadījale puṇye pratimāṃ snāpayecchubhām|| 422 ||

utthitāṃ tu parāṃ brahman pūrvadravyavinirmitām|
yadi citraniviṣṭā syāt pratimā brāhmaṇottama|| 423 ||

pañcarātravidhānena vedyām āvāhya tāṃ budhaḥ|
āsanasthāṃ yathāśaktyā snāpayeta yathāvidhi|| 424 ||

ādau ājyena tailena madhunā tadanantaram|
dadhnā kṣīreṇa ca tataḥ pañcagavyena cāpyatha|| 425 ||

udvartanaṃ tato deyaṃ māṣacūrṇaṃ tataḥ param|
tato masūracūrṇaṃ ca tatastu āmalakāni ca|| 426 ||

rodhraṃ kāleyakaṃ caiva tagaraṃ karṇakaṃ tathā|
siddhārthakaṃ priyaṅguṃ ca tato vai bījapūrakam|| 427 ||

sarvauṣadhyaḥ sarvagandhāḥ sarvabījāni kāñcanam|
maṅgalyāni yathālābhaṃ ratnāni ca kuśodakam|| 428 ||

hastidantoddhṛtā mṛcca vṛṣaśṛṅgoddhṛtā tathā|
nadītīrāt sagosthānādvalmīkāt saṃgamāddhradāt|| 429 ||

indrasthānācca sarasastathā parvatamastakāt|
etaiḥ saṃsnāpya deveśaṃ dadyādgorocanaṃ śubham|| 430 ||

tatastu kalaśā deyā yathāśakti svalaṃkṛtāḥ|
jātipallavasampūrṇāḥ phalapūrṇāstu kāñcanāḥ|| 431 ||

puṇyāhavedaśabdena vīṇāveṇuraveṇa ca|
sūtamāgadhaśabdena tathā vandisvanena ca|| 432 ||

evaṃ saṃsnāpya govindaṃ svanuliptaṃ svalaṃkṛtam|
suvāsasaṃ pūjayeta jātīpuṣpaiḥ sakuḍmalaiḥ|| 433 ||

dhūpaṃ ca sarajo deyaṃ dīpaṃ dadyāt suśobhanam|
tatastu pūjayeddevaṃ paramānnaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|| 434 ||

mātraratnapradānaiśca pūjyā bhāgavatāstataḥ|
tato 'agnihavanaṃ kāryaṃ viprāḥ pūjyāstvanantaram|| 435 ||

vāsobhirbhūṣaṇai ratnairgobhiraśvairgajairdhanaiḥ|
yathāvibhavato vipra bhoktavyaṃ tadanantaram|| 436 ||

trayodaśyāṃ tataḥ pūjyā janā ye raṅgajīvinaḥ|
jhallamallādayo brahman svavittasyānurūpataḥ|| 437 ||

caturdaśyāṃ na bhoktavyaṃ bhoktavyaṃ payasāpi vā|
pañcadaśyāṃ tataḥ pūjyo devadevo janārdanaḥ|| 438 ||

paurṇamāsīṃ tu samprāpya na bhoktavyaṃ tadā divā|
tataścandrodaye prāpte pūjanīyāśca kṛttikāḥ|
kārttikeyaṃ tathā khaḍgaṃ varuṇaṃ sahutāśanam|| 439 ||

mālyairgandhaistathā dhūpairbhakṣyairuccāvacaistathā|
paramānnaistathā śākairvahnisaṃtarpaṇaistathā|| 440 ||

ikṣūṇāṃ ca vikāraiśca dīpavṛkṣaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|
kulmāṣairlopikābhiśca dvijānāṃ paripūjanaiḥ|| 441 ||

evaṃ kṛtvā tadā pūjāṃ māsadattaṃ tu dīpakam|
gṛhādbahiryat tu dattaṃ piṭake tat tu kārayet|| 442 ||

apāṃ samīpe nītvā tu sabhakṣyaṃ tu pravāhayet|
tasya mūlaṃ tu kartavyaṃ tato vai candanārcitam|| 443 ||

kṣīreṇa pūrṇaṃ taṃ kṛtvā matsyaṃ tu sikatāmayam|
muktānetraṃ nyaset tasmiṃstaṃ ca vipre nivedayet|| 444 ||

balivardaṃ tato deyaṃ śvetaṃ śaktyā viśeṣataḥ|
sarvasasyadharaṃ ramyaṃ sarvagandhasamanvitam|| 445 ||

savāsasaṃ dvija dadyāt kāntāre sopatiṣṭhati|
yāmyaṃ mārgaṃ hi kāntāraṃ tena yānti vipaścitaḥ|| 446 ||

yāvanti romakūpāṇi tasya dāntasya kaśyapa|
tāvadvarṣasahasrāṇi svarge modanti tatpradāḥ|| 447 ||

pūjayitvā tato viṣṇuṃ raktamālyādibhiḥ svayam|
bhoktavyaṃ gorasaprāyaṃ suptavyaṃ cāpyanantaram|| 448 ||

devotthāpanam etaddhi kartavyaṃ dinapañcakam|
pañcāham etacca tathā suptavyaṃ sthaṇḍile budhaiḥ|| 449 ||

dine dine ca snātavyaṃ nadītoye suśītale|
pūjanīyo harirdevo brāhmaṇāḥ sahutāśanāḥ|| 450 ||

varjanīyaṃ tadā māṃsaṃ prayatnādapi kāśyapa|
daityadānavayakṣāśca piśācā rākṣasaiḥ saha|
varjayanti tadā māṃsaṃ māṃsādā dinapañcakam|| 451 ||

evaṃ sampūjya deveśaṃ sarvakāmasamanvitam|
āyuṣaḥ pāram āsādya viṣṇuloke mahīyate|| 452 ||

svavittaśaktyā kartavyam apyuktaṃ nyūnam eva tu|
prāpnotīdaṃ phalaṃ sarvaṃ vittaśāṭhyaṃ vivarjayet|| 453 ||

iti nīlamate devotthāpanam|

nīla uvāca |

kārttikyāṃ samatītāyāṃ samprāpte prathame 'ahani|
kaśmīrā nirmitā pūrvaṃ kaśyapena mahātmanā|| 454 ||

tasmāt tatradine kāryam utsavaṃ sarvamānavaiḥ|
svāśitaiḥ svanuliptāṅgaiḥ sucittaiḥ sujanāvṛtaiḥ|| 455 ||

śrotavyaṃ gītavādyādi tathā sevyaṃ ca maṅgalam|
pānaṃ ca pānapaiḥ peyaṃ vastraṃ dhāryaṃ tathā navam|| 456 ||

iti nīlamate navasaṃvatsarapraveśaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

tasyātitoṣam āyāti sagaṇo bhāskaraḥ svayam|
 |
eṣa eva vidhiḥ kāryastathā māghasya saptamīm|| 457 ||

āṣāḍhasaptamīṃ caiva yaśovijayakāṅkṣibhiḥ|
saptamītritayaṃ caiva dhruvam etaddvijottama|| 458 ||

saptamīṣvatha sarvāsu |
 sūryaloke mahīyate|| 459 ||

iti nīlamate saptamyaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

paurṇamāsīṃ tu samprāpya mārgaśīrṣasya mānavaḥ|
naktāśī pūjayeccandraṃ śuklamālyādibhistathā|
annairbhakṣyaprakāraiśca dīpadānaistathā phalaiḥ|| 460 ||

lavaṇānāṃ pradānaiśca vahnipūjābhireva ca|
pūjanairbrāhmaṇānāṃ ca subhagānāṃ tathaiva ca|| 461 ||

raktavastrayugaṃ deyaṃ subhagā brāhmaṇī tu yā|
svasā pitṛsvasā yā ca mitrapatnī tu yā bhavet|| 462 ||

dhruvam eṣā tu kartavyā paurṇamāsī vicakṣaṇaiḥ|
kāryāścānyāḥ svaśaktyā vā na vā kāryā dvijottama|| 463 ||

kāntaṃ rūpam avāpnoti saubhāgyaṃ vipulaṃ striyaḥ|
strībhirviśeṣataḥ kāryāḥ paurṇamāsyastathā dvija|| 464 ||

iti nīlamate paurṇamāsyaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

yasmiṃstu vāsare vipra prathamaṃ patate himam|
tatra pūjyastu himavān hemantaśiśirāvubhau|| 465 ||

mama pūjā ca kartavyā sthānanāgasya cāpyatha|
phalapattraṃ pradātavyaṃ nage merūdbhave tathā|| 466 ||

bakapuṣpāṇi deyāni dhūpaṃ guggulujaṃ śubham|
baliḥ kāryaḥ prayatnena kulmāṣeṇa dvijottama|| 467 ||

kulmāṣabhojanaṃ deyaṃ saghṛtaṃ brāhmaṇeṣu ca|
utsavaṃ ca sadā kāryaṃ gītanṛttasamākulam|
viśeṣavacca bhoktavyaṃ bhojanaṃ ca yathecchakam|| 468 ||

iti nīlamate himapātaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

navo madyaśca pītavyo madyapaiḥ patite hime|
śyāmā devī ca sampūjyā puṣpadhūpānulepanaiḥ|| 469 ||

annairbhakṣyaiḥ phalairmūlaiḥ svanuliptaiḥ svalaṃkṛtaiḥ|
himopari niviṣṭaiśca guruprāvaraṇāmbaraiḥ|| 470 ||

mitrabhṛtyāptasambandhi- sahitaiśca yathāsukham|
bhojyaṃ viśeṣavat kāryaṃ śrotavyaṃ gītavāditam|
draṣṭavyaṃ puṃścalīnṛttaṃ pūjanīyāstathā striyaḥ|| 471 ||

iti nīlamate navamadyapānam|

nīla uvāca |

pauṣakṛṣṇāṣṭamīṃ kāryaṃ śrāddhaṃ śākairdvijottama|
māghakṛṣṇāṣṭamīṃ māṃsaiḥ phālguṇasya sitāṣṭamīm|
apūpaiḥ kārayecchrāddhaṃ nityam eva samāhitaḥ|| 472 ||

navamīṣu ca tāsveva strīṇāṃ śrāddhaṃ tu kārayet|
śrāddhaṃ kṛtvā prayatnena brahmacārī vasenniśām|| 473 ||

iti nīlamate aṣṭakātrayam|

nīla uvāca |

paurṇamāsī tu pauṣasya puṣyayuktā yadā bhavet|
gaurasarṣapakalkena tadā tūtsādito naraḥ|
ghṛtena snāpanaṃ kuryāt svaśarīrasya mānavaḥ|| 474 ||

tato virūkṣitaḥ snātaḥ sarvauṣadhiyutairghaṭaiḥ|
nārāyaṇaṃ tathā śakraṃ somaṃ pūṣabṛhaspatī|| 475 ||

pūjayitvārghamālyādi- naivedyaiśca pṛthak pṛthak|
mantraistathoktadaivatyaiḥ kṛtvāgnihavanaṃ dvija|| 476 ||

ahatāmbarasaṃvītaḥ svanuliptaḥ svalaṃkṛtaḥ|
pūjayitvā dhanairviprān maṅgalālabdhipūrvakam|| 477 ||

gṛthapāyasam aśnīyāt pūjayitvā dvijottamān|
saṃvītam ahataṃ kartre deyaṃ kālavide bhavet|| 478 ||

evaṃ kṛtvā naraḥ puṣṭiṃ prāpnoti dhanadhānyataḥ|
snānam eva vidhānena kartavyaṃ pāpanāśanam|| 479 ||

iti nīlamate pauṣīnāma|

nīla uvāca |

ghṛtena snāpayeddevaṃ svaśaktyā madhusūdanam|
harabhakto haraṃ devaṃ yadā syāduttarāyaṇam|| 480 ||

ājyaṃ dattvā tathārcāsu kṛtāḥ śailena yā dvija|
arcārūpam athājyena punarutsādayennaraḥ|
arcāḥ pūjyāstathā yatnāt sājyā māsatrayaṃ budhaiḥ|| 481 ||

brāhmaṇeṣu ca dātavyam indhanaṃ śaktitastathā|
tṛṇaṃ dadyādgavām arthe yathāśaktyā dvijātiṣu|| 482 ||

evaṃ yaḥ kurute samyak sa ripūnadhitiṣṭhati|
kāyāgnidīptisaubhāgyaṃ labhate cottamāṃ gatim|| 483 ||

iti nīlamate uttarāyaṇam|

nīla uvāca |

pauṣyāṃ tu samatītāyāṃ kṛṣṇā yā dvādaśī bhavet|
tasyām upoṣitaḥ snātastilairdattvā tilodakam|| 484 ||

kṛtvā tilaiśca naivedyaṃ tilairhomaṃ tathaiva ca|
tilāśca deyā vipreṣu sarvapāpāpanuttaye|| 485 ||

iti nīlamate tiladvādaśī|

nīla uvāca |

tasyāstvanantaraṃ brahman yā syāt kṛṣṇacaturdaśī|
anarkābhyudite kāle snātavyaṃ śītale jale|| 486 ||

vitastāyāṃ viśokāyāṃ candravatyām athāpi vā|
tathā harṣapathāyāṃ vā trikoṭyāṃ vā dvijottama|| 487 ||

sindhuṃ prāpyātha vā puṇyāṃ tathā kanakavāhinīm|
anyāṃ vā saritaṃ puṇyāṃ hradāṃścaiva sarāṃsi ca|| 488 ||

yamasya nāmni dātavyāḥ sapta sapta jalāñjalīḥ|
ekaikasmin dvijaśreṣṭha tāni nāmāni me śṛṇu|| 489 ||

yamāya dharmarājāya mṛtyave cāntakāya ca|
vaivasvatāya kālāya sarvaprāṇaharāya ca|| 490 ||

snātvā ca pūjā kartavyā dharmarājasya vai tadā|
puṣpairdhūpaistathā gandhaiḥ kṛsareṇa ca bhūriṇā|| 491 ||

vahnipūjā ca kartavyā ghṛtayuktaistadā tilaiḥ|
kṛsaraṃ bhojanīyāśca brāhmaṇendrāḥ sadakṣiṇam|| 492 ||

evaṃ kṛtvā naraḥ śuddho mucyate sarvakilbiṣaiḥ|
mahāpātakayuktaścenna bhaveta dvijottama|
mahāpātakināṃ mokṣaḥ prāyaścittairvinā kutaḥ|| 493 ||

iti nīlamate tārārātriḥ|

nīla uvāca |

śravaṇena yutā saiva yadi pañcadaśī bhavet|
tasyāṃ snānādikaṃ sarvam akṣayaṃ parikīrtitam|| 494 ||

iti nīlamate śravaṇāmāvāsyā|

nīla uvāca |

māghamāsi site pakṣe caturthī yā bhaveddvija|
umāsampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ tasyāṃ saubhāgyam īpsunā|| 495 ||

dīpānnamālyadhūpaiścāpi ārdrakeṇa guḍena ca|
kusumbhalavaṇābhyāṃ ca kuṅkumāñjanakaṅkaṇaiḥ|| 496 ||

kundapuṣpaiḥ samānītaiḥ prayatnādapi kāśyapa|
pūjyāśca subhagāstatra yoṣitāstu pativratāḥ|| 497 ||

yāsāṃ jīvanti nāthāśca svasṛprabhṛtayaśca yāḥ|
tathaivāśvayuje māsi tathā jyeṣṭhe ca kārayet|| 498 ||

sarvāścaturthīḥ śraddhāvāṃścaturthītritayaṃ dhruvam|
kārayeta naro brahman nārī kuryādviśeṣataḥ|| 499 ||

iti nīlamate caturthyaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

paurṇamāsyāṃ tu māghasya śrāddhaṃ kṛtvā tilairnaraḥ|
kākānāṃ bhojanaṃ dadyāt prabhūtabalisaṃyutam|| 500 ||

iti nīlamate māghī|

nīla uvāca |

māghyāṃ tu samatītāyām aṣṭamyāṃ tu dinatrayam|
kāryaṃ svalpamahīmānaṃ vidhiṃ tasya nibodha me|| 501 ||

caturviṃśatisaṃkhyāyāṃ tretāyāṃ raghunandanaḥ|
harirmanuṣyo bhavitā rāmo daśarathātmajaḥ|| 502 ||

tasmāt kālāt paraṃ kāryaṃ mahīmānam athālpakam|
tasmādeva paraṃ kāryaṃ mahīmānaṃ tathā bṛhat|| 503 ||

aṣṭamyāṃ sarvasasyaistu caruḥ kāryaḥ prayatnataḥ|
tenāpūpaistathā pūjyā dvijāḥ sambandhibāndhavāḥ|
rāmapatnī tathā pūjya sītā devī prayatnataḥ|| 504 ||

navamyāṃ piṣṭabhojyena madhuyuktena bhojayet|
brāhmaṇādyān yathāśakti pūjayeta karīṣiṇīm|| 505 ||

bahuprakārasaṃyuktaṃ daśamyām odanaṃ tataḥ|
kārayet tena sampūjyā dvijamitrānuyāyinaḥ|| 506 ||

ātmapūjā prakartavyā śrotavyaṃ gītavāditam|
maṅgalālabhanaṃ kāryaṃ nityam eva dinatrayam|| 507 ||

iti nīlamate mahīmānam|

nīla uvāca |

saiva cecchravaṇopetā yadā syāddvādaśī dvija|
sopavāso hariṃ devaṃ tasyāṃ sampūjayedbudhaḥ|| 508 ||

tilavacca tathā tasyāṃ pūrvoktaṃ karma kārayet|
sarvaṃ tadakṣayaṃ tasyāṃ kṛtaṃ bhavati mānada|| 509 ||

iti nīlamate śravaṇadvādaśī|

nīla uvāca |

tasyāṃ tu samatītāyāṃ yā syāt kṛṣṇacaturdaśī|
tasyām upoṣitaḥ snātvā pūjayeta maheśvaram|| 510 ||

ghṛtakambalahīnaṃ tu liṅgaṃ saṃsnāpayedbudhaḥ|
devotthānavidhānoktairdravyaiśca vidhinā tadā|| 511 ||

sampūjya gandhamālyādi- raktavastrānulepanaiḥ|
naivedyairvividhairbrahman vahnibrāhmaṇatarpaṇaiḥ|| 512 ||

bhuktvā rātrau tataḥ kāryaṃ nṛttagītaiḥ prajāgaram|
śrotavyāḥ śivadharmāśca prādurbhāvāśca tatkṛtāḥ|| 513 ||

paiṣṭāśca paśavaḥ kāryā naivedyaṃ śaṃkarasya ca|
pañcadaśyāṃ ca sampūjyastatrāpi dvijapuṃgava|| 514 ||

kulmāṣalopikāmiśraṃ bhoktavyaṃ bhojanaṃ tathā|
tasmin māsi dhruvaṃ pūjyo devaḥ kṛṣṇacaturdaśīm|| 515 ||

icchayā pūjanīyaḥ syāccheṣamāseṣu vā na vā|
sampūjya rudralokastho gāṇapatyam avāpnuyāt|| 516 ||

iti nīlamate śivarātriḥ|

nīla uvāca |

phālguṇasya tu māsasya śuklapakṣe dvijottama|
mahīmānaṃ yathā kāryaṃ tathā me gadataḥ śṛṇu|| 517 ||

anaśnadbhirathāṣṭamyāṃ naraiḥ snātairalaṃkṛtaiḥ|
pradoṣasamaye deyā dīpakāstu himopari|| 518 ||

devatānāṃ pitṛṇāṃ ca bhoktavyaṃ tadanantaram|
dvitīye 'ahani madhyāhne dhānyadāmaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|| 519 ||

pūjanīyā gṛhā vipra devāgārā viśeṣataḥ|
tadā sītā ca sampūjyā gandhamālyādibhistathā|| 520 ||

anantaraṃ ca bhoktavyaṃ bhojanaṃ ca viśeṣavat|
utsavaṃ caiva kartavyaṃ gītanṛttasamākulam|| 521 ||

nityadānaṃ ca sapakvānnam ṛte tasmin dine sadā|
nānyat kiṃcit pradātavyaṃ labdhaṃ grāhyaṃ prayatnataḥ|| 522 ||

tṛtīye 'ahani kartavyaṃ pratikarma tathātmanaḥ|
maṅgalālabhanaṃ kāryam utsavaṃ ca viśeṣavat|| 523 ||

āśritānāṃ dvijātīnāṃ śilpisambandhināṃ tathā|
tasminnahani dātavyaṃ grāhyaṃ caivāpyupāyanam|| 524 ||

madyaṃ tu madyapaiḥ peyaṃ brāhmaṇaiḥ pānakāḥ śubhāḥ|
śayyāsthānaṃ ca kartavyaṃ dhūpagandhādhivāsitam|| 525 ||

tasminnahani no kāryo vimukhaḥ kaścideva tu|
strībhirbhāvyaṃ prahṛṣṭābhiḥ suvastrābhistathaiva ca|| 526 ||

svāśitābhiḥ sugandhābhiḥ svanuliptābhireva tu|
bhūṣaṇairbhūṣitābhiśca krīḍitavyaṃ naraiḥ saha|| 527 ||

iti nīlamate mahīmānam|

nīla uvāca |

phālguṇyāṃ tu tato rātrau prāpte candrodaye śubhe|
pūjā kāryā śaśāṅkasya hyaryamṇaścāpyanantaram|| 528 ||

gītairnṛttaistathā vādyai rātrau kāryaḥ prajāgaraḥ|
dvitīye 'ahni tataḥ prāpte prekṣā deyā dvijottama|| 529 ||

nartakānāṃ naṭānāṃ ca cāraṇānāṃ tathaiva ca|
tāvadetadbhavet kāryaṃ yāvat syāt kṛṣṇapañcamī|| 530 ||

bhojanaṃ parpaṭaprāyaṃ bhoktavyaṃ dinapañcakam|
pratikarma tathā kāryaṃ strījanasya tathātmanaḥ|| 531 ||

iti nīlamate phālguṇī|

nīla uvāca |

tasyām eva tu pañcamyāṃ kaśmīrā tu rajasvalā|
yasmādbhavati kartavyā tasyāḥ pūjā tato dvija|| 532 ||

ramyā śailamayī kāryā kaśmīrā tāṃ ca pūjayet|
abhyaṅgavastradānena naivedyaṃ ca nivedayet|| 533 ||

puṣpadhūpādyalaṃkāraṃ na dātavyaṃ dinatrayam|
naivedye gorasaṃ sarvaṃ varjanīyaṃ dvijottama|| 534 ||

strībhistu pūjā kartavyā na manuṣyaiḥ kathaṃcana|
snāpyā strībhirbhaveddevī kṛṣṇapakṣāṣṭamīṃ tu tām|| 535 ||

anantaraṃ dvijaiḥ snāpyā sarvauṣadhiyutairghaṭaiḥ|
tato gandhaistato bījaistato ratnaistataḥ phalaiḥ|| 536 ||

snāpayitvā ca tāṃ devīṃ gandhairmālyaiśca pūjayet|
vastrālaṃkaraṇaiścānnairviśeṣairgorasodbhavaiḥ|
maudgaiḥ paiṣṭaistrikoṇaiśca tathā taṇḍulaśālibhiḥ|| 537 ||

kartavyaṃ devayajanaṃ bandhūnāṃ caiva dāpayet|
vahnipūjā ca kartavyā kartavyaṃ dvijapūjanam|| 538 ||

susnātābhiḥ prahṛṣṭābhiḥ svāśitābhirdvijottama|
strībhirbhāvyaṃ sugandhābhiḥ suvastrābhiśca taddinam|| 539 ||

bhojanaṃ preṣanīyaṃ ca tathā mitragṛhe dvija|
tantrīvādyaṃ sumadhuraṃ śrotavyaṃ svāśitaiḥ sukham|| 540 ||

iti nīlamate rājñīsnāpanam|

nīla uvāca |

tataḥprabhṛti kaśmīrā ṛtusnātā dvijottama|
garbhaṃ gṛhṇātyataḥ kāryaṃ kṛṣyārambhaṃ tataḥ param|| 541 ||

dine daivajñanirdiṣṭe kṣetraṃ kṛtvā suhṛdvṛtaḥ|
pūjayet pṛthivīṃ devīṃ goyugaṃ surabhiṃ hayam|| 542 ||

baladevaṃ mahādevaṃ vāmadevaṃ divākaram|
oṣadhīśaṃ niśānāthaṃ parjanyendrau pracetasam|| 543 ||

rāmaṃ salakṣmaṇaṃ sītāṃ śeṣaṃ ca dharaṇīdharam|
brahmāṇaṃ kaśyapaṃ vahniṃ vāyuṃ gaganam eva ca|
mālyairgandhaistathā dhūpairnaivedyaiśca pṛthak pṛthak|| 544 ||

vahnisampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ tato brāhmaṇapūjanam|
brāhmaṇānāṃ tato deyā dakṣiṇā vittaśaktitaḥ|| 545 ||

tatastu vāpayedbījaṃ puruṣo lakṣaṇānvitaḥ|
svāśitaśca suvastraśca svanuliptaḥ svalaṃkṛtaḥ|| 546 ||

bījaṃ suvarṇatoyāktaṃ sasuvarṇaṃ ca vāpayet|
puṇyāhadvijaghoṣeṇa vādyaśabdena bhūriṇā|| 547 ||

halena vāhayedbhūmiṃ pūrvaṃ prākpravaṇāṃ śubhām|
svalaṃkṛtena bhoktavyaṃ kṣetramadhye tathā dvija|| 548 ||

suhṛdbhāryāśritaiḥ sārdhaṃ vādyaśabdairmanoharaiḥ|
utsavaṃ caiva kartavyaṃ gītanṛttasamākulam|| 549 ||

iti nīlamate kṣṛyārambhaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

phālguṇyāṃ samatītāyāṃ yā dvijaikādaśī bhavet|
tasyāṃ strībhirbhavet pūjyaśchandodeva iti smṛtiḥ|| 550 ||

manuṣyaistu na kartavyā tasya pūjā kathaṃcana|
brahmaṇo varadānena strībhiḥ pūjām avāptavān|| 551 ||

jalodbhavānāṃ māṃsena bhakṣyairuccāvacaistathā|
mālyairdhūpaiśca vividhaiḥ kuṅkumena sugandhinā|| 552 ||

evaṃ sampūjanaṃ kṛtvā dvādaśyāṃ pūjayedbudhaḥ|
dvāreṇādau viniṣkālya gavākṣeṇa praveśayet|
svaveśmano yathākāmaṃ sthāpayeta tadā dvija|| 553 ||

iti nīlamate chandodevapūjā|

nīla uvāca |

tataścaturdaśīṃ prāpya tām eva dvijapuṃgava|
sampūjya śaṃkaraṃ kāryaṃ rātrau tu mahadutsavam|| 554 ||

tasyāṃ vipra caturdaśyāṃ nikumbhaḥ śaṃkaraṃ tadā|
sampūjayati dharmātmā sānuyatro mahābalaḥ|| 555 ||

tasyāṃ tadā prakartavyaṃ niśi nityaṃ prajāgaram|
pūjā ca devadevasya śambhoḥ kāryā prayatnataḥ|| 556 ||

pūjanīyo nikumbhastu piśācādhipatirbalī|
piśācānāṃ ca dātavyā balayaśca susaṃskṛtāḥ|| 557 ||

palalollopikāmiśrā matsyamāṃsāmiṣairyutāḥ|
vṛkṣamūleṣu goṣṭheṣu gṛheṣu vividheṣvapi|| 558 ||

catuṣpatheṣu rathyāsu catvareṣu nadīṣu ca|
śūnyālayeṣu mukhyeṣu girīṇāṃ śikhareṣu ca|| 559 ||

aṭṭālakaśmaśāneṣu rājamārgeṣu kāśyapa|
tāṃ rātriṃ rakṣaṇaṃ kāryaṃ bālakānāṃ gṛhe gṛhe|| 560 ||

puṃścalīsahitairneyā krīḍamānairniśā tu sā|
brahmacaryeṇa gītena nṛttairvādyairmanoharaiḥ|| 561 ||

iti nīlamate piśācacaturdaśī|

nīla uvāca |

tataḥ pañcadaśīṃ prāpya hyantyāṃ saṃvatsarasya tu|
śrāddhaṃ kṛtvā pradātavyaṃ śunām annaṃ yathecchakam|| 562 ||

iti nīlamate antyāmāvāsyā|

nīla uvāca |

caitraśuklasamārambhe prathame 'ahani kāśyapa|
pitāmahasya kartavyā tadā pūjā vicakṣaṇaiḥ|| 563 ||

puṣpairnānāvidhairgandhairvastrālaṃkāradhūpanaiḥ|
hutāśapūjanairbrahman brāhmaṇānāṃ ca tarpaṇaiḥ|| 564 ||

iti nīlamate brahmapūjā|

nīla uvāca |

tasminnevāhni kartavyā mahāśāntirdvijottama|
āḍhyena rakṣaṇārthāya śriyastatprāpanāya ca|| 565 ||

tasminnevāhni kartavyā pūjā kālasya kāśyapa|
tasmin kālasya gaṇanā pravṛttā pūrvam eva tu|| 566 ||

tasminnahani vai sṛṣṭaṃ brahmaṇedaṃ jagat purā|
sūryodaye dvijaśreṣṭha ityevam anuśusruma|| 567 ||

pūjanīyāstathā devā brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarāḥ|
graharkṣaśāntiḥ kartavyā daivajñavidhicoditā|| 568 ||

pūjanīyā grahāḥ sarve nakṣatrāṇi ca mānada|
kālasyāvayavāḥ sarve ye ca saṃvatsarādayaḥ|| 569 ||

kālakalpāvubhau pūjyau manavaśca caturdaśa|
atītāśca bhaviṣyāśca teṣāṃ nāmāni me śṛṇu|| 570 ||

svāyambhuvo manuḥ pūrvaṃ manuḥ svārociṣastathā|
auttamastāmasaścaiva raivataścākṣuṣastathā|| 571 ||

vaivasvato 'arkasāvarṇau brahmasāvarṇa eva ca|
bhadreśadakṣasāvarṇau raucyo bhautyastathaiva ca|| 572 ||

sampūjanīyā devendrās tathā brahmaṃścaturdaśa|
viśvabhucca vipaścicca sucittiśca nidhistathā|| 573 ||

vibhurmanojavaścaiva tejasvī ca tathā baḍiḥ|
adbhutaśca tathā śāntirvṛṣo devavarastathā|| 574 ||

ṛtudhāmā ca devendraḥ śuciḥ śuklaścaturdaśa|
yugānāṃ ca tathā pujā kartavyā dvijasattama|| 575 ||

pañca saṃvatsarāḥ pūjyāḥ pūjyaṃ caivāyanadvayam|
ṛtuṣaṭkaṃ tathā pūjyaṃ māsā dvādaśa cāpyatha|| 576 ||

dvau pakṣau tithayaścaiva pūjyāḥ pañcadaśaiva tu|
karaṇāśca muhūrtāśca rāśayaśca pṛthak pṛthak|| 577 ||

marīcim atryaṅgirasau pulastyaṃ pulahaṃ kratum|
bhṛguṃ sanatkumāraṃ ca sanakaṃ ca sanandanam|| 578 ||

dharmaṃ vasiṣṭhaṃ satyaṃ ca kāmārthau ca hutāśanam|
vasurudrā/llokapālā/llokālokanivāsinaḥ|| 579 ||

sudhāmānaṃ śaṅkhapādaṃ ketumantaṃ tathaiva ca|
tathā hiraṇyaromāṇaṃ dikpālāṃścaiva pūjayet|| 580 ||

śakrādyān brāhmaṇaśreṣṭha dakṣaputryastathaiva ca|
satī khyātiḥ smṛtiḥ svāhā hyanasūyā tathā svadhā|| 581 ||

prītiḥ kṣamā ca sambhūtiḥ saṃnatiścāpyarundhatī|
kīrtirlakṣmīrdhṛtirmedhā puṣṭiḥ śraddhā kriyā matiḥ|| 582 ||

buddhirlajjā vasuḥ śāntistuṣṭiḥ siddhistathā ratiḥ|
arundhatī vasurdāśī lambā bhānurmarutvatī|| 583 ||

saṃkalpā ca muhūrtā ca sādhyā viśvā ca kāśyapa|
aditirditirdanuḥ kālā danāyuḥ siṃhikā muniḥ|| 584 ||

kadrūḥ krodhā irā pravā vinatā surabhiḥ khaśā|
kṛśāśvaśca tathā pūjyaḥ suprabhā ca tathā jayā|| 585 ||

bahuputraśca sampūjyastasya patnidvayaṃ tathā|
patnīcatuṣkasaṃyuktaṃ pūjyaṃ cāriṣṭaneminam|| 586 ||

ṛddhiṃ vṛddhiṃ tathā nidrāṃ dhaneśaṃ naḍakūbaram|
śaṅkhapadmau nidhī pūjyau bhadrakālī sarasvatī|| 587 ||

vedopavedavedāṅga- vidyāsthānāni kṛtsnaśaḥ|
nāgā yakṣāḥ piśācāśca tathaiva garuḍāruṇau|| 588 ||

jambuḥ śākaḥ kuśaḥ krauñcaḥ śalmalirdvīpa eva ca|
gomedaḥ puṣkaraścaiva dvīpāḥ pūjyāḥ pṛthak pṛthak|| 589 ||

lavaṇaḥ kṣīra ājyaśca dadhimaṇḍaḥ surodakaḥ|
tathaivekṣurasodaśca pūjyaḥ svādūdakastathā|| 590 ||

uttarāḥ kuravaḥ pūjyā ramyo hairaṇvatastathā|
bhadrāśvaḥ ketumālaśca varṣaścaiva ilāvṛtaḥ|| 591 ||

harivarṣaḥ kiṃpuruṣo varṣo bhāratasaṃjñitaḥ|
bhāratasya tathā bhedāḥ pūjanīyāśca ye nava|| 592 ||

indradyumnaṃ kaśerumāṃstāmravarṇo gabhastimān|
nāgadvīpastathā saumyo gāndharvo vāruṇastathā|| 593 ||

ayaṃ ca navamo dvīpastathā sāgarasaṃvṛtaḥ|
catvāraḥ sāgarāḥ pūjyāstathā pātālasaptakam|| 594 ||

rukmabhaumaḥ śilābhaumaḥ pātālo nīlamṛttikaḥ|
raktabhaumaḥ pītabhaumaḥ śvetaḥ kṛṣṇakṣitistathā|| 595 ||

kālāgnirudraḥ śeṣaśca varāhaśca tathā hariḥ|
bhūrbhuvaḥ svarmahaścaiva janaśca tapasā saha|
satyalokastathā lokāḥ pūjanīyā dvijottama|| 596 ||

pṛthivī āpastathā tejaḥ pavanaṃ paṃ ca pūjayet|
manobuddhī tathātmānam avyaktaṃ puruṣaṃ tathā|| 597 ||

himavān hemakūṭaśca niṣadho nīlaparvataḥ|
śvetaśca śṛṅgavān merurmālyavān gandhamādanaḥ|| 598 ||

parvatapravaraḥ pūjyo yo nāmnā mānasottaraḥ|
mahendro malayaḥ sahyaḥ śuktimānṛkṣavānapi|| 599 ||

vindhyaśca pāriyātraśca kailāsaśca nagottamaḥ|
bhāgīrathī pāvanī ca hlādinī hrādinī tathā|| 600 ||

sītā vaṅkṣuśca sindhuśca sapta gaṅgāśca mānada|
suprabhā kāñcanākṣī ca viśālā mānasahradā|| 601 ||

sarasvatyoghanāmā ca suverurvimalodakā|
puṣkarādyāni tīrthāni vitastādyaśca nimnagāḥ|| 602 ||

śacī vanaspatirgaurī dhūmrorṇā rucirākṛtiḥ|
sinīvālī kuhūḥ rākā tathā cānumatiḥ śubhā|| 603 ||

āyatirniyatiḥ prajñā matirvelā ca dhāriṇī|
devau dhātāvidhātārau sapta cchandāṃsi cāpyatha|| 604 ||

airāvaṇaṃ ca surabhim uccaiḥśravasam eva ca|
dhanvantariṃ dhruvaṃ caiva śastrāṇyastrāṇi cāpyatha|| 605 ||

vināyakaṃ kumāraṃ ca tathaiva ca vināyakān|
 || 606 ||

śākho viśākhaḥ skandaśca naigameṣastathaiva ca|
marutaśca grahāścaiva rogāṇām adhipo jvaraḥ|| 607 ||

ṛṣayo vālakhilyāśca kaśyapāgastyanāradāḥ|
tathaivāpsarasaḥ pūjyāḥ pūjyā devāśca somapāḥ|| 608 ||

ādityā vasavo rudrās viśvedevāstathāśvinau|
bhṛgavo 'aṅgirasāḥ sādhyā marutaśca mahābalāḥ|| 609 ||

dhātā mitro 'aryamā pūṣā śakro 'aṃśo varuṇo bhagaḥ|
tvaṣṭā vivasvān savitā viṣṇurdvādaśa bhānavaḥ|| 610 ||

dharo dhruvaśca bhojaśca āpaścaivānilānalau|
pratyūsaśca prabhāsaśca vasavo 'aṣṭau prakīrtitāḥ|| 611 ||

aṅgārakaṃ tathā sūryaṃ nirṛtiṃ ghoṣam eva ca|
ajaikapāccāhirbudhnyo dhūmaketuṃ dhvajaṃ tathā|| 612 ||

havanaṃ ceśvaraṃ mṛtyuṃ kapālim atha kaṅkaṇim|
ekādaśaite vijñeyā rudrāstribhuvaneśvarāḥ|| 613 ||

kraturdakṣo vasuḥ satyaḥ kālaḥ kāmo dhvanistathā|
kuruvāgdanujo viśve rocamānaśca te daśa|| 614 ||

nāsatyadasrau vijñeyāvaśvinau vadatāṃ vara|
bhuvano bhānavaścaiva sujanyaḥ sujanastathā|| 615 ||

tyājaḥ suvaśca mūrdhā ca dakṣaśca vyaśca bandhukaḥ|
prasavaścāvyayaścaiva bhṛgavo dvādaśa smṛtāḥ|| 616 ||

ātmā hi āyurmano dakṣo madaḥ prāṇastathaiva ca|
haviṣmāṃśca gaviṣṭhaśca ṛtuḥ satyastathaiva ca|| 617 ||

ityete 'aṅgirasaḥ putrā daśa devā mahābalāḥ|
mano madaśca prāṇaśca naraḥ pālaśca vīryavān|| 618 ||

ditirhayo hayaścaiva haṃso nārāyaṇastathā|
vibhuścāpi prabhuścāpi sādhyā dvādaśa kīrtitāḥ|| 619 ||

ekajyotirdvijyotiśca trijyotirjyotireva ca|
ekacakro dvicakraśca tricakraśca mahābalāḥ|| 620 ||

indraśca kūramo 'adṛśyastataḥ prakṛtsnakṛt tathā|
asitaśca samitaśca sasmitaśca mahābalaḥ|| 621 ||

ṛtajit satyajiccaiva suṣeṇaḥ senajit tathā|
agnimitro 'arimitraśca prabhamitro 'aparājitaḥ|| 622 ||

ṛtaśca ṛtavān dhartā nidhartā varuṇo dhruvaḥ|
vidhāraṇo nāmā tathā devadevo mahābalaḥ|| 623 ||

īdṛkṣaścāpyadṛkṣaśca ihādṛk cāmitāśanaḥ|
kṛtinaḥ prasakṛddakṣaḥ samaraśca mahāyaśāḥ|| 624 ||

dhātā hyugro dhanurbhīmo 'abhiyuktaḥ sadāsahaḥ|
dyutirvasuratho 'adṛśyo vāmaḥ kāmo jayo virāṭ|| 625 ||

ete hyekonapañcāsanmarutaḥ parikīrtitāḥ|
viśvakarmā tathā pūjyaḥ sarvaśilpapravartakaḥ|| 626 ||

āyudhaṃ vāhanaṃ chattram āsanaṃ cihnadundubhī|
sampūjyā vidhivadbrahman gandhamālyānulepanaiḥ|| 627 ||

dīpadhūpapradānaiśca naivedyaiśca pṛthak pṛthak|
eteṣāṃ pūjanaṃ kṛtvā pūjanīyā viśeṣataḥ|| 628 ||

graho nāgastathā māso yaḥ syāt saṃvatsaraprabhuḥ|
graho bhaviṣyadvarṣasya tathā māsasya vārakaḥ|| 629 ||

daivajñavaktrādvijñeyau grahamāsau vicakṣaṇaiḥ|
daivajñādeva vijñeyaṃ māsavarṣasya vārakam|| 630 ||

eteṣāṃ pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ bahvannakusumotkaraiḥ|
phalavedāt tathā jñātvā nāgavarṣasya vārakam|| 631 ||

tasya pūjā prayoktavyā bhakṣyabhojyapuraḥsarā|
tato 'agnihavanaṃ kāryaṃ sarveṣām anupūrvaśaḥ|| 632 ||

oṃkārapūrvakaṃ brahman ghṛtākṣatayavaistilaiḥ|
tān pṛthak pṛthaguddiśya deyā vipreṣu dakṣiṇā|| 633 ||

brāhmaṇā bhojanīyāśca suhṛtsambandhibāndhavāḥ|
viśeṣavacca bhoktavyaṃ kāryaṃ ca mahadutsavam|| 634 ||

pūjanīyā dvijaśreṣṭha tathā jyotiṣikā dvijāḥ|
dhanadhānyaughavastraiśca pūjyāśca dvijapuṃgavāḥ|| 635 ||

phalavedavidaścaiva itihāsavidaśca ye|
vācakāḥ pūjanīyāśca dakṣiṇābhimukhā dvija|| 636 ||

ātmaśobhā ca kartavyā puṣpālaṃkāradhūpanaiḥ|
kathiteyaṃ mahāśāntiḥ sarvāghavinisūdanī|| 637 ||

sarvotpātapraśamanī kaliduḥsvapnanāśinī|
āyuḥpradā puṣṭikarī dhanasaubhāgyavardhinī|| 638 ||

vyādhiśatrupraśamanī rājyarāṣṭravivardhinī|
maṅgalyā ca pavitrā ca lokadvayasukhāvahā|| 639 ||

caitramāsasamārambhe ye mayābhihitāstava|
te sarve brahmasadanaṃ tadā yānti dvijottama|| 640 ||

brāhmī sabhā kāmarūpā viśeṣeṇa sadānagha|
dhārayatyacalaṃ rūpam anirdeśyaṃ manoharam|| 641 ||

tasyāṃ sabhāyāṃ brahmāṇam anirdeśyaṃ visaṃyutam|
yathoktāstu namasyanti hyupāsanti stuvanti ca|| 642 ||

viśvāvasuḥ śāliśirā gandharvau ca hahāhuhū|
nāradapramukhāścānye gāyante ca jagadgurum|| 643 ||

upanṛtyanti deveśaṃ devarāmāḥ sahasraśaḥ|
urvaśī menakā rambhā miśrakeśī hyalambuṣā|| 644 ||

viśvācī ca ghṛtācī ca pañcacūlā tilottamā|
sānumatyamalā vargā prādhānyena tathetarāḥ|| 645 ||

tadā pitāmaho devaḥ sarvadevasamāgame|
abde niyuṅkte mānuṣye grahādīṃstatra pālakān|| 646 ||

kṛtvopāsāṃ jagadbharturyānti sthānānyataḥ param|
svāni svāni dvijaśreṣṭha hṛṣṭapuṣṭā divaukasaḥ|| 647 ||

iti nīlamate navasaṃvatsaraḥ|

nīla uvāca |

caitramāsi site pakṣe pañcamyāṃ dvijasattama|
śriyastu pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ proktā śrīpañcamī tu sā|| 648 ||

sarvā yā pañcamī pūjyā caitramāsi dhruvaṃ tathā|
yastu sampūjayet sarvāṃ pañcamīṃ tu karīṣiṇīm|| 649 ||

nāsau vimucyate lakṣmyā yāvajjīvaṃ dvijottama|
viṣṇulokam avāpnoti dehabhede dvijottama|| 650 ||

iti nīlamate śrīpañcamī|

nīla uvāca |

 |
skandasya tatra kartavyā pūjā mālyaiḥ sugandhibhiḥ|| 651 ||

gandhālaṃkāravāsāṃsi kukkuṭaṃ ca nivedayet|
ghaṇṭām ajaṃ krīḍanakaṃ naivedyaṃ ca manoharam|| 652 ||

dhruveyaṃ caitraṣaṣṭhī syāt pariśeṣāstu kāmataḥ|
yaḥ karoti gṛhe tasya virogāḥ sarvabālakāḥ|| 653 ||

iti nīlamate caitraṣaṣṭhī|

nīla uvāca |

tām eva navamīṃ prāpya sopavāso naraḥ śuciḥ|
sampūjayedbhadrakālīṃ puṣpadhūpānnasampadā|| 654 ||

sarvā yā navamī pūjyā bhadrakālī sureśvarī|
kāryasiddhim avāpnoti tasyāṃ pūjayitā naraḥ|| 655 ||

iti nīlamate caitranavamī|

nīla uvāca |

ekādaśyāṃ tu caitrasya śuklapakṣe dvijottama|
tasyāṃ pūjā prakartavyā vāstorbrāhmaṇapuṃgava|| 656 ||

puṣpālaṃkāradhūpādyaiḥ śākaiśca vividhaistathā|
apūpairvividhākārairvahnipūjādvijārcanaiḥ|| 657 ||

iti nīlamate vāstupūjā|

nīla uvāca |

dvādaśī yā tu caitrasya śuklā nityam upoṣitaiḥ|
vāsudevasya kartavyā pūjā dvija yathāvidhi|| 658 ||

iti nīlamate caitradvādaśī|

nīla uvāca |

caitraśuklatrayodaśyāṃ kāmadevaṃ tu pūjayet|
paṭṭasthaṃ vividhairmālyairgandhairuccāvacaistathā|| 659 ||

ātmaśobhā ca kartavyā pūjanīyā gṛhastriyaḥ|
iyaṃ dhruvā vinirdiṣṭā śeṣāḥ kāryā na vā dvija|| 660 ||

dvādaśyāṃ śītatoyasya kumbhaṃ puṣpopaśobhitam|
kāmadevāgrataḥ sthāpya pallavaiścopaśobhitam|| 661 ||

anarkābhyudite kāle snāpyā syāt tena vāriṇā|
dayitā dvijaśārdūla svayaṃ kāntena kāśyapa|| 662 ||

iti nīlamate madanatrayodaśī|

nīla uvāca |

caitramāsi site pakṣe pañcadaśyāṃ dvijottama|
yoddhuṃ yāti nikumbhastu piśācān vālukārṇave|| 663 ||

tasmāt teṣāṃ tu madhyāhne ekaikasya gṛhe gṛhe|
pūjā kāryā prayatnena yathāvat tannibodha me|| 664 ||

piśācaṃ mṛṇmayaṃ kṛtvā kākṣyaṃ ca dvijasattama|
gandhairmālyaistathā vastrairalaṃkāraiśca pūjayet|| 665 ||

bhakṣyaiśca lopikāpūpairmāṃsaiḥ pānaistathaiva ca|
āyudhairvividhākāraiśchattropānahayaṣṭibhiḥ|| 666 ||

 |
nṛbhiḥ śuṣkānnasampūrṇe sthāyibhakṣyāyute tathā|| 667 ||

kuddālapiṭake cobhe tathā tasya nivedayet|
geyam ānaddhavādyaṃ ca tantrīdvayaṃ ca vādayet|| 668 ||

madhyāhne taṃ tu sampūjya prāpte candrodaye punaḥ|
pūrvavat pūjayedvidvān svavittasyānurūpataḥ|| 669 ||

tathā kṛtasvastyayano brāhmaṇastu visarjayet|
visarjyamāne tasmiṃstu tantrīvādyaṃ ca vādayet|| 670 ||

tasyānuvrajanaṃ kāryaṃ dvitīye 'ahani kāśyapa|
āroḍhavyaṃ bhavecchailaṃ samīpastham iti sthitiḥ|| 671 ||

gṛham āgamya kartavyam utsavaṃ gītavāditaiḥ|
suhṛdbhiḥ saha bhoktavyaṃ bhojanaṃ tu viśeṣavat|| 672 ||

iti nīlamate piśācapūjanam|

nīla uvāca |

irā nāmāpsaraḥ pūrvaṃ prāptā śakreṇa kāśyapa|
viśvāvasusamprayuktā suropasthānavarjitā|| 673 ||

sthāvaratvam anuprāptā himavatyacalottame|
bahudhā sā vibhaktāṅgī nikumbhe nirgate bahiḥ|| 674 ||

yadā jātā irā devī irāpuṣpopaśobhitā|
irāvāṭaṃ tato gatvā nārīputragaṇānvitaḥ|| 675 ||

suvāsāḥ svanuliptāṅgaḥ sucittaḥ susamāhitaḥ|
irāsampūjanaṃ kuryāt puṣpairanyairvicakṣaṇaḥ|| 676 ||

nivedayecca vividhān bhakṣyabhojyān sadīpakān|
irāvāṭaṃ tato gatvā bhoktavyaṃ tu viśeṣavat|| 677 ||

irāpuṣpaistataḥ pūjyā dvijastrīmitrabāndhavāḥ|
raktasūtranibaddhāni irāpuṣpāṇi kārayet|| 678 ||

bibhṛyādātmanā tāni strīṣu dadyādviśeṣataḥ|
śrotavyaṃ gītavādyādi draṣṭavyaṃ nartanaṃ tathā|| 679 ||

irāpuṣpasamāyuktaṃ pānaṃ peyaṃ viśeṣavat|
irā deveṣu dātavyā tathā prīyanti devatāḥ|| 680 ||

irāsahasraṃ yo dadyāt keśavāya samāhitaḥ|
tasya tuṣyati deveśaḥ svargalokaṃ ca gacchati|| 681 ||

irayā pūjayedrudraṃ brahmāṇaṃ śaśinaṃ ravim|
śubhāṃ karīṣiṇīṃ durgāṃ sarvāstuṣyanti devatāḥ|| 682 ||

irā nāgeṣu dayitā dayitā me viśeṣataḥ|
irāvāṭe tu yaḥ pūjāṃ karoti mama kāśyapa|
irāpuṣpairbhṛśaṃ tena tuṣṭirme upajāyate|| 683 ||

iti nīlamate irāmañjarīpūjā|

nīla uvāca |

śukle vaiśākhamāsasya tṛtīyāyāṃ dvijottama|
yavānutpādayāmāsa kṛtaṃ ca kṛtavān yugam|| 684 ||

kāryaṃ tasyāṃ yavairhomaṃ yavān dadyāddvijātaye|
yavaiḥ sampūjayedviṣṇuṃ bhoktavyāśca tathā yavāḥ|| 685 ||

gaṅgāsampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ tasminnahani kāśyapa|
brahmalokāt tripathagāṃ pṛthivyām avatārayat|| 686 ||

japahomau tathā śrāddhaṃ tapaḥ snānādikaṃ ca yat|
akṣayaṃ sarvam uddiṣṭaṃ dānaṃ svalpam api dhruvam|| 687 ||

sindhutīre prayatnena dvitīyāyām upoṣitaiḥ|
tṛtīyāyāṃ tu kartavyaṃ manujaistu yathāvidhi|| 688 ||

iti nīlamate akṣayatṛtīyā|

nīla uvāca |

viṣṇurdevo jagannāthaḥ prāpte brahman kalau yuge|
aṣṭaviṃśatime bhāvī buddho nāma jagadguruḥ|| 689 ||

puṣyayukte niśānāthe vaiśākhe māsi kāśyapa|
tasmāt kālādathārabhya kāle bhāvinyataḥ param|| 690 ||

śukle sampūjanaṃ tasya yathā kāryaṃ tathā śṛṇu|
sarvauṣadhaiḥ sarvaratnaiḥ sarvagandhaistathaiva ca|| 691 ||

buddhārcāsnāpanaṃ kāryaṃ śākyoktairvacanaistathā|
sudhāsitāśca kartavyāḥ śākyāvāsāḥ prayatnataḥ|| 692 ||

kvaciccitrayutāḥ kāryāścaityā devagṛhāstathā|
utsavaṃ ca tathā kāryaṃ naṭanartakasaṃkulam|| 693 ||

śākyānāṃ pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ cīvarāhārapustakaiḥ|
sarvam etadbhavet kāryaṃ yāvat prāptā bhavenmaghā|| 694 ||

dinatrayaṃ ca kartavyaṃ naivedyaṃ vidhivaddvija|
puṣpavastrādipūjā ca dānaṃ dīnajanasya ca|| 695 ||

iti nīlamate buddhajanmāhaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

paurṇamāsīṃ tu samprāpya vaiśākhasya dvijottama|
madhuyuktaistilaiḥ kāryaṃ tadā brāhmaṇapūjanam|
tilaiḥ snānaṃ tilairhomaṃ tilaiḥ śrāddhaṃ tathaiva ca|| 696 ||

pātradānaṃ tu kartavyaṃ dīpadāhaṃ surālaye|
tilā deyāśca vipreṣu bhakṣaṇīyāstathā tilāḥ|| 697 ||

māghakṛṣṇe dvijaśreṣṭha ekādaśyām upoṣitiaḥ|
dvādaśyāṃ sakalaḥ kāryo vaiśākhokto mayā vidhiḥ|| 698 ||

vaiśākhyāṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ tu brāhmaṇān sapta pañca vā|
kṣaudrayuktaistilaiḥ kṛṣṇairarcayedatha vetaraiḥ|| 699 ||

prīyatāṃ dharmarājeti yadā manasi vartate|
yāvajjīvakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ tatkṣaṇādeva naśyati|| 700 ||

iti nīlamate vaiśākhī|

 |

nīla uvāca |

oṣadhīnāṃ tato rājā saṃpakvo bhavate yavaḥ|
yavānnena tadābhyarcya devatāḥ pitarastathā|| 701 ||

tato 'anuliptaḥ sragvī ca navavāsā yathāvidhi|
vādyabrāhmaṇaghoṣeṇa yavānnaṃ prāśayennaraḥ|| 702 ||

iti nīlamate yavāgrāyaṇam|

nīla uvāca |

jyaiṣṭhyāṃ tu samatītāyāṃ yā dvijendrāṣṭamī bhavet|
tasyāṃ vināyakaḥ pūjyaḥ sagaṇo modakotkaraiḥ|| 703 ||

bhakṣyairmālyaistathā gandhaiḥ kulmāṣeṇa ca bhūriṇā|
gītairvādyaiḥ sumadhurairbrāhmaṇānāṃ ca tarpaṇaiḥ|| 704 ||

sarvāsu vā pūjanīyastvaṣṭamīṣu vināyakaḥ|
kāryasiddhim avāpnoti sopavāsastu pūjayet|| 705 ||

iti nīlamate vināyakāṣṭamyaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

āṣāḍhamāsaṃ samprāpya svātiyogaṃ tathaiva ca|
vāyoḥ sampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ gandhairmālyairdvijottama|| 706 ||

bhūriṇā paramānnena saktunā vividhena ca|
sumanobhirvicitraiśca kusumaiśca tathāparaiḥ|| 707 ||

iti nīlamate svātiyogaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

āṣāḍhaśuklapakṣānte kartavyaṃ dinapañcakam|
devaprasvāpane brahmannutsavaṃ gītavāditaiḥ|| 708 ||

ekādaśīcaturdaśyordhanahotraṃ ca kārayet|
niśādvayaṃ tataḥ kāryaṃ rātrijāgaraṇaṃ tathā|
dvādaśyāṃ pañcadaśyāṃ ca dvijasātvatapūjanam|| 709 ||

trayodaśyāṃ ca kartavyaṃ prekṣādānaṃ yathāvidhi|
dhanaṃ ca śaktyā dātavyaṃ ye narā raṅgajīvinaḥ|| 710 ||

hiṃsātmakaistu kiṃ tasya yajñaiḥ kāryaṃ mahātmanaḥ|
prasvāpe ca prabodhe ca pūjito yena keśavaḥ|| 711 ||

iti nīlamate prasvāpanam|

nīla uvāca |

āṣāḍhānte vaiśvadevaṃ nakṣatraṃ prāpya paṇḍitaḥ|
viśvān sampūjayeddevān vāyūktavidhinā tadā|| 712 ||

iti nīlamate vaiśvadevapūjā|

nīla uvāca |

saktūn gorasasaṃmiśrān brāhmaṇebhyaḥ pradāpayet|
himaṃ ca śarkarāṃ caiva śākaṃ haritakaṃ tathā|| 713 ||

chattropānahamālyādi dakṣiṇāyanavāsare|
vāridhānyaśca sampūrṇāḥ śītatoyena mānada|| 714 ||

iti nīlamate dakṣiṇāyanam|

nīla uvāca |

āṣāḍhyāṃ samatītāyāṃ yadā syāddvija rohiṇī|
tadā tu kaśyapaḥ pūjyo deśasyāsya pravartakaḥ|| 715 ||

gandhamālyādinaivedyairbrāhmaṇānāṃ ca pūjayā|
tasminnahani rohiṇyaḥ pūjanīyāḥ savatsakāḥ|| 716 ||

iti nīlamate rohiṇīsaṃyogaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

tatastu śrāvaṇīṃ prāpya vitastāsindhusaṃgame|
snātvā sampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ devadevasya śārṅgiṇaḥ|| 717 ||

svastivācya dvijān paścāt krīḍitavyaṃ yathāsukham|
viśeṣavacca bhoktavyaṃ tatra vai dvijapuṃgava|| 718 ||

sāmadhvanaiśca śrotavyastasminnahanai kāśyapa|
krīḍitavyaṃ viśeṣeṇa kumārībhistathā jale|| 719 ||

śravaṇarkṣam anuprāpte candramāsyatha kāśyapa|
snātasya śravaṇe puṣṭiḥ sarvasmin sarvadā bhavet|| 720 ||

iti nīlamate śrāvaṇī|

nīla uvāca |

śrāvaṇyāṃ samatītāyāṃ yā syāt kṛṣṇāṣṭamī dvija|
tasyām utpadyate devo mānuṣye madhusūdanaḥ|| 721 ||

bhārāvatāraṇārthaṃ hi tasyāṃ vai dvijasattama|
aṣṭaviṃśe tu samprāpte dvāparānte tu naḥ śrutam|| 722 ||

tatrāhani tu kartavyaṃ tasmāt kālāt tadā param|
pūjanaṃ devadevasya devyāścaiva yathāvidhi|| 723 ||

devakī ca yaśodā ca tathā pūjye dvijottama|
gandhairmālyaistathā bhakṣyairyavagodhūmasambhavaiḥ|| 724 ||

sagorasairbhakṣyabhojyaiḥ phalaiśca vividhaistathā|
evaṃ sampūjanaṃ kṛtvā rātrau kuryānmahotsavam|| 725 ||

tathā jāgaraṇaṃ kāryaṃ navamyāṃ dvijasattama|
anarkābhyuditakāle striyaḥ kausumbhavāsasaḥ|| 726 ||

nadītīre śubhe ramye vivikte sarase 'api vā|
nayeyuḥ pratimāḥ sarvā gītavādyairmanoharaiḥ|| 727 ||

tasminnahani bhoktavyaṃ bhojanaṃ yavasambhavam|
yuktam ikṣuvikāraiśca maricaiśca ghṛtena ca|| 728 ||

iti nīlamate kṛṣṇajanmāhaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

tataḥ pañcadaśīṃ prāpya kṛṣṇāṃ pitryarkṣasaṃyutām|
pitṛṇāṃ tarpaṇaṃ kāryaṃ śrāddhaṃ kāryaṃ prayatnataḥ|| 729 ||

subhāṣito barhiṣado 'agniṣvāttāstathaiva ca|
kravyādaścopahūtāśca ājyapāśca sukālinaḥ|| 730 ||

sarve pitṛgaṇāḥ pūjyāḥ puṣpadhūpānnasampadā|
tilaiḥ kāryaḥ prayatnena śrāddhastasmin dvijottama|| 731 ||

iti nīlamate maghāmāvāsyā|

nīla uvāca |

prauṣṭhapadasya māsasya śuklapakṣe dine dine|
pūjanīyo mahendrastu śacī devī tathaiva ca|| 732 ||

paṭe kṛtastu sarvena yathāśaktyā dvijottama|
daivajñenoktavidhinā sa tu pūjyo mahīkṣitā|| 733 ||

brāhmaṇānāṃ tu kartavyaṃ pūjanaṃ goprajīvinā|
bhakṣyairannaiḥ phalairmūlai ratnairvastraiḥ sadhūpakaiḥ|| 734 ||

mahendraḥ sagaṇaḥ pūjyaḥ sāyudhaśca savāhanaḥ|
indrapakṣasya yā madhye śuklā brāhmaṇa pañcamī|| 735 ||

tasyāṃ me pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ gandhadhūpānnasampadā|
mālyavastropahāraiśca vahnibrāhmaṇatarpaṇaiḥ|| 736 ||

prekṣādānaiśca vividhairbhūmiśobhābhireva ca|
tasminnevāhni kartavyaṃ sthānanāgasya cāpyuta|| 737 ||

 |

iti nīlamate prauṣṭhapadī|

nīla uvāca |

kanyāmadhyam anuprāpte sahasrakiraṇe dvija|
rātryante sopavāsena pūjyo 'agastyo munirbhavet|| 738 ||

pūrṇakumbhaiḥ sakūṣmāṇḍairyavairdhānyairghṛtena ca|
jātīpadmotpalaiḥ śubhraiścandanena sitena ca|| 739 ||

dhenvā vṛṣeṇa vastraiśca ratnaiḥ sāgarasambhavaiḥ|
chattropānahadaṇḍaiśca pādukābhistathaiva ca|| 740 ||

bhūriṇā paramānnena phalamūlaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|
annaprakārairbhakṣyaiśca vahnibrāhmaṇabhojanaiḥ|| 741 ||

saṃvatsaraṃ tu tyāgena phalasyaikasya kāśyapa|
agastyapūjāṃ kṛtvaivaṃ daivajñaṃ pūjayet tataḥ|| 742 ||

tena saṃdarśitaṃ paśyet tadāgastyaṃ mahāmunim|
kāmānabhīṣṭān āpnoti dṛṣṭvāgastyamuniṃ naraḥ|| 743 ||

iti nīlamate agastyadarśanam|

nīla uvāca |

dhānye pakve site pakṣe dine daivajñacodite|
devān pitṛn samabhyarcya jalam agniṃ dvijāṃstathā|| 744 ||

dvijātipūjanaṃ kṛtvā daivajñasya ca pūjanam|
navavastraparīdhānaḥ svanuliptaḥ svalaṃkṛtaḥ|| 745 ||

sragvī pūrvamukhaḥ śuklo brahmaghoṣapuraḥsaraḥ|
śṛṇvan vāditraghoṣaṃ ca gītaśabdaṃ tathaiva ca|| 746 ||

maṇḍalaṃ rudhiraṃ kṛtvā bhūmau paṭṭe paṭe 'api vā|
madhye brahmāṇam ālikhya tathānantaṃ ca bhoginam|| 747 ||

tato likhyeta dikpālān svāṃ svāṃ diśam avasthitān|
teṣāṃ tu pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ dhūpamālyānulepanaiḥ|| 748 ||

vastrai ratnaiḥ phalairbhakṣyairvahnibrāhmaṇatarpaṇaiḥ|
guḍopetāstathā dhānāḥ sarvasasyasamudbhavāḥ|| 749 ||

brāhmaṇānāṃ pradātavyā bhṛtyabandhujanasya ca|
svayaṃ tu dhānā bhoktavyā rātrau na tu divā budhaiḥ|| 750 ||

divā dhānāsu vasati rātrau ca dadhisaktuṣu|
alakṣmīḥ kovidāreṣu kapittheṣu sadā sthitā|| 751 ||

iti nīlamate dhānyapākaḥ|

 |

iti nīlamate dhanadacaturthī|

nīla uvāca |

tām eva pañcamīṃ prāpya pūjyo devo jaleśvaraḥ|
pūjanīyā umā devī yathā tu dhanadastathā|| 752 ||

iti nīlamate varuṇapañcamī|

nīla uvāca |

tām eva ṣaṣṭīṃ samprāpya snāpanīyāḥ kumārakāḥ|
alaṃkṛtāstu kartavyāḥ prāpya tām eva saptamīm|| 753 ||

ātmapūjā naraiḥ kāryā strīṇāṃ bālajanasya ca|
tatastām aṣṭamīṃ prāpya krīḍitavyaṃ yathāsukham|| 754 ||

sindūrakardamāktāṅgairnṛtyavādyapuraḥsaram|
snātvā sampūjanīyā tu devī nāmnā aśokikā|| 755 ||

śayyāsanaṃ nivedyaṃ syāt sottaracchadanaṃ śubham|
puṣpānnadhūpanaṃ sarvaṃ bhoktavyaṃ guḍasaṃyutam|| 756 ||

umāyāḥ pūjanaṃ kāryaṃ tasyāṃ saubhāgyam īpsunā|
dhūpānnadīpamālyaiśca ārdrakena guḍena vā|| 757 ||

kusumbhalavaṇābhyāṃ vā kuṅkumāñjanakaṅkaṇaiḥ|
ārāmam atha gatvā tu toyavṛkṣopaśobhitam|
 || 758 ||

iti nīlamate aśokikāṣṭamī|

 |

iti godhūmanavamī|

nīla uvāca |

tatastoye tu vaitaste snātavyaṃ dinasaptakam|
daśamyādau dvijaśreṣṭha yatāhāreṇa sādhunā|| 759 ||

vitastājanmadivasāt tryahaṃ pūrvaṃ tryahaṃ param|
vitastājanmadivasaṃ taṃ ca brahmaṃstrayodaśīm|| 760 ||

pūjanīyā vitastā syāt tathā taṃ dinasaptakam|
gandhairmālyaiḥ sanaivedyairdīpadānaiḥ suśobhanaiḥ|| 761 ||

mālikābhirvicitrābhii raktasūtraiḥ sakaṅkaṇaiḥ|
phalaiśca vividhairbrahman vahnibrāhmaṇatarpaṇaiḥ|
kartavyaṃ syādviśeṣeṇa vitastāsindhusaṃgame|| 762 ||

vitastājanmadivasādyadūrdhvaṃ syāddinatrayam|
prekṣādānaṃ ca kartavyaṃ pūjanīyā naṭādayaḥ|| 763 ||

iti nīlamate vitastotsavam|

nīla uvāca |

vitastotsavamadhye tu śuklā yā dvādaśī dvija|
sopavāso hariṃ devaṃ pūjayeta vicakṣaṇaḥ|| 764 ||

eṣā dhruvā vinirdiṣṭā śeṣāḥ kāryā na vā dvija|
mahatī sā vinirdiṣṭā dvādaśī sarvadā śubhā|| 765 ||

dvādaśī budhasaṃyuktā mahatyapi ca kīrtitā|
tasyāṃ japyaṃ tathā snānaṃ dānaṃ śrāddhādikaṃ tathā|| 766 ||

proktavān dvādaśaguṇaṃ svayaṃ me madhusūdanaḥ|
budhaśravaṇasaṃyuktā dvija sā dvādaśī yadi|| 767 ||

atyantamahatī nāma tasyāṃ sarvam athākṣayam|
tasyāṃ snātvā naraḥ samyaṅnadīdvitayasaṃgame|| 768 ||

phalam āpnoti yat proktaṃ saṃnihatyāṃ ravigrahe|
upānahau tathā chattraṃ pūrṇakumbhaṃ tathaiva ca|| 769 ||

vastrayugmaṃ tathānnādyaṃ yaḥ prayacchati vai tadā|
śeṣāṇāṃ yadi cet kartā śvetadvīpe mahīyate|| 770 ||

iti nīlamate dvādaśyaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

saiva cecchravaṇopetā yadi cet syāt tu dvādaśī|
tasya syuricchato lokā yāvadindrāścaturdaśa|| 771 ||

tasminnahani saṃsthāpya vitastāsindhusaṃgamāt|
mṛttikāṃ snānakāle tu snātavyaṃ satataṃ tayā|
saṃgamasnānajaṃ puṇyaṃ tenāpnoti naraḥ sadā|| 772 ||

iti nīlamate mahādvādaśī|

nīla uvāca |

tatastvanantare pakṣe śrāddhaṃ kāryaṃ dine dine|
caturdaśīṃ varjayitvā śyāmākaistu viśeṣataḥ|| 773 ||

śastreṇa nihatā ye tu tebhyo dharmyā caturdaśī|
sarvaṃ pakṣaṃ bhavecchrāddham evam evāha keśavaḥ|| 774 ||

tribhāgahīnaṃ pakṣaṃ vā tribhāgam antyam eva vā|
vittaśaktyā tu kartavyaṃ trayodaśyāṃ sadaiva hi|| 775 ||

parapākaratiryo vai yo 'api caivādhano bhavet|
karmajīvī bhavedyo vai tenāpi dvijapuṃgava|| 776 ||

yathā kathaṃcicchrāddhaṃ tu kartavyaṃ syāt trayodaśīm|
atra gāthāḥ pitṛgītā nibodha gadato mama|| 777 ||

api naḥ sa kule jāyādyo no dadyāt trayodaśīm|
pāyasaṃ madhusaṃmiśraṃ varṣāsu ca maghāsu ca|| 778 ||

iti nīlamate śrāddhapakṣaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

śrāddhapakṣasya yā madhye caturthī dvijasattama|
dikpālapūjanaṃ kāryaṃ prauṣṭhapadyāṃ sadā budhaiḥ|| 779 ||

āyudhāni ca pūjyāni rātrau durgāgṛhe tathā|
snātvā prabhāte sampūjya sarvāṇyuktāni kāśyapa|| 780 ||

bhuktvā tu śāntiḥ kartavyā kiṃcicchiṣṭe divākare|
nīrājanākhyā vijñeyā śālihotravicakṣaṇaiḥ|| 781 ||

jyotiṣāṃ pāṭhakādyaiśca kalpeṣu ātharvaṇeṣu ca|
ataḥ paraṃ pūjanīyā janā ye raṅgajīvinaḥ|| 782 ||

iti nīlamate nīrājananavamī|

nīla uvāca |

athāśvayukcaturthyāṃ tu devīpūjā vidhīyate|
navamyuktavidhānena sarvopakaraṇādibhiḥ|| 783 ||

pūjyāśca subhagāstatra yāśca nāryaḥ pativratāḥ|
yāsāṃ jīvanti nāthāśca svasṛprabhṛtayaśca yāḥ|| 784 ||

yathaivāśvayuje māsi tathā māghe ca pūjayet|
yathā māghe tathā jyeṣṭhe caturthītritayaṃ tadā|| 785 ||

iti nīlamate caturthītritayam|

nīla uvāca |

āśvayuje śuklapakṣe svātinā saṃgataḥ śaśī|
yadā tadoccaiḥśravasaḥ pūjā kāryā prayatnataḥ|| 786 ||

pūjanīyāśca turagā yadi syānnavamī dvija|
śāntisvastyayanaṃ kāryaṃ tadā teṣāṃ dine dine|| 787 ||

dhānyaṃ bhallātakaṃ kuṣṭhaṃ vacāsiddhārthakāni ca|
pañcaraṅgeṇa sūtreṇa tathā badhnīta paṇḍitaḥ|| 788 ||

vāyavyairvāruṇaiḥ sauraiḥ śākrairmantraiśca vaiṣṇavaiḥ|
vaiśvadevaistathāgneyairhotavyo 'agnirdine dine|| 789 ||

turagā yantraṇīyāśca puruṣaiḥ śastrapāṇibhiḥ|
tāḍanaṃ vāhanaṃ caiva tadā teṣāṃ visarjayet|| 790 ||

iti nīlamate aśvadīkṣā|

nīla uvāca |

tataḥ śākram anuprāpte nakṣatraṃ tu niśākare|
kumudairāvaṇau padmaḥ puṣpadanto 'atha vāmanaḥ|| 791 ||

supratīko 'añjano nīlaḥ pūjanīyā gajottamāḥ|
namaskṛtya yathāśvoktaṃ vidhiṃ nāgeṣu kārayet|| 792 ||

iti nīlamate hastidīkṣā|

nīla uvāca |

tato 'aṣṭamyāṃ pūjanīyā bhadrakālī yathāvidhi|
upoṣitairarghadhūpairvastrairmālyādibhistathā|
dīpai ratnaistathā bhakṣyaiḥ phalairmūlaistathaiva ca|| 793 ||

āmiṣairvividhaiḥ śākairvahnibrāhmaṇatarpaṇaiḥ|
bilvapattreṇa ca tathā candanena ghṛtena ca|| 794 ||

pānakairvividhākāraiḥ sasyaiḥ śilpijanaistadā|
bhūśobhābhirnṛttagītai rātrijāgaraṇena ca|| 795 ||

durgāgṛhe pustakānāṃ pūjā kāryā tathā dvija|
svakānāṃ śilpibhāṇḍānāṃ kāryā śilpijanena ca|| 796 ||

vādyabhāṇḍāni cānyāni kavacāni tathaiva ca|
āyudhāni ca pūjyāni rātrau śilpibhiḥ svāni tu|| 797 ||

śṛṇvan vādyavaraṃ hṛṣṭo maṅgalālabdhapūrvakam|
prāśnīyāddadhisaṃyuktaṃ brāhmaṇenābhimantritam|| 798 ||

āśritopaśritān mitrān phalavedavidastathā|
pūjayeta ca kartavyaṃ strībhirgatvā phaladrumam|| 799 ||

devīṃ sampūjya puṣpādi- dīpadhūpānnasampadā|
dattvānnapiṇḍaṃ śyenāsya tena piṇḍo 'abhinanditaḥ|| 800 ||

 |
suhṛtsambandhiviprāṇāṃ tathāśritajanasya ca|| 801 ||

dātavyaṃ bhojanaṃ vipra vasante 'atha śaradyapi|
aṣṭamyāṃ vā caturthyāṃ vā caturdaśyāṃ tathaiva ca|| 802 ||

navamyām atha dātavyaṃ śukle pakṣe 'atha vetare|
yathā pūjā kṛtā devyāstathā bhoktavyam agrataḥ|| 803 ||

iti nīlamate kālikāpūjā|

nīla uvāca |

evam eva pradātavyaṃ gṛhadevyā vicakṣaṇaiḥ|
brahman saṃvatsarasyādau śyenapiṇḍavivarjitam|
tacca deyaṃ gṛhe vipra na tu vṛkṣe kathaṃcana|| 804 ||

iti nīlamate gṛhadevīpūjā|

nīla uvāca |

yadā pakvā bhaveddrākṣā vāṭaṃ caiva suśobhanam|
gantavyaṃ strīsahāyena bhṛtyamitrānvitena ca|| 805 ||

anuliptena snātena sragvinā ca suvāsasā|
 || 806 ||

drākṣāvāṭe ca sampūjyā śyāmā devī dvijottama|
puṣpadhūpānnabhakṣyādyairbrāhmaṇānāṃ ca tarpaṇaiḥ|| 807 ||

kulmāṣeṇa prabhūtena ghṛtena madhunā tathā|
brāhmaṇānāṃ ca dātavyā drākṣā prathamato dvija|| 808 ||

tatastu paścādbhoktavyaṃ mṛdvīkabahusaṃyutam|
kartavyam utsavaṃ cānu gītanṛttasamākulam|| 809 ||

iti nīlamate śyāmādevīpūjā|

nīla uvāca |

pratimāsaṃ tu puṣyeṇa rājñaḥ snānaṃ vidhīyate|
jyotiṣoktavidhānena sarvakāmaphalapradam|| 810 ||

janmarkṣaṃ ca gate candre sarveṇaivaṃ tathā dvija|
candrasya pūjā kartavyā nakṣatragrahayostathā|| 811 ||

nakṣatradevatā yāśca puṣpadhūpānnasampadā|
viprāgnipūjanaṃ kāryam utsavaṃ gītavāditaiḥ|| 812 ||

saṃvatsarasyātha kāryo lakṣahomo mahīkṣitā|
koṭihomastathā kārya eka eva dvijottama|
tayorvidhānaṃ vijñeyaṃ kalpeṣu ātharvaṇeṣu ca|| 813 ||

nityānyetāni karmāṇi nimitteṣvaparāṇi ca|
saṃmantrya saha kāryāni sāṃvatsarapurohitaiḥ|| 814 ||

iti nīlamate rājakarmāṇi|

nīla uvāca |

abhiṣekāhni kartavyaṃ pratisaṃvatsaraṃ nṛpaiḥ|
abhiṣekaṃ dvijaśreṣṭha yathā pūrvaṃ tathā sadā|| 815 ||

purodhāḥ sopavāsastu juhuyājjātavedasi|
gaṇaṃ caiva pratirathaṃ śabdavarṣagaṇāvubhau|| 816 ||

āyuṣyam abhayaṃ caiva tathā svastyayanaṃ param|
vaiṣṇavāni ca mantrāṇi śākrāṇi ca yathāvidhi|| 817 ||

sāvitrabrāhmaraudrāṇi vāruṇāni tathaiva ca|
nagaraṃ ca tathā kāryaṃ patākādhvajasaṃkulam|| 818 ||

nīrajaskaṃ tathā kāryaṃ rājamārgaṃ jalaiḥ śubhaiḥ|
pauraiḥ snātaiḥ suvastraiśca bhāvyaṃ maṅgalapāṇibhiḥ|| 819 ||

gantavyaṃ vāramukhyābhistathā rājaniveśanam|
pauramukhyaistathā bāhyairgaṇamukhyaistathaiva ca|| 820 ||

śobhanīyaṃ ca nagaraṃ mārgāśca naṭanartakaiḥ|
rājā snātaḥ punaḥ snāpyaḥ pañcagavyena dhārmikaḥ|| 821 ||

mṛttāmraraupyasauvarṇaiḥ snāpanīyastathā ghaṭaiḥ|
toyasya payaso dadhnaḥ sarpiṣaśca tathāyutaiḥ|| 822 ||

śūdraviṭkṣatraviprāṇāṃ gaṇamukhyairyathādiśam|
paścāddakṣiṇataḥ prāk ca uttareṇa yathākramam|| 823 ||

snānakāle ca kartavyaṃ mahat kalakalaṃ tathā|
vāditraśaṅkhapuṇyāhaṃ sūtavandijanaiḥ saha|| 824 ||

sāmantaistu tathā bhāvyaṃśchattracāmarapāṇibhiḥ|
rājā snātaḥ punaḥ snāpyo mṛttikābhiryathākramam|| 825 ||

parvatordhvamṛdā rājñaḥ śiraḥ saṃśodhayet tadā|
śodhyau karṇau ca valmīkācchakrasthānācca kandharā|| 826 ||

rājaveśmagṛhadvārāddhṛdayaṃ tasya śodhayet|
devālayamṛdā pṛṣṭhaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ tu tathā bhujam|| 827 ||

gajadantoddhṛtamṛdā vṛṣaśṛṅgamṛdāparam|
veśyādvārāt kaṭī cāsya ūrū kamalinīmṛdā|| 828 ||

gosthānācca tathā jaṅghe pādau cobhau saromṛdā|
pauraiḥ snātaiḥ suvastraiśca bhāvyaṃ maṅgalapāṇibhiḥ|| 829 ||

mṛdbhiḥ snātastataḥ snāpyo rājā sarvauṣadhaiḥ śubhaiḥ|
sarvagandhaiḥ sarvaratnaiḥ sarvabījaistataḥ param|| 830 ||

sarvapuṣpaiḥ sarvaphalairdūrvāgorocanāṅkuraiḥ|
tato bhadrāsanagataṃ tīrthatoyaiḥ śubhāmbaram|| 831 ||

yathāśakti samānītaiḥ puraskṛtya purodhasam|
nṛpatistvabhiṣektavyo daivajñavacanānnaraiḥ|| 832 ||

brāhmaṇaiḥ kṣatriyairvaiśyaiḥ śūdramukhyaistathaiva ca|
mukhyābhirvāramukhyābhirvaṇigbhiśca yathocitam|| 833 ||

tataḥ snāto 'anuliptāṅgaḥ kṛtadaivajñapūjanaḥ|
ābaddhamukuṭaḥ sragvī baddhapaṭṭo vibhūṣitaḥ|| 834 ||

maṅgalālabhanaṃ kṛtvā dattvā pūrṇahutiṃ tataḥ|
pūjayeta dhanaughena daivajñaṃ sapurodhasam|| 835 ||

yathāśakti dvijāṃścānyānabhayaṃ caiva ghoṣayet|
āghātasthānagān sarvān visṛjeta yathā paśūn|| 836 ||

mokṣayedbandhanāt sarvānṛte lokasya kaṇṭakān|
vyāghracarmottare ramye tathā siṃhāsane śubhe|| 837 ||

upaveśyo bhavedrājā svayaṃ gṛhapurodhasā|
paśyeran prakṛtīḥ sarvāḥ svayaṃ maṅgalapāṇayaḥ|| 838 ||

chattrāyudhādyaṃ sampūjya gajasaṃghāṃsturaṃgamān|
āruhyālaṃkṛtaṃ nāgaṃ visṛjeddhanasaṃcayān|| 839 ||

prakramya nagaraṃ sarvaṃ praviśya ca tathā gṛham|
sāmantapauramukhyāṃśca dhanenārcya visarjayet|| 840 ||

iti nīlamate vatsarābhiṣekaḥ|

nīla uvāca |

nityaṃ rājñā samutthāya pūjanīyā varadvijāḥ|
vahnisampūjanaṃ kāryaṃ draṣṭavyaṃ vadanaṃ ghṛte|| 841 ||

śrotavyaṃ tithinakṣatraṃ kartavyaṃ vaidyabhāṣitam|
sabhāgatena draṣṭavyo vyavahārastathā samaḥ|| 842 ||

vimānanā na kartavyā kasyacicca kadācana|
svabhedo rakṣitavyaśca nityaṃ brāhmaṇapuṃgava|| 843 ||

durgatvādasya deśasya paracakrabhayaṃ vinā|
svabhedeneha naśyanti baddhamūlā narādhipāḥ|| 844 ||

nityaṃ saṃnihitā devāḥ kaśmīrāmaṇḍale dvija|
teṣāṃ bhaktiḥ sadā kāryā nāgānāṃ brāhmaṇeṣu ca|| 845 ||

pūjyāḥ piśācāśca tathā balipūrveṇa karmaṇā|
deśānukāraḥ kartavyo janaḥ kāryaḥ svadhiṣṭhitaḥ|| 846 ||

āgataśca janaḥ sarvaḥ pūjanīyo digantarāt|
daṇḍo 'aparādhapratimaḥ kāryaḥ sarvasya kāśyapa|| 847 ||

nogradaṇḍo bhavedrājā kṣameta na ca kasyacit|
kārayeta tathā rājyaṃ rājaśāstropadeśataḥ|| 848 ||

iti nīlamate rājadharmāḥ|

nīla uvāca |

nikumbhe nirgate brahmaṃstathā caivāpyanāgate|
ṣaṇmāsamadhye kartavyā yātrā devagṛhe nṛpaiḥ|| 849 ||

saṃvatsare sakṛt kāryaṃ devatānāṃ gṛhe gṛhe|
yātrotsavaṃ yathā brahmaṃstathā me gadataḥ śṛṇu|| 850 ||

vināyakagṛhe yātrā caturthyāṃ sapraśasyate|
ṣaṣṭyāṃ kumārasya gṛhe saptamyāṃ savitustathā|| 851 ||

durgāgṛhe navamyāṃ ca pañcamyāṃ śrīgṛhe tathā|
aṣṭamyāṃ vā caturthyāṃ vā mahādevaniveśane|| 852 ||

śakraveśmani cāṣṭamyāṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ kalābhṛtaḥ|
dhanadasya caturthyāṃ tu pañcamyāṃ varuṇasya tu|
caturdaśyāṃ caturthyāṃ vā kāryaṃ yamaniveśmani|| 853 ||

pañcamī dvādaśī caiva paurṇamāsī tathaiva ca|
sarveṣām eva nāgānāṃ yātrākarmaṇi pūjitaḥ|| 854 ||

śuklapañcadaśī śastā sarvadeveṣu kāśyapa|
sarvāsu kāryaṃ tithiṣu tathātithiniveśane|| 855 ||

yātrāṃ tu kartukāmena pūrvam eva dine śubhe|
vināyakapateḥ pūjā kartavyā modakotkaraiḥ|| 856 ||

dvitīye 'ahani kartavyā grahaśāntistataḥ param|
tṛtīye 'ahani kartavyaṃ gandharvāṇāṃ ca pūjanam|| 857 ||

caturthe 'ahani kartavyaṃ piśācānāṃ ca pūjanam|
pañcame 'ahani kartavyaṃ sthānanāgasya pūjanam|| 858 ||

ṣaṣṭe 'ahani ca kartavyaṃ brāhmaṇānāṃ ca pūjanam|
saptame 'ahani kartavyaṃ dīnānāthajanasya ca|| 859 ||

evaṃ tu pūjanaṃ kṛtvā yathāvaddinasaptakam|
tatastu snāpanaṃ kāryaṃ vidhinā yena tacchṛṇu|| 860 ||

sudhāvadātaṃ kartavyaṃ citritaṃ devatāgṛham|
evaṃ tu pūjanaṃ kṛtvā kusumotkarabhūṣitam|| 861 ||

tato 'arcāsnāpanaṃ kāryaṃ kalpaśākhāvidhānataḥ|
puṇyahagītaghoṣeṇa subhagānartitena ca|| 862 ||

tato 'arcāsnāpanasyārthaṃ paurairnṛttapuraḥsaram|
ānītavyaṃ ca mahatā vibhavenodakaṃ śubham|| 863 ||

tīrthāt saṃnihitādrājan gajapṛṣṭhādhirohitam|
aśvayāne 'atha goyāne narayāne tathā punaḥ|| 864 ||

pratimāṃ tena samprāpya snāpayeta yathāvidhi|
rājābhiṣekakathitaṃ kāryaṃ ca nagare vidhim|| 865 ||

prāpte 'atha yātrādivase kūṭāgāraṃ tu kārayet|
vastrairmālyaistathā ratnaiḥ patākābhiralaṃkṛtam|| 866 ||

āropya pratimāṃ tatra tatsarūpāṃ tathāparām|
kūṭāgāraśca voḍhavyasturagairgobhireva ca|| 867 ||

balibhiḥ puruṣairvāpi mālyavastrādyalaṃkṛtaiḥ|
nṛpeṇa so 'anugantavyaḥ sabalenātha pṛṣṭhataḥ|| 868 ||

pradhānenānugantavyo nṛpahīne tathā pure|
djūpapūjā pradātavyā sthāne sthāne tathāparaiḥ|| 869 ||

kṣveditotkṛṣṭaśabdaiśca jayavādyasvanaistathā|
pathā samena nagaraṃ bhrāmyet kūṭagṛhaṃ śubham|| 870 ||

tathā praveśya pratimāṃ devaveśmani kāśyapa|
mahāntam utsavaṃ kāryaṃ gītanṛttasamākulam|| 871 ||

dvitīye 'ahani dātavyā prekṣā raṅgopajīvinām|
teṣāṃ śaktyā dhanaṃ deyaṃ mallādīnāṃ dvijottama|| 872 ||

prekṣākāle ca kartavyaṃ prekṣakāṇāṃ ca pūjanam|
manuṣyāṇāṃ dvijaśreṣṭha tāmbūlakusumādibhiḥ|| 873 ||

odanaṃ vikiredbhaktyā sapuṣpaphalasaṃyutam|
bhūtānāṃ khalvadṛśyānāṃ prekṣakāṇāṃ dvijottama|| 874 ||

iti nīlamate yātrotsavam|

nīla uvāca |

ityuktavān sa nāgendro brāhmaṇaṃ taṃ yaśasvinam|
etat prayatnāt kartavyam ārogyāyurdhanepsubhiḥ|| 875 ||

vittaśaktyā kariṣyanti ye janā nīlabhāṣitam|
teṣām ārogyam āyuśca dhanaṃ ca bhavitā bahu|| 876 ||

dehabhede gamiṣyanti devalokaṃ na saṃśayaḥ|
putrapautrāstathaiteṣāṃ svargāyurdhanabhāginaḥ|| 877 ||

bhaviṣyanti āyuṣopetā nātra kāryā vicāraṇā|
tavāpi viditaṃ rājan sakalaṃ nīlabhāṣitam|| 878 ||

loke deśe 'adhikam ataḥ śrutvā kuru yathāsukham|
kāladoṣasamucchinnaṃ yat kiṃcinnīlabhāṣitam|| 879 ||

tat sarvaṃ kuru rājendra mama vākyena mānada|
nīlavākyaṃ na kriyate bhavatīhodakaplavaḥ|| 880 ||

ativṛṣṭiranāvṛṣṭirdurbhikṣaṃ maraṇaṃ tathā|
akāle rājamaraṇaṃ rājadaṇḍaśca dāruṇaḥ|| 881 ||

himasyaiva prapatanaṃ bhūri caivopajāyate|
tasmācchrāvyaṃ tu lokānāṃ bahuśo nīlabhāṣitam|| 882 ||

tatkartāro bhaviṣyanti paśudhānyadhanairyutāḥ|
tvaṃ cāpi vijayī śaśvat sarvatraiva bhaviṣyasi|| 883 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

evam ukto 'api gonando bṛhadaśvena bhūmipaḥ|
prāvartayat samucchinnān ācārān kāladoṣataḥ|| 884 ||

tasmāt sa balabhadreṇa mathurāyāṃ nipātitaḥ|
nīloktaṃ vacanaṃ rājā kaśmīraḥ sakalaṃ yadi|| 885 ||

karotyakāle maraṇaṃ naiva tasyopajāyate|
tasmin deśe tathātaṅko naiva kaścidbhaviṣyati|| 886 ||

iti nīlamate ācāramāhātmyam|

janamejaya uvāca |

kāśmīrakastu gonando bṛhadaśvena bhāṣitam|
śrutvā svakīyam ācāraṃ kim apṛcchadataḥ param|| 887 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

kāśmīrakastu gonando bṛhadaśvena bhāṣitam|
śrutvovāca muniśreṣṭhaṃ bṛhadaśvaṃ narādhipaḥ|| 888 ||

gonanda uvāca |

prādhānyena tu ye nāgāḥ kaśmīrāyāṃ kṛtālayāḥ|
nāmatastu samācakṣva śrotum icchāmi tānaham|| 889 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

nāgānām adhipo nīlo vāsukiścāpi takṣakaḥ|
kambalāśvatarau nāgau karkoṭakadhanaṃjayau|| 890 ||

ailapatro hyanantaśca nāgau nandopanandakau|
kulikaḥ śvetaśaṅkhau ca pālāśaḥ kheḍimo baḍiḥ|| 891 ||

lelihānaḥ śaṅkhapālo nāgau candananandanau|
nāgau nīlamahānīlau nāgau vāṭikaṣaṇḍikau|| 892 ||

dvau padmau dvau mahāpadmau dvau kālau dvau ca kacchapau|
dvau samudrau samudrāṇau dvau gajau dvau ca takṣakau|| 893 ||

hastikarṇāvubhau nāgau dvau hastī vāmanāvubhau|
mahiṣau dvau varāhau dvau kupanau dvau ca pannagau|| 894 ||

pānīyaścāpyanīkaśca kanakākṣaḥ kaliṅgakaḥ|
arjunaḥ puṇḍarīkaśca dhanado naḍakūbaraḥ|| 895 ||

khedaḥ śapālaḥ khīreśo lāhuro ledirastathā|
kheḍaśca pharathāḍaśca jayantastausamastathā|| 896 ||

sūdanau dvau supārśvaśca sunāsaḥ pañcahastakaḥ|
pradyumnaścāndhakaḥ śambhuḥ sālvo mūleśvaro ghṛṣaḥ|| 897 ||

ugholasāhunīmadhyau nāgau gandhilapicchalau|
sudhādo mūṣikādaśca piśitādo ghaṭodaraḥ|| 898 ||

nārāyaṇo niruddhaśca vāsudevo jalaṃdhamaḥ|
pātraśca mānasaścaiva tathaivottaramānasaḥ|| 899 ||

amānasaḥ kapālī ca nāgaḥ saṃkarṣaṇastathā|
śatadhāraḥ khilecāro rohiṇyākhyo 'atha śaktitaḥ|| 900 ||

ākhuphālau halāhaśca nāgaḥ kānasarastathā|
suśravo devapālaśca nāgendro 'atha balāhakaḥ|| 901 ||

candrasūryāvubhau nāgau śuciśuklau vidūrathaḥ|
phelādaḥ sukumāraśca khiḍīvo vijayo jayaḥ|| 902 ||

ūrūco gophaṇo vāyuḥ śukro vaiśravaṇo 'apamaḥ|
maṇḍūkanāso gāndhāro nāgaḥ śūrpārakadhvaniḥ|| 903 ||

samalo lolubho babhrurbindurbindusaro naḍaḥ|
tittirirhastibhadraśca nāgo grahapatistathā|| 904 ||

aparājitaḥ paṇḍito gopatirdurjayo 'aṣṭakaḥ|
nāgo himasaraścaiva nāgaḥ phalasaraḥ paraḥ|| 905 ||

tathā ca nāgo 'astyasaro nāgo nīlasaro vihā|
aśīlākṣo 'akṣipālaśca prahlādo yamakastathā|| 906 ||

aniṣṭaḥ sumukho vedaḥ khaṅgapuccho vibhīṣaṇaḥ|
mauhūrtikaḥ priyasvāmī kumāraścandano 'aparaḥ|| 907 ||

kapālaḥ śaraṇaḥ kheḍo nāgaśca pūraṇastathā|
kadambaścāpado vālī vibhūtiḥ kālakuñjaraḥ|| 908 ||

ḍavaścakradharaḥ śvabhro bhavo dehārako guḍaḥ|
andhaḥ paṅgustathā kuṣṭhī kāṇo badhiravaṇṭhakau|| 909 ||

anāgapādaḥ kitavaḥ sūkaraḥ prasavotkaṭau|
sādhīyaḥ śatapādaśca yogaḥ śatamukho druhaḥ|| 910 ||

atinidro 'atibahubhugbindunādaḥ śirojaḍaḥ|
kāmalākṣo viśālākṣaḥ suvarṇākṣo bhayānakaḥ|| 911 ||

bhuvīro dharmalāṭavo daityarājaḥ ṣaḍaṅgulaḥ|
gandharvo dhṛtarāṣṭraśca kusumaḥ kuharaḥ kuhaḥ|| 912 ||

mahākṣaśca vaṭūsaśca kaṭūso devadānavau|
nakṣatro maṣakaḥ pīto gautamaḥ suśubho jihā|| 913 ||

svargaḥ śiśiravāsī ca śrīvāsaḥ śrīdharaḥ khagaḥ|
lāṅgalī balabhadraśca surūpaḥ pañcahastakaḥ|| 914 ||

kāmarūpo darīkarṇaḥ saptaśīrṣo bahūdaraḥ|
sunetro bahunetraśca hanūmānaṅgado haraḥ|| 915 ||

haṭhakaḥ pāṭalaḥ pātho malo vimalako maṭaḥ|
nāgaḥ śatamukhaścaiva citrāśvo dadhivāhanaḥ|| 916 ||

suśīmaḥ kāliyaḥ kālaḥ pāṭanaḥ khadirastathā|
atriśca śavalaścaiva varṇako lalanastathā|| 917 ||

heliyāro hemiyāso balīnaḥ keluko nimiḥ|
cāṭako lelihānaśca pañcāsyaḥ piṅgalodaraḥ|| 918 ||

kṛtaṃ tretā dvāparaśca samaḥ saṃvatsarastathā|
khalvāṭo bahuromā ca kāpotiḥ puṣpasāhvayiḥ|| 919 ||

rāṣṭreśvaraḥ śinīraśca śatānando 'atikopanaḥ|
ānando 'atha jayānandastriśīrṣo jaṭilastathā|| 920 ||

gandhasomastathā gārgya iniṭirminiṭistathā|
airāvataḥ sakauravyo māṣādaḥ kumudaprabhaḥ|| 921 ||

havotsavaḥ śaṭhaḥ śāṇyaḥ śatrughno rāmalakṣamaṇau|
mahādevaḥ kāmapālo gośirāḥ sayudhiṣṭhiraḥ|| 922 ||

ḍāṅgakūyo viśākhaśca samo rovā mahodaraḥ|
makaro makarākṣaśca naḍvalo balavāñchikhī|| 923 ||

candapāṭanakaḥ kākaḥ kebuko brāhmaṇapriyaḥ|
karavīro jarasaṃdho niśācaradivācarau|| 924 ||

ulliñjaliśca vatsaśca māṭharo vaṭharo viṭaḥ|
hovaraḥ karavālaśca tapano ghāśirāstathā|| 925 ||

karkaraḥ karavāṭaśca varaghoṣaḥ sumaṅgalaḥ|
gullakaḥ śambaraḥ śāmī payo mahānihāgajaḥ|| 926 ||

karahāṭaḥ kusūrātro dhaumyo nāgo 'atha gālavaḥ|
ukholaśca śikholaśca vahnirūpo hiraṇmayaḥ|| 927 ||

satyākulaḥ kulūṣaśca kṛpāṇaḥ kūṭako hariḥ|
kimūdhaḥ śalabhaścaiva kiṃśukaḥ priyasārakaḥ|| 928 ||

mālākulo 'abhraśikharo vasiṣṭhaḥ savanāmukhaḥ|
nāgau rājamahārājau subhadrabhadravāliśau|| 929 ||

vīrabrahmāśanau nāgau nāgau sārasapukkasau|
ḍakkakaśca tathā cakko ghoṣo vaṃśanagastathā|| 930 ||

vidyādharaśca yakṣaśca virasaḥ sasyavardhanaḥ|
bhadrāśvo gajanetraśca kaṇāraḥ kumudastathā|| 931 ||

ānakaḥ kānavaḥ śambaḥ śaṇḍamarkau giripriyaḥ|
ugrāyudho 'abhimanyuśca amaraścāmṛtāśanaḥ|| 932 ||

ajakarṇo 'atha gonāsaḥ sṛgālaḥ kālakānanaḥ|
brāhmaṇaḥ kṣatriyo vaiśyaḥ śūdro dīpto vihaṃgamaḥ|| 933 ||

śaṅkhākṣaḥ kamalākṣaśca maṇināgo baheṭakaḥ|
jayantaḥ kūpano viśvaḥ śākhāmukhasuvarcalau|| 934 ||

guhaḥ sumālī mālī ca mālyavān ānṛtaḥ paraḥ|
kṣāttro masmanako bhīṣmaḥ kāśmīramadhuvāliśau|| 935 ||

bhīmākṣo bhīmanādaśca nāgau hāluṣakāluṣau|
mahendrendrasudhāmānaḥ śāliyo māliyastathā|| 936 ||

sahasradhāro dyutimān vibhūtiḥ kavaḍāśvarau|
śavalo bahurūpaśca bhadrāśvaścottarīyakaḥ|| 937 ||

maṇikaṇṭhaḥ kalolaśca śūravālo 'atha nūpuraḥ|
kuśakuṇḍo 'atulyasaśca aṭaḥ śubhro vitāraṇaḥ|| 938 ||

arabindaḥ sakalhāro bindumān dramiṭo vaṭaḥ|
sāgarau dvau tathā gāṅgau vaitasto yāmunāvubhau|| 939 ||

citropacitrau surabhirbhūtalāmbaracāriṇau|
upacitraḥ kaṅkataśca nāgau nāradaparvatau|| 940 ||

viśvāvasuḥ pārijāto gallullullo jalullusaḥ|
nāgaśca mākṣikasvāmī bhūrjilaścikurastathā|| 941 ||

akadhro bahukaṣaśca keśapiṅgaladhūsarau|
lambakarṇo gaṇḍulaśca nāgaḥ śrīmāḍhakastathā|| 942 ||

āvartakṛccandrasāro nāgaḥ karhasurastathā|
lambako 'atha caturvedaḥ puṣkaratritayaṃ tathā|| 943 ||

ākṣoṭanāgaḥ ṭaṅkaśca śyeno vaṭṭilakācarau|
kṣīrakumbho nikumbhaśca vikumbhaḥ samarapriyaḥ|| 944 ||

palighāno vighānaśca caṇḍo bhogī jarānvitaḥ|
bhogo bhārgavato raudro rudro bhojakadehilau|| 945 ||

rohiṇo 'atha bharadvājo dadhinakraḥ pratardanaḥ|
nāgau jānavarevau dvau śatrumitrau sakardamau|| 946 ||

paṅkaśca kardamo rambho bahubhogo bahūdaraḥ|
matsyo bhīto bahūtsaśca karaḍirvinatāpriyaḥ|| 947 ||

tāmrākaro 'atha rajato vanamālī sabhāvakaḥ|
nāgo jyotiṣiko vaidyo dhaurasāro janārdanaḥ|| 948 ||

nyagrodho ḍumbaro 'aśvattho balipuṣpo balipriyaḥ|
aṅgārakaḥ śanaiścārī nāgaḥ kuñjarako budhaḥ|| 949 ||

kaligṛtsau kuṭilako nāgau rāhubṛhaspatī|
caurakastaskaraḥ ketuḥ sūtapauro gavāvubhau|| 950 ||

ajakarṇo 'aśvakarṇaśca vidyunmālī darīmukhaḥ|
orāṇo 'arocano hāsī nartano gāyanastathā|| 951 ||

kambhāṭaśca subhāṭaśca bahuputro niśācaraḥ|
mayūraḥ kokilastrātā malayo yavanapriyaḥ|| 952 ||

koṭapālo mahīpālo gopālaḥ pāṭalaḥ śuciḥ|
rājādhirājo vinataḥ svargo vimalako maṇiḥ|| 953 ||

cakrahasto gadāhastaḥ śūlī pāśī magastathā|
nāgaścitrakaro vatso vatso bakapatistathā|| 954 ||

śītārto yavamālī ca rāvaṇo rākṣasākṛtiḥ|
yajvādātā tathā hotā bhoktā bhogapatistathā|| 955 ||

ete prādhānyato rājan nāgeśāḥ kīrtitā mayā|
eteṣāṃ yat parīvāraṃ putrapautrādikaṃ ca yat|| 956 ||

na tacchakyaṃ mayā rājan vaktuṃ varṣaśatairapi|
sarveṣām eva nāgānāṃ puṇyāni bhavanānyuta|| 957 ||

sarve varapradā nāgāḥ sarve nīlam anuvratāḥ|
sarve 'atidayitā rājan vāsukeḥ sumahātmanaḥ|| 958 ||

dikpālānatha te vakṣye kaśmīrāyāṃ nibodha tān|
pūrvasyāṃ diśi rājendra nāgo bindusaraḥ smṛtaḥ|| 959 ||

dakṣiṇena tathā nāgo nāmnā śrīmāḍhakaḥ smṛtaḥ|
uttareṇa tathā rājan proktastūttaramānasaḥ|| 960 ||

paścimena ca rājendra nāmnailāpatra ucyate|
evaṃ nāgasahasrāṇi prayutānyarbudāni ca|| 961 ||

tārkṣyajaṃ tu bhayaṃ tyaktvā vasantīha gatavyathāḥ|
ye coktāstu mayā nāgāsteṣāṃ madhyāt ṣaḍaṅgulaḥ|| 962 ||

eko vivāsito rājñā nīlenāmitatejasā|
sthānaṃ ṣāḍaṅgulaṃ rājan mahāpadmasya dhīmataḥ|| 963 ||

tṛtīyasya tu yaddattaṃ tatra jātaṃ jalāśayam|
yojanāyāmavistāraṃ samudram iva cāparam|| 964 ||

chadmanāpahṛtaṃ yacca mahāpadmena pārthiva|
nīlasyānumate pūrvaṃ viṣvagaśvānnarādhipāt|| 965 ||

iti nīlamate nāgāyatanakīrtanam|

gonanda uvāca |

ṣaḍaṅgulaḥ kathaṃ rājñā nīlenāsmādvivāsitaḥ|
kathaṃ ca viṣvagaśvasya sthānaṃ jātaṃ jalāśayam|
etat sarvaṃ samācakṣva kuśalo hyasi dhārmika|| 966 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

pūrvam eva satīdeśe mahāpadmaṃ bhujaṃgamam|
viditvaiva kṛtasthānaṃ vainateyo hyabādhata|| 967 ||

tasya putrāṃstathā sarvān āśritopāśritān khagaḥ|
ākramya bhakṣayāmāsa śataśo 'atha sahasraśaḥ|| 968 ||

svajane bhakṣyamāne 'atha mahāpadmo bhujaṃgamaḥ|
ājagāma mahānāgaṃ nīlaṃ śaraṇam añjasā|| 969 ||

sthānaṃ ca prārthayāmāsa kaśmīrāyāṃ janeśvara|
tam uvāca tadā nīlo mahāpadmaṃ bhujaṃgamam|| 970 ||

nāgā bhujaṃgaśārdūla sarve teha kṛtālayāḥ|
nāsti sthānaṃ tu vasasi yatra nāgendrasattama|| 971 ||

na taṃ deśaṃ prapaśyāmi sūkṣmam apyamaraprabha|
nāgairnādhiṣṭhitaṃ yat tu sarvam eva bhujaṃgama|| 972 ||

parīvāreṇa bahunā tathā tvaṃ parivāritaḥ|
kiṃ tvasti sāmprataṃ śūnyaṃ sthānaṃ ṣāḍaṅgulaṃ śubham|| 973 ||

mayā nirvāsito nāgastasmāddeśāt ṣaḍaṅgulaḥ|
mānuṣāṇāṃ sa dārāṇi haratyaharahaḥ purā|| 974 ||

ito nirvāsya dattaṃ ca sthānaṃ tasya tato mayā|
uśīrake giriśreṣṭhe dārveṣu bhujagottama|| 975 ||

mayāpi sthānapālo 'asau yuktyā tatra niveśitaḥ|
gṛhītaścānurāgeṇa janaḥ kāśmīrako mayā|| 976 ||

ṣaḍaṅgulaśca nāgendraḥ same pathi niveśitaḥ|
pūjyamāno janaistatra sukham āste ṣaḍaṅgulaḥ|| 977 ||

madvākyāccābhayaṃ dattaṃ tatrāsya hariṇā svayam|
sthāne ṣāḍaṅgule ramye viṣvagaśvasya bhūpateḥ|| 978 ||

yadetannagaraṃ ramyaṃ nāmnā candrapuraṃ puram|
atra te dadmi vasatiṃ kuru tatra jalāśayam|| 979 ||

sthāne ṣāḍaṅgule ramye durvāsā munisattamaḥ|
unmattaveṣaḥ pracchanno nāptavān sampratiśrayam|| 980 ||

śaptaṃ tena saroṣeṇa bhavitedaṃ jalāśayam|
na cāpi viditaṃ nāga munivākyaṃ tu kasyacit|| 981 ||

mayaiva kevalaṃ jñātaṃ tasyaivānugrahānmuneḥ|
tasmāt tvaṃ vasatiṃ tatra kuru pannaga māciram|| 982 ||

kiṃ tvabhyarthaya bhūpālaṃ viṣvagaśvaṃ narādhipam|
chadmanā yācanaṃ tasya tvayā kāryaṃ mahīpateḥ|| 983 ||

sa cchadmanā yācyamāno lobhādyo na pradāsyati|
avaśyakaraṇīye 'arthe pārthivaḥ syādvimānitaḥ|| 984 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

evam uktastu nīlena mahāpadmo bhujaṃgamaḥ|
bhūtvā tu brāhmaṇo vṛddho yayau candrapuraṃ puram|| 985 ||

sa dadarśa mahīnāthaṃ viṣvagaśvaṃ dayāparam|
dṛṣṭvā cāyācata tadā yathā viṣṇurbaliṃ tathā|| 986 ||

brāhmaṇa uvāca |

rājaṃścandrapure śubho dīyatāṃ me pratiśrayaḥ|
paryāptaṃ yat kuṭumbasya mahato me dayāpara|| 987 ||

viṣvagaśva uvāca |

dadāni te 'ahaṃ viprendra sthānaṃ candrapura śubham|
gṛhāṇa yāvat paryāptaṃ sakuṭumbasya te dvija|| 988 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

pratigrahajalaṃ gṛhya svastivācya bhujaṃgamaḥ|
uvāca mantriṇāṃ madhye nāgarūpe narādhipam|| 989 ||

hastyaśvarathasaṃyuktaḥ svajanaiḥ parivāritaḥ|
niryāhi nagarādasmāt sadhanadravyasaṃcayaḥ|| 990 ||

sakuṭumbasya paryāptaṃ nagaraṃ me narādhipa|
jalāśayaḥ suvistīrṇo bhavitā śīghram eva tu|| 991 ||

tataḥ sa rājā dharmātmā sapaurahayakuñjaraḥ|
sārthamātracayo gatvā svapurādyojanadvayam|
paścimena tadā cakre nagaraṃ sumanoharam|| 992 ||

viṣvagaśvapuraṃ nāma tadetadbhuvi viśrutam|
tatrovāsa sukhī rājā brāhmaṇān paripūjayan|| 993 ||

nagaraṃ plāvayāmāsa mahāpadmo bhujaṃgamaḥ|
tatrāste saparīvāraḥ sukhī bhujagasattamaḥ|| 994 ||

mahāpadmasarastacca yojanāyāmavistṛtam|
supuṇyaṃ ramaṇīyaṃ ca satāṃ hṛdayanandanam|| 995 ||

mahāpadmaprabhāvena duṣṭagrahavivarjitam|
tatrāste sa sukhī nāgaḥ kuṭumbaparivāritaḥ|| 996 ||

bṛhadaśva uvāca |

śṛṇu rājan kathāṃ divyāṃ sarvakalmaṣanāśinīm|

dharmātmā tīrthayātrārthaṃ jagāmābhīpsitāṃ gatim|| 1384 ||

bahu mene tatjātmānaṃ gonandaḥ samarapriyaḥ|
sa praśaśāsa vasudhāṃ rājā dharmānusārataḥ|| 1385 ||

janamejaya uvāca |

bhūyo vitastāmāhātmyaṃ kathayasva dvijottama|
tacchrutvaiva gamiṣyāmi dvijendra gatakilbiṣaḥ|| 1386 ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca |

harasya dayitā bhāryā satī dākṣāyaṇī śubhā|
saivomā kathitā rājan prāpte vaivasvate 'antare|| 1387 ||

himādritanayā saiva yamunā pāpanāśinī|
manvantarānte saivoktā naurgariṣṭhā jagattraye|| 1388 ||

kaśmīrā kathitā devī vitastā saiva nimnagā|
pātālādutthitā devī śūlaghātāt taraṅgiṇī|| 1389 ||

snātastu toye vaitaste svayam ātmānam ātmanā|
vijānāti gataśeṣa- kalmaṣāllaghubhāvinam|| 1390 ||

vitastāto mahīnātha na gaṅga vyatiricyate|
kevalaṃ jāhnavītoye puruṣasyāsthisaṃcayaḥ|
vitastāto 'adhiko rājan snānādyaṃ tulyam eva ca|| 1391 ||

bhagīrathena gaṅgeyaṃ purā rājñāvatāritā|
asthiplāvanakāmena sagarāṇāṃ mahātmanām|| 1392 ||

teneyaṃ kathitā rājan praśastā tatra karmaṇi|
vitastā vai nadī puṇyā sarvapāpaharā śubhā|| 1393 ||

vaitastena tu toyena jaṭharasthena ye mṛtāḥ|
sarve svargam anuprāptā yathā te somapāyinaḥ|| 1394 ||

na yajñairdakṣiṇāvadbhistarpitā devatāstathā|
tṛptiṃ samadhigacchanti yathā ca salilaiśca taiḥ|| 1395 ||

pātre tīrthe yathākāle mṛṣṭānnairvividhaiḥ kṛtaiḥ|
yathā tṛpyanti pitaraḥ śrāddhaiḥ śuddhaiśca tairjalaiḥ|| 1396 ||

nāgairbahuvidhākāraiḥ saridbhiḥ satataṃ gatām|
tīrthairdevaiśca ṛṣibhirgandharvairyakṣarākṣasaiḥ|
abhigaccheta medhāvī janmasāphalyakāraṇāt|| 1397 ||

snātamātraṃ vitastāyāṃ jānāti varuṇo naram|
jalādhipena jñātasya narake patanaṃ kutaḥ|| 1398 ||

pāpānāṃ narake ghore patatāṃ duṣkṛtātmanām|
hastratrāṇapradā devī vitastāghanisūdanī|| 1399 ||

svargarohaṇaniḥśreṇīṃ manorathaphalapradām|
vitastāṃ ye gamiṣyanti te yāsyantyamarāvatīm|| 1400 ||

haṃsasārasayukena cakravākopaśobhinā|
vimānenārkavarṇena kiṅkiṇījālamālinā|| 1401 ||

devarāmāgaṇāḍhyena vīṇāmurajanādinā|
pulinairvividhairyuktāṃ padmotpalavibhūṣitām|| 1402 ||

gokulārāvabahulāṃ hambhāravanināditām|
matsyakacchapasambādhāṃ sutīrthāṃ kāmadāyinīm|
amṛtasvādusalilāṃ nṛṇāṃ dṛṣṭimanoharām|| 1403 ||

māteva varadāṃ devīm abhigacchanti ye narāḥ|
te 'abhigacchanti rājendra pratiṣṭhāṃ bhuvi mānavāḥ|| 1404 ||

samudrarūpasya harasya bhāryāṃ himālayasyādripatestanūjām|
susvādutoyām ṛṣivaryajuṣṭāṃ tāṃ tvaṃ pavitrāṃ praṇamasva rājan|| 1405 ||

sindhustrikoṭī ca tathā viśokā puṇyā nadī harṣapathā śivā ca|
puṇyā sukhā candravatī sugandhā puṇyodakā kilbiṣanāśinī ca|| 1406 ||

kūlāraṇiḥ pāpaharā ca kṛṣṇā nadī supuṇyā madhumatyathāpi|
nadī paroṣṇī ca tathātra puṇyā prayānti divyāṃ varadāṃ vitastām|| 1407 ||

gaṅgā nadī śambhujaṭākalāpe candreṇa devena tathā vibhinnā|
proktā nṛloke nṛpa candrabhāgā āyāti puṇyāṃ vitatāṃ vitastām|| 1408 ||

tīrthāni puṇyāni sarāṃsi rājan nadyastaḍākā vividhāśca kūpāḥ|
āyānti sarve varadāṃ vitastāṃ trayodaśīṃ bhādrapadasya śuklām|| 1409 ||

kasyāsti śaktirnṛpa te 'atra vaktuṃ devīguṇān varṣaśatairanekaiḥ|
bhaktyā mayoktāṃ ca niśamya kāṃcidbhaktiṃ kuruṣvātra sadā nṛvīra|| 1410 ||

śrutvā vitastāmāhātmyaṃ mucyate sarvakilbiṣaiḥ|
śrutvā nīlamataṃ sarvaṃ daśadhenuphalaṃ labhet|| 1411 ||

ityevam uktaṃ janamejayasya vyāsasya śiṣyeṇa mahāvratena|
kṣiptaṃ na yadgranthabahutvabhītyā samagraśāstraiḥ khalu bhārate vai|| 1412 ||

sarvatra naitadviṣayopayogyaṃ tadā na cakre bhagavān mahātmā|
atīva hṛdye bahuvistare 'api janapriye bhāratapūrṇacandre|| 1413 ||

iti nīlamate vitastāmāhātmyam|

sampūrṇaṃ cedaṃ nīlamataṃ nāmeti||
