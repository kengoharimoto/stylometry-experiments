।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ।।  
evaṃ prajāsanniveśaṃ śrutvā ca ṛṣipuṅgavaḥ।  
papraccha nipuṇaḥ sūtaṃ pṛthivyāyāma vistarau ।। 34.1 ।।  
  
kati dvīpāḥ samudrā vā parvatāśca kati prabhoḥ।  
kiyanti caiva varṣāṇi teṣu nadyaśca kāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 34.2 ।।  
  
mahābhūtapramāṇañca lokā lokau tathaiva ca।  
paryāyapārimāṇyañca gatiścandrārkayostathā।  
etat prabrūhi naḥ sarvaṃ vistareṇa yathā tathā ।। 34.3 ।।  
  
।।sūta uvāca।।  
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi pṛthivyāyāmavistaram।  
saṅkhyāścaiva samudrāṇāṃ dvīpānāñcaiva vistaram ।। 34.4 ।।  
  
yāvanti caiva varṣāṇi teṣu nadyaśca yāḥ smṛtāḥ।  
mahābhūtapramāṇañca lokālokau tathaiva ca।  
paryāyapārimāṇyañca gatiścandrārkayostathā ।। 34.5 ।।  
  
dvīpabhedasahasrāṇi saptasvantargatāni vai।  
na śakyante pramāṇena vaktuṃ varṣaśatairapi ।। 34.6 ।।  
  
saptadvīpantu vakṣyāmi candrādityagrahaiḥ saha।  
yeṣāṃ manuṣyāstarkeṇa pramāṇāni pracakṣate ।। 34.7 ।।  
  
acintyāḥ khalu ye bhāvā na tāṃstarkeṇa bhāvayet।  
prakṛtibhyaḥ paraṃ yacca tannityañca prajakṣyate ।। 34.8 ।।  
  
navavarṣaṃ pravakṣyāmi jambūdvīpaṃ yathā tathā ।  
vistarānmaṇḍalāccaiva yojanaistannibodhata ।। 34.9 ।।  
  
śatamekaṃ sahasrāṇāṃ yojanānāṃ pramāṇataḥ।  
nānājanapadākīrṇaiḥ puraiśca vividhaiḥ śubhaiḥ ।। 34.10 ।।  
  
siddhacāraṇagandharvaparvatairupaśobhitam।  
sarvadhātunibaddhaiśca śilājālasamudbhavaiḥ ।  
parvataprabhavābhiśca nadībhiḥ parvataistathā ।। 34.11 ।।  
  
jambūdvīpaḥ pṛthuḥ śrīmān sarvvataḥ parivāritaḥ।  
navabhiścāvṛtaḥ sarvvairbhuvanairbhūtabhāvanaiḥ।  
lāvaṇena samudreṇa sarvvataḥ parivāritaḥ ।। 34.12 ।।  
  
jambūdvīpasya vistārāt samena tu samantataḥ।  
jambūdvīpasya vistārāt samena tu samantataḥ।  
prāgāyatāḥ suparvāṇaḥ ṣaḍime varṣaparvvatāḥ।  
avagāḍhā ubhayataḥ samudrau pūrvapaśvimau ।। 34.13 ।।  
  
himaprāyaśca himavān hemakūṭaśca hemavān।  
taruṇādityavarṇābho hairaṇyo niṣadhaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 34.14 ।।  
  
cāturvarṇastu sauvarṇo meruścocyatamaḥ smṛtaḥ।  
plutākṛtipramāṇaśca caturasraḥ samucchritaḥ ।। 34.15 ।।  
  
nānāvarṇastu pārśveṣu prajāpatiguṇānvitaḥ।  
nābhivandhanasambhūto brahmaṇo'vyaktajanmanaḥ ।। 34.16 ।।  
  
pūrvvataḥ śvetavarṇo'sau brahmaṇyastasya tena tat।  
pītaśca dakṣiṇenāsau tena vaiśyatvamiṣyate ।। 34.17 ।।  
  
bhṛṅgapatranibhaśvāsau pakṣvimena mahābalaḥ।  
tenāsya śūdratā dṛṣṭā merornānārthakāraṇāt ।। 34.18 ।।  
  
pārśvamuttaratastasya raktavarṇaṃ svabhāvataḥ।  
tenāsya kṣatratā casyāditi varṇāḥ prakīrtitāḥ।  
vyaktaḥ svabhāvataḥ prokto varṇataḥ parimāṇataḥ ।। 34.19 ।।  
  
nīlaśca vaiḍhūryyamayaḥ śvetaśrṛṅgo hiraṇmayaḥ।  
mayūrabarhavarṇastu śātakaumbhastu śrṛṅgavān ।। 34.20 ।।  
  
ete parvvatarājānaḥ siddhacāraṇasevitāḥ।  
teṣāmantaraviṣkambho navasāhasra ucyate ।। 34.21 ।।  
  
madhye tvilāvṛtaṃ yastu mahāmeroḥ samantataḥ।  
navaiva tu sahasrāṇi vistīrṇaḥ parvvatastu saḥ।  
madhye tasya mahāmerornirdhūma iva pāvakaḥ ।। 34.22 ।।  
  
vedyarddhaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ meroruttarārddhaṃ tathottaram।  
varṣāṇi yāni saptātra teṣāṃ ye varṣaparvvatāḥ।  
dve dve sahasre vistīrṇā yojanāni samucchrayāt ।। 34.23 ।।  
  
jambūdvīpasya vistārātteṣāmāyāma ucyate।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrāṇi śate dve madhyamau girī ।। 34.24 ।।  
  
nīlaśca niṣadhaścaiva tābhyāṃ hīnāstu ye'pare।  
śvetaśca hemakūṭaśca himavān śrṛṅgavāṃśca yaḥ ।। 34.25 ।।  
  
navatirdvīvaśītirdvau sahasrāṇyāyatāstu ye ।  
teṣāṃ madhye janapadāstāni varṣāṇi sapta vai ।। 34.26 ।।  
  
sampātaviṣamaistaistu parvvatairāvṛtāni ca।  
santatāni nadībhedairagamyāni parasparam।  
vasanti teṣu satvāni nānā jātīni bhāgaśaḥ ।। 34.27 ।।  
  
idaṃ haimavataṃ varṣaṃ bhārataṃ nāma viśrutam।  
hemakūṭaṃ paraṃ tasmānnāmnā kimpuruṣaṃ smṛtam ।। 34.28 ।।  
  
naiṣadhaṃ hemakūṭantu harivarṣaṃ taducyate।  
harivarṣātparañcaiva merośca tadilāvṛtam ।। 34.29 ।।  
  
ilāvṛtaparaṃ nīlaṃ ramyakaṃ nāma viśrutam।  
ramyātparataraṃ śvetaṃ viśrutantaddhiraṇmayam।  
hiraṇmayātparañcāpi śrṛṅgavāṃstu kurusmṛtam ।। 34.30 ।।  
  
dhanuḥsaṃsthe ca vijñeye dve varṣe dakṣiṇottare।  
dīrghāṇi tatra catvāri madhyamantadilāvṛtam ।। 34.31 ।।  
  
arvāk ca niṣadhasyātha vedyarddhaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ smṛtam।  
paraṃ nīlavato yacca vedyarddhantu taduttaram।  
vedyarddhaṃ dakṣiṇe trīṇi varṣāṇi trīṇi cottare ।। 34.32 ।।  
  
tayormadhye tu vijñeyaṃ merumadhyamilāvṛtam।  
dakṣiṇena tu nīlasya niṣadhasyottareṇa tu ।। 34.33 ।।  
  
udagāyato mahāśailo mālyavān nāma parvvataḥ।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrorurānīlaniṣadhāyataḥ।  
āyāmataśvatustriśatsahasrāṇi prakīrtitaḥ ।। 34.34 ।।  
  
tasya pratīcyāṃ vijñeyaḥ parvato gandhamādanaḥ।  
āyāmādatha vistārān mālyavāniti viśrutaḥ ।। 34.35 ।।  
  
parimaṇḍalayormadhye meruruttamaparvataḥ ।  
caturvarṇaḥ susauvarṇaścaturasraḥ samucchritaḥ।  
avyaktā dhātavaḥ sarvve samutpannā jalādayaḥ ।। 34.36 ।।  
  
avyaktāt pṛthivī padmaṃ meruparvatakarṇikam।  
catuṣpathaṃ samutpannaṃ vyaktaṃ pañcaguṇaṃ mahat ।। 34.37 ।।  
  
tataḥ sarvvāḥ samutpannā vṛttayo dvijasattamāḥ।  
naikakalpārjitaiḥ puṇyaurve vidhaiḥ prāgupārjitaiḥ ।। 34.38 ।।  
  
kṛtātmabhirvinītātmā mahātmā puruṣottamaḥ।  
mahādevo mahāyogī jagajjyeṣṭo maheśvaraḥ ।  
sarvalokagato'nanto hyamūrtitvādajāyata ।। 34.39 ।।  
  
na tasya prākṛtī mūrttirmāṃsamedo'sthisambhavā ।  
yogāccaiveśvaratvācca sarvvātmāgata eva saḥ ।। 34.40 ।।  
  
tannimittaṃ samutpannaṃ lokapadmaṃ sanātanam।  
kalpaśeṣasya tasyādau kālasya gatirīdṛśī ।। 34.41 ।।  
  
tasmin padme samutpanno devadevaścaturmukhaḥ।  
prajāpatipatirbrahmā īśāno jagataḥ prabhuḥ ।। 34.42 ।।  
  
tasya bījāni sargo hi puṣkarasya yathārthavat।  
kṛtsnaḥ prajānisargeṇa vistareṇeha kathyate ।। 34.43 ।।  
  
yadabjaṃ vaiṣṇavaṃ kāryyaṃ tatastannābhito'bhavat।  
padmākārā samutpannā pṛthivī savanadrumā ।। 34.44 ।।  
  
tadasya lokapadmasya vistareṇa prakāśitam।  
varṇamānaṃ vibhāgena kramaśaḥ śrṛṇuta dvijāḥ ।। 34.45 ।।  
  
mahādvīpāstu vikhyātāścatvāraḥ patrasaṃsthitāḥ।  
tataḥ karṇikasaṃsthāno merurnāma mahābalaḥ ।। 34.46 ।।  
  
nānāvarṇeṣu pārśveṣu pūrvataḥ śveta ucyate।  
pītantu dakṣiṇaṃ tasya śṛṅgaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ tathāparam ।। 34.47 ।।  
  
uttaraṃ tasya raktaṃ vai śobhivarṇasamanvitam।  
merustu śobhate śubhro rājavatsa tu dhiṣṭitaḥ ।। 34.48 ।।  
  
taruṇādityavarṇābho vidhūma iva pāvakaḥ।  
caturaśītisāhasra utsedhena prakīrttitaḥ ।। 34.49 ।।  
  
praviṣṭaḥ ṣoḍaśādhastādvistṛtastāvadeva tu ।  
sa śarāvasthitaḥ pūrvaṃ dvātriṃśanmūrdhni vistṛtaḥ ।। 34.50 ।।  
  
vistārāt triguṇaścāsya pariṇāhaḥ samantataḥ।  
maṇḍalena pramāṇena tryasre'rddhantu tadiṣyate ।। 34.51 ।।  
  
catvāriṃśatsahasrāṇi yojanānāṃ samantataḥ।  
aṣṭābhiradhikāni syustryasre māne prakīrttitam ।। 34.52 ।।  
  
caturasreṇa mānena pariṇāhaḥ samantataḥ।  
catuḥṣaṣṭisahasrāṇi yojanānāṃ vidhīyate ।। 34.53 ।।  
  
sa parvato mahādivyo divyauṣadhisamanvitaḥ।  
bhuvanairāvṛtaḥ sarvo jātarūpamayaiḥ śubhaiḥ ।। 34.54 ।।  
  
tatra devagaṇāḥ sarve gandharvoragarākṣasāḥ ।  
śailarājaiḥ pradṛśyante śubhāścāpsarasāṅgaṇāḥ ।। 34.55 ।।  
  
sa tumeruḥ parivṛto bhuvanairbhūtabhāvanaḥ।  
catvāro yasya deśā vai nānāpārśveṣvadhiṣṭitāḥ ।। 34.56 ।।  
  
bhadrāśvo bharataścaiva ketumālaśca paścimaḥ।  
uttarāḥ kuravaścaiva kṛtapuṇyapratiśrayāḥ ।। 34.57 ।।  
  
karṇikā tasya padmasya samantātparimaṇḍalā।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrāṇi navatiḥ ṣaṭ prakīrttitāḥ।  
catvāraścāpyaśītiśca antarāntara dhiṣṭitāḥ ।। 34.58 ।।  
  
triśatañca sahasrāṇi yojanānāṃ pramāṇataḥ।  
tasya keśarajālāni vistīrṇāni samantataḥ ।। 34.59 ।।  
  
śatasāhasrikāyāmā sāśītipṛthulāyatā।  
catvāri tasya patrāṇi yojanānāṃ caturddiśam ।। 34.60 ।।  
  
tatra yāsau mayā pūrvaṃ karṇiketyabhiśabditā।  
tāṃ varṇyamānāmekāgrāḥ samāsena nibodhata ।। 34.61 ।।  
  
śatāśrimenaṃ mene'triḥ sahasrāśrimṛṣirbhṛguḥ।  
aṣṭāśrimenaṃ sāvarṇiścaturasrantu bhāguriḥ ।। 34.62 ।।  
  
vārṣāyaṇistu sāmudraṃ śarāvañcaiva gālavaḥ।  
ūrddhvaveṇīkṛtaṃ gārgyaḥ kroṣṭakiḥ parimaṇḍalam ।। 34.63 ।।  
  
yadyadyasya hi yatpārśca parvatādhipater्ṛṣiḥ।  
tattadevāsya vedāsau brahmainaṃ veda kṛtsnaśaḥ ।। 34.64 ।।  
  
maṇiratnamayaṃ citraṃ nānāvarṇaprabhāyutam।  
anekavarṇanicayaṃ sauvarṇamaruṇaprabham ।। 34.65 ।।  
  
kāntaṃ sahasraparvāṇaṃ sahasrodakakandaram।  
sahasra śatapatrantaṃ viddhi meruṃ nagottamam ।। 34.66 ।।  
  
maṇiratnārpitastambhairmaṇicitritavedikaiḥ।  
suvarṇamaṇicitrāṅgaṃ tathā vidrumatoraṇaiḥ ।। 34.67 ।।  
  
vimānayānaiḥ śrīmadbhiḥ śatasaṅkhyairdivaukasām।  
prabhādīpitaparyantaṃ meruṃ parvaṇi parvaṇi ।। 34.68 ।।  
  
tasya parvasahasre'smin nānāśrayavibhūṣite।  
sarvadeva nikāyāni sanniviṣṭānyanekaśaḥ ।। 34.69 ।।  
  
tamāvasaccorddhvatale devadevaścaturmukhaḥ।  
brahmā brahmavidāṃ śreṣṭo variṣṭhastridivaukasām ।। 34.70 ।।  
  
mahābhuvanasampūrṇaiḥ sarvvaiḥ kāmaphalapradaiḥ ।  
mahāsurasahasraistaṃ dikṣvanekasamākulam ।। 34.71 ।।  
tatra brahmasabhā ramyā brahmarṣigaṇasevitā ।  
nāmnā manovatī nāma sarvalokeṣu viśrutā ।। 34.72 ।।  
  
tatreśānasya devasya sahasrādityavarcasam।  
mahāvimānaṃ saṃsthāpya mahimnā vartate sadā ।। 34.73 ।।  
  
tatra sarṣigaṇā devāścaturvaktrasya te tadā।  
tadeva tejasāṃ rāśirdevānāṃ tatra kīrttyate ।। 34.74 ।।  
  
tatrāste śrīpatiḥ śrīmān sahasrākṣaḥ purandaraḥ।  
upāsyamānastridaśairmahāyogaiḥ surarṣibhiḥ ।। 34.75 ।।  
  
tatra lokapateḥ sthānamādityasamavarcasaḥ।  
mahendrasya mahārājñaḥ sarvvasiddhairnamaskṛtam ।। 34.76 ।।  
  
tamindralokaṃ lokasya ṛddhyā paramayā yutam।  
dīpyate tvamaraśreṣṭaistridaśairnityasevitam ।। 34.77 ।।  
  
dvitīye'pyantarataṭe vaidiśye pūrvvadakṣiṇe।  
nānādhātuśataścitraiḥ suramyamatitejasam ।। 34.78 ।।  
  
naikaratnārthitatalamanekastambhasaṃyutam।  
jāmbūnadakṛtodyānaṃ nānāratnasuvedikam ।। 34.79 ।।  
  
kūṭāgārairvinikṣiptamanekairbhavanottamaiḥ ।  
mahāvimānaṃ prathitaṃ bhāskaraṃ jātavedasam ।। 34.80 ।।  
  
sā hi tejovatī nāma hutāśasya mahāsabhā।  
sākṣāttatra suraśreṣṭhaḥ sarvadevamukho'nalaḥ ।। 34.81 ।।  
  
śikhāśatasahasrāḍhyo jvālāmālī vibhā vasuḥ।  
stūyate hūyate caiva tatra sarṣigaṇaiḥ suraiḥ ।। 34.82 ।।  
  
adhidaivakṛtaṃ viprairviśeṣaḥ sa tu ucyate।  
sa vibhāgañca tejaśca sarva eva na saṃśayaḥ ।। 34.83 ।।  
  
bhogāntaramanuprāpta ekatejo vibhuḥ smṛtaḥ।  
pṛthak‌tvañca hi yuktyā tu kāryakāraṇamiśritam ।। 34.84 ।।  
  
tamagniṃ lokalokajñaistadvīryaiṃstatparā kramaiḥ ।  
mahātmabhirmahāsiddhairmahābhāgairnamaskṛtam ।। 34.85 ।।  
  
tṛtīye'pyantarataṭe evameva mahāsabhā।  
vaivasvatasya vijñeyā loke khyātā susaṃyamā ।। 34.86 ।।  
  
tathā caturthadigdeśe nair्ṛtyādhipateḥ sabhā।  
nāmnā kṛṣṇāṅganā nāma virūpākṣasya dhīmataḥ ।। 34.87 ।।  
  
pañcame'pyantarataṭe evameva mahāsabhā।  
vaivasvatasya vijñeyā nāmnā śubhavatī satī।  
udakādhipateḥ khyātā varuṇasya mahātmanaḥ ।। 34.88 ।।  
  
parottare tathā deśe ṣaṣṭe'ntarataṭe śive।  
vāyorgandhavatī nāma sabhā sarvaguṇottarā ।। 34.89 ।।  
sapta me'pyantarataṭe nakṣatrādhipateḥ sabhā।  
nāmnā mahodayā nāma śuddhavaiḍhūryyavedikā ।। 34.90 ।।  
  
tathāṣṭame'ntarataṭe īśānasya mahātmana

।। sūta uvāca ।।  
yattadvai karṇikāmūlamiti vai samprakīrtitam।  
tadyojanasahasrāṇāṃ saptatīnāmadhaḥ smṛtam ।। 35.1 ।।  
  
catvariṃśattathāṣṭau ca sahasrāṇyatra maṇḍalam।  
śailarājāvṛtaṃ ramyaṃ merumūlamiti śrutiḥ ।। 35.2 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ girisahasrāṇāmanekeṣu mahocchritāḥ।  
dikṣu sarvāsu paryyantairmaryyādāḥ parvatāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 35.3 ।।  
  
nikuñjakandaranadīguhānirjharaśobhitāḥ।  
bahuprāsādakaṭakai staṭaiśca kusumojjvalaiḥ ।। 35.4 ।।  
  
nitambapuṣpamālaughaiḥ sānubhirdhātumaṇḍitaiḥ ।  
śikharairhemakapilairnaikaprasravaṇāvṛtaiḥ।  
śobhitā girayaḥ sarvve puṣṭai ratna samarpitaiḥ ।। 35.5 ।।  
  
vihaṅgaśatasaṃjuṣṭaiḥ kuñjairanupamairapi।  
sihaśārdūlaśarabhairnaikaiścāmaravāraṇaiḥ।  
nānāvarṇākṛtidharaiḥ sevitā vividhairnagaiḥ ।। 35.6 ।।  
  
saptāśca harikṛṣṇāṅgamekaikaṃ daśaparvatam।  
bāhyamābhyantarā ye tu trivāhāstu samāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 35.7 ।।  
  
jaṭharo devakūṭaśca pūrvasyāṃ diśi parvatau।  
tau dakṣiṇottarāyāmāvānīlaniṣadhāyatau ।। 35.8 ।।  
  
kailāso himavāṃścaiva dakṣiṇottaraparvatau।

tatastṛtīyaṃ dvīpānāṃ śālmalaṃ dvīpamuttamam।  
śālmalena samudrastu dvīpenekṣurasodakaḥ।  
plakṣadvīpasya vistārād‌dviguṇena samāvṛtaḥ ।। 49.29 ।।  
  
tatrāpi parvatāḥ sapta vijñeyā ratnayonayaḥ।  
ratnākarāstathā nadyasteṣu varṣeṣu saptasu ।। 49.30 ।।  
  
prathamaḥ sūryasaṅkāśaḥ kumudo nāma parvataḥ।  
sarvadhātumayaiḥ śrṛṅgaiḥ śilājālasamudgataiḥ ।। 49.31 ।।  
  
dvitīyaḥ parvatastasya unnato nāma viśrutaḥ।  
haritālamayaiḥ śrṛṅgairdivamāvṛtya tiṣṭhati ।। 49.32 ।।  
  
tṛtīyaḥ parvatastasya balāhaka iti śrutaḥ।  
jātyañjanamayaiḥ śrṛṅgairdivamāvṛtya tiṣṭhati ।। 49.33 ।।  
  
caturthaḥ parvato droṇo yatrauṣadhyo mahābalāḥ।  
viśalyakaraṇī caiva mṛtasañjīvanī tathā ।। 49.34 ।।  
  
kaṅkastu pañcamastatra parvataḥ sumahodayaḥ।  
divyapuṣpaphalo peto vṛkṣavīrutsamāvṛtaḥ ।। 49.35 ।।  
  
ṣaṣṭhastu parvatastatra mahiṣo meghasannibhaḥ।  
yasmin so'gnirnivasati mahiṣo nāma vārijaḥ ।। 49.36 ।।  
  
saptamaḥ parvatastatra kakujhānnāma bhāṣyate।  
tatra ratnānyanekāni svayaṃ varṣati vāsavaḥ।  
prajāpatimupādāya prājāpatye vidhistvayam ।। 49.37 ।।  
  
ityete parvatāḥ sapta śālmale maṇibhūṣitāḥ ।  
teṣāṃ varṣāṇi vakṣyāmi saptaiva tu śubhāni vai।  
kumudāt prathamaṃ śvetamunnatasya tu lohitam ।। 49.38 ।।  
  
balāhakasya jīmūtaṃ droṇasya haritaṃ smṛtam ।  
kaṅkasya vaidyutaṃ nāma mahiṣasya tu mānasam ।। 49.39 ।।  
  
kakudaḥ suprabhaṃ nāma saptaitāni tu saptadhā।  
varṣāṇi parvatāṃścaiva nadīsteṣu nibodhata ।। 49.40 ।।  
  
pānitoyā vitṛṣṇā ca candrā śukrā vimocanī।  
nivṛttiḥ saptamī tāsāṃ prativarṣaṃ tu tāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 49.41 ।।  
  
tāsāṃ samīpagāścānyāḥ śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ।  
aśakyāḥ parisaṃkhyātuṃ śraddheyāstu bubhūṣatā ।। 49.42 ।।  
  
ityeṣa sanniveśo vaḥ śālmalasyāpi kīrtitaḥ।  
plakṣavṛkṣeṇa saṃkhyātastasya madhye mahādrumaḥ ।। 49.43 ।।  
  
śālmalirvipulaskandha stasya nāmnā sa ucyate।  
śālmalistu samudreṇa surodena samantataḥ।  
vistārācchālmalasyaiva samena tu samantataḥ ।। 49.44 ।।  
  
uttareṣu tu dharmajñā dvīpeṣu śrṛṇuta prajāḥ।  
yathāśrutaṃ yathānyāyaṃ bruvato me nibodhata ।। 49.45 ।।  
  
kuśadvīpaṃ pravakṣyāmi caturthaṃ taṃ samāsataḥ।  
surodakaḥ parivṛtaḥ kuśadvīpena sarvataḥ ।। 49.46 ।।  
  
saptaiva girayastatra varṇyamānānnibodhata।  
śālmalasya tu vistārād dviguṇena samantataḥ ।। 49.47 ।।  
  
kukhadvīpe tu vijñeyaḥ parvato vidrumoccayaḥ।  
dvīpasya prathamastasya dvitīyo hemaparvataḥ ।। 49.48 ।।  
  
tṛtīyo dyutimānnāma jīmūtasadṛśo giriḥ।  
caturthaḥ puṣpavānnāma pañcamastu kuśeśayaḥ ।। 49.49 ।।  
  
ṣaṣṭo harigirirnāma saptamo mandaraḥ smṛtaḥ ।  
mandā iti hyapāṃ nāma mandaro dāraṇādapām ।। 49.50 ।।  
  
teṣāmantaraviṣkambho dviguṇaḥ parivāritaḥ।  
udbhidaṃ prathamaṃ varṣaṃ dvitīyaṃ veṇumaṇḍalam ।। 49.51 ।।  
  
tṛtīyaṃ svairathākāraṃ caturthaṃ lavaṇaṃ smṛtam।  
pañcamaṃ dhṛtimadvarṣaṃ ṣaṣṭhaṃ varṣa prabhākaram।  
saptamaṃ kapilaṃ nāma saptaite varṣaparvatāḥ ।। 49.52 ।।  
  
eteṣu devagandharvāḥ prabhāstu jagadīśvarāḥ।  
viharanti ramante ca dṛśyamānāstu varṣaśaḥ ।। 49.53 ।।  
  
na teṣu dasyavaḥ santi mlecchajātyastathaiva ca।  
gauraprāyo janaḥ sarvaḥ kramācca mriyate tathā ।। 49.54 ।।  
  
tatrāpi nadyaḥ saptaiva dhutapāpāḥ śivāstathā।  
pavitrā santatiścaiva dyutigarbhā mahī tathā ।। 49.55 ।।  
  
anyāstābhyaḥ parijñātāḥ śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ।  
abhigacchanti tāḥ sarvā yato varṣati vāsavaḥ ।। 49.56 ।।  
  
ghṛtodena kuśadvīpo bāhyataḥ parivāritaḥ।  
vijñeyaḥ sa tu vistārāt kuśadvīpasamena tu ।। 49.57 ।।  
  
ityeṣa sanniveśo vaḥ kuśadvīpasya varṇitaḥ।  
krauñcadvīpasya vistāraṃ vakṣyāmyahamataḥ param ।। 49.58 ।।  
  
kuśadvīpasya vistārād dviguṇaḥ sa tu vai smṛtaḥ।  
ghṛtodakasamudro vai krauñcadvīpena saṃvṛtaḥ ।। 49.59 ।।  
  
tasmin dvīpe nagaśreṣṭhaḥ krauñcasthaḥ prathamo giriḥ।  
krauñcātparo vāmanako vāmanādandhakārakaḥ ।। 49.60 ।।  
  
andhakārātparaścāpi divāvṛnnāma parvataḥ।  
divāvṛtaḥ paraścapi divindo girirucyate ।। 49.61 ।।  
  
divindātparataścāpi puṇḍarīko mahāgiriḥ।  
puṇḍarīkātparaścāpi procyate dundubhisvanaḥ ।। 49.62 ।।  
  
ete ratnamayāḥ sapta krauñcadvīpasya parvatāḥ।  
bahuvṛkṣaphalo petā nānāvṛkṣalatāvṛtāḥ ।। 49.63 ।।  
  
paraspareṇa dviguṇā viṣkambhādvarṣaparvvatāḥ ।  
varṣāṇi tatra vakṣyāmi nāmatastu nibodhata ।। 49.64 ।।  
  
krauñcasya kuśalī deśo vāmanasya manonugaḥ।  
manonugātparaścoṣṇastṛtīyo deśa ucyate ।। 49.65 ।।  
  
uṣṇātparaḥ prāvarakaḥ prāvarādandhakārakaḥ।  
andhakārakadeśāttu munideśaḥ paraḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 49.66 ।।  
  
munideśātparaścaiva procyate dundubhisvanaḥ।  
siddhacāraṇasaṅkīrṇo gauraprāyo janaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 49.67 ।।  
  
tatrāpi nadyaḥ saptaiva prati varṣa smṛtāḥ śubhāḥ ।  
gaurī kumudvatī caiva sandhyā rātrirmanojavā ।  
khyātiśca puṇḍarīkā ca gaṅgā saptavidhā smṛtā ।। 49.68 ।।  
  
tāsāṃ samudragāścānyā nadyo yāstu samīpagāḥ।  
anugacchanti tāḥ sarvā vipulāḥ subahūdakāḥ ।। 49.69 ।।  
  
krauñcadvīpaḥ samudreṇa dadhimaṇḍodakena tu।  
āvṛtaḥ sarvataḥ śrīmān krauñcadvīpasamena tu ।। 49.70 ।।  
  
plakṣadvīpādayo hyete samāsena prakīrttitāḥ।  
teṣāṃ nisargo dvīpānāmānupūrvyeṇa sarvaśaḥ ।। 49.71 ।।  
  
na śakyaṃ vistarādvaktu mapi varṣaśatairapi ।  
nisargo'yaṃ prajānāṃ tu saṃhāro yaśca tāsu vai ।। 49.72 ।।  
  
ata ūrddvaṃ pravakṣyāmi śākadvīpasya yo vidhiḥ।  
śākadvīpasya kṛtsnasya yathāvadiha niścayāt।  
śrṛṇudhvaṃ vai yathātattvaṃ bruvato me yathārthavat ।। 49.73 ।।  
  
krauñcadvīpasya vistārād dviguṇastasya vistaraḥ।  
parivārya samudraṃ sa dadhimaṇḍodakaṃ sthitaḥ ।। 49.74 ।।  
  
tatra puṇyā janapadāścirācca mriyate janaḥ।  
kuta eva tu durbhikṣañcarāvyādhibhayaṃ kutaḥ ।। 49.75 ।।  
  
tatrāpi parvatāḥ śubhrāḥ saptaiva maṇibhūṣitāḥ।  
ratnākarāstathā nadyastāsāṃ nāmāni me śrṛṇu ।। 49.76 ।।  
  
devarṣigandharvayutaḥ prathamo merurucyate।  
prāgāyataḥ sa sauvarṇa udayo nāma parvataḥ ।। 49.77 ।।  
  
tatra meghāstu vṛṣṭyarthaṃ prabhavanti ca yānti ca।  
tasyāpareṇa sumahān jaladhāro mahāgiriḥ ।। 49.78 ।।  
  
tasmānnityamupādatte vāsavaḥ paramaṃ jalam।  
tato varṣaṃ prabhavati varṣākāle prajāsviha ।। 49.79 ।।  
  
tasyāpare raivatako yatra nityaṃ pratiṣṭitā ।  
revatī divi nakṣatraṃ pitāmahakṛto giriḥ ।। 49.80 ।।  
  
tasyāpareṇa sumahān śyāmo nāma mahāgiriḥ।  
tasmācchayāmatvamāpannāḥ prajāḥ pūrvamimāḥ kila ।। 49.81 ।।  
  
tasyāpareṇa rajato mahānasto giriḥ smṛtaḥ।  
tasyāpareṇāmbikeyo durgaḥ śailo himācitaḥ ।। 49.82 .  
āmbikeyātparo ramyaḥ sarvauṣadhisamanvitaḥ ।  
sa caiva keśarītyukto yato vāyuḥ pravāyati ।। 49.83 ।।  
  
śrṛṇudhvaṃ nāmatastāni yathāvadanupūrvaśaḥ।  
udayasyodayaṃ varṣaṃ jaladaṃ nāma viśrutam ।। 49.84 ।।  
  
dvitīyaṃ jaladhārasya sukumāramiti smṛtam।  
raivatasya tu kaumāraṃ śyāmasya tu maṇīcakam ।। 49.85 ।।  
  
astasyāpi śubhaṃ varṣaṃ vijñeyaṃ kusumottaram।  
āmbikeyasya modākaṃ kesareṣu mahādrumam ।। 49.86 ।।  
  
dvīpasya parimāṇañca hrasvadīrghatvameva ca।  
śākadvīpena vikhyātastasya madhye vanaspatiḥ ।  
śāko nāma mahāvṛkṣastasya pūjāṃ prayuñjate ।। 49.87 ।।  
  
etena devagandharvāḥ siddhāśca saha cāraṇaiḥ।  
viharanti ramante ca dṛśyamānāśca taiḥ saha ।। 49.88 ।।  
  
tatra puṇyā janapadāścāturvarṇasamanvitāḥ।  
teṣu nadyaśca saptaiva prativarṣaṃ samudragāḥ ।  
viddhi nāmnaśca tāḥ sarvā gaṅgāstāḥ saptadhā smṛtāḥ ।। 49.89 ।।  
  
prathamā sukumārīti gaṅgā śivajalā tathā।  
anu taptā ca nāmnaiva nadī samparikīrttitā ।। 49.90 ।।  
  
kumārī nāmataḥ siddhā dvitīyā sā punaḥ satī ।  
nandā ca pārvatī caiva tṛtīya parikīrttitā ।। 49.91 ।।  
  
śivetikā caturthī syāt tridivā ca punaḥ smṛtā।  
ikṣuśca pañcamī jñeyā tathaiva ca punaḥ kratuḥ ।। 49.92 ।।  
  
dhenukā ca mṛtā caiva ṣaṣṭī saṃparikīrttitā।  
etāḥ sapta mahāgaṅgāḥ prativarṣaṃ śivodakāḥ।  
bhāvayanti janaṃ sarvaṃ śākadvīpanivāsinam ।। 49.93 ।।  
  
anugacchanti tāstvanyā nadīnadyaḥ sahasraśaḥ।  
bahūdaka parisrāvā yato varṣati vāsavaḥ ।। 49.94 ।।  
  
tāsāntu nāmadheyāni parimāṇaṃ tathaiva ca।  
na śakyaṃ parisaṃkhyātuṃ puṇyāstāḥ sariduttamāḥ।  
tāḥ pibanti sadā hṛṣṭā nadīrjanapadāstu te ।। 49.95 ।।  
  
śaṃśapāyana vistīrṇo dvīpo'sau cakrasaṃsthitaḥ।  
nadījalaiḥ praticchannaḥ parvataiścābhrasannibhaiḥ ।। 49.96 ।।  
  
sarvadhātuvicitraiśca maṇi vidrumabhūṣitaiḥ।  
puraiśca vividhākāraiḥ sphītairjanapadairapi ।। 49.97 ।।  
  
vṛkṣaiḥ puṣpaphalopetaiḥ samantāddhanadhānyavān।  
kṣīrodena samudreṇa sarvataḥ parivāritaḥ।  
śākadvīpastu vistārāt samena tu samantataḥ ।। 49.98 ।।  
  
tasmin janapadāḥ puṇyāḥ parvatāntarite śubhāḥ ।  
varṇāśramasamākīrṇā deśāste sapta vai smṛtāḥ ।। 49.99 ।।  
  
na saṅkaraśca teṣvasti varṇāśramakṛtaḥ vkacit ।  
dharmasya cāvyabhīcārādekāntasukhitāḥ prajāḥ ।। 49.100 ।।  
  
na teṣu lobho māyā vā īrṣyāsūyā'dhṛtiḥ kutaḥ।  
viparyayo na teṣvasti etat svābhāvikaṃ smṛtam ।। 49.101 ।।  
  
karotpattirnateṣvasti na daṇḍo na ca daṇḍakāḥ।  
svadharmeṇaiva dharmajñāste rakṣanti parasparam ।। 49.102 ।।  
  
etāvadeva śakyaṃ vai tasmin dvīpe nivāsinām।  
puṣkaraṃ saptamaṃ dvīpaṃ pravakṣyāmi nibodhata ।। 49.103 ।।  
  
puṣkareṇa tu dvīpena vṛtaḥ kṣīrodako bahiḥ।  
śākadvīpasya vistārād‌dviguṇena samantataḥ ।। 49.104 ।।  
  
puṣkare parvataḥ śrīmān eka eva mahāśilaḥ।  
citrai rmaṇimayaiḥ śīlaiḥ śikharaistu samucchritaiḥ ।। 49.105 ।।  
  
dvīpasya tasya pūrvārddhe citrasānuḥ sthito mahān।  
parimaṇḍalasahasrāṇi vistīrṇaḥ pañcaviṃśatiḥ ।। 49.106 ।।  
  
ūrddvañcaiva catustriśatsahasrāṇi samācitaḥ।  
dvīpārddhasya paristomaḥ parvato mānasottamaḥ ।। 49.107 ।।  
  
sthito velāsamīpe tu navacandra ivoditaḥ।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrāṇi ūrddhvaṃ pañcaśaducchritaḥ ।। 49.108 ।।  
  
tāvadeva sa vistīrṇaḥ sarvataḥ parimaṇḍalaḥ।  
sa evaṃ dvīpapaścārddhe mānasaḥ pṛthivīdharaḥ ।। 49.109 ।।  
  
eka eva mahāsānuḥ sanniveśāddvidhā kṛtaḥ।  
svādūdakenodadhinā sarvataḥ parivāritaḥ ।। 49.110 ।।  
  
puṣkaradvīpavistārādvistīrṇo'sau samantataḥ।  
tasmin dvīpe smṛtau dvau tu puṇyau janapadau śubhau।  
abhito mānasasyātha parvatasyānumaṇḍalau ।। 49.111 ।।  
  
mahāvītantu yadvarṣaṃ bāhyato mānasasya tat।  
tasyaivābhyantare yattu dhātakīkhaṇḍamucyate ।। 49.112 ।।  
  
daśavarṣasahasrāṇi tatra jevanti mānavāḥ।  
ārogyasukhabhūyiṣṭā mānasīṃ siddhimāsthitāḥ ।। 49.113 ।।  
  
samamāyuśca rūpañca tasmin varṣadvaye sthitam।  
adhamottamau na teṣvāstāṃ tulyāste rūpaśīlataḥ ।। 49.114 ।।  
  
na tatra vañcako nerṣyā na stenā na bhayaṃ tathā।  
nigraho na ca daṇḍo'sti na lobho na parigrahaḥ ।। 49.115 ।।  
  
satyānṛtaṃ na tatrāsti dharmādharmau tathaiva ca।  
varṇāśramāṇāṃ vārttā vā pāśupālyaṃ vaṇikkriyā ।। 49.116 ।।  
  
trayī vidyā daṇḍanītiḥ śuśrūṣā śalyameva ca।  
varṣadvaye sarvametat puṣkarasya na vidyate ।। 49.117 ।।  
  
na tatra nadyo varṣañca śītoṣṇaṃ vā na vidyate ।  
udbhijjānyudakānyatra giriprasravaṇāni ca ।। 49.118 ।।  
  
uttarāṇāṃ kurūṇāñca tulyakālo janaḥ sadā।  
sarvatra susukhastatra jarāklamavivarjitaḥ ।। 49.119 ।।  
  
ityeṣa dhātakīkhaṇḍo mahāvīte tathaiva ca ।  
ānupūrvyādvidhiḥ kṛtsnaḥ puṣkarasya prakīrtitaḥ ।। 49.120 ।।  
  
svādūdakenodadhinā puṣkaraḥ parivāritaḥ।  
vistarānmaṇḍalāccaiva puṣkarasya tathaiva ca ।। 49.121 ।।  
  
evaṃ dvīpāḥ samudraistu sapta saptabhirāvṛtāḥ।  
dvīpasyānantaro yastu samudrastu samantataḥ ।। 49.122 ।।  
  
evaṃ dvīpasamudrāṇāṃ vṛddhirjñeyā parasparāt।  
apāñcaiva samudrekāt samudrā iti saṃjñitāḥ ।। 49.123 ।।  
  
ṛṣayo nivasantyasmin prajā yasmāccaturvidhāḥ।  
tasmādvarṣamiti proktaṃ prajānāṃ sukhadantu tat ।। 49.124 ।।  
  
ṛṣa ityeva ṛṣayaḥ vṛṣaḥ śaktiprabandhane।  
iti prabandhanāt siddhaṃ varṣatvaṃ tena teṣu tat ।। 49.125 ।।  
  
śuklapakṣe candravṛddhau samudraḥ pūryate tadā।  
prakṣīyamāṇe bahule kṣīyate'stamite khage ।। 49.126 ।।  
  
āpūryamāṇe udadhiḥ svata evābhipūryate।  
tato'pakṣīyamāṇe'pi svātmanaivāpakṛṣyate ।। 49.127 ।।  
  
ukhāsthamagnisaṃyogāt jalamudricyate yathā।  
tathā mahodadhigataṃ toyamudricyate tataḥ ।। 49.128 ।।  
  
anyūnāhyatiriktāśca varddhantyāpo hrasanti ca।  
udayāstamitaiścendoḥ pakṣayoḥ śuklakṛṣṇayoḥ।  
kṣayavṛddhirevamudadheḥ somavṛddhikṣayātpunaḥ ।। 49.129 ।।  
  
daśottarāṇi pañcaiva aṅgulīnāṃ śatāni tu।  
apāṃ vṛddhiḥ kṣayo dṛṣṭaḥ samudrāṇāntu parvasu ।। 49.130 ।।  
  
dvirāpatvāt smṛtā dvīpāḥ sarvataścodakāvṛtāḥ।  
udakasyādhānaṃ yasmācca tasmādudadhirucyate ।। 49.131 ।।  
  
aparvāṇastu girayaḥ parvabhiḥ parvatāḥ smṛtāḥ ।  
plakṣadvīpe tu gomedaḥ parvatastena cocyate ।। 49.132 ।।  
  
śālmaliḥ śālmaladvīpe pūjyate ca mahādrumāḥ।  
kuśadvīpe kuśastambastasya nāmnā sa ucyate ।। 49.133 ।।  
  
krauñcadvīpe giriḥ krauñco madhye janapadasya ha।  
śakadvīpe drumaḥ śākastasya nāmnā sa ucyate ।। 49.134 ।।  
  
nyagrodhaḥ puṣkaradvīpe tatra taiḥ sa namaskṛtaḥ।  
mahādevaḥ puṣkare tu brahmā tribhuvaneśvaraḥ ।। 49.135 ।।  
  
tasminnivasati brahmā sādhyaiḥ sārddhaṃ prajāpatiḥ।  
upāsate tatra devāstrayastriṃśanmaharṣibhiḥ।  
sa tatra pūjyate caiva devairdevottamottamaḥ ।। 49.136 ।।  
  
jambūdvīpātpravarttante ratnāni vividhāni ca।  
dvīpeṣu teṣu sarveṣu prajānāṃ hi kramāstviha ।। 49.137 ।।  
  
sarvaso brahmacaryeṇa satyena ca damena ca।  
ārogyāyuḥpramāṇāddhi dviguṇañca samantataḥ ।। 49.138 ।।  
  
etasmin puṣkaradvīpe yaduktaṃ varṣakadvayam।  
gopāyati prajāstatra svayaṃ sajjanamaṇḍitāḥ ।। 49.139 ।।  
  
īśvaro daṇḍamudyamya brahmā tribhuvaneśvaraḥ।  
saviṣṇuḥ saśivo devaḥ sapitā sapitāmahaḥ ।। 49.140 ।।  
  
bhojanañcāprayatnena tatra svayamupasthitam ।  
ṣaḍrasaṃ sumahāvīryaṃ bhuñjate ca prajāḥ sadā ।। 49.141 ।।  
  
pareṇa puṣkarasyātha āvṛtyā yaḥ sthito mahān ।  
svādūdakaḥ samudrastu samantātpariveṣṭitaḥ ।। 49.142 ।।  
  
pareṇa tasya mahatī dṛśyate loka saṃsthitiḥ।  
kāñcanī dviguṇā bhūmiḥ sarvā caikaśilopamā ।। 49.143 ।।  
  
tasmātpareṇa śailastu maryādānte tu maṇḍalam।  
prakāśaścāprakāśaśca lokālokaḥ sa ucyate ।। 49.144 ।।  
  
ālokastasya cārvāktu nirālokastataḥ param।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrāṇi daśa tasyocchrayaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 49.145 ।।  
  
tāvāṃśca vistarastasya pṛthivyāṃ kāmagaśca saḥ।  
āloke lokaśabdastu nirāloke salokatā।  
lokārthaṃ sammato loko nirālokastu bāhyataḥ ।। 49.146 ।।  
  
lokavistāramātrantu ālokaḥ sarvato bahiḥ।  
 paricchinnaḥ samantācca udakenāvṛtaśca saḥ।  
nirālokātparaścāpi aṇḍamāvṛtya tiṣṭhati ।। 49.147 ।।  
  
aṇḍasyāntastvime lokāḥ saptadvīpā ca medinī।  
bhūrloko'tha bhuvarlokaḥ svarloko'tha mahastathā ।। 49.148 ।।  
  
janastapastathā satya etāvān lokasaṅ‌grahaḥ।  
ekāvāneva vijñeyo lokāntaścaiva tatparaḥ ।। 49.149 ।।  
  
kumbasthāyī bhavedyādṛk pratīcyāndiśi candramāḥ।  
āditaḥ śuklapakṣasya vapuraṇḍasya tadvidham ।। 49.150 ।।  
  
aṇḍānāmīdṛśānāntu koṭyo jñeyāḥ sahasraśaḥ।  
tiryagūrdhvamadhastācca kāraṇasyāvyayātmanaḥ।  
kāraṇaiḥ prākṛtaistatra hyāvṛtaṃ pratisaptabhiḥ ।। 49.151 ।।  
  
daśādhikyena cānyonyaṃ dhārayanti parasparam।  
parasparāvṛtāḥ sarve utpannāśca parasparāt ।। 49.152 ।।  
  
aṇḍasyāsya samantāttu sanniviṣṭo ghanodadhiḥ।  
samantādyena toyena dhāryamāṇaḥ sa tiṣṭhati ।। 49.153 ।।  
  
bāhyato ghanatoyasya tiryagūrddhvānumaṇḍalam।  
dhāryamāṇaṃ samantāttu tiṣṭhate ghanatejasā ।। 49.154 ।।  
  
ayoguḍanibho vahniḥ samantānmaṇḍalākṛtiḥ।  
samantāddhanavātena dhāryamāṇaḥ sa tiṣṭhati।  
ghanavātastathākāśandhārayāṇastu tiṣṭhati ।। 49.155 ।।  
  
bhūtādiśca tathākāśaṃ bhūtādyañcāpyasau mahān ।  
mahān vyāpto hyanantena avyaktena tu dhāryate ।। 49.156 ।।  
  
anantamaparivyaktandaśadhā sūkṣma eva ca।  
anantamakṛtātmānamanādinidhanañca tat ।। 49.157 ।।  
  
atītya para to ghoramanālambamanāmayam।  
naikayojanasāhasraṃ viprakṛṣṭaṃ tamovṛtam ।। 49.158 ।।  
  
tama eva nirālokamamaryādamadeśikam।  
devānāmapyavidiṃtaṃ vyavahāravivarjjitam ।। 49.159 ।।  
  
tamaso'nte ca vikhyātamākāśānte ca bhāsvaram।  
maryādāyāmatastasya śivasyāyatanaṃ mahat ।। 49.160 ।।  
  
tridaśānāmagamyantu sthānaṃ divyamiti śrutiḥ।  
mahato devadevasya maryādāyāṃ vyavasthitam ।। 49.161 ।।  
  
candrādityāvataptāstu ye lokāḥ prathitā budhaiḥ।  
te lokā ityabhihitā jagataśca na saṃśayaḥ ।। 49.162 ।।  
  
rasātalatalāt sapta saptaivorddhvatalāḥ kṣitau।  
saptaskandhāstathā vāyoḥ sabrahmasadanā dvijāḥ ।। 49.163 ।।  
  
āpātālāddivaṃ yāvadatra pañcavidhā gatiḥ।  
pramāṇametajjagata eṣa saṃsārasāgaraḥ ।। 49.164 ।।  
  
anādyantā prayātyevaṃ naikajātisamudbhavāḥ।  
vicitrā jagataḥ sā vai pravṛttiranavasthitā ।। 49.165 ।।  
  
yathaitad bhautikaṃ nāma nisargabahuvistaram।  
atīndriyairmahābhāgaiḥ siddhairapi na lakṣyate ।। 49.166 ।।  
  
pṛthivyāñcāgnivāyūnāṃ mahatastamasastathā।  
īśvarasya tu devasya anantasya dvijottamāḥ ।। 49.167 ।।  
  
kṣayo vā parimāṇaṃ vā anto vāpi na vidyate।  
ananta eṣa sarvatra sarvasthāneṣu paṭyate।  
tasya coktaṃ mayā pūrvaṃ tasminnāmānukīrttane ।। 49.168 ।।  
  
ya eṣa śivanāmnā hi tadvaḥ kārtsnyena kīrtitam।  
sa eṣa sarvatra gataḥ sarvasthāneṣu pūjyate ।। 49.169 ।।  
  
bhūmau rasātale caiva ākāśe pavane'nale।  
arṇaveṣu ca sarveṣu divi caiva na saṃśayaḥ ।। 49.170 ।।  
  
tathā tapasi vijñeya eṣa eva mahādyutiḥ।  
anekadhā vibhaktāṅgo mahāyogī maheśvaraḥ।  
sarvalokeṣu lokeśa ijyate bahudhā prabhuḥ ।। 49.171 ।।  
  
evaṃ parasparotpannā dhāryante ca parasparān।  
ādhārādheyabhāvena vikārāste vikāriṇaḥ ।। 49.172 ।।  
  
pṛthvyādayo vikārāste paricchinnāḥ parasparam।  
parasparādhikāścaiva praviṣṭāśca parasparam ।। 49.173 ।।  
  
yasmādviṣṭāśca te'nyonyaṃ tasmāt sthairyamupāgatāḥ।  
prāgāsan hyaviśeṣāstu viśeṣānyonyaveśanāt।  
pṛthivyādyāśca vāyvantāḥ paricchinnāstrayastu te ।। 49.174 ।।  
  
guṇāpacayasāreṇa paricchedo viśeṣataḥ।  
śeṣāṇāṃ tu paricchedaḥ saukṣmyānneha vibhāvyate ।। 49.175 ।।  
  
bhūtebhyaḥ paratastebhyo hyālokaḥ parataḥ smṛtaḥ।  
bhūtānyāloka ākāśe paricchinnāni sarvaśaḥ ।। 49.176 ।।  
  
pātre mahati pātrāṇi yathaivāntargatāni tu।  
bhavantyanyonyahīnāni parasparasamāśrayāt।  
tathā hyāloka ākāśe bhedāstvantargatā matāḥ ।। 49.177 ।।  
  
kṛtsnānyetāni catvāri anyonyasyādhikāni tu ।  
yāvadetāni bhūtāni tāvadutpatti rucyate ।। 49.178 ।।  
  
jantūnāmiha saṃskāro bhūteṣvantargato matāḥ।  
pratyākhyāya ca bhūtāni kāryotpattirna vidyate ।। 49.179 ।।  
  
tasmātparimitā bhedāḥ smṛtāḥ kāryātmakāstu te।  
kāraṇātmakāstathaiva syurbhedā ye mahadādayaḥ ।। 49.180 ।।  
  
ityeṣa sanniveśo vo mayā prokto vibhāgaśaḥ।  
saptadvīpasamudrāyā yāthātathyena vai dvijāḥ ।। 49.181 ।।  
  
vistārānmaṇjalāccaiva prasaṃkhyātena caiva hi ।  
vaiśvarūpaṃ pradhānasya parimāṇaikadeśikam ।। 49.182 ।।  
  
adiṣṭānaṃ bhaganato yasya sarvamidaṃ jagat ।  
evaṃ bhūtagaṇāḥ sapta sanniviṣṭāḥ parasparam ।। 49.183 ।।  
  
etāvān sanniveśastu mayā śakyaḥ prabhāvitu m।  
etāvadeva śrotavyaṃ sanniveśe tu pārthiva ।। 49.184 ।।  
  
sapta prakṛtayastvetā dhārayanti parasparam।  
tāsvalpaparimāṇena prasaṅkhyātumihocyate।  
asaṅkhyeyāḥ prakṛtayastiryagūrddhvamadhaśca yāḥ ।। 49.185 ।।  
  
tārakāsanniveśaśca yāvaddivyaṃ tu maṇjalam।  
maryādāsanniveśastu bhūmestadanumaṇḍalam।  
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi pṛthivyāṃ vai dvijottamāḥ ।। 49.186 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte bhuvanavinyāso nāmaikonapañcāśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 49 ।
