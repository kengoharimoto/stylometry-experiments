pañcame 'dhyāyaḥ

strīṇāṃ śubhalakṣaṇavarṇanam

susaṃtur uvāca
ṣaṭtriṃśad ābdikaṃ caryaṃ gurau traivedikaṃ vratam
tad ardhikaṃ pādikaṃ vā grahaṇaṃtikam eva ca||

vedān adhītya vedau vā vedaṃ vāpi nṛpottama
aviplutabrahmacaryo gṛhasthāśramam āvaset||

taṃ pratītaṃ svadharmeṇa brahmadāyaharaṃ pituḥ
sragviṇaṃ talpa āsīnam arhayet prathamaṃ gavā||

guruṇā samanujñātaḥ samāvṛtto yathāvidhi
udvaheta dvijo bhāryāṃ savarṇaṃ lakṣaṇānvitām||

śatānīka uvāca

lakṣaṇaṃ divjaśārdūla strīṇaṃ vada mahāmune
kīdṛglakṣaṇasaṃyuktā kanyā syātsukhadā nṛpa||

sumaṃtur uvāca

yaduktaṃ brahmaṇā pūrvaṃ strīlakṣaṇam anuttamam
śreyase sarvalokānāṃ śubhāsubhaphalapradam||

tate vacmi mahābāho śṛṇuṣvekamanā nṛpa
śrutena yena jānīṣe kanyāṃ śobhanalakṣaṇām||

sukhāsīnaṃ suraśreṣṭhamabhigamya maharṣayaḥ
papracchur lakṣaṇaṃ striiṇaaṃ yatpṛṣṭo 'haṃ tvayādhunā||

praṇamya 'sirasā devam idaṃ vacanam aburvan
bhagavanbrūhi naḥ sarvaṃ strīṇāṃ lakṣaṇam uttamam||

"reyase sarvalokānāṃ śubhāśubhaphalapradam
praśastām apraśastāṃ ca jānīmo yena kanyakām||

teṣāṃ tadvacanaṃ śrutvā viriṃco vākyam abravīt
śṛṇudhvaṃ dvijaśārdūlā vacmi yuṣmāsv aśeṣataḥ||

pratiṣṭhitatalau samyagraktāṃ bhojasamaprabhau
īdṛśau caraṇau dhanyau yoṣitāṃ bhogavardhanau||

karālairati nirmāṃsai rūkṣair ardhaśirānvitaiḥ
dāridyaṃ durbhagatvaṃ ca prāpnuvaṃti na saṃśayaḥ||

aṃgulyaḥ saṃhatā vṛttāḥ snigdhāḥ sūkṣmanakhās tathā
kurvaṃtyatyaṃ tamaiśvaryaṃ rājabhāvaṃ ca yoṣitaḥ||

hasvāḥ sujīvitaṃ hasvā viralā vittahānaye
dāridraṃ mūlam agāsu preṣyaṃ ca pṛthulāsu ca||

paraspara samārūḍhais tanubhir vṛttaparvabhiḥ
bahūn api patīnhatvā dāsī bhavati vai dvijāḥ||

aṃguṣṭhonnataparvāṇas tuṃgāgrāḥ komalānvitāṃ
ratnakāṃcanalābhāya viparītā vipattaye||

subhagatvaṃ nakhaiḥ snigdhair ātāmraiś ca dhanāḍhyatā
putāḥ syur unnatair ebhiḥ susūkṣmaiś cāpi rājatā||

pāṃḍūraiḥ sphuṭitai rūkṣair nīlair dhūmrais tathā svaraiḥ
niḥsvatā bhavati strīṇaṃ pītaiś cābhakṣyabhakṣaṇam||

gulphāḥ snigdhāś ca vṛttāś ca samārūḍhaśirās tathā
yadi syur nūpurāndadhyurbāṃdhavādyaiḥ samāpnuyuḥ||

aśirāḥ śarakāṃḍābhāḥ suvṛttālpatanūruhāḥ
jaṃghāḥ kurvaṃti saubhāgyaṃ yānaṃ ca gajavājibhiḥ||

kliśyate roma jaṃghā strī bhramaty uddhatapiṃḍikā
kākajaṃghā patiṃ hanti vācāṭā kapilā ca yā||

jānubhiścaiva mārjārasiṃhajānvanukāribhiḥ
śriyamāpyasubhāgyatvaṃ prāpnuvaṃti sutāṃstathā||

gha¯tābhairadhvagā nāryo nirmaṃsaiḥ kulaṭāḥ striyaḥ
śirālair api hiṃsrāḥ syur viśliṣṭair dhanavarjitāḥ||

atyaṃtakuṭilai rūkṣaiḥ sphuṭitāgrair guḍaprabhaiḥ
anekajais tathā romaiḥ keśaiś cāpi tathāvidhaiḥ||

atyantapiṃgalā nārī viṣatulyeti niścitam
saptāhābhyantare pāpā patiṃ hanyān na samśayaḥ||

hastihastanibhair vṛttai raṃbhābhaiḥ karamopamaiḥ
prāpnuvaṃty ūrubhiḥ śaśvatstriyaḥ sukhamanaṃgajam||

daurbhārgyaṃ baddhamāṃsaiś ca bandhanaṃ romaśorubhiḥ
tanubhir vadhamityāhurmadhyacchidreṣvanīśatā

sandhyāvarṇaṃ samaṃ cāru sūkṣmaromānvitaṃ pṛthu
jaghanaṃ śasyate strīṇāṃ ratisaukhyakaraṃ dvija||

aromako bhago yasyāḥ samaḥ suśliṣṭasaṃsthitaḥ
api nīcakulotpannā rājapatnī bhavaty asau||

aśvatthapatrasadṛśaḥ kūrmapṛṣṭhonnatas tathā
śaśibiṃbanibhaś cāpi tathaiva kalaśākṛtiḥ
bhagaḥ śastatamaḥ strīṇaṃ ratisaubhāgyavardhanaḥ||

tilapuṣpanibho yaś ca yady agre khurasaṃnibhaḥ
dvāvapyetau parapreṣyaṃ kurvāte ca daridratām||

ulūkhalanibhaiḥ śokaṃ maraṇaṃ vivṛtānanaiḥ
virūpaiḥ pūtinirmāṃsair gajasaṃnibharomabhiḥ
dauḥśīlyaṃ durbhagatvaṃ ca dāridryamadhigacchati||

kapitthaphalasaṃkāśaḥ pīno valivarjitaḥ
sphītaḥ praśasyate strīṇāṃ niṃditaś cānyathā dvijāḥ||

payodharabharānamrapracalattrivalīguruḥ
madhyaḥ śubhāvahaḥ strīṇāṃ romarājīvibhūṣitaḥ||

paṇavābhair mṛdaṃgābhais tathā madhye yavopamaiḥ
prāpnuvaṃti bhayāvāsakleśadauḥ śīlyam īdṛśaiḥ||

avakrānulbaṇaṃ pṛṣṭhamaromaśamagarhitam
nānāstaraṇaparyaṃkaratisaukhyakaraṃ param||

kubjamadroṇikaṃ pṛṣṭhaṃ romaśaṃ yadi yoṣitaḥ
svapnāṃtare sukhaṃ tasyā nāsti hanyāt patiṃ ca sā||

vipulaiḥ sukumāraiś ca kukṣibhiḥ subahuprajāḥ
maṇḍūkakukṣir yā nārī rājānaṃ sā prasūyate||

unnatair balibhir vaṃdhyāḥ suvṛtaiḥ kulaṭāḥ striyaḥ
jārakarmaratās tāḥ syuḥ pravrajyāṃ ca samāpnuyuḥ||

unnatā ca nataiḥ kṣudrā viṣamair viṣamāśayā
āyuraiśvaryasaṃpannā vanitā hṛdayaiḥ samaiḥ||

suvṛttam unnataṃ pīnam adūronnatam āyatam
stanayugmam idaṃ śastam ato .anyad asukhāvaham||

unnatiḥ prathame garbhe dvayor ekasya bhūyasī
vāme tu jāyate kanyā dakṣiṇe tu bhavet sutaḥ||

dīrghe tu cūcuke yasyāḥ sā strī dhūrtā ratipriyā
suvṛtte tu punar yasyā dveṣṭi sā puruṣaṃ sadā||

stanaiḥ sarpaphaṇākāraiḥ śvajihvākṛtibhis tathā
dāridryam adhigacchaṃti stiryaḥ puruṣaveṣṭitāḥ
avaṣṭabdhaghaṭītulyā bhavaṃti hi tathā dvijāḥ||

susamaṃ māṃsalaṃ cāru śiro romavivarjitam
vakṣo yasyā bhavennāryā bhogānbhukte yathepsitān||

hiṃsrā bhavati vakreṇa dauḥśīlyaṃ romaśena tu
nirmāṃsena tu vaidhavyaṃ vistīrṇe kalahapriyā||

catasro raktagambhīrā rekhāḥ snigdhāḥ kare striyāḥ
yadi syuḥ sukham āpnoti vicchinnābhiranīśatā||

rekhāḥ kaniṣṭhikāmūlādyasyāḥ praptāḥ (sic.) pradeśinīm
śatamāyurbhavet tasyāstrayāṇām unnatau kramāt||

saṃvṛttāḥ samaparvāṇas tīkṣaṇāgrāḥ komalatvacaḥ
samā hyaṃgulayo yasyāḥ sā nārī bhogavardhinī||

bandhujīvāruṇais tuṃgair nakhair aiśvaryam āpnuyāt
kharair vakrair vivarṇābhaiḥ śvetaprītair anīśatā||
