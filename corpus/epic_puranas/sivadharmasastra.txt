atha paśupatimate śivadharmaśāstre śivabhaktirnāma prathamo 'adhyāyaḥ
śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ oṃ namaḥ śivāya
devadeva mahādeva śāntaṅkamalalocana
jagadārtiharaṃ devaṃ śūlapāṇinnamostute
namas tuṅgaśiraścumbivandracāmaracārabhe
trailokyanagarārambhe mūlastambhāya śambhave
sarvakāraṇaśeṣasya jagataḥ sarvadā śivam
gobrāhmaṇanṛpāṇāñca śivaṃ bhavatu sarvataḥ
śivamādau śivaṃ madhye śivamante ca sarvadā
sarveṣāṃ śivabhaktānāṃ manujānāñca naḥ śivam
merupṛṣṭhe sukhāsinamṛṣisaṃghaiḥ samāvṛtam
lokānugrahakartāraṃ sarvajñaṃ nandikeśvaram
teṣāṃ madhye samutthāya munibrahmasutottamaḥ
sanatkumāraḥ sutapāḥ pṛcchati sma yathāvidhiḥ
sanatkumāra uvāca
bhagavan sarvadharmajña śivadharmaparāyaṇaḥ
śrotukāmāḥ paraṃ dharmamimaṃ sarve samāgatāḥ
agniṣṭomādayo yajñā bahuvittakṛyānvitāḥ
nātyantaphalabhūyiṣṭhā bahvāyāsasamanvitāḥ
na śakyante yataḥ kartum alpavittairdvijātibhiḥ
sukhopāyamato brūhi sarvakāmārthasādhakam
hitāya sarvasatvānāṃ śivadharmaṃ sanātanam
nandikeśvara uvāca
śrūyatāmabhidhāsyāmi sukhopāyamahatphalam
paramasarvadharmāṇāṃ śivadharmaṃ śivātmakam
śivena kathitaṃ pūrvaṃ pārvatyāḥ ṣaṇmukhasya ca
gaṇānāṃ devamukhyānāmasmākañca viśeṣataḥ
ajñānārṇavamagnānāṃ sarveṣāṃ prāṇināmayam
śivadharmāntu yaḥ śrīmān uddhārārthamudāhṛtam
yena yaṃ śāntacetaskaiḥ śivabhaktaiḥ śivārthibhi
saṃsevyate paro dharmaste rudro nātra saṃśayaḥ
ekakālaṃ dvikālaṃ vā trikālaṃ nityameva vā
ye smaranti virūpākṣaṃ vijñeyāste gaṇeśvarāḥ
kīrtayiṣyanti ye rudraṃ sakṛd apy aśubhair narāḥ
sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyante saptajanmakṛtair api
ye 'arcayanti sadā rudraṃ na te prakṛti mānuṣāḥ
rudralokāt paribhraṣṭāste rudrāste rudrā nātra saṃśayaḥ
jātenātmadruhā yena nārcito bhagavan śivaḥ
suciraṃ saṃsaratyasmin asāre duḥkhasāgare
sahānistatmahacchidraṃ samohaḥ sāndhamūkatā
yaḥ kṣaṇaṃ vā muhūrtaṃ vā śarvameko nu cintayet
durlabhaṃ prāpya mānuṣyaṃ ye 'arcayanti sadā śivam
teṣāṃ hi saphalaṃ janma kṛtārthāste narottamāḥ
śivabhaktiparā ye tu bhavapraṇatamānasaḥ
bhavasaṃsmaraṇe yukta na te duḥkhasya bhāginaḥ
bhuvanāni manojñāni vicitrā abharaṇāstriyaḥ
dhanaṃ vā tuṣṭiparyantaṃ śivapūjāvidherphalam
ye vāñchanti mahābhogān rājyañca tridaśātmake
te 'arcayanti sadākālaṃ harasya caraṇāmbujam
saubhāgyakāntimadrūpaṃ satyaṃ tyāgaprabhāvatām
śauryaṃśca jagataḥ khyātiṃ śivamarcayato bhavet
nārudraḥ saṃsmaredrudraṃ nārudro rudramarcayet
nārudraḥ kīrtayedrudraṃ nārudro rudramāpnuyāt
śivadharmasya sāro 'ayaṃ śive bhaktiḥ suniścalā
sā cāṣṭāṅgī śivenoktā kāryā nityaṃ prayatnataḥ
śrībhagavan uvāca
madbhaktajanavātsalyaṃ pūjāyāś cānumodanam
svayaṃ me 'abhyarcanaṃ kuryāt mamārthe cāṅgaceṣṭitam
matkathāśravaṇe bhaktiḥ svaranetrāṅgavikriya
mamānusaṃsmaren nityaṃ yaś ca mām upajīvati
bhaktir aṣṭavidhā hy eṣā yasmin mleccho 'api vartate
sa viprendro muniḥ śrīmān sa yatiḥ sa ca paṇḍitaḥ
na me priyaścaturvedo madbhaktaḥ śvapavo 'api yaḥ
tasmai deyantato grāhyaṃ sa ca pūjyo yathāhyaham
śivabhaktāya yaddānaṃ bhaktyā kiñcit pradīyate
tadahantatra gṛhṇāmi tenānanta taducyate
patraṃ puṣpaṃ phalaṃ toyaṃ yo me bhaktyā prayacchati
tasyāhaṃ na praṇasyāmi sa ca me na praṇasyati
yo māṃ na sarvagamyaścennaraḥ sarva mayi sthitam
sa māṃ parvatadurgeṣu mārgamāṇo vipadyate
yo māṃ sarvagataṃ paśyet sarvaśca mayi saṃsthitaḥ
tasyāhaṃ nityamātmasthaḥ sa ca nityaṃ mayisthitaḥ
nandikeśvaraḥ uvāca
sudurlabhā śive bhaktiḥ durlabhañca śivārcanam
durlabhañca śive dānaṃ śivahomaśca durlabhaḥ
sudurlabhaṃ śivajñānaṃ tadabhyāso 'api durlabhaḥ
sudurlabhataraṃ yajñaṃ japadhyānaṃ śivātmakam
sudurlabhā tapaḥprāptistadabhyāso 'api durlabhaḥ
sudurlabhamidaṃ jñātvā śavabhaktiṃ samāśrayet
ityeṣa vaḥ samāsena śivadharmo 'akhilakramāt
nirdiṣṭaḥ prathame 'adhyāye śeṣo 'asau ca pravistaraḥ
namastebhyo manuṣyebhyo ye śivaṃ śaraṇaṅgatāḥ
yeṣāṃ divā ca rātrau ca nityaṃ śivagatasmṛtiḥ
namaskārādisaṃyuktaṃ śiva ityakṣaradvayam
jihvāgre vartate yasya saphalantasya jīvitam
imaṃ yaḥ paṭhate nityaṃ śṛuṇuyādvāpi bhaktitaḥ
sa muktaḥ sarvapāpaistu śivaloke mahīyate
ḍākinyo vividhākārā rākṣasā pretanāyakāḥ
na tasya pīḍāṅkurvanti tathānyepi hi hiṃsakāḥ
śatravo nāśamāyānti saṅgrāme jayamāpnuyāt
sarvarogairnabādhyate āpado na spṛśanti tam
dhanamāyuryaśavidyāṃ prabhāvamatulaṃ labhet
śubhenopacayaṃ yāti nityaṃ pūrṇamanorathaḥ
iti śivadharmaśāstre śivabhaktirnāma prathamo 'adhyāyaḥ
atha paśupatimate śivadharme mahāśāstre
śivadharmapraśnādhyāyo nāma dvitīyo 'adhyāyaḥ
namaḥ śivāya
sanatkumāra uvāca
liṅgārcanavidhiṃ puṇyaṃ śrotumicchāmi tattvataḥ
tvatprasādādviśeṣeṇa bhagavannandikeśvara
yat puṇyaṃ sthāpite liṅge kṛte yacca śivālaye
sammārjane ca yatpuṇyaṃ yatpuṇyamupalepane
nīrājanauṣadhī bīja snāne maṅgalavādane
puṇyaṃ yaccārghyadānena toyasnānena yadbhavet
pañcagavyapayasnāne dadhisnāne ca yadbhavet
ghṛtasnāne ca yatpuṇyaṃ ghṛtābhyaṅge ca yatphalam
madhusnāne ca yat puṇyaṃ puṇyamikṣurasena ca
udvartanāntarasnāne kuśapuṣpodakena ca
suvarṇaratnatoyaiśca gandhacandanavāribhiḥ
karpūrāgurutoyena svacchatoyena yatphalam
vilepanaiśca gandhādyairvilipte yatphalaṃ labhet
tālavṛntapradānena pradāne cāmarasya ca
muktapuṣpārcane yacca mālayā liṅgapūraṇe
puṣpamaṇḍapakāryaśca puṣpamālāvalambane
pūjābhaktiviśeṣaiśca vedyāṃ mālāvaveṣṭane
puṣpajātiviśeṣeṣu dhūpeṣvapi ca yatphalam
vastrālaṅkāradāne ca dīpadarpaṇadarśane
bhakṣyabhojyānnapānādi phalakandanivedane
pradakṣiṇe namaskāre praṇāmastotrajaṃ phalam
pratākādhvajachatrāṇāṃ ghaṃṭāvāditraśaṃkhayā
vitānavastrapūjāyāṃ balihome kriyāsu ca
gavāṃśca bhṛtyanāgānāṃ bhūmidāne ca yatphalam
grāmakheṭapurāṇāśca daśadāne ca yatphalam
tatpālānāpahāre ca puṇyāpuṇyantu yadbhavet
yatināmannadānāni pātropakaraṇāni ca
datvā kiṃ labhate puṇyaṃ datvā cāpi pratiśrayam
yadapyanyadbhavet sūkṣmamajñānānna pracoditam
tatsarvaṃ kathayāsmākaṃ bhaktānāmanukampayā
iti śivadharmapraśnādhyāyo dvitīyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāya
nandikeśvara uvāca
tvamatīva śive bhaktaḥ padmayonisutottama
tena te nikhilaṃ vakṣye śṛṇuṣvekāgramānasaḥ
pūrvamekārṇave ghore naṣṭe sthāvarajaṅgame
vivādaḥ sumahānāsīd brahmāviṣṇoḥ parasparam
ahaṃ kartā tvahaṃ kartā na madanojagatpatiḥ
evamāha hariṃ brahmā brahmāṇaṃśca haristathā
tayordarpāpahārāya prabodhārthaśca vetayoḥ

viśe devā mahātmāno raupyaṃ liṅgaṃ manoramam
yajanti vidhivadbhaktyā tena viśvatvamāpnuvan

bhāvanāmātrasantuṣṭā umādevi varapradā
sākṣādāgatya rūpeṇa śāntenāmitatejasā
śāntiṃ karotu me prītā bhaktānāṃ bhaktavatsalā
padmarāgadyutiḥ saumye raktamālyānulepanaḥ
abālo bālarūpeṇa ṣaḍvaktraḥ śikhivāhana
pūrṇenduvadanaḥ saumyastriśikhaḥ śaktisaṃyutaḥ
kṛttikomāgnirudrāṃśasamudbhūtaḥ surārcakaḥ
kārtikeyo mahātejo varadānaikatatparaḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me nityaṃ balaṃ saukhyañca me sadā
śvetavastraparīdhānastryakṣaḥ kanakasuprabhaḥ
śūlapāṇi mahāprājño nandīśaḥ śivabhāvitaḥ
śivārcanaparonityaṃ śivadhyānaikatatparaḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me śānto dharme ca matimuttamam
mahodaro mahākāyaḥ snigdhāñjanacayacchaviḥ
ekadaṃṣṭrotkaṭo devo gajavaktro mahābalaḥ
nāgayajñopavīti ca nānābharaṇabhūṣitaḥ
sarvārthasampadādhāro gaṇādhyakṣo varapradaḥ
rudrasya tanayo devo nāyako 'atha vināyakaḥ
karotu me mahāśāntiṃ karmasiddhiñca sarvadā
indranīlanibhastryakṣo dīptaśūlāyudhodyataḥ
raktāmbaradharaḥ śrīmān kṛṣṇāṅgo nāgabhūṣaṇaḥ
pāpāpanodanamalam alakṣmīmalanāśanam
karotu me mahāśāntiṃ mahākālo mahābalaḥ
pītavastraparīdhānā kanyārūpāsvalaṅkṛtā
gaṇamātāmbikāstryakṣā puṇyā gaurīśvareśvarī
sarvasiddhikarī devī prasādaparamāparā
śāntiṃ karotu me mātā siddhaścānu prayacchatu
snigdhaśyāmena varṇena mahāmahiṣamardanam
dhanuścakrapraharaṇā khaḍgapaṭṭisadhāriṇī
ātarjanyudyatakarā sarvopadravanāśanī
sarvamaṅgala mātā me śivamācaratu dhruvam
niemāṃsena śarīreṇa snāyvasthisunibandhanaḥ
atisūkṣmo 'ativikāntastryakṣo bhṛṅgiritirmahān
rudrātmajo mahāvīro rudraikāhitamānasaḥ
sopi me śāntabhāvena śāntimāśu prayacchatu
pracaṇḍagaṇasainyeśo mahāṭaṃkākṣadhārakaḥ
akṣamālārpitakara tryakṣaścaṇḍeśvaro varaḥ
caṇḍapāpāpaharaṇo brahmahatyādiśodhanaḥ
karotu me mahāyogī kalyāṇānāmparamparām
śaṅkhakundendutulyābhaḥ kaṇṭhe marakataprabhaḥ
akṣamālī śivāgrasthaḥ svayaṃ jñānavyavasthitaḥ
caturmukhaścatuḥpādastrinetraḥ sarvadojjvalaḥ
kṣitipatirvṛṣo devo dharmo dharmatamottamaḥ
īśaṃ vahati pṛṣṭhena tasmāddharmo jagatprabhuḥ
vṛṣo vṛṣavaraḥ śrīmān karotu mama śāntikam
padmāsanaḥ padmanibhaḥ caturvadanapaṅkajaḥ
kamaṇḍaludharaḥ śrīmān devagandharvapūjitaḥ
śive dhyānaikaparamaḥ śivasadbhāvabhāvitaḥ
brahmaśabdena divyena brahmaśāntiṃ karotu me
tārkṣāsanaścaturbāhuḥ śaṅkacakragadādharaḥ
śyāmaḥ pītāmbaradharo mahābalaparākramaḥ
yajñadehottamo devo mādhavo madhusūdanaḥ
śivaprasādasampannaḥ śivadhyānaparāyaṇaḥ
sarvapāpapraśamakaḥ sarvāsuranikṛntakaḥ
sarvadā śāntabhāvena viṣṇuḥ śāntiṃ karotu me
ārhantaḥ śāntacetaṣka viśvātman viśvayātitaḥ
digvāsāmalapaṅkaśca saumyacittasamāhitaḥ
saṃvartalocanaḥ śāntaḥ śivajñānaikacintakaḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me śāntaḥ śivayogena bhāvitaḥ
jitendriyaḥ samādhisthaḥ pātracīvarabheṣitaḥ
varadābhayapāṇiśca jñānadhyānarataḥ sadā
yogadṛṣṭiḥ sadāyuktaḥ śivajñānena bhāvitaḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me deva sarvasatvahite rataḥ
pītavarṇena dehena hāreṇa suvicitriṇā
sarvāṅgasundarī devi vijayā jayakāriṇī
śivārcanaratā nityaṃ śivajāpyaparāyaṇā
dharitrī lokamātā ca nityaṃ rakṣāṃ karotu me
kṣīrodādutthito gāvo lokānāṃ hitakāmyayā
prīṇayanti sadā devān viprāṃścaiva viśeṣataḥ
nityaṃ tu devatātmānaḥ kurvantu mama śāntikam
padmarāgaprabhā devi caturvadanapaṅkajā
akṣamālārpitakarā kamaṇḍaludharā śubhā
brahmāṇi saumyavadanā śivapūjāparāyaṇā
śāntiṃ karotu me prītā brahmaśabdena sarvadā
himaśailanibhā devi mahāvṛṣabhavāhanā
triśūlahastā varadā nāgābharaṇabhūṣitā
caturbhujā caturvaktrā trinetrā pāpahāriṇī
ārtiharatu me prītā rudrāṇī nityamujvalā
mayūravāhanā devi sindūrārūṇavigrahā
śaktihastā mahārūpā sarvālaṅkārabhūṣitā
rudrabhaktā mahāvīryā rudrārcanaratā sadā
kaumārī varadā devi śāntimāśu dadātu me
śaṃkhacakragadāhastā śyāmā pitāmbaraprīyā
caturbhujā tārkṣayānā vaiṣṇavī surapūjitā
śivārcanaratā nityaṃ śivaikāhitamānasā
śāntikarotu me nityaṃ sarvāsuravimardinī
airāvatagajārūḍha vajrahastā mahābalā
netrāṇāntu sahasreṇa bhūṣitā kanakaprabhā
siddhagandharvanamitā sarvālaṅkārabhūṣitā
aindrī devī sadākālaṃ śāntimāśu karotu me
varāhaghoṇā vikaṭā varāhavaravāhanā
śyāmāvadātāṃ vipulā śaṃkhacakragadāyudhā
tarjayanī sadā vighān arcayantī sadā śivam
vārāhī varadā devī kṣamārogyaṃ dadātu me
ardhakeśā koṭarākṣā nirmāṃsā snāyubandhanā
karālavadanā ghorā khaṅgakaṭṭārakodyata
kapālamālinī kruddhā khaṭvāṅgavaradhāriṇī
āraktapiṅganayanā gajacarmāvaguṇṭhitā
nānānāgopavītāṅgī pretasthānanivāsinī
śivārūpeṇa ghoreṇa śivārāvabhayaṅkarā
cāmuṇḍā caṇḍarūpeṇa mahārakṣāṃ karotu me
apītena śarīreṇa nānābharaṇabhūṣitā
ātarjayantī vighnāni jvalatkhaṭvāṅgadhāriṇī
dhvāṃkṣāsanā mahāvīrā sarvapāpapraṇāśanī
lambauṣṭhi varadā devī śāntimāśu karotu me
ākāśamātaro divyāstathānyālokamātaraḥ
sarvamātṛmahādevyaḥ sāyudhavyagrapāṇayaḥ
jagadvyāpyāvatiṣṭhante valikāmā mahodayā
rudrabhaktyā mahāvīryā rudrārcā hitamānasā
śāntiṃ kurvantu me nityaṃ mātaraḥ surapūjitāḥ
ye rudrā raudrakarmaṇo rudrasthānanivāsinaḥ
saumyāścaiva tu ye kecit sthāṇusthānanivāsinaḥ
mātaro rudrarūpāśca gaṇānāmadhipaśca ye
vighnabhūtāstathā cānye digvidikṣu samāśritāḥ
sarve suprītamanasaḥ pratigṛhṇantu me balim
siddhimāśu prayacchantu bhayebhyaḥ pāntu māṃ sadā
aindryāṃ diśi gaṇā ye tu vajrahastā mahābalāḥ
suśvetākṣāḥ śvetanibhāstathā vai śvetalohitāḥ
dvyāntarikṣa bhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
rudrapraṇāmamanasaḥ śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
yāmyāndiśi gaṇā ye tu satataṃ daṇḍapāṇayaḥ
kṛṣṇaḥ kṛṣṇanibhāḥ kruddhāstathā vai kṛṣṇalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
rudraikāhitacetaskā śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
nairṛtyāntu gaṇāḥ krūrā rākṣasā mṛtyurūpiṇaḥ
sunīlākṣā nīlanibhāḥ tathā vai nīlalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
rudrasya dhyānaniratāḥ śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
vāruṇyāṃ vai gaṇā ye tu satataṃ pāśapāṇayaḥ
suśyāmākṣāḥ śyāmanibhāsthā vai śyāmalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivabhaktiparāḥ sarve kurvantu mama śāntikam
vāyavyāntu gaṇā ye tu satataṃ dhvajapāṇayaḥ
supītākṣāḥ pītanibhāstathā vai pītalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivabhaktiparāḥ sarve kṣamaṃ kurvantu me sadā
uttarasyāṃ gaṇā ye tu satataṃ nidhipāṇayaḥ
śavalākṣā śavalanibhāstathā vai śavalalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣa bhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivapūjāsamudyuktā kṣemaṃ kurvantu me sadā
aiśānyāṃ vai gaṇā ye tu praśāntāḥ śūlapāṇayaḥ
sūkṣmāḥ susūkṣmasadṛśāstathā vai sūkṣmalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivapūjāsamudyuktā kṣemaṃ kurvantu me sadā
adhobhāge gaṇā ye tu satataṃ śūlapāṇayaḥ
dhūmrā dhūmranibhāssarve tathā vai dhūmralohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivaṃ kurvantu me nityamalakṣmīmalanāśanam
ardhabhāgebhāge gaṇā ye tu mahābalaprarākramāḥ
susūkṣmākṣāḥ sūkṣmanibhāstathā vai sūkṣmalohitāḥ
divyantarīkṣabhaumāśca pātālatalavāsinaḥ
śivapūjāsamādyukta aśubhaṃ nāśayantu me
ete gaṇāḥ mahātmāno mahābalaparākramāḥ
śivaṃ sampūjya yatnena balinteṣāṃ vinikṣipet
tataḥ suprītamanasaḥ śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
amarāvatī nāma purī pūrvabhāge vyavasthitā
vidyādharasamākīrṇā siddhagandharvaśobhitā
ratnaprākārarucirā sarvaratnopaśobhitā
tatra devapatiḥ śrīmān vajrapāṇirmahābalaḥ
netrāṇāntu sahasreṇa śobhatena virājate
airāvatagajārūḍho hemavarṇamahādyutiḥ
devendraḥ satataṃ hṛṣṭaḥ parameśārcane rataḥ
śivadhyāne na sampannaḥ śivabhaktisamanvitaḥ
śivapraṇāmaparamaḥ karotu mama śāntikam
āgneyedigvibhāge tu purī tejovatī śubhā
nānādevasamākīrṇā ratnajvālāsamujjvalā
tatra jvālāparītāṅgo dīptāṅgārasamadyutiḥ
bhūmikṛddehināṃ devo jvalanaḥ pāpanāśanaḥ
śivapūjājapodyuktaḥ śivasmaraṇabhāvitaḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me devastathā pāpaparikṣayam
vaivasvatī nāma purī dakṣiṇena vyavasthitā
surāsuraśatānīka pitṛrakṣoragālayāḥ
tatrendranīlasaṃkāśo raktāyatalocanaḥ
mahāmahiṣamārūḍhaḥ kṛṣṇasragvastrabhūṣaṇaḥ
antakotha mahātejaḥ śivadharmaparāyaṇaḥ
śivapūjāsamudyuktaḥ kṣemārogyaṃ dadātu me
nairṛte tu diśo bhāge purā kṛṣṇeti viśrutāḥ
mahārakṣogaṇākīrṇā piśācabhūtasaṃkulā
tatra jīmūtasaṃkāśo raktasragvastrabhūṣaṇaḥ
khaḍgapāṇirmahātejāḥ karālavadanojvalāḥ
jakṣendro nairṛtirnityaṃ śivārcanarataḥ sadā
karotu me mahāśāntiṃ śivaśaktisamutsukaḥ
paścime tu diśo bhāge purī śuddhāvatī śubhā
nānāgaṇasamākīrṇā nānākinnaraśobhitā
tatra mauktikasaṃkāśaḥ paripiṅgalalocanaḥ
paṭavyāptāntarālīno dhvajachatrāyudhodyotaḥ
śuklavastraparīdhānaḥ pāśahasto mahābalaḥ
varuṇaḥ parayā bhaktyā śivaikahitamānasaḥ
rogaśokārtisantāpannityaṃ nirṇāśayantu me
vāyavye digbhāge tu purīgandhavatī śubhā
ṛṣisiddhagaṇādhyuṣṭā hemaprākāratoraṇā
tiṣṭhantāmreṇa dehena kṛṣṇapiṅgalalocanaḥ
pavanaḥ paramo devaḥ parameśvarabhāvitaḥ
kṣamārogyaṃ balaṃ śāntiṃ karotu satataṃ mama
mahodayā nāma purī uttareṇa mahojvalā
anekayakṣasaṃkīrṇā nānāratnopaśobhitā
tatra devo gadāhastaścitrasragvastrabhūṣaṇaḥ
hrasvabāhurmahātejāḥ paripiṅgalalocanaḥ
kubero varadaḥ śrīmān harapādārcane rataḥ
śāntiṃ karotu me śāntaḥ prīta prītena tejasā
yaśovatī purī ramyā iśānīndiśamāśritā
nānāgaṇasamākīṇa nānāsurakṛtālayā
tejaḥ prākāraparyantā niraupamyā mahodayā
yatra mauktikasaṃkāśaḥ śaśāṅkakṛtabhūṣaṇaḥ
trinetraśāntarūpātmā akṣamālādharoharaḥ
īśānaḥ paramo devaḥ sarvadevottamottamaḥ
so 'api sarvātmabhāvena śāntimāśu prayacchatu
bhūrloke 'atha bhuvarloke svarloke nivasanti ye
devatā divyasaṃyuktāḥ śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
maharloke janoloke tapoloke sthitāśca ye
te 'api pramuditā devāḥ śivaṃ kurvantu me sadā
satyaloke ca ye devāḥ prabhājojvalavigrahāḥ
śivabhaktāḥ sumanaso bhayaṃ nirṇāśayantu me
girikandaradurgeṣu vareṣu nivasanti ye
rudrārcanaparā devā rakṣāṃ kurvantu me sadā
mahātale ca santi sma rasātaleṣu saṃsthitāḥ
talātale ca ye santi ye santi sutale janāḥ
nitale saṃsthitā bhūtvā vitale nivasanti ye
tale ca nilayāye ca rakṣāṃ kurvantu sarvadā
śaraccandrāṃśugaureṇa dehenāmalatejasā
sarasvatī śive bhaktā śāntimāśu karotu me
cāru cāmīkarahastā sarojotpalapallavā
śivabhaktā tu śrīdevī śrīmadbhūtiṃ dadātu me
caraṇā mukhacandreṇa vicitrakusumojvalā
jayādevī śive bhaktā sarvakāmandadātu me
hāreṇa suvicitreṇa bhāskarojvalatejasā
rudrasutā aparājitā karotu vijayaṃ mama
sindūrarājaratnena karṇāntāyatalocanaḥ
kiraṇadvayasaṃyuktaḥ saptasaptikavāhanaḥ
gabhastimālī bhagavan śivapūjārcane rataḥ
karotu me mahāśāntiṃ grahapīḍanivāraṇī
jagadāpyāyanakara amṛtādhāraśītalaḥ
somaḥ saumyena bhāvena grahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
padmarāganibhāṅgena dehena piṅgalalocanā
aṅgārakantu me nityaṃ grahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
kuṅkumacchavidehena cāpodyatakaraḥ sadā
śivabhakto budhaḥ śrīmān grahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
dhātucāmīkaracchāḥ sarvajñānakṛtālayaḥ
bṛhaspatiḥ sadākālam īśānārcanatatparaḥ
sā api me śāntacittena paramena samāhitaḥ
grahapīḍāṃ vinirjitya karotu vijayaṃ sadā
himakundendutulyābhaḥ suradaityendrapūjitaḥ
śukraḥ śivārcanarato grahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
bhinnāñjanacayacchāyaḥ saraktanayanadyutiḥ
śanaiścara śive bhakto grahapīḍāṃ vyapahatu
nīlāñjananibhaḥ śrīmān saiṃhikeyomahabalaḥ
śivapūjāparo rāhurgrahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
dhūmākārograhaḥ keturaiśānyāṃ diśi saṃsthitaḥ
vartulātīvavistīrṇe locane ca subhīṣaṇaḥ
palālabhūmasaṃkāśo grahapīḍāpahārakaḥ
ghoradraṃṣṭro karālaśca karotu vijayaṃ mama
khaṅgasphaṭikahastā ca vareṇyo varadaḥ śubhaḥ
śivabhaktaśca janmā ca grahapīḍāṃ vyapohatu
ete grahā mahātmāno maheśārcanabhāvitāḥ
śāntiṃ kurvantu me hṛṣṭāḥ sadākālaṃ hitaiṣiṇaḥ
mukhe yasya sthito mṛtyuḥ vṛṣṭirnāma mahābalā
ṣaṇmukhāvighnakārī ca pucche ca vijayaṃkarī
tṛtīyā saptamī caiva daśamī tu caturdaśī
caturthī cāṣṭamī caiva ekādaśī tu pūrṇimā
eteṣu vighnapātreṣu kṛṣṇe śukle samāvahā
kurvantu me labdhavarāḥ śāntiśca paramipsitam
amāvāsī mahāpuṇyā pitṛdevasamanvitā
śāntiśca paramāṃ puṇyāṃ śivasya paramātmanaḥ
śivatejaḥ samāyuktāḥ karotu mama śāntikam
pratipacca mahāśāntā dvitīyā ca manoharā
tṛtīyā ca tithiḥ śrīmān caturthī ca mahāyaśāḥ
pañcamī nāma śāntātmā ṣaṣṭhī ca tithiruttamā
saptamī ca tithiḥ puṇyā aṣṭamī ca mahābalā
titithiḥ śūlabhṛtacaiṣā pāpahā paramā smṛtā
navamī tithiratyugrā durgrāhyā parikīrtitāḥ
daśamī śobhanā caiva tithi ekādaśī tathā
dvādaśī caiva śāntātmā tathā tithi trayodaśī
caturdaśī mahāvīryātithi śaṅkarajātmajā
pūrṇamāsyā paripūrṇātmā tithayaḥ satatojvalā
satatantu śubhātmānasthithayeśca krameṇa tu
pakṣadvaye sadā hyete candragatyānugāminaḥ
śāntiṃ kurvantu me nityaṃ śivājñānuvidhāyinaḥ
viṣkumbhaḥ pritirāyuṣmān saubhāgyaśobhanastathā
atigaṇḍaḥ sukarmā ca dhṛtiḥ śūlasthathaiva ca
gaṇḍo vṛddhirdhruvaścaiva vyāghāto harṣaṇastathā
vajraḥ siddhirvyatīpāto variyān parighaḥ śivaḥ
siddhiḥ sādhyaḥ śubhaḥ śuklo brahma aindraśca vai dhṛti
candrasya bhānoścotpannā yogāścaite mahābalāḥ
śivabhaktiparāḥ sarve śivājñānuvidhāyinaḥ
śāntiṃ kurvantu me nityaṃ tathā kilviṣanāśanam
kṛttikā paramā devī rohiṇī rucirānanā
śrīmanmṛgaśirābhadrā ārdrā ca paramojvalā
punarvasustathā puṣyā aśleṣā atha mahābalā
nakṣatramātaro hyetāḥ prabhāmālāvibhūṣitāḥ
mahādevārcane saktā mahādevānubhāvitāḥ
pūrvabhāge sthitā hyetāḥ śāntiṃ kurvantu me sadā
māghā sarvaguṇopetā pūrvā caiva tu phālguṇī
uttarā phālguṇī śreṣṭhā hastā citrā tathottamā
svātirviśākhā varadā dakṣiṇasthānasaṃśritā
arcayanti mahākālaṃ devaṃ tribhuvaneśvaram
nakṣatramātaro hyetāstejasā paribhūṣitāḥ
mamāpi śāntikaṃ nityaṃ kurvantu śivacoditāḥ
anurādhā tathā jyeṣṭhā mūlaṛddhibalānvitā
pūrvāṣāḍhā mahāvīryā āṣāḍhā cottarā śubhā
abhijinnātmana kṣetraṃ śravaṇā paramojvalā
etā paścimato dīptā rājante rājamūrtayaḥ

atha paśupatimate śivadharme mahāśāstre nandikeśvaraprokte
śivabhaktyādya śākhopaśākhā dvādaśo 'adhyāyaḥ

nandikeśvara uvāca
atha saṃkṣepato vakṣye śivabhaktānukampayā
ādyaśākhopaśākhārthaṃ śivabhaktisamudbhavam

snānaivedyavastrādyan nādadīta priyāṇyapi
tyaktanacchivamuddiśya taddānenaiva tatphalam
catuṣpathaṃ nadīṃ śailaṃ śivayoginameva ca
avatīrya namaskuryād dṛṣṭvā liṅgaṃ dṛdhavrataḥ
hastiyānāśvayānebhyaḥ śivayogitapasvinam
tatrastho 'pi namaskṛtya śivaloke mahīyate
mṛgāśvoṣṭrapayo matsyamāṃsalaśunagṛñjanam
kavakādyāni vīryañca kutsitañca vivarjayet
sarvadā nogratapasāṃ aśvamedhāyutasya ca
phalaṃ prāpnotyayatnena matsyamāṃsavivarjanāt
sarvadoṣāśrayaṃ madyamatidūrād vivarjayet
kva madyaṃ kva śive bhaktiḥ kva śaucaṃ kva śivārcanam
sarvatīrtheṣu yatpuṇyaṃ sarvayajñeṣu yatphalam
tatphalaṃ labhate sarvaṃ yo madyaṃ parivarjayet
garbhādijanmasaṃskāraśmaśānāntādibhojanam
rajasvalābhisaṃspṛṣṭaṃ śivabhakto vivarjayet
na gobrāhmaṇabhasmāgniliṅgacchāyāṃ padaiḥ spṛśet
na laṅghayeta nirmālyaṃ nāpsu tiṣṭeta nagnakaḥ
dhārayecchivanirmālyaṃ bhaktyā lobhānna bhakṣayet
bhakṣaṇānnarakaṃ gacchet tadvilaṅghya tvadhagatim
na tatra snānapūjādyaṃ pratigṛhṇāni śaṅkaraḥ
yatra naivedyanirmālyam alaṅghya pūjayecchivam
bhūpradeśaṃ yathā kaścit svadehamaladūṣitam
nāśrayeta tathā rudro na taṃ nirmālyadūṣitam
asaṃspṛśyo bhavet so 'api yathā svāṅgamalaspṛśī
tasmācca na spṛśelliṅgaṃ na te nirmālyadūṣitaḥ
yatra naivedyanirmālyaṃ saṅkīrṇaṃ kalaśādibhiḥ
snānantu kriyate bhaktyā na taṃ gṛhṇāti śaṅkaraḥ
mūrtayo yāḥ śivasyāṣṭau tāsu nindāṃ vivarjayet
gurośca śivabhaktānāṃ nṛpasādhutapasvinām
aṣṭamyādis u sarveṣu pakṣayorubhayorapi
śmaśrukarmākṣunābhyaṅgaprasthānāni vivarjayet
kuryād viśeṣataḥ pūjāṃ naktaṃ cātra samācaret
strīdyūtamadyamāṃsādi yatnāt parvasu varjayet
trikālamekakālaṃ vā nityaṃ snānaṃ samācaret
śaucañca mṛjjalaiḥ kuryād gandhalepāpanuttaye
yo 'aśnīyādannamasnātaḥ pāpamadyāt sa kevalam
tasmāt snātvā prabhuñjīta bhasmanā salilena vā
prātaḥ snātvāmbhasā gacched bhasmanā vā śivaṃ padam
pāpakañcukamutsṛjya kulasaptakamuddharet
kuryāt praticaturmāsaṃ mahāpūjāpravarddhanam
bhojanaṃ ca pradātavyaṃ śaktyā dānaśca kalpayet
trisaptakulasaṃyuktaḥ samastaiśvaryasaṃyutaḥ
gacchecchivapuraṃ ramyaṃ cāturmāsapravartanāt
daive parvotsave śrāddhe puṇyeṣu divaseṣu ca
śivaṃ sampūjya naivedyair bhojayecchivayoginaḥ
pitaraḥ sarvadevāśca śivamāśritya saṃsthitāḥ
prīte śive tu te sarve suprītāḥ syurna saṃśayaḥ
jñānavairāgyasampannaṃ bhasmaniṣṭhaṃ jitendriyam
bhojayecchāntamanasaṃ śrāddheṣu śivayoginam
yadā svarāditattvānāṃ parijñānaṃ vivekataḥ
bhavet sādharmyavaidharmyāt tat syājjñānasya lakṣaṇam
nāniṣṭaṃ dveṣṭi samprāptanna ceṣṭam abhinandati
praītitāpaviṣādānāṃ vairāgyamativartanam
bhasmaniṣṭhasya divase darśanenāpi yatphalam
na tacchakyaṃ gṛhasthena prāptuṃ kratuśatairapi
vaśyendriyaḥ prasannātmā na ca dviṣṭi na kāṅkṣati
śabdādiviṣayān bhogān sa vijñeyo jitendriyaḥ
jñānavairāgyasampannaḥ śivaśāntena cetasā
yuktaḥ ṣaḍaṅgayogena śivayogī prakīrtitaḥ
śivadharmodbhavaṃ jñānaṃ jñānādvairāgyasambhavaḥ
jñānavairāgyayuktasya śivayogaḥ pravartate
śivayogāśca sarvajñaḥ paripūrṇāstu nityaśaḥ
svātmanyavasthitaḥ śuddhaḥ śivavat sa śivo bhavet
sarveṣāmeva bhūtānām uttamaḥ puruṣaḥ smṛtaḥ
puruṣebhyo dvijaḥ śreṣṭho dvijebhyo granthapāragaḥ
granthajñādvedaviduṣastebhyastattvārthacintakaḥ
nānātattvavidarthajñāt pratibuddho viśiṣyate
nānātattvajñakoṭibhyo viśiṣṭāḥ śivayoginaḥ
yogināṃ śatakoṭibhyaḥ paramaḥ śivayogirāṭ
yogajñā yoganiṣṭhāśca pitaro yogasambhavāḥ
bhukte priyanti te sarve viśeṣācchivayogibhiḥ
sarvayajñatapodānaiḥ kṛtairdattaiśca yatphalam
tatphalaṃ labhate sarvaṃ sambhojya śivayoginam
yaścāgnirudrakālātmā dakṣiṇāhavanistathā
ṛgyajuḥ sāmasaṃyogairvedayajña prakīrtitaḥ
brahmacaryaṃ tapo maunaṃ kṣāntirāhāralāghavam
ityetattapasāṃ rūpaṃ sughoraṃ pañcalakṣaṇam
yadiṣṭañca viśiṣṭaśca nyāyapātrañca yadbhavet
tattadguṇavate deyam ityetaddānalakṣaṇam
nivartanasahasrāḍhyāṃ sarvasasyaprarohiṇīm
dadyādbhūmiṃ jalopetāṃ bhūmidānaṃ taducyate
ekacchatrāṃ mahīṃ kṛtvā dvijebhyo pratipādayet
sampūrṇāṃ yo nṛpaḥ kaścid bhūmidānaṃ taducyate
ahorātraṃ na bhuñjīta copavāsasya lakṣaṇam
catvāriṃśasamāyuktaṃ piṇḍānāṃ hi śatadvayam
niyamādyadyathākāmamiti cāndrāyaṇaṃ smṛtam
ṛṣibhiḥ sarvaśāstrajñais taponiṣṭhair jitendriyaiḥ
devaiś ca sevitaṃ tīrthaṃ kṣitau tīrthan tad ucyate
rudrāvatārasthānāni puṇyakṣetrāṇi nirdiśet
mṛtānān teṣu rudratvaṃ śivakṣetreṣu dehinām
kṣamā aspṛhā dayā satyaṃ dānaṃ śīlantapaḥ śrutam
etad antāṅgam uddiṣṭaṃ paraṃ pātrasya lakṣaṇam
yajñopavāsadānāni tapastīrthaphalāni ca
sampūjya vibhavair bhaktyā labhate śivayoginam
śive bhaktiḥ kṣamā satyam aśeṣendriyasaṃyamaḥ
śivabhakteṣu maitrī ca śivadharmasya lakṣaṇam
śivayogī śivajñānī śivadharmarataśca yaḥ
ity etat trividhaṃ jñeyaṃ śivapātrasya laks aṇam
śivam uddiśya dātavyam anapekṣya gṛṇāśrayam
vittamīśvarabhaktena śivadānasya lakṣaṇam
śivabhaktaṃ dvijaśreṣṭhaṃ yaḥ śrāddhādiṣu bhojayet
kulasaptakam uddhṛtya śivaloke mahīyate
bahunātra kim uktena śivabhaktan tu bhojayet
tasmācchrāddhe viśeṣeṇa puṇyeṣu divaseṣu ca
śivamuddhiśya viprendraṃ śivabhaktantu bhojayet
asaṃyataḥ saṃyato vā sarvāvasthāṅgato 'api vā
yo vā so vā sa vā śive bhakta śivavatpātramuttamam
samparkādarthalābhādvā pāpī rudraṃ prapūjayet
gṛheśrāddhasya yatpuṇyam araṇye tacchavādhikam
śivāśrameṣu vijñeyaṃ tatpuṇyamayutādhikam
nivedayīta yaḥ saumyamāsanaṃ dṛdu vetrajam
nāgadantamayaṃ vāpi siṃhāsanapatirbhavet
kaupīnacchādanaṃ vāso nivedya śivayogine
yugakoṭisahasrāṇi rudraloke mahīyate
aurṇaṃ prāvaraṇaṃ datvā vikīrṇaṃ śivayogine
lomni lomni suvarṇasya dattasya phalamāpnuyāt
mṛgadvīpyādikaṃ dharma tilapiṣṭavimarditam
mṛduraktakaṣāyeṇa nivedya śivayogine
yāvattadromasaṃkhyānaṃ mṛgadvīpyādicarmaṇi
tāvadvarṣasahasrāṇi rudraloke mahīyate
jalasthalasthapatrāṇi yo dadyācchivayogine
trāṇārthaṃ sarvajantūnāṃ so 'abdalakṣaṃ vaseddivi
triveṣṭāmbhaḥ samāyuktaḥ yaḥ pradadyātkamaṇḍalum
mahāsarāṃsi yatkṛtvā puṇyaṃ tatsakalaṃ labhet
mṛdamānīya saślakṣṇaṃ prāṇidoṣavivarjitām
nivedya śivayogibhyaḥ kṣitidānaphalaṃ labhet
bhṛdbhasmādhārapātrāṇi vaṃśālābukṛtāni ca
nivedya śivayogibhyaḥ sarvatīrthaphalaṃ labhet
dantadhāvanamuddiṣṭaṃ nivedya śivayogine
divyastrībhogasaṃyuktaṃ divi ramyaṃ puraṃ labhet
yogapaṭṭopavītāni nivedya śivayogine
vastrayugmasahasrasya dattasya phalamāpnuyāt
mṛṇmayālābukādyādyaṃ dambhṛtaṃ bhaikṣabhājanam
nivedya śivayogibhyaḥ sadāsatraphalaṃ labhet
śivabhaktāya yo dadyādupānatkāṣṭhapāduke
sa medhyāśvasahasrasya dattasya phalamāpnuyāt
kālmāṣadaṇḍaṃ yo dadyādannaṃ vā śivayogine
balāvaṣṭambhayuktābhyāṃ sa syāddaṇḍapraṇāyakaḥ
tālavaṃśātapatraṃ vā yo dadyācchivayogine

pātrāntaraviśeṣāṇi viśeṣācchivayogine

śatārdhārdhapramāṇena sa rudro nātra saṃśayaḥ

upadeṣṭānumantā ca kartā kārayitā ca yaḥ

oṃ bhavāya jalamūrtaye namaḥ

oṃ bhīmā a akāśamūrtaye namaḥ
