---
title: 038

---
śrutvā pādaṃ tṛtīyantu krāntaṃ sūtena dhīmatā।  
tataścaturthaṃ papracchuḥ pādaṃ vai ṛṣisattamāḥ ।। 38.1 ।।  
  
      ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।  
pādaḥ krāntastṛtīyo'yamanuṣaṅgeṇa yastvayā।  
caturthaṃ vistarātpādaṃ saṃhāraṃ parikīrttaya ।। 38.2 ।।  
  
manvantarāṇi sarvāṇi pūrvāṇyevāparaiḥ saha।  
saptarṣīṇāmathaiteṣāṃ sāmpratasyāntare manoḥ ।। 38.3 ।।  
  
vistarāvayavañcaiva nisargasya mahātmanaḥ।  
vistareṇānupūrvyā ca sarvameva bravīhi me ।। 38.4 ।।  
  
         sūta uvāca।।  
bhavatāṃ kathayiṣyāmi sarvametadyathātatham।  
pādaṃ tvimaṃ sasaṃhāraṃ caturthaṃ munisattamāḥ ।। 38.5 ।।  
  
manorvaivasvatasyemaṃ sāṃpratasya mahātmanaḥ।  
vistareṇānupūrvyā ca nisargaṃ śrṛṇuta dvijāḥ ।। 38.6 ।।  
  
manvantarāṇāṃ saṃkṣepaṃ bhaviṣyaiḥ saha saptabhiḥ।  
pralayañcaiva lokānāṃ bruvato me nibodhata ।। 38.7 ।।  
  
etānyuktāni vai samyak saptasaptasu vai mayā।  
manvantarāṇi saṃkṣepācchṛṇutānāgatāni me ।। 38.8 ।।  
  
sāvarṇasya pravakṣyāmi manorvaivasvatasya ha।  
bhaviṣyasya bhaviṣyanti samāsāttu nibodhata ।। 38.9 ।।  
  
anāgatāśca saptaiva smṛtāstviha maharṣayaḥ।  
kauśiko gātavaścaiva jāmadagnyaśca bhārgavaḥ ।। 38.10 ।।  
  
dvaipāyano vasiṣṭhaśca kṛpaḥ śāradvatastathā।  
ātreyo dīptimāṃścaiva ṛṣyaśrṛṅgastu kāśyapaḥ ।। 38.11 ।।  
  
bhāradvājastathā drauṇiraśvatthāmā mahāyaśāḥ।  
ete sapta mahātmāno bhaviṣyāḥ paramarṣayaḥ ।। 38.12 ।।  
  
sutapā ścāmitābhāśca sukhāścaiva gaṇāstrayaḥ।  
teṣāṃ gaṇāstu devānāṃ ekaiko viṃśakaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 38.13 ।।  
  
nāmatastu pravakṣyāmi nibodhadhvaṃ samāhitāḥ।  
ritastapaśca śukraśca dyutirjyotiḥ prabhākarau ।। 38.14 ।।  
  
prabhāso bhāsakṛddharmmastejoraśmiṛturvirāṭ।  
arciṣmān dyotano bhānuryaśakīrttirbudho dhṛtiḥ।  
viṃśatiḥ sutapā hyete nāmabhiḥ parikīrtitāḥ ।। 38.15 ।।  
  
prabhurvibhurvibhāsaśca jetā hantā'rihā rituḥ।  
sumatiḥ pramatirdīptiḥ samākhyāto mahomahān ।। 38.16 ।।  
  
deho munirnayo jyeṣṭhaḥ samaḥ satyaśca viśrutaḥ।  
ityete hyamitābhāstu viṃśatiḥ parikīrttitāḥ ।। 38.17 ।।  
  
damo dātā vidaḥ somo vittavaidyau yamo nidhiḥ।  
homaṃ havyaṃ hutaṃ dānaṃ deyaṃ dātā tapaḥ śamaḥ ।। 38.18 ।।  
  
dhruvaṃ sthānaṃ vidhānañca niyamaśceti viṃśatiḥ।  
mukhyā hyete samākhyātāḥ sāvarṇe prathame'ntare ।। 38.19 ।।  
  
mārīcasyaiva te putrāḥ kaśyapasya mahātmanaḥ।  
sāmpratasya bhaviṣyanti sāvarṇasyāntare manoḥ ।। 38.20 ।।  
  
teṣāmindro bhaviṣyastu balirvairocanaḥ purā।  
vīravāṃścāvarīyāṃśca nirmohaḥ satyavāk kṛtī ।। 38.21 ।।  
  
cariṣṇurājyo viṣṇuśca vācaḥ sumatireva ca।  
sāvarṇasya manoḥ putrā bhaviṣyanti navaiva tu ।। 38.22 ।।  
  
nava cānyeṣu vakṣyāmi sāvarṇeścāntareṣu vai।  
sāvarṇamanavaścānye bhaviṣyā brahmaṇaḥ sutāḥ ।। 38.23 ।।  
  
merusāvarṇinaste vai dṛṣṭā ye divyadṛṣṭibhiḥ।  
dakṣasya te hi dauhitrāḥ priyāyā duhituḥ sutāḥ ।। 38.24 ।।  
  
mahatā tapasā yuktā merupṛṣṭe mahaujasaḥ।  
brahmādibhiste janitā dakṣeṇaiva ca dhīmatā ।। 38.25 ।।  
  
maharlokagatāvṛtya bhaviṣyā merumāśritāḥ।  
mahābhāvāttu te pūrvaṃ jajñire cākṣuṣe'ntare ।। 38.26 ।।  
  
          ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।  
dakṣeṇa janitāḥ putrāḥ kanyāyāmātmanaḥ katham।  
bhavettu brahmaṇaścaiva dharmeṇa ca mahātmanaḥ ।। 38.27 ।।  
  
         sūta uvāca।।  
ato bhaviṣyān vakṣyāmi sāvarṇamanavastu ye।  
teṣāṃ janma prabhāvañca namaskṛtya pracetase ।। 38.28 ।।  
  
vaivasvate hyupaspṛṣṭe kiñcicchiṣṭe ca cākṣuṣe।  
jajñire manavaste hi bhaviṣyānāgatāntare ।। 38.29 ।।  
  
prācetasasya dakṣasya dauhitrā manavastu ye।  
sāvarṇā nāmataḥ pañca catvāraḥ paramarṣijāḥ ।। 38.30 ।।  
  
saṃjñāputrastu sāvarṇa eko vaivasvatastathā।  
jyeṣṭhaḥ saṃjñāsuto nāma manurvaivasvataḥ prabhuḥ ।। 38.31 ।।  
  
vaivasvate'ntare prāpte samutpattistayoḥ śubhā।  
caturddaśaite manavaḥ kīrttitāḥ kīrttivarddhanāḥ ।। 38.32 ।।  
  
vede śrutau purāṇe ca sarve te prabhaviṣṇavaḥ।  
prajānāṃ patayaḥ sarve bhūtānāṃ patayaḥ sthitāḥ ।। 38.33 ।।  
  
tairiyaṃ pṛthivī sarvā saptadvīpā saparvatā।  
pūrṇaṃ yugasahasraṃ vai paripālyā nareśvaraiḥ ।। 38.34 ।।  
  
prajābhistapasā caiva vistaraṃ teṣu vakṣyate।  
caturddaśaiva te jñeyāḥ sargāḥ svāyambhuvādayaḥ ।। 38.35 ।।  
  
manvantarādhikāreṣu vartante ca sakṛt sakṛt।  
vinivṛttādhikārāste maharlokaṃ samāśritāḥ ।। 38.36 ।।  
  
samatītāstu ye teṣāmaṣṭau ṣaṣṭāstathāpare।  
pūrveṣu sāmprataścāyaṃ śāntirvaivasvataḥ prabhuḥ ।। 38.37 ।।  
  
ye śiṣṭāstān pravakṣyāmi saha devarṣidānavaiḥ।  
saha prajānisargeṇa sarvvāṃstvanāgatān dvijān ।। 38.38 ।।  
  
vaivasvatanisargeṇa teṣāṃ jñeyastu vistaraḥ।  
anyūnā nātiriktāste yasmāt sarvve vivasvataḥ ।। 38.39 ।।  
  
punaruktā bahutvāttu vakṣye na teṣu vistaram।  
manvantareṣu bhāvyeṣu bhūteṣvapi tathaiva ca ।। 39.40 ।।  
  
kule kule nisargāṃstu tasmādbhūyo vibhāgaśaḥ ।  
teṣāmeva hi śiṣṭārthaṃ vistareṇa krameṇa ca ।। 39.41 ।।  
  
dakṣasya kanyā dharmiṣṭā suvratā nāma viśrutā।  
sarvakanyāvaśiṣṭā tu śreṣṭā dharmaparā sutā।  
gṛhītvā tāṃ pitā kanyāṃ jagāma brahmaṇo'ntike ।। 39.42 ।।  
  
vairājastamupāsīnaṃ dharmeṇa ca bhavena ca।  
bhavadharmasamīpasthaṃ dakṣaṃ brahmābyabhāṣata ।। 39.43 ।।  
  
dakṣa kanyā taveyaṃ vai janayiṣyati suvratā।  
caturo vai manūn putrāṃścāturvarṇyakarāñchubhān ।। 38.44 ।।  
  
brahmaṇo vacanaṃ śrutvā dakṣo dharmo bhavastadā।  
tāṃ kanyāṃ manasā jagmustrayaste brahmaṇā saha ।। 38.45 ।।  
  
satyābhidhyāyināṃ teṣāṃ sadyaḥ kanyā vyajāyata।  
sadṛśānurūpāṃsteṣāṃ caturo vai kumārakān ।। 38.46 ।।  
  
saṃsiddhāḥ kāryakaraṇe sambhūtāste śriyānvitaḥ।  
upabhogasamarthaiśca sadyojātaiḥ śarīrakaiḥ ।। 38.47 ।।  
  
te dṛṣṭvā tān svayambuddhvā brahmā vyāhāhiṇastadā।  
saṃrabdhā vai vyakarṣanta mama putro mametyuta ।। 38.48 ।।  
  
abhidhyānānmanotpannānūcurvai te parasparam।  
yo yasya vapuṣā tulyo bhajatāṃ sa tu taṃ sutam ।। 38.49 ।।  
  
yasya yaḥ sadṛśaścāpi rūpe vīrya ca nāmataḥ।  
taṃ gṛhṇātu subhadraṃ vo varṇato yasya yaḥ samaḥ ।। 38.50 ।।  
  
dhruvaṃ rūpaṃ pituḥ putraḥ so'nurudhyati sarvadā।  
tasmādātmasamaḥ putraḥ piturmātuśca jāyate ।। 38.51 ।।  
  
evaṃ te samayaṃ kṛtvā savarṇān jagṛhuḥ sutān।  
yasmāt savarṇāsteṣāṃ vai brahmādīnāṃ kumārakāḥ ।। 38.52 ।।  
  
savarṇā manavastasmāt savarṇatvaṃ hi te yataḥ।  
mananānmānanāccaiva tasmātte manavaḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 38.53 ।।  
  
cākṣuṣasyāntare'tīte prāpte vaivasvatasya ha।  
ruceḥ prajāpateḥ putro raucyo nāmābhavatsutaḥ ।। 38.54 ।।  
  
bhūtyāmutpādito yastu bhautyo nāmābhavatsutaḥ।  
vaivasvate'ntare rājā dvau manū tu vivasvatoḥ ।। 38.55 ।।  
  
vaivasvato manuryaśca sāvarṇo yaśca viśrutaḥ।  
jyeṣṭhaḥ saṃjñāsuto vidvān manurvaivasvataḥ prabhuḥ ।। 38.56 ।।  
  
savarṇāyāḥ sutaścānyaḥ smṛto vaivasvato manuḥ ।  
savarṇā manavo ye ca catvārastu maharṣijāḥ ।। 38.57 ।।  
  
tapasā sambhṛtātmānaḥ sveṣu manvantareṣu vai।  
bhaviṣyeṣu bhaviṣyanti sarvakāryārthasādhakāḥ ।। 38.58 ।।  
  
prathamaṃ merusāvarṇerdakṣaputrasya vai manoḥ।  
putrā marīcigarbhāśca suśarmāṇaśca te trayaḥ ।  
sambhūtāśca mahātmānaḥ sarve vaivasvate'ntare ।। 38.59 ।।  
  
dakṣaputrasya putrāste rohitasya prajāpateḥ।  
bhaviṣyasya bhaviṣyastu ekaiko dvādaśo gaṇaḥ ।। 38.60 ।।  
  
aiśvaryasaṃgrāho rāho bāhuvaśastathaiva ca।  
pārā dvādaśa vijñeyā uttarāṃstu nibodhata ।। 38.61 ।।  
  
vājiyo vājijiccaiva prabhṛtiśca kakudyatha।  
dadhikrāvāyapakvāśca praṇīto vijayo madhuḥ ।। 38.62 ।।  
  
tejasmānnathavo dvau tu dvādaśaite marīcayaḥ।  
suśarmmāṇastu vakṣyāmi nāmatastu nibodhata ।। 38.63 ।।  
  
varṇastathāpyaṅgaviśvau muraṇyo vrajano mataḥ।  
amito dravaketuśca jambhosthājasraśakrakāḥ ।। 38.64 ।।  
  
sunemirdyutapāścaiva suśarmmāṇaḥ prakīrttitāḥ।  
teṣāmindrastadā bhāvyo hyadbhuto nāma nāmataḥ ।। 38.65 ।।  
  
skandaḥ somapratīkāśaḥ kārtikeyastu pāvakaḥ।  
māghātithiśca paulastyo vasuḥ kāśyapa eva ca ।। 38.66 ।।  
  
jyotiṣmān bhārgavaścaiva dyutimānaṅgirāstathā।  
vasitaścaiva vāsiṣṭha ātreyo havyavāhanaḥ ।। 38.67 ।।  
  
sutapāḥ paulavaścaiva saptaite rohitāntare।  
dhṛtiketudīptiketuśāpahastā nirāmayaḥ ।। 38.68 ।।  
  
pṛthuśravāstathā nāko bhūridyumno bṛhadrathaḥ।  
prathamasya tu sāvarṇernava putrāḥ prakīrtitāḥ ।। 38.69 ।।  
  
daśame tvatha paryāye dharmaputrasya vai manoḥ।  
dvitīyasya tu sāvarṇerbhāvyasyaivāntare manoḥ ।। 38.70 ।।  
  
sukhāmanā viruddhāśca dvāveva tu gaṇau smṛtau।  
tviṣivantaśca te sarve śatasaṅkhyāśca te samāḥ ।। 38.71 ।।  
  
prāṇā nāyacchataḥ prokta ṛṣibhiḥ puruṣeṣu vai।  
devāste vai bhaviṣyanti dharmaputrasya vai manoḥ ।। 38.72 ।।  
  
teṣāmindrastathā vidvān bhaviṣyaḥ śāntirucyate।  
haviṣmān paulahaḥ śrīmān sukīrtiścāpi bhārgavaḥ ।। 38.73 ।।  
  
āpomūrtistathātreyo vasiṣṭhaścāpi yaḥ smṛtaḥ।  
paulastyaḥ pratipaścāpi nābhāgaścaiva kāśyapaḥ।  
abhimanyuścāṅgirasaḥ saptaite paramarṣayaḥ ।। 38.74 ।।  
  
sukṣetraścottamaujāśca bhūriṣeṇaśca vīryavān।  
śatānīko nirāmitro vṛṣaseno jayadrathaḥ ।। 38.75 ।।  
  
bhūridyumnaḥ suvarccāśca daśaite mānavāḥ smṛtāḥ।  
ekādaśe tu paryyāye sāvarṇe vai tṛtīyake ।। 38.76 ।।  
  
nirmmāṇaratayo devāḥ kāmajā vai manojavāḥ।  
gaṇāstvete trayaḥ khyātā devatānāṃ mahātmanām ।। 38.77 ।।  
  
ekaikaṃ triṃśatasteṣāṃ gaṇāstu tridivaukasām।  
māsasyāhāni triṃśattu yāni vai kavayo viduḥ ।। 38.78 ।।  
  
nirmmāṇaratayo devā rātrayastu vihaṅgamāḥ।  
gaṇāste vai trayaḥ proktā devatānāṃ bhaviṣyati ।। 38.79 ।।  
  
manojavā muhūrttāstu iti devāḥ prakīrttitāḥ ।  
ete hi brahmaṇaḥ putrā bhaviṣyā manavaḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 38.80 ।।  
  
teṣāmindro vṛṣo nāma bhaviṣyaḥ surarāṭ tataḥ।  
teṣāṃ saptarṣayaścāpi kīrttyamānānnibodhata ।। 38.81 ।।  
  
haviṣmān kāśyapaścāpi vapuṣmān yaśca bhārgavaḥ।  
vāruṇiścaiva cātreyo vāsiṣṭo bhaga eva ca ।। 38.82 ।।  
  
puṣṭiścāṅgiraso jñeyaḥ paulastyo niścarastathā।  
paulaho hyagnitejāśca devā hyekādaśe'ntare ।। 38.83 ।।  
  
sarvvavegaḥ sudharmmā ca devānīkaḥ purovahaḥ।  
kṣemadharmā gṛheṣuśca hyādarśaḥ pauṇḍrako manaḥ ।। 38.84 ।।  
  
sāvarṇasya tu te putrāḥ prājāpatyasya vai manoḥ।  
dvādaśe tvatha paryāye rudraputrasya vai manoḥ ।। 38.85 ।।  
  
caturthe ṛtusāvarṇe devāstasyāntare śrṛṇu।  
pañcaiva tu gaṇāḥ proktā devatānāmanāgatāḥ ।। 38.86 ।।  
  
haritā rohitāścaiva devāḥ sumanasastathā।  
sukarmmāṇaḥ supārāśca pañca devagaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 38.87 ।।  
  
brahmaṇo mānasā hyete ekaiko daśako gaṇaḥ।  
aruntijo hariścaiva vidvān yaśca sahasraśaḥ ।। 38.88 ।।  
  
parvatānucaraścaiva hyapoṃ'śuśca manojavaḥ।  
urjjā svāhā svadhā tārā daśaite rohitāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 38.89 ।।  
  
tapojānirbhṛtiścaiva vācā bandhuśca yaḥ smṛtaḥ ।  
rajaścaiva tu rājaśca svarṇapādastathaiva ca ।। 38.90 ।।  
  
vyuṣṭirvidhiśca vai devo daśaite rohitāḥ smṛtāḥ ।  
uṣitādyāstu ye devāstrayastriśatprakīrtitāḥ ।। 38.91 ।।  
  
devān sumanaso viddhi sukarmāṇo nibodhata।  
suparvā vṛṣabhaḥ pṛṣṭaḥ kṛpidyumnau vipaścitaḥ ।। 38.92 ।।  
  
vikramaśca kramaścaiva nibhṛtaḥ kānta eva ca।।  
ete sukarmaṇo devāḥ sutāṃścaiṣāṃ nibodhata ।। 38.93 ।।  
  
varyyoditastathā jiṣṭo varcasvī dyutimān haviḥ।  
śubho havikṛtātprāptirvyāpṛtho daśamastathā ।। 38.94 ।।  
  
supārā mānasāstvete devā vai samprakīrttitāḥ।  
teṣāmindrastu vijñeya ṛtadhāmā mahāyaśāḥ ।। 38.95 ।।  
  
kṛtirvasiṣṭhaputrastu hyātreyaḥ sutapāstathā।  
tapomūrtiścāṅgirasastapasvī kāśyapastathā ।। 38.96 ।।  
  
tapo'śayānaḥ paulastyaḥ pulahaśca taporatiḥ।  
bhārgavaḥ saptamastveṣāṃ vijñeyastu tapomatiḥ ।। 38.97 ।।  
  
ete saptarṣayaḥ siddhā anye sāvarṇike'ntare।  
devavānupadevaśca devaśreṣṭo vidūrathaḥ ।। 38.98 ।।  
  
mitravān mitrabinduśca mitraseno hyamitrahā।  
mitrabāhuḥ suvarcāśca dvādaśaite manoḥ sutāḥ ।। 38.99 ।।  
  
trayo daśe tu paryāye bhāvyā raucyāntare punaḥ।  
traya eva gaṇāḥ proktā devānāntu svayambhuvā ।। 38.100 ।।  
  
brahmaṇo mānasāḥ putrāste hi sarve mahātmanaḥ।  
sutrāmāṇaḥ sudharmmāṇaḥ sukarmmāṇaśca te trayaḥ ।। 38.101 ।।  
  
tridaśānāṃ gaṇāḥ proktā bhaviṣyāḥ somapāyinaḥ।  
trayastriṃśaddevatā yāḥ prābhaviṣyanta yājñikaiḥ ।। 38.102 ।।  
  
ājyena pṛṣadājyena grahaśreṣṭena caiva hi।  
devairdevāstrayastriṃśatpṛthaktvena nibodhata ।। 38.103 ।।  
  
sutrāmāṇaḥ prayājyāstu hyādyājyāstu sāmpratam।  
sukarmaṇo'nuyājyāstu pṛṣadājyāśinastu ye ।। 38.104 ।।  
  
upayājyāḥ sudharmāṇa iti devāḥ prakīrttitāḥ ।

anādhāramidaṃ sarvaṃ trailokyaṃ vai bhaviṣyati ।। 38.122 ।।  
  
tataḥ sthānāni śūnyāni sthānināṃ tāni vai dvijāḥ।  
prabhraśyanti vimuktāni tārāṛkṣagrahaistathā ।। 38.123 ।।

evaṃ deveṣvatīteṣu maharlokājjanaṃ prati।  
bhūtādiṣvavaśiṣṭeṣu sthāvarānteṣu cāpyuta ।। 38.128 ।।  
  
śūnyeṣu lokasthāneṣu mahānteṣu bhūrādiṣu।  
deveṣu ca gateṣūrddhvaṃ sāyojyaṃ kalpavāsinām ।। 38.129 ।।  
  
saṃhṛtya tāṃstato brahmā devarṣipitṛdānavān।  
saṃsthāpayati vai sargaṃ mahadṝṣṭyā yugakṣaye ।। 38.130 ।।  
  
tatra yugasahasrāntamaharyardbrahmaṇo viduḥ।  
rātriṃ yugasahasrāntāmahorātravido janāḥ ।। 38.131 ।।  
  
naimittikaḥ prākṛtiko yaścaivātyantiko'rthataḥ।  
trividhaḥ sarvvabhūtānāmityeṣa pratisañcaraḥ ।। 38.132 ।।  
  
brāhmo naimittikastasya kalpadāhaḥ prasaṃyamaḥ।  
pratisarge tu bhūtānāṃ prākṛtaḥ karaṇakṣayaḥ ।। 38.133 ।।  
  
jñānāccātyantikaḥ proktaḥ kāraṇānāmasambhavaḥ ।  
tataḥ saṃhṛtya tān brahmā devāṃstrailokyavāsinaḥ ।। 38.134 ।।  
  
aharante prakurute sargasya pralayaṃ punaḥ।  
suṣupsurbhagavān brahmā prajāḥ saṃharate tadā ।। 38.135 ।।  
  
tato yugasahasrānte saṃprāpte ca yugakṣaye।  
tatrātmasthāḥ prajāḥ karttuṃ prapede sa prajāpatiḥ ।। 38.136 ।।  
  
tadā bhavatyanāvṛṣṭistadā sā śata vārṣikī।  
tathā yānyalpasārāṇi sattvāni pṛthivītale ।। 38.137 ।।  
  
tānyevātra pralīyante bhūmitvamupayānti ca।  
saptaraśmiratho bhūtvā hyudatiṣṭhadvibhāvasuḥ ।। 38.138 ।।  
  
asahyaraśmirbhagavān pibannambho gabhastibhiḥ।  
haritā raśmayastasya dīpyamānāstu saptabhiḥ ।। 38.139 ।।

tasmādudakaṃ sūryyasya tapato'ti hi kathyate।  
nāvṛṣṭyā tapate sūryo nāvṛṣṭyā pariviṣyate ।। 38.141 ।।  
  
nāvṛṣṭyā paricinvanti vāriṇā dīpyate raviḥ।

       ।।sūta ūvāca।।  
pratyāhāraṃ pravakṣyāmi parasyānte svayambhuvaḥ।  
brahmaṇaḥ sthitikāle tu kṣīṇe tasmiṃstadā prabhoḥ ।। 40.1 ।।  
  
yathedaṃ kurute'dhyātmaṃ susūkṣmaṃ viśvamīśvaraḥ ।  
avyaktāngrasate vyaktaṃ pratyāhāre ca kṛtsnaśaḥ ।। 40.2 ।।

upasthite mahāghore hyapratyakṣe tu kasyacit ।। 40.3 ।।  
  
ante drumasya yamprāpte paścimasya manostadā ।  
ante kaliyuge tasminkṣīṇe saṃhāra ucyate ।। 40.4 ।।  
  
samprakṣāle tadā vṛtte pratyāhāre hyupasthite।

guṇamātrāḥ pravarttante puruṣādhiṣṭitāstridhā ।। 40.54 ।।  
  
ūrddhvaṃ devātmakaṃ sattvamadhobhāgātmakaṃ tamaḥ।  
tayoḥ pravarttakaṃ madye ihaivāvarttakaṃ rajaḥ ।। 40.55 ।।  
  
ityevaṃ parivarttante trayaḥ srotoguṇātmakāḥ।  
lokeṣu sarvabhūtānāṃ tanna kāryaṃ vijānatā ।। 40.56 ।।  
  
avidyāpratyayārambhā ārabhyante hi mānavaiḥ ।  
etāstu gatayastisraḥ śubhāḥ pāpātmikāḥ smṛtāḥ ।। 40.57 ।।

atatddarśanātso'tha trividhaṃ badhyate tataḥ ।। 40.58 ।।  
  
prākṛtena ca bandhena tathā vaikārikena ca।  
dakṣiṇābhistṛtīyena baddho'tyantaṃ vivarttate ।। 40.59 ।।

anitye nityasaṃjñā ca duḥkhe ca sukhadarśanam ।। 40.60 ।।

yeṣāmete manodoṣā jñānadoṣā viparyyayāt ।। 40.61 ।।  
  
rāgadveṣanivṛttiśca tajjñānaṃ samudāhṛtam।  
ajñānaṃ tamaso mūlaṃ karmmadvayaphalaṃ rajaḥ।  
karmmajastu punardeho mahāduḥkhaṃ pravarttate ।। 40.62 ।।  
  
śrotrajā netrajā caiva tvagjihvāghrāṇatastathā।  
punarbhavakarī duḥkhā karmmaṇāṃ jāyate tu sā ।। 40.63 ।।  
  
satṛṣṇo'bhihito bālaḥ svakṛtaiḥ karmmaṇaḥ phalaiḥ।  
tailapālīkavajjīvastatraiva pari varttate ।। 40.64 ।।  
  
tasmātsthūlamanarthānāmajñānamupadiśyate ।  
taṃ śaktamavadhāryyaikaṃ jñāne yatnaṃ samācaret ।। 40.65 ।।  
  
jñānādvijayate sarvaṃ tyāgād‌buddhirvirajyate।  
vairāgyācchuddhyate cāpi śuddhaḥ sattvena mucyate ।। 40.66 ।।  
  
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi rāgaṃ bhūtāpahāriṇam।  
abhiṣaṅgāya yo yasmādviṣayo'pyavaśātmanaḥ ।। 40.67 ।।  
  
aniṣṭamabhiṣaṅgaṃ hi prītitāpaviṣādanam।  
duḥkhalābhe na tāpañca sukhānusmaraṇaṃ tathā ।। 40.68 ।।  
  
ityeṣa vaiṣayo rāgaḥ sambhūtyāḥ kāraṇaṃ smṛtam।  
brahmādau sthāvarānte vai saṃsāre hyādhibhautike।  
ajñānapūrvakaṃ tasmādajñānantu vivarjayet ।। 40.69 ।।  
  
yasya cārṣaṃ na pramāṇaṃ śiṣṭācāraṃ tathaiva ca।  
varṇāśramavirodhī yaḥ śiṣṭaśāstravirodhakaḥ ।। 40.70 ।।  
  
eṣa mārgo hi niradhitiryagyonau ca kāraṇam।  
tiryagyonigatañcaiva kāraṇaṃ sa nirucyate ।। 40.71 ।।  
  
vividhā yātanā sthāne tiryagyonau ca ṣaḍvidhe।  
kāraṇe viṣaye caiva pratighātastu sarvaśaḥ ।। 40.72 ।।  
  
anaiśvaryyantu tatsarvaṃ pratighātātmakaṃ smṛtam ।  
ityeṣā tāmasī vṛttirbhūtādīnāṃ caturvidhā ।। 40.73 ।।  
  
sattvasthamātrakaṃ cittaṃ yathā sattvapradarśanāt।  
tattvānāñca tathā tattvaṃ dṛṣṭvā vai tattvadarśanāt ।। 40.74 ।।  
  
sattvakṣetrajñanānātvametajjñānārthadarśanam।  
nānātvadarśanaṃ jñānaṃ jñānādvai yogamucyate ।। 40.75 ।।  
  
tena baddhasya vai bandho mokṣo muktasya tena ca ।  
saṃsāre vinivṛtte tu mukto liṅgena mucyate ।। 40.76 ।।  
  
niḥsambandho hyacaitanyaḥ svātmanyevāvatiṣṭhate।  
svātmavyavasthitaścāpi virūpākhyena likhyate ।। 40.77 ।।  
  
ityetallakṣaṇaṃ proktaṃ samāsājjñānamokṣayo।  
sa cāpi trividhaḥ prokto mokṣo vai tattvadarśibhiḥ ।। 40.78 ।।  
  
pūrvaṃ viyogo jñānena dvitīyo rāgasaṃkṣayāt।  
liṅgābhāvāttu kaivalyaṃ kaivatyāttu nirañjanam ।। 40.79 ।।  
  
nirañjanatvācchuddhastu tato netā na vidyate।  
tṛṣṇākṣayāttṛtīyastu vyākhyātaṃ mokṣakāraṇam ।। 40.80 ।।  
  
nimittamapratīghāte iṣṭaśabdādilakṣaṇe।  
aṣṭāvetāni rūpāṇi prākṛtāni yathākramam ।। 40.81 ।।  
  
kṣetrajñeṣvavasajyante guṇamātrātmakāni tu।  
ata urddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi vairāgyaṃ doṣadarśanāt ।। 40.82 ।।  
  
divye ca mānuṣe caiva viṣaye pañcalakṣaṇe।  
apradveṣo'nabhiṣvaṅgaḥ karttavyo doṣadarśanāt ।। 40.83 ।।  
  
tāpaprītiviṣādānāṃ kāryyantu parivarjanam।  
evaṃ vairāgyamāsthāya śarīrī nirmamo bhavet ।। 40.84 ।।  
  
anityamaśivaṃ duḥkhamiti buddhyānucintya ca।  
viśuddhaṃ kāryyakaraṇaṃ satvābhyeti tarāntu yaḥ ।। 40.85 ।।  
  
paripavkakaṣāyo hi kṛtsnāndoṣānprapaśyati।  
tataḥ prayāṇakāle hi doṣairnaimittikaistathā ।। 40.86 ।।  
  
ūṣmā prakupitaḥ kāye tīvravāyusamīritaḥ।  
sa śarīramupāśritya kṛtsnāndoṣānruṇaddhi vai ।। 40.87 ।।  
  
prāṇasthānāni bhindanhi chindanmarmāṇyatītya ca।  
śaityātprakupito vāyurūrddhvantu kramate tataḥ ।। 40.88 ।।  
  
sa cāyaṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ prāṇasthāneṣvavasthitaḥ।  
samāsātsaṃvṛte jñāne saṃvṛteṣu ca karmmasu ।। 40.89 ।।  
  
sa jīvo'nabhyadhiṣṭānaḥ karmmabhiḥ svaiḥ purākṛtaiḥ।  
aṣṭāṅgaprāṇavṛttīrvai sa vicyāvayate punaḥ ।। 40.90 ।।  
  
śarīraṃ prajahaṃso vai nirucchvāsastato bhavet।  
evaṃ prāṇaiḥ parityakto mṛta ityabhidhīyate ।। 40.91 ।।  
  
yatheha loke khadyotaṃ nīyamānamitastataḥ ।  
rañjanaṃ tadvadhe yattu netā netā na vidyate ।। 40.92 ।।  
  
tṛṣṇākṣayastṛtīyastu vyākhyātaṃ mokṣalakṣaṇam।  
śabdādye viṣaye doṣaviṣaye pañcalakṣaṇe ।। 40.93 ।।  
  
apradveṣo'nabhiṣvaṅgaḥ prītitāpavivarjanam ।  
vairāgyakāraṇaṃ hyete prakṛtīnāṃ layasya ca ।। 40.94 ।।  
  
aṣṭau prakṛtayo jñeyāḥ pūrvoktā vai yathākramam।  
avyaktādyastu vijñeyā bhūtāntāḥ prakṛterlayāḥ ।। 40.95 ।।  
  
varṇāśramācārayuktāḥ śiṣṭāḥ śāstrāvirodhinaḥ।  
varṇāśramāṇāṃ dharmmo'yaṃ devasthāneṣu kāraṇam ।। 40.96 ।।  
  
brahmādīni piśācāntānyaṣṭau sthānāni devatā।  
aiśvaryyamaṇimādyaṃ hi kāraṇaṃ hyaṣṭalakṣaṇam ।। 40.97 ।।  
  
nimittamapratīghāta iṣṭe śabdādilakṣaṇe।  
aṣṭāvetāni rūpāṇi prākṛtāni yathākramam ।। 40.98 ।।  
  
kṣetrajñeṣvanusajyante gaṇamātrātmakāni tu।  
prāvṛṭ‌kāle pṛthaktvena paśyantīha na cakṣuṣā ।। 40.99 ।।  
  
paśyantyevaṃvidhaṃ siddhā jīvaṃ divyena cakṣuṣā।  
śvāviti śvānapānaśca yonīḥ praviśatastathā ।। 40.100 ।।  
  
tiryyagūrddhvamadhastācca dhāvato'pi yathākramam।  
jīvaprāṇāstathā liṅgaṃ kāraṇañca catuṣṭayam ।।. 40.101 ।।  
  
paryyāyavācakaiḥ śabdairekārthaiḥ so'bhilikhyate।  
vyaktāvyakte pramāṇo'yaṃ sa vai rūpaṃ tu kṛtsnaśaḥ ।। 40.102 ।।  
  
avyaktānta gṛhītaṃ ca kṣetrajñādhiṣṭitañca yat ।  
evaṃ jñātvā śucirbhūttvā jñānādvai vipramucyate ।। 40.103 ।।  
  
naṣṭañcaiva yathā tattvaṃ tattvānāṃ tattvadarśanam।  
yatheṣṭaṃ pariniryyāti bhinne dehe sunirvṛte ।। 40.104 ।।  
  
bhidyate karaṇañcāpi hyavyaktājñāninastataḥ।  
mukto guṇaśarīreṇa prāṇādyena tu sarvaśaḥ ।। 40.105 ।।  
  
nānyaccharīramādatte dagdhe bīje yathāṅkuraḥ।  
jīvikaḥ sarvasaṃsārādbījaśārīramānasaḥ ।। 40.106 ।।  
  
jñānāccaturddaśācchuddhaḥ prakṛtiṃ so'nuvarttate।  
prakṛtiṃ satyamityāhu rvikāro'nṛtamucyate ।। 40.107 ।।  
  
tatsadbhāvo'nṛtaṃ jñeyaṃ sadbhāvaḥ satyamucyate।  
anāmarūpakṣetrajñanāmarūpaṃ pracakṣate ।। 40.108 ।।  
  
yasmātkṣetraṃ vijānāti tasmātkṣetrajña ucyate।  
kṣetrapratyayato yasmātkṣetrajñaḥ śubha ucyate ।। 40.109 ।।  
  
kṣetrajñaḥ smaryyate tasmātkṣetraṃ tajjñairvibhāṣyate।  
kṣetratvapratyayaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ kṣetrajñaḥ pratyayī sadā ।। 40.110 ।।  
  
kṣayaṇāt karaṇāccaiva kṣatatrāṇāttathaiva ca।  
bhojyatvādviṣayatvācca kṣetraṃ kṣetravido viduḥ ।। 40.111 ।।  
  
mahadādyaṃ viśeṣāntaṃ savairūpyaṃ vilakṣaṇam।  
vikāralakṣaṇaṃ tadvai sākṣarakṣarameva ca ।। 40.112 ।।  
  
tameva ca vikārantu yasmādvai kṣarate punaḥ।  
tasmācca kāraṇāccaiva kṣaramityabhidhīyate ।। 40.113 ।।  
  
sukhaduḥkhamohabhāvā bhojyamityabhidhīyate।  
acetatvāddhi viṣayastaddhi dharmmavibhuḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 40.114 ।।  
  
na kṣīyate na kṣarati vikāraprasṛtantu tat।

yasmātpuryyanuśete ca tasmātpuruṣa ucyate।  
purapratyayiko yasmātpūruṣetyabhidhīyate ।। 40.116 ।।  
  
puruṣaṃ kathayasvātha kathantajjñairvibhāṣyate।  
śuddho nirañjanābhāso jñānājñānavivarjitaḥ ।। 40.117 ।।  
  
asti nāstīti so'nyo vā baddho mukto gataḥ sthitaḥ।  
nairhetikāntanirddeśyasūktastasminna vidyate ।। 40.118 ।।

bhāvagrāhyamanumānyaṃ cintayanna pramuhyate ।। 40.119 ।।

evaṃ jñātvā sa vijñātā tataḥ śāntiṃ niyacchati।  
kāryye ca kāraṇe caiva buddhyādau bhautike tadā ।। 40.121 ।।  
  
saṃprayukto viyukto vā jīvato vā mṛtasya ca।  
vijñātā na ca dṛśyeta pṛthaktveneha sarvvaśaḥ ।। 40.122 ।।  
  
svenātmānaṃ tamātmānaṃ kāraṇātmā niyacchati।  
prakṛtau kāraṇe caiva svātmanyevopatiṣṭhati ।। 40.123 ।।  
  
asti nāstīti so'nyo vā ihāmutreti vā punaḥ।  
ekatvaṃ vā pṛthaktvaṃ vā kṣetrajñapuruṣeti vā ।। 40.124 ।।  
  
ātmavān sa nirātmā vā cetano'cetano'pi vā।  
karttā vā so'pyakarttā vā bhoktā vā bhojyameva vā ।। 40.125 ।।  
  
yajjñātvā na nivarttante kṣetrajñe tu nirañjane।  
avācyaṃ tadanākhyānādagrāhyatvādahetuni ।। 40.126 ।।  
  
apratarkyamacintyatvādavāpyatvācca sarvaśaḥ।  
nābhilimpati tattattvaṃ samprāpya manasā saha ।। 40.127 ।।  
  
kṣetrejñe nirguṇe śuddhe śānte kṣīṇe nirañjane।  
vyape(ye)tasukhaduḥkhe ca niruddhe śāntimāgate ।। 40.128 ।।  
  
nirātmake punastasminvācyāvācyo na vidyate।  
etau saṃhāravistārau vyaktāvyaktau tataḥ punaḥ ।। 40.129 ।।  
  
sṛjate grasate caiva grastaḥ paryavatiṣṭhate।  
kṣetrajñādhiṣṭitaṃ sarvaṃ punaḥ sarvaṃ pravarttate ।। 40.130 ।।  
  
adhiṣṭānapravṛttena tasya te buddhipūrvakam।  
sādharmyavaidharmyakṛtasaṃyogo vidhitastayoḥ।  
anādimān sa saṃyogo mahāpuruṣajaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 40.131 ।।  
  
yāvacca sargapratisargakālastāvacca tiṣṭhati susannirudhya।  
pūrvaṃ hitavye tadabuddhipūrvaṃ pravarttate tatpuruṣārthameva ।। 40.132 ।।  
  
eṣā nisargapratisargapūrvaṃ pradhānikī ceśvarakāritā ca।  
anādyanantā hyabhimānapūrvakaṃ vitrāsayantī jagadabhyupaiti ।। 40.133 ।।  
  
ityeṣa prākṛtaḥ sargastṛtīyo hetulakṣaṇaḥ।  
ukto hyasmiṃstadātyantaṃ kavibhistatpramucyate ।। 40.134 ।।  
  
ityeṣa pratisargovastrividhaḥ kīrttito mayā।।  
vistareṇānupūrvyā ca bhūyaḥ kiṃ varttayāmyaham ।। 40.135 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte catvāriṃśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 40 ।।

            ।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।

kathaṃ prapatsyate sargastannaḥ prabrūhi pṛcchatām ।। 41.7 ।।

tasmādvāco nivarttante hyaprāpya manasā saha ।। 41.10 ।।  
  
avyaktavat parokṣatvādghrahaṇaṃ taddurāsadam।  
vikāraiḥ pratisaṃdṛṣṭe guṇasāmye nivarttate ।। 41.11 ।।  
  
pradhānaṃ puruṣāṇāñca sādharmmyeṇaiva tiṣṭhati।

kṣetrajñasya pradhānasya puruṣasya prapatsyate ।। 41.19 ।।  
  
brahmāṇḍe prathamaḥ so'tha bhavitā ceśvaraḥ punaḥ।  
tato jñeyasya kṛtsnasya sarvabhūtapatiḥ śivaḥ ।। 41.20 ।।  
  
īśvaraḥ sarvvamuktānāṃ brahmā brahmamayo mahān।  
ādi devaḥ pradhānasyānugrahāya pravakṣyate ।। 41.21 ।।  
  
anādyau svayamutpannāvubhau sūkṣmau tu tau smṛtau ।  
anādisaṃyogayutau sarvakṣetrajñameva ca ।। 41.22 ।।  
  
abuddhi pūrvakaṃ yuktau maśakau tu varau tadā।  
apratyayamanādyaṃ ca sthitāvudakamapsyaśaḥ ।। 41.23 ।।  
  
pravṛtte pūrvataḥ pūrvaṃ punaḥ sarge prapatsyate।  
ajñāguṇaiḥ pravarttante rajaḥsattvatamātmakam ।। 41.24 ।।  
  
pravṛttikāle rajasābhipannamahattvabhūtādiviśeṣyatāñca।  
viśeṣatāṃ cendriyatāñca yānti guṇāvasāne patibhirmanuṣyāḥ ।। 41.25 ।।  
  
satyābhidhyāyinastasya dhyāyinaḥ sannimittakam।  
rajaḥsattvatamā vyaktā vidharmmāṇaḥ parasparam ।। 41.26 ।।  
  
ādyante saṃprapatsyante kṣetratajjñāstu sarvaśaḥ।  
saṃsiddhakāryyakaraṇā utpadyante'bhimāninaḥ ।। 41.27 ।।  
  
sarve satvāḥ prapadyante hyavyaktātpūrvameva ca।  
prasūte yā ca suvahāḥ sādhikāścāpyasādhikā ।। 41.28 ।।  
  
saṃsarantastu te sarve stānaprakaraṇaiḥ saha।  
kāryyāṇi pratipatsyante utpadyante punaḥ punaḥ ।। 41.29 ।।  
  
guṇamātrātmakāścaiva dharmmādharmmau parasparam।  
ārapsantīha cānyonyaṃ vareṇānugraheṇa ca ।। 41.30 ।।  
  
sarve tulyāḥ prasṛṣṭārthaṃ sarggadau yānti vikriyām।  
guṇāstatpratidhāvante tasmāttattasya rocate ।। 41.31 ।।  
  
guṇāste yāni sarvāṇi prāk dṛṣṭeḥ pratipedire।  
tānyeva pratipadyante sṛṣṭamānāḥ punaḥ punaḥ ।। 41.32 ।।  
  
hiṃsrāhiṃsre mṛdukrūre dharmmādharmmāvṛtānṛte।  
tadbhāvitāḥ prapadyante tasmāttattasya rocate ।। 41.33 ।।  
  
mahābhūteṣu nānātvamindriyārtheṣu mūrttiṣu।  
viprayogāśca bhūtānāṃ yuṇobhyaḥ saṃpravarttate ।। 41.34 ।।  
  
ityeṣa vo mayā khyātaḥ punaḥ sargaḥ samāsataḥ।  
samāsādeva vakṣyāmi brahmaṇo'tha samudbhavam ।। 41.35 ।।  
  
avyaktātkāraṇāttasmānnityātsadasadātmakāt।  
pradhāna puruṣābhyāntu jāyate ca maheśvaraḥ ।। 41.36 ।।  
  
sa punaḥ sambhāvayitā jāyate brahmasaṃjñitaḥ।  
sṛjate sa punarlokānabhimānaguṇātmakān ।। 41.37 ।।  
  
ahaṅkārastu mahatastasmādbhūtāni cātmanaḥ।  
yugapat sampravarttante bhūtānyevendriyāṇi ca।  
bhūtabhedāśca bhūtebhya iti sargaḥ pravarttate ।। 41.38 ।।  
  
vistarāvayavasteṣāṃ yathāprajñaṃ yathāśrutam।  
kīrttitaṃ vo yathā pūrvaṃ tathaivābhyupadhāryyatām ।। 41.39 ।।  
  
etacchrutvā naimiṣeyāstadānīṃ lokotpattiṃ saṃsthitiṃ ca vyayañca।  
tasmin satre'vabhṛthaṃ prāpya śuddhāḥ puṇyaṃ lokamṛṣayaḥ prāpnuvanti ।। 41.40 ।।  
  
yathā yūyaṃ vidhivaddevatādīniṣṭvā caivāvabhṛthaṃ prāpya śuddhāḥ।  
tyaktvā dehānāyuṣo'nte kṛtārthānpuṇyāँllokānprāpya yatheṣṭaṃ cariṣyatha ।। 41.41 ।।  
  
ete te naimiṣeyā vai iṣṭvā sṛṣṭvā ca vai tadā।  
jagmuścāvabhṛdhasnātāḥ svargaṃ sarve tu satriṇaḥ ।। 41.42 ।।  
  
viprāstathā yūyamapi ceṣṭvā bahuvidhairmakhaiḥ ।  
āyuṣo'nte tataḥ svargaṃ gantāraḥ stha dvijottamāḥ ।। 41.43 ।।  
  
prakriyā prathame pāde kathāvastuparigrahaḥ।  
anuṣaṅga upodvāta upasaṃhāra eva ca ।। 41.44 ।।  
  
evametaccatuṣpādaṃ purāṇaṃ lokasammatam।  
uvāca bhagavān sākṣādvāyurlokahite rataḥ ।। 41.45 ।।  
  
naimiṣe satramāsādya munibhyo munisattamāḥ ।  
tatprasādādasaṃdigdhaṃ bhūtotpattilayāni ca ।। 41.46 ।।  
  
prādhānikīmimāṃ sṛṣṭiṃ tathaiveśvarakāritām।  
samyagviditvā medhāvī na mohamadhigacchati ।। 41.47 ।।  
  
idaṃ yo brāhmaṇo vidvānitihāsaṃ purātanam।  
śrṛṇuyācchrāvayedvāpi tathādhyāpayate'pi ca ।। 41.48 ।।  
  
sthāneṣu sa mahendrasya modate śāśvatīḥ samāḥ।  
brahmasāyujyago bhūtvā brahmaṇā saha mokṣyate ।। 41.49 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ kīrttimatāṃ kīrttiṃ prajeśānāṃ mahātmanām।  
prathayanpṛthivīśānāṃ brahmabhūyāya gacchati ।। 41.50 ।।  
  
dhanyaṃ yaśasyamāyuṣyaṃ puṇyaṃ vedaiśca sammatam।  
kṛṣṇadvaipāyanenoktaṃ purāṇaṃ brahmavādinā ।। 41.51 ।।  
  
manvantareśvarāṇāṃ ca yaḥ kīrtiṃ prathayedimām।  
devatānāmṛṣīṇāñca bhūridraviṇatejasām।  
sa sarvairmucyate pāpaiḥ puṇyañca mahadāpnuyāt ।। 41.52 ।।  
  
yaścedaṃ śrāvayedvidvānsadā parvaṇi parvaṇi।  
dhūtapāpmā jitasvargo brahmabhūyāya kalpate ।। 41.53 ।।  
  
yaścedaṃ śrāvayecchrāddhe brāhmaṇānpādamantataḥ।  
akṣayaṃ sārvakāmīyaṃ pitṝṃstaccopatiṣṭhati ।। 41.54 ।।  
  
yasmātpurā hyanantīdaṃ purāṇaṃ tena cocyate।  
niruktamasya yo veda sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyate ।। 41.55 ।।  
  
tathaiva triṣu varṇeṣu ye manuṣyāḥ pradhānataḥ।  
itihāsamimaṃ śrutvā dharmāya vidadhe matim ।। 41.56 ।।  
  
yāvantyasya śarīreṣu romakūpāṇi sarvaśaḥ।  
tāvatkoṭi sahasrāṇi varṣāṇāṃ divi modate।  
brahmasāyujyago bhūtvā daivataiḥ saha modate ।। 41.57 ।।  
  
sarvvapāpaharaṃ puṇyaṃ pavitrañca yaśasvi ca।  
brahmā dadau śāstramidaṃ purāṇaṃ mātariśvane ।। 41.58 ।।  
  
tasmāccośanasā prāptaṃ tasmāccāpi bṛhaspatiḥ।  
bṛhaspatistu prevāca savitre tadanantaram ।। 41.59 ।।  
  
savitā mṛtyave prāha mṛtyuścendrāya vai punaḥ।  
indraścāpi vasiṣṭāya so'pi sārasvatāya ca ।। 41.60 ।।  
  
sārasvatastridhāmne ca tridhāmā ca śaradvate।  
śaradvatastriviṣṭāya so'ntarikṣāya dattavān ।। 41.61 ।।  
  
varṣiṇe cāntarikṣo vai so'pi trayyāruṇāya ca।  
trayyāruṇo dhanañjaye sa ca prādātkṛtañjaye ।। 41.62 ।।  
  
kṛta ñjayāttṛṇañjayo bharadvājāya so'pyatha।  
gautamāya bharadvājaḥ so'pi niryyantare punaḥ ।। 41.63 ।।  
  
niryantarastu provāca tathā vājaśravāya ca।  
sa dadau somaśūṣmāya sa dadau tṛṇabindave ।। 41.64 ।।  
  
tṛṇabindustu dakṣāya dakṣaḥ provāca śaktaye।  
śakteḥ parāśaraścāpi garbhasthaḥ śrutavānidam ।। 41.65 ।।  
  
parāśarājjātukarṇastasmāddvaipāyanaḥ prabhuḥ।  
dvaipāyanātpunaścāpi mayā prāptaṃ dvijottamāḥ ।। 41.66 ।।  
  
        śāṃśapāyana uvāca ।।  
  
mayā vai tatpunaḥ proktaṃ putrāyāmitabuddhaye।  
ityeva vācā brahmādiguruṇā samudāhṛtā ।। 41.67 ।।  
  
namaskāryyaśca guravaḥ prayatnena manīṣibhiḥ।  
dhanyaṃ yaśasyamāyuṣyaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvārthasādhakam ।। 41.68 ।।  
  
pāpaghnaṃ niyamenedaṃ śrotavyaṃ brāhmaṇaiḥ sadā।  
nāśucau nāpi pāpāya nāpyasaṃvatsaroṣite ।। 41.69 ।।  
  
nāśraddadhānāviduṣe nāputrāya kathañcana।  
nāhitāya pradātavyaṃ pavitramidamuttamam ।। 41.70 ।।  
  
avyaktaṃ vai yasya yoniṃ vadanti vyaktaṃ dehaṃ kālamantargatañca ।  
vahniṃ vakraṃ candra sūryyau ca netre diśaḥ śrotre ghrāṇamāhuśca vāyum ।। 41.71 ।।  
  
vāyo vedāṃścāntarikṣaṃ śarīraṃ kṣitiṃ pādau tārakā romakūpān ।  
sarvāṇi cāṅgāni tathaiva tāni vidyāssarvā yasya pucchaṃ vadanti ।। 41.72 ।।  
  
taṃ devadevaṃ jananaṃ janānāṃ sarvveṣu lokeṣu pratiṣṭitañca।  
varaṃ varāṇāṃ varadaṃ maheśvaraṃ brahmāṇamādiṃ prayato namasye ।। 41.73 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte sṛṣṭivarṇanaṃ nāma ekacatvāriṃśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 41 ।।
