---
title: 010

---
etacchrutvā vacastasya sutasya viditātmanaḥ।  
uttaraṃ paripapracchuḥ sūtaputraṃ dvijātayaḥ ।। 10.1 ।।  
            ।।śāṃśapāyana uvāca।।  
kathaṃ dvitīyamutpannā bhavānī prāksatī tu yā।  
āsīddākṣāyaṇī pūrvamumā kathama jāyata ।। 10.2 ।।  
menāyāṃ pitṛkanyāyāṃ janayāmāsa śailaruṭ।  
kecaite pitaraścaiva yeṣāṃ menā tu mānasī ।। 10.3 ।।  
mainākaścaiva dauhitro dauhitrī ca tathā hyumā।  
ekaparṇā tathā caiva tathā yā caikapāṭalā ।। 10.4 ।।  
gaṅgā caiva saricchreṣṭā sarvāsāṃ pūrvajā tathā।  
pūrvameva mayoddiṣṭaṃ śrṛṇudhvaṃ mama sarvaśaḥ ।। 10.5 ।।  
aneke pitaraścaiva varttante vka ca vā punaḥ।  
śrotumicchāmi bhadrante śrāddhasya ca paraṃ vidhim ।। 10.6 ।।  
putrāśca te smṛtāḥ keṣāṃ kathañca pitarastu te ।  
pitaraḥ kathamutpannāḥ kasya putrāḥ kimātmakāḥ ।। 10.7 ।।  
svarge tu pitaro'nye ye devānāmapi devatāḥ।  
evaṃ vai śrotumicchāmi pitṝṇāṃ sargamuttamam।  
yathāvaddattamasmābhiḥ śrāddhaṃ prīṇāti vai pitṝn ।। 10.8 ।।  
yadarthaṃ tena dṛśyante tatra kiṃ kāraṇaṃ smṛtam।  
svarge hi ke tu varttante pitaro narake tu ke ।। 10.9 ।।  
abhisandhāya pitaraṃ pituśca pitaraṃ tathā।  
pituḥ pitāmahañcaiva triṣu piṇḍeṣu nāmataḥ ।। 10.10 ।।  
kāni śrāddhāni deyāni kathaṃ gacchanti vai pitṝn ।  
kathañca śaktāste dātuṃ narakasthāḥ phalaṃ punaḥ ।। 10.11 ।।  
ke ceha pitaro nāma kān yajāmo vayaṃ punaḥ ।  
devā api putṝn svarge yajantīti hi naḥ śrutam ।। 10.12 ।।  
etadicchāmi vai śrotuṃ vistareṇa bahuśruta।  
spaṣṭābhidhānamarthaṃ vai tadbhavānvaktumarhati ।। 10.13 ।।  
ṛṣīṇāntu vacaḥ śrutvā sūtastattvārthadarśivān।  
ācacakṣe yathāpraśraṃ ṛṣīṇāṃ mānasaṃ tataḥ ।। 10.14 ।।  
       ।।sūta uvāca।।  
atra vo varṇayiṣyāmi yathāprajñaṃ yathāśrutam।  
manvantareṣu jāyante pitaro devasūnavaḥ ।। 10.15 ।।  
atītānāgate jyeṣṭāḥ kaniṣṭāḥ kramaśastu te।  
devaiḥ sārddhaṃ purātītāḥ pitaro ye'ntareṣu vai।  
varttante sāmprataṃ ye tu tānvai vakṣyāmi niścayāt ।। 10.16 ।।  
śrāddhañcaiṣāṃ manuṣyāṇāṃ śrāddhameva pravarttate।  
devānasṛjan brahmā nāyakṣanniti vai punaḥ ।  
tamutsṛjya tadātmānamasṛjaṃste phalārthinaḥ ।। 10.17 ।।  
te śaptā brahmaṇā mūḍhā naṣṭasaṃjñā bhaviṣyatha।  
na sma kiñcidvijānanti tato loke hyamuhyata ।। 10.18 ।।  
te bhūyaḥ praṇatāḥ sarve yācanti sma pitāmaham।  
anugrahāya lokānāṃ punastānabravītprabhuḥ ।। 10.19 ।।  
prāyaścittaṃ caradhvaṃ vai vyabhicāro hi yaḥ kṛtaḥ ।  
putrānsvānparipṛcchadhvaṃ tato jñānamavāpsyatha ।। 10.20 ।।  
tataste svānsutāṃścaiva prāyaścittajighṛkṣavaḥ।  
apṛcchan saṃyatātmāno vidhivacca mitho mithaḥ ।। 10.21 ।।  
tebhyaste niyatātmānaḥ putrāḥ śaṃsuranekadhā।  
prāyaścittāni dharmajñā vāṅmanaḥ karmmajāni tu ।। 10.22 ।।  
te putrānabruvanprītā labdhasaṃjñā divaukasaḥ।  
yūyaṃ vai pitaro'smākaṃ ye vayaṃ pratibodhitāḥ।  
dharmajñānañca kāmaśca ko varo vaḥ pradīyatām ।। 10.23 ।।  
punastānabravīdbrahmā yūyaṃ vai satyavādinaḥ।  
tasmādyaduktaṃ yuṣmābhistattathā na tadanyathā ।। 10.24 ।।  
uktañca pitaro'smākamiti vai tanayāḥ svakāḥ।  
pitaraste bhaviṣyanti tebhyo'yaṃ dīyatāṃ varaḥ ।। 10.25 ।।  
tenaiva vacasā putrā brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ।  
putrāḥ pitṛttvamājagmuḥ putratvaṃ pitaraḥ punaḥ ।। 10.26 ।।  
tasmātte pitaraḥ putrāḥ pitṛtve teṣu tatsmṛtam ।  
evaṃ smṛtvā pitṝn putrānputrāśca pitarastathā।  
vyājahāra punarbrahmā pidṝnātmavivṛddhaye ।। 10.27 ।।  
yo hyaniṣṭvā pitṝñchrāddhe kriyāṃ kāñicitkariṣyati।  
rākṣasā dānavāścaiva phalaṃ prāpsyanti tasya tat ।। 10.28 ।।  
śrāddhairāpyāyitāścaiva pitaraḥ somamavyayam।  
āpyāyyamānā yuṣmābhirvarddhayiṣyanti nityaśaḥ ।। 10.29 ।।  
śrāddhairāpyāyitaḥ somo lokānāpyāyayiṣyati ।  
kṛtsraṃ saparvatavanaṃ jaṅgamājaṅgamairvṛtam ।। 10.30 ।।  
śrāddhāni puṣṭikāmāśca ye kariṣyanti mānavāḥ।  
tebhyaḥ puṣṭiṃ prajāścaiva dāsyanti pitaraḥ sadā ।। 10.31 ।।  
śrāddhe yebhyaḥ pradāsyanti trīn piṇḍān nāmagotrataḥ।  
sarvatra varttamānāste pitaraḥ prapitāmaham।  
teṣāmāpyāyayiṣyanti śrāddhadānena vai prajāḥ ।। 10.32 ।।  
evamājñā kṛtā pūrvaṃ brahmaṇā parameṣṭinā।  
tenaitatsarvathā siddhaṃ dānamadhyayanaṃ tapaḥ ।। 10.33 ।।  
te tu jñānapradātāraḥ pitaro vo na saṃśayaḥ।  
ityete pitaro devā devāśca pitaraḥ punaḥ ।  
anyonyaṃ pitaro hyete devāśca pitaraśca ha ।। 10.34 ।।  
etadbrahmavacaḥ śrutvā sūtasya vihitātmanaḥ ।  
papracchurmunayo bhūyaḥ sūtaṃ tasmādyaduttaram ।। 10.35 ।।  
            ।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ।।  
kiyanto vai pitṛgaṇāḥ kasminkāle ca te gaṇāḥ ।  
varttante devapravarā devānāṃ somavarddhanāḥ ।। 10.36 ।।  
        ।।sūta uvāca।।  
etadvo'haṃ pravakṣyāmi pitṛsargamanuttamam।  
śaṃyuḥ papraccha yatpūrvaṃ pitaraṃ vai bṛhaspatim ।। 10.37 ।।  
bṛhaspatimupāsīnaṃ sarvajñānārthakovidam।  
putraḥ śaṃyurimaṃ praśnaṃ papraccha vinayānvitaḥ ।। 10.38 ।।  
ka ete pitaro nāma kiyantaḥ ke ca nāmataḥ।  
samudbhūtāḥ kathañcaite pitṛtvaṃ samupāgatāḥ ।। 10.39 ।।  
kasmācca pitaraṃ pūrvaṃ yajñe yujyanti nityaśaḥ।  
kriyāśca sarvā varttante śrāddhapūrvā mahātmanām ।। 10.40 ।।  
kasmai śrāddhāni deyāni kiñca dattaṃ mahāphalam।  
keṣu vāpyakṣayaṃ śrāddhaṃ tīrtheṣu ca nadīṣu ca ।। 10.41 ।।  
keṣu vai sarvamāpnoti śrāddhaṃ kṛtvā dvijottamaḥ।  
kaśca kālo bhaveccrāddhe vidhiḥ kaścānuvarttate ।। 10.42 ।।  
etadicchāmi bhagavan vistareṇa yathātatham।  
vyākhyātumānupūrvyeṇa yatra codāhṛtaṃ mayā ।। 10.43 ।।  
bṛhaspatiridaṃ samyagevaṃ pṛṣṭo mahāmatiḥ।  
vyājahārānupūrvyeṇa praśnaṃ praśnavidāṃ varaḥ ।। 10.44 ।।  
          ।।bṛhaspatiruvāca।।  
kathayiṣyāmi te tāta yanmāṃ tvaṃ paripṛcchasi।  
vinayena yathānyāyaṃ gambhīraṃ praśnamuttamam ।। 10.45 ।।  
dyaurantarikṣaṃ pṛthivī nakṣatrāṇi diśastathā।  
sūryācandramasau caiva tathāhorātrameva ca ।। 10.46 ।।  
na babhūvustadā tāta tamobhūtamidaṃ jagat।  
brahmaiko duścaraṃ tatra cacāra paramaṃ tapaḥ ।। 10.47 ।।  
śaṃyustamabravīdbhūyaḥ pitaraṃ brahmavittamam।  
sarvadaiva vratasnātaṃ sarvajñānavidāṃvaram ।। 10.48 ।।  
kīdṛśaṃ sarvabhūte śastapastepe prajāpatiḥ।  
evamukto bṛhattejā bṛhaspatiruvāca tam ।। 10.49 ।।  
sarveṣāṃ tapasāṃ yuktistapoyogamanuttamam।  
dhyāyaṃstadā tadbhagavāṃstena lokanavāsṛjat ।। 10.50 ।।  
bhūtabhavyāni jñānāni lokānvedāṃśca kṛtsnaśaḥ।  
yogamāviśya tatsṛṣṭaṃ brahmaṇā yogacakṣuṣā ।। 10.51 ।।  
lokāḥ sāntānikā nāma yatra tiṣṭhanti bhāsvarāḥ।  
te vairājā iti khyātā devānāṃ divi devatāḥ ।। 10.52 ।।  
yogena tapasā yuktaḥ pūrvameva tadā prabhuḥ।  
devānasṛjata brahmā yogaṃ yuktvā sanātanam ।। 10.53 ।।  
ādidevā iti khyātā mahāsattvā mahaujasaḥ।  
sarvakāmapradāḥ pūjyā devadānavamānavaiḥ ।। 10.54 ।।  
teṣāṃ sapta samākhyātā gaṇāstrailokyapūjitāḥ ।  
amūrttayastrayasteṣāṃ catvārastu sumūrttayaḥ ।। 10.55 ।।  
upariṣṭātrayasteṣāṃ varttante bhāvamūrttayaḥ।  
teṣāmadhastādvarttante catvāraḥ sūkṣamamūrttayaḥ ।। 10.56 ।।  
tato devāstato bhūmireṣā lokaparamparā।  
loke varttanti te hyasmiṃstebhyaḥ parjanyasambhavaḥ ।  
vṛṣṭirbhavati tairvṛṣṭyā lokānāṃ sambhavaḥ punaḥ ।। 10.57 ।।  
āpyāyayanti te yasmātsomañcānnañca yogataḥ।  
ūcustānvai pitṝṃstasmāllokānāṃ lokasattamāḥ ।। 10.58 ।।  
manojavāḥ svadhābhakṣāḥ sarvakāmaparicchadāḥ।  
lobhamohabhayāpetā niścitāḥ śokavarjitāḥ ।। 10.59 ।।  
ete yogaṃ parityajya prāptā lokānsudarśanān।  
divyāḥ puṇyā mahātmāno vipāpmāno bhavantyuta ।। 10.60 ।।  
tato yugasahasrānte jāyante brahmavādinaḥ।  
pratilabhya punaryogaṃ mokṣaṃ gacchantyamūrttayaḥ ।। 10.61 ।।  
vyaktāvyaktaṃ parityajya mahāyogabalena vā ।  
naśyantyulkeva gagane kṣīṇavidyutprabheva ca ।। 10.62 ।।  
utsṛjya dehajātāni mahāyogabalena ca।  
nirākhyopākhyatāṃ yānti saritaḥ sāgare yathā ।। 10.63 ।।  
kriyayā gurupūjābhirye ca kurvanti nityaśaḥ।  
tābhirāpyāyayantyete pitaro yogavarddhanāḥ ।। 10.64 ।।  
śrāddhe prītāḥ punaḥ somaṃ pitaro yogamāsthitāḥ।  
āpyāyayanti yogena trailokyaṃ yena jīvati ।। 10.65 ।।  
tasmācchrāddhāni deyāni yogibhyo yatnataḥ sadā।  
pitṝṇāṃ hi balaṃ yogo yogātsomaḥ pravarttate ।। 10.66 ।।  
sahasraśastu viprānvai bhojayedyāvadāgatān ।।  
ekastu yogavitprītaḥ sarvānarhati tacchṛṇu ।। 10.67 ।।  
kalpitānāṃ sahasreṇa snātakānāṃ śatena ca।  
yogācāryeṇa yadbhuktaṃ trāyate mahato bhayāt ।। 10.68 ।।  
gṛhasthānāṃ sahasreṇa vānaprasthaśatena ca।  
brahmacārisahasreṇa yogī hyeko viśiṣyate ।। 10.69 ।।  
nāstiko vā vikarmā vā saṅkīrṇastaskaro'pi vā।  
nānyatra kāraṇaṃ dānaṃ yogeṣvāha prajāpatiḥ ।। 10.70 ।।  
pitarastasya tuṣyanti suvṛṣṭeneva karṣakāḥ।  
putro vāpyatha vā pautro dhyāninaṃ bhojayiṣyati ।। 10.71 ।।  
alābhe dhyānibhikṣūṇāṃ bhojayedbrahmacāriṇau ।  
tadalābhepyudāsīnaṃ gṛhasthamapi bhojayet ।। 10.72 ।।  
yastiṣṭedekapādena vāyubhakṣaḥ śataṃ samāḥ ।  
dhyānayogī parastasmāditi brahmānuśāsanam ।। 10.73 ।।  
siddhā hi viprarūpeṇa caranti pṛthivīmimām।  
tasmādatithimāyāntamabhigacchetkṛtāñjaliḥ ।। 10.74 ।।  
pūjayeccārghyapātreṇa veśmanā bhojanena ca।  
ūrvoḥ sāgaraparyantāṃ devā yogeśvarāḥ sadā।  
nānārūpaiścarantyete prajā dharmeṇa pālayan ।। 10.75 ।।  
tasmāddadyācca vai dānaṃ viprāyātithaye naraḥ।  
pradānāni pravakṣyāmi phalañcaiṣāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 10.76 ।।  
aśvamedhasahasreṇa rājasūyaśatena ca।  
puṇḍarīkasahasreṇa yogiṣvāvasatho varam ।। 10.77 ।।  
ādya eṣa gaṇaḥ proktaḥ pitṝṇāmamitaujasām।  
bhāvayansaptakālānvai sthita eṣa gaṇastadā ।। 10.78 ।।  
ata ūraddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi sarvān pitṛgaṇānpunaḥ ।  
santatiṃ saṃsthitiñcaiva bhāvanāñca yathākramam ।। 10.79 ।।  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte śrāddhaprakriyārambho nāma daśamo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 10 ।।

title: 011

---
             ।।sūta uvāca।।  
sapta medhāvatāṃ śreṣṭāḥ svarge pitṛgaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ।  
catvāro mūrtimantaśca trayasteṣāmamūrttayaḥ।  
teṣāṃ lokavisargantu kīrttayiṣye nibodhata ।। 11.1 ।।  
  
yā vai duhitarasteṣāṃ dohitrāścaiva ye smṛtāḥ।  
dharmamūrttidharāsteṣāṃ ye trayaḥ paramā gaṇāḥ ।। 11.2 ।।  
  
nāmāni lokasargañca teṣāṃ vakṣye samāsataḥ।  
lokā virajaso nāmnā yatra tiṣṭhanti bhāsvarāḥ ।। 11.3 ।।  
  
amūrttayaḥ pitṛgaṇāḥ putrāste vai prajāpateḥ।  
virajasya dvijāḥ śreṣṭā vairājā iti viśrutāḥ ।  
eṣa vai prathamaḥ kalpo vairājānāṃ prakīrttitaḥ ।। 11.4 ।।  
  
teṣāntu mānasī kanyā menā nāma mahāgireḥ।  
patnī himavataḥ śubhra yasyāṃ maināka ucyate ।। 11.5 ।।  
  
jātaḥ sarvaiṣadhidharaḥ sarvaratnākarātmavān।  
parvataḥ pravaraḥ puṇyaḥ krauñcastasyātmajo'bhavat ।। 11.6 ।।  
  
tisraḥ kanyāstu menāyāṃ janayāmāsa śailarāṭ।  
aparṇāmekaparṇāñca tṛtīyāmekapāṭalām ।। 11.7 ।।  
  
āśrite dve hyaparṇā tu aniketā tapo'carat।  
nyagrodhamekaparṇī tu pāṭalāmekapāṭalā।  
śataṃ varṣasahasrāṇi duścaraṃ devadānavaiḥ ṣa।। 11.8 ।।  
  
āhāramekaparṇena ekaparṇī samācarat।  
pāṭalenaiva caikena vidadhyādekapāṭalā ।। 11.9 ।।  
  
pūrṇe pūrṇe sahasre dve āhāraṃ vai prajakratuḥ ।  
ekā tatra nirāhārā tāṃ mātā pratyabhāṣata ।। 11.10 ।।  
  
niṣedhayantī hyumeti mātā srehena duḥkhitā।  
sā tathoktatayā devī mātrā duścaracāriṇī ।। 11.11 ।।  
  
umeti sā mahābhāgā triṣu lokeṣu viśrutā ।  
tatheti nāmnā tenāsau niruktā karmaṇā śubhā ।। 11.12 ।।  
  
etattu trikumārīkaṃ jagatsyāsyati śāśvatam।  
etāsāṃ tapasā dṛptaṃ yāvadbhūmiddhariṣyati ।। 11.13 ।।  
  
tapaḥśarīrāstāḥ sarvāstisro yogabalānvitāḥ।  
devyastāḥ sumahābhāgāḥ sarvvāśca sthirayauvanāḥ ।। 11.14 ।।  
  
sarvāśca brahmavādinyaḥ sarvā ścaivorddhvaretasaḥ।  
umā tāsāṃ variṣṭā ca śreṣṭā ca varavarṇinī ।। 11.15 ।।  
  
mahāyogabalopetā mahādevamupasthitā।  
dantakāṇvośanā tasyāḥ putro vai bhṛgu nandanaḥ ।। 11.16 ।।  
  
asitasyaikaparṇī tu patnī sādhvī dṛḍhavratā।  
dattā himavatā tasmai yogācāryāya dhīmate।  
devalaṃ suṣuve sā tu brahmiṣṭhaṃ mānasaṃ sutam ।। 11.17 ।।  
  
yā caitāsāṃ kumārīṇāṃ tṛtīyā tvekapāṭalā।  
putraṃ śataśilākasya jaigīṣavyamupasthitā ।। 11.18 ।।  
  
tasyāpi śaṅkhalikhitau smṛtau putrāvayonijau।  
ityetā vai mahābhāgāḥ kanyā himavataḥ śubhāḥ ।। 11.19 ।।  
  
rudrāṇī sā tu pravarā svaguṇairatiricyate।  
anyonyaprītiranayorumāśaṅkarayoryathā ।। 11.20 ।।  
  
śleṣaṃ saṃsaktayorjñātvā śaṅkitaḥ kila vṛtrahā।  
tābhyāṃ maithunasaktābhyāmapatyodbhavabhīruṇā।  
tayoḥ sakāśamindreṇa preṣito havyavāhanaḥ ।। 11.21 ।।  
  
anayo rativighnañca tvamācara hutāśana।  
sarvatra gata eva tvaṃ na doṣo vidyate tadā ।। 11.22 ।।  
  
ityevamukte tu tathā vahninā ca tathā kṛtam।  
umādehaṃ samutsṛjya śukraṃ bhūmau visarjitam ।। 11.23 ।।  
  
tato ruṣitayā devyā śapto'gniḥ śāṃśapāyana।  
idaṃ coktavatī vahniṃ roṣagadghadayā girā ।। 11.24 ।।

evamukte tu pitṛbhistadā trailokyabhāvanaḥ।  
prajānāmadhipo brahmā viśavānitīdamabravīt ।। 14.7 ।।  
  
         ।।brahmovāca।।  
mahātejā mahādevastapasā taistu tāpitaḥ।  
tapasā tena suprītaḥ kaṃ kāmaṃ vidadhāmi vaḥ ।। 14.8 ।।  
  
evamuktāstadā viśve brahmaṇā lokakartṛṇā।  
ūcuste sahitāḥ sarvve brahmāṇaṃ lokabhāvinam ।। 14.9 ।।  
  
śrāddhe'smākaṃ bhavedaṃśo hyeṣa naḥ kāṃkṣito varaḥ ।  
pratyuvāca tato brahmā tānvai tridivapūjitān ।। 14.10 ।।  
  
bhaviṣyatyevameveti kāṃkṣito vo varastu yaḥ।  
pitṛbhistu tathetyuktvā evametanna saṃśayaḥ ।। 14.11 ।।  
  
sahāsmābhistu vo bhāvyaṃ yatkiñcit kriyate tviha।  
asmākaṃ kalpite śrāddhe yuṣmānagrāsanaṃ ha vai ।। 14.12 ।।  
  
bhaviṣyati manuṣyeṣu satyametadbravīmi te।  
mālyairgandhaistathānnena yuṣmānagrerjayiṣyati ।। 14.13 ।।  
  
pradātā ceti yuṣmākamasmākaṃ dāsyate tataḥ।  
visarjanamathāsmākaṃ pūrvvaṃ paścāttu devatāḥ ।। 14.14 ।।  
  
rakṣaṇañcaiva śrāddhasya ātithyañca vidhidvayam।  
bhūtānāṃ devatānāñca pitṝṇāṃ śrāddha karmaṇi।  
evaṃ vidhikṛtaḥ samyak sarvvametadbhaviṣyati ।। 14.15 ।।  
  
evaṃ dattvā varaṃ teṣāṃ brahmā pitṛgaṇaiḥ saha।।  
bhūtānugrahakṛddevaḥ sañcacāra yathāsukham ।। 14.16 ।।  
  
vede pañca mahāyajñā narāṇāṃ samudāhṛtāḥ ।  
etānpañca mahāyajñānnirvapetsatataṃ naraḥ ।। 14.17 ।।  
  
yatra yāsyanti dātāraḥ saṃsthānaṃ vai nibhodhata।  
nirbhayaṃ nirahaṅkāraṃ niḥśokaṃ nirvyathaklamam।  
brahmasthānamavāpnoti sarvakāmapuraskṛtam ।। 14.18 ।।  
  
śūdreṇāpi prakarttavyāḥ pañcaite mantravarjitāḥ।  
ato'nyathā tu yo bhuṅkte sa ṛṇaṃ nityamaśnute ।। 14.19 ।।  
  
ṛṇañca bhuṅkte pāpātmā yaḥ pacedātmakāraṇāt।  
tasmānnirvarttayetpañca mahāyajñānsadā budhaḥ ।। 14.20 ।।  
  
naivedyaṃ kecidicchanti jīvatyapi prayatnataḥ।  
udakpūrvvaṃ baliṃ kuryādudakumbhaṃ tathaiva ca ।। 14.21 ।।  
  
baliṃ suviditaṃ kuryāduccāduccataraṃ kṣipet ।  
paraśrṛṅgagavāṃ pūrvva baliṃ sūkṣmaṃ samutkṣipet ।। 14.22 ।।  
  
na vinedyo bhavet piṇḍaḥ pitṝṇāṃ yastu jīvati।  
iṣṭenānnena bhakṣyaiśca bhojayeta yathāvidhi।  
vidhānaṃ vedavihitametadvakṣyāmi yatnataḥ ।। 14.23 ।।  
  
devadevā mahātmāno hyetepi pitaro hyuta।  
icchanti kiñcidācāryyāḥ paścāt piṇḍanivedanam ।। 14.24 ।।  
  
pūjanañcaiva viprāṇāṃ sarvvameva hi nityaśaḥ।  
taddhi dharmārthakuśalānityuvāca bṛhaspatiḥ ।। 14.25 ।।  
  
pūrvvaṃ nivedayetpiṇḍaṃ paścādviprāṃśca bhojayet।  
yogātmāno mahātmānaḥ pitaro yoga sambhavāḥ।  
somamāpyāyayantyete pitaro yogamāsthitāḥ ।। 14.26 ।।  
  
tasmāddadyāc‌chuciḥ piṇḍān yogibhyastatparāyaṇaḥ ।  
pitṝṇāṃ hi bhavedetatsākṣādiva hutaṃ haviḥ ।। 14.27 ।।  
  
brāhmaṇānāṃ sahasrebhyo yogī cāgrāsane yadi।  
yajamānañca bhoktṝṃśca naurivāmbhasi tārayet ।। 14.28 ।।  
  
asatāṃ pragraho yatra satāñcaiva vimānanā।  
daṇḍo devakṛtastatra sadyaḥ patati tāruṇaḥ ।। 14.29 ।।  
  
hitvāgamaṃ sadharmāṇaṃ bāliśaṃ yatra bhojayet।  
ādikarma samutsṛjya dātā tatra vinaśyati ।। 14.30 ।।  
  
piṇḍamagnau sadā dadyādbhogārthī tu prayatnataḥ ।  
prajārthī pata (tna)ye dadyānmadhyamaṃ tatra pūrvakam ।। 14.31 ।।  
  
uttamāṃ dyutimanvicchan goṣu nityaṃ prayacchati।  
prajñāṃ pūjāṃ yaśaḥ kīrttiṃ goṣu nityaṃ prayacchati ।। 14.32 ।।  
  
prārthayandīrghamāyuśca vāyasebhyaḥ prayacchati।  
saukumāryamathānvicchan kukkuṭebhyaḥ prayacchati ।। 14.33 ।।  
  
evametatsamuddiṣṭaṃ piṇḍanirvapaṇāt phalam।  
ākāśaṃ śamayedvāpi sthitau sudakṣiṇāmukhaḥ।  
pitṝṇāṃ sthānamākāśaṃ dakṣiṇā caiva digbhavet ।। 14.34 ।।  
  
ekaṃ viprāḥ punaḥ prāhuḥ piṇḍoddharaṇamagrataḥ।  
anujñāte tu tairviprairvānamudviyatāmiti ।। 14.35 ।।  
  
puṣpāṇāṃ caphalānāṃ ca bhakṣyāṇāmannatastathā।  
agramuddhṛtya sarveṣāṃ juhuyājjātavedasi ।। 14.36 ।।  
  
bhakṣyamannaṃ tathā peyamanuttamaphalāni ca।  
hutvā cāgnau tataḥ piṇḍānnirvapeddakṣiṇāmukhaḥ ।। 14.37 ।।  
  
snigdhairbhakṣyaiḥ sugandhaiśca tarpayeta rasaistathā।  
ekāgraḥ paryupāsīta prayataḥ prāñjaliḥ sthitaḥ।  
tatparaḥ śraddadhānaśca kāmānāpnoti mānavaḥ ।। 14.38 ।।  
  
akṣudratvaṃ kṛtajñatvaṃ dākṣiṇyaṃ satkṛtañca yat।  
tato yajñañca dānañca prayacchanti pitāmahāḥ ।। 14.39 ।।  
  
ataḥ paraṃ vidhiṃ saumyaṃ bhuktavatsu dvijātiṣu।  
ānupūrvyeṇa vidhinā tanme nigadataḥ śrṛṇu ।। 14.40 ।।  
  
prokṣya bhūmimathoddhṛtya pūrvaṃ pitṛparāyaṇaḥ।  
tato'tra vikiraṃ kuryāt vidhidṛṣṭena karmaṇā ।। 14.41 ।।  
  
svadhāṃ vācya tato viprā vidhivadbhūri dakṣiṇān।  
annaśeṣamanujñāpya satkṛtya dvijasattamān ।  
prāñjaliḥ prayataścaiva anugamya visarjayet ।। 14.42 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte śrāddhakalpo nāma caturdaśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 14 ।।

        ।।bṛhaspatiruvāca।।  
sakṛdabhyarcitāḥ prītā bhavanti pitaro'nyayāḥ।  
yogātmāno mahātmāno vipāpmāno mahaujasaḥ ।। 15.1 ।।  
  
pretya ca svargalābhāya kāmaiśvaryaṃ suvistaram ।  
yeṣāṃ cāpyanugṛhṇanti mokṣaprāptikrameṇa tu ।। 15.2 ।।  
  
tāni vakṣyāmyahaṃ saumyāḥ sarāṃsi saritastathā।  
tīrthāni caiva puṇyāni deśāñjailāṃstathāśramān ।। 15.3 ।।  
  
puṇyo yastriṣu lokeṣvamarakaṇṭakaparvataḥ।  
parvataḥ pravaraḥ puṇyaḥ siddhacāraṇasevitaḥ ।। 15.4 ।।  
  
yatra varṣasahasrāṇi prayutānyarbudāni ca।  
tapaḥ suduścaraṃ tepe bhagavānaṅgirāḥ purā ।। 15.5 ।।  
  
yatra mṛtyorgatirnāsti tathaivāsurarakṣasām।  
na bhayaṃ caiva vā'lakṣmīryāvadbhūmirdhariṣyati ।। 15.6 ।।  
  
tejasā yaśasā caiva bhrājate sa nagottamaḥ।  
śrṛṅgamālyavato nityaṃ vahniḥ saṃvartako yathā ।। 15.7 ।।  
  
mṛdavaśca sugandhāśca hemābhāḥ priyadarśanāḥ ।  
śāntāḥ kuśā iti khyātāḥ pibandakṣiṇanarmadām ।। 15.8 ।।  
  
dṛṣṭavān svargasopānaṃ bhagavānaṅgirāḥ purā।  
agnihotre mahātejāḥ prastarārthakuśottamān ।। 15.9 ।।  
  
teṣu darbheṣu yaḥ piṇḍānamarakaṇṭakaparvate।  
dadyātsakṛdapi prājñastasya vakṣyāmi yatphalam ।। 15.10 ।।  
  
tadbhavatyakṣayaṃ śrāddhaṃ pitṝṇāṃ prītivarddhanam ।  
antarddhānaṃ ca gacchanti kṣetramāsādya tatsadā ।। 15.11 ।।  
  
tatra jvālārasaḥ puṇyo dṛśyate'dyāpi sarvaśaḥ।  
saśalyānāṃ ca sattvānāṃ viśalyakaraṇī nadī ।। 15.12 ।।  
  
prāgdakṣiṇā tu sāvarttā vāpī sā parvatottame।  
kaliṅgadeśapārśvārddhe pitṝṇāṃ prītivarddhanam ।। 15.13 ।।  
  
siddhakṣetramṛṣiśreṣṭā yaduktaṃ paramaṃ bhuvi।  
sammato devadaityānāṃ ślokamapyuśanā jagau ।। 15.14 ।।  
  
dhanyāste puruṣā loke ye prāpyāmarakaṇṭakam।  
pitṝnsantarpayiṣyanti śrāddhe pitṛparāyaṇāḥ ।। 15.15 ।।  
  
alpena tapasā siddhiṃ gamiṣyanti na saṃśayaḥ।  
sakṛdevārcitāstatra svargamamarakaṇṭake ।। 15.16 ।।  
  
mahendraparvate ramye puṇyaṃ śakraniṣevitam।  
tatrāruhya bhavet prītiḥ śrāddhaṃ caiva mahatphalam ।। 15.17 ।।  
  
bilvādhaḥśikhare yuktā divyaṃ cakṣuḥ pravarttate।  
adṛśyaṃ caiva bhūtānāṃ devavaccarate mahīm ।। 15.18 ।।  
  
saptagodāvare caiva gokarṇe ca tapovane।  
aśvamedhaphalaṃ tatra snātvā ca labhatenaraḥ ।। 15.19 ।।  
  
dhūtapāpasthalaṃ prāpya pūtaḥ snātvā bhavennaraḥ।  
rudrastatra tapastepe devadevo maheśvaraḥ ।। 15.20 ।।  
  
gokarṇe varṇitaṃ viprairnastikānāṃ nidarśanam।  
abrāhmaṇasya sāvitrī paṭhataḥ sampraṇaśyati ।। 15.21 ।।  
  
devarṣibhavane śrṛṅge siddhacāraṇasevite।  
āruhya taṃ tu niyamāttato yānti triviṣṭapam ।। 15.22 ।।  
  
divyaiśvandanavṛkṣaiśca pādapairupaśobhitam।  
āpaḥ svādanasampṛktā vahanti satataṃyataḥ ।। 15.23 ।।  
  
nadī pravarttate tābhyastāmraparṇīti nāmataḥ।  
yoṣeva samahākhedā dakṣiṇaṃ yāti sāgaram ।। 15.24 ।।  
  
nadyāstasyāstu yā āpo mūrcchamānā mahodadhau।  
śaṅkhā bhavanti muktāśca jāyante śaṅkhamuktikāḥ ।। 15.25 ।।  
  
udakānayanaṃ kṛtvā śaṅkhamauktikasaṃyutam।  
ādhibhirvyādhibhiścaiva muktā yāntyamarāvatīm ।। 15.26 ।।  
  
candanebhyaḥ prayuktānāṃ śaṅkhānāṃ mauktikasya ca।  
pāpa kartṝnapi pitṝṃśtārayanti yathā śrutiḥ ।। 15.27 ।।  
  
candratīrthe kumāryyāntu kāveryyāṃ prabhave'kṣaye।  
śrīparvatasya tīrtheṣu vaikṛte ca tathā girau ।। 15.28 ।।  
  
ekasthā yatra dṛśyante vṛkṣā hyośiraparvate।  
pālāśāḥ khādirā bilvā plakṣāśvatthavikaṅkatāḥ ।। 15.29 ।।  
  
etaddhi maṇḍalaṃ siddhaṃ yajñīyaṃ dvijasattamāḥ।  
asmin muktvā jano'ṅgāni kṣipraṃ yātyamarāvatīm ।। 15.30 ।।  
  
karmāṇi svaprayuktāni sidhyanti prabhavātyaye ।  
duṣprasaktāni pitṛṣu prayuktāni bhavantyuta ।। 15.31 ।।  
  
pitṝṇāṃ duhitā puṇyā narmadā saritāṃ varā।  
tatra śrāddhāni dattāni akṣayāṇi bhavantyuta ।। 15.32 ।।  
  
māṭharasya vane puṇye siddhacāraṇasevite।  
antarddhānaṃ na gacchanti sattāstasminmahāgirau ।। 15.33 ।।  
  
vindhye caiva girau puṇye dharmādharmanidarśanam।  
pāpadhārāṃ na paśyanti dhārāṃ paśyanti sādhavaḥ ।। 15.34 ।।  
  
tasyāṃ na dṛśyate pāpaṃ keṣāñcit pāpakarmaṇām।  
spaṣṭā bhavati sā dhārā prāyaśaḥ śubhakarmaṇām ।। 15.35 ।।  
  
kauśalāyāṃ mataṅgasya vāpī pāpaniṣūdanī।  
snātāstasyāṃ divaṃ yānti kāmacāravihaṅgamāḥ ।। 15.36 ।।  
  
kumārakośalātīrthe parvate pālapañjare।  
pāṇḍu kūle samudrānte paṇḍārakavane tathā ।। 15.37 ।।  
  
vimale ca vipāpe ca satkṛtya prabhave'bhaye।  
bhīvṛkṣe gṛdhrakūṭe ca jambūmārge ca nityaśaḥ ।। 15.38 ।।  
  
asitasya guroḥ puṇye yogācāryasya dhīmataḥ।  
tatrāpi śrāddhamānantyamasitāyāñca nityaśaḥ ।। 15.39 ।।  
  
puṣkareṣvakṣayaṃ śrāddhaṃ tapaścaiva mahāphalam।  
mahodadhau prabhāse ca tasmādevaṃ vinirddiśet ।। 15.40 ।।  
  
devikāyāṃ vṛṣo nāma kūpaḥ siddhaniṣevitaḥ।  
samutpatanti tasyāpo gavāṃ śabdena nityaśaḥ ।। 15.41 ।।  
  
yogeśvaraiḥ sadā juṣṭaḥ sarvapāpabahiṣkṛtaiḥ।  
dadyācchrāddhantu yastasmiṃstasya vakṣyāmi yatphalam ।। 15.42 ।।  
  
akṣayaṃ sārvakāmīyaṃ śrāddhaṃ prīṇāti vai pitṝn।  
jātavedaḥ śilā tatra sākṣādagneḥ sanātanī ।। 15.43 ।।  
  
yastvāgniṃ praviśettatra nākapṛṣṭe sa modate।  
agniḥ śāntaḥ punarjātastasmindattaṃ tadakṣayam ।। 15.44 ।।  
  
daśāśvamadike tīrthe tīrthe pañcāśvamedhike।  
yathoddiṣṭaṃ phalaṃ teṣāṃ kratūnāṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ ।। 15.45 ।।  
  
khyātaṃ hayaśiro nāma tīrthaṃ sadyo varapradam।  
śrāddhaṃ tatra tadākṣayyaṃ dattvā svarge ca modate ।। 15.46 ।।  
  
śrāddhaṃ kumbhe vimucyanti jñeyaṃ pāpaniṣūdanam।  
śrāddhaṃ tatrākṣayaṃ proktaṃ japyahomatapāṃsi ca ।। 15.47 ।।  
  
ajhatuṅge śubhe tīrthe tarpayetsatataṃ pitṝn।  
dṛśyate parvasu cchāyā yatra nityaṃ divaukasām।  
pṛthivyāmakṣayaṃ dattaṃ nirujā yatra pāṇḍavāḥ ।। 15.48 ।।  
  
yogeśvaraiḥ sadā juṣṭaṃ sarvapāpabahiṣkṛtaiḥ।  
dadyācchrāddhantu yastasmistasya vakṣyāmi yatphalam ।। 15.49 ।।  
  
arcitāstena vai sākṣādbhavanti pitaraḥ sadā।  
asmiँlloke vaśī yaḥ syātpretya svarge sa modate ।। 15.50 ।।  
  
prāyaśaḥ pravaraḥ puṇyaḥ śivo nāma hradastathā।  
tatra vyāsasaraḥ puṇyaṃ divyaṃ brahmasarastathā ।। 15.51 ।।  
  
ujjantaḥ parvataḥ puṇyo vasiṣṭhasya mahātmanaḥ।  
ṛgyajuḥsāmaśirasaḥ kāpotaḥ puṣpasāhvayaḥ।  
ākhyātaḥ pañcamo vedaḥ sṛṣṭvā hyeteṣu brahmaṇā ।। 15.52 ।।  
  
gatvaitān mucyate pāpāddvijo vahniḥ sanātanaḥ ।।  
  
śrāddhañcānantyameteṣu japya homatapāṃsi ca ।। 15.53 ।।  
  
puṇḍarīke mahātīrthe puṇḍarīkasamaṃ phalam।  
brahmatīrthe mahātīrthe aśvamedhaphalaṃ labhet ।। 15.54 ।।  
  
sindhusāgarasambhede tadā pañcanade'kṣayam ।  
kīrakātmā tataḥ puṇyo maṇḍavāyāṃ ca parvate ।। 15.55 ।।  
  
deyaṃ saptahrade śrāddhaṃ mānase ca viśeṣataḥ।  
mahākūṭe ca vande ca girau trirau trikakude tathā ।। 15.56 ।।  
  
sandhyāyāṃ ca mahāvedyāṃ dṛśyate mahadadbhutam।  
aśraddadhānānnābhyeti sābhyeti ca dhṛtavratān ।। 15.57 ।।  
  
jātavedaḥ śilā tatra sākṣādagneḥ sanātanī।  
śrāddhāni cāgnikāryaṃ ca tatra kuryāt sadākṣayam ।। 15.58 ।।  
  
saṃśrayitvaikamekena sāyāhnaṃ prati nityaśaḥ।  
tasmindeyaṃ sadā śrāddhaṃ pitṝṇāmakṣayārthinā ।। 15.59 ।।  
  
kṛtātmā vā'kṛtātmā vā yatra vijñāyate naraḥ।  
svargyamārgapradaṃ nāma tīrthaṃ sadyo varapradam।  
vairāṇyutsṛjya tasmistu divaṃ saptarṣayo gatāḥ ।। 15.60 ।।  
  
adyāpi tāni dṛśyante vairāṇyeva gatāni tu।  
snātvā svargamavāpnoti tasmistīrthottame naraḥ ।। 15.61 ।।  
  
khyātamāyatanaṃ tatra nandisiddha niṣevitam।  
nandīśvarasya yā mūrttirdurājārairna dṛśyate ।। 15.62 ।।  
  
dṛśyante kāñcanā yūpāḥ sañciṣye bhāskarodaye।  
kṛtvā pradakṣiṇaṃ te tu gacchantyantarhitā divam ।। 15.63 ।।  
  
sarvataśca kurukṣetraṃ sutīrthañca viśeṣataḥ।  
puṇyaṃ sanatkumārasya yogeśasya mahātmanaḥ।  
kīrttyate ca tilāndattvā pitṝṇāṃ vai sadā'kṣayam ।। 15.64 ।।  
  
ojase cākṣayaṃ śrāddhaṃ dharmarājaniveśane।  
śrāddhaṃ dattamamāvasyāṃ vidhinā ca yathākramam ।। 15.65 ।।  
  
punaḥ sannihitānāṃ vai kurukṣetre viśeṣataḥ।  
arcayedvā pitṝṃstatra satputrastvanṛṇo bhavet ।। 15.66 ।।  
  
vinaśane sarasvatyāṃ plakṣapraśravaṇe tathā।  
vyāsatīrthe sarasvatyāṃ brahmakṣetre viśeṣataḥ ।। 15.67 ।।  
  
deyamoṅkārapaṭhanaiḥ śrāddhamakṣayamicchatā।  
sarvataścaiva gaṅgyāṃ maināke ca nagottame ।। 15.68 ।।  
  
yamunāprabhave caiva sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyate ।  
atyuṣṇāścātiśītāśca āpastatra nidarśanam ।। 15.69 ।।  
  
yamasya bhaginī puṇyā mārttaṇḍaduhitā tathā।  
tatrākṣayaṃ tadā śrāddhaṃ pitṛbhiḥ pūrvakīrttitam ।। 15.70 ।।  
  
brahmānugahrade snātvā sadyo bhavati brāhmaṇaḥ।  
tasmin hi śrāddhamānantyaṃ japahomatapāṃsi ca ।। 15.71 ।।  
  
sthāṇubhūtaścaraṃstatra vasiṣṭo vai mahātapāḥ।  
adyāpi yatra dṛśyante pādapā maṇicarccitāḥ ।। 15.72 ।।  
  
tulā tu dṛśyate yatra dharmādharmapradarśinī।  
yayā vai tulitaṃ vipraistīrthānāṃ phalamuttamam ।। 15.73 ।।  
  
pitṝṇāṃ duhitā yogā gandhakālīti viśrutā।  
caturtho brahmaṇaścāṃśaḥ parāśarakulodvahaḥ ।। 15.74 ।।  
  
vyasya tvekaṃ caturddhā tu vedaṃ dhīmān mahāmuniḥ।  
mahāyogaṃ mahātmānaṃ yo vyāsaṃ janayiṣyati ।। 15.75 ।।  
  
acchodakaṃ nāma saro yatrācchodā samucchritā ।  
matsyayonau punarjātā niyogādvāraṇena tu ।। 15.76 ।।  
  
tasyā yatrāśramaḥ puṇyaḥ puṇyakṛdbhirniṣevitaḥ।  
sakṛddattaṃ tu vai śrāddhamakṣayaṃ samudāhṛtam।  
tasyāṃ yoga samādhāne dattaṃ yugapadudbhavet ।। 15.77 ।।  
  
kuberatuṅge vyāmocce vyāsatīrthe tathaiva ca।  
puṇyaḥ sa brāhmaṇo dadyāchrāddhamānantyamakṣayam ।। 15.78 ।।  
  
siddhaistu sevitā nityaṃ dṛśyate nākṛtātmabhiḥ।  
anivarttanaṃ tu nandāyāṃ vedyāṃ prāguttare diśi ।। 15.79 ।।  
  
siddhakṣetraṃ tu vai juṣṭaṃ yatprāpya na nivarttate।  
mahālaye padaṃ nyastaṃ mahādevena dhīmatā ।। 15.80 ।।  
  
devyālaye tapastaptvā ekapādena īśvaraḥ।  
nīhāraśca yugaṃ divyamumātuṅge sthitaṃ jalam ।। 15.81 ।।  
  
umā tuṅge bhṛgostuṅge brahmatuṅge mahālaye।  
kādravatyāṃ ca śāṇḍilyāṃ guhāyāṃ vāmanasya ca ।। 15.82 ।।  
  
gatvā caitāni pūtaḥ syācchrāddhamakṣayameva ca।  
japo homastathā dhyānaṃ yatkicitsukṛtaṃ bhavet ।। 15.83 ।।  
  
brahmacaryaṃ yajante vai gurubhaktāḥ śataṃ samāḥ ।  
evamādīni sadyastāṃ snātvā prāpnoti satphalm ।। 15.84 ।।  
  
kumāradhārā tatraiva dṛṣṭā pāpapraṇāśanī।  
yānāsanaṃ ca tatraiva sadyaḥ syādyat pradṛśyate ।। 15.85 ।।  
  
śailakīrttipurābhyāśe kāmānāpnoti puṣkalān।  
adṛśyaḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ devavaccarate mahīm ।। 15.85 ।।  
  
kāśyapasya mahātīrthaṃ kālasarpiriti śrutam।  
tatra śrāddhāni deyāni nityamakṣayamicchatā ।। 15.86 ।।  
  
kāśyapasya mahātīrthaṃ kālasarpiriti śrutam।  
tatra śrāddhāni deyāni nityamakṣayamicchatā ।। 15.87 ।।  
  
akṣayaṃ tu bhavecchrāddhaṃ śālagrāmasamantataḥ।  
dṛṣṭyā na dṛśyate tatra pratyakṣamakṛtātmanā ।। 15.88 ।।  
  
pratyādeśo hyaśiṣṭānāṃ śiṣṭānāṃ ca niveśanam ।  
tatra caiva hrade puṇye divyo vai nāgarāḍ yataḥ ।। 15.89 ।।  
  
piṇḍaṃ gṛhṇāti hisatāṃ na gṛhṇātyasatāṃ hi saḥ।  
atipradīptairbhujagairbhoktumannaṃ na śakyate ।। 15.90 ।।  
  
pratyakṣaṃ dṛśyate dharmastīrthayoranayordvayoḥ।  
devadāruvane cāpi cārayestaṃ nidarśanam ।। 15.91 ।।  
  
vidhūtāni tu pāpāni dṛśyante sukṛtātmanā ।  
bhāgīrathyāṃ prayāge ca nityamakṣayyamucyate ।। 15.92 ।।  
  
kālañjare daśārṇāyāṃ naimiṣe kurujāṅgale।  
vārāṇasyāṃ nagaryāṃ tu deyaṃ śrāddhaṃ tu yatnataḥ ।। 15.93 ।।  
  
tasyāṃ yogeśvaro nityaṃ tattasyāṃ dattamakṣayam।  
dattvā caiteṣu pūtaḥ syācchrāddhamānantyameva ca ।। 15.94 ।।  
  
tapo homastathā dhyānaṃ yat kiñcitsukṛtaṃ bhavet।  
lauhitye vaitaraṇyāṃ vai svarṇavedyāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 15.95 ।।  
  
sakṛdeva samudrānte dṛśyate puṇyakarmabhiḥ।  
gayāyāṃ dharmapṛṣṭe ca sarasi brahmaṇastathā ।। 15.96 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ gṛdhrakūṭe ca śrāddhaṃ dattaṃ mahāphalam।  
himañca patate tatra samantāt pañcayojanam ।। 15.97 ।।  
  
bharatasyāśrame puṇye'raṇyaṃ puṇyatamaṃ smṛtam ।  
mataṅgasya padaṃ tatra dṛśyate māṃsa cakṣuṣā ।। 15.98 ।।  
  
khyāpitaṃ dharmmasarvvasvaṃ lokasyāsya nidarśanam।  
evaṃ pañcavanaṃ puṇyaṃ puṇyakṛdbhirniṣevitam।  
yasminpāṇḍuviśāleti tīrthaṃ sadyo nidarśanam ।। 15.99 ।।  
  
tulāmānaistathā cāpaiḥ śāstraiśca vividhaistathā।  
unmajjanti tathā lagne ye vai pāpakṛto janāḥ ।। 15.100 ।।  
  
tṛtīyāyāṃ tathā pāde niḥsvare pāvamaṇḍale।  
mahāhrade vai kauśikyāṃ dattaṃ śrāddhaṃ mahāphalam ।। 15.101 ।।  
  
muṇḍapṛṣṭe padaṃ nyastaṃ mahādevena dhīmatā।  
bahūndaveyugāṃstaptvā tapastīvraṃ suduścaram ।। 15.102 ।।  
  
alpenāpyatra kālena naro dharmmaparāyaṇaḥ।  
pāpmānamutsṛjatyāśu jīrṇatvacamivoragaḥ ।। 15.103 ।।  
  
siddhānāṃ prītijananaiḥ pāpā nāñca bhayaṅkaraiḥ।  
lelihānairmahābhogai rakṣitantu divāniśam ।। 15.104 ।।  
  
nāmnā kanakanandīti tīrthaṃ trailokyaviśrutam।  
udīcyāṃ muṇḍapṛṣṭhasya devarṣi gaṇasevitam।  
tatra srātvā divaṃ yānti kāmacārā vihaṅgamāḥ ।। 15.105 ।।  
  
dattaṃ cāpi tathā śrāddhamakṣayaṃ samudāhṛtam।  
ṛṇaistribhistadā snātvā nikṣiṇoti narottamaḥ ।। 15.106 ।।  
  
tīre tu sarasastatra devasyāyatanaṃ mahat।  
āruhya tajjapaṃstatra siddho yāti divaṃ tataḥ ।। 15.107 ।।  
  
uttaraṃ mānasaṃ gatvā siddhiṃ prāpnotyanuttamām ।  
tatra gatvā puraśreṣṭhaṃ dṛśyate mahadadbhutam ।। 15.108 ।।  
  
tasminnirvarttayecchrāddhaṃ yathāśakti yathābalam।  
kāmān sa labhate divyān mokṣopāyaṃ ca nityaśaḥ ।। 15.109 ।।  
  
mānase sarasi śreṣṭe dṛśyate mahadadbhutam।  
divaścyutā mahābhāgā hyantarikṣe virājate ।। 15.110 ।।  
  
gaṅgā tripathagā devī somapādāccyutā bhuvi।  
ākāśe dṛśyate tatra toraṇaṃ sūryyasannibham ।। 15.111 ।।  
  
jāmbūnadamayaṃ divyaṃ svargadvāramivāyatam।  
yataḥ pravarttate bhūyaḥ pūrvvasāgaramantimam ।। 15.112 ।।  
  
pāvanī sarvvabhūtānāṃ dharmajñānāṃ viśeṣataḥ।  
candrabhāgā ca sindhuśca ubhau mānasasannibhau।  
sāgaraṃ paścimaṃ yāti divyasindhurnadīvaraḥ ।। 15.113 ।।  
  
parvvato himavānnāma nānādhātuvibhūṣitaḥ।  
yojanānāṃ sahasrāṇi āyato'śītirucyate ।। 15.114 ।।  
  
siddhacāraṇasaṅkīrṇaḥ siddhacāraṇasevitaḥ।  
tatra puṣkariṇī ramyā suṣumnā nāma viśrutā ।। 15.115 ।।  
  
daśavarṣasahasrāṇi tatra jātastu jīvati।  
śrāddhaṃ bhavati cānantyaṃ tasyāṃ dattaṃ mahodayam।

śailasānuṣu tuṅgeṣu kandareṣu guhāsu ca।  
upahvaranitambeṣu tathā prasravaṇeṣu ca ।। 15.119 ।।  
  
pulineṣvāpagānāṃ ca tathaiva prabhave yuge।  
mahodadhau gavāṃ goṣṭe saṅgameṣu vaneṣu ca ।। 15.120 ।।

pradakṣiṇaṃ diśaṃ gatvā sarvvakāmacikīrṣavaḥ ।। 15.122 ।।

traivarṇye vihite sthāne dharmmavarṇāśrame tathā।  
kopasthānasya saṃtyāgātprāpyate pitṛpūjanam ।। 15.124 ।।  
  
tīrthānyanusaran dhīraḥ śraddadhāno jitendriyaḥ।  
kṛtapāpaśca śuddhyeta kiṃ punaḥ śubhakarmmakṛt ।। 15.125 ।।  
  
tiryagyoniṃ na gacchecca kudeśe na ca jāyate।

hetudraṣṭā ca pañcaite na tīrthaphalamaśrnute ।। 15.127 ।।  
  
gurutīrthe parā siddhistīrthānāṃ paramaṃ padam ।  
dhyānaṃ tīrthaparaṃ tasmādbrahmatīrthaṃ sanātanam ।। 15.128 ।।

buddhiṃ manasi saṃyamya sarvveṣāṃ tu nivarttanam ।। 15.130 ।।

anāhārātkṣayaṃ yāti vidyādanaśanaṃ tapaḥ।  
nigrahādbuddhimanaso ramyā buddhistu jāyate ।। 15.132 ।।

parinirvāti śuddhātmā yathā vahnirnirindhanaḥ ।। 15.133 ।।

          ।।bṛhaspatiruvāca।।  
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi dānāni ca phalāni ca।  
śrāddhakarmmāṇi medhyāni varjanīyāni yāni ca ।। 16.1 ।।  
  
himaprapatane kuryyādāharedvā himantataḥ।  
agnihotramataḥ puṇyaṃ paramaṃ hi tataḥ smṛtam ।। 16.2 ।।  
  
naktantu varjayecchrāddhaṃ rāhoranyatra darśanāt।  
sarvvasvenāpi karttavyaṃ kṣipraṃ vai rāhudarśane ।। 16.3 ।।  
  
uparāge na kuryyādyaḥ paṅke gauriva sīdati।  
kurvāṇastūddharetpāpānmagnānnauriva sāgare ।। 16.4 ।।

viṣāṇaṃ varjayetkhāḍgamasūyānāśanāya vai ।। 16.5 ।।

śyāmākāstu tathotpannāḥ pitrarthamapi pūjitāḥ।  
vipruṣastasya nāsābhyāmasaktābhyāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 16.7 ।।  
  
śleṣmāṇaḥ śītalā hṛdyā madhurāśca tathekṣavaḥ।  
śyāmākairikṣubhiścaiva pitṝṇāṃ sārvvakāmikam।  
kuryyādāgrayaṇaṃ yastu sa śīghraṃ siddhimāpnuyāt ।। 16.8 ।।

nāgaraṃ cātra vai deyaṃ dīrghamūlakameva ca ।। 16.10 ।।

laśunaṃ gṛñjanañcaiva palāṇḍuḥ piṇḍamūlakam।  
karambhādyāni cānyāni hīnāni rasagandhataḥ ।। 16.12 ।।

vraṇebhyo visphuranto vai patitā raktabindavaḥ।

durgandhaṃ phenilaṃ caiva tathā vai palvalodakam।

rāṣṭramiṣṭamavāpnoti svargaṃ mokṣaṃ yaśaskaram ।। 16.19 ।।

pāṣaṇḍavaikṛtasthāne naiṣā sṛṣṭiḥ svayambhuvā।  
dviśrāddhakaśca nirgranthāḥ śākyā puṣṭikalaṃśakāḥ ।। 16.30 ।।  
  
ye dharmmaṃ nānuvarttante te vai nagnādayo janāḥ।  
vṛthājaṭī vṛthāmudrī vṛthānagnaśca yo dvijaḥ ।। 16.31 ।।  
  
vṛthāvratī vṛthā jāpī te vai nagnādayo janāḥ।  
kulandhamā niṣādāśca tathā puṣṭivināśakāḥ ।। 16.32 ।।  
  
kṛtakarmmākṣitāstvete kupathāḥ parikīrttitāḥ।  
ebhirnirvṛttaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ vā śrāddhaṃ gacchanti mānavāḥ ।। 16.33 ।।  
  
brahmaghnaśca kṛtaghnaśca nāstikā gurutalpagāḥ।  
dasyavaśca nṛśaṃsāśca darśanenaiva varjitāḥ ।। 16.34 ।।  
  
ye cānye pāpa karmmāṇaḥ sarvvāṃstān parivarjayet।  
devadevarṣinindāyāṃ ratāṃścaiva viśeṣataḥ ।। 16.35 ।।  
  
asurān yātudhānāṃśca dṛṣṭamebhirvrajantyuta।  
brāhmaṃ kṛtayugaṃ proktaṃ tretā tu kṣatriyaṃ smṛtam ।। 16.36 ।।  
  
vaiśyaṃ dvāparamityāhuḥ śūdraṃ kaliyugaṃ smṛtam ।।  
  
          ।।pitara ūcuḥ ।।  
  
vedāḥ kṛtayuge pūjyāstretāyāṃ tu surāstathā ।। 16.37 ।।  
  
yuddhāni dvāpare nityaṃ pāṣaṇḍāśca kalau yuge।  
apamānāpavitraśca kukkuṭo grāmasūkaraḥ ।। 16.38 ।।  
  
śvā caiva darśanādeva hanti śrāddhaṃ na saṃśayaḥ।  
śāvasūtakasaṃspṛṣṭo dīrgharogibhireva ca ।। 16.39 ।।  
  
malinaiḥ patitaiścaiva na draṣṭavyaṃ kathañcana।  
annaṃ paśyeyurete vai naitatsyāddhavyakavyayoḥ ।। 16.40 ।।  
  
tatsaṃspṛṣṭaṃ pradhānārthaṃ saṃskāraśvāpado bhavet।  
haviṣāṃ saṃhatānāṃ tu pūrvvameva vivarjitam।  
mṛtsaṃyuktābhiradbhiśca prokṣaṇaṃ ca vidhīyate ।। 16.41 ।।  
  
siddhārthakaiḥ kṛṣṇatilaiḥ kāryyaṃ vāpyavakīraṇam।  
gurusūryyāgnivastūnāṃ darśanaṃ vāpi yatnataḥ ।। 16.42 ।।  
  
āsanārūḍhamāneṣu pādopahatameva ca।  
amedhyairjaṅgamairdṛṣṭaṃ śuṣkaṃ paryuṣitaṃ ca yat ।। 16.43 ।।  
  
asitaṃ pariduṣṭaṃ ca tathaivāgrāvalehitam।  
śarkarākeśapāṣāṇaiḥ kīṭairyaccāpyupadrutam ।। 16.44 ।।  
  
piṇyākamathitaṃ caiva tathā tilayavādiṣu।  
siddhākṣatāśca ye bhakṣyāḥ pratyakṣalavaṇīkṛtāḥ ।। 16.45 ।।  
  
vāsasā cāvadhūtāni varjyāni śrāddhakarmaṇi।  
santi vedavirodhena kecidvijñānamāninaḥ ।। 16.46 ।।  
  
ajajñapatayo nāma te śrāddhasya yathā rajaḥ।  
dadhi śākaṃ tathā'bhakṣyāḥ śuktaṃ caiva vivarjitam ।। 16.47 ।।  
  
vārtākuṃ varjayeddadyātsarvānabhiṣavānapi।  
saindhavaṃ lavaṇaṃ yacca tathā mānasasambhavam ।। 16.48 ।।  
  
pavitraṃ paramaṃ hyetat pratyakṣamapi varttate।  
agnau nikṣipya badhnīyāddhastau prakṣipya yatnataḥ ।। 16.49 ।।  
  
gamayenmastakaṃ caiva brahmatīrthaṃ hi tatsmṛtam।  
dravyāṇāṃ prokṣaṇaṃ kāryyaṃ tathaivāvapanaṃ punaḥ ।। 16.50 ।।  
  
nidhāya cādbhiḥ siñceta tathaivāpsu niveśanam।  
ariṣṭatumule bilvaṃ tviṅgudaśvadanānyapi ।। 16.51 ।।  
  
vidalānāñca sarvveṣāṃ dharmavacchaucamiṣyate।  
tathā dantāsthidārūṇāṃ śrṛṅgāṇāñcāvalekhanam ।। 16.52 ।।  
  
sarveṣāṃ mṛnmayānāntu punardāha udāhṛtaḥ।  
maṇivajrapravālānāṃ muktāśaṅkhamaṇestathā ।। 16.53 ।।  
  
siddhārthakānāṃ kalkena tilakalkena vā punaḥ ।  
syācchaucaṃ sarvavālānāmāvikānāñca sarvaśaḥ ।। 16.54 ।।  
  
 āvi kānāñca sarvveṣāṃ mṛdbhiradbhirvidhīyate।  
ādyantayostu śaucānāmadbhiḥ prakṣālanaṃ punaḥ ।। 16.55 ।।  
  
tathā kārppāsikānāñca bhasmanā samudāhṛtam।  
phalapuṣpa śalākānāṃ plāvanañcādbhiriṣyate ।। 16.56 ।।  
  
saṃmārjanaṃ prokṣaṇañca bhūmeścaivopalepanam।  
niṣkramya bāhyato grāmādvāyupūtā vasundharā ।। 16.57 ।।  
  
dhanuṣmatpakṣiṇāñcaiva mṛdbhiḥ śaucaṃ vidhīyate।  
evameṣa samuddiṣṭaḥ śaucānāṃ vidhiruttamaḥ।  
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi tanme nigadataḥ śrṛṇu ।। 16.58 ।।  
  
prātargṛhātpaścimadakṣiṇena iṣukṣepañcākṣamātraṃ padañca।  
kuryyātpurīṣaṃ śirovaguṇṭya na ca spṛśettatra śiraḥ kareṇa ।। 16.59 ।।  
  
śuṣkaistṛṇairvā kāṣṭairvā patrairveṇudalena vā।  
mṛṇmayairbhājanairvāpi tirodhāya vasundharām ।। 16.60 ।।  
  
uddhṛtodakamādāya mṛttikāñcaiva vāgyataḥ।  
divā udaṅmukhaḥ kuryyādrātrau vai dakṣiṇāmukhaḥ ।। 16.61 ।।  
  
dakṣiṇena ca hastena gṛhṇīyādvai kamaṇḍalum।  
śaucañca vāmahastena gude tisrastu mṛttikāḥ ।। 16.62 ।।  
  
daśa cāpi punardadyādvāmahastakrameṇa tu ।  
dvābhyāṃ vāpi punardadyāddhastānāṃ pañca mṛttikāḥ ।। 16.63 ।।  
  
mṛdā prakṣālya pādau ca ācamya ca yathāvidhi।  
āpastyājyāstrayaścaiva sūryyāgnipavanāmbhasām ।। 16.64 ।।  
  
kuryyāt sannihitaṃ nityaṃ prājñastīrthe kamaṇḍalum।  
asatkāryaṃ kāryametairyathāvatpādadhāvanam ।। 16.65 ।।  
  
ācamanaṃ dvitīyena devakāryaṃ tataḥ param।  
upavāsa strirātrantu duṣṭahaste hyudāhṛtaḥ ।। 16.66 ।।  
  
viprakṛṣṭena kṛcchreṇa prāyaścittamudāhṛtam।  
spṛṣṭvā śvānaṃ śvapākaṃ vā taptakṛcchraṃ samācaret ।। 16.67 ।।  
  
mānuṣāsthīni saṃspṛśya upoṣyaṃ śuddhikāraṇam ।  
trirātramuktaṃ sasnehamekarātramato'nyathā ।। 16.68 ।।  
  
kāraskarāḥ pulindāśca tathāndhaśabarādaya।  
pītvā cāpobhūtilaye gatvā caiva yugandharām ।। 16.69 ।।  
  
sindhoruttaraparyyantaṃ tathā divyantare śatam।  
pāpadeśāśca ye kecitpāparadhyuṣitā janaiḥ ।। 16.70 ।।  
  
śiṣṭaiśca varjitā ye ca brāhmaṇairvedapāragaiḥ।  
gatvā deśānapuṇyāṃstu kṛtsnaṃ pāpaṃ samaśnute ।। 16.71 ।।  
  
manovyaktirathāgniśca kāle caivopalepanam।  
vikhyāpanañca śaucānāṃ nityamajñānameva ca ।। 16.72 ।।  
  
atonyathā tu yaḥ kuryyānmohācchaucasya saṅkaram।  
piśācān yātudhānāṃśca phalaṃ gacchatyasaṃśayam ।। 16.73 ।।  
  
śaucamaśraddadhānaśca mlecchajātiṣu jāyate।  
ayajñāścaiva pāpo vā tiryyagyonigato'pi vā ।। 16.74 ।।  
  
śaucena mokṣaṃ kurvāṇaḥ svargavāsī bhavennaraḥ।  
śucikāmā hi devā vai devairetadudāhṛtam ।। 16.75 ।।  
  
bībhatsamaśuciñcaiva varjayanti surāḥ sadā।  
trīṇi śaucāni kurvanti nyāyataḥ śubhakarmaṇaḥ ।। 16.76 ।।  
  
brahmaṇyāyātitheyāya śaucayuktāya dhīmate।  
pitṛbhaktāya dāntāya sānukrośāya ca dvijāḥ ।। 16.77 ।।  
  
taistaiḥ prītāḥ prayacchanti pitaro yogavarddhanāḥ।  
manasā kāṃkṣitān kāmāṃstrailokyaprabhavāniti ।। 16.78 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte śrāddhakalpo nāma ṣoḍaśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 16 ।।

           ।।ṛṣaya ūcuḥ।।  
aho dhīmaṃstvayā sūta śrāddhakalpastu kīrtitaḥ।  
śruto naḥ śrāddhakalpo vai ṛṣibhiḥ parikīrtitaḥ ।। 17.1 ।।  
  
atīva vistaro yasya viśeṣeṇa prakīrtitaḥ।  
vada śeṣaṃ mahāprājña ṛṣestasya yathāmatam ।। 17.2 ।।  
  
         ।।sūta uvāca।।  
kīrtayiṣyāmi te viprā ṛṣestasya matantu tat।  
śrāddhaṃ prati mahābhāgāstanme śrṛṇuta vistarāt ।। 17.3 ।।  
  
uktaṃ śrāddhaṃ mayā pūrvaṃ vidhiśca śrāddhakarmmaṇi।  
pariśiṣṭaṃ pravakṣyāmi brāhmaṇānāṃ yathākramam ।। 17.4 ।।  
  
na mīmāṃsyāḥ sadā viprāḥ pavitraṃ hyetaduttamam।  
daive pitrye ca satataṃ śrūyate vai parīkṣaṇam ।। 17.5 ।।  
  
yasmin doṣāḥ pradṛśyeran sadbhirvā varjitastu yaḥ।  
jānīyādvāpi saṃvāsādvarjjayettaṃ prayatnataḥ ।। 17.6 ।।  
  
avijñātaṃ dvijaṃ śrāddhe parīkṣeta sadā budhaḥ ।  
siddhā hi viprarūpeṇa caranti pṛthivīmimām ।। 17.7 ।।  
  
tasmādatithimāyāntamabhigacchet kṛtāñjaliḥ।  
pūjayeccāpi pādyena pādābhyañjanabhojanaiḥ ।। 17.8 ।।  
  
urvīṃ sāgaraparyantāṃ devā yogeśvarāstathā।  
nānā rūpaiścarantyete prajā dharmmeṇa pālayan ।। 17.9 ।।  
  
arcayitvā tato dadyādviprāyātithaye naraḥ।  
vyañjanāni ca bhakṣyāṇi phalaṃ teṣāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 17.10 ।।  
  
āgniṣṭomaṃ tu payasā prāpnuyādvai tathāśrutam।  
sarpiṣā tu śubhaṃ cakṣuḥ ṣoḍaśāhaphalaṃ labhet।  
madhunā tvatirātrasya phalaṃ ca samavāpnuyāt ।। 17.11 ।।  
  
tatprāpnuyācchraddadhāno naro vai sarvaiḥ kāmairbhojayedyastu viprān।  
sarvārthadaḥ sarvaviprātithau yaḥ phalaṃ bhuṅkte sarvamedhasya nityam ।। 17.12 ।।  
  
yastu śrāddhe'tithiṃ prāpya daive vāpyavamanyate।  
taṃ vai devā viśvayanti hotā yadvat parāṃ vasum ।। 17.13 ।।  
  
devāśca pitaraścaiva vahniścaiva hi tān dvijān ।  
āviśya bhuñjate tadvai lokānugrahakāraṇāt ।। 17.14 ।।  
  
apūjitā dahantyete dadyuḥ kāmāṃśca pūjitāḥ।  
sarvasvenāpi tasmādvai pūjayedatithīn sadā ।। 17.15 ।।  
  
vānaprastho gṛhasthaśca gṛhamabhyāgato'thavā।  
bālāḥ khinnā yatiścaiva jānīyādatithīn sadā ।। 17.16 ।।  
  
abhyāgato yācakaḥ syādatithiḥ syādayācakaḥ।  
atitheratithiḥ śreṣṭhaḥ so'tithiryoga ucyate ।। 17.17 ।।  
  
na ghoro nāpi saṅkīrṇo nāvidyo na viśeṣavit।  
na ca santo na samṛddho na sevī nācaro'tithiḥ ।। 17.18 ।।  
  
pipāsitāya śrāntāya bhrāntāyātibubhukṣate।  
tasmai satkṛtya dātavyaṃ yajñasya phalamicchatā ।। 17.19 ।।  
  
āruhya bhṛgutuṅgoṣu gatvā puṇyāṃ sarasvatīm।  
āpagāṃ tu nadī puṇyāṃ gaṅgā devī mahānadī ।। 17.20 ।।  
  
himavatprabhavā nadyo yāścānyā ṛṣipūjitāḥ ।  
sarastīrthābhisaṃvedyā nadī navavahāstathā ।। 17.21 ।।  
  
gatvaitān mucyate pāpaiḥ svarge nityaṃ mahīyate।  
daśarātramaśaucantu proktaṃ vai mṛtasūtake ।। 17.22 ।।  
  
brāhmaṇasya viśeṣeṇa kṣatriye dvādaśa smṛtam।  
arddhaṃ māsantu vaiśyasya māsācchūdrastu śudhyati ।। 17.23 ।।  
  
udakyā sarvavarṇānāṃ trirātreṇa tu śuddhayati।  
udakyāṃ sūtikāñcaiva śvānamantyāvasāyinam ।। 17.24 ।।  
  
nagnādīn mṛtahārāṃśca spṛṣṭvā'śaucaṃ vidhīyate।  
snātvā sacailo mṛdbhistu dvādaśabhistu śudhyati ।। 17.25 ।।  
  
etadeva bhavecchaucaṃ maithune navabhistathā।  
mṛdā prakṣālya hastau tu kuryācchaucavidhinnaraḥ ।। 17.26 ।।  
  
prakṣālya cādbhirhastau ca snātvā caiva mṛdā punaḥ।  
mṛdaṃ guhye tato dvistu punareva mṛdaṃ budhaḥ ।। 17.27 ।।  
  
evaṃ śauca vidhirdṛṣṭaḥ sarvavarṇeṣu nityadā।  
paridadyānmṛdastisro hastapādāvasecanam ।। 17.28 ।।  
  
āraṇyaṃ śaucametattu grāmyaṃ vakṣyāmyataḥ param।  
mṛdastisraḥ pādayostu hastayostisra eva ca ।। 17.29 ।।  
  
mṛdaḥ pañcadaśāmedhye hastādīnāṃ vibhāgaśaḥ।  
anirṇikte mṛdaṃ dadyānmṛdante tvadbhireva tu ।। 17.30 ।।  
  
kaṇṭhaṃ śiro vā prāvṛtya rathyā pādagatastu vā।  
akṛtvā pādayoḥ śaucamācānto'pyaśucirbhavet ।। 17.31 ।।  
  
prakṣālya pātraṃ niḥkṣipya ācamyābhyukṣaṇaṃ punaḥ।  
dravyasyānyasya tu tathā kuryādabhyukṣaṇaṃ punaḥ ।। 17.32 ।।  
  
puṣpādīnāṃ tṛṇānāñca prokṣaṇaṃ haviṣāntathā।  
parāhṛtānāṃ dravyāṇāṃ nidhāyābhyukṣaṇaṃ tathā ।। 17.33 ।।  
  
prokṣitantu haret kiñci cchrāddhe daiva tathā punaḥ।  
uttareṇa haredvedyāṃ dakṣiṇena visarjayet ।। 17.34 ।।  
  
vicchinnaṃ syādviparyāse daive pitrye tathaiva ca।  
dakṣiṇena tu hastena dakṣiṇāṃ vedimālikhet ।। 17.35 ।।  
  
karābhyāmeva devānāṃ pitṝṇāṃ vikaraṃ śubham।  
kṣubhitasvapnayoścaiva tathā mūtrapurīṣayoḥ ।। 17.36 ।।  
  
niṣṭīvite yathā vyakte bhuktvā viparidhāya ca।  
ucchiṣṭasya ca saṃsparśe tathā pādāvasecane ।। 17.37 ।।  
  
utsṛṣṭasya sumambhāṣe hyaśuciṃ prayatasya ca।  
sandeheṣu ca sarveṣu śikhāṃ muktvā tathaiva ca ।। 17.38 ।।  
  
vinā yajñopavītena mohāttu yadyupaspṛṣet।  
auṣṭhasya dantasaṃsparśe darśane cāntyavāsinām ।। 17.39 ।।  
  
jiṅvayā caiva saṃspṛśya dantāsaktaṃ tathaiva ca।  
saśabdamaṅgulībhiśca praṇataścāvalo kayan ।। 17.40 ।।  
  
yaścādharme sthito mohādāyānto'pyaśucirbhavet।  
upaviśya śucau deśe praṇataḥ prāgudaṅmukhaḥ ।। 17.41 ।।  
  
pādau prakṣālya hastau tu antarjānurupa spṛśet।  
prasannāstriḥ pibeccāpaḥ prayataḥ susamāhitaḥ ।। 17.42 ।।  
  
dvireva mārjanaṃ kuryātsakṛdabhyukṣaṇaṃ tataḥ।  
khāni mūrddhānamātmānaṃ hastau pādau tathaiva ca ।। 17.43 ।।  
  
abhyukṣaṇaṃ tathā tasya yadyamīmāṃsitaṃ bhavet।  
evamācamanaṃ tasya vedā yajñāstapāṃsi ca ।। 17.44 ।।  
  
dānāni brahmacaryañca bhavanti saphalāstathā।  
kriyāṃ yaḥ kurute mohādanācamyaiva nāstikaḥ ।। 17.45 ।।  
  
bhavanti ca vṛthā tasya kriyā hyetā na saṃśayaḥ।  
vāgbhāvaśuddhanirṇiktamaduṣṭaṃ vāpyaninditam ।। 17.46 ।।  
  
medhyānyetāni jñeyāni duṣṭamebhyo viparyayaḥ।  
na vaktavyaḥ sadā vipraḥ kṣudhito nāsti kiñcana ।। 17.47 ।।  
  
tasmai satkṛtya yo dadyādayūpo yajña ucyate।  
apluṣṭānnaṃ śrṛtānnantu kṛśavṛttimayācakam ।। 17.48 ।।  
  
ekāntaśīlaṃ hrīmantaṃ sadā śrāddheṣu bhojayet ।  
yo dadātyāntimabhyeśca sa brahmaghno durātmavān ।। 17.49 ।।  
  
api jātiśataṃ gatvā na sa mucyeta kilbiṣāt।  
viṣamaṃ bhojayedviprānekapaṃktyā ca yo naraḥ ।। 17.50 ।।  
  
niyukto vā'niyukto vā paṃkt‌yā harati duṣkṛtam।  
pāpena gṛhyate kṣipramiṣṭāpūrttaṃ ca naśyati ।। 17.51 ।।  
  
yatistu sarvvaviprāṇāṃ sarvveṣāmagrya utsave।  
itihāsapañcamān vedān yaḥ paṭettu dvijottamaḥ ।। 17.52 ।।  
  
anantaraṃ yathāyogyaṃ niyoktavyo vijānatā।  
trivedo'nantarastasya dvivedastadanantaraḥ ।। 17.53 ।।  
  
ekavedastathā paścānnyāyādhyāyī tataḥ param।  
pāvanāyaiva paṃktyā vai tān pravakṣye nibodhata ।। 17.54 ।।  
  
ya ete pūrvvanirddiṣṭāḥ sarvve te hyanupūrvvaśaḥ।  
ṣaḍaṅgī vinayī yogī sarvvatantrastathaiva ca ।। 17.55 ।।  
  
yāyāvaraśca pañcaite vijñeyāḥ paṅktipāvanāḥ ।  
aṣṭādaśānāṃ vidyānāmekasmin pārago'pi yaḥ ।। 17.56 ।।  
  
yathāvadvarttamānaśca sarve te paṅktipāvanāḥ।  
triṇāciketāstraividyo yaśca dharmmān paṭeddvijaḥ ।। 17.57 ।।  
  
bārhaspatye tathā śāstre pāraṃ yaśca dvijo gataḥ।  
sarva te pāvanā viprāḥ paṅktīnāṃ samudāhṛtāḥ ।। 17.58 ।।  
  
āmantritastu yaḥ śrāddhe yoṣitaṃ sevate dvijaḥ।  
pitarastasya taṃ māsaṃ tasya retasi śerate ।। 17.59 ।।  
  
śrāddhaṃ dattvā ca bhuktvā ca maithunaṃ yo niṣevate।  
pitarastasya taṃ māsaṃ retaḥsthā nātra saṃśayaḥ ।। 17.60 ।।  
  
tasmādatithaye deyaṃ bhojayedbrahmacāriṇam।  
dhyānaniṣṭāya dātavyaṃ sānukrośāya dhārmikam ।। 17.61 ।।  
  
yatiṃ vā vālakhilyān vā bhojayecchrāddhakarmmaṇi।  
vānaprasthopakurvvāṇaḥ pūjāmātreṇa toṣitaḥ ।। 17.62 ।।  
  
gṛhasthaṃ bhojayedyastu viśvedevāstu pūjitāḥ।  
vānaprasthena ṛṣayo vālakhilyaiḥ purandaraḥ ।। 17.63 ।।  
  
yatīnāṃ pūjane cāpi sākṣādbrahmā tu pūjitaḥ।  
āśramāḥ pāvanāḥ pañca upadhābhiranāśramāḥ ।। 17.64 ।।  
  
catvāra āśramāḥ pūjyāḥ śrāddhe daive tathaiva ca।  
caturāśramabāhyebhyaḥ śrāddhaṃ naiva pradāpayet ।। 17.65 ।।  
  
sa tiṣṭedvā bubhukṣustu caturāśramabāhyataḥ।  
ayati rmokṣavādī ca ubhau tau paṅktidūṣakau ।। 17.66 ।।  
  
vṛthāmuṇḍāśca jaṭilāḥ sarve kārppaṭikāstathā।  
nirghṛṇān bhinnavṛttāṃśca sarvabhakṣān vivarjayet ।। 17.67 ।।  
  
kārukādīnanācārān sarvavedabahiṣkṛtān।  
gāyanān devavṛttāṃśca havyakavyeṣu varjayet ।। 17.68 ।।  
  
dvijeṣvapi kṛtaṃ nityaṃ śrāddhakarmmaṇi varjayet ।। 17.69 ।।  
  
eteṣu varttate yaśca kṛtsvavarṇaṃ sa gacchati।  
yo'śnāti saha śūdreṇa sarve te paṅktidūṣakāḥ ।। 17.70 ।।  
  
vyāpādanaṃ śaktinibarhaṇaṃ kṛṣirvāṇijyakāryyaṃ paśupālanaṃ ca।  
śuśrūṣaṇaṃ vāpyaguro raho vā kāryyaṃ naitadvidyate brāhmaṇasya ।। 17.71 ।।  
  
ye tu viprāḥ sthitā nityaṃ jñānino dhyāninastathā।  
mithyāsaṅkalpinaḥ sarve durvṛttā vā dvijātayaḥ ।। 17.72 ।।  
  
mithyātattvavido varjjyāstathā dambhaviṣūcakāḥ।  
upapātakasaṃyuktāḥ pātakaiśca viśeṣataḥ ।। 17.73 ।।  
  
vede niyogadātāro lobhamohaphalārthinaḥ।  
brahmavikrayiṇaścaiva śrāddhakarmmaṇi varjitāḥ ।। 17.74 ।।  
  
na niyogo'sti vedānāṃ yo niyuṅkte sa pāpakṛt।  
bhoktā vedaphalādbhraśyeddātā dānaphalāttathā ।। 17.75 ।।  
  
bhṛto'dhyāpayate yastu bhṛtakādhyāpitastu yaḥ ।  
nārhatastāvapi śrāddhaṃ brahmaṇaḥ kriyavikrayī ।। 17.76 ।।  
  
krayavikrayiṇau caiva jīvitārthaṃ vigarhitau।  
vṛttireṣā tu vaiśyasya brāhmaṇasya tu pātakam ।। 17.77 ।।  
  
prāhurvedān vedavido vedān yaścopajīvati।।  
ubhau tau nārhataḥ śrāddhaṃ putrikāpatireva ca ।। 17.78 ।।  
  
vṛthā dārāṃśca yo gacchedyo yajeta vṛthādhvare।  
nārhatastāvapi śrāddhaṃ dvijo yaścaiva nāstikaḥ ।। 17.79 ।।  
  
ātmārthaṃ yaḥ pacedannaṃ na devātithikārakaḥ।  
nārhatastāvapi śrāddhaṃ patitau brahmarākṣasau ।। 17.80 ।।  
  
striyo naktaṃparā yeṣāṃ paradāraratāśca ye।  
arthakāma ratāścaiva na tān śrāddheṣu bhojayet ।। 17.81 ।।  
  
varṇāśramāṇāṃ dharmmeṣu viruddhāḥ śrāddhakarmmaṇi।  
stenaśca sarvayājī ca sarve te paṅktidūṣakāḥ ।। 17.82 ।।  
  
yaśca sūkaravadbhuṅkte yaśca pāṇitale dvijaḥ।  
na tadaśnanti pitaro yaśca vāmaṃ samaśnute ।। 17.83 ।।  
  
strīśūdrāyānupetāya śrāddhocchiṣṭaṃ na dāpayet।  
yo dadyā drāgamohāttu na tadgacchetpitṝnsadā ।। 17.84 ।।  
  
tasmānna deyamucchiṣṭamannādyaṃ śrāddhakarmmaṇi।  
anyatra dadhisarpirbhyāṃ śiṣye putrāya nānyathā ।। 17.85 ।।  
  
anucchiṣṭantu dātavyamannādyaṃ vai viśeṣataḥ।  
puṣpamūlaphalairvāpi tuṣṭiṃ gacchanti cānnataḥ ।। 17.86 ।।  
  
yāvantyannāni pūtāni yāvaduṣṇaṃ na muñcati।  
tāvadaśnanti pitaro yāvadaśnanti vāgyatāḥ ।। 17.87 ।।  
  
dānaṃ pratigraho homo bhojanaṃ balireva ca।  
sāṅguṣṭena tathā kāryyaṃ nāsurebhyo yathā bhavet ।। 17.88 ।।  
  
etānyeva ca sarvvāṇi dānādīni viśeṣataḥ।  
antarjānvaviśeṣeṇa tadvadācamanaṃ bhavet ।। 17.89 ।।  
  
muṇḍāñjaṭilakāṣāyān śrāddhakāle'pi varjjayet।  
śikhibhyo vā tridaṇiḍabhyaḥ śrāddhaṃ yatnāt pradāpayet ।। 17.90 ।।  
  
ye tu vrate sthitā nityaṃ jñānino dhyāninastathā।  
devabhaktā mahātmānaḥ punīyurdarśanādapi ।। 17.91 ।।  
  
sarvvaṃ yogeśvarairvyāptaṃ trailokyaṃ vai nirantaram ।  
tasmāt paśyanti te sarve yatkiñcijjagatīgatam ।। 17.92 ।।  
  
vyaktāvyaktaṃ vaśīkṛtya sarvvasyāpi ca yatparam।  
sadasacceti yairdṛṣṭaṃ sadasacca mahātmanām ।। 17.93 ।।  
  
sarvvajñānāni dṛṣṭāni mokṣādīni mahātmanām।  
tasmātteṣu sadāsaktaḥ prāpnotyanuttamaṃ śubham ।। 17.94 ।।  
  
ṛco hi yo veda sa veda vedān yajūṃṣi yo veda sa veda yajñam।  
sāmāni yo veda sa veda brahma yo mānasaṃ veda sa veda sarvvam ।। 17.95 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte śrāddhakalpe brāhmaṇaparīkṣā nāma saptadaśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 17 ।। *

sā śaśāva tataḥ krodhāt savarṇā jananī yamam ।। 22.55 ।।  
  
padā tarjayase yasmāt pitṛbhāryāṃ yaśasvinīm।  
tasmāttavaiṣa caraṇaḥ patiṣyati na saṃśayaḥ ।। 22.56 ।।  
  
yamastu tena śāpena bhṛśaṃ pīḍitamānasaḥ।  
manunā saha dharmmātmā pituḥ sarvvaṃ nyavedayat ।। 22.57 ।।  
  
bhṛśaṃ śāpabhayodvignaḥ saṃjñāvākyairvinirjitaḥ।  
bālyādvā yadi vā mohānmāṃ bhavāṃstrātumarhati ।। 22.58 ।।  
  
śapto'hamasmiँllokeśa jananyā tapatāṃ vara।  
tava prasādo nastrātuṃ hyetasmānmahato bhayāt ।। 22.59 ।।  
  
vivasvānevamuktastu yamaṃ provāca vai prabhuḥ।  
asaṃśayaṃ putra mahadbhaviṣyaṃ tatra kāraṇam ।। 22.60 ।।  
  
yena tvāmāviśatkrodho dharmmajñaṃ satyavādinam।  
na śakyametanmithyā tu karttuṃ māturvacastava ।। 22.61 ।।  
  
kṛmayo māṃsamādāya yāsyanti tu mahīṃ tava।  
tataḥ pādaṃ mahāprājña punaḥ saṃprāpsyase sukham ।। 22.62 ।।  
  
kṛtamevaṃ vacaḥ satyaṃ mātustava bhaviṣyati।  
śāpasya parihāreṇa tvaṃ ca trātā bhaviṣyasi ।। 22.63 ।।  
  
ādityastvabravīt saṃjñāṃ kimarthaṃ tanayeṣu vai।  
tulyeṣvapyadhikaḥ sneha ekasmin kriyate tvayā ।। 22.64 ।।  
  
sā tatpariharantī vai nācacakṣe vivasvataḥ।  
ātmanā sa samādhāya yogantathyamapaśyata ।। 22.65 ।।  
  
tāṃ śaptukāmo bhagavān nāśāya kupitaḥ prabhuḥ ।  
sā tatsarvaṃ yathātattvamācacakṣe vivasvataḥ ।। 22.66 ।।  
  
vivasvānatha tacchrutvā kruddhastvaṣṭāramabhyayāt।  
tvaṣṭā tu taṃ yathānyāyamarcayitvā vibhāvasum ।। 22.67 ।।  
  
nirddagdhukāmaṃ roṣeṇa sāntvayāmāsa vai śanaiḥ।  
tavātitejasā yuktamidaṃ rūpaṃ na śobhate ।। 22.68 ।।  
  
asahantī tu tat saṃjñā vane carati śādvale।  
drakṣyate tāṃ bhavānadya svāṃ bhāryāṃ śubhacāriṇīm ।। 22.69 ।।  
  
ślāghyāṃ yauvanasampannāṃ yogamāsthāya gopate।  
anukūlaṃ bhavedevaṃ yadi syāt samayo mataḥ ।। 22.70 ।।  
  
rūpaṃ vivarttayedante ādyaṃ śreṣṭhamarindama।  
rūpaṃ vivasvatastvāsīttiryagūrdhvamadhastathā ।। 22.71 ।।  
  
tenāsau vrīḍito devo rūpeṇa tu divaspatiḥ।  
tasmāttvaṣṭā sa cakraṃ tu bahumene mahātapāḥ ।। 22.72 ।।  
  
anujñātastatastvaṣṭā rūpanirvarttanāya tu।  
tato'bhyupagamāttvaṣṭā mārttaṇḍasya vivasvataḥ ।। 22.73 ।।  
  
bhramimāropya tattejaḥ śātayāmāsa tasya vai।  
tattu nirbhāsitantejastejasāpahṛtena tu ।। 22.74 ।।  
  
kāntāt kāntataraṃ draṣṭumaśubhaṃ śuśubhe tataḥ।  
dadarśa yogamāsthāya svāmbhāryāṃ vaḍavāṃ tathā ।। 22.75 ।।  
  
adṛśyāṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ tejasā niyamena ca।  
aśvarūpeṇa mārttaṇḍastāṃ mukhe samabhāvayat ।। 22.76 ।।  
  
maithunāya viceṣṭantī parapuṃsopaśaṅkayā।  
sā tanniradhamacchukraṃ nāsikābhyāṃ vivasvataḥ ।। 22.77 ।।  
  
devau tasmādajāyetāmaśvinau bhiṣajāṃ varau ।  
nāsatyaścaiva dasraśca smṛtau dvāvaśvināviti ।। 22.78 ।।  
  
mārttaṇḍasya sutāvetāvaṣṭamasya prajāpateḥ।  
tāntu rūpeṇa kāntena darśayāmāsa bhāskaraḥ ।। 22.79 ।।  
  
sā taṃ dṛṣṭvāttadā bhāryā tutoṣa ca mumoha ca।  
yamastu tena śāpena bhṛśampīḍitamānasaḥ ।। 22.80 ।।  
  
dharmmeṇa rañjayāmāsa dharmmarājastatastu saḥ ।  
so'labhat karmaṇā tena śubhena paramadyutiḥ ।। 22.81 ।।  
  
pitṝṇāmādhipatyañca lokapālatvameva ca।  
manuḥ prajāpatiścaiva sāvarṇiḥ sa mahāyaśāḥ ।। 22.82 ।।  
  
bhāvyasau nāgate tasmin manuḥ sāvarṇike'ntare।  
merupṛṣṭe suramye vai adyāpi carate prabhuḥ ।। 22.83 ।।  
  
bhrātā śanaiścarastatra grahatvaṃ sa tu labdhavān।  
tvaṣṭā'nu tena rūpeṇa viṣṇoścakramakalpayat।  
mahā'pratihataṃ yuddhe dānava prativāraṇe ।। 22.84 ।।  
  
yavīyasī tayoryā tu yamunā ca yaśasvinī।  
abhavat sā saricchreṣṭā yamunā lokabhāvinī ।। 22.85 ।।  
  
yastu jyeṣṭo mahātejāḥ sargo yasya tu sāmpratam।  
vistaraṃ tasya vakṣyāmi manorvaivasvatasya ha ।। 22.86 ।।  
  
idaṃ tu janma devānāṃ śrṛṇuyādvā paṭeta vā।  
vaivasvatasya putrāṇāṃ saptānāntu mahaujasām।  
āpadaṃ prāpya mucyeta prāpnuyācca mahadyaśaḥ ।। 22.87 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇai vāyuprokte śrāddhakalpe varuṇavaṃśavarṇanaṃ nāma dvāviṃśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 22 ।। *
