title: 043

---
          ।।vāyuruvāca।।  
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi gayāmāhātmyamuttamam।  
yacchrutvā sarvvapāpebhyo mucyate nātrasaṃśayaḥ ।। 43.1 ।।  
  
           ।।sūta uvāca।।  
sanakādyairmmahābhāgairdevarṣiḥ sa ca nāradaḥ।  
sanatkumāraṃ papraccha praṇamya vidhipūrvvakam ।। 43.2 ।।  
  
      nārada uvāca।।  
sanatkumāra me brūhi tīrthaṃ tīrthottamottamam।  
tārakaṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ paṭhatāṃ śrṛṇvatāṃ tathā ।। 43.3 ।।  
  
sanatkumāra uvāca।।  
vakṣye tīrthavaraṃ puṇyaṃ śrāddhādau sarvatārakam।  
gayātīrthaṃ sarvadeśe tīrthebhyo'pyadhikaṃ śrṛṇu ।। 43.4 ।।  
  
gayāsurastapastepe brahmaṇā kratave'rthitaḥ।  
prāptasya tasya śirasi śilāṃ dharmo hyadhārayat ।। 43.5 ।।  
  
tatra brahmā'karodyāgaṃ sthitaścāpi gadādharaḥ।  
phalgutīrthādirūpeṇa niścalārthamaharniśam।  
gayāsurasya viprendrabrahmādyairdaivataiḥ saha ।। 43.6 ।।  
  
kṛtayajño dadau brahmā brāhmaṇebhyo gṛhādikam।  
svetakalpe tu vārāhe gayāyāgamakārayat ।। 43.7 ।।  
  
gayānāmnā gayā khyātā kṣetraṃ brahnamabhikāṃkṣitam।  
kāṃkṣanti pitaraḥ putrānnarakādbhayabhīravaḥ ।। 43.8 ।।  
  
gayāṃ yāsyati yaḥ putraḥ sa nastrātā bhaviṣyati।  
gayāprāptaṃ sutaṃ dṛṣṭvā pitṝṇāmutsavo bhavet।  
padbhyāmapi jalaṃ spṛṣṭvā so'smabhyaṃ kiṃ na dāsyati ।। 43.9 ।।  
  
gayāṃ gatvānnadātā yaḥ pitarastena putriṇaḥ ।  
pakṣatrayanivāsī ca punātyāsaptamaṃ kulam।  
no cetpañcadaśāhaṃ vā saptarātriṃ trirātrikam ।। 43.10 ।।  
  
mahākalpakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ gayāṃ prāpya vinaśyati।  
piṇḍaṃ dadyācca pitrāderātmano'pi tilairvinā ।। 43.11 ।।  
  
brahmahatyā surāpānaṃ steyaṃ gurvvaṅganāgamaḥ।  
pāpaṃtatsaṅgajaṃ sarvvaṃ gayāśrāddhādvinaśyati ।। 43.12 ।।  
  
ātmajo'pyanyajo vāpi gayābhūmau yadā tadā।  
yannāmnā pātayetpiṇḍaṃ taṃ nayedbrahma śāśvatam ।। 43.13 ।।  
  
brahmajñānaṃ gayāśrāddhaṃ gogṛhe maraṇaṃ tathā।  
vāsaḥ puṃsāṃ kurukṣetre muktireṣā caturvvidhā ।। 43.14 ।।  
  
brahmajñānena kiṃ kāryyaṃ gogṛhe maraṇena kim।  
vāsena kiṃ kurukṣetre yadi putro gayāṃ vrajet ।। 43.15 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ sarvvakāleṣu piṇḍaṃ dadyādvicakṣaṇaḥ।  
adhimāse janmadine cāste'pi guruśukrayoḥ ।। 43.16 ।।  
  
na tyaktavyaṃ gayāśrāddhaṃ siṃhasthe'pi bṛhaspatau।  
tathā daivapramādena prahateṣu vraṇeṣu ca।  
punaḥ karmmādhikārī ca śrāddhakṛdbrahmalokabhāk ।। 43.17 ।।  
  
sakṛdgayābhigamanaṃ sakṛtpiṇḍasya pātanam।  
durlabhaṃ kiṃ punarnnityamasminneva vyavasthitiḥ ।। 43.18 ।।  
  
pramādānmriyate kṣetre brahmādermuktidāyake।  
brahmajñānādyathā muktirlabhyate nātra saṃśayaḥ ।। 43.19 ।।  
  
kīṭakādimṛtānāñca pitṝṇāṃ tāraṇāya ca।  
tasmātsarvvaprayatnena karttavyaṃ suvicakṣaṇaiḥ ।। 43.20 ।।  
  
brahmaprakalpitānviprānhavyakavyādinā'rccayet।  
taistuṣṭaistoṣitāḥ sarvvāḥ pitṛbhiḥ saha devatāḥ ।। 43.21 ।।  
  
muṇḍanaṃ copavāsaśca sarvvatīrtheṣvayaṃ vidhiḥ।  
varjayitvā kurukṣetraṃ viśālāṃ virajāṃ gayām ।। 43.22 ।।  
  
daṇḍaṃpradarśayedbhikṣurgayāṃ gatvā na piṇḍadaḥ।  
daṇḍaṃ nyastā viṣṇupade pitṛbhiḥ saha mucyate ।। 43.23 ।।  
  
na daṇḍī kilbiṣaṃ dhatte puṇyaṃ vā paramārthataḥ।  
ataḥ sarvvāṃ kriyāṃ tyaktvā viṣṇuṃ dhyāyati bhāvukaḥ ।। 43.24 ।।  
  
saṃnyasetsarvvakarmmāṇi vedamekaṃ na saṃnyaset।  
muṇḍaṃ kuryyācca pūrvve'sminpaścime dakṣiṇottare ।। 43.25 ।।  
  
sārddhaṃ krośadvayaṃ mānaṃ gayeti brahmaṇeritam।  
pañcakrośaṃ gayākṣetraṃ krośamekaṃ gayāśiraḥ ।। 43.26 ।।  
  
tanmadhye sarvvatīrthāni trailokye yāni santi vai।  
śrāddhakṛdyo gayākṣetre pitṝṇāmanṛṇo hi saḥ ।। 43.27 ।।  
  
śirasi śrāddhakṛdyastu kulānāṃ śatamuddharet।  
gṛhāccalitamātreṇa gayāyāṃ gamanaṃ prati।  
svargā rohaṇasopānaṃ pitṝṇāñca pade pade ।। 43.28 ।।  
  
pade pade'śvamedhasya yatphalaṃ gacchato gayām।  
tatphalañca bhavennūnaṃ samagraṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ ।। 43.29 ।।  
  
pāyasenāpi caruṇā saktunā piṣṭakena vā।  
taṇḍulaiḥ phalamūlādyairgayāyāṃ piṇḍapātanam ।। 43.30 ।।  
  
tilakalkena khaṇḍena guḍena saghṛtena vā।  
kevalenaiva dadhnā vā ūrjena madhunā'tha vā ।। 43.31 ।।  
  
piṇyākaṃ saghṛtaṃ khaṇḍaṃ pitṛbhyo'kṣayamityuta।  
ijyate vārttavaṃ bhojyaṃ haviṣyānnaṃ munīritam ।। 43.32 ।।  
  
ekataḥ sarvvavastūni rasavanti madhūni hi।  
smṛtvā gadādharāṅaghryabjaṃ phalgutīrthāmbu caikataḥ ।। 43.33 ।।  
  
piṇḍāsanaṃ piṇḍadānaṃ punaḥ pratyavanejanam।  
dakṣiṇā cānna saṅkalpastīrthaśrāddheṣvayaṃ vidhiḥ ।। 43.34 ।।  
  
nāvāhanaṃ na digbandho na doṣo dṛṣṭisambhavaḥ।  
sakāruṇyena karttavyaṃ tīrthaśrāddhaṃ vicakṣaṇaiḥ ।। 43.35 ।।  
  
anyatrāvāhitāḥ kāle pitaro yāntyamuṃ prati।  
tīrthe sadā vasantyete tasmādāvāhanaṃ na hi ।। 43.36 ।।  
  
tīrthaśrāddhaṃ prayacchadbhiḥ puruṣaiḥ phalakāṅkṣibhiḥ।  
kāmaṃ krodhaṃ tathā lobhaṃ tyaktvā kāryyā kriyā'niśam ।। 43.37 ।।  
  
brahmacāryyakabhojī ca bhūśāyī satyavākchuciḥ।  
sarvvabhūtahite raktaḥ sa tīrthaphalamaśnute ।। 43.38 ।।  
  
tīrthānyanusarandhīraḥ pāṣaṇḍaṃ pūrvvatastyajet।  
pāṣaṇḍhaḥ sa ca vijñeyo yo bhavetkāmakārataḥ ।। 43.39 ।।  
  
tīrtheṣu ye narā dhīrāḥ karmma kurvvanti tadghatāḥ।  
yathā brahmavido vedyaṃ vastu cānanyacetasaḥ।  
praviśanti pareśākhyaṃ brahma brahmaparāyaṇāḥ ।। 43.40 ।।  
  
yāste vaitaraṇī nāma nadī trailokyaviśrutā।  
sā'vatīrṇā gayākṣetre pitṝṇāṃ tāraṇāya vai।  
snāto godo vaitaraṇyāṃ triḥsaptakulamuddharet ।। 43.41 ।।  
  
tathā'kṣayavaṭaṃ gatvā viprānsantoṣayiṣyati।  
brahmaprakalpitānviprānhavyakavyādinā'rccayet।  
taistuṣṭaistoṣitāḥ sarvvāḥ pitṛbhiḥ saha devatāḥ ।। 43.42 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ na hi tatsthānaṃ yatra tīrthaṃ na vidyate।  
sānnidhyaṃ sarvvatīrthānāṃ gayātīrthaṃ tato varam ।। 43.43 ।।  
  
mīne meṣe sthite sūryye kanyāyāṃ kārmuke ghaṭe।  
durllabhaṃ triṣu lokeṣu gayāyāṃ piṇḍapātanam ।। 43.44 ।।  
  
makare varttamāne ca grahaṇe candrasūryyayoḥ ।  
durllabhaṃ triṣu lokeṣu gayāśrāddhaṃ sudurllabham ।। 43.45 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ piṇḍadānena yatphalaṃ labhate naraḥ।  
na tacchakyaṃ mayā vaktuṃ kalpakoṭiśatairapi ।। 43.46 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmāhātmyaṃ nāma tricatvāriṃśo'dyāyaḥ ।। 43 ।।

      nārada uvāca।।  
gayāsuraḥ kathaṃ jātaḥ kiṃprabhāvaḥ kimātmakaḥ ।  
tapastaptaṃ kathaṃ tena kathaṃ dehapavitratā ।। 44.1 ।।  
  
        sanatkumāra uvāca।।  
viṣṇornābhyambujājjāto brahmā lokapitāmahaḥ।  
prajāḥ sasarja saṃproktaḥ pūrvvaṃ devena viṣṇunā ।। 44.2 ।।  
  
āsureṇaiva bhāvena hyasurānasṛjatpurā।  
saumanasyena bhāvena devānsumanaso'sṛjat ।। 44.3 ।।  
  
gayāsuro'surāṇāñca mahābalaparākramaḥ।  
yojanānāṃ sapādañca śataṃ tasyocchrayaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 44.4 ।।  
  
sthūlaḥ ṣaṣṭiryojanānāṃ śreṣṭo'sau vaiṣṇavaḥ smṛtaḥ।  
kolāhalagirivare tapastepe sudāruṇam ।। 44.5 ।।  
  
bahuvarṣasahasrāṇi nirucchvāsaṃ sthiro'bhavat।  
tattapastāpitā devāḥ saṃkṣobhaṃ paramaṃ gatāḥ ।। 44.6 ।।  
  
brahmalokaṃ gatā devāḥ procuste'tha pitāmaham।  
gayāsurādrakṣa deva brahmā devāṃstato'bravīt ।। 44.7 ।।  
  
vrajāmaḥ śaṅkaraṃ devā brahmādyāśca gatāḥ śivam।  
kailāse cābruvannatvā rakṣa deva mahāsurāt ।। 44.8 ।।  
  
brahmādyānabravīcchambhurvrajāmaḥ śaraṇaṃ harim।  
kṣīrābdhau devadeveśaḥ sa naḥ śreyo vidhāsyati।  
brahmā maheśvaro devā viṣṇuṃ natvā pratuṣṭuvuḥ ।। 44.9 ।।  
  
         devā ūcuḥ ।।  
  
ॐ namo viṣṇavo bhartre sarvveṣāṃ prabhaviṣṇave।  
rociṣṇave jiṣṇave ca rākṣasādigrasiṣṇave ।। 44.10 ।।  
  
dhariṣṇave'khilasyāsya yogināṃ pārayiṣṇave ।  
varddhiṣṇave hyanantāya namo bhrājṣṇive namaḥ ।। 44.11 ।।  
  
            sanatkumāra uvāca।।  
evaṃ stuto vāsudevaḥ surāṇāṃ darśanaṃ dadau।  
kimarthamāgatā devā viṣṇunoktāstamabruvan ।। 44.12 ।।  
  
gayāsurabhayāddeva rakṣāsmānabravīddharim।  
brahmādyā yāntu taṃ daityamāgamiṣyāmyahaṃ tataḥ ।। 44.13 ।।  
  
keśave garuḍārūḍho varaṃ dātuṃ gayāsure।  
sarvve svaṃ svaṃ samāsthāya yayurvāhanamuttamam ।। 44.14 ।।  
  
ūcustaṃ vāsudevādyāḥ kimarthaṃ tapyate tvayā।  
santuṣṭāḥ svāgatāḥ sarve varaṃ brūhi gayāsura ।। 44.15 ।।  
  
        gayāsura uvāca।।  
yadituṣṭāḥ stha me devā brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarāḥ।  
sarvvadevadvijātibhyo yajñatīrthaśiloccayāt ।। 44.16 ।।  
  
devebhyo'tipavitro'hamṛṣibhyo'pi śivāvyayāt।  
mantrebhyo devadevebhyo yogibhyaścāpi sarvvaśaḥ ।। 44.17 ।।  
  
nyāsibhyaścāpi karmmibhyo dharmmibhyaśca tathā punaḥ।  
jñātibhyo'tipavitrebhyaḥ pavitraḥ syāṃ sadā surāḥ ।। 44.18 ।।  
  
pavitramastu taṃ devā daityamuktvā yayurdivam।  
daityaṃ dṛṣṭvā ca spṛṣṭvā ca sarvve haripuraṃ yayuḥ ।। 44.19 ।।  
  
śūnyaṃ lokatrayaṃ jātaṃ śūnyā yamapurī hyabhūt।  
yama indrādibhiḥ sārddhaṃ brahmalokaṃ tato'gamat ।। 44.20 ।।  
  
brahmāṇamūcire devā gayāsuravilopitāḥ।  
tvayā datto'dhikāro vai gṛhāṇa tvaṃ pitāmaha ।। 44.21 ।।  
  
brahmābravīttato devānvrajāmo viṣṇumavyayam।  
brahmādayo'bruvanviṣṇuṃ tvayā dattavare'sure ।। 44.22 ।।  
  
taddarśanādyayuḥ svargaṃ śūnyaṃ loka trayaṃ hyabhūt।  
devairukto vāsudevo brahmāṇaṃ sa vaco'bravīt ।। 44.23 ।।  
  
gatvāsuraṃ prārthayasva yajñārthaṃ dehi dehakam।  
viṣṇūktaḥ sasuro brahmā gatvā'paśyanmahāsuram ।। 44.24 ।।  
  
gayāsuro'bravīddṛṣṭvā brahmāṇaṃ tridaśaiḥ sahaḥ।  
saṃpūjyotthāya vidhivatpraṇtaḥ śraddhayānvitaḥ ।। 44.25 ।।  
  
         ।।gayāsura uvāca।।  
adya me saphalaṃ janma adya me saphalaṃ tapaḥ।  
yadāgato'tithirbrahmā sarvvaṃ prāptaṃ mayādya vai ।। 44.26 ।।  
  
yoginyogāṅgavitsarvvalokasvāminpitarguro।  
yadarthamā gato brahmanstatkāryyaṃ karavāṇyaham ।। 44.27 ।।  
  
       ।।brahmovāca।।  
pṛthivyāṃ yāni tīrthāni dṛṣṭāni bhramatā mayā।  
yajñārthaṃ na tu te tāni pavitrāṇi śarīrataḥ ।। 44.28 ।।  
  
tvayā dehe pavitratvaṃ prāptaṃ viṣṇuprasādataḥ।  
ataḥ pavitraṃ dehaṃ tvaṃ yajñārthaṃ dehime'sura ।। 44.29 ।।  
  
       ।।gayāsura uvāca।।  
dhanyo'haṃ deva deveśa yaddehaṃ prārthyate tvayā।  
pitṛvaṃśaḥ kṛtārtho me dehe yāgaṃ karoṣi cet ।। 44.30 ।।  
  
tvayaivotpādito dehaḥ pavitrastu tvayā kṛtaḥ।  
sarvveṣāmupakārāya yāgo'vaśyaṃ bhavatviti ।। 44.31 ।।  
  
ityuktvā so'patadbhūmau śvetakalpe gayāsuraḥ।  
naiṛtīṃ diśamāśritya tadā kolāhale girau ।। 44.32 ।।  
  
śiraḥ kṛtvottare daityaḥ pādau kṛtvā tu dakṣiṇe।  
brahmā sambhṛtasambhāro mānasānṛtvijo'sṛjat ।। 44.33 ।।  
  
agniśarmāṇammṛtaṃ śaunakaṃ yāñjaliṃ mṛdum।  
kumuthiṃ veda kauṇḍilyaṃ hārītaṃ kāśyapaṃ kṛpam ।। 44.34 ।।  
  
gargaṃ kauśikavāsiṣṭau muniṃ bhārgavamavyayam।  
vṛddhaṃ pārāśaraṃ kaṇvaṃ māṇḍavyaṃ śrutikevalam ।। 44.35 ।।  
  
śvetaṃ sutālaṃ damanaṃ suhotraṃ kaṅkameva ca।  
laukākṣiñca mahābāhuṃ jaigīṣavyaṃ tathaiva ca ।। 44.36 ।।  
  
dadhipañcamukhaṃ vipramṛṣabhaṃ karkameva ca।  
kātyāyanaṃ gobhilañca munimugramagāvratam ।। 44.37 ।।  
  
supālakaṃ gautamañca tathā vedaśirovratam।  
jaṭāmālinamavyagraṃ cāṭuhāsañca dāruṇam ।। 44.38 ।।  
  
ātreyaṃ cāpyaṅgirasamaupamanyuṃ mahā vratam।  
gokarṇañca guhāvāsaṃ śikhaṇḍinamumāvratam ।। 44.39 ।।  
  
etānanyāṃśca viprendrānvedhā lokapitāmahaḥ ।  
parikalpyākarodyāgaṃ gayāsuraśarīrake ।। 44.40 ।।  
  
agniśarmāpi pañcāgnīnmukhādetānathāsṛjat।  
dakṣiṇāgniṃ gārhapatyāhavanīyau tapo'vyayaḥ ।। 44.41 ।।  
  
satyāvasathyau devarṣe yeṣu yajñāḥ pratiṣṭitāḥ।  
yajñasya ca pratiṣṭārthaṃ viprebhyo dakṣiṇāṃ dadau ।। 44.42 ।।  
  
hutvā pūrṇāhutiṃ brahnā snātvā cāvabhṛthena tu।  
yajñayūpaṃ suraiḥ sārddhaṃ samānīya vyaropayat ।। 44.43 ।।  
  
brahmaṇaḥ sahasā śreṣṭe sarasyevāśritaṃ śubham।  
calitaścakito brahmā dharmmarājamabhāṣata ।। 44.44 ।।  
  
jātā gṛhe tava śilā samānīyāvicārayan।  
daityasya śīghraṃ śirasi tāṃ dhāraya mamājñayā ।। 44.45 ।।  
  
niścalārthaṃ yamaḥ śrutvā dhārayanmastake śilām।  
śilāyāṃ dhāritāyāntu saśilaścāsuro'calat ।। 44.46 ।।  
  
devānūce'tha rudrādīñchilāyāṃ niścalāḥ kila।  
tiṣṭhantu devāḥ sakalāstathetyuktvā ca te sthitāḥ ।। 44.47 ।।  
  
devāḥ pādairlakṣayitvā tathāpi calito'suraḥ।  
brahmātha vyākulo viṣṇuṃ gataḥ kṣīrābdhiśāyinam।  
tuṣṭāva praṇato bhūtvā natvā cādṛtya taṃ prabhum ।। 44.48 ।।  
  
        ।.brahmovāca।।  
brahmāṇḍasya pate nātha namāmi jagatāṃ patim।  
katiṃ kīrttimatāṃ nṝṇāṃ buktimuktipradāyakam ।। 44.49 ।।  
  
viṣvakseno'bravīdviṣṇuṃ deva tvāṃ stauti padmajaḥ।  
harirāhānaya tvaṃ taṃ viṣṇūktaḥ sa tamānayat।

iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmāhātmyaṃ nāma pañcacatvāriṃśo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 45 ।।

          ।।sanatkumāra uvāca।।  
vakṣye śilāyā māhātmyaṃ śrṛṇu nārada muktidam।  
yasyā gāyanti devāśca māhātmyaṃ munipuṅgavāḥ ।। 46.1 ।।  
  
śilā sthitā pṛthivyāṃ sā devarūpātipāvanī।  
vicitrākhyaṃ śilātīrthaṃ triṣu lokeṣu viśrutam ।। 46.2 ।।  
  
tasyāḥ saṃsparśanāllokāḥ sarve haripuraṃ yayuḥ।  
śūnye lokatraye jāte śūnyā yamapurī hyabhūt ।। 46.3 ।।  
  
yama indrādibhirgatvā ūce brahmāṇamadbhutam।  
adhikāraṃ gṛhāṇātha yamadaṇḍaṃ pitāmaha ।। 46.4 ।।  
  
yamamūce tato brahmā svagṛhe dhārayasva tām।  
brahmokto dharmarājastu gṛhe tāṃ samadhārayat ।। 46.5 ।।  
  
yamo'dhikāraṃ svaṃ cakre pāpināṃ śāsanādikam।  
evaṃvidhā gurutarā śilā jagati viśrutā ।। 46.6 ।।  
  
yathā brahnā yathā viṣṇuryathā devo maheśvaraḥ।  
brahmāṇḍe ca yathā meru statheyaṃ devarūpiṇī ।। 46.7 ।।  
  
gayāsurasya śirasi gurutvāddhāritā yataḥ ।  
ataḥ pavitrayoryogaḥ pitṝṇāṃ mokṣadāyakaḥ ।। 46.8 ।।  
  
pavitrayordvayoryoge hayamedha majo'karot।  
bhāgārthamāgatāndṛṣṭvā viṣṇvādīnabravīcchilā ।। 46.9 ।।  
  
śilāsthitipratijñāṃ tu kurvantu pitṛmuktaye।  
tathetyuktvā śilāyānte devā viṣṇvādayaḥ sthitāḥ ।। 46.10 ।।  
  
śilārūpeṇa mūrttyā ca padarūpeṇa devatāḥ।  
mūrttāmūrtasvarūpeṇa sthitāḥ pūrvapratijñayā ।। 46.11 ।।  
  
daityasya muṇḍa pṛṣṭe tu yasmātsā saṃsthitā śilā।  
tasmātsa muṇḍapṛṣṭādriḥ pitṝṇāṃ brahmalokadaḥ ।। 46.12 ।।  
  
ācchāditaḥ śilāpādaḥ prabhāsenādriṇā yataḥ।  
bhāsito bhāskareṇeti prabhāsaḥ parikalpitaḥ ।। 46.13 ।।  
  
prabhāsaṃ hi vinirbhidya śilāṅguṣṭo vinirgataḥ।  
aṅguṣṭotthita īśo'pi prabhāseśaḥ prakīrttitaḥ ।। 46.14 ।।  
  
śilāṅguṣṭaikateśo yaḥ sā ca pretaśilā smṛtā।  
piṇḍadānādyatastasyāṃ pretatvānmucyate naraḥ ।। 46.15 ।।  
  
mahānadīprabāsādyoḥ saṅgame snānakṛnnaraḥ।  
rāmo devyā saha snāto rāmatīrthaṃ tataḥ smṛtam ।। 46.16 ।।  
  
prārthito'tra mahānadyā rāma snāto bhaveti ca।  
rāmatīrthaṃ tato bhūtvā triṣu lokeṣu viśrutam ।। 46.17 ।।  
  
janmāntaraśataṃ sāgraṃ yatkṛtaṃ duṣkṛtaṃ mayā।  
tatsarvaṃ vilayaṃ yātu rāmatīrthābhiṣecanāt ।। 46.18 ।।  
  
mantreṇānena yaḥ snātvā śrāddhaṃ kurvīta mānavaḥ।  
rāmatīrthe piṇḍadastu viṣṇulokaṃ prayātyasau।  
tathetyuktvā sthito rāmaḥ sītayā bharatāgrajaḥ ।। 46.19 ।।  
  
rāma rāma mahābāho devānāmabhayaṅkara।  
tvāṃ namasye'tra deveśaṃ mama naśyatu pātakam ।। 46.20 ।।  
  
mantreṇānena yaḥ snātvā śrāddhaṃ kuryātsapiṇḍakam ।  
pretatvāttasya pitaro vimuktāḥ pitṛtāṃ yayuḥ ।। 46.21 ।।  
  
āpastvamasi deveśa jyotiṣāṃ patireva ca।  
pāpa nāśaya me deva manovākkāyakarmajam ।। 46.22 ।।  
  
namaskṛtya prabhāseśaṃ bhāsamānaṃ śivaṃ vrajet।  
tañca śambhuṃ namaskṛtya kuryādyamabaliṃ tataḥ ।। 46.23 ।।  
  
rāme vanaṃ gate śailamāgatya bharataḥ sthitaḥ ।  
pitṛpiṇḍādikaṃ kṛtvā rāmaṃ saṃsthāpya tatra ca ।। 46.24 ।।  
  
rāmaṃ sītāṃ lakṣmaṇañca munīnsthāpitavānprabhuḥ।  
bharatasyāśrame puṇye nityaṃ puṇyatamairvṛtam।  
mataṅgasya padaṃ tatra dṛśyate sarvamānuṣaiḥ ।। 46.25 ।।  
  
sthāpitaṃ dharmasarvasvaṃ lokasyāsya nidarśanāt।  
mataṅgasya pade śrāddhī sarvāṃstārayate pitṝn ।। 46.26 ।।  
  
rāmatīrthe naraḥ snātvā rāmaṃ sītāṃ samarcya ca।  
rāmeśvaraṃ praṇamyātha na dehī jāyate punaḥ ।। 46.27 ।।  
  
śilāyā jaghanaṃ bhūyaḥ samākrāntaṃ nagena tu।  
dharmarājena saṃprokto na gaccheti nagaḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 46.28 ।।  
  
yamarājadharmarājau niścalārthaṃ vyavasthitau।  
tābhyāṃ baliṃ prayacchāmi pitṝṇāṃ mukti hetave ।। 46.29 ।।  
  
dvau śvānau śyāmaśabalau vaivasvatakulodbhavau।  
tābhyāṃ baliṃ prayacchāmi syātāmetāvahiṃsakau ।। 46.30 ।।  
  
aindravāruṇa vāyanyayāmyanaiṛtyasaṃsthitāḥ।  
vāyasāḥ pratigṛhṇantu bhūyo piṇḍaṃ mayā sthitam ।। 46.31 ।।  
  
yamo'si yamadūto'si vāyaso'si mahābala।  
saptajanmakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ baliṃ bhuktvā vināśaya ।। 46.32 ।।  
  
rāme vanaṃ gate śailamāgatya bharatena hi।  
pituḥ piṇḍādikaṃ kṛtvā rāmeśaḥ sthāpito'tra vai ।। 46.33 ।।  
  
snātvā natvā ca rāmeśaṃ rāmasītā samanvitam।  
tatra śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍañca kṛtvā viṣṇupuraṃ vrajet।  
pitṛbhiḥ saha dharmātmā kulānāñca śataiḥsaha ।। 46.34 ।।  
  
rāmaṃ sītāṃ lakṣmaṇañca munīnsthāpitavānprabhuḥ।  
bharatasyāśrame puṇye nityaṃ puṇyatamairvṛtam।  
mataṅgasya padaṃ tatra dṛśyate sarvamānuṣaiḥ ।। 46.35 ।।  
  
vahnirdvau varuṇau rudrāścatvāraḥ pitṛmokṣadāḥ।  
bharatāśramamāsādya tānnametpūjayennaraḥ ।। 46.36 ।।  
  
pāpebhyaścopapāpebhyo mucyate pitṛbhiḥ saha।  
yatra kutrāpi devarṣe bharatasyāśrame naraḥ ।  
snātaḥ śrāddhādikaṃ kuryāttatkalpe'pi na hīyate ।। 46.37 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ cākṣayaṃ śrāddhaṃ japahomatapāṃsi ca।  
sarvamānantyamāhurvai yaddattaṃ bharatāśrame ।। 46.38 ।।  
  
caturyugasvarūpeṇa catasro ravimūrttayaḥ ।  
dṛṣṭāḥ spṛṣṭāḥ pūjitāstāḥ pitṝṇāṃ muktidāyakāḥ ।। 46.39 ।।  
  
muktirvāṃmana ityeva tārakākhyo vidhiḥ paraḥ।  
saṃsārārṇavataptānāṃ nāvāvetau sureśvarau।  
tārakaṃ brahma viśveṣāṃ mṛtānāṃ jīvatāmidam ।। 46.40 ।।  
  
trivikramañca brahmāṇaṃ yaḥ paśyetpuruṣottamam।  
pitṛbhiḥ saha dharmātmā sa yāti paramāṃ gatim ।। 46.41 ।।  
  
śilāyā vāmapāde'pi tathābhyudyantako giriḥ ।  
sthāpitaḥ piṇḍadastatra pitṝnbrahmapuraṃ nayet ।। 46.42 ।।  
  
naimiṣāraṇyapārśve tu īce brahmā suraiḥ saha।  
mukhyasaṃjñaṃ hi tattīrthaṃ devāstatra pade sthitāḥ ।। 46.43 ।।  
  
triṣu teṣu padeṣveva tīrtheṣu munisattama।  
yatkiñcidaśubhaṃ karma tatpraṇaśyati nārada ।। 46.44 ।।  
  
tannaimiṣavanaṃ puṇyaṃ sevitaṃ puṇyapūruṣaiḥ।  
tatra vyāsaḥ śukaḥ pailaḥ kaṇvo vedhāḥ śivo hariḥ ।। 46.45 ।।  
  
teṣāṃ darśanamātreṇa mucyate pātakairnaraḥ।  
vāmahaste śilāyāstu tathā codyantako giriḥ ।। 46.46 ।।  
  
sa parvataḥ samānīto hyagastyena mahātmanā।  
tatra brahmā haraścaiva tapaścograñca cakratuḥ ।। 46.47 ।।  
  
tatrāgastyasya hi varaṃ kuṇḍaṃ trailokyadurlabham।  
yatra munyaṣṭakaṃ siddhaṃ tapastaptvā śivaṃ gatam।  
kuṇḍe munyaṣṭakaṃ natvā pitṝnbrahmapuraṃ nayet ।। 46.48 ।।  
  
agastyenātha devarṣe hyudayādrermahātmanā।  
śilāyā vāmahaste'pi sthāpito girirāṭ śubhaḥ।  
vāditrādyairdivyagītairāḍhyo vāditrako giriḥ ।। 46.49 ।।  
  
tatra vidyādharo nāma gandharvāpsarasāṅgaṇaiḥ।  
sameto'dyāpi kītāni divyāni saha gīyate ।। 46.50 ।।  
  
mohanaśca sunīthaśca śailūjo mohanottamaḥ।  
parvato nāradadhyānī saṅgītī puṣpadantakaḥ।  
hāhā hūhūprabhṛtayo gītadānaṃ prajakrire ।। 46.51 ।।  
  
tathā citraratho nāma sarvagandharvasaṃvṛtaḥ।  
gāyati madhurāṇyeva gītānyadrau mahotsavam ।। 46.52 ।।  
  
ataḥ sa parvato devaiḥ sevyate'dyāpi nityaśaḥ।  
dharmajāstatra deveśo haro bhasmāṅgarāgavān ।। 46.53 ।।  
  
pārvatyā sahito rudraḥ parvate gītanādite।  
modate pūjitodyeyaḥ pitṝṇāṃ paramāṃ gatim ।। 46.54 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ paramātmā hi gopatirvā gadādharaḥ।  
hīyate vaiṣṇavī māyā tathā rudrārccayā mune ।। 46.55 ।।  
  
śilāyā dakṣiṇe haste bhasmakūṭo girirdhṛtaḥ।  
dharmmarājena tatrāste hyagastyaḥ saha bhāryyayā ।। 46.56 ।।  
  
agastyasya pade snātaḥ piṇḍado brahmalokagaḥ।  
brahmaṇastu varaṃ lebhe māhātmyaṃ bhuvi durlabham ।। 46.57 ।।  
  
lopāmudrāṃ tathā bhāryyāṃ pitṝṇāṃ paramāṃ gatim।  
tatrāgastyeśvaraṃ dṛṣṭvā mucyate brahmahatyayā ।। 46.58 ।।  
  
agastyañca sabhāryyañca pitṝnbrahmapuraṃ nayet।  
daṇḍinātha tapastepe sītādrerdakṣiṇe girau ।। 46.59 ।।  
  
vaṭo vaṭeśvarastatra sthitaśca prapitāmahaḥ।  
tadagre rukmiṇīkuṇḍaṃ paścime kapilā nadī।  
kapileśo nadītīre hyamāsomasamāgame ।। 46.60 ।।  
  
kapilāyāṃ naraḥ snātvā kapileśaṃ samarcya ca।  
kṛte śrāddhe piṇḍadāne pitaro mokṣamāpnuyuḥ ।। 46.61 ।।  
  
tatrāgnidhārā girivarādāgatodyantakādanu।  
tatra sārasvataṃ kuṇḍaṃ sarasvatyā prakalpitam ।। 46.62 ।।  
  
śukrastatra sutaiḥ sārddhaṃ ṣaṇḍāmarkkādibhiḥ prabhuḥ।  
tatra tatra munīndrāṇāṃ padeṣu munisattama।  
śrāddhapiṇḍādikṛtsnātaḥ pitṝṃstārayate naraḥ ।। 46.63 ।।  
  
śilāyā vāmahaste'pi gṛdrakūṭo girirdhṛtaḥ।  
gṛdhrarūpeṇa saṃsiddhāstapastaptvā maharṣayaḥ ।। 46.64 ।।  
  
ato girirgṛdhrakūṭastatra gṛdhreśvaraḥ sthitaḥ।  
dṛṣṭvā gṛdhreśvaraṃ natvā yāyācchambhoḥ padaṃ naraḥ ।। 46.65 ।।  
  
tatra gṛdhre guhāyāñca piṇḍadaḥ śivalokabhāk ।  
tatra gṛdhre vaṭaṃ natvā prāptakāmo divaṃ vrajet ।। 46.66 ।।  
  
ṛṇamokṣaṃ pāpamokṣaṃ śivaṃ dṛṣṭvā śivaṃ vrajet।  
śūrakṣetrañca tatrāste piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn ।। 46.67 ।।  
  
ādipālena giriṇā samākrāntaṃ śilodaram।  
tatrāste gajarūpeṇa vighreśo vignanāśanaḥ।  
taṃ dṛṣṭvā mucyate vighnaiḥ pitṝn brahmapuraṃ nayet ।। 46.68 ।।  
  
nitambe muṇḍapṛṣṭhasya devadāruvanaṃ tvabhūt।  
muṇḍapṛṣṭāravindrādrī dṛṣṭvā pāpaṃ vināśayet।  
gayānābhau suṣumnāyāṃ piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn ।। 46.69 ।।  
  
śilāyā vāmapāde tu sthāpitaḥ pretaparvataḥ।  
dharmarājena pāpebhyo giriḥ pretaśilāhvayaḥ ।। 46.70 ।।  
  
pādena dūre nikṣiptaḥ śilāyāḥ pādabhārataḥ।  
gataḥ śilāyāḥ saṃsargātpretakūṭaḥ pavitratām ।। 46.71 ।।  
  
pretakuṇḍañca tatrāste devāstatra pade sthitāḥ।  
tatra kuṇḍādikaṃ kṛtvā pretatvānmocayetpitṝn ।। 46.72 ।।  
  
pṛthaksthitāśca bahavo vighnakāriṇa eva te।  
śrāddhādikāriṇāṃ nṝṇāṃ tīrthe pitṛvimuktaye।  
pretā dhānuṣkarūpeṇa karagrahaṇakārakāḥ ।। 46.73 ।।  
  
pādāṅkitāṃ muṇḍapṛṣṭāṃ mahādevanivāsinīm।  
tāṃ dṛṣṭvā sarvalokaśca muktaḥ pāpopapātakaiḥ ।। 46.74 ।।  
  
gayāśirasi puṇye ca sarvapāpairvivarjite।  
gretādivarjitaṃ yasmāttato'tipāvanaṃ varam ।। 46.75 ।।  
  
kīkaṭeṣu gayā puṇyā puṇyaṃ rājagṛhaṃ vanam।  
cyavanasyāśramaṃ puṇyaṃ nadī puṇyā punaḥpunā ।। 46.76 ।।  
  
vaikuṇṭo lohadaṇḍaśca gṛdhrakūṭaśca śoṇakaḥ।  
atra śrāddhādinā sarvānpitṝnbrahmapuraṃ nayet ।। 46.77 ।।  
  
krauñcarūpeṇa hi munirmuṇḍapṛṣṭe tapo'karot।  
tasya pādāṅkito yasmātkrauñcapādastataḥ smṛtaḥ ।। 46.78 ।।  
  
snāto jalāśaye tatra nayetsvargaṃ svakaṃ kulam।  
baliḥ kākaśilāyāñca kākebhya ṛṇamokṣadaḥ ।। 46.79 ।।  
  
muṇḍapṛṣṭhasya sānau hi lomaśo lomaharṣaṇaḥ।  
dvāvetau paramaṃ taptvā tapaḥsiddhiṃ parāṃ gatau ।। 46.80 ।।  
  
āhūtāstu saricchreṣṭā lomaśena mahānadī।  
śarāvatī vetravatī candrabhāgā sarasvatī ।। 46.81 ।।  
  
kāverī sindhurīrā ca candanā ca saridvarā।  
vāsiṣṭī sarayūrgaṅgā yamunā gaṇḍakīndirā ।। 46.82 ।।  
  
mahāvaitaraṇī nāmnā nikṣarā ca divaukasaḥ ।  
sāvavyalakanandā ca hyudīcī kankāhvayā ।। 46.83 ।।  
  
kauśikī brahmadā jyeṣṭā sarvasyāghavimocinī।  
kṛṣṇavalvā carmmavatī dve nadyau muktidāyike ।। 46.84 ।।  
  
āhūte saritāṃ śreṣṭe lomahrarṣeṇa sāhasāt ।  
tapasastu prabhāveṇa narmmadā munipuṅgava।  
tāsu sarvāsu yaḥ snātvā piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn ।। 46.85 ।।  
  
brahmayoniṃ praviśyātha nirgacchedyastu mānavaḥ।  
paraṃ brahma sa yātīha vimukto yonisaṅkaṭāt ।। 46.86 ।।  
  
nikṣarāyāṃ puṣkariṇyāṃ snātaḥ śrāddhādikaṃ naraḥ।  
kuryyātkrauñcapade divye niyamādvāsaratrayam।  
sarvānpitṝnnayetsvargaṃ pañcapāpina eva ca ।। 46.87 ।।  
  
janārdano bhasmakūṭe tasya haste tu piṇḍadaḥ।  
ātmano'pyathavānyeṣāṃ savyenāpi tilairvinā।  
jīvatāṃ dadhisaṃmiśraṃ sarve te viṣṇulokagāḥ ।। 46.88 ।।  
  
yastu piṇḍo mayā dattastava haste janārdana।  
yaduddiśya tvayā deyastasminpiṇḍo mṛte prabho ।। 46.89 ।।  
  
eṣa piṇḍo mayā dattastava haste janārdana।  
antakāle gate mahyaṃ tvayā deyo gayāśire ।। 46.90 ।।  
  
janārdana namastubhyaṃ namaste pitṛmokṣada।  
pitṛpate namaste'stu namaste pitṝūpiṇe ।। 46.91 ।।  
  
gayāyāṃ pitṝūpeṇa svayameva janārdanaḥ।  
taṃ dṛṣṭvā puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ mucyate ca ṛṇatrayāt ।। 46.92 ।।  
  
namaste puṇḍarīkākṣa ṛṇatrayavimocaka।  
lakṣmīkānta namaste'stu pitṝṇāṃ mokṣado bhava ।। 46.93 ।।  
  
vāmajānu susaṃpātya natvā bhīmo janārdanam।  
śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍakaṃ kṛtvā bhrātṛbhirbrahmalokabhāk।  
pitṛbhiḥ saha dharmātmā kulānāñca śatena ca ।। 46.94 ।।  
  
śilāyāṃ vyaktarūpeṇa vyaktāvyaktātmanā sthitaḥ।  
lakṣmīśo vibhudhaiḥ sārddhaṃ tasmāddevamayī śilā ।। 46.95 ।।

śrāddhaṃ kṛtvodyato dātuṃ pitrādibhyaśca piṇḍakam ।। 49.80 ।।  
piturvinirgatau hastau gayāśirasi śantanoḥ।  
nādātpiṇḍaṃ karo bhīṣmo dadau viṣṇupade tataḥ ।। 49.81 ।।  
śantanuḥ prāha santuṣṭaḥ śāstrārthe niścalo bhavān।  
trikāladṛṣṭirbhavat cānte viṣṇuśca te gatiḥ ।। 49.82 ।।  
svecchayā maraṇaṃ cāstu ityuktvā muktimāgataḥ।  
kanakeśañca kedāraṃ nārasiṃhaṃ ca vāmanam ।। 49.83 ।।  
udaṅmārge samabhyarccya pitṝnsarvvāṃśca tārayet।  
gayāśirasi yaḥ piṇḍānyeṣāṃ nāmnā tu nirvapet ।। 49.84 ।।  
narakasthā divaṃ yānti svargasthā mokṣamāpnuyuḥ।  
gayāśirasi yaḥ piṇḍaṃ śamīpatrapramāṇataḥ ।। 49.85 ।।  
kandamūlaphalādyairvā dadyātsvargaṃ nayetpitṝn।  
padāni yatra dṛśyante viṣṇvādīnāṃ tadagrataḥ ।। 49.86 ।।  
śrāddhaṃ kṛtvā sapiṇḍañca teṣāṃ lokānnayetpitṝn।  
sarvvatra muṇḍapṛṣṭādriḥ padairebhiḥ sulakṣitaḥ ।। 49.87 ।।  
prayānti pitaraḥ sarvve brahmalokamanāmayam।  
hetyasurasya yacchīrṣaṃ gadayā taddvidhākṛtam ।। 49.88 ।।  
tataḥ prakṣālitā yasmāttīrthaṃ tacca vimuktaye।  
gadālolamiti khyātaṃ sarvveṣāmuttamottamam ।। 49.89 ।।  
gadālole mahātīrthe gadāprakṣālanāddhareḥ।  
snānaṃ karomi siddhyarthamakṣayaṃ padamāpnuyām ।। 49.90 ।।  
pañcame'hni gadālole snātvā kuryyātsapiṇḍakam।  
śrāddhaṃ pitṝnbrahmalokaṃ nayedātmānameva ca ।। 49.91 ।।  
brahmaprakalpitānviprān havyakavyādinārccayet।  
taistuṣṭaistoṣitāḥ sarvvāḥ pitṛbhiḥ saha devatāḥ ।। 49.92 ।।  
kṛte śrāddhe'kṣayavaṭe annenaiva prayatnataḥ।  
pitṝnnayedbrahmalokamakṣayantu sanātanam ।। 49.93 ।।  
vaṭavṛkṣasamīpe tu śākenāpyudakena vā।  
ekasminbhojite vipre koṭirbhavati bhojitā ।। 49.94 ।।  
deyaṃ dānaṃ ṣoḍaśakaṃ gayātīrthapurodhase।  
vastraṃ gandhādibhiḥ putraiḥ samyaksaṃpūjya yatnataḥ ।। 49.95 ।।  
gayātīrthavaṭe caiva pitṝṇāṃ dattamakṣayam ।  
dṛṣṭvā natvā ca saṃpūjya vaṭeśaṃ susamāhitaḥ ।। 49.96 ।।  
pitṝnnayedbrahmalokamakṣayantu sanātanam।  
gayāyāṃ dharmmapṛṣṭe ca sarasi brahmaṇasta thā ।। 49.97 ।।  
gayāśīrṣe vaṭe caiva pitṝṇāṃ dattamakṣayam।  
ekārṇave vaṭasyāgre yaḥ śete yoganidrayā ।। 49.98 ।।  
bālarūpadharastasmai namaste yogaśāyine।  
saṃsāravṛkṣaśastrāyāśeṣapāpaharāya ca ।। 49.99 ।।  
akṣayabrahmadātre ca namo'kṣayavaṭāya vai।  
kalau māheśvarā lokā yena tasmādgadādharaḥ।  
liṅgarūpo'bhavattañca vande śrīprapitāmaham ।। 49.100 ।।  
iti śrīvāyupurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmāhātmyaṃ nāmonapañcāśattamo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 49 ।

         ।।sanatkumāra uvāca।।  
yajñañcakre gayo rājā bahvannaṃ bahudakṣiṇam।  
yatra dravyasamūhānāṃ saṃkhyā karttuṃ na śakyate ।। 50.1 ।।  
  
sthitā gayāyāmannādiparvvatāḥ pañcaviṃśatiḥ।  
praśaṃsanti dvijāstatra deśe deśe supūjitāḥ ।। 50.2 ।।  
  
naiva pūrvvaṃ kepyakurvvanna kariṣyanti cāpare।  
sikatā vā yathā loke yathā ca divi tārakāḥ ।। 50.3 ।।  
  
tathā bahusuvarṇādyairasaṅkhyātāstu dakṣiṇāḥ ।  
naiveha pūrve ye kecinna kariṣyanti cāpare ।। 50.4 ।।  
  
praśaṃsanti dvijāstṛptā deśe deśe supūjitāḥ।  
gayaṃ viṣṇvādayastuṣṭā varaṃ brūhīti cābruvan ।। 50.5 ।।  
  
gayastānprārthayāmāsa hyabhiśaptāśca ce purā।  
brahmaṇā te dvijāḥ pūtā bhavantu kratupūjitāḥ ।। 50.6 ।।  
  
gayāśrāddhavidhānāya dvijā mūrttāścadurddaśa।  
teṣāṃ vākyaṃ prakurvvīta yadi brahmā svayaṃ bhavet ।। 50.7 ।।  
  
gautamaṃ kāśyapaṃ kautsaṃ kauśikaṃ kaṇvameva ca।  
bhāradvājaṃ hyauśanasaṃ vātsyaṃ pārāśaraṃ tathā ।। 50.8 ।।  
  
haritkumāramāṇḍavyaṃ lokākṣiṃ lokasaṃmahat।  
vāsiṣṭhañca tathātreyaṃ gotrāṇyeṣāṃ caturddaśa ।। 50.9 ।।  
  
gayāpurīti mannāmnā khyātā brahma purī yathā।  
evamastu varaṃ dattvā tathā cāntardadhuḥ surāḥ ।। 50.10 ।।  
  
gayaśca bhogānsambhujya viṣṇulokaṃ paraṃ yayau।  
viśālāyāṃ viśālo'bhūdrājā'putro 'bravīddvijān ।। 50.11 ।।  
  
kathaṃ putrādayo me syurviśālaṃ cābruvandvijāḥ।  
gayāyāṃ piṇḍadānena tava sarvvaṃ bhaviṣyati ।। 50.12 ।।  
  
viśālo'pi gayāśīrṣa piṇḍadaḥ putravānabhūt।  
dṛṣṭvākāśe sitaṃ raktaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ puruṣamabravīt ।। 50.13 ।।  
  
ke yūyaṃ teṣu caivaikaḥ sitaḥ proce viśālakam।  
ahaṃ sitaste janaka indralokādihāgataḥ ।। 50.14 ।।  
  
mama putra pitā rakto brahmahā pāpakṛttamaḥ ।  
ayaṃ pitāmahaḥ kṛṣṇa ṛṣayo yena ghātitāḥ ।। 50.15 ।।  
  
avīcinarakaṃ prāptau muktau tvatpiṇḍadānataḥ।  
pitṝnpitāmahāṃścaiva tathaiva prapitāmahān ।। 50.16 ।।  
  
prīṇayāmīti yattoyaṃ tvayā dattamarindama।  
tenāsmadyugapadyogo jāto vākyena sattama ।। 50.17 ।।  
  
muktiḥ kṛtā tvayā putra vrajāmaḥ svargamuttamam।  
evaṃ putraiḥ pitṝṇāṃ ca kartavyā muktiruttamā ।। 50.18 ।।  
  
tvañca rājyaṃ ciraṃ kṛtvā bhuktvā bhogāṃśca durllabhān।  
yajñānsadakṣiṇānkṛtvā yāyādviṣṇupuraṃ tataḥ ।। 50.19 ।।  
  
evaṃ labdhavaro rājā rājyaṃ kṛtvā divaṃgataḥ।  
pretarājaḥ saha pretairgayāśrāddhāddivaṃ gataḥ ।। 50.20 ।।  
  
pretaḥ kaścidvimuktyarthaṃ vaṇijaṃ kañcidabravīt।  
mama nāmnā gayāśīrṣe piṇḍanirvvāpaṇaṃ kuru ।। 50.21 ।।  
  
pretabhāvavimuktyarthaṃ tvaṃ gṛhāṇa dhanaṃ mama।  
tadvanaṃ sarvvamādāya gayāśrāddhavyayaṃ kuru ।। 50.22 ।।  
  
ṣoḍaśapañcabhāgāṃśca tubhyaṃ vai dattavānaham।  
svanāmāni yathānyāyaṃ samyagākhyātavānaham ।। 50.23 ।।  
  
gatvā gayāṃ gayāśīrṣa pretarājāya piṇḍakam।  
samadādbandhubhiḥ sārddhaṃ svapitṛbhyastato dadau ।। 50.24 ।।  
  
pretaḥ pretatvanirmmukto vaṇik svagṛhamāgataḥ ।  
evaṃ gayasya śambhośca kṣetraṃ viṣṇo ravestathā ।। 50.25 ।।  
  
upoṣito'tha gāyatrītīrthe mahānadīsthite।  
gāyatryāḥ purataḥ snātvā prātaḥsandhyāmupāsayet ।। 50.26 ।।  
  
śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍakaṃ kṛtvānayedbrahmaṇyatāṃ kulam।  
tīrthe samudite snātvā sāvitryāḥ purato naraḥ ।। 50.27 ।।  
  
sandhyāmupāsya madhyāhne nayetkulaśatandivam।  
piṇḍadānaṃ tataḥ kuryyātpitṝṇāṃ muktikāmyayā ।। 50.28 ।।  
  
prācīsarasvatītīrthe snātvā cāpi yathāvidhi।  
sandhyāmupāsya sāyāhne viṣṇulokaṃ nayetpitṝn ।। 50.29 ।।  
  
bahujanmakṛtātsandhyālopānmuktastrisandhya kṛt।  
viśālāyāṃ lelihāne tīrthe ca bharatāśrame ।। 50.30 ।।  
  
pādāṅkite muṇḍapṛṣṭe gadādharasamīpataḥ।  
tīrthe cākāśagaṅgāyāṃ girikarṇamukheṣu ca ।। 50.31 ।।  
  
snāto'tha piṇḍado brahmalokaṃ kulaśataṃ nayet।  
devanadyāṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ snātaḥ svargaṃ nayetpitṝn ।। 50.32 ।।  
  
snāto godo vaitaraṇyāṃ triḥsaptakulamuddharet।  
satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ tu nārada ।। 50.33 ।।  
  
ekaviṃśatikulānyāhustārayennātra saṃśayaḥ।  
yā sā vaitaraṇī nāma nadī trailokyaviśrutā ।। 50.34 ।।  
  
sāvatīrṇā gayākṣetre pitṝṇāṃ taraṇāya vai।  
godāvaryyāṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ yamunāyāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 50.35 ।।  
  
devanadyāṃ gopracāre śrāddhadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn।  
puṣkariṇyāṃ ghṛtakulyāṃ madhukulyāṃ tathaiva ca ।। 50.36 ।।  
  
koṭitīrthe rukmiṇīye piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn।  
trirātropoṣaṇenaiva tīrthābhigamanena ca ।। 50.37 ।।  
  
adattvā kāñcanaṅgāśca daridro jāyate naraḥ।  
ghṛtakulyā madhukulyā devikā ca mahānadī ।। 50.38 ।।  
  
śilāyāḥ saṅgamo yatra madhusravā prakīrtitā।  
ayutaṃ cāśvamedhānāṃ snānakṛllabhate naraḥ ।। 50.39 ।।  
  
śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍakaṃ kṛtvā piṇḍadānaṃ tathaiva ca।  
kulānāṃ śatamuddhṛtya viṣṇulokaṃ nayennaraḥ ।। 50.40 ।।  
  
daśāśca medhike haṃsatīrthe cāmarakaṅkaṭe।  
koṭitīrthe rukmakuṇḍe piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn ।। 50.41 ।।  
  
vaitaraṇyāṃ ghṛtakulyāṃ madhukulyāṃ tathaiva ca।  
koṭitīrthe naraḥ snātvā dṛṣṭvā koṭīśvarañca yaḥ ।। 50.42 ।।  
  
koṭijanma bhavedvipro dhanāḍhyo vedapāragaḥ ।  
mārkaṇḍeyeśakocīśau natvā syātpitṛtārakaḥ ।। 50.43 ।।  
  
rukma pārijātavane pārvvatyā saha śaṅkaraḥ।  
rahasye saṃsthito reme yugānāmayutaṃ purā ।। 50.44 ।।  
  
marīciḥ phalapuṣpārthaṃ pārijātavanaṃ gataḥ।  
dṛṣṭvā śapto maheśena yasmātsukhavighātakaḥ ।। 50.45 ।।  
  
duḥkhī bhaveti tadbhīto maricistuṣṭuve śivam।  
tuṣṭaḥ provāca taṃ śambhurvṛṇīṣva varamuttamam ।। 50.46 ।।  
  
śāpād bhavatu muktirmme marīciḥ prāha śaṅkaram।  
bhavedgayāyāṃ muktiste śivoktaḥ prayayau gayām ।। 50.47 ।।  
  
śilāsthitastapastepe sarvveṣāṃ duṣkarañca yat।  
marīcirīśvarācchaptaḥ kṛṣṇatvamagamatpurā ।। 50.48 ।।  
  
tapasā dāruṇeneha sa vipraḥ śuklatāṃ gataḥ ।  
harirūce marīciñca varaṃ vṛṇuhi putraka ।। 50.49 ।।  
  
kimalabhyaṃ tvayi tuṣṭe marīciḥ prāha mādhavam।  
haraśāpādvimukto'haṃ śilā bhavatu pāvanī ।। 50.50 ।।  
  
pitṛmuktikarī ca syāttathetyuktvā divaṃ gataḥ।  
divaukasāṃ puṣkariṇīṃ samāsādya naraḥ śuciḥ ।। 50.51 ।।  
  
yatra dattaṃ pitṛbhyastu bhavatyakṣayamityuta।  
tatra snāto divaṃ yāti svaśarīreṇa mānavaḥ ।। 50.52 ।।  
  
pāpmānaṃ prajahātyeṣa jīrṇatvacamivoragaḥ।  
tatpaṅgajavanaṃ puṇyaṃ puṇyakṛdbhirniṣevitam ।। 50.53 ।।  
  
pāṇḍuśilā vai tatrāste śrāddhaṃ yatrākṣayaṃ bhavet।  
yudhiṣṭirastu tasyāṃ hi śrāddhaṃ karttuṃ yayau mune ।। 50.54 ।।  
  
tatra kāle pāṇḍunoktaṃ maddhaste dehi piṇḍakam।  
hastaṃ tyaktvā śilāyāñca piṇḍadānañcakāra saḥ ।। 50.55 ।।  
  
śilāyāṃ piṇḍadānena prahṛṣṭo vyāsanandanaḥ।  
varaṃ dadau svaputrāya rājyaṃ kuru mahītale ।। 50.56 ।।  
  
akaṇṭakantu sampūrṇaṃ tvaṃ me trātā hi putraka।  
svargaṃ vraja śarīreṇa bhrātṛbhiḥ parivāritaḥ ।। 50.57 ।।  
  
dṛṣṭimātreṇa sampūtānnarakasthāndivaṃ naya।  
ityuktvā prayayau pāṇḍuḥ śāśvataṃ padamavyayam ।। 50.58 ।।  
  
udbhijjāḥ svedajā vāpi hyaṇḍajā ye jarāyujāḥ।  
madhusravāṃ samāsādya mṛtāḥ svargapuraṃ yayuḥ ।। 50.59 ।।  
  
daśāśvamedhike haṃsatīrthe śrāddhāddivaṃ vrajet।  
daśāśvamedhahaṃsau ca natvā śivapuraṃ vrajet ।। 50.60 ।।  
  
bharatasyāśrame śrāddhānnayedbrahmālayaṃ pitṝn।  
mataṅgasya pade śrāddhī brahmalokaṃ nayetpitṝn ।। 50.61 ।।  
  
nirmmathyāgniṃ śamīgarbhe vidhirviṣṇvādibhiḥ saha।  
lebhe tīrthantu yajñārthaṃ triṣu lokeṣu viśrutam ।। 50.62 ।।  
  
makhasaṃjñantu tattīrthaṃ pitṝṇāṃ mukti dāyakam।  
snātvā ca tarpaṇaṃ kṛtvā piṇḍado muktimāpnuyāt ।। 50.63 ।।  
  
pitṝnsvargaṃ nayennatvā saṅgame'ṅgārakeśvarau।  
gayākūṭe piṇḍadānādaśvamedhaphalaṃ labhet ।। 50.64 ।।  
  
bhasmakūṭe bhasmanāthaṃ natvā ca tārayetpitṝn ।  
tyaktapāpo bhavenmuktaḥ saṅgame snānamācaret ।। 50.65 ।।  
  
iṣṭiṃ cakre'śvamedhākhyaṃ vasiṣṭo munisattamaḥ।  
iṣṭito nirgataḥ śambhurvaraṃ vṛṇu vasiṣṭhakam ।। 50.66 ।।  
  
prāheti taṃ vasiṣṭo'pi śiva tuṣṭo'si me yadi।  
vastavyaṃ cātra deveśa tathetyuktvā śivaḥ sthitaḥ ।। 50.67 ।।  
  
piṇḍado dhenukāraṇye kāmadhenupadeṣu ca।  
snātvā natvātha sampūjya brahmalokaṃ nayetpitṝn ।। 50.68 ।।  
  
karddamāle gayānābhau muṇḍapṛṣṭhasamīpataḥ।  
snātvā śrāddhādikaṃ kṛtvā pitṝṇāmanṛṇo bhavet ।। 50.69 ।।  
  
phalgucaṇḍīśmaśānākṣīmaṅgalādyāḥ samarccayet।  
gayāyāñca vṛṣotsargātriḥ saptakulamuddharet ।। 50.70 ।।  
  
yatra tatra sthitā devā ṛṣayo'pi jitendriyāḥ ।  
ādyaṃ gadādharaṃ dhyāyañchrāddhapiṇḍādidānataḥ ।। 50.71 ।।  
  
kulānāṃ śatamuddhṛtya brahmalokaṃ nayet pitṝn।  
gayā gayo gayā dityo gāyatrī ca gadādharaḥ ।। 50.72 ।।  
  
gayā gayāsuraścaiva ṣaṅgayā muktidāyakāḥ।  
gayākhyānamidaṃ puṇyaṃ yaḥ paṭetsatataṃ naraḥ ।। 50.73 ।।  
  
śrṛṇuyācchraddhayā yastu sa yāti paramāṃ gatim।  
pāṭhayedvā gayākhyānaṃ viprebhyaḥ puṇyakṛnnaraḥ ।। 50.74 ।।  
  
gayāśrāddhaṃ kṛtaṃ tena kṛtaṃ tena suniścitam।  
gayāyā mahimānañca hyabhyasedyaḥ samāhitaḥ ।। 50.75 ।।  
  
teneṣṭaṃ rājasūyena aśvamedhena nārada।  
likhedvā lekhayedvāpi pūjayedvāpi pustakam ।। 50.76 ।।  
  
tasya gehe sthirā lakṣmīḥ suprasannā bhaviṣyati।  
upākhyānamidaṃ puṇyaṃ gṛhe tiṣṭhati pustakam ।। 50.77 ।।  
  
sarpāgnicaurajanitaṃ bhayaṃ tatra na vidyate।  
śrāddhakāle paṭedyastu gayāmāhātmyamuttamam ।। 50.78 ।।  
  
vidhihīnantu tatsarvaṃ pitṝṇāntu gayāsamam।  
yāni tīrthāni trailokye tāni dṛṣṭāni tatra vai।  
yena jñātaṃ gayākhyānaṃ śrutaṃ vā paṭitaṃ mune ।। 50.79 ।।  
  
           ।।sūta uvāca।।  
sanatkumāro munipuṅgavāya puṇyāṃ kathāñcātha nivedya bhaktyā।  
svamāśramaṃ puṇyavanairupetaṃ visṛjya saṃgītaguruṃ jagāma ।। 50.80 ।।  
  
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmahātmyaṃ nāma pañcāśattamo'dhyāyaḥ ।। 50 ।। samāptamidaṃ vāyupurāṇam ।। * ।
