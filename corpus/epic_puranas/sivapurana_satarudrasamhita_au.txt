śaunaka uvāca |

vyāsaśiṣya mahābhāga sūta jñānadayānidhe|
vada śambhvavatārāṃśca yairakārṣīt satāṃ śivam|| 1 ||

sūta uvāca |

mune śaunaka sadbhaktyā dattacitto jitendriyaḥ|
avatārāñchivasyāhaṃ vacmi te munaye śṛṇu|| 2 ||

etat pṛṣṭaḥ purā nandī śivamūrtiḥ satāṃ gatiḥ|
sanatkumāreṇa mune tam uvāca śivaṃ smaran|| 3 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

asaṃkhyātā hi kalpeṣu vibhoḥ sarveśvarasya vai|
avatārāstathāpīha vacmyahaṃ tān yathāmati|| 4 ||

ekonaviṃśakaḥ kalpo vijñeyaḥ śvetalohitaḥ|
sadyojātāvatārastu prathamaḥ parikīrtitaḥ|| 5 ||

tasmiṃstat paramaṃ brahma dhyāyato brahmaṇastathā|
utpannastu śikhāyuktaḥ kumāraḥ śvetalohitaḥ|| 6 ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā puruṣaṃ brahmā brahmarūpiṇam īśvaram|
jñātvā dhyātvā sa hṛdaye vavande prayatāñjaliḥ|| 7 ||

sadyojātaṃ śivaṃ buddhvā jaharṣa bhuvaneśvaraḥ|
muhurmuhuśca sadbuddhyā paraṃ taṃ samacintayat|| 8 ||

tato 'asya dhyāyataḥ śvetāḥ prādurbhūtā yaśasvinaḥ|
kumārāḥ paravijñānaparabrahmasvarūpiṇaḥ|| 9 ||

sunando nandanaścaiva viśvanandopanandanau|
śiṣyāstasya mahātmāno yaistad brahmasamāvṛtam|| 10 ||

sadyojātaśca vai śambhurdadau jñānaṃ ca vedhase|
sargaśaktim api prītyā prasannaḥ parameśvaraḥ|| 11 ||

(iti sadyojātāvatāraḥ 1)

tato viṃśatimaḥ kalpo rakto nāma prakīrtitaḥ|
brahmā yatra mahātejā raktavarṇam adhārayat|| 12 ||

dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya prādurbhūto vidheḥ sutaḥ|
raktamālyāmbaradharo raktākṣo raktabhūṣaṇaḥ|| 13 ||

sa taṃ dṛṣṭvā mahātmānaṃ kumāraṃ dhyānam āśritaḥ|
vāmadevaṃ śivaṃ jñātvā praṇanāma kṛtāñjaliḥ|| 14 ||

tatastasya sutā hi āsaṃścatvāro raktavāsasaḥ|
virajāśca vivāhaśca viśoko viśvabhāvanaḥ|| 15 ||

vāmadevaḥ sa vai śambhurdadau jñānaṃ ca vedhase|
sargaśaktim api prītyā prasannaḥ parameśvaraḥ|| 16 ||

(iti vāmadevāvatāraḥ 2)

ekaviṃśatimaḥ kalpaḥ pītavāsā iti smṛtaḥ|
brahmā yatra mahābhāgaḥ pītavāsā babhūva ha|| 17 ||

dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya vidherjātaḥ kumārakaḥ|
pītavastrādikaprauḍho mahātejā mahābhujaḥ|| 18 ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā dhyānasaṃyuktaṃ jñātvā tatpuruṣaṃ śivam|
praṇanāma tato buddhyā gāyatrīṃ śāṃkarīṃ vidhiḥ|| 19 ||

japitvā tu mahādevīṃ sarvalokanamaskṛtām|
prasannastu mahādevo dhyānayuktena cetasā|| 20 ||

tato 'asya pārśvato divyāḥ prādurbhūtāḥ kumārakāḥ|
pītavastrā hi sakalā yogamārgapravartakāḥ|| 21 ||

(iti tatpuruṣāvatāraḥ 3)

tatastasmin gate kalpe pītavarṇe svayambhuvaḥ|
punaranyaḥ pravṛttastu kalpo nāmnā śivastu saḥ|| 22 ||

ekārṇave saṃvyatīte divyavarṣasahasrake|
sraṣṭukāmaḥ prajā brahmā cintayāmāsa duḥkhitaḥ|| 23 ||

tato 'apaśyanmahātejāḥ prādurbhūtaṃ kumārakam|
kṛṣṇavarṇaṃ mahāvīryaṃ dīpyamānaṃ svatejasā|| 24 ||

dhṛtakṛṣṇāmbaroṣṇīṣaṃ kṛṣṇayajñopavītinam|
kṛṣṇena maulinā yuktaṃ kṛṣṇasnānānulepanam|| 25 ||

sa taṃ dṛṣṭvā mahātmānam aghoraṃ ghoravikramam|
vavande devadeveśam adbhutaṃ kṛṣṇapiṅgalam|| 26 ||

aghoraṃ tu tato brahmā brahmarūpaṃ vyacintayat|
tuṣṭāva vāgbhiriṣṭābhirbhaktavatsalam avyayam|| 27 ||

athāsya pārśvatas kṛṣṇāḥ kṛṣṇasnānānulepanāḥ|
catvārastu mahātmānaḥ sambabhūvuḥ kumārakāḥ|| 28 ||

kṛṣṇaḥ kṛṣṇaśikhaścaiva kṛṣṇāsyaḥ kṛṣṇakaṇṭhadhṛk|
iti te vyaktanāmānaḥ śivarūpāḥ sutejasaḥ|| 29 ||

evaṃbhūtā mahātmāno brahmaṇaḥ sṛṣṭihetave|
yogaṃ pravartayāmāsurghorākhyaṃ mahadadbhutam|| 30 ||

(iti+aghorāvatāraścaturthaḥ 4)

athānyo brahmaṇaḥ kalpaḥ prāvartata munīśvarāḥ|
viśvarūpa iti khyāto nāmataḥ paramādbhutaḥ|| 31 ||

brahmaṇaḥ putrakāmasya dhyāyato manasā śivam|
prādurbhūtā mahānādā viśvarūpā sarasvatī|| 32 ||

tathāvidhaḥ sa bhagavān īśānaḥ parameśvaraḥ|
śuddhasphaṭikasaṃkāśaḥ sarvābharaṇabhūṣitaḥ|| 33 ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā praṇanāmāsau brahmeśānam ajaṃ vibhum|
sarvagaṃ sarvadaṃ sarvaṃ surūpaṃ rūpavarjitam|| 34 ||

īśāno 'api tathādiśya sanmārgaṃ brahmaṇe vibhuḥ|
saśaktiḥ kalpayāṃcakre sa bālāṃścaturaḥ śubhān|| 35 ||

jaṭī muṇḍī śikhaṇḍī ca arddhamuṇḍaśca jajñire|
yogenādiśya saddharmaṃ kṛtvā yogagatiṃ gatāsḥ|| 36 ||

(iti+īśānāvatāras+pañcamaḥ 5)

evaṃ saṃkṣepataḥ proktaḥ sadyādīnāṃ samudbhavas|
sanatkumāra sarvajña lokānāṃ hitakāmyayā|| 37 ||

atha teṣāṃ mahāprājña vyavahāraṃ yathāyatham|
trilokahitakāraṃ hi sarvaṃ brahmāṇḍasaṃsthitam|| 38 ||

īśānaḥ puruṣo 'aghoro vāmasaṃjñastathaiva ca|
brahmasaṃjño maheśasya mūrtayaḥ pañca viśrutāḥ|| 39 ||

īśānaḥ śivarūpaśca garīyān prathamaḥ smṛtaḥ|
bhoktāraṃ prakṛteḥ sākṣāt k.etrajñam adhitiṣṭhati|| 40 ||

śaivastatpuruṣākhyaśca svarūpo hi dvitīyakaḥ|
guṇāśrayātmakaṃ bhogyaṃ sarvajñam adhitiṣṭhati|| 41 ||

sharmāya svāṅgasaṃyuktaṃ buddhitattvaṃ pinākinaḥ|
aghorākhyasvarūpo yastiṣṭhatyantastṛtīyakaḥ|| 42 ||

vāmadevāhvayo rūpaścaturthaḥ śaṃkarasya hi|
ahaṃkṛteradhiṣṭhāno bahukāryakaraḥ sadā|| 43 ||

īśānāhvasvarūpo hi śaṃkarasyeśvaraḥ sadā|
śrotrasya vacasaścāpi vibhorvyomnastathaiva ca|| 44 ||

tvakpāṇisparśavāyūnām īśvaraṃ rūpam aiśvaram|
puruṣākhyaṃ vicārajñā matimantaḥ pracakṣate|| 45 ||

vapuṣaśca rasasyāpi rūpasyāgnestathaiva ca|
aghorākhyam adhiṣṭhānaṃ rūpam āhurmanīṣiṇas|| 46 ||

raśanāyāśca pāyośca rasasyāpāṃ tathaiva ca|
īśvaraṃ vāmadevākhyaṃ svarūpaṃ śāṃkaraṃ smṛtam|| 47 ||

ghrāṇasya caivopasthasya gandhasya ca bhuvastathā|
sadyojātāhvayaṃ rūpam īśvaraṃ śāṃkaraṃ viduḥ|| 48 ||

ime svarūpāḥ śambhorhi vandanīyāḥ prayatnataḥ|
śreyorthibhirnarairnityaṃ śreyasām ekahetavaḥ|| 49 ||

yaḥ paṭhecchṛṇuyādvāpi sadyādīnāṃ samudbhavam|
sa bhuktvā sakalān kāmān prayāti paramāṃ gatim|| 50 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ śivasya pañcabrahmāvatāravarṇanaṃ nāma prathamo 'adhyāyas|| 1 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

śṛṇu tāta maheśasyāvatārān paramān prabho|
sarvakāryakarā/lloke sarvasya sukhadān mune|| 1 ||

tasya śambhoḥ pareśasya mūrtyaṣṭakam ayaṃ jagat|
tasmin vyāpya sthitaṃ viśvaṃ sūtre maṇigaṇā iva|| 2 ||

śarvo bhavastathā rudra ugro bhīmaḥ paśoḥ patiḥ|
īśānaśca mahādevo mūrtayaścāṣṭa viśrutāḥ|| 3 ||

bhūmyambhognimarudvyomakṣetrajñārkaniśākarāḥ|
adhiṣṭhitāśca śarvādyairaṣṭarūpaiḥ śivasya hi|| 4 ||

dhatte carācaraṃ viśvaṃ rūpaṃ viśvaṃbharātmakam|
śaṃkarasya maheśasya śāstrasyaiveti niścayaḥ|| 5 ||

saṃjīvanaṃ samastasya jagataḥ salilātmakam|
bhava ityucyate rūpaṃ bhavasya paramātmanaḥ|| 6 ||

bahirantarjagadviśvaṃ bibharti spandate svayam|
ugra ityucyate sadbhii rūpam ugrasya sat prabho|| 7 ||

sarvāvakāśadaṃ sarvavyāpakaṃ gaganātmakam|
rūpaṃ bhīmasya bhīmākhyaṃ bhūpavṛndasya bhedakam|| 8 ||

sarvātmanām adhiṣṭhānaṃ sarvakkṣetranivāsakam|
rūpaṃ paśupaterjñeyaṃ paśupāśanikṛntanam|| 9 ||

sandīpayajjagat sarvaṃ divākarasamāhvayam|
īśānākhyaṃ maheśasya rūpaṃ divi visarpati|| 10 ||

āpyāyayati yo viśvam amṛtāṃśurniśākaraḥ|
mahādevasya tad rūpaṃ mahādevasya cāhvayaṃ || 11 ||

ātmā tasyāṣṭamaṃ rūpaṃ śivasya paramātmanaḥ|
vyāpiketaramūrtīnāṃ viśvaṃ tasmācchivātmakam|| 12 ||

śākhāḥ puṣpyanti vṛkṣasya vṛkṣamūlasya secanāt|
tadvad asya vapurviśvaṃ puṣpyate ca śivārcanāt|| 13 ||

yatheha putrapautrādeḥ prītyā prīto bhavet pitā|
tathā viśvasya samprītyā prīto bhavati śaṃkaraḥ|| 14 ||

kriyate yasya kasyāpi dehino yadi nigrahaḥ|
aṣṭamūrteraniṣṭaṃ tat kṛtam eva na saṃśayaḥ|| 15 ||

aṣṭamūrtyātmanā viśvam adhiṣṭhāyāsthitaṃ śivam|
bhajasva sarvabhāvena rudraṃ paramakāraṇam|| 16 ||

iti proktāḥ svarūpāste vidhiputrāṣṭaviśrutāḥ|
sarvopakāraniratāḥ sevyāḥ śreyorthibhirnaraiḥ|| 17 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ śivāṣṭamūrtuvarṇanaṃ nāma dvitīyo 'adhyāyas|| 2 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

śṛṇu tāta mahāprājña vidhikāmaprapūrakam|
arddhanārīnarākhyaṃ hi śivarūpam anuttamam|| 1 ||

yadā sṛṣṭāḥ prajāḥ sarvā na vyavarddhanta vedhasā|
tadā cintākulo 'abhūt sa tena duḥkhena duḥkhitaḥ|| 2 ||

nabhovāṇī tadābhūdvai sṛṣṭiṃ mithunajāṃ kuru|
tacchrutvā maithunīṃ sṛṣṭiṃ brahmā kartum amanyata|| 3 ||

nārīṇāṃ kulam īśānānnirgataṃ na purā yataḥ|
tato maithunajāṃ sṛṣṭiṃ karuṃ śeke na padmabhūḥ|| 4 ||

prabhāveṇa vinā śambhorna jāyerannimāḥ prajāḥ|
evaṃ saṃcintayan brahmā tapaḥ kartuṃ pracakrame|| 5 ||

śivayā parayā śaktyā saṃyuktaṃ parameśvaram|
saṃcintya hṛdaye prītyā tepe sa paramaṃ tapaḥ|| 6 ||

tīvreṇa tapasā tasya saṃyuktasya svayambhuvaḥ|
acireṇaiva kālena tutoṣa sa śivo drutam|| 7 ||

tataḥ pūrṇacidīśasya mūrtim āviśya kāmadām|
arddhanārīnaro bhūtvā tato brahmāntikaṃ haraḥ|| 8 ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā śaṃkaraṃ devaṃ śaktyā paramayānvitaṃ |
praṇamya daṇḍavadbrahmā sa tuṣṭāva kṛtāñjaliḥ|| 9 ||

atha devo mahādevo vācā meghagabhīrayā|
sambhavāya susamprīto viśvakartā maheśvaraḥ|| 10 ||

īśvara uvāca |

vaysa vatsa mahābhāga mama putra pitāmaha|
jñātavānasmi sarvaṃ tat tattvataste manoratham|| 11 ||

prajānām eva vṛddhyarthaṃ tapastaptaṃ tvayādhunā|
tapasā tena tuṣṭo 'asmi dadāmi ca tavepsitam|| 12 ||

ityuktvā paramodāraṃ svabhāvamadhuraṃ vacaḥ|
pṛthak cakāra vapuṣo bhāgāddevīṃ śivāṃ śivaḥ|| 13 ||

tāṃ dṛṣṭvā paramāṃ śaktiṃ pṛthagbhūtāṃ śivāgatām|
praṇipatya vinītātmā prārthayāmāsa tāṃ vidhiḥ|| 14 ||

brahmovāca |

devadevena sṛṣṭo 'aham ādau tvatpatinā śive|
prajāḥ sarvā niyuktāśca śambhunā paramātmanā|| 15 ||

manasā nirmitāḥ sarve śive devādayo mayā|
na vṛddhim upagacchanti sṛjyamānāḥ punaḥ punaḥ|| 16 ||

mithunaprabhavām eva kṛtvā sṛṣṭimataḥ param|
saṃvarddhayitum icchāmi sarvā eva mama prajāḥ|| 17 ||

na nirgataṃ purā tvatto nārīṇāṃ kulam avyayam|
tena nārīkulaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ mama śaktirna vidyate|| 18 ||

sarvāsām eva śaktīnāṃ tvattaḥ khalu samudbhavaḥ|
tasmāt tvāṃ paramāṃ śaktiṃ prārthayāmyakhileśvarīm|| 19 ||

śive nārīkulaṃ sraṣṭuṃ śaktiṃ dehi namo 'astu te|
carācaraṃ jagadviddhi hetormātaḥ śivaṃ priye|| 20 ||

anyaṃ tvattaḥ prārthayāmi varaṃ ca varadeśvari|
dehi me taṃ kṛpāṃ kṛtvā jaganmāto namo 'astu te|| 21 ||

carācaravivṛddhyartham īśenaikena sarvage|
dakṣasya mama putrasya putrī bhava bhavāmbike|| 22 ||

evaṃ saṃyācitā devī brahmaṇā parameśvarī|
tathāstviti vacaḥ procya tacchaktiṃ vidhaye dadau|| 23 ||

tasmāddhi sā śivā devī śivaśaktirjaganmayī|
śaktim ekāṃ bhruvormadhyāt sasarjātmasamaprabhām|| 24 ||

tām āha prahasan prekṣya śaktiṃ devavaro haraḥ|
kṛpāsindhurmaheśāno līlākārī bhavāmbikām|| 25 ||

śiva uvāca |

tapasārādhito devi brahmaṇā parameṣṭhinā|
prasannā bhava suprītyā kuru tasyākhilepsitam|| 26 ||

tām ājñāṃ parameśasya śirasā pratigṛhya sā|
brahmaṇo vacanāddevī dakṣasya duhitābhavat|| 27 ||

dattvaivam atulāṃ śaktiṃ brahmaṇe sā śivā mune|
viveśa dehaṃ śambhorhi śambhuścāntardadhe prabhuḥ|| 28 ||

tadāprabhṛti loke 'asmin striyā bhāgaḥ prakalpitaḥ|

etat te kathitaṃ tāta śivarūpaṃ mahottamam|

na dṛṣṭam eva cāpaśyaṃ hi āyurvarṣādataḥ param|| 53 ||

viprayorityuktavatoḥ śilādaḥ putravatsalaḥ|
tam āliṅgya ca duḥkhārtto rurodātīva visvaram|| 54 ||

mṛtavat patitaṃ dṛṣṭvā pitaraṃ ca pitāmaham|
pratyavocat prasannātmā smṛtvā śivapadāmbujam|| 55 ||

kena tvaṃ tāta duḥkhena vepamānaśca rodiṣi|
duḥkhaṃ te kuta utpannaṃ jñātum icchāmi tattvataḥ|| 56 ||

pitovāca |

tavālpamṛtyuduḥkhena duḥkhito 'atīva putraka|
ko me duḥkhaṃ haratu vai śaraṇaṃ taṃ prayāmi hi|| 57 ||

putra uvāca |

devo vā dānavo vāpi yamaḥ kālo 'atha vāpi hi|
ṛdhyeyuryadyapi hyete mām anye 'api janāstathā|| 58 ||

athāpi cālpamṛtyurme na bhaviṣyati mā tudaḥ|
satyaṃ bravīmi janakaṃ śapathante karomyaham|| 59 ||

pitovāca |

kiṃ tapaḥ kiṃ parijñānaṃ ko yogaśca prabhuśca te|
yena tvaṃ dāruṇaṃ duḥkhaṃ vañcayiṣyasi putra me|| 60 ||

putra uvāca |

na tāta tapasā mṛtyuṃ vañcayiṣye na vidyayā|
mahādevasya bhajanānmṛtyuṃ jeṣyāmi nānyathā|| 61 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

ityuktvāhaṃ pituḥ pādau praṇamya śirasā mune|
pradakṣiṇīkṛtya ca tam agacchaṃ vanam uttamam|| 62 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ nandikeśāvatāravarṇanaṃ nāma ṣaṣṭho 'adhyāyas|| 6 ||

nandikeśvara uvāca |

tatra gatvā mune 'ahaṃ vai sthitvaikāntasthale sudhīḥ|
atapaṃ tapa ugraṃ sanmunīnām api duṣkaram|| 1 ||

hṛtpuṇḍarīkasuṣire dhyātvā devaṃ triyambakam|
tryakṣaṃ daśabhujaṃ śāntaṃ pañcavaktraṃ sadā śivam|| 2 ||

rudrajāpyam akārṣaṃ vai paramadhyānam āsthitaḥ|
saritaścottare puṇye hyekacittaḥ samāhitaḥ|| 3 ||

tasmiñjāpye 'atha samprītaḥ sthitaṃ māṃ parameśvaraḥ|
tuṣṭo 'abravīnmahādevaḥ somaḥ somārddhabhūṣaṇaḥ|| 4 ||

śiva uvāca |

śailāde varado 'ahaṃ te tapasānena toṣitaḥ|
sādhu taptaṃ tvayā dhīman brūhi yat te manogatam|| 5 ||

sa evam ukto devena śirasā pādayornataḥ|
astavaṃ parameśānaṃ jarāśokavināśanam|| 6 ||

atha māṃ nandinaṃ śambhurbhaktyā paramayā yutam|
aśrupūrṇekṣaṇaṃ samyak pādayoḥ śirasā natam|| 7 ||

utthāpya parameśānaḥ pasparśa paramārtihā|
karābhyāṃ saṃmukhābhyāṃ tu saṃgṛhya vṛṣabhadhvajaḥ|| 8 ||

nirīkṣya gaṇapāṃścaiva devīṃ himavataḥ sutām|
uvāca māṃ kṛpādṛṣṭyā samīkṣya jagatāṃ patiḥ|| 9 ||

vatsa nandin mahāprājña mṛtyorbhītiḥ kutastava|
mayaiva preṣitau viprau matsamastvaṃ na saṃśayaḥ|| 10 ||

amaro jarayā tyakto 'aduḥkhī gaṇapatiḥ sadā|
avyayaścākṣayaśceṣṭaḥ sapitā sasuhṛjjanaḥ|| 11 ||

madbalaḥ pārśvago nityaṃ mameṣṭo bhavitāniśam|
na jarā janma mṛtyurvā matprasādādbhaviṣyati|| 12 ||

nanīśvara uvāca |

evam uktvā śiromālāṃ kuśeśayamayīṃ nijām|
samunmucya babandhāśu mama kaṇṭhe kṛpānidhiḥ|| 13 ||

tayāhaṃ mālayā vipra śubhayā kaṇṭhasaktayā|
tryakṣo daśabhujaścāsaṃ dvitīya iva śaṃkaraḥ|| 14 ||

tata eva samādāya hastena parameśvaraḥ|
uvāca brūhi kiṃ te 'adya dadāmi varam uttamam|| 15 ||

tato jaṭāśritaṃ vāri gṛhītvā hāranirmalam|
uktvā nandī bhavetīha visasarja vṛṣadhvajaḥ|| 16 ||

tataḥ pañcamitā nadyaḥ prāvartanta śubhāvahāḥ|
sutoyāśca mahāvegā divyarūpā ca sundarī| 17||

jaṭodakā trisrotāśca vṛṣadhvaniritīva hi|
svarṇodakā jambunadī pañcanadyaḥ prakīrtitāḥ|| 18 ||

etat pañcanadaṃ nāma śivapṛṣṭhatamaṃ śubham|
japeśvarasamīpe tu pavitraṃ paramaṃ mune|| 19 ||

yaḥ pañcanadam āsādya snātvā japteśvareśvaram|
pūjayecchivasāyujyaṃ prayātyeva na saṃśayaḥ|| 20 ||

atha śambhuruvācomām abhiṣiñcāmi nandinam|
gaṇendraṃ vyāhariṣyāmi kiṃ vā tvaṃ manyase 'avyaye|| 21 ||

umovāca |

dātum arhasi deveśa nandine parameśvara|
mahāpriyatamo nātha śailādistanayo mama|| 22 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

tataḥ sa śaṃkaraḥ svīyān sasmāra gaṇapān varān|
svatantraḥ parameśānaḥ sarvado bhaktavatsalaḥ|| 23 ||

smaraṇādeva rudrasya samprāptāśca gaṇeśvarāḥ|
asaṅkhyātā mahāmodāḥ śaṃkarākṛtayo 'akhilāḥ|| 24 ||

te gaṇeśāḥ śivaṃ devīṃ praṇamyāhuḥ śubhaṃ vacaḥ|
te praṇamya karau baddhvā nataskandhā mahābalāḥ|| 25 ||

gaṇeśā ūcuḥ |

kimarthaṃ ca smṛtā deva hy ājñāpaya mahāprabho|
kiṃkarān naḥ samāyātāṃstripurārdana kāmada|| 26 ||

kiṃ sāgarāñchoṣayāmo yamaṃ vā saha kiṃkarais|
hanmo mṛtyuṃ mahāmṛtyuṃ viśeṣaṃ vṛddhapadmajam|| 27 ||

baddhvendraṃ saha devaiśca viṣṇuṃ vā pārṣadaiḥ saha|
ānayāmaḥ susaṃkruddhān daityān vā dānavaiḥ saha|| 28 ||

kasyādya vyasanaṃ ghoraṃ kariṣyāmastavājñayā|
kasya vādyotsavo deva sarvakāmasamṛddhaye|| 29 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

iti ākarṇya vacasteṣāṃ gaṇānāṃ vīravādinām|
uvāca tān sa praśaṃsya gaṇeśān parameśvaraḥ|| 30 ||

śiva uvāca |

nandīśvaro 'ayaṃ putro me sarveṣām īśvareśvaraḥ|
priyo gaṇāgraṇīḥ sarvaiḥ kriyatāṃ vacanaṃ mama|| 31 ||

sarve prītyābhiṣiñcadhvaṃ madgaṇānāṃ gatimpatim|
adyaprabhṛti yuṣmākam ayaṃ nandīśvaraḥ prabhuḥ|| 32 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

evam uktāḥ śaṃkareṇa gaṇapāḥ sarva eva te|
evam astviti samprocya sambhārān āharaṃstataḥ|| 33 ||

tato devāśca sendrāśca nārāyaṇamukhāstathā|
munayaḥ sarvato lokā ājagmurmuditānanāḥ|| 34 ||

pitāmaho 'api bhagavanniyogācchaṃkarasya vai|
cakāra nandinaḥ sarvam abhiṣekaṃ samāhitaḥ|| 35 ||

tato viṣṇustataḥ śakro lokapālāstathaiva ca|
ṛṣayastuṣṭuvuścaiva pitāmahapurogamāḥ|| 36 ||

stutimatsu tatassteṣu viṣṇuḥ sarvajagatpatiḥ|
śirasyañjalim ādhāya tuṣṭāva ca samāhitaḥ|| 37 ||

prāñjaliḥ praṇato bhūtvā jayaśabdaṃ cakāra ca|
tato gaṇādhipāḥ sarve tato devāstato 'asurāḥ|| 38 ||

evaṃ stutaścābhiṣikto devaiḥ sabrahmakaistadā|
nandīśvaro 'ahaṃ viprendra niyogāt parameśituḥ|| 39 ||

udvāhaśca kṛtastatra niyogāt parameṣṭhinaḥ|
mahotsavayutaḥ prītyā viṣṇubrahmādibhirmama|| 40 ||

marutāṃ ca sutā devī suyaśāstu manoharā|
patnī sā me 'abhavaddivyā manonayananandinī|| 41 ||

labdhaṃ śaśiprabhaṃ chatraṃ tayā tatra vibhūṣitam|
camāraiścāmarāsaktahastāgraiḥ strīgaṇairyutam|| 42 ||

saṃhāsanaṃ ca paramaṃ tayā cādhiṣṭhitaṃ mayā|
alaṃkṛto mahālakṣmyā mukuṭādyaiḥ subhūṣaṇaiḥ|| 43 ||

labdho hāraśca paramo devyāḥ kaṇṭhagatastathā|
vṛṣendraśca śito nāgaḥ siṃhaḥ siṃhadhvajastathā|| 44 ||

rathaśca hemahāraśca candrabimbasamaḥ śubhaḥ|
anyānyapi ca vastūni labdhāni hi mayā mune|| 45 ||

evaṃ kṛtavivāho 'ahaṃ tayā patnyā mahāmune|
pādau vavande śambhośca śivāyā brahmaṇo hareḥ|| 46 ||

tathāvidhaṃ trilokeśaḥ sapatnīkaṃ ca māṃ prabhuḥ|
provāca parayā prītyā sa śivo bhaktavatsalaḥ|| 47 ||

īśvara uvāca |

śṛṇu satputra tātastvaṃ suyaśeyaṃ tava priyā|
dadāmi te varaṃ prītyā yat te manasi vāñchitam|| 48 ||

sadāhaṃ tava nandīśa saṃtuṣṭo 'asmi gaṇeśvara|
devyā ca sahito vatsa śṛṇu me paramaṃ vacaḥ|| 49 ||

sadeṣṭaśca viśiṣṭaśca paramaiśvaryasaṃyutaḥ|
mahāyogī maheṣvāsaḥ sapitā sapitāmahaḥ|| 50 ||

ajeyaḥ sarvajetā ca sadā pūjyo mahābalaḥ|
ahaṃ yatra bhavāṃstatra yatra tvaṃ tatra cāpyaham|| 51 ||

ahaṃ ca te pitā putra paramaiśvaryasaṃyutaḥ|
bhaviṣyati gaṇādhyakṣo mama bhakto mahābalaḥ|| 52 ||

pitāmaho 'api te vatsa tathāstu niyamā ime|
matsamīpaṃ gamiṣyanti mayā dattavarāstathā|| 53 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

tato devī mahābhāgā nandinaṃ varadābravīt|
varaṃ brūhīti māṃ putra sarvān kāmān yathepsitān|| 54 ||

tacchrutvā vacanaṃ devyāḥ prāvocat sāñjalistadā|
bhaktirbhavatu me devi pādayoste sadā varā|| 55 ||

śrutvā mama vaco devī hyevam astviti sābravīt|
suyaśāṃ tāṃ ca suprītyā nandipriyatamāṃ śivām|| 56 ||

devyuvāca |

vatse varaṃ yatheṣṭaṃ hi trinetrā janmavarjitā|
putrapautraistu bhaktirme tathā ca bhartureva hi|| 57 ||

nandyuvāca |

tadā brahmā ca viṣṇuśca sarve devagaṇāśca vai|
tābhyāṃ varān daduḥ prītyā suprasannāḥ śivājñayā|| 58 ||

sānvayaṃ māṃ gṛhītveśastataḥ sambandhibāndhavaiḥ|
āruhya vṛṣam īśāno gato devyā nijaṃ gṛham|| 59 ||

viṣṇvādayaḥ surāḥ sarve praśaṃsanto hyamī tadā|
svadhāmāni yayuḥ prītyā saṃstuvantaḥ śivaṃ śivam|| 60 ||

iti te kathito vatsa svāvatāro mahāmune|
sadānandakaraḥ puṃsāṃ śivabhaktipravarddhanaḥ|| 61 ||

ya idaṃ nandino janma varadānaṃ tathā mama|
abhiṣekaṃ vivāhaṃ ca śṛṇuyācchrāvayet tathā|| 62 ||

paṭhedvā pāṭhayedvāpi śraddhāvān bhaktisaṃyutaḥ|
iha sarvasukhaṃ bhuktvā paratra labhate gatim|| 63 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ nandikeśvarāvatārābhiṣekavivāhavarṇanaṃ nāma saptamo 'adhyāyaḥ|| 7 ||

iti tricatvāriṃśo 'avatāraḥ|| 43 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

sanatkumāraḥ sarvajña śṛṇu tvaṃ bhairavīṃ kathām|
yasyāḥ śravaṇamatreṇa śaivī bhaktirdṛḍhā bhavet|| 1 ||

bhairavaḥ pūrṇarūpo hi śaṃkarasya parātmanaḥ|
mūḍhāstaṃ vai na jānanti mohitāḥ śivamāyayā|| 2 ||

sanatkumāra no vetti mahimānaṃ maheśituḥ|
caturbhujo 'api viṣṇurvai caturvaktro 'api vai vidhiḥ|| 3 ||

citram atra na kiṃcidvai durjñeyā khalu śāmbhavī|
tayā saṃmohitāḥ sarve nārcayantyapi tatparam|| 4 ||

veda cedyadi vātmānaṃ sa eva parameśvaraḥ|
tadā vihanti te sarve svecchayā na hi ke 'api tam|| 5 ||

sarvago 'api maheśāno nekṣyate mūḍhabuddhibhiḥ|
devavadbudhyate loke yaḥ .atīto manasāṃ girām|| 6 ||

atretihāsaṃ vakṣye 'ahaṃ paramarṣe purātanam|
śṛṇu taṃ śraddhayā tāta paramaṃ jñānakāraṇam|| 7 ||

meruśṛṅge 'adbhute ramye sthitaṃ brahmāṇam īśvaram|
jagmurdevarṣayaḥ sarve sutattvaṃ jñātum icchayā|| 8 ||

tatrāgatya vidhiṃ natvā papracchuste mahādarāt|
kṛtāñjalipuṭāḥ sarve nataskandhā munīśvarāḥ|| 9 ||

devarṣaya ūcuḥ |

devadeva prajānātha sṛṣṭikṛllokanāyaka|
tattvato vada cāsmabhyaṃ kim ekaṃ tattvam avyayam|| 10 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

sa māyayā maheśasya mohitaḥ padmasambhavaḥ|
avijñāya paraṃ bhāvaṃ sambhāvaṃ pratyuvāca ha|| 11 ||

brahmovāca |

he surā ṛṣayaḥ sarve sumatyā śṛṇutādarāt|
vacmyahaṃ paramaṃ tattvam avyayaṃ vai yathārthataḥ|| 12 ||

jagadyonirahaṃ dhātā svayambhūraja īśvaraḥ|
anādibhāg ahaṃ brahma hyeka ātmā nirañjanaḥ|| 13 ||

pravartako hi jagatām aham eva nivartakaḥ|
saṃvartako madadhiko nānyaḥ kaścit surottamāḥ|| 14 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

tasyaivaṃ vadato dhāturviṣṇustatra sthito mune|
provāca prahasan vākyaṃ saṃkriddho mohito 'ajayā|| 15 ||

na caitaducito brahman yogayuktasya mūrkhatā|
avijñāya paraṃ tattvaṃ vṛthaitat te nigadyate|| 16 ||

kartā vai sarvalokānāṃ paramātmā paraḥ pumān|
yajño nārāyaṇo devo māyādhīśaḥ parā gatiḥ|| 17 ||

mamājñayā tvayā brahman sṛṣṭireṣā vidhīyate|
jagatāṃ jīvanaṃ naiva mām anādṛtya ceśvaram|| 18 ||

evaṃ triprakṛtau mohāt parasparajayaiṣinau|
procaturnigamāṃścātra pramāṇe sarvathā tanau|| 19 ||

praṣṭavyāste viśeṣeṇa sthitā mūrtidharāśca te|
papracchatuḥ pramāṇajñānityuktvā caturo 'api tān|| 20 ||

vidhiviṣṇū ūcatuḥ |

vedāḥ pramāṇaṃ sarvatra pratiṣṭhā paramāmitāḥ|
yūyaṃ vadata viśrabdhaṃ kim ekaṃ tattvam avyayam|| 21 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

iti ākarṇya tayorvācaṃ punaste hi ṛgādayaḥ|
avadaṃstattvataḥ sarve saṃsmarantaḥ paraṃ prabhum|| 22 ||

yadi mānyā vayaṃ devau sṛṣṭisthitakarau vibhū|

nandīśvara uvāca |

śrutyuktavidhim ākarṇya procatustau surau śrutīḥ|

ṛgveda uvāca |

yajurveda uvāca |

sāmaveda uvāca |

atharvaveda uvāca |

nandīśvara uvāca |

śrutyuktam idam ākarṇyātīvamāyāvimohitau|

vidhiharī ucatuḥ |

he vedāḥ kim idaṃ yūyaṃ bhāṣante gatacetanāḥ|
kiṃ jātaṃ vo 'adya sarvaṃ hi naṣṭaṃ suvayunaṃ param|| 30 ||

kathaṃ pramathanātho 'asau ramamāṇo nirantaram|

praṇava uvāca |

nandīśvara uvāca |

prādurāsīt tatas jyotirubhayorantare mahat|

brahmovāca |

nandīśvara uvāca |

īśvara uvāca |

viśvaṃ bhartuṃ samartho 'asi bhīṣaṇādbhairavaḥ smṛtaḥ|

āmardayiṣyati bhavān ruṣṭo duṣṭātmano yataḥ|

yataḥ pāpāni bhaktānāṃ bhakṣayiṣyasi tatkṣaṇāt|

nandīśvara uvāca |

viśvānara uvāca |

nandīśvara uvāca |

devakāryārtham utpannāḥ śivarūpāḥ sukhāspadam|| 27 ||

te rudrāḥ kāśyapā vīrā mahābalaparākramāḥ|
daityāñjaghnuśca saṃgrāme devasāhāyyakāriṇaḥ|| 28 ||

tadrudrakṛpayā devā daityāñjitvā ca nirbhayāḥ|
cakruḥ svarājyaṃ sarve te śakrādyāḥ svasthamānasāḥ|| 29 ||

adyāpi te mahārudrāḥ sarve śivasvarūpakāḥ|
devānāṃ rakṣaṇārthāya virājante sadā divi|| 30 ||

aiśānyāṃ puri te vāsaṃ cakrire bhaktavatsalāḥ|
viramante sadā tatra nānālīlāviśāradāḥ|| 31 ||

teṣām anucarā rudrāḥ koṭiśaḥ parikīrtitāḥ|
sarvatra saṃsthitāstatra trilokeṣvabhibhāgaśaḥ|| 32 ||

iti te varṇitāstātāvatārāḥ śaṃkarasya vai|
ekādaśamitā rudrāḥ sarvalokasukhāvahāḥ|| 33 ||

idam ākhyānam amalaṃ sarvapāpapraṇāśakam|
dhanyaṃ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṃ sarvakāmapradāyakam|| 34 ||

ya idaṃ śṛṇuyāt tāta śrāvayedvai samāhitaḥ|
iha sarvasukhaṃ bhuktvā tato muktiṃ labheta saḥ|| 35 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyām ekādaśāvatāravarṇanaṃ nāmāṣṭādaśo 'adhyāyaḥ|| 18 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

athānyaccaritaṃ śambhoḥ śṛṇu prītyā mahāmune|
yathā babhūva durvāsāḥ :saṃkaro dharmahetave|| 1 ||

brahmaputro babhūvātitapasvī brahmavit prabhuḥ|
anasūyāpatirdhīmān brahmājñāpratipālakaḥ|| 2 ||

sunirdeśādbrahmaṇo hi sastrīkaḥ putrakāmyayā|
sa tryakṣakulanāmānaṃ yayau ca tapase girim|| 3 ||

prāṇān āyamya vidhivannirvindhyātaṭinītaṭe|
tapaścacāra sumahadadvandvo 'abdaśataṃ muniḥ|| 4 ||

ya eka īśvaraḥ kaścidavikāro mahāprabhuḥ|
sa me putravaraṃ dadyāditi niścitamānasaḥ|| 5 ||

bahukālo vyatīyāya tasmiṃstapati sat tapaḥ|
āvirbabhūva tatkāt tu śucirjvālā mahīyasī|| 6 ||

tayāsannakhilā lokā dagdhaprāyā munīśvarāḥ|
tathā suraṣayaḥ sarve pīḍitā vāsavādayaḥ|| 7 ||

atha sarve vāsavādyā surāśca munayo mune|
brahmasthānaṃ yayuḥ śīghraṃ tajjvālātiprapīḍitāḥ|| 8 ||

natvā nutvā vidhiṃ devāstat svaduḥkhaṃ nyavedayan|
brahmā saha suraistāta viṣṇulokaṃ yayāvaram|| 9 ||

tatra gatvā ramānāthaṃ natvā nutvā vidhiḥ suraiḥ|
svaduḥkhaṃ tat samācakhyau viṣṇave 'anantakaṃ mune|| 10 ||

viṣṇuśca vidhinā devai rudrasthānaṃ yayau drutam|
haraṃ praṇamya tatretya tuṣṭāva parameśvaram|| 11 ||

stutvā bahutayā viṣṇuṃ svaduḥkhaṃ ca nyavedayat|
śarvaṃ jvālāsamudbhūtam atreśca tapasaḥ param|| 12 ||

atha tatra sametāstu brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarāḥ|
mune saṃmantrayāṃcakruranyonyaṃ jagatāṃ hitam|| 13 ||

tadā brahmādayo devāstrayaste varadarṣabhāḥ|
jagmustadāśramaṃ śighraṃ varaṃ dātuṃ tadarṣaye|| 14 ||

svacihnacihnitāṃstān sa dṛṣṭvātrirmunisattamaḥ|
praṇanāma ca tuṣṭāva vāgbhiriṣṭābhirādarāt|| 15 ||

tataḥ sa vismito viprastānuvāca kṛtāñjaliḥ|
brahmaputro vinītātmā brahmaviṣṇuharābhidhān|| 16 ||

atriruvāca |

he brahman he hare rudra pūjyāstrijagatāṃ matāḥ|
prabhavaśca "īśvarāḥ sṛṣṭirakṣāsaṃhārakārakāḥ|| 17 ||

eka eva mayā dhyātaḥ "īśvaraḥ putrahetave|
yaḥ kaścit "īśvaraḥ khyāto jagatāṃ svastriyā saha|| 18 ||

yūyaṃ trayaḥ surāḥ kasmādāgatā varadarṣabhāḥ|
etanme saṃśayaṃ chittvā tato dattepsitaṃ varam|| 19 ||

iti śrutvā vacastasya pratyūcuste surāstrayaḥ|
yādṛk kṛtaste saṃkalpastathaivābhūnmunīśvara|| 20 ||

vayaṃ trayo bhaveśānāḥ samānā varadarṣabhāḥ|
asmadaṃśabhavāstasmādbhaviṣyanti sutāstrayaḥ|| 21 ||

viditā bhuvane sarve pitroḥ kīrtivivardhanāḥ|
ityuktāste trayo devāḥ svadhāmāni yayurmudā|| 22 ||

varaṃ labdhvā muniḥ so 'atha jagāma svāśrayaṃ mudā|
yuto 'anusūyayā prītyā brahmānandaprādo mune|| 23 ||

atha brahmā hariḥ śambhuravateruḥ striyāṃ tataḥ|
putrarūpaiḥ prasannātmanānālīlāprakāśakāḥ|| 24 ||

vidheraṃśādvidhurjajñe 'anusūyāyāṃ munīśvarāt|
āvirbabhūvodadhitaḥ kṣipto devaiḥ sa eva hi|| 25 ||

viṣṇoraṃśāt striyāṃ tasyām atrerdatto vyajāyata|
saṃnyāsapaddhatiryena vardhitā paramā mune|| 26 ||

durvāsā muniśārdūlaḥ śivāṃśānmunisattamaḥ|
jajñe tasyāṃ striyām atrervaradharmapravartakaḥ|| 27 ||

bhūtvā rudraśca durvāsā brahmatejovivardhanaḥ|
cakre dharmaparīkṣāṃ ca bahūnāṃ sa dayāparaḥ|| 28 ||

sūryavaṃśe samutpanno yo 'ambarīṣo nṛpo 'abhavat|
tatparīkṣām akārṣīt sa tāṃ śṛṇu tvaṃ munīśvara|| 29 ||

so 'ambarīṣo nṛpavaraḥ saptadvīparasāpatiḥ|
niyamaṃ hi cakārāsāvekādaśyā vrate dṛḍham|| 30 ||

ekādaśyā vrataṃ kṛtvā dvādaśyāṃ caiva pāraṇām|
kariṣyāmīti sudṛḍhasaṃkalpastu narādhipaḥ|| 31 ||

jñātvā tanniyamaṃ tasya durvāsā munisattamaḥ|
tadantikaṃ gataḥ śiṣyairbahubhiḥ śaṃkarāṃśajaḥ|| 32 ||

pāraṇe dvādaśīṃ svalpāṃ jñātvā yāvat sa bhojanam|
kartuṃ vyavasitastāvadāgataṃ sa nyamantrayat|| 33 ||

tataḥ snānārtham agamaddurvāsāḥ śiṣyasaṃyutaḥ|
vilambaṃ kṛtavāṃstatra parīkṣārthaṃ munirbahu|| 34 ||

dharmavighnaṃ tadā jñātvā sa nṛpaḥ śāstraśāsanāt|
jalaṃ prāśyāsthitastatra tadāgamanakāṃkṣayā|| 35 ||

etasminnantare tatra durvāsā munirāgataḥ|
kṛtāśanaṃ nṛpaṃ jñātvā parīkṣārthaṃ dhṛtākṛtiḥ|| 36 ||

cukrodhāti nṛpe tasmin parīkṣārthaṃ vṛṣasya saḥ|
provāca vacanaṃ tūgraṃ sa muniḥ śaṃkarāṃśajaḥ|| 37 ||

durvāsā uvāca |

māṃ nimantrya nṛpābhojya jalaṃ pītaṃ tvayādhama|
darśayāmi phalaṃ tasya duṣṭadaṇḍadharo hyaham|| 38 ||

ityuktvā krodhatāmrākṣo nṛpaṃ dagdhuṃ samudyataḥ|
samuttasthau drutaṃ cakraṃ tatsthaṃ rakṣārtham aiśvaram|| 39 ||

prajajvālāti taṃ cakraṃ muniṃ dagdhuṃ sudarśanam|
śivarūpaṃ tam ajñātvā śivamāyāvimohitam|| 40 ||

etasminnantare tatra vyomavāṇyuvācāśarīriṇī|
ambarīṣaṃ mahātmānaṃ brahmabhaktaṃ ca vaiṣṇavam|| 41 ||

vyomavāṇyuvāca |

sudarśanam idaṃ cakraṃ haraye śambhunārpitam|
śāntaṃ kuru prajvalitam adya durvāsase nṛpa|| 42 ||

durvāsāyaṃ śivaḥ sākṣāt sa cakraṃ haraye 'arpitam|
evaṃ sādhāraṇamuniṃ na jānīhi nṛpottama|| 43 ||

tava dharmaparīkṣārtham āgato 'ayaṃ munīśvaraḥ|
śaraṇaṃ yāhi tasyāśu bhaviṣyatyanyathā layaḥ|| 44 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

ityuktvā ca nabhovāṇī virarāma munīśvara|
astāvīt sa harāṃśaṃ tam ambarīṣo 'api cādarāt|| 45 ||

ambarīṣa uvāca |

yadyasti dattam iṣṭaṃ ca svadharmo vā svanuṣṭhitaḥ|
kulaṃ no vipradaivaṃ harerastraṃ praśāmyatu|| 46 ||

yadi no bhagavān prīto madbhakto bhaktavatsalaḥ|
sudarśanam idaṃ cāstraṃ praśāmyatu viśeṣataḥ|| 47 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

iti stuvati rudrāgre śaivaṃ cakraṃ sudarśanam|
aśāmyat sarvathā jñātvā taṃ śivāṃśaṃ sulabdhadhīḥ|| 48 ||

athāmbarīṣaḥ sa nṛpaḥ praṇanāma ca taṃ munim|
śivāvatāraṃ saṃjñāya svaparīkṣārtham āgatam|| 49 ||

suprasanno babhūvātha sa muniḥ śaṃkarāṃaśajaḥ|
bhuktvā tasmai varaṃ dattvā svābhīṣṭaṃ svālayaṃ yayau|| 50 ||

ambarīṣaparīkṣāyāṃ durvāsaścaritaṃ mune|
proktam anyaccaritaṃ tvaṃ śṛṇu tasya munīśvara|| 51 ||

punardāśaratheścakre parīkṣāṃ niyamena vai|
munirūpeṇa kālena yaḥ kṛto niyamo mune|| 52 ||

tadaiva muninā tena saumitriḥ preṣito haṭhāt|
taṃ tatyāja drutaṃ romo bandhuṃ paṇavaśānmune|| 53 ||

sā kathā vihitā loke munibhirbahudhoditā|
nāto me vistarāt proktā jñātā yat sarvathā budhaiḥ|| 54 ||

niyamaṃ sudṛḍhaṃ dṛṣṭvā suprasanno 'abhavanmuniḥ|
durvāsāḥ suprasannātmā varaṃ tasmai pradattavān|| 55 ||

śrīkṛṣṇaniyamasyāpi parīkṣāṃ sa cakāra ha|
tāṃ śṛṇu tvaṃ muniśreṣṭha kathayāmi kathāṃ ca tām|| 56 ||

brahmaprārthanayā viṣṇurvasudevaḥ suto 'abhavat|
dharābhārāvatārārthaṃ sādhūṇāṃ rakṣaṇāya ca|| 57 ||

hatvā duṣṭān mahāpāpān brahmadrohakarān khalān|
rarakṣa nikhilān sādhūn brāhmaṇān kṛṣṇanāmabhāk|| 58 ||

brahmabhaktiṃ cakārāti sa kṛṣṇo vasudevajaḥ|
nityaṃ hi bhojayāmāsa surasān brāhmaṇān bahūn|| 59 ||

brahmabhakto viśeṣeṇa kṛṣṇaśceti prathām agāt|
saṃdraṣṭukāmaḥ sa muniḥ kṛṣṇāntikam agānmune|| 60 ||

rukmiṇīsahitaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ sannaṃ kṛtvā rathe svayam|
saṃyojya saṃsthito vāhaṃ suprasanna uvāha tam|| 61 ||

munī rathāt samuttīrya dṛṣṭvā tāṃ dṛḍhatāṃ parām|
tasmai bhūtvā suprasanno vajrāṅgatvavaraṃ dadau|| 62 ||

dyunadyām ekadā snānaṃ kurvan nagno babhūva ha|
lajjito 'abhūnmuniśreṣṭho durvāsāḥ kautukī mune|| 63 ||

tajjñātvā draupadī snānaṃ kurvatī tatra cādarāt|
tallajjāṃ chādayāmāsa bhinnasvāñcaladānataḥ|| 64 ||

tadādāya pravāhenāgataṃ svanikaṭaṃ muniḥ|
tenācchādya svaguhyaṃ ca tasyai tuṣṭo babhūva saḥ|| 65 ||

draupadyai ca varaṃ prādāt tadañcalavivardhanam|
pāṇḍavān sukhinaścakre draupadī tadvarāt punaḥ|| 66 ||

haṃsaḍimbhau nṛpau kaucitsvāvamānaukarau khalau|
dattvā nideśaṃ ca harernāśayāmāsa sa prabhuḥ|| 67 ||

brahmatejoviśeṣeṇa sthāpayāmāsa bhūtale|
saṃnyāsapaddhatiṃ caiva yathāśāstravidhikramam|| 68 ||

bahūnuddhārayāmāsa sūpadeśaṃ vibodhya ca|
jñānaṃ dattvā viśeṣeṇa bahūn muktāṃścakāra saḥ|| 69 ||

itthaṃ cakre sa durvāsā vicitraṃ caritaṃ bahu|
dhanyaṃ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṃ śṛṇvataḥ sarvakāmadam|| 70 ||

ya idaṃ śṛṇuyādbhaktyā durvāsaścaritaṃ mudā|
śrāvayedvā parān yaśca sa sukhīha paratra ca|| 71 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ durvāsaścaritravarṇanaṃ nāma ekonaviṃśo 'adhyāyas|| 19 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

ataḥ paraṃ śṛṇu prītyā hanumaccaritaṃ mune|
yathā cakārāśu haro hīlāstadrūpato varāḥ|| 1 ||

cakāra suhitaṃ prītyā rāmasya parameśvaraḥ|
tat sarvaṃ caritaṃ vipra śṛṇu sarvasukhāvaham|| 2 ||

ekasmin samaye śambhuradbhuto 'atikaraḥ prabhuḥ|
dadarśa mohinīrūpaṃ viṣṇoḥ sa hi vasadguṇaḥ|| 3 ||

cakre svaṃ kṣubhitaṃ śambhuḥ kāmabāṇahato yathā|
svaṃ vīryaṃ pātayāmāsa rāmakāryārtham īśvaraḥ|| 4 ||

tadvīryaṃ sthāpayāmāsuḥ patre saptarṣayaśca te|
preritā manasā tena rāmakāryārtham ādarāt|| 5 ||

tairgautamasutāyāṃ tadvīryaṃ śambhormaharṣibhiḥ|
karṇadvārā tathāñjanyāṃ rāmakāryātham āhitam|| 6 ||

tataśca samaye tasmāddhanumāniti nāmabhāk|
śambhurjajñe kapitanurmahābalaparākramaḥ|| 7 ||

hanumān sa kapīśānaḥ śiśureva mahābalaḥ|
ravibimbaṃ babhakṣāśu jñātvā laghuphalaṃ prage|| 8 ||

devaprāthanayā taṃ so 'atyajajjñātvā mahābalam|
śivāvatāraṃ ca prāpa varān dattān suraṣibhiḥ|| 9 ||

svajananyantikaṃ prāgādatha so 'atipraharṣitaḥ|
hanūmān sarvam ācakhyau tasyai tadvṛttam ādarāt|| 10 ||

tadājñayā tato dhīraḥ sarvavidyām ayatnataḥ|
sūryāt papāṭha sa kapirgatvā nityaṃ tadantikam|| 11 ||

sūryājñayā tadaṃśasya sugrīvasyāntikaṃ yayau|
māturājñām anu prāpya rudrāṃśaḥ kapisattamaḥ|| 12 ||

jyeṣṭhabhrātrā vālinā hi svastrībhoktrā tiraskṛtaḥ|
ṛṣyamūkagirau tena nyavasat sa hanūmatā|| 13 ||

tato 'abhūt sa sukaṇṭhasya mantrī kapivaraḥ sudhīḥ|
sarvathā suhitaṃ cakre sugrīvasya harāṃśujaḥ|| 14 ||

tatrāgatena sabhrātrā hṛtabhāryeṇa duḥkhinā|
kārayāmāsa rāmeṇa tasya sakhyaṃ sukhāvaham|| 15 ||

ghātayāmāsa rāmaśca vālinaṃ kapikuñjaram|
bhrātṛpatnyāśca bhoktāraṃ pāpinaṃ vīramāninam|| 16 ||

tato rāmājñayā tāta hanūmān vānareśvaraḥ|
sa sītānveṣaṇaṃ cakre bahubhirvānaraiḥ sudhīḥ|| 17 ||

jñātvā laṅkāgatāṃ sītāṃ gatastatra kapīśvaraḥ|
drutam ullaṅghya sindhuṃ tam anistīryaṃ paraiḥ sa vai|| 18 ||

cakre 'adbhutacaritaṃ sa tatra vikramasaṃyutam|
abhijñānaṃ dadau prītyā sītāyai svaprabhorvaram|| 19 ||

sītāśokaṃ jahārāśu sa vīraḥ kapināyakaḥ|
śrāvayitvā rāmavṛttaṃ tatprāṇāvanakārakam|| 20 ||

tadabhijñānam ādāya nivṛtto rāmasannidhim|
rāvaṇārāmam āhatya jaghāna bahurākṣasān|| 21 ||

tadaiva rāvaṇasutaṃ hatvā sabahurākṣasam|
sa mahopadravaṃ cakre mahotistatra nirbhayaḥ|| 22 ||

yadā dagdho rāvaṇenāvaguṇṭhya vasanāni ca|
tailābhyaktāni sudṛḍhaṃ mahābalavatā mune|| 23 ||

utplutyotplutya ca tadā mahādevāṃśajaḥ kapiḥ|
dadāha laṅkāṃ nikhilāṃ kṛtvā vyājaṃ tam eva hi|| 24 ||

dagdhvā laṅkāṃ vañcayitvā bibhīṣaṇagṛhaṃ tataḥ|
apatadvāridhau vīrastataḥ sa kapikuñjaraḥ|| 25 ||

svapucchaṃ tatra nirvāpya prāpa tasya paraṃ taṭam|
akhinnaḥ sa yayau rāmasannidhiṃ giriśāṃśajaḥ|| 26 ||

avilambena sujavo hanūmān kapisattamaḥ|
rāmopakaṇṭham āgatya dadau sītāśiromaṇim|| 27 ||

tatastadājñayā vīraḥ sindhau setum abandhayat|
vānaraiḥ sa samānīya bahūn girivarān balī|| 28 ||

gatvā tatra tato rāmastartukāmo yathā tataḥ|
śivaliṅgaṃ samānarca pratiṣṭhāpya jayepsayā|| 29 ||

tadvarāt sa jayaṃ prāpya varaṃ tīrtvodadhiṃ tataḥ|
laṅkām āvṛtya kapibhii raṇaṃ cakre sa rākṣasaiḥ|| 30 ||

jaghānāthāsurān vīro rāmasainyaṃ rarakṣa saḥ|
śaktikṣataṃ lakṣmaṇaṃ saṃjīvinyā hyajīvayat|| 31 ||

sarvathā sukhinaṃ cakre sarāmaṃ lakṣmaṇaṃ hi saḥ|
sarvasainyaṃ rarakṣāsau mahādevātmajaḥ prabhuḥ|| 32 ||

rāvaṇaṃ parivārāḍhyaṃ nāśayāmāsa viśramaḥ|
sukhīcakāra devān sa mahābalagrahaḥ kapiḥ|| 33 ||

mahīrāvaṇasaṃjñaṃ sa hatvā rāmaṃ salakṣmaṇam|
tatsthānādānayāmāsa svasthānaṃ paripālya ca|| 34 ||

rāmakāryaṃ cakārāśu sarvathā kapipiṅgavaḥ|
asurān namayāmāsa nānālīlāṃ cakāra ca|| 35 ||

sthāpayāmāsa bhūloke rāmabhaktiṃ kapīśvaraḥ|
svayaṃ bhaktavaro bhūtvā sītārāmasukhapradaḥ|| 36 ||

lakṣmaṇaprāṇadātā ca sarvadevamadāpahaḥ|
rudrāvatāro bhagavān bhaktoddhārakaraḥ sa vai|| 37 ||

hanumān sa mahāvīro rāmakāryakaraḥ sadā|
rāmadūtābhidho loke daityaghno bhaktavatsalaḥ|| 38 ||

iti te kathitaṃ tāta hanumaccaritaṃ varam|
dhanyaṃ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṃ sarvakāmaphalapradam|| 39 ||

ya idaṃ śṛṇuyādbhaktyā śrāvayedvā samāhitaḥ|
sa bhuktvehākhilān kāmānante mokṣaṃ labhet param|| 40 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ hanumadavatāracaritravarṇaṃ nāma viṃśo 'adhyāyaḥ|| 20 ||

nandīśvara uvāca |

atha prītyā śṛṇu mune 'avatāraṃ paramaṃ prabhoḥ|
śaṃkarasyātmabhūputra śṛṇvatāṃ sarvakāmadam|| 1 ||

ekadā muniśārdūla girijāśaṃkarāvubhau|
vihartukāmau saṃjātau svecchayā parameśvarau|| 2 ||

bhairavaṃ dvārapālaṃ ca kṛtvābhyantaram āgatau|
nānāsakhigaṇaiḥ prītyā sevitay naraśīlitau|| 3 ||

ciraṃ vihṛtya tatra dvau svatantrau parameśvarau|
babhūvatuḥ prasannau tau nānālīlākarau mune|| 4 ||

athonmattākṛtirdevī svatantrā līlayā śivā|
āgatā dvāri tadrūpā prabhorājñām avāpa sā|| 5 ||

tāṃ devīṃ bhairavaḥ so 'atha nārīdṛṣṭyā vilokya ca|
niṣiṣeda bahirgantuṃ tadrūpeṇa vimohitaḥ|| 6 ||

nārīdṛṣṭyā sudṛṣṭā sā bhairaveṇa yadā mune|
kruddhābhavacchivā devī taṃ śaśāpa tadāmbikā|| 7 ||

śivovāca |

nārīdṛṣṭyā paśyasi tvaṃ yato māṃ puruṣādhama|
ato bhava dharāyāṃ hi mānuṣastvaṃ ca bhairava|| 8 ||

nāndīśvara uvāca |

itthaṃ yadābhavacchapto bhairavaḥ śivayā mune|
hāhākāro mahān āsīdduḥkham āpa sa līlayā|| 9 ||

tataśca śaṃkaraḥ śīghraṃ tam āgatya munīśvara|
āśvāsayadbhairavaṃ hi nānānunayakovidaḥ|| 10 ||

tacchāpādbhairavaḥ so 'atha kṣitāvavataran mune|
manuṣyayonyāṃ vaitālasaṃjñakaḥ śaṃkarecchayā|| 11 ||

tatsnehataḥ śivaḥ so 'api kṣitāvavataradvibhuḥ|
śivayā saha sallīlo laukikīṃ gatim āśritaḥ|| 12 ||

maheśāhvaḥ śivaścāsīcchāradā girijā mune|
sulīlāṃ cakratuḥ prītyā nānālīlāviśāradau|| 13 ||

iti te kathitaṃ tāta maheśacaritaṃ varam|
dhanyaṃ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṃ sarvakāmaphalapradam|| 14 ||

ya idaṃ śṛṇuyādbhaktyā śrāvayedvā samāhitaḥ|
sa bhuktvehākhilān bhogānante mokṣam avāpnuyāt|| 15 ||

iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāyāṃ śatarudrasaṃhitāyāṃ maheśāvatāravarṇanaṃ nāmaikaviṃśo 'adhyāyaḥ|| 21 ||
