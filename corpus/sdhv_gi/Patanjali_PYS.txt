samādhiḥ sa ca sārvabhaumaḥ cittasya dharmaḥ kṣiptam mūḍham vikṣiptam ekāgram niruddham iti cittabhūmayaḥ
tatra vikṣipte cetasi vikṣepa upasarjanī bhūtaḥ samādhiḥ na yoga pakṣe vartate yaḥ tu ekāgre cetasi
sadbhūtam artham pradyotayati kṣiṇoti ca kleśān karma bandhanāni ślathayati nirodham abhimukham karoti sa
saṃprajñātaḥ yogaḥ iti ākhyāyate sa ca vitarka anugataḥ vicāra anugataḥ ānanda anugataḥ smita anugataḥ iti
upariṣṭāt nivedayiṣyāmaḥ sarva vṛtti nirodhe tu asaṃprajñātaḥ samādhiḥ tasya lakṣaṇa abhidhitsayā idam
sūtram pravavṛte yogaḥ citta vṛtti nirodhaḥ sarva śabda agrahaṇāt saṃprajñātaḥ u pi yogaḥ iti ākhyāyate
cittam hi prakhyā pravṛtti sthiti śīla tvāt triguṇam prakhyā rūpam hi citta sattvam rajaḥ tamobhyām
saṃsṛṣṭam aiśvarya viṣaya priyam bhavati tat eva tamasā anuviddham adharma ajñāna avairāgya anaiśvarya
upagam bhavati tat eva prakṣīṇa moha āvaraṇam sarvatas pradyotamānam anuviddham rajaḥ mātrayā dharma jñāna
vairāgya aiśvarya upagam bhavati tat eva rajaḥ leśa mala apetam sva rūpa pratiṣṭham sattva puruṣa anyatā
khyāti mātram dharma megha dhyāna upagam bhavati tat param prasaṃkhyānam iti ācakṣate dhyāyinaḥ citi
śaktiḥ apariṇāminī apratisaṃkramā darśita viṣayā śuddhā ca anantā ca sattva guṇa ātmikā ca iyam atas
viparītā viveka khyātiḥ iti atas tasyām viraktam cittam tām api khyātim niruṇaddhi tad avastham saṃskāra
upagam bhavati sa nirbījaḥ samādhiḥ na tatra kiṃcid saṃprajñāyate iti a saṃprajñātaḥ dvividhaḥ sa yogaḥ
citta vṛtti nirodhaḥ iti tad avasthe cetasi viṣaya abhāvāt buddhi bodha ātmā puruṣaḥ kiṃsvabhāvaḥ iti tadā
draṣṭuḥ svarūpe vasthānam svarūpa pratiṣṭhā tadānīm citiśaktiḥ yathā kaivalye vyutthāna citte tu sati
tathā api bhavantī na tathā katham tarhi darśita viṣaya tvāt vṛtti sārūpyam itaratra vyutthāne yāḥ citta
vṛttayaḥ tad aviśiṣṭa vṛttiḥ puruṣaḥ tathā ca sūtram ekam eva darśanam khyātiḥ eva darśanam iti cittam
ayaskānta maṇi kalpam saṃnidhi mātra upakāri dṛśya tvena svam bhavati puruṣasya svāminaḥ tasmāt citta
vṛtti bodhe puruṣasya anādiḥ saṃbandhaḥ hetuḥ tāḥ punar niroddhavyāḥ bahu tve sati cittasya vṛttayaḥ
pañcatayyaḥ kliṣṭa akliṣṭāḥ kleśa hetukāḥ karma āśaya pracaye kṣetrībhūtāḥ kliṣṭāḥ khyāti viṣayāḥ kliṣṭāḥ
kliṣṭa pravāha patitāḥ api akliṣṭāḥ kliṣṭa chidreṣu api akliṣṭāḥ bhavanti akliṣṭa chideṣu kliṣṭāḥ iti
tathājātīyakāḥ saṃskārāḥ vṛttibhiḥ eva kriyante saṃskāraiḥ ca vṛttayaḥ iti evam vṛtti saṃskāra cakram
aniśam āvartate tat evaṃbhūtam cittam avasita adhikāram ātma kalpena vyavatiṣṭhate pralayam vā gacchati
iti tāḥ kliṣṭāḥ ca akliṣṭāḥ ca pañcadhā vṛttayaḥ pramāṇa viparyaya vikalpa nidrā smṛtayaḥ pratyakṣa
anumāna āgamāḥ pramāṇāni indriya praṇālikayā cittasya bāhya vastu uparāgāt tad viṣayā sāmānya viśeṣa
ātmanaḥ rthasya viśeṣa avadhāraṇa pradhānā vṛttiḥ pratyakṣam pramāṇam phalam a viśiṣṭaḥ pauruṣeyaḥ citta
vṛtti bodhaḥ pratisaṃvedī puruṣaḥ iti upariṣṭāt upapādayiṣyāmaḥ anumeyasya tulya jātīyeṣu anuvṛttaḥ bhinna
jātīyebhyaḥ vyāvṛttaḥ saṃbandhaḥ yaḥ tad viṣayā sāmānya avadhāraṇa pradhānā vṛttiḥ anumānam yathā deśa
antara prāpteḥ gatimat candra tārakam caitra vat vindhyaḥ ca aprāptiḥ agatiḥ āptena dṛṣṭaḥ numitaḥ vā
arthaḥ paratra sva bodha saṃkrāntaye śabdena upadiśyate śabdāt tad artha viṣayā vṛttiḥ śrotuḥ āgamaḥ yasya
aśraddheya arthaḥ vaktā na dṛṣṭa anumita arthaḥ saḥ āgamaḥ plavate mūla vaktari tu dṛṣṭa anumita arthe
nirviplavaḥ syāt viparyayaḥ mithyājñānam a tad rūpa pratiṣṭham sa kasmāt na pramāṇam yatas pramāṇena
bādhyate bhūta artha viṣaya tvāt pramāṇasya tatra pramāṇena bādhanam apramāṇasya dṛṣṭam tat yathā dvi
candra darśanam sat viṣayeṇa eka candra darśanena bādhyate iti sā iyam pañca parvā bhavati avidyā avidyā
asmitā rāga dveṣa abhiniveśāḥ kleśāḥ iti ete eva sva saṃjñābhiḥ tamaḥ mohaḥ mahāmohaḥ tāmisraḥ
ndhatāmisraḥ iti ete citta mala prasaṅgena abhidhāsyante śabda jñāna anupātī vastu śūnyaḥ vikalpaḥ sa na
pramāṇa upārohī na viparyaya upārohī ca vastu śūnya tve pi śabda jñāna māhātmya nibandhanaḥ vyavahāraḥ
dṛśyate tat yathā caitanyam puruṣasya svarūpam iti yadā citiḥ eva puruṣaḥ tadā kim atra kena vyapadiśyate
bhavati ca vyapadeśe vṛttiḥ yathā caitrasya gauḥ iti tathā pratiṣiddha vastu dharmaḥ niṣkriyaḥ puruṣaḥ
tiṣṭhati bāṇaḥ sthāsyati sthitaḥ iti gati nivṛttau dhātu artha mātram gamyate tathā anutpatti dharmā
puruṣaḥ iti utpatti dharmasya abhāva mātram avagamyate na puruṣa anvayī dharmaḥ tasmāt vikalpitaḥ sa
dharmaḥ tena ca asti vyavahāraḥ iti sā ca saṃprabodhe pratyavamarśāt pratyaya viśeṣaḥ katham sukham aham
asvāpsam prasannam me manaḥ prajñām me viśāradīkaroti duḥkham aham asvāpsam styānam me manaḥ bhramati
anavasthitam gāḍham mūḍhaḥ ham asvāpsam gurūṇi me gātrāṇi klāntam me cittam alasam muṣitam iva tiṣṭhati
iti sa khalu ayam prabuddhasya pratyavamarśaḥ na syāt asati pratyaya anubhave tad āśritāḥ smṛtayaḥ ca tad
viṣayāḥ na syuḥ tasmāt pratyaya viśeṣaḥ nidrā sā ca samādhau itara pratyaya vat niroddhavyā iti anubhūta
viṣaya asaṃpramoṣaḥ smṛtiḥ kim pratyayasya cittam smarati āhosvit viṣayasya iti grāhya uparaktaḥ pratyayaḥ
grāhya grahaṇa ubhaya ākāra nirbhāsaḥ tad jātīyakam saṃskāram ārabhate sa saṃskāraḥ sva vyañjaka añjanaḥ
tad ākārām eva grāhya grahaṇa ubhaya ātmikām smṛtim janayati tatra grahaṇa ākāra pūrvā buddhiḥ grāhya
ākāra pūrvā smṛtiḥ sā ca dvayī bhāvita smartavyā ca abhāvita smartavyā ca svapne bhāvita smartavyā jāgrat
samaye tu abhāvita smartavyā iti sarvāḥ smṛtayaḥ pramāṇa viparyaya vikalpa nidrā smṛtīnām anubhavāt
prabhavanti sarvāḥ ca etāḥ vṛttayaḥ sukha duḥkha moha ātmikāḥ sukha duḥkha mohāḥ ca kleśeṣu vyākhyeyāḥ
sukha anuśayī rāgaḥ duḥkha anuśayī dveṣaḥ mohaḥ punar avidyā iti etāḥ sarvāḥ vṛttayaḥ niroddhavyāḥ āsām
nirodhe saṃprajñātaḥ vā samādhiḥ bhavati a saṃprajñātaḥ vā iti atha āsām nirodhe kaḥ upāyaḥ iti abhyāsa
vairāgyābhyām tad nirodhaḥ citta nadī nāma ubhayatas vāhinī vahati kalyāṇāya vahati pāpāya ca yā tu
kaivalya prāgbhārā viveka viṣaya nimnā sā kalyāṇa vahā tatra vairāgyeṇa viṣaya srotaḥ khilīkriyate viveka
darśana abhyāsena viveka srotaḥ udghāṭyate iti ubhaya adhīnaḥ citta vṛtti nirodhaḥ tatra sthitau yatnaḥ
bhyāsaḥ cittasya avṛttikasya praśānta vāhi tā sthitiḥ tad arthaḥ prayatnaḥ vīryam utsāhaḥ tat
saṃpipādayiṣayā tat sādhana anuṣṭhānam abhyāsaḥ sa tu dīrgha kāla nairantarya satkāra āsevitaḥ dṛḍha
bhūmiḥ dīrgha kāla āsevitaḥ nirantara āsevitaḥ satkāra āsevitaḥ tapasā brahmacaryeṇa vidyayā śraddhayā ca
saṃpāditaḥ satkāravān dṛḍha bhūmiḥ bhavati vyutthāna saṃskāreṇa drāk iti eva anabhibhūta viṣayaḥ iti
arthaḥ dṛṣṭa ānuśravika viṣaya vitṛṣṇasya vaśīkāra saṃjñā vairāgyam striyaḥ anna pānam aiśvaryam iti dṛṣṭa
viṣaye vitṛṣṇasya svarga vaidehya prakṛti laya tva prāptau ānuśravika viṣaye vitṛṣṇasya divya adivya
viṣaya saṃprayoge cittasya viṣaya doṣa darśinaḥ prasaṃkhyāna balāt anābhoga ātmikā heya upādeya śūnyā
vaśīkāra saṃjñā vairāgyam tat param puruṣa khyāteḥ guṇa vaitṛṣṇyam dṛṣṭa ānuśravika viṣaya doṣa darśī
viraktaḥ puruṣa darśana abhyāsāt tad śuddhi praviveka āpyāyita buddhiḥ guṇebhyaḥ vyakta avyakta
dharmakebhyaḥ viraktaḥ iti tat dvayam vairāgyam tatra yat uttaram tat jñāna prasāda mātram yasya udaye
sati yogī pratyudita khyātiḥ evam manyate prāptam prāpaṇīyam kṣīṇāḥ kṣetavyāḥ kleśāḥ chinnaḥ śliṣṭa parvā
bhava saṃkramaḥ yasya avicchedāt janitvā mriyate mṛtvā ca jāyate iti jñānasya eva parā kāṣṭhā vairāgyam
etasya eva hi nāntarīyakam kaivalyam iti atha upāya dvayena niruddha citta vṛtteḥ katham ucyate
saṃprajñātaḥ samādhiḥ iti vitarka vicāra ānanda asmitā rūpa anugamāt saṃprajñātaḥ vitarkaḥ cittasya
ālambane sthūlaḥ ābhogaḥ sūkṣmaḥ vicāraḥ ānandaḥ hlādaḥ eka ātmikā saṃvid asmitā tatra prathamaḥ catuṣṭaya
anugataḥ samādhiḥ sa vitarkaḥ dvitīyaḥ vitarka vikalaḥ sa vicāraḥ caturthaḥ smitā mātraḥ iti sarve ete sa
ālambanāḥ samādhayaḥ atha asaṃprajñātaḥ samādhiḥ kimupāyaḥ kiṃsvabhāvaḥ vā iti virāma pratyaya abhyāsa
pūrvaḥ saṃskāra śeṣaḥ sarva vṛtti prati astamaye saṃskāra śeṣaḥ nirodhaḥ cittasya samādhiḥ asaṃprajñātaḥ
tasya param vairāgyam upāyaḥ sa ālambanaḥ hi abhyāsaḥ tad sādhanāya na kalpate iti virāma pratyayaḥ
nirvastukaḥ ālambanīkriyate sa ca artha śūnyaḥ tad abhyāsa pūrvakam hi cittam nirālambanam abhāva prāptam
iva bhavati iti eṣa nirbījaḥ samādhiḥ asaṃprajñātaḥ sa khalu ayam dvividhaḥ upāya pratyayaḥ bhava
pratyayaḥ ca tatra upāya pratyayaḥ yoginām bhavati bhava pratyayaḥ videha prakṛti layānām videhānām
devānām bhava pratyayaḥ te hi sva saṃskāra mātra upayogena cittena kaivalya padam iva anubhavantaḥ sva
saṃskāra vipākam tathājātīyakam ativāhayanti tathā prakṛti layāḥ sa adhikāre cetasi prakṛti līne kaivalya
padam iva anubhavanti yāvat na punar āvartate dhikāra vaśāt cittam iti śraddhā vīrya smṛti samādhi prajñā
pūrvakaḥ itareṣām upāya pratyayaḥ yoginām bhavati śraddhā cetasaḥ saṃprasādaḥ sā hi jananī iva kalyāṇī
yoginam pāti tasya hi śraddadhānasya viveka arthinaḥ vīryam upajāyate samupajāta vīryasya smṛtiḥ
upatiṣṭhate smṛti upasthāne ca cittam an ākulam samādhīyate samāhita cittasya prajñā vivekaḥ upāvartate
yena yathārtham vastu jānāti tad abhyāsāt tad tad viṣayāt ca vairāgyāt asaṃprajñātaḥ samādhiḥ bhavati te
khalu nava yoginaḥ mṛdu madhya adhimātra upāyāḥ bhavanti tat yathā mṛdu upāyaḥ madhya upāyaḥ dhimātra
upāyaḥ iti tatra mṛdu upāyaḥ trividhaḥ mṛdu saṃvegaḥ madhya saṃvegaḥ tīvra saṃvegaḥ iti tathā madhya
upāyaḥ tathā adhimātra upāyaḥ iti tatra adhimātra upāyānām tīvra saṃvegānām āsannaḥ samādhi lābhaḥ samādhi
phalam ca bhavati iti mṛdu madhya adhimātra tvāt viśeṣaḥ mṛdu tīvraḥ madhya tīvraḥ dhimātra tīvraḥ iti
viśeṣaḥ tad viśeṣāt api mṛdu tīvra saṃvegasya āsannaḥ tatas madhya tīvra saṃvegasya āsannataraḥ tasmāt
adhimātra tīvra saṃvegasya adhimātra upāyasya api āsannatamaḥ samādhi lābhaḥ samādhi phalam ca iti kim
etasmāt eva āsannatamaḥ samādhiḥ bhavati atha asya lābhe bhavati kaścid upāyaḥ na vā iti īśvara
praṇidhānāt vā praṇidhānāt bhakti viśeṣāt āvarjitaḥ īśvaraḥ tam anugṛhṇāti abhidhyāna mātreṇa tad
abhidhyāna mātrāt api yoginaḥ āsannatamaḥ samādhi lābhaḥ samādhi phalam ca bhavati iti atha pradhāna
puruṣa vyatiriktaḥ kaḥ yam īśvaraḥ nāma iti kleśa karma vipāka āśayaiḥ aparāmṛṣṭaḥ puruṣa viśeṣaḥ īśvaraḥ
avidyā ādayaḥ kleśāḥ kuśala akuśalāni karmāṇi tad phalam vipākaḥ tad anuguṇāḥ vāsanāḥ āśayāḥ te ca manasi
vartamānāḥ puruṣe vyapadiśyante sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti yathā jayaḥ parājayaḥ vā yoddhṛṣu vartamānaḥ
svāmini vyapadiśyate yaḥ hi anena bhogena aparāmṛṣṭaḥ sa puruṣa viśeṣaḥ īśvaraḥ kaivalyam prāptāḥ tarhi
santi ca bahavaḥ kevalinaḥ te hi trīṇi bandhanāni chittvā kaivalyam prāptāḥ īśvarasya ca tad saṃbandhaḥ na
bhūtaḥ na bhāvī yathā muktasya pūrvā bandha koṭiḥ prajñāyate na evam īśvarasya yathā vā prakṛti līnasya
uttarā bandha koṭiḥ saṃbhāvyate na evam īśvarasya sa tu sadā eva muktaḥ sadā eva īśvaraḥ iti prakṛṣṭa
sattva upādānāt īśvarasya śāśvatikaḥ utkarṣaḥ sa kim sa nimittaḥ āhosvit nirnimittaḥ iti tasya śāstram
nimittam śāstram punar kiṃnimittam prakṛṣṭa sattva nimittam etayoḥ śāstra utkarṣayoḥ īśvara sattve
vartamānayoḥ anādiḥ saṃbandhaḥ etasmāt etat bhavati sadā eva īśvaraḥ sadā eva muktaḥ iti tat ca tasya

dvayoḥ tulyayoḥ ekasmin yugapad kāmite rthe navam idam astu purāṇam idam astu iti ekasya siddhau itarasya
prākāmya vighātāt ūna tvam prasaktam dvayoḥ ca tulyayoḥ yugapad kāmita artha prāptiḥ na asti arthasya
viruddha tvāt tasmāt yasya sāmya atiśayaiḥ vinirmuktam aiśvaryam saḥ eva īśvaraḥ sa ca puruṣa viśeṣaḥ iti
kim ca tatra niratiśayam sarvajña bījam yat idam atīta anāgata pratyutpanna pratyeka samuccaya atīndriya
grahaṇam alpam bahu iti sarvajña bījam etat vivardhamānam yatra niratiśayam sa sarvajñaḥ asti kāṣṭhā
prāptiḥ sarvajña bījasya sa atiśaya tvāt parimāṇa vat iti yatra kāṣṭhā prāptiḥ jñānasya sa sarvajñaḥ sa ca
puruṣa viśeṣaḥ iti sāmānya mātra upasaṃhāre ca kṛta upakṣayam anumānam na viśeṣa pratipattau samartham iti
tasya saṃjñā ādi viśeṣa pratipattiḥ āgamataḥ paryanveṣyā tasya ātma anugraha abhāve bhūta anugrahaḥ

provāca iti saḥ eṣaḥ pūrveṣām api guruḥ kālena anavacchedāt pūrve hi guravaḥ kālena avacchidyante yatra
avaccheda arthena kālaḥ na upāvartate saḥ eṣa pūrveṣām api guruḥ yathā asya sargasya ādau prakarṣa gatyā
siddhaḥ tathā atikrānta sarga ādiṣu api pratyetavyaḥ tasya vācakaḥ praṇavaḥ vācyaḥ īśvaraḥ praṇavasya kim

saṃbandhaḥ saṃketena avadyotyate ayam asya pitā ayam asya putraḥ iti sarga antareṣu api vācya vācaka śakti
apekṣaḥ tathā eva saṃketaḥ kriyate saṃpratipatti nitya tayā nityaḥ śabda artha saṃbandhaḥ iti āgaminaḥ
pratijānate vijñāta vācya vācaka tvasya yoginaḥ tad japaḥ tad artha bhāvanam praṇavasya japaḥ praṇava

bheda jam duḥkham na āpnoti kasmāt tri tva upalabdhi sāmarthyāt iti atra api tāpakasya rajasaḥ sattvam eva
tapyam kasmāt tapi kriyāyāḥ karma stha tvāt sattve karmaṇi tapi kriyā na apariṇāmini niṣkriye kṣetrajñe
darśita viṣaya tvāt sattve tu tapyamāne tad ākāra anurodhī puruṣaḥ api anutapyate iti dṛśya svarūpam
ucyate prakāśa kriyā sthiti śīlam bhūta indriya ātmakam bhoga apavarga artham dṛśyam prakāśa śīlam sattvam
kriyā śīlam rajaḥ sthiti śīlam tamaḥ iti ete guṇāḥ paraspara uparakta pravibhāgāḥ pariṇāminaḥ saṃyoga
viyoga dharmāṇaḥ itara itara upāśrayeṇa upārjita mūrtayaḥ paraspara aṅga aṅgi tve pi asaṃbhinna śakti
pravibhāgāḥ tulya jātīya atulya jātīya śakti bheda anupātinaḥ pradhāna velāyām upadarśita saṃnidhānāḥ guṇa
tve pi ca vyāpāra mātreṇa pradhāna antar ṇīta anumita astitāḥ puruṣa artha kartavya tayā prayukta
sāmarthyāḥ saṃnidhi mātra upakāriṇaḥ yaskānta maṇi kal etat dṛśyam iti ucyate tat etat bhūta indriya
ātmakam bhūta bhāvena pṛthivī ādinā sūkṣma sthūlena pariṇamate tathā indriya bhāvena śrotra ādinā sūkṣma
sthūlena pariṇamataḥ iti tat tu na aprayojanam api tu prayojanam urarīkṛtya pravartate iti bhoga apavarga
artham hi tat dṛśyam puruṣasya iti tatra iṣṭa aniṣṭa guṇa svarūpa avadhāraṇam avibhāga āpannam bhogaḥ
bhoktuḥ svarūpa avadhāraṇam apavargaḥ iti dvayoḥ atiriktam anyat darśanam na asti tathā ca uktam ayam tu
khalu triṣu guṇeṣu kartṛṣu akartari ca puruṣe tulya atulya jātīye caturthe tad kriyā sākṣiṇi upanīyamānān
sarva bhāvān upapannān anupaśyan adarśanam anyat śaṅkate iti tau etau bhoga apavargau buddhi kṛtau buddhau
eva vartamānau katham puruṣe vyapadiśyete iti yathā vijayaḥ parājayaḥ vā yoddhṛṣu vartamānaḥ svāmini
vyapadiśyate sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti evam bandha mokṣau buddhau eva vartamānau puruṣe vyapadiśyete
sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti buddheḥ eva puruṣa artha aparisamāptiḥ bandhaḥ tad artha avasāyaḥ mokṣaḥ iti
etena grahaṇa dhāraṇa ūha apoha tattva jñāna abhiniveśāḥ buddhau vartamānāḥ puruṣe dhyā āropita sadbhāvāḥ
sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti dṛśyānām guṇānām svarūpa bheda avadhāraṇa artham idam ārabhyate viśeṣa
aviśeṣa liṅga mātra aliṅgāni guṇa parvāṇi tatra ākāśa vāyu agni udaka bhūmayaḥ bhūtāni śabda sparśa rūpa
rasa gandha tanmātrāṇām aviśeṣāṇām viśeṣāḥ tathā śrotra tvac cakṣuḥ jihvā ghrāṇāni buddhīndriyāṇi vāc pāṇi
pāda pāyu upasthāḥ karmendriyāṇi ekādaśam manaḥ sarva artham iti etāni asmitā lakṣaṇasya aviśeṣasya
viśeṣāḥ guṇānām eṣa ṣoḍaśakaḥ viśeṣa pariṇāmaḥ ṣaṭ aviśeṣāḥ tat yathā śabda tanmātram sparśa tanmātram
rūpa tanmātram rasa tanmātram gandha tanmātram ca iti eka dvi tri catur pañca lakṣaṇāḥ śabda ādayaḥ pañca
aviśeṣāḥ ṣaṣṭhaḥ ca aviśeṣaḥ smitā mātraḥ iti ete sattā mātrasya ātmanaḥ mahataḥ ṣaṣ aviśeṣa pariṇāmāḥ yat
tat parama viśeṣebhyaḥ liṅgamātram mahat tattvam tasmin ete sattā mātre mahati ātmani avasthāya vivṛddhi
kāṣṭhām anubhavanti pratisaṃsṛjyamānāḥ ca tasmin eva sattā mātre mahati ātmani avasthāya yat tat niḥsattā
asattam niḥsat asat niḥsat avyaktam aliṅgam pradhānam tat pratiyanti eṣa teṣām liṅga mātraḥ pariṇāmaḥ
niḥsatta asattam ca a liṅga pariṇāmaḥ iti aliṅga avasthāyām na puruṣa arthaḥ hetuḥ na aliṅga avasthāyām
ādau puruṣa artha tā kāraṇam bhavati iti na tasyāḥ puruṣa artha tā kāraṇam bhavati iti na asau puruṣa
artha kṛtā iti nityā ākhyāyate trayāṇām tu avasthā viśeṣāṇām ādau puruṣa artha tā kāraṇam bhavati sa ca
arthaḥ hetuḥ nimittam kāraṇam bhavati iti anityā ākhyāyate guṇāḥ tu sarva dharma anupātinaḥ na pratyastam
ayante na upajāyante vyaktibhiḥ eva atīta anāgata vyaya āgamavatībhiḥ guṇa anvayinībhiḥ upajanana apāya
dharmakāḥ iva pratyavabhāsante yathā devadattaḥ daridrāti kasmāt yatas sya mriyante gāvaḥ iti gavām eva
maraṇāt tasya daridrāṇam na sva rūpa hānāt iti samaḥ samādhiḥ liṅga mātram aliṅgasya pratyāsannam tatra
tat saṃsṛṣṭam vivicyate kramāt ativṛttayaḥ tathā ṣaṭ aviśeṣāḥ liṅgamātre saṃsṛṣṭāḥ vivicyante pariṇāma
krama niyamāt tathā teṣu aviśeṣeṣu bhūta indriyāṇi saṃsṛṣṭāni vivicyante tathā ca uktam purastāt na
viśeṣebhyaḥ param tattva antaram asti iti viśeṣāṇām na asti tattva antara pariṇāmaḥ teṣām tu dharma
lakṣaṇa avasthā pariṇāmāḥ vyākhyāyiṣyante vyākhyātam dṛśyam atha draṣṭuḥ svarūpa avadhāraṇa artham idam
ārabhyate draṣṭā dṛśi mātraḥ pratyaya anupaśyaḥ dṛśi mātraḥ iti dṛkśaktiḥ eva viśeṣaṇa aparāmṛṣṭā iti
arthaḥ sa puruṣaḥ bhuddheḥ pratisaṃvedī sa buddheḥ na sarūpaḥ na atyantam virūpaḥ iti na tāvat sarūpaḥ
kasmāt jñāta ajñāta viṣaya tvāt pariṇāminī hi buddhiḥ tasyāḥ ca viṣayaḥ go ādiḥ ghaṭa ādiḥ vā jñātaḥ ca
ajñātaḥ ca iti pariṇāmi tvam darśayati sadā ajñāta viṣaya tvam tu puruṣasya apariṇāmi tvam paridīpayati
kasmāt na hi buddhiḥ ca nāma puruṣa viṣayaḥ ca syāt agṛhītā gṛhītā ca iti siddham puruṣasya sadā ajñāta
viṣaya tvam tatas ca apariṇāmi tvam iti kim ca para arthā buddhiḥ saṃhatya kāri tvāt sva arthaḥ puruṣaḥ
iti tathā sarva artha adhyavasāyaka tvāt triguṇā buddhiḥ triguṇa tvāt acetanā iti guṇānām tu upadraṣṭā
puruṣaḥ iti atas na sarūpaḥ astu tarhi virūpaḥ iti na atyantam virūpaḥ kasmāt śuddhaḥ pi asau pratyaya
anupaśyaḥ yatas pratyayam bauddham anupaśyati tam anupaśyan a tad ātmā api tad ātmakaḥ iva pratyavabhāsate
tathā ca uktam apariṇāminī hi bhoktṛ śaktiḥ apratisaṃkramā ca pariṇāminī arthe pratisaṃkrāntā iva tad
vṛttim anupatati tasyāḥ ca prāpta caitanya upagraha rūpāyāḥ buddhi vṛtteḥ anukāra mātra tayā buddhi vṛtti
aviśiṣṭā hi jñāna vṛttiḥ iti ākhyāyate tad arthaḥ eva dṛśyasya ātmā dṛśi rūpasya puruṣasya karma rūpa tām
āpannam dṛśyam iti tad arthaḥ eva dṛśyasya ātmā bhavati sva rūpam bhavati iti arthaḥ sva rūpam tu para
rūpeṇa pratilabdha ātmakam bhoga apavarga artha tāyām kṛtāyām puruṣeṇa na dṛśyate iti sva rūpa hānāt asya
nāśaḥ prāptaḥ na tu vinaśyati kasmāt kṛtārtham prati naṣṭam api a naṣṭam tad anya sādhāraṇa tvāt kṛtārtham
ekam puruṣam prati dṛśyam naṣṭam api nāśam prāptam api anaṣṭam tad anya puruṣa sādhāraṇa tvāt kuśalam
puruṣam prati nāśam prāptam api akuśalān puruṣān prati na kṛtārtham iti teṣām dṛśeḥ karma viṣaya tām
āpannam labhate eva para rūpeṇa ātma rūpam iti atas ca dṛś darśana śaktyoḥ nitya tvāt anādiḥ saṃyogaḥ
vyākhyātaḥ iti tathā ca uktam dharmiṇām anādi saṃyogāt dharma mātrāṇām api anādiḥ saṃyogaḥ iti saṃyoga sva
rūpa abhidhitsayā idam sūtram pravavṛte sva svāmi śaktyoḥ sva rūpa upalabdhi hetuḥ saṃyogaḥ puruṣaḥ svāmī
dṛśyena svena darśana artham saṃyuktaḥ tasmāt saṃyogāt dṛśyasya upalabdhiḥ yā sa bhogaḥ yā tu draṣṭuḥ sva
rūpa upalabdhiḥ sā upavargaḥ darśana kārya avasānaḥ saṃyogaḥ iti darśanam viyogasya kāraṇam uktam darśanam
adarśanasya pratidvandvi iti adarśanam saṃyoga nimittam uktam na atra darśanam mokṣa kāraṇam adarśana
abhāvāt eva bandha abhāvaḥ sa mokṣaḥ iti darśanasya bhāve bandha kāraṇasya adarśanasya nāśaḥ iti atas
darśanam jñānam kaivalya kāraṇam uktam kiṃca idam adarśanam nāma kim guṇānām adhikāraḥ āhosvit dṛśi
rūpasya svāminaḥ darśita viṣayasya pradhāna cittasya anutpādaḥ svasmin dṛśye vidyamāne yaḥ darśana abhāvaḥ
kim arthavat tā guṇānām atha avidyā sva cittena saha niruddhā sva cittasya utpatti bījam kim sthiti
saṃskāra kṣaye gati saṃskāra abhivyaktiḥ yatra idam uktam pradhānam sthityā eva vartamānam vikāra akaraṇāt
apradhānam syāt tathā gatya eva vartamānam vikāra nitya tvāt apradhānam syāt ubhayathā ca asya vṛttiḥ
pradhāna vyavahāram labhate na anyathā kāraṇa antareṣu api kalpiteṣu eva samānaḥ carcaḥ darśana śaktiḥ eva
adarśanam iti eke pradhānasya ātma khyāpana arthā pravṛttiḥ iti śruteḥ sarva bodhya bodha samarthaḥ prāc
pravṛtteḥ puruṣaḥ na paśyati sarva kārya karaṇa samartham dṛśyam tadā na dṛśyate iti ubhayasya api
adarśanam dharmaḥ iti eke tatra idam dṛśyasya sva ātma bhūtam api puruṣa pratyaya apekṣam darśanam dṛśya
dharma tvena bhavati tathā puruṣasya anātma bhūtam api dṛśya pratyaya apekṣam puruṣa dharma tvena iva
adarśanam avabhāsate darśanam jñānam eva adarśanam iti kecid abhidadhati iti ete śāstra gatāḥ vikalpāḥ
tatra vikalpa bahu tvam etat sarva puruṣāṇām guṇānām saṃyoge sādhāraṇa viṣayam yaḥ tu pratyak cetanasya
sva buddhi saṃyogaḥ tasya hetuḥ avidyā viparyaya jñāna vāsanā vāsi tā ca na kārya niṣṭhām puruṣa khyātim
buddhiḥ prāpnoti sa adhikārā punar āvartate sā tu puruṣa khyāti paryavasānām kārya niṣṭhām prāpnoti carita
adhikārā nivṛtta adarśanā bandha kāraṇa abhāvāt na punar āvartate atra kaścid paṇḍaka upākhyānena
udghāṭayati mugdhayā bhāryayā abhidhīyate paṇḍaka ārya putra apatyavatī me bhaginī kimartham nāma na aham
iti sa tām āha mṛtaḥ te ham apatyam utpādayiṣyāmi iti tathā idam vidyamānam jñānam citta nivṛttim na
karoti vinaṣṭam kariṣyati iti kā pratyāśā tatra ācārya deśīyaḥ vakti nanu buddhi nivṛttiḥ eva mokṣaḥ
darśana kāraṇa abhāvāt buddhi nivṛttiḥ tat ca adarśanam bandha kāraṇam darśanāt nivartate tatra citta
nivṛttiḥ eva mokṣaḥ kimartham asthāne eva asya mati vibhramaḥ heyam duḥkham heya kāraṇam ca saṃyoga ākhyam
sa nimittam uktam atas param hānam vaktavyam tad abhāvāt saṃyoga abhāvaḥ hānam tad dṛśeḥ kaivalyam tasya
adarśanasya abhāvāt buddhi puruṣa saṃyoga abhāvaḥ ātyantikaḥ bandhana uparamaḥ iti arthaḥ etat dhānam tad
dṛśeḥ kaivalyam puruṣasya amiśrībhāvaḥ punar asaṃyogaḥ guṇaiḥ iti arthaḥ duḥkha kāraṇa nivṛttau duḥkha
uparamaḥ hānam tadā svarūpa pratiṣṭhaḥ puruṣaḥ iti uktam atha hānasya kaḥ prāpti upāyaḥ iti viveka khyātiḥ
aviplavā hāna upāyaḥ sā tu anivṛtta mithyājñānā plavate yadā mithyājñānam dagdha bīja bhāvam vandhya
prasavam saṃpadyate tadā vidhūta kleśa rajasaḥ sattvasya pare vaiśāradye parasyām vaśīkāra saṃjñāyām
vartamānasya viveka pratyaya pravāhaḥ nirmalaḥ bhavati sā viveka khyātiḥ aviplavā hāna upāyaḥ tatas
mithyājñānasya dagdha bīja bhāva upagamaḥ punar ca aprasavaḥ iti eṣa mokṣasya mārgaḥ hānasya upāyaḥ iti
tasya saptadhā prānta bhūmiḥ prajñā tasya iti pratyudita khyāteḥ pratyāmnāyaḥ saptadhā iti aśuddhi āvaraṇa
mala apagamāt cittasya pratyaya antara anutpāde sati sapta prakārā eva prajñā vivekinaḥ bhavati tat yathā
parijñātam heyam na asya punar parijñeyam asti kṣīṇāḥ heya hetavaḥ na punar eteṣām kṣetavyam asti
sākṣātkṛtam nirodha samādhinā hānam bhāvitaḥ viveka khyāti rūpaḥ hāna upāyaḥ iti eṣā catuṣṭayī kāryā
vimuktiḥ prajñāyāḥ citta vimuktiḥ tu trayī carita adhikārā buddhiḥ guṇāḥ giri śikhara taṭa cyutāḥ iva
grāvāṇaḥ niravasthānāḥ sva kāraṇe pralaya abhimukhāḥ saha tena astam gacchanti na ca eṣām pravilīnānām
punar asti utpādaḥ prayojana abhāvāt iti etasyām avasthāyām guṇa saṃbandha atītaḥ sva rūpa mātra jyotiḥ
amalaḥ kevalī puruṣaḥ iti etām saptavidhām prānta bhūmi prajñām anupaśyan puruṣaḥ kuśalaḥ iti ākhyāyate
pratiprasave pi cittasya muktaḥ kuśalaḥ iti eva bhavati guṇa atīta tvāt iti siddhā bhavati viveka khyātiḥ
hāna upāyaḥ iti na ca siddhiḥ antareṇa sādhanam iti etat ārabhyate yogāṅga anuṣṭhānāt aśuddhi kṣaye jñāna
dīptiḥ viveka khyāteḥ yogāṅgāni aṣṭau abhidhāyiṣyamāṇāni teṣām anuṣṭhānāt pañca parvaṇaḥ viparyayasya
aśuddhi rūpasya kṣayaḥ nāśaḥ tad kṣaye samyak jñānasya abhivyaktiḥ yathā yathā ca sādhanāni anuṣṭhīyante
tathā tathā tanu tvam aśuddhiḥ āpadyate yathā yathā ca kṣīyate tathā tathā kṣaya krama anurodhinī jñānasya
api dīptiḥ vivardhate sā khalu eṣā vivṛddhiḥ prakarṣam anubhavatyāḥ viveka khyāteḥ ā guṇa puruṣa sva rūpa
vijñānāt iti arthaḥ yogāṅga anuṣṭhānam aśuddheḥ viyoga kāraṇam yathā paraśuḥ chedyasya viveka khyāteḥ tu
prāpti kāraṇam yathā dharmaḥ sukhasya na anyathā kāraṇam kati ca etāni kāraṇāni śāstre bhavanti nava eva
iti āha tat yathā utpatti sthiti abhivyakti vikāra pratyaya āptayaḥ viyoga anyatva dhṛtayaḥ kāraṇam
navadhā smṛtam iti tatra utpatti kāraṇam manaḥ bhavati vijñānasya sthiti kāraṇam manasaḥ puruṣa artha tā
śarīrasya iva āhāraḥ iti abhivyakti kāraṇam yathā rūpasya ālokaḥ tathā rūpa jñānam vikāra kāraṇam manasaḥ
viṣaya antaram yathā agniḥ pākyasya pratyaya kāraṇam dhūma jñānam agni jñānasya prāpti kāraṇam yogāṅga
anuṣṭhānam viveka khyāteḥ viyoga kāraṇam tat eva aśuddheḥ anya tva kāraṇam yathā suvarṇasya suvarṇakāraḥ
evam ekasya strī pratyayasya avidyā mūḍha tve dveṣaḥ duḥkha tve rāgaḥ sukha tve tattva jñānam mādhyasthye
dhṛti kāraṇam śarīram indriyāṇām tāni ca tasya mahābhūtāni śarīrāṇām tāni ca parasparam sarveṣām
tairyagyauna mānuṣa daivatāni ca paraspara artha tvāt iti evam nava kāraṇāni tāni ca yathāsaṃbhavam
padārtha antareṣu api yojyāni yogāṅga anuṣṭhānam tu dvidhā eva kāraṇa tvam labhate iti tatra yoga aṅgāni
avadhāryante yama niyama āsana prāṇāyāma pratyāhāra dhāraṇā dhyāna samādhayaḥ ṣṭāv aṅgāni yathākramam eṣām
anuṣṭhānam svarūpam ca vakṣyāmaḥ tatra ahiṃsā satya asteya brahmacarya aparigrahāḥ yamāḥ tatra ahiṃsā
sarvathā sarvadā sarva bhūtānām anabhidrohaḥ uttare ca yama niyamāḥ tad mūlāḥ tad siddhi para tayā eva tad
pratipādanāya pratipādyante tad avadāta rūpa karaṇāya eva upādīyante tathā ca uktam sa khalu ayam
brāhmaṇaḥ yathā yathā vratāni bahūni samāditsate tathā tathā pramāda kṛtebhyaḥ hiṃsā nidānebhyaḥ
nivartamānaḥ tām eva avadāta rūpām ahiṃsām karoti satyam yathā arthe vāc manase yathā dṛṣṭam yathā
anumitam tathā vāc manaḥ ca iti paratra sva bodha saṃkrāntaye vāc uktā sā yadi na vañcitā bhrāntā vā
pratipatti vandhyā vā bhavet iti eṣā sarva bhūta upakāra artham pravṛttā na bhūta upaghātāya yadi ca evam
api abhidhīyamānā bhūta upaghāta parā eva syāt na satyam bhavet pāpam eva bhavet tena puṇya ābhāsena puṇya
pratirūpakeṇa kaṣṭam tamaḥ prāpnuyāt tasmāt parīkṣya sarva bhūta hitam satyam brūyāt steyam aśāstra
pūrvakam dravyāṇām paratas svīkaraṇam tad pratiṣedhaḥ punar aspṛhā rūpam asteyam iti brahmacaryam gupta
indriyasya upasthasya saṃyamaḥ viṣayāṇām arjana rakṣaṇa kṣaya saṅga hiṃsā doṣa darśanāt asvīkaraṇam
aparigrahaḥ iti ete yamāḥ te tu jāti deśa kāla samaya anavacchinnāḥ sārvabhaumāḥ mahāvratam tatra ahiṃsā
jāti avacchinnā matsya vadhakasya matsyeṣu eva na anyatra hiṃsā sā eva deśa avacchinnā na tīrthe haniṣyāmi

susthitaṃmanya tayā mṛtyunā keśeṣu gṛhītam iva ātmānam na bhāvayiṣyati tathā ca asya chidra antara prekṣī
nityam yatna upacaryaḥ pramādaḥ labdha vivaraḥ kleśān uttambhayiṣyati tatas punar aniṣṭa prasaṅgaḥ evam
asya saṅga smayau a kurvataḥ bhāvitaḥ rthaḥ dṛḍhībhaviṣyati bhāvanīyaḥ ca arthaḥ bhimukhībhaviṣyati iti
yathā apakarṣa paryantam dravyam paramāṇuḥ evam parama apakarṣa paryantaḥ kālaḥ kṣaṇaḥ yāvatā vā samayena
calitaḥ paramāṇuḥ pūrva deśam jahyāt uttara deśam upasaṃpadyeta sa kālaḥ kṣaṇaḥ tad pravāha avicchedaḥ tu
kramaḥ kṣaṇa tad kramayoḥ na asti vastu samāhāraḥ iti buddhi samāhāraḥ muhūrta ahar rātra ādayaḥ sa khalu
ayam kālaḥ vastu śūnyaḥ buddhi nirmāṇaḥ śabda jñāna anupātī laukikānām vyutthita darśanānām vastu svarūpaḥ
iva avabhāsate kṣaṇaḥ tu vastu patitaḥ krama avalambī kramaḥ ca kṣaṇa ānantarya ātmā tam kāla vidaḥ kālaḥ
iti ācakṣate yoginaḥ na ca dvau kṣaṇau saha bhavataḥ kramaḥ ca na dvayoḥ saha bhuvoḥ asaṃbhavāt pūrvasmāt
uttara bhāvinaḥ yat ānantaryam kṣaṇasya sa kramaḥ tasmāt vartamānaḥ eva ekaḥ kṣaṇaḥ na pūrva uttara kṣaṇāḥ
santi iti tasmāt na asti tad samāhāraḥ ye tu bhūta bhāvinaḥ kṣaṇāḥ te pariṇāma anvitāḥ vyākhyeyāḥ tena
ekena kṣaṇena kṛtsnaḥ lokaḥ pariṇāmam anubhavati tad kṣaṇa upārūḍhāḥ khalu amī sarve dharmāḥ tayoḥ kṣaṇa
tad kramayoḥ saṃyamāt tayoḥ sākṣātkaraṇam tatas ca viveka jam jñānam prādurbhavati tasya viṣaya viśeṣaḥ
upakṣipyate jāti lakṣaṇa deśaiḥ anyatā anavacchedāt tulyayoḥ tatas pratipattiḥ tulyayoḥ deśa lakṣaṇa
sārūpye jāti bhedaḥ nya tāyāḥ hetuḥ gauḥ iyam baḍavā iyam iti tulya deśa jātīya tve lakṣaṇam anya tva
karam kāla akṣī gauḥ svastimatī gauḥ iti dvayoḥ āmalakayoḥ jāti lakṣaṇa sārūpyāt deśa bhedaḥ nyatva karaḥ
idam pūrvam idam uttaram iti yadā tu pūrvam āmalakam anya vyagrasya jñātuḥ uttara deśe upāvartyate tadā
tulya deśa tve pūrvam etat uttaram etat iti pravibhāga anupapattiḥ asaṃdigdhena ca tattva jñānena
bhavitavyam iti atas idam uktam tatas pratipattiḥ viveka ja jñānāt iti katham pūrva āmalaka sahakṣaṇaḥ
deśaḥ uttara āmalaka sahakṣaṇāt deśāt bhinnaḥ te ca āmalake sva deśa kṣaṇa anubhava bhinne anya deśa kṣaṇa
anubhavaḥ tu tayoḥ anya tve hetuḥ iti etena dṛṣṭāntena paramāṇoḥ tulya jāti lakṣaṇa deśasya pūrva paramāṇu
deśa sahakṣaṇa sākṣātkaraṇāt uttarasya paramāṇoḥ tad deśa anupapattau uttarasya tad deśa anubhavaḥ bhinnaḥ
sahakṣaṇa bhedāt tayoḥ īśvarasya yoginaḥ nya tva pratyayaḥ bhavati iti apare tu varṇayanti ye ntyāḥ
viśeṣāḥ te nyatā pratyayam kurvanti iti tatra api deśa lakṣaṇa bhedaḥ mūrti vyavadhi jāti bhedaḥ ca
anyatve hetuḥ kṣaṇabhedaḥ tu yogi buddhi gamyaḥ eva iti atas uktam mūrti vyavadhi jāti bheda abhāvāt na
asti mūla pṛthaktvam iti vārṣagaṇyaḥ tārakam sarva viṣayam sarvathāviṣayam akramam ca iti viveka jam
jñānam tārakam iti sva pratibhā uttham anaupadeśikam iti arthaḥ sarva viṣayam na asya kiṃcid aviṣayībhūtam
iti arthaḥ sarvathā viṣayam atīta anāgata pratyutpannam sarvam paryāyaiḥ sarvathā jānāti iti arthaḥ
akramam iti eka kṣaṇa upārūḍham sarvam sarvathā gṛhṇāti iti arthaḥ etat viveka jam jñānam paripūrṇam asya
eva aṃśaḥ yoga pradīpaḥ madhumatīm bhūmim upādāya yāvat asya parisamāptiḥ iti prāpta viveka ja jñānasya
aprāpta viveka ja jñānasya vā sattva puruṣayoḥ śuddhi sāmye kaivalyam iti yadā nirdhūta rajaḥ tamaḥ malam
buddhi sattvam puruṣasya anyatā pratīti mātra adhikāram dagdha kleśa bījam bhavati tadā puruṣasya śuddhi
sārūpyam iva āpannam bhavati tadā puruṣasya upacarita bhoga abhāvaḥ śuddhiḥ etasyām avasthāyām kaivalyam
bhavati īśvarasya anīśvarasya vā viveka ja jñāna bhāginaḥ itarasya vā na hi dagdha kleśa bījasya jñāne
punar apekṣā kācid asti sattva śuddhi dvāreṇa etat samādhi jam aiśvaryam jñānam ca upakrāntam
paramārthataḥ tu jñānāt adarśanam nivartate tasmin nivṛtte na santi uttare kleśāḥ kleśa abhāvāt karma
vipāka abhāvaḥ carita adhikārāḥ ca etasyām avasthāyām guṇāḥ na puruṣasya punar dṛśya tvena upatiṣṭhante
tatpuruṣasya kaivalyam tadā puruṣaḥ sva rūpa mātra jyotiḥ amalaḥ kevalī bhavati iti śrī pātañjale
sāṃkhyapravacane yogaśāstre vyāsa bhāṣye vibhūti pādaḥ tṛtīyaḥ janma auṣadhi mantra tapaḥ samādhi jāḥ
siddhayaḥ deha antaritā janmanā siddhiḥ oṣadhibhiḥ asura bhavaneṣu rasāyanena iti evamādiḥ tapasā saṃkalpa
siddhiḥ kāmarūpī yatra tatra kāma gaḥ iti evamādi samādhi jāḥ siddhayaḥ vyākhyātāḥ tatra kāya indriyāṇām
anya jātīya pariṇatānām jāti antara pariṇāmaḥ prakṛti āpūrāt pūrva pariṇāma apāye uttara pariṇāma upajanaḥ
teṣām apūrva avayava anupraveśāt bhavati kāya indriya prakṛtayaḥ ca svam svam vikāram anugṛhṇanti āpūreṇa
dharma ādi nimittam apekṣamāṇāḥ iti nimittam aprayojakam prakṛtīnām varaṇa bhedaḥ tu tatas kṣetrika vat na
hi dharma ādi nimittam tad prayojakam prakṛtīnām bhavati na kāryeṇa kāraṇam pravartyate iti katham tarhi
varaṇa bhedaḥ tu tatas kṣetrika vat yathā kṣetrikaḥ kedārāt apām pūrṇāt kedāra antaram piplāvayiṣuḥ samam
nimnam nimnataram vā na apaḥ pāṇinā apakarṣati āvaraṇam tu āsām bhinatti tasmin bhinne svayam eva apaḥ
kedāra antaram āplāvayanti tathā dharmaḥ prakṛtīnām āvaraṇa dharmam bhinatti tasmin bhinne svayam eva
prakṛtayaḥ svam svam vikāram āplāvayanti yathā vā saḥ eva kṣetrikaḥ tasmin eva kedāre na prabhavati
audakān bhaumān vā rasān dhānya mūlāni anupraveśayitum kim tarhi mudga gavedhuka śyāmāka ādīn upakarṣati
apakṛṣṭeṣu teṣu svayam eva rasāḥ dhānya mūlāni anupraviśanti tathā dharmaḥ nivṛtti mātre kāraṇam
adharmasya śuddhi aśuddhyoḥ atyanta virodhāt na tu prakṛti pravṛttau dharmaḥ hetuḥ bhavati iti atra
nandīśvara ādayaḥ udāhāryāḥ viparyayeṇa api adharmaḥ dharmam bādhate tatas ca aśuddhi pariṇāmaḥ iti tatra
api nahuṣa ajagara ādayaḥ udāhāryāḥ yadā tu yogī bahūn kāyān nirmimīte tadā kim eka manaskāḥ te bhavanti
atha aneka manaskāḥ iti nirmāṇa cittāni asmitā mātrāt asmitā mātram citta kāraṇam upādāya nirmāṇa cittāni
karoti tatas sa cittāni bhavanti iti pravṛtti bhede prayojakam cittam ekam anekeṣām bahūnām cittānām
katham eka citta abhiprāya puraḥsarā pravṛttiḥ iti sarva cittānām prayojakam cittam nirmimīte tatas
pravṛtti bhedaḥ tatra dhyāna jam anāśayam pañcavidham nirmāṇa cittam janma auṣadhi mantra tapaḥ samādhi
jāḥ siddhayaḥ iti tatra yat eva dhyāna jam cittam tat eva anāśayam tasya eva na asti āśayaḥ rāga ādi
pravṛttiḥ na atas puṇya pāpa abhisaṃbandhaḥ kṣīṇa kleśa tvāt yoginaḥ iti itareṣām tu vidyate karma āśayaḥ
yatas karma a śukla a kṛṣṇam yoginaḥ trividham itareṣām catuṣpadī khalu iyam karma jātiḥ kṛṣṇā śukla kṛṣṇā
śukla a śukla a kṛṣṇā ca iti tatra kṛṣṇā durātmanām śukla kṛṣṇā bahis sādhana sādhyā tatra para pīḍā
anugraha dvāreṇa eva karma āśaya pracayaḥ śuklā tapaḥ svādhyāya dhyānavatām sā hi kevale manasi āyatta
tvāt abahir sādhana adhīnā na parān pīḍayitvā bhavati aśukla akṛṣṇā saṃnyāsinām kṣīṇa kleśānām carama
dehānām iti tatra aśuklam yoginaḥ eva phala saṃnyāsāt akṛṣṇam ca anupādānāt itareṣām tu bhūtānām pūrvam
eva trividham iti tatas tad vipāka anuguṇānām evā abhivyaktiḥ vāsanānām tatas iti trividhāt karmaṇaḥ tad
vipāka anuguṇānām eva iti yad jātīyasya karmaṇaḥ yaḥ vipākaḥ tasya anuguṇāḥ yāḥ vāsanāḥ karma vipākam
anuśerate tāsām eva abhivyaktiḥ na hi daivam karma vipacyamānam nāraka tiryak manuṣya vāsanā abhivyakti
nimittam saṃbhavati kiṃtu daiva anuguṇāḥ eva asya vāsanāḥ vyajyante nāraka tiryak manuṣyeṣu ca evam
samānaḥ carcaḥ jāti deśa kāla vyavahitānām api ānantaryam smṛti saṃskārayoḥ eka rūpa tvāt sa yadi jāti
śatena vā dūra deśa tayā vā kalpa śatena vā vyavahitaḥ punar ca sva vyañjaka añjanaḥ evā udiyāt drāk iti
evam pūrva anubhūta vṛṣadaṃśa vipāka abhisaṃskṛtā vāsanā upādāya vyajyeta kasmāt yatas vyavahitānām api
āsām sadṛśam karma abhivyajñakam nimittībhūtam iti ānantaryam eva kutas ca smṛti saṃskārayoḥ eka rūpa tvāt
yathā anubhavāḥ tathā saṃskārāḥ te ca karma vāsanā anurūpāḥ yathā ca vāsanāḥ tathā smṛtiḥ iti jāti deśa
kāla vyavahitebhyaḥ saṃskārebhyaḥ smṛtiḥ smṛteḥ ca punar saṃskārāḥ iti evam ete smṛti saṃskārāḥ karma
āśaya vṛttilābha vaśāt vyajyante atas ca vyavahitānām api nimitta naimittika bhāva anucchedāt ānantaryam
eva siddham iti tāsām anādi tvam ca āśiṣaḥ nitya tvāt tāsām vāsanānām āśiṣaḥ nitya tvāt anādi tvam yā iyam
ātma āśīḥ mā na bhūvam bhūyāsam iti sarvasya dṛśyate sā na svābhāvikī kasmāt jāta mātrasya jantoḥ
ananubhūta maraṇa dharmakasya dveṣa duḥkha anusmṛti nimittaḥ maraṇa trāsaḥ katham bhavet na ca svābhāvikam
vastu nimittam upādatte tasmāt anādi vāsanā anuviddham idam cittam nimitta vaśāt kāścid eva vāsanāḥ
pratilabhya puruṣasya bhogāya upāvartate iti ghaṭa prāsāda pradīpa kalpam saṃkoca vikāsi cittam śarīra
parimāṇa ākāra mātram iti apare pratipannāḥ tathā ca antara abhāvaḥ saṃsāraḥ ca yuktaḥ iti vṛttiḥ eva asya
vibhunaḥ cittasya saṃkoca vikāsinī iti ācāryaḥ tat ca dharma ādi nimitta apekṣam nimittam ca dvividham
bāhyam ādhyātmikam ca śarīra ādi sādhana apekṣam bāhyam stuti dāna abhivādana ādi cittamātra adhīnam
śraddhā ādi adhyātmikam tathā ca uktam ye ca ete maitrī ādayaḥ dhyāyinām vihārāḥ te bāhya sādhana
niranugraha ātmānaḥ prakṛṣṭam dharmam abhinirvartayanti tayoḥ mānasam balīyaḥ katham jñāna vairāgye kena
atiśayyete daṇḍaka araṇyam ca citta bala vyatirekeṇa śārīreṇa karmaṇā śūnyam kaḥ kartum utsaheta samudram
agastya vat vā pibet hetu phala āśraya ālambanaiḥ saṃgṛhīta tvāt eṣām abhāve tad abhāvaḥ hetuḥ dharmāt
sukham adharmāt duḥkham sukhāt rāgaḥ duḥkhāt dveṣaḥ tatas ca prayatnaḥ tena manasā vācā kāyena vā
parispandamānaḥ param anugṛhṇāti upahanti vā tatas punar dharma adharmau sukha duḥkhe rāga dveṣau iti
pravṛttam idam ṣaṣ aram saṃsāra cakram asya ca pratikṣaṇam āvartamānasya avidyā netrī mūlam sarva kleśānām
iti eṣa hetuḥ phalam tu yam āśritya yasya pratyutpanna tā dharma ādeḥ na hi apūrva upajanaḥ manaḥ tu sa
adhikāram āśrayaḥ vāsanānām na hi avasita adhikāre manasi nirāśrayāḥ vāsanāḥ sthātum utsahante yat
abhimukhībhūtam vastu yām vāsanām vyanakti tasyāḥ tat ālambanam evam hetu phala āśraya ālambanaiḥ etaiḥ
saṃgṛhītāḥ sarvāḥ vāsanāḥ eṣām abhāve tad saṃśrayāṇām api vāsanānām abhāvaḥ na asti asataḥ saṃbhavaḥ na ca
asti sataḥ vināśaḥ iti dravya tvena saṃbhavantyaḥ katham nivartiṣyante vāsanāḥ iti atīta anāgatam
svarūpataḥ sti adhva bhedāt dharmāṇām bhaviṣyat vyaktikam anāgatam anubhūta vyaktikam atītam sva vyāpāra
upārūḍham vartamānam trayam ca etat vastu jñānasya jñeyam yadi ca etat svarūpataḥ na abhaviṣyat na idam
nirviṣayam jñānam udapatsyata tasmāt atīta anāgatam svarūpataḥ sti iti kiṃca bhoga bhāgīyasya vā apavarga
bhāgīyasya vā karmaṇaḥ phalam utpitsu yadi nirupākhyam iti tad uddeśena tena nimittena kuśala anuṣṭhānam
na yujyeta sataḥ ca phalasya nimittam vartamānīkaraṇe samartham na apūrva upajanane siddham nimittam
naimittikasya viśeṣa anugrahaṇam kurute na apūrvam utpādayati iti dharmī ca aneka dharma svabhāvaḥ tasya
ca adhva bhedena dharmāḥ pratyavasthitāḥ na ca yathā vartamānam vyakti viśeṣa āpannam evam atītam anāgatam
ca katham tarhi svena eva vyaṅgyena svarūpeṇa anāgatam asti svena ca anubhūta vyaktikena sva rūpeṇa atītam
iti vartamānasya evā adhvanaḥ sva rūpa vyaktiḥ iti na sā bhavati atīta anāgatayoḥ adhvanoḥ ekasya ca
adhvanaḥ samaye dvau adhvanau dharmi samanvāgatau bhavataḥ eva iti na a bhūtvā bhāvaḥ trayāṇām adhvanām
iti te vyakta sūkṣmāḥ guṇa ātmānaḥ te khalu amī tri adhvanaḥ dharmāḥ vartamānāḥ vyakta ātmānaḥ tīta
anāgatāḥ sūkṣma ātmānaḥ ṣaṣ a viśeṣa rūpāḥ sarvam idam guṇānām saṃniveśa viśeṣa mātram iti paramārthataḥ
guṇa ātmānaḥ tathā ca śāstra anuśāsanam guṇānām paramam rūpam na dṛṣṭi patham ṛcchati yat tu dṛṣṭi patham
prāptam tat māyā iva su tucchakam iti yadā tu sarve guṇāḥ katham ekaḥ śabdaḥ ekam indriyam iti pariṇāma
eka tvāt vastu tattvam prakhyā kriyā sthiti śīlānām guṇānām grahaṇa ātmakānām karaṇa bhāvena ekaḥ
pariṇāmaḥ śrotram indriyam grāhya ātmakānām śabda tanmātra bhāvena ekaḥ pariṇāmaḥ śabdaḥ viṣayaḥ iti śabda
ādīnām mūrti samāna jātīyānām ekaḥ pariṇāmaḥ pṛthivī paramāṇuḥ tanmātra avayavaḥ teṣām ca ekaḥ pariṇāmaḥ
pṛthivī gauḥ vṛkṣaḥ parvataḥ iti evamādiḥ bhūta antareṣu api sneha auṣṇya praṇāmi tva avakāśa dānāni
upādāya na asti arthaḥ vijñāna visahacaraḥ asti tu jñānam artha visahacaram svapna ādau kalpitam iti anayā
diśā ye vastu svarūpam apahnuvate jñāna parikalpanā mātram vastu svapna viṣaya upamam na paramārthataḥ sti
iti ye āhuḥ te tathā iti pratyupasthitam idam sva māhātmyena vastu katham apramāṇa ātmakena vikalpa jñāna
balena vastu svarūpam utsṛjya tat eva apalapantaḥ śraddheya vacanāḥ syuḥ kutas ca etat anyāyyam vastu
sāmye citta bhedāt tayoḥ vibhaktaḥ panthāḥ bahu citta ālambanībhūtam ekam vastu sādhāraṇam tat khalu na
eka citta parikalpitam na api aneka citta parikalpitam kiṃtu sva pratiṣṭham katham vastu sāmye citta
bhedāt dharma apekṣam cittasya vastu sāmye sukha jñānam bhavati adharma apekṣam tatas eva duḥkha jñānam
avidyā apekṣam tatas eva mūḍha jñānam samyak darśana apekṣam tatas eva mādhyasthya jñānam iti kasya tat
cittena parikalpitam na ca anya citta parikalpitena arthena anyasya citta uparāgaḥ yuktaḥ tasmāt vastu
jñānayoḥ grāhya grahaṇa bheda bhinnayoḥ vibhaktaḥ panthāḥ na anayoḥ saṃkara gandhaḥ u pi asti iti sāṃkhya
pakṣe punar vastu triguṇam calam ca guṇa vṛttam iti dharma ādi nimitta apekṣam cittaiḥ abhisaṃbadhyate
nimitta anurūpasya ca pratyayasya utpadyamānasya tena tena ātmanā hetuḥ bhavati kecid āhuḥ eva arthaḥ
bhogya tvāt sukha ādi vat iti te etayā dvārā sādhāraṇa tvam bādhamānāḥ pūrva uttara kṣaṇeṣu vastu rūpam
eva apahnuvate na ca eka citta tantram vastu tat apramāṇakam tadā kim syāt eka citta tantram ced vastu
syāt tadā citte vyagre niruddhe vā a svarūpam eva tena aparāmṛṣṭam anyasya aviṣayībhūtam apramāṇakam
agṛhīta svabhāvakam kenacid tadānīm kim tat syāt saṃbadhyamānam ca punar cittena kutas utpadyeta ye ca
asya anupasthitāḥ bhāgāḥ te ca asya na syuḥ evam na asti pṛṣṭham iti udaram api na gṛhyeta tasmāt
svatantraḥ ṛthaḥ sarva puruṣa sādhāraṇaḥ svatantrāṇi ca cittāni prati puruṣam pravartante tayoḥ saṃbandhāt
upalabdhiḥ puruṣasya bhogaḥ iti tad uparāga apekṣi tvāt cittasya vastu jñāta ajñātam ayaskānta maṇi kalpāḥ
viṣayāḥ ayaḥ sadharmakam cittam abhisaṃbandhya uparañjayanti yena ca viṣayeṇa uparaktam cittam sa viṣayaḥ
jñātaḥ tatas nyaḥ punar ajñātaḥ vastunaḥ jñāta ajñāta sva rūpa tvāt pariṇāmi cittam yasya tu tat eva
cittam viṣayaḥ tasya sadā jñātāḥ citta vṛttayaḥ tad prabhoḥ puruṣasya apariṇāmi tvāt yadi citta vat
prabhuḥ api puruṣaḥ pariṇamet tatas tad viṣayāḥ citta vṛttayaḥ śabda ādi viṣaya vat jñāta ajñātāḥ syuḥ
sadā ajñāta tvam tu manasaḥ tad prabhoḥ puruṣasya apariṇāmi tvam anumāpayati syāt āśaṅkā cittam eva su
ābhāsam viṣaya ābhāsam ca bhaviṣyati iti agni vat na tat su ābhāsam dṛśya tvāt yathā itarāṇi indriyāṇi
śabda ādayaḥ ca dṛśya tvāt na su ābhāsāni tathā manaḥ pi pratyetavyam na ca agniḥ atra dṛṣṭāntaḥ na hi
agniḥ ātma svarūpam aprakāśam prakāśayati prakāśaḥ ca ayam prakāśya prakāśaka saṃyoge dṛṣṭaḥ na ca sva
rūpa mātre sti saṃyogaḥ kiṃca su ābhāsam cittam iti agrāhyam eva kasyacid iti śabda arthaḥ tat yathā sva
ātma pratiṣṭham ākāśam na para pratiṣṭham iti arthaḥ sva buddhi pracāra pratisaṃvedanāt sattvānām
pravṛttiḥ dṛśyate kruddhaḥ ham bhītaḥ ham amutra me rāgaḥ mutra me krodhaḥ iti etat sva buddheḥ a grahaṇe
na yuktam iti eka samaye ca ubhaya anavadhāraṇam na ca ekasmin kṣaṇe sva para rūpa avadhāraṇam yuktam
kṣaṇika vādinaḥ yat bhavanam sā eva kriyā tat eva ca kārakam iti abhyupagamaḥ syāt matiḥ sva rasa
niruddham cittam citta antareṇa samanantareṇa gṛhyate iti citta antara dṛśye buddhi buddheḥ atiprasaṅgaḥ
smṛti saṃkaraḥ ca atha cittam ced citta antareṇa gṛhyeta buddhi buddhiḥ kena gṛhyate sā api anyayā sā api
anyayā iti atiprasaṅgaḥ smṛti saṃkaraḥ ca yāvantaḥ buddhi buddhīnām anubhavāḥ tāvatyaḥ smṛtayaḥ
prāpnuvanti tad saṃkarāt ca eka smṛti anavadhāraṇam ca syāt iti evam buddhi pratisaṃvedinam puruṣam
apalapadbhiḥ vaināśikaiḥ sarvam eva ākulīkṛtam te tu bhoktṛ svarūpam yatra kvacana kalpayantaḥ na nyāyena
saṃgacchante kecid tu sattva mātram api parikalpya asti sa sattvaḥ yaḥ etān pañca skandhān nikṣipya anyān
ca pratisaṃdadhāti iti uktvā tatas eva punar trasyanti tathā skandhānām mahat nirvedāya virāgāya
anutpādāya praśāntaye guroḥ antike brahmacaryam cariṣyāmi iti uktvā sattvasya punar sattvam eva apahnuvate
sāṃkhya yoga ādayaḥ tu pravādāḥ sva śabdena puruṣam eva svāminam cittasya bhoktāram upayanti iti katham
citeḥ a pratisaṃkramāyāḥ tad ākāra āpattau sva buddhi saṃvedanam apariṇāminī hi bhoktṛ śaktiḥ
apratisaṃkramā ca pariṇāminī arthe pratisaṃkrāntā iva tad vṛttim anupatati tasyāḥ ca prāpta caitanya
upagraha svarūpāyāḥ buddhi vṛtteḥ anukāri mātra tayā buddhi vṛtti aviśiṣṭā hi jñāna vṛttiḥ ākhyāyate tathā
ca uktam na pātālam na ca vivaram girīṇām na eva andhakāram kukṣayaḥ na udadhīnām guhā yasyām nihitam
brahma śāśvatam buddhi vṛttim a viśiṣṭām kavayaḥ vedayante iti atas ca etat abhyupagamyate draṣṭṛ dṛśya
uparaktam cittam sarva artham manaḥ hi mantavyena arthena uparaktam tat svayam ca viṣaya tvāt viṣayiṇā
puruṣeṇa ātmīyayā vṛttyā abhisaṃbaddham tat etat cittam eva draṣṭṛ dṛśya uparaktam viṣaya viṣayi nirbhāsam
cetana acetana svarūpa āpannam viṣaya ātmakam api a viṣaya ātmakam iva acetanam cetanam iva sphaṭika maṇi
kalpam sarva artham iti ucyate tat anena citta sārūpyeṇa bhrāntāḥ kecid tat eva cetanam iti āhuḥ apare
cittamātram eva idam sarvam na asti khalu ayam gavādiḥ ghaṭa ādiḥ ca sa kāraṇaḥ lokaḥ iti anukampanīyāḥ te
kasmāt asti hi teṣām bhrānti bījam sarva rūpa ākāra nirbhāsam cittam iti samādhi prajñāyām prajñeyaḥ rthaḥ
pratibimbībhūtaḥ tasya ālambanībhūta tvāt anyaḥ sa ced arthaḥ cittamātram syāt katham prajñayā eva prajñā
rūpam avadhāryeta tasmāt pratibimbībhūtaḥ rthaḥ prajñāyām yena avadhāryate sa puruṣaḥ iti evam grahītṛ
grahaṇa grāhya svarūpa citta bhedāt trayam api etat jātitaḥ pravibhajante te samyak darśinaḥ taiḥ
adhigataḥ puruṣaḥ kutas ca tat asaṃkhyeya vāsanābhiḥ citram api para artham saṃhatya kāri tvāt tat etat
cittam asaṃkhyeyābhiḥ vāsanābhiḥ eva citrīkṛtam api para artham parasya bhoga apavarga artham na sva
artham saṃhatya kāri tvāt gṛha vat saṃhatya kāriṇā cittena na sva arthena bhavitavyam na sukha cittam
sukha artham na jñānam jñāna artham ubhayam api etat para artham yaḥ ca bhogena apavargeṇa ca arthena
arthavān puruṣaḥ saḥ eva paraḥ na paraḥ sāmānya mātram yat tu kiṃcid param sāmānya mātram sva rūpeṇa
udāharet vaināśikaḥ tat sarvam saṃhatya kāri tvāt para artham eva syāt yaḥ tu asau paraḥ viśeṣaḥ sa na
saṃhatya kārī puruṣaḥ iti yathā prāvṛṣi tṛṇa aṅkurasya udbhedena tad bīja sattā anumīyate tathā mokṣa
mārga śravaṇena yasya roma harṣa aśru pātau dṛśyete tatra api asti viśeṣa darśana bījam apavarga bhāgīyam
karma abhinirvartitam iti anumīyate tasya ātma bhāva bhāvanā svābhāvikī pravartate yasya abhāvāt idam
uktam svabhāvam muktvā doṣāt yeṣām pūrvapakṣe ruciḥ bhavati aruciḥ ca nirṇaye bhavati tatra ātma bhāva
bhāvanā kaḥ ham āsam katham aham āsam kim svid idam katham svid idam ke bhaviṣyāmaḥ katham vā bhaviṣyāmaḥ
iti sā tu viśeṣa darśinaḥ nivartate kutas cittasya eva eṣa vicitraḥ pariṇāmaḥ puruṣaḥ tu asatyām avidyāyām
śuddhaḥ citta dharmaiḥ aparāmṛṣṭaḥ iti tatas sya ātmabhāva bhāvanā kuśalasya nivartate iti tadā viveka
nimnam kaivalya prāgbhāram cittam tadānīm yat asya cittam viṣaya prāgbhāram ajñāna nimnam āsīt tat asya
anyathā bhavati kaivalya prāgbhāram viveka ja jñāna nimnam iti tad chidreṣu pratyaya antarāṇi
saṃskārebhyaḥ pratyaya viveka nimnasya sattva puruṣa anya tā khyāti mātra pravāhiṇaḥ cittasya tad chidreṣu
pratyaya antarāṇi asmi iti vā mama iti vā jānāmi iti vā na jānāmi iti vā kutas kṣīyamāṇa bījebhyaḥ pūrva
saṃskārebhyaḥ iti hānam eṣām kleśa vat uktam yathā kleśāḥ dagdha bīja bhāvāḥ na praroha samarthāḥ bhavanti
tathā jñāna agninā dagdha bīja bhāvaḥ pūrva saṃskāraḥ na pratyaya prasūḥ bhavati jñāna saṃskārāḥ tu citta
adhikāra samāptim anuśerate iti na cintyante prasaṃkhyāne pi akusīdasya sarvathā viveka khyāteḥ
dharmameghaḥ samādhiḥ yadā ayam brāhmaṇaḥ prasaṃkhyāne a kusīdaḥ na kiṃcid prārthayate tatra api
viraktasya sarvathā viveka khyātiḥ eva bhavati iti saṃskāra bīja kṣayāt na asya pratyaya antarāṇi
utpadyante tadā asya dharmameghaḥ nāma samādhiḥ bhavati tatas kleśa karma nivṛttiḥ tad lābhāt avidyā
ādayaḥ kleśāḥ sa mūla kāṣam kaṣitāḥ bhavanti kuśala akuśalāḥ ca karma āśayāḥ sa mūla ghātam hatāḥ bhavanti
kleśa karma nivṛttau jīvan eva vidvān vimuktaḥ bhavati kasmāt yasmāt viparyayaḥ bhavasya kāraṇam na hi
kṣīṇa viparyayaḥ kaścid kenacid kvacid jātaḥ dṛśyate iti tadā sarva āvaraṇa mala apetasya jñānasya
ānantyāt jñeyam alpam sarvaiḥ kleśa karma āvaraṇaiḥ vimuktasya jñānasya ānantyam bhavati āvarakeṇa tamasā
abhibhūtam āvṛtam anantam jñāna sattvam kvacid eva rajasā pravartitam udghāṭitam grahaṇa samartham bhavati
tatra yadā sarvaiḥ āvaraṇa malaiḥ apagatam bhavati tadā bhavati asya ānantyam jñānasya ānantyāt jñeyam
alpam saṃpadyate yathā ākāśe khadyotaḥ yatra idam uktam andhaḥ maṇim avidhyat tam anaṅguliḥ āvayat agrīvaḥ
tam pratyamuñcat tam ajihvaḥ bhyapūjayat iti tatas kṛtārthānām pariṇāma krama samāptiḥ guṇānām tasya
dharma meghasya udayāt kṛtārthānām guṇānām pariṇāma kramaḥ parisamāpyate na hi kṛta bhoga apavargāḥ
parisamāpta kramāḥ kṣaṇam api avasthātum utsahante atha kaḥ yam kramaḥ nāma iti kṣaṇa pratiyogī pariṇāma
aparānta nirgrāhyaḥ kramaḥ kṣaṇa ānantarya ātmā pariṇāmasya apara antena avasānena gṛhyate kramaḥ na hi an
anubhūta krama kṣaṇā purāṇa tā vastrasya ante bhavati nityeṣu ca kramaḥ dṛṣṭaḥ dvayī ca iyam nityatā
kūṭastha nityatā pariṇāmi nityatā ca tatra kūṭastha nitya tā puruṣasya pariṇāmi nitya tā guṇānām yasmin
pariṇamyamāne tattvam na vihanyate tat nityam ubhayasya ca tattva anabhighātāt nitya tvam tatra guṇa
dharmeṣu buddhi ādiṣu pariṇāma apara anta nirgrāhyaḥ kramaḥ labdha paryavasānaḥ nityeṣu dharmiṣu guṇeṣu
alabdha paryavasānaḥ kūṭastha nityeṣu svarūpa mātra pratiṣṭheṣu mukta puruṣeṣu svarūpa astitā krameṇa eva
anubhūyate iti tatra api alabdha paryavasānaḥ śabda pṛṣṭhena astikriyām upādāya kalpitaḥ iti atha asya
saṃsārasya sthityā gatyā ca guṇeṣu vartamānasya asti krama samāptiḥ na vā iti avacanīyam etat katham asti
praśnaḥ ekānta vacanīyaḥ sarvaḥ jātaḥ mariṣyati iti om bhoḥ iti atha sarvaḥ mṛtvā janiṣyate iti vibhajya
vacanīyam etat pratyudita khyātiḥ kṣīṇa tṛṣṇaḥ kuśalaḥ na janiṣyate itaraḥ tu janiṣyate tathā manuṣya
jātiḥ śreyasī na vā śreyasī iti evam paripṛṣṭe vibhajya vacanīyaḥ praśnaḥ paśūn adhikṛtya śreyasī devān
ṛṣīn ca adhikṛtya na iti ayam tu avacanīyaḥ praśnaḥ saṃsāraḥ yam antavān atha anantaḥ iti kuśalasya asti
saṃsāra krama samāptiḥ na itarasya iti anyatara avadhāraṇe doṣaḥ tasmāt vyākaraṇīyaḥ eva ayam praśnaḥ iti
guṇa adhikāra krama samāptau kaivalyam uktam tad svarūpam avadhāryate puruṣa artha śūnyānām guṇānām
pratiprasavaḥ kaivalyam svarūpa pratiṣṭhā vā citi śaktiḥ iti kṛta bhoga apavargāṇām puruṣa artha śūnyānām
yaḥ pratiprasavaḥ kārya kāraṇa ātmakānām guṇānām tat kaivalyam svarūpa pratiṣṭhā punar buddhi sattva
anabhisaṃbandhāt puruṣasya citi śaktiḥ eva kevalā tasyāḥ sadā tathā eva avasthānam kaivalyam iti iti śrī
pātañjale sāṃkhyapravacane yogaśāstre vyāsa bhāṣye kaivalya pādaḥ caturthaḥ
