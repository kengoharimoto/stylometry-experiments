yūyam teṣu ca eva ekaḥ sitaḥ proce viśālakam ayam pitāmahaḥ kṛṣṇaḥ ṛṣayaḥ yena ghātitāḥ avīci narakam prāptau muktau tvad piṇḍa dānataḥ pitṝn pitāmahān ca eva tathā eva prapitāmahān prīṇayāmi iti yat toyam tvayā dattam arindama evam putraiḥ pitṝṇām ca kartavyā muktiḥ uttamā tvam ca rājyam ciram kṛtvā bhuktvā bhogān ca durllabhān yajñān sa dakṣiṇān kṛtvā yāyāt viṣṇupuram tatas evam labdha varaḥ rājā rājyam kṛtvā divaṃgataḥ pretarājaḥ saha pretaiḥ gayāśrāddhāt divam gataḥ pretaḥ kaścid vimukti artham vaṇijam kañcid abravīt mama nāmnā gayāśīrṣe piṇḍa nirvvāpaṇam kuru preta bhāva vimukti artham tvam gṛhāṇa dhanam mama tat vanam sarvvam ādāya gayā śrāddha vyayam kuru ṣoḍaśa pañca bhāgān ca tubhyam vai dattavān aham sva nāmāni yathānyāyam samyak ākhyātavān aham gatvā gayām gayāśīrṣa pretarājāya piṇḍakam evam gayasya śambhoḥ ca kṣetram viṣṇoḥ raveḥ tathā upoṣitaḥ atha gāyatrītīrthe mahā nadī sthite gāyatryāḥ puratas snātvā prātar sandhyām upāsayet śrāddham sapiṇḍakam kṛtvā ānayet brahmaṇya tām kulam tīrthe samudite snātvā sāvitryāḥ puratas naraḥ piṇḍa dānam tatas kuryyāt pitṝṇām mukti kāmyayā prācīsarasvatī tīrthe snātvā ca api yathāvidhi sandhyām upāsya sāyāhne viṣṇu lokam nayet pitṝn viśālāyām lelihāne tīrthe ca bharatāśrame pāda aṅkite muṇḍa pṛṣṭe gadādhara samīpatas tīrthe ca ākāśagaṅgāyām girikarṇa mukheṣu ca snātaḥ atha piṇḍa daḥ brahma lokam kula śatam nayet devanadyām vaitaraṇyām snātaḥ svargam nayet pitṝn satyam satyam punar satyam vaitaraṇyām tu nārada ekaviṃśati kulāni āhuḥ tārayet na atra saṃśayaḥ yā sā vaitaraṇī nāma nadī trailokya viśrutā sā avatīrṇā gayā kṣetre pitṝṇām taraṇāya vai godāvaryām vaitaraṇyām yamunāyām tathā eva ca devanadyām gopracāre śrāddha daḥ svar nayet pitṝn puṣkariṇyām ghṛtakulyām madhukulyām tathā eva ca koṭitīrthe rukmiṇīye piṇḍa daḥ svar nayet pitṝn a dattvā kāñcanaṅgāḥ ca daridraḥ jāyate naraḥ ghṛtakulyā madhukulyā devikā ca mahā nadī śilāyāḥ saṅgamaḥ yatra madhusravā prakīrtitā ayutam ca aśvamedhānām snāna kṛt labhate naraḥ śrāddham sapiṇḍakam kṛtvā piṇḍa dānam tathā eva ca kulānām śatam uddhṛtya viṣṇu lokam nayet naraḥ daśā ca medhike haṃsatīrthe cāmarakaṅkaṭe koṭitīrthe rukmakuṇḍe piṇḍa daḥ svar nayet pitṝn vaitaraṇyām ghṛta kulyām madhu kulyām tathā eva ca koṭitīrthe naraḥ snātvā dṛṣṭvā koṭīśvaram ca yaḥ mārkaṇḍeya īśa kocīśau natvā syāt pitṛ tārakaḥ rukma pārijāta vane pārvvatyā saha śaṅkaraḥ marīciḥ phala puṣpa artham pārijāta vanam gataḥ dṛṣṭvā śaptaḥ maheśena yasmāt sukha vighātakaḥ duḥkhī bhava iti tad bhītaḥ mariciḥ tuṣṭuve śivam tuṣṭaḥ provāca tam śambhuḥ vṛṇīṣva varam uttamam śāpāt bhavatu muktiḥ me marīciḥ prāha śaṅkaram śilā sthitaḥ tapaḥ tepe sarvveṣām duṣkaram ca yat marīciḥ īśvarāt śaptaḥ kṛṣṇa tvam agamat purā tapasā dāruṇena iha sa vipraḥ śukla tām gataḥ hariḥ ūce marīcin ca varam vṛṇuhi putraka kim alabhyam tvayi tuṣṭe marīciḥ prāha mādhavam hara śāpāt vimuktaḥ aham śilā bhavatu pāvanī divaukasām puṣkariṇīm samāsādya naraḥ śuciḥ tatra snātaḥ divam yāti sva śarīreṇa mānavaḥ pāpmānam prajahāti eṣa jīrṇa tvacam iva uragaḥ tat paṅgaja vanam puṇyam puṇya kṛdbhiḥ niṣevitam pāṇḍuśilā vai tatra āste śrāddham yatra akṣayam bhavet yudhiṣṭiraḥ tu tasyām hi śrāddham karttum yayau mune tatra kāle pāṇḍunā uktam mad haste dehi piṇḍakam hastam tyaktvā śilāyām ca piṇḍa dānan cakāra saḥ śilāyām piṇḍa dānena prahṛṣṭaḥ vyāsa nandanaḥ sampūrṇam tvam me trātā hi putraka svargam vraja śarīreṇa bhrātṛbhiḥ parivāritaḥ dṛṣṭi mātreṇa sampūtān naraka sthān divam naya iti uktvā prayayau pāṇḍuḥ śāśvatam padam avyayam udbhid jāḥ sveda jāḥ vā api hi aṇḍa jāḥ ye jarāyu jāḥ madhusravām samāsādya mṛtāḥ svargapuram yayuḥ daśāśvamedhike haṃsatīrthe śrāddhāt divam vrajet daśāśvamedha haṃsau ca natvā śiva puram vrajet bharatasya āśrame śrāddhān nayet brahmālayam pitṝn nirmmathya agnim śamīgarbhe vidhiḥ viṣṇu ādibhiḥ saha lebhe tīrtham tu yajña artham triṣu lokeṣu viśrutam makha saṃjñam tu tat tīrtham pitṝṇām mukti dāyakam snātvā ca tarpaṇam kṛtvā piṇḍa daḥ muktim āpnuyāt pitṝn svargam nayet natvā saṅgame aṅgāraka īśvarau gayākūṭe piṇḍa dānāt aśvamedha phalam labhet bhasmakūṭe bhasma nātham natvā ca tārayet pitṝn iṣṭim cakre aśvamedha ākhyam vasiṣṭaḥ muni sattamaḥ iṣṭitaḥ nirgataḥ śambhuḥ varam vṛṇu vasiṣṭhakam prāha iti tam vasiṣṭaḥ api śiva tuṣṭaḥ asi me yadi vastavyam ca atra deveśa tathā iti uktvā śivaḥ sthitaḥ piṇḍadaḥ dhenukā araṇye kāmadhenu padeṣu ca karddamāle gayā nābhau muṇḍapṛṣṭha samīpatas snātvā śrāddha ādikam kṛtvā pitṝṇām anṛṇaḥ bhavet phalgu caṇḍī śmaśānākṣī maṅgala ādyāḥ samarccayet gayāyām ca vṛṣotsarga atriḥ sapta kulam uddharet yatra tatra sthitāḥ devāḥ ṛṣayaḥ api jita indriyāḥ ādyam gadādharam dhyāyan śrāddha piṇḍa ādi dānataḥ kulānām śatam uddhṛtya brahma lokam nayet pitṝn gayā gayaḥ gayā dityaḥ gāyatrī ca gadādharaḥ gayāḥ gaya asuraḥ ca eva ṣaṅgayāḥ mukti dāyakāḥ gayā ākhyānam idam puṇyam yaḥ paṭet satatam naraḥ śrṛṇuyāt śraddhayā yaḥ tu sa yāti paramām gatim pāṭhayet vā gayā ākhyānam viprebhyaḥ puṇya kṛt naraḥ gayā śrāddham kṛtam tena kṛtam tena su niścitam gayāyāḥ mahimānam ca hi abhyaset yaḥ samāhitaḥ tena iṣṭam rājasūyena aśvamedhena nāradaḥ upākhyānam idam puṇyam gṛhe tiṣṭhati pustakam śrāddha kāle paṭet yaḥ tu gayāmāhātmyam uttamam vidhi hīnam tu tat sarvam pitṝṇām tu gayā samam yāni tīrthāni trailokye tāni dṛṣṭāni tatra vai yena jñātam gayā ākhyānam śrutam vā paṭitam mune sanatkumāraḥ muni puṅgavāya puṇyām kathām ca atha nivedya bhaktyā svam āśramam puṇya vanaiḥ upetam visṛjya saṃgīta gurum jagāma iti śrī mahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmahātmyam nāma pañcāśattamaḥ adhyāyaḥ
