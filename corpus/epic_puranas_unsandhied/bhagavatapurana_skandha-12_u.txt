nidrāloḥ kamaṭha ākṛteḥ bhagavataḥ śvāsa anilāḥ pāntu vaḥ yad saṃskāra kalā anuvartana vaśāt velā nibhena ambhasām yāta āyātam atandritam jala nidheḥ na adya api viśrāmyati purāṇa saṅkhyā sambhūtim mārkaṇḍam nava vāhnam ca daśa pañca catur śatam catur daśa bhaviṣyam syāt tathā pañca śatāni ca daśa aṣṭau brahma vaivartam laiṅgam ekādaśa eva tu catur viṃśati vārāham ekāśīti sahasrakam skāndam aṣṭadaśa sāhasram śrī bhāgavatam iṣyate idam bhagavatā pūrvam brahmaṇe nābhi paṅkaje sthitāya bhava bhītāya kāruṇyāt samprakāśitam ādi madhya avasāneṣu vairāgya ākhyāna saṃyutam hari līlā kathā vrāta
