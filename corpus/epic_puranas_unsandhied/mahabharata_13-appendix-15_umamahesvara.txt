bhavet bhāryā eteṣām yena kena cit dātāraḥ saṃvimṛśya eva dātum arhanti na anyathā uttamānām tu varṇānām mantravat pāṇi saṃgrahaḥ tadā prabhṛti sā nārī daśa rātram vivarjya ca manasā karmaṇā vācā hi anukūlā ca sā bhavet iti bhartṛ vratam kuryāt patim uddiśya śobhanā tadā prabhṛti sā nārī tat tat kuryāt pati priyam pativratā tvam nārīṇām etat eva samāsatas tādṛśā sā bhavet nityam yādṛśaḥ tu bhavet patiḥ śubha aśubha samācāre etat vṛttam samāsatas daivatam satatam sādhvī bhartāram yā anupaśyati etasmin kāraṇe deva paurāṇā śrūyate śrutiḥ kathayāmi prasādāt te śṛṇu deva samāhitaḥ tayoḥ ekā dharma kāmā devān uddiśya bhaktitaḥ cakāra vipulam dharmam pūjana arcanayā anvitam aparā dharma kāmā ca patim uddiśya śobhanā devapriyāyām tiṣṭhatyām puṇya lokam jagāma sā devapriyā ca tiṣṭhatī vilalāpa su duḥkhitā tām yamaḥ lokapālaḥ tu babhāṣe puṣkalam vacaḥ mā śocīḥ tvam nivartasva na lokāḥ santi te śubhe daivatam hi patiḥ nāryāḥ sthāpitam sarva daivataiḥ avamatya śubhe tam tvam katham lokān gamiṣyasi tadā gantāsi lokān tān yān gacchanti pativratāḥ na anyathā śakyate prāptum patnīnām lokaḥ uttamaḥ umā patimatyāḥ divārātram vṛttāntaḥ śrūyatām śubhaḥ patyuḥ pūrvam samutthāya prātaḥkarma samādiśet tatas paurvāhṇikam kāryam svayam kuryāt yathāvidhi nivedya ca tathā āhāram yathā saṃpadyatām iti śuśrūṣā poṣaṇa vidhau pati priya cikīrṣayā bhartuḥ niṣkramaṇe kāryam saṃsmaret apramādāt āgatam tu patim dṛṣṭvā sahasā paricārakaiḥ svayam kurvīta samprītyā kāya śrama haram param pādya āsanābhyām śayanaiḥ vākyaiḥ ca hṛdaya priyaiḥ atithīnām āgamane prīti yuktā sadā bhavet karmaṇā vacanena api toṣayet atithīn sadā maṅgalyam gṛha śaucam ca sarva upakaraṇāni ca sā yatnam paramam kuryāt tasya sāhāyya kāraṇāt yugaṃdharā bhavet sādhvī pateḥ dharma arthayoḥ sadā vihāra kāle vai bhartuḥ jñātvā bhāvam hṛdi sthitam alaṃkṛtya yathāyogam hāva bhāva samanvitā kaṭhorāṇi na vācyāni anyadā pramadā antare yasyām kāmī bhavet bhartā tasyāḥ prīti karā bhavet anantaram tathā anyeṣām bhojana avekṣaṇam caret dāsī dāsa balīvardān caṇḍālān ca śvnaḥ tathā anāthān kṛpaṇān ca eva bhikṣukān ca tathā eva ca kupitam vā artha hīnam vā śrāntam vā upacaret patim yathā sa tuṣṭaḥ svasthaḥ ca bhavet saṃtoṣayet patim tathā kuṭumba cintāyām vivāde ca artha saṃśaye ca sahāya artham tadā priya hitam vadet apriyam vā hitam brūyāt tasya kāma artha kāṅkṣayā bahis ālokanam ca eva moham vrīḍām ca paiśunam bahu āśi tvam divāsvapnam evamādīni varjayet rahasi eka āsanam sādhvī na kuryāt ātmajaiḥ api pratidadyāt atiyācñām atīrṣyām ca dūratas parivarjayet bāla vat vṛddha vat nāryāḥ sadā eva ātura vat patiḥ spṛhā yasyāḥ yathā patau sā nārīṇām pativratā patiḥ hi daivatam strīṇām patiḥ bandhuḥ patiḥ gatiḥ na anyām gatim aham paśye pramadānām yathā patiḥ matiḥ kāya kleśena mahatā puruṣaḥ prāpnuyāt phalam yathāsukham patimatī sarvam pati anukūlāt jīdṛśam dharma saukaryam paśyadhvam pramadāḥ prati tapaḥ caryā ca dānam ca patau tasyāḥ samarpitau rūpam kulam yaśaḥ svargaḥ sarvam tasmin pratiṣṭhitam evaṃvṛtta samācārā sva vṛttena eva śobhanā patinā ca samam gacchet puṇya lokān sva karmaṇā evaṃbhūtaḥ api bhartā vai strīṇām bhūṣaṇam uttamam āḍhyam vā rūpa yuktam vā virūpam dhana varjitam yā patim toṣayet sādhvī sā nārīṇām pativratā daridrān ca virūpān ca pramūḍhān kuṣṭha saṃyutān evaṃvidhān patīn deva toṣayitvā pativratā evam pravartamānāyāḥ patiḥ pūrvam mriyeta ced umā etat evam mayā proktam strīṇām dharma āśritam hitam saumya śīlāḥ śubha ācārāḥ sarvāḥ tāḥ saṃbhavanti hi atha aśubham pravakṣyāmi strīṇām vṛttam maheśvara āsuryaḥ ca eva paiśācyaḥ rākṣasyaḥ ca bhavanti yāḥ tāsām vṛttam aśeṣeṇa śrūyatām loka kāraṇāt nyāyāt vā anyathā proktāḥ bhāva doṣa samanvitāḥ bhartṝn apacaranti eva rāga dveṣa balātkṛtāḥ pravṛddha viṣayāḥ nityam pratikūlam vadanti ca sva buddhi niratāḥ bhūtvā jīvanti ca yathā tathā punar bhavanti pāpiṣṭhāḥ viṣamam vṛttam āśritāḥ anavasthita maryādāḥ bahu veṣāḥ vyaya priyāḥ asaṃtuṣṭāḥ ca lubdhāḥ ca īrṣyā krodha yutāḥ bhṛśam bhoga priyāḥ hita dveṣyāḥ kāma bhoga parāyaṇāḥ patīn paribhavanti eva pratikūla parāyaṇāḥ prāyaśas anṛta bhūyiṣṭhāḥ gurūṇām pratilomakāḥ evam yukta samācārāḥ āsuram bhāvam āśritāḥ aparāḥ pāpa kārinyaḥ satatam kalaha priyāḥ paruṣāḥ rūkṣa vacanāḥ nirghṛṇāḥ nirapatrapāḥ niḥsnehāḥ kopanāḥ ca eva bhartṛ putra sva bandhuṣu bandhubhiḥ bhartsitāḥ bhūtvā gṛha kāryāṇi kurvate atha vā bhartsitāḥ deva nīca vṛttāḥ sva bandhuṣu nirdayāḥ niranukrośāḥ kuṭumba artha vilopanāḥ dharma artha rahitāḥ ghorāḥ satatam kurvate kriyāḥ anartha nipuṇāḥ pāpāḥ para prāṇeṣu nirdayāḥ evam yukta samācārāḥ striyaḥ paiśācyam āśritāḥ aparāḥ moha saṃyuktāḥ nirlajjāḥ rodana priyāḥ aśuddhāḥ mala digdha aṅgāḥ pāna māṃsa ratāḥ bhṛśam duṣprasādāḥ mahā krodhāḥ svapna śīlāḥ nirantaram tāmasyaḥ naṣṭa tattva arthāḥ manda śīlāḥ mahā udarāḥ bhuñjanti vividham siddham bhojanam tīvra saṃbhramāḥ hitvā anyān eva gacchanti sarvathā bhṛśa nirlajjāḥ dharma saṃdigdhāḥ pratikūlāḥ samantataḥ evam yukta samācārāḥ striyaḥ rākṣasyam āśritāḥ evaṃvidhānām sarvāsām na paratra iha vā sukham etāsām eva ku strīṇām mṛtānām ca maheśvara asaṃśayam ciram kālam narake vāsaḥ iṣyate narakāt hi vimuktānām katham cit kāla paryayāt iṣyate janma mānuṣyam kaṣṭam tatra api bhuñjate tāsām duṣkṛta saṃyogāt duḥkham janma antareṣu api daridrāḥ kleśa bhūyiṣṭhāḥ virūpāḥ kutsitāḥ paraiḥ vidhavāḥ durbhagāḥ vā api labhante duḥkham īdṛśam śata varṣa sahasra antam ātmānam vyabhicāriṇī abhisaṃyānti tāḥ lokān puṇyān parama śobhanān avamatya tu yāḥ pūrvam patim duṣṭena cetasā mānāḥ ca satatam bhartṝṇām pratikūla tām bhartrā anumaraṇam kāle yāḥ kurvanti tathāvidhāḥ kāmāt krodhāt bhayāt mohāt apahāsyāḥ bhavanti tāḥ ādi prabhṛti ku strīṇām tathā anumaraṇam vṛthak ādi prabhṛti yā sādhvī bhartuḥ priya parāyaṇā ūrdhvam gacchati sā tatra bhartrā anumaraṇam gatā evam mṛtāyāḥ vai lokān aham paśyāmi cakṣuṣā spṛhaṇīyān sura gaṇaiḥ yān gacchanti pativratāḥ mṛtam ca patim uddiśya kuryāt ca evaṃvidham tapaḥ evam gacchati sā nārī patīnām lokam uttamam ramaṇīyam anirdeśyam duṣprāpam deva mānuṣaiḥ prāpnuyāt tādṛśam lokam kevalam yā pativratā nāradaḥ evam vadatīm rudrāṇīm lajjā bhāva samanvitām toṣayām āsa deveśaḥ vācā saṃpūjayan priyām daivata pramadāḥ ca api deva nadyaḥ ca saṃgatāḥ praṇamya śirasā devīm stutibhiḥ ca api tuṣṭuvuḥ pūjayām āsuḥ apare devadevam mudā yutāḥ saṃprahṛṣṭa manāḥ śarvaḥ saṃvādam cintayan muhur śṛṇu kalyāṇe mad vākyam yat idam tu tvayā mayā kṛtam saṃvādam abhavat tat trilokam gamiṣyati puṇyam pavitram ākhyānam bhavitā tat na saṃśayaḥ yaḥ idam śrāvayet vidvān saṃvādam tu āvayoḥ priye śuciḥ bhūtvā narān yuktān sa yajña phalam āpnuyāt ye tu enam śṛṇuyuḥ nityam dharmyam tad gata mānasāḥ śravaṇāt eva teṣām tu mahat dharma phalam bhavet kathayet śṛṇuyāt vā api yaḥ kaḥ cit nityam āhnikam yaḥ imam paṭhate nityam saṃvādam ca āvayoḥ śubham nāradaḥ devīm devam vayam tatra na apaśyāma prabhāvataḥ evam purā mayā āścaryam dṛṣṭam haimavate vane caratā tīrtha yātrā artham purā keśiniṣūdana bhīṣmaḥ tat adya kathitam sarvam tava keśava śṛṇvataḥ vaiśaṃpāyanaḥ
