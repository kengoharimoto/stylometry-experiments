dharma udbhavam jñānam jñānāt vairāgya sambhavaḥ śiva yogāḥ ca sarvajñaḥ paripūrṇāḥ tu nityaśas sarveṣām eva bhūtānām uttamaḥ puruṣaḥ smṛtaḥ puruṣebhyaḥ dvijaḥ śreṣṭhaḥ dvijebhyaḥ grantha pāragaḥ nānā
