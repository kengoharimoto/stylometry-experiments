sraṣṭṛ tvam ātma sambodhaḥ tu adhiṣṭātṛ tvam eva ca atha yāni daśa etāni śaṅkare sarvān devān ṛṣīn ca eva sametān asuraiḥ saha jñānena ca munīn sarvān yogāt bhūtāni sarvaśas ṛṣayaḥ ūcuḥ yogam tapaḥ ca satyam ca dharmam ca api mahā mune māheśvarasya jñānasya sādhanañca prajakṣva naḥ tat sarvam śrotum icchāmi yogam māheśvaram prabho vāyuḥ uvāca pañca dharmāḥ purāṇe tu rudreṇa samudāhṛtāḥ māheśvaryam yathā proktam rudraiḥ akliṣṭa karmabhiḥ ādityaiḥ vasubhiḥ sādhyaiḥ aśvibhyām ca eva sarvaśas yama śukra purogaiḥ ca pitṛ kāla antakaiḥ tathā bahubhiḥ te dharmāḥ paryupāsitāḥ te vai prakṣīṇa karmāṇaḥ śārada ambara nirmalāḥ upāsate muni gaṇāḥ sandhāya ātmānam ātmani guru priya hite yuktāḥ gurūṇām vai priya īpsavaḥ maheśvareṇa ye proktāḥ pañca dharmāḥ sanātanāḥ prāṇāyāmaḥ tathā dhyānam pratyāhāraḥ atha dhāraṇā smaraṇam ca eva yoge asmin pañca dharmāḥ prakīrttitāḥ teṣām krama viśeṣeṇa lakṣaṇam kāraṇam tathā pravakṣyāmi tathā tattvam yathā rudreṇa bhāṣitam prāṇāyāma gatiśvā api prāṇasya āyāmaḥ ucyate prāṇānām ca nirodhaḥ tu sa prāṇāyāma saṃjñitaḥ prāṇāyāma pramāṇam tu mātrāḥ vai dvādaśa smṛtāḥ mandaḥ dvādaśa mātraḥ tu udvātāḥ dvādaśa smṛtāḥ madhyamaḥ ca dvis udvātaḥ caturviṃśati mātrikaḥ uttamaḥ tatra tris udvātaḥ mātrāḥ ṣaṭtriṃśat ucyate sveda kampa viṣādānām jananaḥ hi uttamaḥ smṛtaḥ iti etat trividham proktam prāṇāyāmasya lakṣaṇam pramāṇam ca samāsena lakṣaṇam ca nibodhata siṃhaḥ vā kuñjaraḥ vā api tathā anyaḥ vā mṛgaḥ vane gṛhītaḥ sevyamānaḥ tu mṛduḥ samupajāyate tathā prāṇaḥ durādharṣaḥ sarveṣām akṛtātmanām sa ca eva hi yathā siṃhaḥ kuñjaraḥ vā api durbalaḥ kāla antara vaśāt yogāt gamyate parimarddanāt paridhāya manodevam tathā jīvati mārutaḥ yathā siṃhaḥ gajaḥ vā api vaśya tvāt avatiṣṭhate abhayāya manuṣyāṇām mṛgebhyaḥ saṃpravarttate yathā paricitaḥ ca ayam vāyuḥ vai viśvatomukhaḥ paridhyāyamānaḥ saṃruddhaḥ śarīre kilbiṣam dahat prāṇāyāmena yuktasya viprasya niyata ātmanaḥ sarve doṣāḥ praṇaśyanti sattvasthaḥ ca eva jāyate tapāṃsi yāni tapyante vratāni niyamāḥ ca ye sarva yajña phalam ca eva prāṇāyāmaḥ ca tad samaḥ ap bindum yaḥ kuśa agreṇa māsi māsi saṃvatsara śatam sāgram prāṇāyāmam ca tad samam prāṇāyāmaiḥ daheḥ doṣān dhāraṇābhiḥ ca kilbiṣam pratyāhāreṇa viṣayān dhyānena anāśvarān guṇān tasmāt yuktaḥ sadā yogī prāṇāyāma paraḥ bhavet iti śrī ma hāpurāṇe vāyuprokte manvantarādivarṇanam nāma daśamaḥ adhyāḥ
