śrīśivamahāpurāṇam
atha śrīśivamahāpurāṇaṃ vidyeśvarasaṃhitā prārabhyate
śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ śrīgurubhyo namaḥ śrisarasvatyai namaḥ atha śivapuraṇe prathamā vidyeśvarasaṃhitāprārabhyate ādyantamaṃgalamajātasamānabhāvamāryaṃ tamīśamajarāmaramātmadevam
paṃcānanaṃ prabalapaṃcavinodaśīlaṃ saṃbhāvaye manasiśaṃkaramambikeśam
vyāsa uvāca
dharmakṣetre mahākṣetre gaṃgākālindisaṃgame
munayaḥ śaṃsitātmanassatyavrataparāyaṇāḥ
tatra satraṃ samākarṇya vyāsaśiṣyo mahāmuniḥ
ājagāma munīndraṣṭuṃ sūtaḥ paurāṇikottamaḥ
taṃ dṛṣṭvā sūtamāyāntaṃ harṣitā munayastadā
tato vinayasaṃyuktā procuḥ sāṃjalayaścate
suprasannā mahātmānaḥ stutiṃ kṛtvāyathāvidhi
purāṇavidyāmakhilāṃ vyāsātpratyarthamīyivān
tasmādāścaryyabhūtānāṃ kathānāṃ tvaṃ hi bhājanam
ratnānāmurusārāṇāṃ ratnākara ivārṇavaḥ
tvaṃ maddiṣṭavaśādasya darśanārthamihāgataḥ
tattvaṃ śrutaṃ sma naḥ sarvaṃ pūrvameva śubhāśubham
na tṛptimadhigacchāmaḥ śravaṇecchā muhurmuhuḥ
idānīmekamevāsti śrotavyaṃ sūta sanmate
prāpte kaliyuge ghore narāḥ puṇyavivarjitāḥ
durācāraratāḥ sarve satyavārtāparāṅmukhāḥ
parāpavādaniratāḥ paradravyābhilāṣiṇaḥ
parastrīsaktamanasaḥ parahiṃsāparāyaṇāḥ
dehātmadṛṣṭayā mūḍhā nāstikāḥ paśubuddhayaḥ
mātṛpitṛkṛtadveṣāḥ strīdevāḥ kāmakiṃkarāḥ
tyaktasvajātikarmāṇaḥ prāyaśaḥparavaṃcakāḥ
trikālasaṃdhyayā hīnā brahmabodhavivarjitāḥ
adayāḥ paṃḍitaṃmanyāssvācāravratalopakāḥ
kṛṣyudyamaratāḥ krūrasvabhāvā malināśayāḥ
kṣatriyāśca tathā sarve svadharmatyāgaśīlinaḥ
asatsaṃgāḥ pāparatā vyabhicāraparāyaṇāḥ
aśūrā araṇaprītāḥ palāyanaparāyaṇāḥ
kucauravṛttayaḥ śūdrāḥ kāmakiṃkaracetasaḥ
śastrāstravidyayā hīnā dhenuviprāvanojjhitāḥ
śaraṇyāvanahīnāśca kāminyūtimṛgāssadā
prajāpālanasaddharmavihīnā bhogatatparāḥ
prajāsaṃhārakā duṣṭā jīvahiṃsākarā mudā
vaiśyāḥ saṃskārahīnāste svadharmatyāgaśīlinaḥ
kupathāḥ svārjanaratāstulākarmakuvṛttayaḥ
gurudevadvijātīnāṃ bhaktihīnāḥ kubuddhayaḥ
abhojitadvijāḥ prāyaḥ kṛpaṇā baddhamuṣṭayaḥ
kāminījārabhāveṣu suratā malināśayāḥ
lobhamohavicetaskāḥ pūrtādisuvṛṣojjhitāḥ
tadvacchūdrāśca ye kecidbrāhmaṇācāratatparāḥ
ujjvalākṛtayo mūḍhāḥ svadharmatyāgaśīlinaḥ
kartārastapasāṃ bhūyo dvijatejopahārakāḥ
śiśvalpamṛtyukārāśca mantroccāraparāyaṇāḥ
śāligrāmaśilādīnāṃ pūjakāhomatatparāḥ
pratikūlavicārāśca kuṭilā dvijadūṣakāḥ
dhanavantaḥ kukarmmāṇo vidyāvanto vivādinaḥ
ākhyāyopāsanā dharmavaktāro dharmalopakāḥ
subhūpākṛtayo daṃbhāḥ sudātāro mahāmadāḥ
viprādīnsevakānmatvā manyamānā nijaṃ prabhum
svadharmarahitā mūḍhāḥ saṃkarāḥ krūrabuddhayaḥ
mahābhimānino nityaṃ caturvarṇavilopakāḥ
sukulīnānnijānmatvā caturvarṇairvivartanāḥ
sarvavarṇabhraṣṭakarā mūḍhāssatkarmakāriṇaḥ
striyaśca prāyaśo bhraṣṭā bhartravajñānakārikāḥ
śvaśuradrohakāriṇyo nirbhayā malināśanāḥ
kuhāvabhāvaniratāḥ kuśīlāssmaravihvalāḥ
jārasaṃgaratā nityaṃ svasvāmivimukhāstathā
tanayā mātṛpitrośca bhaktihīnā durāśayāḥ
avidyāpāṭhakā nityaṃ rogagrasitadehakāḥ
eteṣāṃ naṣṭabuddhīnāṃ svadharmatyāgaśīlinām
iti ciṃtākulaṃ cittaṃ jāyate satataṃ hi naḥ
laghūpāyena yenaiṣāṃ bhavetsadyoghanāśanam
vyāsa uvāca
ityākarṇya vacasteṣāṃ munīnāṃ bhāvitātmanām
manasā śaṃkaraṃ smṛtvā sūtaḥ provāca tānmunīn
iti śrīśaivemahāpurāṇe vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyāṃ munipraśnavarṇanonāmaprathamo adhyāyaḥ
sūta uvāca
sādhupṛṣṭaṃ sādhavo vastrailokyahitakārakam
guruṃ smṛtvā bhavatsnehādvakṣye tacchṛṇutādarāt
vedāntasārasarvasvaṃ purāṇaṃ śaivamuttamam
tasyādhyayanamātreṇa purāṇasya dvijottamāḥ
tāvatkalimahotpātāḥ saṃcariṣyanti nirbhayāḥ
tāvatsarvāṇi śāstrāṇi vivadanti parasparam
tāvatsvarūpaṃ durbodhaṃ śivasya mahatāmapi
tāvadyamabhaṭāḥ krūrāḥ saṃcariṣyanti nirbhayāḥ
tāvatsarvāṇi tīrthāni vivadanti mahītale
tāvatsarvāṇi mantrāṇi vivadanti mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
tāvatsarvāṇi kṣetrāṇi vivadanti mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
tāvatsarvāṇi pīṭhāni vivadanti mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
tāvatsarvāṇi dānāni vivadanti mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
yāvacchivapurāṇaṃ hi nodeṣyati mahītale
asya śaivapurāṇasya kīrtanaśravaṇāddvijāḥ
tathāpi tasya māhātmyaṃ vakṣye kiṃcittu vonaghāḥ
cittamādhāya śṛṇuta vyāsenoktaṃ purā mama
etacchivapurāṇaṃ hi ślokaṃ ślokārddhameva ca
yaḥ paṭhedbhaktisaṃyuktassa pāpānmucyate kṣaṇāt
etacchivapurāṇaṃ hi yaḥ pratyahamataṃdritaḥ
etacchivapurāṇaṃ yassādhāraṇapadecchayā
anyataḥ śṛṇuyātso api matto mucyeta pātakāt
etacchivapurāṇaṃ yo namaskuryādadūrataḥ
sarvadevārcanaphalaṃ sa prāpnoti na saṃśayaḥ
adhīteṣu ca śāstreṣu vedeṣu vyākṛteṣu ca
etacchivapurāṇaṃ hi caturdaśyāmupoṣitaḥ
śivabhaktasabhāyāṃ yo vyākaroti sa uttamaḥ
pratyakṣaraṃ tu gāyatrīpuraścaryyāphalaṃ labhet
iha bhuktvākhilānkāmānante nirvāṇatāṃ vrajet
upoṣitaścaturdaśyāṃ rātrau jāgaraṇānvitaḥ
yaḥ paṭhecchṛṇuyādvāpi tasya puṇyaṃ vadāmyaham
kurukṣetrādinikhilapuṇyatīrtheṣvanekaśaḥ
tatphalaṃ saṃbhavettasya satyaṃ satyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ
ājñāṃ tasya pratīkṣerandevā indrapurogamāḥ
etacchivapurāṇaṃ yaḥ paṭhañchṛṇvanhi nityaśaḥ
samāhitaḥ paṭhedyastu tatra śrīrudrasaṃhitām
triḥ paṭhetpratyahaṃ maunī sa kāmānakhilĀllabhet
tāṃ rudrasaṃhitāṃ yastu sapaṭhedvaṭabilvayoḥ
pradakṣiṇāṃ prakurvāṇo brahmahatyā nivartate
brahmasvarūpiṇī sākṣātpraṇavārthaprakāśikā
kailāsasaṃhitāyāstu māhātmyaṃ vetti śaṃkaraḥ
kṛtsnaṃ tadarddhaṃ vyāsaśca tadarddhaṃ vedmyahaṃ dvijāḥ
tatra kiṃcitpravakṣyāmi kṛtsnaṃ vaktuṃ na śakyate
yajjñātvā tatkṣaṇāllokaścittaśuddhimavāpnuyāt
na nāśayati yatpāpaṃ sā raudrī saṃhitā dvijāḥ
tanna paśyāmyahaṃ loke mārgamāṇoapi sarvadā
śivenopaniṣatsiṃdhumanthanotpāditāṃ mudā
kumārāyārpitāṃ tāṃ vai sudhāṃ pītvā amaro bhavet
brahmahatyādipāpānāṃ niṣkṛtiṃ kartumudyataḥ
māsamātraṃ saṃhitāṃ tāṃ paṭhitvā mucyate tataḥ
pāpaṃ sakṛtkīrtanena saṃhitā sā vināśayet
śivālaye bilvavane saṃhitāṃ tāṃ paṭhettu yaḥ
saṃhitāṃ tāṃ paṭhanbhaktyā yaḥ śrāddhe bhojayeddvijān
caturdaśyāṃ nirāhāro yaḥ paṭhetsaṃhitāṃ ca tām
bilvamūle śivaḥ sākṣātsa devaiśca prapūjyate
ubhe viśiṣṭe vijñeye līlāvijñānapūrite
tadidaṃ śaivamākhyātaṃ purāṇaṃ vedasaṃmitam
mātraṃ rudraikādaśakaṃ kailāsaṃ śatarudrakam
vāyavīyaṃ dharmasaṃjñaṃ purāṇamiti bhedataḥ
saṃhitā dvādaśamitā mahāpuṇyatarā matā
tāsāṃ saṃkhyāṃ bruve viprāḥ śṛṇutādaratokhilam
vidyeśaṃ daśāsāhasraṃ rudraṃ vaināyakaṃ tathā
aumaṃ mātṛpurāṇākhyaṃ pratyekāṣṭasahasrakam
trayodaśasahasraṃ hi rudraikādaśakaṃ dvijāḥ
ṣaṭsahasraṃ ca kailāsaṃ śatarudraṃ tadarddhakam
vāyavīyaṃ khābdhiśataṃ gharmaṃ ravisahasrakam
tadevaṃ lakṣasaṃkhyākaṃ śaivasaṃkhyāvibhedataḥ
śaivaṃ tatra caturthaṃ vai purāṇaṃ saptasaṃhitam
śive saṃkalpitaṃ pūrvaṃ purāṇaṃ granthasaṃkhyayā
śatakoṭipramāṇaṃ hi purā sṛṣṭau suvismṛtam
vyasteṣṭādaśadhā caiva purāṇe dvāparādiṣu
caturlakṣeṇa saṃkṣipte kṛte dvaipāyanādibhiḥ
proktaṃ śivapurāṇaṃ hi caturviṃśatsahasrakam
ślokānāṃ saṃkhyayā saptasaṃhitaṃ brahmasaṃmitam
vidyeśvarākhyā tatrādyā raudrī jñeyā dvitīyikā
tṛtīyā śatarudrākhyā koṭirudrā caturthikā
paṃcamī caiva maumākhyā ṣaṣṭhī kailāsasaṃjñikā
saptamī vāyavīyākhyā saptaivaṃ saṃhitāmatāḥ
sasaptasaṃhitaṃ divyaṃ purāṇaṃ śivasaṃjñakam
etacchivapurāṇaṃ hi saptasaṃhitamādarāt
śrutismṛtipurāṇetihāsāgamaśatāni ca
etacchivapurāṇasya nārhantyalpāṃ kalāmapi
śaivaṃ purāṇamamalaṃ śivakīrtitaṃ tadvyāsena śaivapravaṇena na saṃgṛhītam
saṃkṣepataḥ sakalajīvaguṇopakāre tāpatrayaghnamatulaṃ śivadaṃ satāṃ hi
śaivaṃ purāṇatilakaṃ khalu satpurāṇaṃ vedāntavedavilasatparavastugītam
yo vai paṭhecca śṛṇuyātparamādareṇa śaṃbhupriyaḥ sa hi labhetparamāṃ gatiṃ vai
iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyāṃ dvidīyo adhyāyaḥ
vyāsa uvāca
vedāntasārasarvasvaṃ purāṇaṃ śrāvayādbhutam
iti śrutvā munīnāṃ sa vacanaṃ supraharṣitaḥ
saṃsmarañchaṃkaraṃ sūtaḥ provāca munisattamān
sūta uvāca
śṛṇvantu ṛṣayaḥ sarve smṛtvā śivamanāmayam
purāṇapravaṇaṃ śaivaṃ purāṇaṃ vedasārajam
yatra gītaṃ trikaṃ prītyā bhaktijñānavirāgakam
vedāntavedyaṃ sadvastu viśeṣeṇa pravarṇitam
sūta uvāca
śṛṇvantu ṛṣayaḥ sarve purāṇaṃ vedasārajam
purā kālena mahatā kalpe atīte punaḥpunaḥ
munīnāṃ ṣaṭkulīnānāṃ bruvatāmitaretaram
idaṃ paramidaṃ neti vivādaḥ sumahānabhūt
te abhijagmurvidhātāraṃ brahmāṇaṃ praṣṭumavyayam
tvaṃ hi sarvajagaddhātā sarvakāraṇakāraṇam
kaḥ pumānsarvatattvebhyaḥ purāṇaḥ parataḥ paraḥ
sahabhūteṃdriyaiḥ sarvaiḥ prathamaṃ saṃprasūyate
eṣa devo mahādevaḥ sarvajño jagadīśvaraḥ
ayaṃ tu parayā bhaktyā dṛśyate nā anyathā kvacit
bhaktyā paramayā tasya nityaṃ darśanakāṃkṣiṇaḥ
prasādāddevatābhaktiḥ prasādo bhaktisaṃbhavaḥ
yathehāṃkurato bījaṃ bījato vā yathāṃkuraḥ
tasmādīśaprasādārthaṃ yūyaṃ gatvā bhuvaṃ dvijāḥ
dīrghasatraṃ samākṛdhvaṃ yūyaṃ varṣasahasrakam
amuṣyaivādhvareśasya śivasyaiva prasādataḥ
vedoktavidyāsāraṃ tu jñāyate sādhyasādhanaṃ
munaya ūcuḥ
atha kiṃ paramaṃ sādhyaṃ kiṃvā tatsādhanaṃ param
sādhakaḥ kīdṛśastatra tadidaṃ brūhi tattvataḥ
sādhyaṃ śivapadaprāptiḥ sādhanaṃ tasya sevanam
sādhakastatprasādādyoanityādiphalaniḥspṛhaḥ
parameśapadaprāptaḥ sālokyādikramāttataḥ
tatsādhanaṃ bahuvidhaṃ sākṣādīśena bodhitam
saṃkṣipya tatra vaḥ sāraṃ sādhanaṃ prabravīmyaham
śrotreṇa śravaṇaṃ tasya vacasā kīrtanaṃ tathā
śrotavyaḥ kīrtitavyaśca mantavyaśca maheśvaraḥ
iti śrutipramāṇaṃ naḥ sādhanenāamunā param
sādhyaṃ vrajata sarvārthasādhanaikaparāyaṇāḥ
pratyakṣaṃ cakṣuṣā dṛṣṭvā tatra lokaḥ pravartate
apratyakṣaṃ hi sarvatra jñātvā śrotreṇa ceṣṭate
tasmācchravaṇamevādau śrutvā gurumukhādbudhaḥ
tataḥ saṃsādhayedanyatkīrtanaṃ mananaṃ sudhīḥ
sarvāṃgavyādhayaḥ paścātsarvānaṃdaśca līyate
abhyāsātkleśametadvai paścādādyantamaṃgalam
iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyāṃ sādhyasādhanakhaṇḍe tṛtīyo adhyāyaḥ
munaya ūcuḥ
mananaṃ kīdṛśaṃ brahmañchravaṇaṃ cāpi kīdṛśam
pūjājapeśaguṇarūpavilāsanāmnāṃ yuktipriyeṇa manasā pariśodhanaṃ yat
gītātmanā śrutipadena ca bhāṣayā vā śaṃbhupratāpaguṇarūpavilāsanāmnām
satsaṃgamena bhavati śravaṇaṃ purastātsaṃkīrtanaṃ paśupateratha taddṛḍhaṃ syāt
sūta uvāca
asminsādhanamāhatmye purā vṛttaṃ munīśvarāḥ
yuṣmadarthaṃ pravakṣyāmi śṛṇudhvamavadhānataḥ
purā mama gururvyāsaḥ parāśaramuneḥ sutaḥ
tapaścacāra saṃbhrāntaḥ sarasvatyāstaṭe śubhe
gacchanyadṛchayā tatra vimānenārkarociṣā
dhyānārūḍhaḥ prabuddho asau dadarśa tamajātmajam
praṇipatyāha saṃbhrāntaḥ paraṃ kautūhalaṃ muniḥ
prasannaḥ prāha taṃ prahvaṃ prabhurgaṃbhīrayā girā
satyaṃ vastu mune dadhyāḥ sākṣātkaraṇagocaraḥ
sa śivothāsahāyotra tapaścarasi kiṃ kṛte
evamuktaḥ kumāreṇa provāca svāśayaṃ muniḥ
dharmārthakāmamokṣāśca vedamārge kṛtādarāḥ
bahudhā sthāpitā loke mayā tvatkṛpayā tathā
muktisādhanakaṃ jñānaṃ nodeti paramādbhutam
tapaścarāmi muktyarthaṃ na jāne tatra kāraṇam
itthaṃ kumāro bhagavānvyāsena muninārthitaḥ
samarthaḥ prāha vipreṃdrā niścayaṃ muktikāraṇam
śravaṇaṃ kīrtanaṃ śaṃbhormananaṃ ca mahattaram
śivājñayā tataḥ prāpto bhagavānnaṃdikeśvaraḥ
sa me dayālurbhagavānsarvasākṣī gaṇeśvaraḥ
śravaṇaṃ kīrtanaṃ śaṃbhormananaṃ vedasaṃmatam
trikaṃ ca sādhanaṃ muktau śivena mama bhāṣitam
śravaṇādiṃ trikaṃ brahmankuruṣveti muhurmuhuḥ
evamuktvā tato vyāsaṃ sānugo vidhinaṃdanaḥ
ṛṣaya ūcuḥ
śravaṇāditrayaṃ sūta muktyopāyastvayeritaḥ
śravaṇāditrike aśaktaḥ kiṃ kṛtvā mucyate janaḥ
iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe prathamāyāṃ vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyaṃ sādhyasādhanakhaṇḍe caturtho adhyāyaḥ
sūta uvāca
śravaṇāditrike aśakto liṃgaṃ beraṃ ca śāṃkaram
maṃḍapaṃ gopuraṃ tīrthaṃ maṭhaṃ kṣetraṃ tathotsavam
vastraṃ gaṃdhaṃ ca mālyaṃ ca dhūpaṃ dīpaṃ ca bhaktitaḥ
chatraṃ dhvajaṃ ca vyajanaṃ cāmaraṃ cāpi sāṃgakam
rājopacāravatsarvaṃ dhārayelliṃgaberayoḥ
pradakṣiṇāṃ namaskāraṃ yathāśakti japaṃ tathā
āvāhanādisargāntaṃ nityaṃ kuryātsubhaktitaḥ
siddhimeti śivaprītyā hitvāpi śravaṇādikam
liṃgaberārcanāmātrānmuktāḥ pūrve mahājanāḥ
manuya ūcuḥ
beramātre tu sarvatra pūjyante devatāgaṇāḥ
liṃgebere ca sarvatra kathaṃ saṃpūjyate śivaḥ
sūta uvāca
aho munīśvarāḥ puṇyaṃ praśnametanmahādbhutam
śivaiko brahmarūpatvānniṣkalaḥ parikīrtitaḥ
niṣkalatvānnirākāraṃ liṃgaṃ tasya samāgatam
sakalatvāttathā beraṃ sākāraṃ tasya saṃgatam
sakalākalarūpatvādbrahmaśabdābhidhaḥ paraḥ
tasmātte niṣkale liṃge nārādhyante sureśvarāḥ
abrahmatvācca jīvatvāttathānye devatāgaṇāḥ
tūṣṇīṃ sakalamātratvādarcyante beramātrake
jīvatvaṃ śaṃkarānyeṣāṃ brahmatvaṃ śaṃkarasya ca
vedāntasārasaṃsiddhaṃ praṇavārthe prakāśanāt
evameva purā pṛṣṭo maṃdare naṃdikeśvaraḥ
śivānyadevavaśyānāṃ sarveṣāmapi sarvataḥ
beramātraṃ ca pūjārthaṃ śrutaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ ca bhūriśaḥ
śivamātrasya pūjāyāṃ liṃgaṃ beraṃ ca dṛśyate
naṃdikeśvara uvāca
liṃgaṃ tasyaiva pūjāyāṃ sarvavedeṣu saṃmatam
sakalaṃ ca tathā beraṃ pūjāyāṃ lokasaṃmatam
śivānyeṣāṃ ca jīvatvātsakalatvācca sarvataḥ
beramātraṃ ca pūjāyāṃ saṃmataṃ vedanirṇaye
svāvirbhāve ca devānāṃ sakalaṃ rūpameva hi

aṃsayugmaṃ ca hṛdayaṃ nābhirityevamaṣṭakam
brahmā ca ṛṣayaḥ saptadevatāśca prakīrtitāḥ
ityevaṃ tu samuddiṣṭaṃ bhasmavidbhirmunīśvarāḥ
atha vā mastakaṃ bāhūhṛdayaṃ nābhireva ca
paṃcasthānānyamūnyāhurdhāraṇe bhasmavijjanāḥ
yathāsaṃbhavanaṃ kuryyāddeśakālādyapekṣayā
uddhūlanepyaśaktiścettripuṇḍrādīni kārayet
trinetraṃ triguṇādhāraṃ trivedajanakaṃ śivam
smarannamaḥ śivāyeti lalāṭe tu tripuṇḍrakam
īśābhyāṃ nama ityuktvāpārśvayośca tripuṇḍrakam
bījābhyāṃ nama ityuktvā dhārayettu prakoṣṭhayoḥ
kuryādadhaḥ pitṛbhyāṃ ca umeśābhyāṃ tathopari
bhīmāyeti tataḥ pṛṣṭhe śirasaḥ paścime tathā
iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyāṃ bhasmadhāraṇavarṇanonāma caturviṃśo adhyāyaḥ
sūta uvāca
śainakarṣe mahāprājña śivarūpamahāpate
śṛṇu rudrākṣamāhātmyaṃ samāsātkathayāmyaham
śivapriyatamo jñeyo rudrākṣaḥ parapāvanaḥ
darśanātsparśanājjāpyātsarvapāpaharaḥ smṛtaḥ
lokopakaraṇārthāya śivena paramātmanā
śiva uvāca
śṛṇu devimaheśāni rudrākṣamahimā śive
kathayāmi tavaprītyā bhaktānāṃ hitakāmyayā
divyavarṣasahasrāṇi maheśāni punaḥ purā
svatantreṇa pareśena lokopakṛtikāriṇā
līlayā parameśāni cakṣurunmīlitaṃ mayā
puṭābhyāṃ cārucakṣurbhyāṃ patitā jalabiṃdavaḥ
tatrāśrubindavo jātā vṛkṣā rudrākṣasaṃjñakāḥ
bhūmau gauḍodbhavāṃścakre rudrākṣāñchivavallabhān
mathurāyāmayodhyāyāṃ laṃkāyāṃ malaye tathā
sahyādrau ca tathā kāśyāṃ daśeṣvanyeṣu vā tathā
brāhmaṇāḥ kṣatriyā vaiśyāḥ śūdrā jātā mamājñayā
rudrākṣāste pṛthivyāṃ tu tajjātīyāḥ śubhākṣakāḥ
śvetaraktāḥ pītakṛṣṇā varṇājñeyāḥ kramādbudhaiḥ
svajātīyaṃ nṛbhirdhāryaṃ rudrākṣaṃ varṇataḥ kramāt
śivabhaktairviśeṣeṇa śivayoḥ prītaye sadā
dhātrīphalapramāṇaṃ yacchreṣṭhametadudāhṛtam
adhamaṃ caṇamātraṃ syātprakriyaiṣā parocyate
śṛṇu pārvati suprītyā bhaktānāṃ hitakāmyayā
dhātrīphalasamaṃ yatsyātsarvāriṣṭavināśanam
guṃjayā sadṛśaṃ yatsyātsarvārthaphalasādhanam
ekaikataḥ phalaṃ proktaṃ daśāṃśairadhikaṃ budhaiḥ
rudrākṣadhāraṇaṃ proktaṃ pāpanāśanahetave
yathā ca dṛśyate loke rudrākṣaphaladaḥ śubhaḥ
na tathā dṛśyate anyā ca mālikā parameśvari
samāḥ snigdhā dṛḍhāḥ sthūlāḥ kaṃṭakaiḥ saṃyutāḥ śubhāḥ
rudrākṣāḥ kāmadā devi bhuktimuktipradāḥ sadā
krimiduṣṭaṃ chinnabhinnaṃ kaṃṭakairhīnameva ca
vraṇayuktamavṛttaṃ ca rudrākṣānṣaḍvivarjayet
svayameva kṛtadvāraṃ rudrākṣaṃ syādihottamam
rudrākṣadhāraṇaṃ prāptaṃ mahāpātakanāśanam
rudrasaṃkhyāśataṃ dhṛtvā rudrarūpo bhavennaraḥ
ekādaśaśatānīha dhṛtvā yatphalamāpyate
śatārddhena yutaiḥ paṃcaśatairvai mukuṭaṃ matam
tribhiḥ śataiḥ ṣaṣṭiyuktaistrirāvṛttyā tathā punaḥ
rudrākṣairupavītaṃ va nirmīyādbhaktitatparaḥ
śikhāyāṃ ca trayaṃ proktaṃ rudrakṣāṇāṃ maheśvari
karṇayoḥ ṣaṭ ca ṣaṭcaiva vāmadakṣiṇayostathā
śatamekottaraṃ kaṃṭhe bāhvorvai rudrasaṃkhyayā
kūrparadvārayostatra maṇibaṃdhe tathā punaḥ
śeṣānurvaritānpaṃca sammitāndhārayetkaṭau
etatsaṃkhyā dhṛtā yena rudrākṣāḥ parameśvari
evaṃbhūtaṃ sthitaṃ dhyāne yadā kṛtvāsanairjanam
śiveti vyāharaṃścaiva dṛṣṭvā pāpaiḥ pramucyate
tadabhāve prakāronyaḥ śubhaḥ saṃprocyate mayā
śikhāyāmekarudrākṣaṃ śirasā triṃśataṃ vahet
paṃcāśacca gale dadhyādbāhvoḥ ṣoḍaśa ṣoḍaśa
maṇibaṃdhe dvādaśadviskaṃdhe paṃcaśataṃ vahet
aṣṭottaraśatairmālyamupavītaṃ prakalpayet
evaṃ sahasrarudrākṣāndhārayedyo dṛḍhavrataḥ
ekaṃ śikhāyāṃ rudrākṣaṃ catvāriṃśattu mastake
dvātriṃśatkaṇṭhadeśe tu vakṣasyaṣṭottaraṃ śatam
ekaikaṃ karṇayoḥ ṣaṭṣaḍbāhvoḥ ṣoḍaśa ṣoḍaśa
saṃkhyā prītirdhṛtā yena sopi śaivajanaḥ paraḥ
śivavatpūjanīyo hi vaṃdyassarvairabhīkṣṇaśaḥ
śirasīśānamantreṇa karṇe tatpuruṣeṇa ca
paṃcadaśākṣagrathitāṃ vāmadevena codare
paṃca brahmabhiraṃgaśca trimālāṃ paṃcasapta ca
athavā mūlamantreṇa sarvānakṣāṃstudhārayet
madyaṃ māṃsaṃ tu laśunaṃ palāṇḍuṃ śigrumeva ca
śleṣmāntakaṃ viḍvarāhaṃ bhakṣaṇe varjayettataḥ
valakṣaṃ rudrākṣaṃ dvijatanubhireveha vihitaṃ suraktaṃ kṣattrāṇāṃ pramuditamume pītamasakṛt
chinnaṃ khaṃḍitaṃ bhinnaṃ vidīrṇa
tato vaiśyairdhāryaṃ pratidivasabhāvaśyakamaho tathā kṛṣṇaṃ śūdraiḥ śrutigaditamārgoyamagaje
rudrākṣadhāraṇamidaṃ sukṛtaiśca labhyaṃ tyaktvedametadakhilānnarakānprayānti
ādāvāmalakātsvato laghutarā rugṇāstataḥ kaṃṭakaiḥ saṃdaṣṭāḥ kṛmibhistanūpakaraṇacchidreṇa hīnāstathā
dhāryā naiva śubhepsubhiścaṇakavadrudrākṣamapyantato rudrākṣomama liṃgamaṃgalamume sūkṣmaṃ praśastaṃ sadā
sarvāśramāṇāṃ varṇānāṃ strīśūdrāṇāṃ śivājñayā
dhāryāḥ sadaiva rudrākṣā yatīnāṃ praṇavena hi
divā bibhradrātrikṛtai rātrau vibhraddivākṛtaiḥ
ye tripuṇḍradharā loke jaṭādhāriṇa eva ye
rudrākṣamekaṃ śirasā bibharti tathā tripuṇḍraṃ ca lalāṭamadhye
paṃcākṣaraṃ ye hi japanti mantraṃ pūjyā bhavadbhiḥ khalu te hi sādhavaḥ
yasyāṅge nāsti rudrākṣastripuṇḍraṃ bhālapaṭṭake
mukhe paṃcākṣaraṃ nāsti tamānaya yamālayam
jñātvā jñātvā tatprabhāvaṃ bhasmarudrākṣadhāriṇaḥ
te pūjyāḥ sarvadāsmākaṃ no netavyāḥ kadācana
evamājñāpayāmāsa kālopi nijakiṅkarān
tatheti mattvā te sarve tūṣṇīmāsansuvismitāḥ
ata eva mahādevi rudrākṣotyaghanāśanaḥ
haste bāhau tathā mūrdhni rudrākṣaṃ dhārayettu yaḥ
avadhyaḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ rudrarūpī caredbhuvi
surāsurāṇāṃ sarveṣāṃ vaṃdanīyaḥ sadā sa vai
pūjanīyo hi dṛṣṭasya pāpahā ca yathā śivaḥ
dhyānajñānāvamuktopi rudrākṣaṃ dhārayettu yaḥ
rudrākṣeṇa japanmantraṃ puṇyaṃ koṭiguṇaṃ bhavet
daśakoṭiguṇaṃ puṇyaṃ dhāraṇāllabhate naraḥ
yāvatkālaṃ hi jīvasya śarīrastho bhavetsa vai
tāvatkālaṃ svalpamṛtyurna taṃ devi vibādhate
tripuṃḍreṇa ca saṃyuktaṃ rudrākṣāvilasāṃgakam
mṛtyuṃjayaṃ japantaṃ ca dṛṣṭvā rudraphalaṃ labhet
viṣṇvādidevabhaktāśca dhārayeyurna saṃśayaḥ
rudrabhakto viśeṣeṇa rudrākṣāndhārayetsadā
rudrākṣā vividhāḥ proktāsteṣāṃ bhedānvadāmyaham
ekavaktraḥ śivaḥ sākṣādbhuktimuktiphalapradaḥ
yatra saṃpūjitastatra lakṣmīrdūratarā na hi
viśeṣataḥ sa rudrākṣo govadhaṃ nāśayeddrutam
trivaktro yo hi rudrākṣaḥ sākṣātsādhanadassadā
tatprabhāvādbhaveyurvai vidyāḥ sarvāḥ pratiṣṭhitāḥ
paṃcavaktraḥ svayaṃ rudraḥ kālāgnirnāmataḥ prabhuḥ
agamyāgamanaṃ pāpamabhakṣyasya ca bhakṣaṇam
ṣaḍvaktraḥ kārtikeyastudhāraṇāddakṣiṇe bhuje
brahmahatyādikaiḥ pāpairmucyate nātra saṃśayaḥ
saptavaktro maheśāni hyanaṃgo nāma nāmataḥ
dhāraṇāttasya deveśidaridropīśvaro bhavet
rudrākṣaścāṣṭavaktraśca vasumūrtiśca bhairavaḥ
dhāraṇāttasya pūrṇāyurmṛto bhavati śūlabhṛt
durgā vāta dadhiṣṭhātrī navarūpā maheśvarī
taṃ dhārayedvāmahaste rudrākṣaṃ bhaktitatparaḥ
sarveśvaro bhavennūnaṃ mama tulyo na saṃśayaḥ
daśavaktro maheśāni svayaṃ devo janārdanaḥ
dhāraṇāttasya deveśi sarvānkāmānavāpnuyāt
ekādaśamukho yastu rudrākṣaḥ parameśvari
dvādaśāsyaṃ tu rudrākṣaṃ dhārayetkeśadeśake
ādityāścaiva te sarvedvādaśaiva sthitāstathā
trayodaśamukho viśvedevastaddhāraṇānnaraḥ
sarvānkāmānavāpnoti saubhāgyaṃ maṃgalaṃlabhet
caturdaśamukho yo hi rudrākṣaḥ paramaḥ śivaḥ
dhārayenmūrdhni taṃ bhaktyā sarvapāpaṃ praṇaśyati
tattanmantrāñchṛṇu prītyā kramācchailleśvarātmaje

vinā mantreṇa ho dhatte rudrākṣaṃ bhuvi mānavaḥ
sa yāti narakaṃ ghoraṃ yāvadindrāścaturdaśa
rudrākṣamālinaṃ dṛṣṭvā bhūtapretapiśācakāḥ
ḍākinīśākinī caiva ye cānye drohakārakāḥ
kṛtrimaṃ caiva yatkiṃcidabhicārādikaṃ ca yat
tatsarvaṃ dūrato yāti dṛṣṭvā śaṃkitavigraham
rudrākṣamālinaṃ dṛṣṭvā śivo viṣṇuḥ prasīdati
devīgaṇapatissūryaḥ surāścānyepi pārvati
evaṃ jñātvā tu māhātmyaṃ rudrākṣasya maheśvari
śivasyātipriyau jñeyau bhasmarudrākṣadhāriṇau
paṃcākṣarajapāsaktaḥ paripūrṇaśca sanmukhe
vinā bhasmatripuṃḍreṇa vinā rudrākṣamālayā
pūjitopi mahādevo nābhīṣṭaphaladāyakaḥ
tatsarvaṃ ca samākhyātaṃ yatpṛṣṭaṃ hi munīśvara
bhasmarudrākṣamāhātmyaṃ sarvakāmasamṛddhidam
etadyaḥ śṛṇuyānnityaṃ māhātmyaṃ paramaṃ śubham
rudrākṣabhasmanorbhaktyā sarvānkāmānavāpnuyāt
vidyeśvarasaṃhiteyaṃ kathitā vo munīśvarāḥ
sarvasiddhipradā nityaṃ muktidā śivaśāsanāt
iti śrīśivamahāpurāṇe prathamāyāṃ vidyeśvarasaṃhitāyāṃ sādhyasādhanakhaṇḍe rudrākṣamahātmyavarṇanonāma pañcaviṃśo adhyāyaḥ
