iti śrīvāyupurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmāhātmyaṃ nāmonapañcāśattamo'dhyāyaḥ
yajñañcakre gayo rājā bahvannaṃ bahudakṣiṇam
yatra dravyasamūhānāṃ saṃkhyā karttuṃ na śakyate
sthitā gayāyāmannādiparvvatāḥ pañcaviṃśatiḥ
praśaṃsanti dvijāstatra deśe deśe supūjitāḥ
sikatā vā yathā loke yathā ca divi tārakāḥ
tathā bahusuvarṇādyairasaṅkhyātāstu dakṣiṇāḥ
praśaṃsanti dvijāstṛptā deśe deśe supūjitāḥ
gayaṃ viṣṇvādayastuṣṭā varaṃ brūhīti cābruvan
gayastānprārthayāmāsa hyabhiśaptāśca ce purā
brahmaṇā te dvijāḥ pūtā bhavantu kratupūjitāḥ
gayāśrāddhavidhānāya dvijā mūrttāścadurddaśa
teṣāṃ vākyaṃ prakurvvīta yadi brahmā svayaṃ bhavet
gautamaṃ kāśyapaṃ kautsaṃ kauśikaṃ kaṇvameva ca
bhāradvājaṃ hyauśanasaṃ vātsyaṃ pārāśaraṃ tathā
haritkumāramāṇḍavyaṃ lokākṣiṃ lokasaṃmahat
vāsiṣṭhañca tathātreyaṃ gotrāṇyeṣāṃ caturddaśa
gayāpurīti mannāmnā khyātā brahma purī yathā
evamastu varaṃ dattvā tathā cāntardadhuḥ surāḥ
viśālāyāṃ viśālo'bhūdrājā'putro 'bravīddvijān
kathaṃ putrādayo me syurviśālaṃ cābruvandvijāḥ
gayāyāṃ piṇḍadānena tava sarvvaṃ bhaviṣyati
viśālo'pi gayāśīrṣa piṇḍadaḥ putravānabhūt
dṛṣṭvākāśe sitaṃ raktaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ puruṣamabravīt
ke yūyaṃ teṣu caivaikaḥ sitaḥ proce viśālakam
ayaṃ pitāmahaḥ kṛṣṇa ṛṣayo yena ghātitāḥ
avīcinarakaṃ prāptau muktau tvatpiṇḍadānataḥ
pitṝnpitāmahāṃścaiva tathaiva prapitāmahān
prīṇayāmīti yattoyaṃ tvayā dattamarindama
evaṃ putraiḥ pitṝṇāṃ ca kartavyā muktiruttamā
tvañca rājyaṃ ciraṃ kṛtvā bhuktvā bhogāṃśca durllabhān
yajñānsadakṣiṇānkṛtvā yāyādviṣṇupuraṃ tataḥ
evaṃ labdhavaro rājā rājyaṃ kṛtvā divaṃgataḥ
pretarājaḥ saha pretairgayāśrāddhāddivaṃ gataḥ
pretaḥ kaścidvimuktyarthaṃ vaṇijaṃ kañcidabravīt
mama nāmnā gayāśīrṣe piṇḍanirvvāpaṇaṃ kuru
pretabhāvavimuktyarthaṃ tvaṃ gṛhāṇa dhanaṃ mama
tadvanaṃ sarvvamādāya gayāśrāddhavyayaṃ kuru
ṣoḍaśapañcabhāgāṃśca tubhyaṃ vai dattavānaham
svanāmāni yathānyāyaṃ samyagākhyātavānaham
gatvā gayāṃ gayāśīrṣa pretarājāya piṇḍakam
evaṃ gayasya śambhośca kṣetraṃ viṣṇo ravestathā
upoṣito'tha gāyatrītīrthe mahānadīsthite
gāyatryāḥ purataḥ snātvā prātaḥsandhyāmupāsayet
śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍakaṃ kṛtvānayedbrahmaṇyatāṃ kulam
tīrthe samudite snātvā sāvitryāḥ purato naraḥ
piṇḍadānaṃ tataḥ kuryyātpitṝṇāṃ muktikāmyayā
prācīsarasvatītīrthe snātvā cāpi yathāvidhi
sandhyāmupāsya sāyāhne viṣṇulokaṃ nayetpitṝn
viśālāyāṃ lelihāne tīrthe ca bharatāśrame
pādāṅkite muṇḍapṛṣṭe gadādharasamīpataḥ
tīrthe cākāśagaṅgāyāṃ girikarṇamukheṣu ca
snāto'tha piṇḍado brahmalokaṃ kulaśataṃ nayet
devanadyāṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ snātaḥ svargaṃ nayetpitṝn
satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ tu nārada
ekaviṃśatikulānyāhustārayennātra saṃśayaḥ
yā sā vaitaraṇī nāma nadī trailokyaviśrutā
sāvatīrṇā gayākṣetre pitṝṇāṃ taraṇāya vai
godāvaryyāṃ vaitaraṇyāṃ yamunāyāṃ tathaiva ca
devanadyāṃ gopracāre śrāddhadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn
puṣkariṇyāṃ ghṛtakulyāṃ madhukulyāṃ tathaiva ca
koṭitīrthe rukmiṇīye piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn
adattvā kāñcanaṅgāśca daridro jāyate naraḥ
ghṛtakulyā madhukulyā devikā ca mahānadī
śilāyāḥ saṅgamo yatra madhusravā prakīrtitā
ayutaṃ cāśvamedhānāṃ snānakṛllabhate naraḥ
śrāddhaṃ sapiṇḍakaṃ kṛtvā piṇḍadānaṃ tathaiva ca
kulānāṃ śatamuddhṛtya viṣṇulokaṃ nayennaraḥ
daśāśca medhike haṃsatīrthe cāmarakaṅkaṭe
koṭitīrthe rukmakuṇḍe piṇḍadaḥ svarnayetpitṝn
vaitaraṇyāṃ ghṛtakulyāṃ madhukulyāṃ tathaiva ca
koṭitīrthe naraḥ snātvā dṛṣṭvā koṭīśvarañca yaḥ
mārkaṇḍeyeśakocīśau natvā syātpitṛtārakaḥ
rukma pārijātavane pārvvatyā saha śaṅkaraḥ
marīciḥ phalapuṣpārthaṃ pārijātavanaṃ gataḥ
dṛṣṭvā śapto maheśena yasmātsukhavighātakaḥ
duḥkhī bhaveti tadbhīto maricistuṣṭuve śivam
tuṣṭaḥ provāca taṃ śambhurvṛṇīṣva varamuttamam
śāpād bhavatu muktirmme marīciḥ prāha śaṅkaram
śilāsthitastapastepe sarvveṣāṃ duṣkarañca yat
marīcirīśvarācchaptaḥ kṛṣṇatvamagamatpurā
tapasā dāruṇeneha sa vipraḥ śuklatāṃ gataḥ
harirūce marīciñca varaṃ vṛṇuhi putraka
kimalabhyaṃ tvayi tuṣṭe marīciḥ prāha mādhavam
haraśāpādvimukto'haṃ śilā bhavatu pāvanī
divaukasāṃ puṣkariṇīṃ samāsādya naraḥ śuciḥ
tatra snāto divaṃ yāti svaśarīreṇa mānavaḥ
pāpmānaṃ prajahātyeṣa jīrṇatvacamivoragaḥ
tatpaṅgajavanaṃ puṇyaṃ puṇyakṛdbhirniṣevitam
pāṇḍuśilā vai tatrāste śrāddhaṃ yatrākṣayaṃ bhavet
yudhiṣṭirastu tasyāṃ hi śrāddhaṃ karttuṃ yayau mune
tatra kāle pāṇḍunoktaṃ maddhaste dehi piṇḍakam
hastaṃ tyaktvā śilāyāñca piṇḍadānañcakāra saḥ
śilāyāṃ piṇḍadānena prahṛṣṭo vyāsanandanaḥ
akaṇṭakantu sampūrṇaṃ tvaṃ me trātā hi putraka
svargaṃ vraja śarīreṇa bhrātṛbhiḥ parivāritaḥ
dṛṣṭimātreṇa sampūtānnarakasthāndivaṃ naya
ityuktvā prayayau pāṇḍuḥ śāśvataṃ padamavyayam
udbhijjāḥ svedajā vāpi hyaṇḍajā ye jarāyujāḥ
madhusravāṃ samāsādya mṛtāḥ svargapuraṃ yayuḥ
daśāśvamedhike haṃsatīrthe śrāddhāddivaṃ vrajet
daśāśvamedhahaṃsau ca natvā śivapuraṃ vrajet
bharatasyāśrame śrāddhānnayedbrahmālayaṃ pitṝn
nirmmathyāgniṃ śamīgarbhe vidhirviṣṇvādibhiḥ saha
lebhe tīrthantu yajñārthaṃ triṣu lokeṣu viśrutam
makhasaṃjñantu tattīrthaṃ pitṝṇāṃ mukti dāyakam
snātvā ca tarpaṇaṃ kṛtvā piṇḍado muktimāpnuyāt
pitṝnsvargaṃ nayennatvā saṅgame'ṅgārakeśvarau
gayākūṭe piṇḍadānādaśvamedhaphalaṃ labhet
bhasmakūṭe bhasmanāthaṃ natvā ca tārayetpitṝn
iṣṭiṃ cakre'śvamedhākhyaṃ vasiṣṭo munisattamaḥ
iṣṭito nirgataḥ śambhurvaraṃ vṛṇu vasiṣṭhakam
prāheti taṃ vasiṣṭo'pi śiva tuṣṭo'si me yadi
vastavyaṃ cātra deveśa tathetyuktvā śivaḥ sthitaḥ
piṇḍado dhenukāraṇye kāmadhenupadeṣu ca
karddamāle gayānābhau muṇḍapṛṣṭhasamīpataḥ
snātvā śrāddhādikaṃ kṛtvā pitṝṇāmanṛṇo bhavet
phalgucaṇḍīśmaśānākṣīmaṅgalādyāḥ samarccayet
gayāyāñca vṛṣotsargātriḥ saptakulamuddharet
yatra tatra sthitā devā ṛṣayo'pi jitendriyāḥ
ādyaṃ gadādharaṃ dhyāyañchrāddhapiṇḍādidānataḥ
kulānāṃ śatamuddhṛtya brahmalokaṃ nayet pitṝn
gayā gayo gayā dityo gāyatrī ca gadādharaḥ
gayā gayāsuraścaiva ṣaṅgayā muktidāyakāḥ
gayākhyānamidaṃ puṇyaṃ yaḥ paṭetsatataṃ naraḥ
śrṛṇuyācchraddhayā yastu sa yāti paramāṃ gatim
pāṭhayedvā gayākhyānaṃ viprebhyaḥ puṇyakṛnnaraḥ
gayāśrāddhaṃ kṛtaṃ tena kṛtaṃ tena suniścitam
gayāyā mahimānañca hyabhyasedyaḥ samāhitaḥ
teneṣṭaṃ rājasūyena aśvamedhena nārada
upākhyānamidaṃ puṇyaṃ gṛhe tiṣṭhati pustakam
śrāddhakāle paṭedyastu gayāmāhātmyamuttamam
vidhihīnantu tatsarvaṃ pitṝṇāntu gayāsamam
yāni tīrthāni trailokye tāni dṛṣṭāni tatra vai
yena jñātaṃ gayākhyānaṃ śrutaṃ vā paṭitaṃ mune
sūta uvāca
sanatkumāro munipuṅgavāya puṇyāṃ kathāñcātha nivedya bhaktyā
svamāśramaṃ puṇyavanairupetaṃ visṛjya saṃgītaguruṃ jagāma
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte gayāmahātmyaṃ nāma pañcāśattamo'dhyāyaḥ samāptamidaṃ vāyupurāṇam
