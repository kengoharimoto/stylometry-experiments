sūta uvāca
ṛṣīṇāmagnikalpānāṃ naimiṣāraṇyavāsinām
ṛṣiḥ śrutidharaḥ prājñaḥ sāvarṇirnnāma nāmataḥ
teṣāṃ sopyagrato bhūtvā vāyuṃ vākyaviśāradaḥ
sāvarṇiruvāca
vibho purāṇasaṃ baddhāṃ kathāṃ vai vedasaṃmitām
hiraṇyagarbho bhagavān lalāṭānnīlalohitam
kathaṃ ca viṣṇo rudreṇa sārddhaṃ prītiranuttamā
sarve viṣṇumayā devā sarve viṣṇumayā gaṇāḥ
na ca viṣṇusamā kācidgatiranyā vidhīyate
ityevaṃ satataṃ devā gāyante nātra saṃśayaḥ
sūta uvāca
evamukte tu bhagavān vāyuḥ sāvarṇimabravīt
aho sādhu tvayā sādho pṛṣṭaḥ praśno hyanuttamaḥ
yaccāpi kurute nityaṃ praṇāmaṃ śaṃkarasya ca
vistareṇānupūrvyācca śrṛṇuta bruvato mama
vārāhaḥ sāmpratasteṣāṃ tasya vakṣyāmi vistaram
sāvarṇiruvāca
kiṃ ca pramāṇaṃ kalpasya tatra prabrūhi pṛcchatām
vāyuruvāca
manvantarāṇāṃ saptānāṃ kālasaṃkhyā yathākramam
koṭīnāṃ dve sahasre vai aṣṭau koṭiśatāni ca
dviṣaṣṭiśca tathā koṭyo niyutāni ca saptatiḥ
pūrvoktau ca guṇacchedau varṣāgraṃ labdamādiśet
śataṃ caiva tu koṭīnāṃ koṭīnāmaṣṭasaptatiḥ
mānuṣeṇa pramāṇena yāvadvaivasvatāntaram
eṣa kalpastu vijñeyaḥ kalpārddhadviguṇīkṛtaḥ
anāgatānāṃ saptānāmetadeva yathākramam
pramāṇaṃ kālasaṃkhyāyā vijñeyaṃ matamaiśvaram
niyutānyaṣṭapañcāśattathā'śītiśatāni ca
caturaśītiścavānyāni prayutāni pramāṇataḥ
saptarṣayo manuścaiva devāścendrapurogamāḥ
etat kālasya vijñeyaṃ varṣāgrantu pramāṇataḥ
evaṃ manvantaraṃ teṣāṃ mānuṣāntaḥ prakīrttitaḥ
praṇavāntāśacava ye devāḥ sādhyā devagaṇāśca ye
viśvedevāśacava ye nityāḥ kalpaṃ jīvanti te gaṇāḥ
ṛṣaya ūcuḥ
kasmādvārāhakalpoyaṃ nāmataḥ parikīrtitaḥ
kasmācca kāraṇāddevo varāha iti kīrttyate
ko vā varāho bhagavān kasya yoniḥ kimātmakaḥ
vāyuruvāca
vārāhaśca yathā kalpaḥ kalpatvaṃ kalpanā ca yā
tatsarvaṃ sampravakṣyāmi yathādṛṣṭaṃ yathāśrutam
jñātavyo bhagavānatra hyānandaḥ sāmprataḥ svayam
ṛtukalpastathā ṣaṣṭhaḥ saptamastu kratuḥ smṛtaḥ
sāvitro daśamaḥ kalpo bhuvastvekādaśaḥ smṛtaḥ
uśiko dvādaśastatra kuśikastu trayodaśaḥ
ṛṣabhastu tataḥ kalpo jñeyaḥ paṃcadaśo dvijāḥ
ṛṣayo yatra sambhūtāḥ svaro lokamanoharaḥ
ṣaḍjastu ṣoḍaśaḥ kalpaḥ ṣaḍjanā yatra carṣayaḥ
śiśiraśca vasantaśca nidāgho varṣa eva ca
śaraddhemanta ityete mānasā brahmaṇaḥ sutāḥ
utpannāḥ ṣaḍja saṃsiddhāḥ putrāḥ kalpe tu ṣoḍaśe
yasmājjātaiśca taiḥ ṣaḍabhiḥ sadyo jāto maheśvaraḥ
tasmāt samutthitaḥ ṣaḍjaḥ svarastūdadhisannibhaḥ
tataḥ saptadaśaḥ kalpo mārjālīya iti smṛtaḥ
mārjjālīyaṃ tu tat karma yasmādbrāhmāmakalpayat
prajāpatirmahātejā babhūva tridaśeśvaraḥ
akāmayata gāyatrī yajamānaṃ prajāpatim
tato viṃśatimaḥ kalpo niṣādaḥ parikīrttitaḥ
prajāpatistu taṃ dṛṣṭvā svayambhūprabhavaṃ tadā
virarāma prajāḥ sraṣṭuṃ niṣādastu tapo'tapat
divyaṃ varṣasahasrantu nirāhāro jītendriyaḥ
tamuvāca mahāṃtejā brahmā lokapitāmahaḥ
ūrddhvabāhuṃ tapoglānaṃ duḥkhitaṃ kṣutpipāsitam
niṣīdetyabravīdenaṃ putraṃ śāntaṃ pitāmahaḥ
tasmānniṣādaḥ sambhūtaḥ svarastu sa niṣādavān
ekaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo vijñeyaḥ pañcamo dvijāḥ
prāṇo'pānaḥ samānaśca udāno vyāna eva ca
brahmaṇo mānasāḥ putrāḥ pañcaite brahmaṇaḥ samāḥ
dvāviṃśastu tathā kalpo vijñeyo meghavāhanaḥ
yatra viṣṇurmahābāhurmegho bhūtvā maheśvaram
tasya niḥśvasamānasya bhārākāntasya vai mukhāt
nirjagāma mahākāyaḥ kālo lokaprakāśanaḥ
yastvayaṃ paṭyate viprairviṣṇurvai kaśyapātmajaḥ
prajāpatisutaḥ śrīmān citiśca mithunañca tau
caturviṃśatimaścāpi hyākūtiḥ kalpa ucyate
ākūtiśca tathā devī mithunaṃ sambabhūva ha
prajāḥ sraṣṭuṃ tathākūtiṃ yasmādāha prajāpatiḥ
tasmāt sa puruṣo jñeya ākūtiḥ kalpasaṃjñitaḥ
pañcaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo vijñātiḥ parikīrttitaḥ
vijñātiśacava tathā devī mithunaṃ samprasūyate
vijñātaṃ vai samāsena vijñātistu tataḥ smṛtaḥ
ṣaḍviṃśastu tataḥ kalpo mana ityabhidhīyate
devī ca śāṅkarī nāma mithunaṃ samprasūyate
prajā vai cintamā nasya sraṣṭukāmasya vai tadā
tasmāt prajāsambhavanādbhāvanāsambhavaḥ smṛtaḥ
saptaviṃśatimaḥ kalpo bhāvo vai kalpasaṃjñitaḥ
paurṇamāsī tathā devī mithunaṃ samapadyata
prajā vai sraṣṭukāmasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
dhyāyatastu paraṃ dhyānaṃ paramātmānamīśvaram
agnistu maṇḍalībhūtvā raśmijālasamāvṛtaḥ
bhuvandivañcaviṣṭabhya dīpyate sa mahāvapuḥ
tato varṣasahasrānte sampūrṇe jyotimaṇḍale
aviṣṭayā sahotpannamapasyat sūryamaṇḍalam
yasmādadṛśyo bhūtānāṃ brahmaṇā parameṣṭinā
dṛṣṭastu bhagavān devaḥ sūryyaḥ sampūrṇamaṇḍalaḥ
sarvve yogāśca mantrāśca maṇḍalena sahotthitāḥ
yasmāt kalpo hyayaṃ dṛṣṭastasmāttaṃ darśa mucyate
yasmānmanasi sampūrṇo brahamaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
purā vai bhagavān somaḥ paurṇamāsī tataḥ smṛtā
ubhayoḥ pakṣayorjyeṣṭhamātmano hitakāmyayā
darśañca paurṇamāsañca ye yajanti dvijātayaḥ
na teṣāṃ punarāvṛttirbrahmalokāt kadācana
yo'nāhitāgniḥ prayato vīrādhvānaṃ gatopi vā
tvamagne rudro asuro mahī divastvaṃ śarvvo mārutaṃ pṛṣṭa īśiṣe
tvaṃ pāśagandharvvaśiṣaṃ pūṣā vidhattapāsinā
somaścāgnistu bhagavān kālo rudra iti śrutiḥ
aṣṭāviṃśatimaḥ kalpo bṛhadityabhisaṃjñitaḥ
brahmaṇaḥ putrakāmasya sraṣṭukāmasya vai prajāḥ
aṣṭāśītisahasrāṇāṃ yojanānāṃ pramāṇataḥ
tasmādaṇḍantu vijñeyamabhedyaṃ sūryamaṇḍalam
yutsūryyamaṇḍalañcāpi bṛhatsāma tu bhidyate
bhitvā caivaṃ dvijā yānti yogātmāno dṛḍhavratāḥ
saṅghātamupanītāśca anye kalpā rathantare
ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi kalpānāṃ vistaraṃ śubham
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte kalpanirūpaṇaṃ nāmaikaviṃśodhyāyaḥ
ṛṣaya ūcuḥ
rahasyaṃ vai samākhyātaṃ mantrāṇāñca prakalpanam
tasmādvistarataḥ sarvvāḥ kalpasaṃkhyā bravīhi naḥ
vāyuruvāca
atra vaḥ kathayiṣyāmi kalpasaṃkhyāṃ yathā tathā
yugāgraṃ ca varṣāgrantu brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
savanānāṃ sahasrantu dviguṇaṃ trivṛtaṃ tathā
tasya saṃkhyāṃ pravakṣyāmi purastādvai yathākramam
aṣṭāviṃśatirye kalpā nāmataḥ parikīrtitāḥ
taiṣāṃ purastādvakṣyāmi kalpasaṃjñāṃ yathākramam
ekonaviṃśakaḥ kalpo vijñeyaḥ śrṛtalohitaḥ
śvetoṣṇīṣaḥ śvetamālyaḥ śvetāmbaradharaḥ śikhī
utpannastu mahātejāḥ kumāraḥ pāvakopamaḥ
bhīmaṃ mukhaṃ mahāraudraṃ sughoraṃ śvetalohitam
dīptaṃ dīptena vapuṣā mahāsyaṃ śvetavarccasam
taṃ dṛṣṭvā puruṣaḥ śrīmān brahmā vai viśvatomukhaḥ
kumāraṃ lokadhātāraṃ viśvarūpaṃ maheśvaram
purāṇapuruṣaṃ devaṃ viśvātmā yogināṃ ciram
hṛdi kṛtvā mahādevaṃ paramātmānamīśvaram
jñātvā mumoca deveśo hṛṣṭo hāsaṃ jagatpatiḥ
tato'sya pārśvataḥ śvetā ṛṣayo brahmavarccasaḥ
prādurbhūtā mahātmānaḥ śvetamālyānulepanāḥ
tasyāgre śveta varṇābhaḥ śvetanāmā mahāmuniḥ
tatra te ṛṣayaḥ sarvve sadyojātaṃ maheśvaram
tasmādviśveśvaraṃ devaṃ ye prapaśyanti vai dvijāḥ
prāṇāyāmaparā yuktā brahmaṇi vyavasāyinaḥ
vāyuruvāca
dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
prādurbhūto mahātejāḥ kumāro raktavigrahaḥ
sa taṃ dṛṣṭvā mahādevaṃ kumāraṃ raktavāsasam
dhyānayogaṃ paraṅgatvā bubudhe viśvamīśvaram
evaṃ dhyāto mahādevo brahmaṇā parameṣṭinā
dhyāyatā putrakāmena yasmāttehaṃ pitāmahaḥ
dṛṣṭaḥ paramayā bhaktyā dhyānayogena sattama
tasmāddhayānaṃ paraṃ prāpya kalpe kalpe mahātapāḥ
vetsyase māṃ mahāsattva lokadhātāramīśvaram
evamuktvā tataḥ śarvaḥ aṭṭahāsaṃ mumoca ha
tatastasya mahātmānaścatvāraśca kumārakāḥ
sambabhūvurmamāhātmāno virejuḥ śuddhabuddhayaḥ
virajasva vivāhaśca viśoko viśvabhāvanaḥ
brahmaṇyā brahmaṇastulyā vīrā adhyavasāyinaḥ
raktāmbaradharāḥ sarve raktamālyānulepanāḥ
raktabhasmānuliptāṅgā raktāsyā raktalocanāḥ
tato varṣasahasrānte brahmaṇyā vyavasāyinaḥ
anugrahārthaṃ lokānāṃ śiṣyāṇāṃ hitakāmyayā
dharmopadeśamakhilaṃ kṛtvā te brāhmaṇāḥ svayam
ye'pi cānye dvijaśreṣṭā yuñjānā vāmamīśvaram
prapadyanti mahādeṃ tadbhaktāstatparāyaṇāḥ
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte kalpasaṃkhyānirūpaṇaṃ nāma dvāviṃśo'dhyāyaḥ
vāyuruvāca
ekatriṃśattamaḥ kalpaḥ pītavāsā iti smṛtaḥ
brahma yatra mahātejāḥ pītavarṇatvamāgataḥ
dhyāyataḥ putrakāmasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
prādurbhūto mahātejāḥ kumāraḥ pītavastravān
pītagandhānuliptāṅgaḥ pītamālyadharo yuvā
pītayajñopavītaśca pītoṣṇīṣo mahābhujaḥ
taṃ dṛṣṭvā dhyānasaṃyuktaṃ brahmā lokeśvaraṃ prabhum
apaśyad gāṃ virupāṃ ca maheśvaramukhacyutām
catuṣpadāṃ caturvakrāṃ caturhastāṃ catuḥstanīm
caturnnetrāṃ catuḥśrṛṅgīṃ caturddaṃṣṭrāṃ caturmukhīm
dvātriṃśallokasaṃyuktāmīśvarīṃ sarvatomukhīm
sa tāṃ dṛṣṭvā mahātejāḥ mahādevīṃ maheśvarīm
matiḥ smṛtirbuddhiriti gāyamānaḥ punaḥ punaḥ
ehyehīti mahādevī sottiṣṭhat prāñjalirbhṛśam
viśvamāvṛtya yogena jagatsarvaṃ vaśīkuru
atha vā mahādevena rudrāṇī tvaṃ bhaviṣyasi
brāhmaṇānāṃ hitārthāya paramārthaṃ bhaviṣyasi
athaināṃ putrakāmasya dhyāyataḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
pradadau devadeveśaścatuṣpādāṃ maheśvarīm
tatastāṃ dhyānayogena viditvā parameśvarīm
brahmā lokanamaskāryaḥ prapadya tāṃ maheśvarīm
gāyatrīntu tato raudrīṃ dhyātvā brahmā suyantritaḥ
ityetāṃ vaidikīṃ vidyāṃ raudrīṃ gāyatrīmarpitām
japitvā tu mahādevīṃ rudralokanamaskṛtām
tatastasya mahādevo divyaṃ yogaṃ punaḥ smṛtaḥ
aiśvaryaṃ jñānasampattiṃ vairāgyaṃ ca dadau punaḥ
athāṭṭahāsaṃ mumuce bhīṣaṇaṃ dīptamīśvaraḥ
tato'sya sarvato dīptāḥ prādurbhūtāḥ kumārakāḥ
pītamālyāmbaradharāḥ pītagandhavilepanāḥ
pītoṣṇīṣaśiraskāśca pītāsyāḥ pītamūrddhajāḥ
yogātmānastataḥ snātā brāhmaṇānāṃ hitaiṣiṇaḥ
dharmayogabalopetā ṛṣīṇāṃ dīrghasatriṇām
upadiśya tu te yogaṃ praviṣṭā rudramīśvaram
anye'pi niyatātmāno dhyānayuktā jitondriyāḥ
sraṣṭukāmaḥ prajā brahmā cintayāmāsa duḥkhitaḥ
kṛṣṇaḥ samabhavadvarṇo dhyāyataḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
athāpaśyanmahātejāḥ prādurbhūtaṃ kumārakam
kṛṣṇavarṇaṃ mahāvīryaṃ dīpyamānaṃ svatejasā
kṛṣṇāmbaravaroṣṇīṣaṃ kṛṣṇayajñopavītinam
kṛṣṇena maulinā yuktaṃ kṛṣṇasraganulepanam
sa taṃ dṛṣṭvā mahātmānamamaraṃ ghora mantriṇam
vavande devadeveśaṃ viśveśaṃ kṛṣṇapiṅgalam
prāṇāyāmaparaḥ śrīmān hṛdi kṛtvā maheśvaram
evaṃ vai dhyāyatastasya brahmaṇaḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
mumoca bhagavān rudraḥ aṭṭahāsaṃ mahāsvanam
athāsya pārśvataḥ kṛṣṇāḥ kṛṣṇasraganulepanāḥ
catvārastu mahātmānaḥ sambabhūvuḥ kumārakāḥ
kṛṣṇāḥ kṛṣṇāmbaroṣṇīṣāḥ kṛṣṇāsyāḥ kṛṣṇavāsasaḥ
taiścāṭṭahāsaḥ sumahān hūṅkāraścaiva puṣkalaḥ
namaskāraśca sumahān punaḥ punarudīritaḥ
upāsitvā mahābhāgāḥ śiṣyebyaḥ pradadustataḥ
amalaṃ nirguṇaṃ sthānaṃ praviṣṭā viśvamīśvaram
vinivṛtte tu saṃhāre punaḥ sṛṣṭe carācare
brahmaṇaḥ putrakāmasya dhyāyataḥ parameṣṭinaḥ
prādurbhūtā mahānādā viśvarūpā sarasvatī
viśvamālyāmbaradharaṃ viśvayajñopavītinam
viśvoṣṇīṣaṃ viśvagandhaṃ viśvasthānaṃ mahābhujam
atha taṃ manasā dhyātvā yuktātmā vai pitāmahaḥ
vavande devamīśānaṃ sarveśaṃ sarvagaṃ prabhum
evaṃ dhyānagataṃ tatra praṇamantaṃ pitāmaham
uvāca bhagavānīśaḥ prīto'haṃ te kimicchasi
tatastu praṇato bhūtvā vāgbhiḥ stutvā maheśvaram
uvāca bhagavān brahmā prītaḥ prītena cetasā
yadidaṃ viśvarūpante viśvagaṃ viśvamīśvaram
etadveditumicchāmi kaścāyaṃ parameśvaraḥ
kaiṣā bhagavatī devī catuṣpādā caturmukhī
catuḥśrṛṅgī caturvakrā caturddantā catuḥstanī
caturhastā caturnetrā viśvarupā kathaṃ smṛtā
kinnāmadheyā ko'syātmā kiṃvīryā vāpi karmataḥ
rahasyaṃ sarvamantrāṇāṃ pāvanaṃ puṣṭivarddhanam
śrṛṇuṣvaitatparaṃ guhyamādisarge yathā tatham
yasmin bhavādayo devāḥ ṣaḍviṃśanmanavaḥ smṛtāḥ
brahmasthānamidaṃ cāpi yadā prāptaṃ tvayā vibho
śataṃ śatasahasrāṇāmatītā ye svayambhuvaḥ
purastāttava deveśa tān śrṛṇuṣva mahāmune
tvayi yogaśca sāṅkhyaśca tapo vidyāvidhiḥ kriyā
ṛtaṃ styañca yadbrahma ahiṃsā santatikramāḥ
dhyānaṃ dhyānavapuḥ śāntirvidyā'pidyāmatirdhṛtiḥ
kāntiḥ śāntiḥ smṛtirmedhā lajjā śuddiḥ sarasvatī
tuṣṭiḥ puṣṭiḥ kriyā caiva lajjā kṣāntiḥ pratiṣṭitā
ṣaḍviṃśattadguṇā hyeṣā dvātriśākṣarasaṃjñitā
prakṛtiṃ viddhitāṃ brahmaṃstvatprasūtiṃ maheśvarīm
saiṣā bhagavatī devī tatprasūtiḥ svayambhuvaḥ
pradhānaṃ prakṛtiṃ caiva yadāhustattvacintakāḥ
ajāmetāṃ lohitāṃ śuklakṛṣṇāṃ viśvaṃ saṃprasṛjamānāṃ surūpām
ajo'haṃ vai buddhimānviśvarūpāṃ gāyatrīṃ gāṃ viśvarūpāṃ hi buddhvā
evamuktavā mahādevaḥ aṭṭahāsamathākarot
tato'sya pārśvato divyāḥ sarvarūpāḥ kumārakāḥ
jaṭī muṇḍī śikhaṇḍī ca arddhamuṇḍaśca jajñire
dharmopadeśaṃ niyataṃ kṛtvā yogamayaṃ dṛḍham
śiṣṭānāṃ niyatātmānaḥ praviṣṭā rudramīśvaram

bhagavānuvāca
śvetakalpo yadā hyāsīdahaṃ śvetastato'bhavam
śvetoṣṇīṣaḥ śvetamālyaḥ śvetāmbaradharaḥ śivaḥ
śvetāsthimāṃsaromā ca śvetatvak śvetalohitaḥ
matprasādācca deveśaḥ śvetāṅgaḥ śvetalohitaḥ
śvetavarṇā tadā hyāsīdgāyatrī brahmasaṃjñitā
vijñātaḥ svena tapasā sadyo jātaḥ sanātanaḥ
yadā'haṃ ca punastvāsaṃ lohito nāma nāmataḥ
tadā lohitamāsāsthilohitakṣīrasannibhā
lohitākṣastanavatī gāyatrī gauḥ prakīrtitā
tathāpi hi mahāsattva tvayā'haṃ niyatātmanā
vijñātaḥ śveta varṇena tasmādvarṇottamaḥ smṛtaḥ
tato'haṃ vāmadeveti khyātiṃ yāto mahītale
ye cāpi vāmadevatvaṃ jñāsyantīha dvijātayaḥ
vijñāya cemāṃ rudrāṇīṃ gāyatrīṃ mātaraṃ vibho
yadā tu punarevāyaṃ kṛṣṇavarṇo bhayānakaḥ
matkṛtena ca varṇena matkalpaḥ kṛṣṇa ucyate
tatrā'haṃ kālasaṃkāśaḥ kālo lokaprakāśanaḥ

ye'tītāstairyugaiḥ sārddhaṃ rājānaste tadanvayāḥ
svāyambhuve'ntare pūrvaṃ śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ
eṣa svāyambhuvaḥ sargo yenedaṃ pūritaṃ jagat
ṛṣibhirdaivataiścāpi pitṛgandharvarākṣasaiḥ
yakṣabhūtapiśācaiśca manuṣyamṛgapakṣibhiḥ
taiṣāṃ sṛṣṭiriyaṃ loke yugaiḥ saha vivarttate
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte svāyambhuva vaṃśānukīrttanaṃ nāma trayastriśo'dhyāyaḥ
