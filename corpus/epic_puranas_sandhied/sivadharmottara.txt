mahāvimānaiḥ śrīmadbhiḥ sarvakāmasamanvitaiḥ
tataḥ kālāt kṣitiṃ prāpya rājā bhavati dhārmikaḥ
surūpaḥ daddvijo vāpi sarvavidyārthapāragaḥ
yaḥ sauvarṇaṃ susampūrṇaṃ sarvaratnopaśobhitam
sapidhānaṃ samañjūṣaṃ vidyākoṣasamāśrayam
kāṃsyārakūṭalauhairvā dāruvaṃśādinirmitam
sarvāśmakaṭakopetaṃ dṛdhasūtranibandhanam
kuryāttālakasaṃyuktaṃ vidyāratnakaraṇḍakam
evaṃ vittānusāreṇa kārayitvānurūpataḥ
kṛtvā vastravaraṃ divyamntaḥpuṣpaṃ gṛhānvitam
tasmin puṇyāhaśabdena vidyākoṣagṛhanyaset
kṛtvā pradakṣiṇañcānte namatsarvāṅgikaṃ budhaḥ
bhavet suśobhanantasya cāṣṭahastapramāṇataḥ
śileṣṭakāmṛṇmayaṃ vā saddārukṛtameva vā
kapāṭārgalasaṃyuktaṃ prākārāsanaśobhitam
patākādhvajasaṃyuktaṃ sadvitānasuśobhanam
ghaṇṭācāmarasaṃyukta kiṅkiṇīravakānvitam
ānīya sthāpayedbhaktyā vidyāratnakaraṇḍakam
śivavidyāṃ sadākālaṃ trisandhyamupacārataḥ
dīnāndhakṛpaṇādyāṃśca dakṣayitvā kṣamāpayet
evaṃ yaḥ śivavidyāyāḥ kuryādāyatanaṃ śubham
kulaikaviṃśaduttārya sabhṛtyaparibāritaḥ
prāpyāṣṭaguṇamaiśvaryaṃ śivaloke mahīyate
yastatra jñānamāsādya viṣayān viṣavat tyajet
sa prāpnoti parāṃ muktiṃ rāgī bhogānavāpnuyāt
sṛṣṭyantare punaḥ prāpte pṛthivyāmekarāḍ bhavet
sarvaiśvaryaguṇopetaḥ sarvajñānārthapāragaḥ
yaḥ kārayenmahāramyaṃ suvedīsaṃsthitapuram
pañcabhaumaṃ tribhaumaṃ vā śivajñānaratātmanām
vāstuvidyāvibhaktañca prākāraparivāritam
savīdhyāḥ purataḥ kāryaṃ diśyāgneyāṃ mahānasam
dhūmanirgamanopetaṃ pūrvataḥ satramaṇḍapam
gandhapuṣpagṛhaṃ kāryam aiśānyāṃ paṭasaṃyuta
bhāṇḍāgāraṃ ca kauberyāṃ koṣṭhāgārañca vāyavye
udakāśrayañca vāruṇyāṃ vātāyanasamanvitam
samitkuśendhanasthānam āyudhānāñca nairṛte
abhyāgatālayaṃ ramyaṃ śayyāsanasapādukam
gṛhāntarāṇi sarvāṇi jalajaiḥ kadalīgṛhaiḥ
prākārāntarbahirdadyāt pañcahastapramāṇataḥ
prākārācca bahiḥ kuryāt sarvadikṣu samantataḥ
divyaṃ śivāśramārāmaṃ nānāpuṣpopaśobhitam
sarvarttukaguṇopetaṃ nānāvṛkṣasamanvitam
priyaṅguśirīṣāśokapuñjīvakarañjakaiḥ
punnāganāgabakulaiḥ pāṭalābilvacampakaiḥ
śvetamandāravijayairjātītagarakuñjakaiḥ
suvarṇaśvetayūthībhirbāṇāmrātakuruṇṭakaiḥ
karavīrakarṇikārair nānāvarṇairanekaśaḥ
sthānavinyāsaracitaissanmārgādhārasaṃyutaiḥ
kadalīstambhaṣaṇḍaiśca dāḍimādyairvirājitam
kapāṭagopuropetaṃ parikhāvaṃśasaṃyutam
tṛtīyāñca parādūrdhvaṃ vidyāvyākhyānamaṇḍapam
parārdhaṃ pañcamaṅkāryaṃ śivasya vaḍabhīgṛham
ṣaḍḍhastamaṣṭhapaṭṭaṃ vā cārupaṭṭavinirmitam
sarvavidyāvidhātāraṃ sarvajñaṃ lakulīśvaram
vṛtaṃ śiṣaiḥ praśiṣyaiśca vyākhyānodyatapāṇikam
evaṃ śivāśramaṃ kṛtvā bhaktyā vittānusārataḥ
tatpratiṣṭhāntataḥ kuryāt pūjāsaṃskāralakṣaṇam
vidyādānoktavibhavaiḥ śobhāṃ kṛtvā tathāgrataḥ
pañcabhiḥ pañcagavyena sthāpyaiśānyāṃ śivāṅkramāt
tūryaghoṣaistamānīya sthāpayet puṣpamaṇḍape
tataścandanapuṣpādyaiḥ pūjayitvā'dhivāsayet
japtvā pañcavidhaṃ stotraṃ śivaṃ vijñāpayet tataḥ
adyādhivāsanaṃ deva śvaḥ pratiṣṭhāvidhistava
pavitraiḥ sthāpayed vedyāṃ pādādārabhya pañcabhiḥ
śivaśca pūjayet puṣpaiḥ snānaṃ varjyaṃ sugandhibhiḥ
saśiṣyastaṃ praṇamyeśaṃ gururvyākhyāṃ pravartayet
prāṅmukhodaṅmukho vāpi puṇyāhārthaṃ śivāgrataḥ
dakṣiṇābhirvicitrābhirbhojanādyaiśca pūrvavat
nivedayet tataḥ sthānaṃ sarveṣāṃ śivayoginām
evaṃ yaḥ kārayedādyaḥ śrīmacchivapuraṃ mahat
sagotraṃ triṃśakopetaḥ sabhṛtyaparivāritaḥ
śivavat krīḍate bhogairaṇimādiguṇānvitaḥ
tataḥ kālena mahatā vidyādānaprabhāvataḥ
sthapatiḥ sthāpakā vṛkṣā valīvardāḥ sukarmiṇaḥ
yānti rudrapuraṃ sarve tadadhyakṣāśca ye narāḥ
ye cāpi vṛttibhṛtakāḥ śivāyatanakarmaṇā
te'pi yānti mṛtāḥ svargā śivakarmānubhāvataḥ
śivadāsatvamāpannā naranārīnapuṃsakāḥ
dattāḥ krītāḥ praviṣṭāśca daṇḍopannā balāhṛtāḥ
vijñeyā rudragaṇikāḥ śivāyatanayogataḥ
sāpi yāti mṛtā svargaṃ māturevānubhāvataḥ
te'pi yānti śivaṃ lokaṃ kiṃ punastatsamāśritāḥ
atyalpamapi yaḥ kuryādatiramyaiḥ śivāśramam
kṛtvā sukuṭṭimāṃ bhūmim īśvarāyatanāgrataḥ
tatra maṇḍalakaṃ kuryāt suvṛttaṃ gomayāmbhasā
sūtreṇa sumitaṅkṛtvā nānāvarṇakaśobhitam
vicitrakusumākīrṇaṃ madhye paṅkajaśobhitam
pradīpākṣatasaṃyuktam udakaṃ bhrāmya pāṇinā
mūrdhnā praṇamya medinyāṃ śivāya vinivedayet
yaḥ kuryāt sakṛdapyevaṃ śivamaṇḍalakannaraḥ
kālāt punarihāyātaḥ śrīmān arthapatirbhavet
jñānavān rūpavān bhogī supriyaḥ sudvijaḥ sukhī
vidyāmaṇḍalakaṃ kṛtvā vidyāvyākhyānamaṇḍape
tatrābhyarcya śivajñānaṃ tadvyākhyāṃ śṛṇuyāttataḥ
śivamaṇḍalakādyena vidhinātīva śobhanam
gurumaṇḍalakaṃ kṛtvā śivaloke mahīyate
śivavidyāgurūṇāñca kṛtvā maṇḍalakaṃ trayam
yaḥ śṛṇoti śivajñānaṃ tasya vidyāprasīdati
anena vidhinā jñānaṃ yaḥ śṛṇoti pravakti ca
sa samprāpya śriyaṃ saukhyaṃ dehānte muktimāpnuyāt
ārogyaśālāṃ yaḥ kuryānmahāvaidyapuraskṛtām
sarvopakaroṇopetāṃ tasya puṇyaphalaṃ śṛṇu
dharmārthakāmamokṣāṇāmārogyaṃ sādhanaṃ yataḥ
tasmādārogyadānena dattaṃ syāttaccatuṣṭayam
apyekamārtaṃ vidvāṃsaṃ susthīkṛtya prayatnataḥ
jñānayogarataṃ śāntaṃ rogārtaṃ śivayoginam
brahmaviṣṇusurāḥ sarve vyādhayaḥ svajanā nṛpāḥ
brahmakṣatraviśaḥ śūdrān rogārtān yān prayatnataḥ
ākāśasya yathā nāntaḥ surairapyupalabhyate
tadvādārogyadānasya nāntaḥ saṃvidyate tathā
puṇyamānena mahatā gatvā śivapurannaraḥ
ekaviṃśakulapetaḥ sabhṛtyaparivāritaḥ
āste śivapure tāvadyāvadābhūtasamplavam
jñānād viraktaḥ saṃsāre śivadhyānamupāśritaḥ
svadehaṃ tṛṇavat tyaktvā sarvaduḥkhāntamāpnuyāt
sarvajñaḥ paripūrṇaśca mukta ityabhidhīyate
tasmād bhogāpavargārthaṃ rogārtaṃ samupācaret
viśeṣeṇa ca yogīndramātmanā ca dhanena ca
rogiṇo nodvijet prājñastanmalānāśca sarvataḥ
yo'nugṛhītamātmānaṃ manyamāno dine dine

jñānayogo nāma tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ

kalpakoṭiśataṃ divyaṃ sevyamānaḥ sa tiṣṭhati

viṣṇulokād brahmalokaṃ samprāpya krīḍate punaḥ

tataḥ sa pūrvabhāvena yogīndrān paryupāsate

yogād viraktaḥ saṃsārāt prāpnoti paramaṃ padam

karmayogaḥ samākhyātastapaścāndrāyaṇādikaḥ
svādhyāyaśca japaḥ proktaḥ śivamantraśca sa dvidhā

uttarottaravaiśiṣṭyaṃ sarveṣāṃ parikīrtitam

nāsti jñānaṃ vinā dhyānaṃ nāsti jñānamayoginaḥ
jñānadhyānañca yasyāsti tīrṇastena bhavārṇavaḥ
jñānaṃ prasannamekāgram aśeṣopādhivarjitam

śrutād vivardhate prajñā prajñātaścintayet punaḥ
ārambhakālaḥ śravaṇaṃ kriyākālaśca cintaram

niḥsāram atikaṣṭaṃ ca bhavaṃ bhāvayataḥ śanaiḥ
iti prasannaṃ yajjñānaṃ bhāvanāmayamuttamam
rāgādyairaparāmṛṣṭaṃ tadvijñeyaṃ vimuktidam
ajñānapāśabaddhatvādamuktaḥ puruṣāḥ smṛtaḥ
tasmājjñānena muktiḥ syādajñānāsya parikṣayāt
saṃsārabījamajñānaṃ saṃsārajñaḥ pumān yataḥ
jñāttasya nivṛttiḥ syāt prakāśāttamaso yathā
ajñāne sati rogādyā dharmādharmau ca tadvaśau
dharmādharmavaśāt puṃsaḥ śarīramupajāyate
śarīre sati saṃkleśaiḥ sarvaiḥ saṃyujyate yataḥ
tataḥ kleśaprahāṇārthaṃ punardehanna kāmayet
ajñānavinivṛttau ca rāgādīnāmasambhavaḥ
rāgādyupaśamātpuṃsaḥ puṇyapāparikṣayaḥ

aśarīraśca saṃkleśaiḥ sarvaireva na bādhyate
tasmādajñānamūlāni sarvaduḥkhāni dehinām
nāsti jñānādṛte puṃsāṃ puṇyapāpaparikṣayaḥ
mokṣārthī puruṣastasmājjñānameva sadābhyaset

nahi dehabhṛtā śakyaṃ karma tyaktuṃ śubhāśubham

śuklakṛṣṇaparaḥ sūkṣmaḥ suviśuddhaḥ samādhijaḥ

variṣṭaḥ sarvayajñānāmasaṃkhyātairmahāguṇaiḥ

japena pāpaśuddhiḥ syājjñānadhyāne na mucyate
prakṣīṇāśeṣapāpasya jñānadhyāne bhavenmatiḥ
pāpāpahatabuddhīnāntadvārttāpi sudurlabhā
dhanuṣāṃ koṭibhiryogaḥ samantāt parirakṣyatekṣati

tīrthāni toyapūrṇāni devāḥ pāṣāṇamṛṇmayāḥ
agnau kriyāvatāṃ devā divi devā manīṣiṇām

yaḥ paśyt sarvagaṃ śāntaṃ tasyātmasthaḥ śivaḥ sadā

divasaṃ divasārdhaṃ vā yatra tiṣṭhatti yoginām

evaṃ hi kaṣṭāni tapāṃsi yajñān kleśātmakānalpaphalapradāśca

bahiḥ karmakarā yadvannātīva phalabhāginaḥ

ardhayāmaṃ muhūrtaṃ vā dhyāyataḥ paramaṃ śivam
jñānayogaṃ tataḥ prāpya saṃsārādvinivartate
mṛdumadhyādhimātratvācchraddhā puṃsāṃ tridhā sthitā
ityetajjñānayogasya mahābhāgyamudāhṛtam
tadabhyāsaratānāñca munīnāṃ śāntacetasām
iti śivadharmottare jñānayogo nāma tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāya
śatajanma bhaveddānaṃ taponiṣṭhapratiṣṭhitam
āhūbhūtaḥ samplavasthāpi pradānaṃ śivayoginām
atyalpamapi yaddattaṃ śivajñānārthavedinām
na dānamalpaṃ bahu vā kiñcid asti vijānataḥ
pātre deśe ca kāle ca vidhinā śraddhayā yat
dattaṃ hutaṃ kṛtaṃ ceṣṭaṃ tadanantaphalaṃ bhavet
tilārdhamātrakeṇāpi yatpramāṇena dīyate
yaddhautaṃ jñānasalilaiḥ śīlabhasmapramārjitam
tatpātraṃ sarvapātrāṇām uttamaṃ sārvakāmikam
jñānoḍupena yaḥ puṃsāṃ trātā saṃsārasāgarāt
ajñānāṃ pālanāt trāṇāt tatpātraṃ paramaṃ smṛtam
jñānaplavabalopetastrāti pāpamahārṇavāt
nājñānastārayedavyaṃ kiṃ śilā tārecchilām
dvijānāṃ vedaviduṣāṃ koṭiṃ sambhojya yat phalam
bhikṣāmātrapradānena tatphalaṃ śivayoginām
śivayogī gṛhe yasya bhikṣāṃ gṛhṇāti saṃskṛtām
jñānine śāntacittāya śivadhyānaratāya ca
śraddhayānnaṃ sakṛd datvā sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyate
ekaṃ munivaraṃ śāntam īśvararpitamānasam
bhojayitvā sakṛd bhaktyā sarvakāmānavāpnuyāt
dhyāyamānaḥ śivaṃ yogī bhuṅkte'nnaṃ satataṃ yataḥ
sa gacched īśvarasthānam evaviṃśakulānvitaḥ
śivāya sarvapākāgraṃ nivedyāgnau ca homayet
yonivedyātmanā bhuṅkte sa bhuṅkte kilviṣaṃ naraḥ
kṛṣivṛdhyādivāṇijyakrodhāsatyārjanadibhiḥ
puṃsāṃ pāpāni vardhante sūnādoṣaiśca pañcabhiḥ
sammārjanāñjanantoyam agnikaṇḍanapeṣaṇī
sūnāḥ pañcagṛhasthānānnityaṃ pāpābhivṛddhaye
śivāgnigurupūjābhiḥ pāpairetairna lipyate
tāvadvarṣasahasrāṇi dātā śivapure vaset
ghṛtasūpayutaiḥ sikthaiḥ puṇyaṃ daśaguṇottaram
avadatsa yutairjñeyaṃ puṇyaṃ śataguṇottaram
evaṃ śāliviśeṣeṇa phalaṃ syāduttarottaram
vyañjanāni vicitrāṇi svādūni surabhīṇi ca
koṭiphalādhikānyāhuḥ snigdhāni madhurāṇi ca
kṣīrabhaktena naivedyaṃ daśakoṭiguṇādhikam
pānakānāṃ sugandhānāṃ phalaṃ tulyaṃ rasālayā
tathā phalaviśeṣaiśca svādubhiḥ sadṛśaṃ phalam
guḍakhaṇḍakṛtairbhakṣyai ghṛtena paripācitaiḥ
koṭisahasraguṇitaṃ geyaṃ puṇyaṃ sugandhibhiḥ
dadhiśarkarayā yuktaṃ tatpuṇyaṃ syāddaśādhikam
rasālāṃ sughanāṃ sītāṃ karpūrasurabhīkṛtām
nivedya puṇyamāpnoti koṭikoṭiguṇādhikam
gṛtāśanena vijñeyaṃ rasālātulitaṃ phalam
śarkarākhaṇḍayuktena rasālābhyadhikaṃ phalam
yathā yathā hi svādūni snigdhāni surabhīṇi ca
bhaktyannapānamātrāṇi tatphalāni tathā tathā
yadvā yadvā śivāyānnaṃ liṅgāni gurumūrtaye
bhaktyā nivedya bhuñjānaḥ phalamakṣayamāpnuyāt
evaṃ yaḥ kurute śrāddhaṃ bhaktitaḥ śivayoginām
gaṅgāsnānasamaṃ puṇyaṃ darśanaṃ śivayoginām
śuśrūṣayitvā bhaktyā tu śivaloke mahīyate
yugavat pūjitāstena brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarāḥ
yaḥ śrāddhe pūjayed bhaktyā viśeṣāc chivayogina
nṛtyanti pitarastuṣṭā gāyanti ca pitāmahāḥ
śivayoginamāyāntaṃ dṛṣṭaiva gṛhasaṃsthitam
krīḍantyoṣadhayaḥ sarvā yāsyāmaḥ svargamakṣayam
anugrahāya lokānāṃ śraddhāyāśca parikṣaṇe
tasmādatithimāyāntam anugacchet kṛtāñjaliḥ
svāgatāsanapādyārghasnānānna śayanādibhiḥ
rūpānvitaṃ virūpaṃ vā malinaṃ malināmbaram
yogīndrāśaṅkayā nityam atithinna vicārayet
śivayogiśarīre tu nityaṃ sannihitaḥ śivaḥ
yogīndraṃ pūjayet tasmāt sa sākṣāt pūjitaḥ śivaḥ
yoginaṃ ye dviṣantyajñāḥ sarvapāpavyavasthitāḥ
kṛmibhirbhinnavadanās tāpyamānāśca vahninā
ye śivajñānaniratān pradviṣanti manāgapi
paścād bāhukabaddhāste nīyante yamasādanam
tatrāgnitaptaiḥ sandaṃśaiḥ prapīḍyoraśca pādataḥ
teṣām utpāṭyate jihvā śataśo'tha sahasraśaḥ
vikatthante mahātmānaṃ ye mūḍhāḥ śivayoginam
te yānti śrotṛbhiḥ sārdhaṃ narakeṣu na saṃśayaḥ
ye cāpavādaṃ śṛṇvanti vimūḍhāḥ śivayoginām
te viśeṣeṇa pacyante narakeṣvāmahīkṣayāt
tasmācchrotā tu pāpīyāṃstaddaṇḍaḥ sumahānataḥ
vaktā śrotā'numantā ca prayoktā dūṣaṇasya ca
etaiḥ saṃyujyate pañca pañcaite nārakāḥ smṛtāḥ
iti yogīndrapātrasya mahābhāgyam udāhṛtam
pūjāpavādaṃ viṣayam apātraṃ śṛṇutādhunā
ajñānapaṅkanirmagnam ātmatrāṇavivarjitam
apuṇyagiriṇākrāntam apātraṃ guṇavarjitam
dānam ajñe tathā nyasta saha pātreṇa naśyati
dānaṃ tadvad pātreṣu vinyastaṃ niṣphalaṃ bhavet
jñānāgnirahite pātre tathā dānam anarthakam
yathā ṣaṇḍho'phalaḥ strīṣu yathā gaur gavi cāṣphalā
mānavasya tathā janma jñānahīnasya niṣphalam
tathā dātā grahītā ca patatyajñastathālpadhīḥ
kāruṇyāt sarvabhūtebhyaḥ śraddhayā yatpradīyate
dīnāndhakṛpaṇānāthabālavṛddhakṛśāturān
etān anukampayāmītyarthī gṛhṇāti nispṛhaḥ
adatvā jñāninaḥ pātraṃ nispṛhatvānna te'rthinaḥ
alpikāpi hi tatpūjā paryāptā hyātmanaḥ phalam
dātur evopakārāya yasmād gṛhṛnti nispṛhāḥ
adānādasya dāridrān dānādaiśvaryam āpnuyāt
yasmāddātā prayātyūrdhvam adhastiṣṭhet pratigrahāta
yadidaṃ kaṣṭam arthitvaṃ prāgadānaphalaṃ hi tat
bodhayanti na yāvatte dehīti kṛpaṇāñjanāḥ
kalyāṇamitram āyāntam arthinaṃ ko na pūjayet
āyātyarthī gṛhandātuṃ kastanna pratipūjayet
nārthinaḥ syuḥ katha pūjyāpacamānā dine dine
karamuttānakaṃ kṛtvā dānaṃ gṛhṇāti yo dvijaḥ
dānādūrdhvagatistena dātu darśayatīva saḥ
dātṛyācakayorbhedaḥ karābhyāmeva sūcitaḥ
yaḥ prāptam arthinaṃ dīnaṃ tyaktvā pātramudīkṣate
yadi stabdhāḥ śaṭhā dīnā na syurapriyavādinaḥ
kva nu dānaṃ dayā kṣāntirbhāvyate sādhubhistadā
yadi nāma na deyaṃ syāt samprāptāyārthine gṛhe
kiṃ vākyānyapi naṣṭāni priyāṇyanugatāni ca
pratyutthānābhigamanaṃ svāgataṃ svāsanaṃ priyam
vittaṃ cittānusāreṇa kadā kasya bhaviṣyati
grāsādardhamapi grāsaṃ yuktaṃ dātuṃ sadārthinām
dānaṃ priyavinirmuktaṃ naṣṭam āhur manīṣiṇaḥ
pratyākhyānam api śreyaḥ priyānunayapeśalam
na tu dānam asatkāra pāruṣyamalinīkṛtam
dānaṃ vratāni niyamo yajñā dhyanaṃ hutaṃ tapaḥ
yaḥ śraddhayārcitaṃ dadyāt pratigṛhṇāti cārcitaḥ
guṇānāvahate pañca dāturdānaṃ mahāphalam
vārāṇasī kurukṣetraṃ prayāgaḥ puṣkarāṇi ca
gaṅgā samudratīrañca naimiṣāmarakaṇṭakāḥ
śrīparvato mānasañca gokarṇo vedaparvataḥ
ityādyāḥ kīrtitā deśāḥ surasiddhaniṣevitāḥ
sarve śivāśramāḥ puṇyāḥ sarvā nadyaḥ saparvatāḥ
gosiddharṣinivāsāśca deśāḥ puṇyāḥ prakīrtitāḥ
śivāyatanasaṃsthānāṃ yad alpam api dīyate
tadanantaphalaṃ jñeyaṃ śivakṣetrānubhāvataḥ
grahaṇañ candrasūryābhyāṃ dakṣiṇāyanam uttaram
viṣuvatsavyatīpātaṃ ṣaḍaśītimukhaṃ tathā
dinakṣayāṇi saṃkrāntirjñeyaṃ viṣṇupadaṃ punaḥ
iti kālaḥ samākhyātaḥ puṃsāṃ puṇyavivardhanaḥ
bhaktirbhāvaḥ parā prītiḥ śivadharmaikatānatā
śraddhāmātraṃ ca jananī jñānasya sukṛtasya ca
śraddhāpradhānaṃ vijñeyaṃ satpātrādicatuṣṭayam
śraddhātaḥ kīrtitā tasmānnāśraddhātaḥ phalaṃ bhavet
oṃ namaḥ śivāyaḥ
atha dharmāḥ śivenoktāḥ śivadharmāgamottare
hiṃsādoṣavinirmuktāḥ kleśāyāsavivarjitāḥ
dharmā bhūtahitāḥ śuddhāḥ susūkṣmāḥ sumahāphalāḥ
anantaśāvāvalitāḥ śivamūle ca saṃsthitāḥ
sarve sarvaguṇopetāḥ śivadharmāḥ sanātanāḥ
tārayanty aśivādyamād dhāryante śivabhāvitaiḥ
śivadharmāḥ smṛtās tasmāt saṃsārārṇavatāraṇāḥ
athāhiṃsā kṣamā satyaṃ hrīḥ śraddhendriyasaṃyamaḥ
dānam ijyā japo dhyānaṃ daśakaṃ dharmasādhanam
yathā bhūḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ sthānaṃ sādhāraṇaṃ smṛtā
tathā tacchivabhaktānāṃ tulyaṃ śivapuraṃ smṛtam
yatheha sarvabhūtānāṃ bhogāḥ sādhāraṇāḥ smṛtāḥ
nānāpuṇyaviśeṣeṇa bhogāḥ śivapure tathā
yāpādṛgbhavetpuṇyaṃ śraddhāmātraviśeṣaṇam
bhogās tādṛgvidhāstasya jñeyāḥ sātiśayāḥ pure
sthānaprāptiphale tulye bhogāḥ sātiśayāḥ sthitāḥ
kuryāt puṇyaṃ mahat tasmān mahābhogajigīṣayā
āvartante punaś cānye saṃsāre bhogatatparāḥ
viraktaḥ śāntacittātmā śivajñānam avāpnuyāt
ye cānyāsaktahṛdayā yajantīśaṃ prasaṅgataḥ
teṣām api dadātīśa sthānaṃ bhāvānurūpataḥ
tatrārcayanti ye rudraṃ sakṛd ucchiṣṭakarmaṇā
teṣāṃ piśācalokeṣu bhogānīśaḥ prayacchati
rakṣoloke śivas teṣāṃ mahābhogān prayacchati
trirarcayanti ye rudraṃ madyamāṃsāturā narāḥ
yakṣaloke śivasteṣāṃ divyān bhogān prayacchati
ye gītanṛtyaṃ kurvanti triścaturvā yadṛcchayā
loke khyātiṃ samuddhiśya pañcārcayati yaḥ śivam
kāmāśaktena cittena yaḥ ṣaḍarcayate śivam
jñeyaṃ viṣṇupurādūrdhvaṃ divyaṃ śivapuraṃ mahat
asaṃkhyaistatpuraṃ vyāptaṃ sarvaprāṇyupakārakaiḥ
rudraloka iti proktaścāśeṣaguṇasaṃyuta
sarveṣāṃ śivabhaktānāṃ tatparaṃ parikīrtitam
rudrakṣetramṛtānāñ ca jaṅgamasthāvarātmanām
apyekadivasaṃ bhaktyā yaḥ pūjayati śaṅkaram
so'pi yāti śivasthānaṃ kiṃ punarbahuśo'rcayeta
tasmādīśe sadā bhaktiṃ bhāvayedātmanātmani
tanmāhātmyavicāreṇa bhavarogavirāgataḥ
evaṃ śivānubhāvena nikṛṣṭenāpi karmaṇā
naraiḥ sthānāny avāpyante deśe bhāvānurūpataḥ
dehināṃ karmaniṣṭhānāṃ punarāvartakaṃ smṛtam
ūrdhvaṃ śivapurāj jñeyaṃ sthānatrayam anuttamam
nityaṃ paraṃ viśuddhañ ca skandomāśaṅkarātmakam
skandaṃ śaktidharaṃ śāntaṃ ṣaṇmukhaṃ śikhivāhanam
taptacāmīkaraprakhyaṃ triśikhaṃ bālarūpiṇam
dhyāyamānaḥ prasannātmā guhasthānam avāpnuyāt
kanyārūpām umāṃ śāntāṃ sūryakoṭisamaprabhām
dhyāyamānaḥ sadā bhaktyā devyāḥ sthānam avāpnuyāt
śuddhasphaṭikasaṃkāśaṃ jaṭāmakuṭadhāriṇam
tryakṣaṃ caturbhujaṃ śāntam akṣamālādharaṃ haram
varadābhayahastañca triśūlāsaktapāṇikam
dhyāyamānaḥ śivaṃ nityaṃ śivasthānam avāpnuyāt
ye samprāptāḥ śivasthānaṃ jñānayogaratā narāḥ
na teṣāṃ punarāvṛttir ghore saṃsārasāgare
sarvajñāḥ sarvagāḥ śuddhāḥ paripūrṇā maheśvarāḥ
śivatulyabalāḥ siddhāḥ paraṃ śivapuraṃ gatāḥ
upariṣṭācca lokānāṃ svarūpaṃ cāpyanukramāt
tatrāṣṭaguṇam aiśvaryaṃ pārthivaṃ piśitāśinām
tatsāyujyagatānāñ ca narāṇāṃ tatsamaṃ smṛtam
rakṣas su ṣoḍaśakalaṃ pārthivāñ ca taddvidhā
gandharvāṇañca vāyavyaṃ yākṣañca sakalaṃ smṛtam
pāñcabhautikamindrasya catvāriṃśadguṇaṃ mahat
somasya mānasaṃ divyaṃ yaccādhaḥ pāñcabhautikam
saumyaṃ prājāpatisthānam ahaṃkāraguṇānvitam
catuṣṣaṣṭiguṇaṃ brāhmaṃ bauddhamaiśvaryamuttamam
viṣṇoḥ pradhānikaṃ sūkṣmam aiśvaryam uttamam
śivena śivabhaktānāṃ kalpitaṃ vaiṣṇavāt param
sūkṣmamavyaktikaṃ divyamaiśvaryaṃ sārvakāmikam
anantaguṇamaiśvaryaṃ śivasyātmagataṃ mahat
ādimadhyāntarahitaṃ viśuddhaṃ tatvalakṣaṇam
sarvābhibhāṣakaṃ sūkṣmamanupamyaṃ parātparam
susampūrṇaṃ jagaddhetuḥ paśupāśavimokṣaṇam
yo yāṃ saṃsthānamanuprāptastasya bhogāstadātmakāḥ
aiśvaryaṃ tatpramāṇañca bhavedīśaprasādataḥ
tārārūpavimānānābhimā dṛśyanti koṭayaḥ
aṣṭāviṃśatirevordhvaṃ sudīptāḥ suhṛdātmanām
nāmasaṅkīrtanādvāpi prasaṅgena śivasya yaḥ
ityetā gatayaḥ proktā mahatyaḥ śivakarmaṇām
atyalpālpatareṇāpi puṃsāmīśānabhāvataḥ
atha paśupatimate śivadharme mahāśāstre śivadharmottare ṣaṣṭho'dhyāyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāya
athādhaḥ patanāt puṃsāmadharmaḥ parikīrtitaḥ
narakādau mahāghore pāpānāṃ yātanāḥ smṛtāḥ
sthūlāḥ sūkṣmāḥ susūkṣmāśca koṭibhedairanekadhā
tatra ye pāpanicayāḥ sthūlā narakahetavaḥ
te samāsena kathyante manovākkāyasādhanāḥ
akāvyārbhiniveśaśca caturdhā karma mānasam
parāpavādaḥ paiśunyaṃ caturdhā karma vācikam
abhakṣyabhakṣaṇaṃ hiṃsā mithyākāmanivedanam
parasvānāmupādānaṃ caturdhā karma kāyikam
ye dviṣanti mahādevaṃ saṃsārārṇavatāranam
sumahāpātakopetāste yānti narakāgniṣu
dūṣayanti śivajñānaṃ ye sarvārthaprasādhakam
sumahat pātakaṃ teṣāṃ nirayārṇavagāminām
ye śivajñānavaktāraṃ vidviṣanti gurunnarāḥ
sumahāpātakopetāste yānti narakārṇavam
śivanindā gurunindā śivajñānasya dūṣaṇam
haranti ye ca sammuḍhāḥ śivajñānasya pustakam
sumahāpātakānyāhuranantāni phalāni ca
nābhinandanti ye dṛṣṭvā śivapūjāṃ prakalpitām
na namantyarcitaṃ dṛṣṭvā pūjitaṃ na stuvanti ca
yatheṣṭaceṣṭā niḥśaṅkāḥ santiṣṭhanti ramanti ca
sthānasaṃskārapūjāñca ye na kurvanti parvasu
śivavidyāgurūṇāñca karmayogavyavasthitāḥ
ye tyajanti śivācāraṃ śivabhaktān dviṣanti ca
śivanindāsamānyeva mahānti pātakāni hi
asampūjya śivajñānaṃ ye'dhīyante likhanti
asaṃskṛtapradeśeṣu yatheṣṭaṃ sthāpayanti ca
śivajñānakathākṣepaṃ yaḥ kṛtvā'nyat prabhāṣate
nādhīyīta samarthaśca yaḥ pramādaṃ karoti ca
aśuciścāśucau sthāne yaḥ pravakti śṛṇoti ca
iti sarvaṃ samāsena jñānanindāsamaṃ smṛtam
gurupūjām akṛtvyaiva yaḥ śāstraṃ śrotumicchati
na karoti ca śuśrūṣām ājñāṃ bhaktiśca bhāvataḥ
gurukarmaṇi śāṭhyañca tadupekṣāṃ karoti ca
guruṃ śāntam aśaktaṃ vā videśaprasthitantathā
aribhiḥ paribhūtaṃ vā yaḥ santyajati pāpakṛt
tadbhāryāputramitreṣu yaścāvajñāṃ karoti ca
ityetat pātakaṃ jñeyaṃ gurunindāsamaṃ mahat
mahāpātakinastvete tatsaṃsargī ca pañcamaḥ
krodhād dveṣād bhayāllobhād brāhmaṇasya vadecca yaḥ
marmāntikaṃ mahādoṣaṃ brahmaghnaḥ sa prakīrtitaḥ
brāhmaṇaṃ yaḥ samāhūya yācyamānaṃ sakiñcanam
paścānnāstīti ca brūyāt sa vai brahmaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ
udāsīnaṃ sabhāmadhye brahmahā sa prakīrtitaḥ
mithyā guṇaiḥ svamātmānaṃ nayatyutkarṣaṇaṃ balāt
gurūṇāñca viruddho yaḥ sa vai brahmaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ
kṣutṛṭsantaptadehānāṃ dvijānāṃ bhoktumicchatām
piśunaḥ sarvalokānāṃ randhrānveṣaṇatatparaḥ
udvegajananaḥ krūraḥ sa vai brahmaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ
gavāṃ tṛṣābhibhūtānāṃ jalārthamupasarpatām
paradoṣaṃ parijñāya nṛpakarṇe japetu yaḥ
pāpīyān piśunaḥ kṣudraḥ sarvai brahmahaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ
devadvijagatāṃ bhūmiṃ parvabhuktāṃ harettu yaḥ
praṇaṣṭāmapi kālena tamāhurbrahmaghātakam
dvijavṛtyāpaharaṇe nyāyataḥ samupārjatam
brahmahatyāsamaṃ jñeyaṃ pātakaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ
adhīsa yaḥ śivajñānaṃ parityajati mūḍhadhīḥ

agnihotraparityāgī pañcayajñīyakarmaṇām

pratyekaṃ sarvakoṭīnānnāmatastānnibodhata
tamaḥ śītaṃ tathā coṣṇaḥ pañcādyā nāyakāḥ smṛtāḥ
sughoraḥ sutamastīkṣṇaḥ padmaḥ sañjīvanastathā
mahāmāyī vilomaśca sulomaśca kaṭaṅkataḥ
tīvrakeśaḥ karālaśca vikarālaḥ prakampanaḥ
mahāpadmaḥ suvaktraśca kālasūtraḥ pragarjanaḥ
sūcīmukhaḥ sunemaśca khādakaḥ suprapīḍanaḥ
kumbhīpākaḥ supākaśca krakacaścātidāruṇaḥ
aṅgārarāśibhavanaścāmrak pūyamahāhradaḥ
tīkṣṇāyaḥ khaṇḍaḥ śakunir mahāsaṃvartakaḥ kratuḥ
taptajatuḥ paṅkalepaḥ pūtimāṃso dravastapuḥ
ucchvāsakaśca niśvāsaḥ sudīrghaḥ kūṭaśālmaliḥ
dureṣṭaḥ sumahānādaḥ prvahaḥ supravāhaṇaḥ
ṛkṣameṣavṛkāścānye siṃhavyāghragajānanāḥ
śvaśūkarāśca mahiṣā vṛṣameṣā ajāstathā
grāhakumbhīranakrāsyāḥ sarpakūrmāśca vāyasāḥ
gṛdhrolūkajalokāśca śārdūlakapikarpaṭāḥ
gaṇḍakāḥ pūtivaktrāśca ekākṣāḥ pūtimṛttikāḥ
kaṇadūmāstuṣāgniśca kṛmīṇānnicayastathā
abhedyaścāpratiṣṭhaśca rudhirāṇāñca bhojanaḥ
lalābhakṣātmabhakṣaśca sarvabhakṣaḥ sudāruṇaḥ
saṅkaṭaḥ suviśālaśca vikaṭaḥ kaṭapūtanāḥ
ambarīṣaḥ kaṭāhaśca kaṣṭā vaitaraṇī nadī
sutaptalohaśayanam ekapādaḥ prapūraṇaḥ
asitālavanaṃ ghroraṃ makṣibhṛṅgaprapīḍitam
tilātasīkṣuyantrāṇi kūṭapāśapramardanaḥ
mahācullī sucullī ca taptalohamayī śilā
mūtraviṣṭhāndhakupaśca śālakūpaśca śātanaḥ
muśalolūkhalaṃ yantraṃ śilāśakaṭalāṅgalam
tālapatrāsigahanaṃ mahāpātakamaṇḍapam
bahuduḥkhaṃ mahāduḥkhaṃ kaśmalaṃ śavalaṃ malaṃ
halāhalaṃ virūpañca surūpaśca yamānugaḥ
ekapādastripādaśca tīvraścāvīcirantimaḥ
aṣṭāviṃśatirityete kramaśaḥ pañcanāyakāḥ
koṭīnāmānupūrveṇa pañca pañcaiva nāyakāḥ
rauravādyamavīcyantaṃ narakāṇāṃ śataṃ smṛtam
catvāriṃśacchatādhikaṃ mahānarakamaṇḍalam
eṣu pāpāḥ prapacyante narakarmānurūpataḥ
yātanābhirvicitrābhirnarāḥ karmakṣayā bhṛśam
tathā pāpakṣayāt pāpāḥ śodhyante narakāgniṣu
sugāḍhaṃ taptayorbaddhā taptaśṛṅkhalayā naraḥ
mahāvṛkṣāgraśākhāsu ullambyante'sya kiṅkaraiḥ
dolāntaścāti vegena niḥsaṃjñā yānti yojanam
pādayor badhyate teṣāṃ yamadūtair mahābalaiḥ
tena bhāreṇa mahatā bhṛśamātānitā narāḥ
dhyāyantaḥ svāni karmāṇi tūṣṇīntiṣṭhanti niścalāḥ
tataḥ keśairagnikalpairlohadaṇḍaiḥ sakaṇṭakaiḥ
hanyate kiṅkarairghoraiḥ samantātpāpakāriṇaḥ
tataḥ kṣāreṇa dīptena casterapi viśeṣataḥ
samantataḥ pralipyante kṣatāṅgājarjarīkṛtāḥ
punar vicārya cāṅgeṣu śirasaḥ prabhṛtikramāt
viṣṭhāpūrṇe tathā kūpe kṛmīṇāṃ nicaye tataḥ
medo'sṛkpūryamāṇāyāṃ vāpyāṃ kṣipyanti te punaḥ
bhakṣyante kṛmibhistīkṣṇairlohatuṇḍaiśca vāyasaiḥ
śvabhirdaṃśairvṛkairvyāghrai raudraiśca vikṛtānanaiḥ
pacyante māṃsavaccāpi pradīptāṅgārarāśiṣu
pretāḥ śūleṣu tīkṣṇeṣu narāḥ pāpena karmaṇā
bhṛjyante cātitapteṣu lohabhrāṣṭreṣvanekadhā
tailapūrṇakaṭāheṣu sutapteṣu tataḥ punaḥ
bahuśotpāṭyate jihvā asatyāt priyavādinām
sandaṃśena sutaptena prapīḍyoraśca pādataḥ
jihvārdhakrośavistīrṇā halais tīrṇā halaistīkṣṇaiḥ prapādyate
teṣāṃ vajrajalaukābhirmukhamāpūrya sicyate
tataḥ kṣāreṇa dīptena tāmreṇatrapuṇā punaḥ
itas tataḥ punar vaktraṃ bhṛśamāpūrya hanyate
viṣṭhābhiḥ kṛmipūrṇābhiḥ sopānaccaraṇairnaraiḥ
pariṣvajante cātyugrāṃ pradīptāṃ lohaśālmalīm
hanyante pṛṣṭhadeśe ca punarbhīmairmahāghanaiḥ
dantureṇātikaṣṭena krakacena balīyasā
śiraḥprabhṛti pādānte ghoraiḥ karmabhir ātmajaiḥ
rudanti cchinnasarvāṅgāstataḥ śūlapraropitāḥ
sañcālyamānā vivaśāstiṣṭhanti na mriyanti ca
dehād utpāṭyate māṃsaṃ bhidyante'sthīni mudgaraiḥ
dṛtirākṛṣyate tūrṇaṃ yamadūtair balotkaṭaiḥ
ucchvasantaḥ sadocchvāse bālukāvadanāvṛtāḥ
padbhyāmāsye gude cākṣṇoḥ pāṇau corasi mastake
nikhanynte ghanaistīkṣṇaiḥ sutaptā lohaśaṅkavaḥ
jatupaṅke bhṛśaṃtapte kṣiptāḥ krandanti visvaram
tena tenaiva rūpeṇa hasantīṃ pāradārikām
gāḍhamāliṅgate nārīṃ jvalantīṃ lohanirmitām
pūrvākārāśca puruṣāḥ prajvalantaḥ subhāṣaṇāḥ
duścāriṇīṃ striyaṃ gāḍham āliṅganti vadanti ca
laṃghitaste yathā bhartā pāpaṃ bhuṅkṣva tathādhunā
lohakumbhe vinikṣiptā sapidhānaiḥ śanaiḥ śanaiḥ
mṛdvagnivā prapacyante svapāpaireva mānavāḥ
kṣipyante cāndhakūpeṣu daśyante bhramarairbhṛśam
kṛmibhirbhinnasarvāṅgāḥ śataśo jarjarīkṛtāḥ
sutīkṣṇakṣārakūpeṣu kṣipyante tadantaram
mahājvāle ca narake pāpāḥ phūtkārayā bhṛśam
itaścetaśca dhāvanti dahyamānāḥ sadārcibhiḥ
tayormadhyena vā kṛṣya bāhupṛṣṭhena gāḍhate
baddhāḥ parasparaṃ sarve sudṛḍhaṃ pāparajjubhiḥ
piṇḍabandhaṃ daśantyena śrumarāstīkṣṇalohajāḥ
mānināṃ krodhināścaiva taskarāṇāñca dāruṇāḥ
piṇḍabandhaḥ smṛto yāmye mahājvāle ca yātanā
rajjubhirveṣṭitāṅgāśca praliptāḥ kardamena ca
sutīkṣṇakṣāratoyena karkaśāsu śilāsu ca
āpāpasaṃkṣayātpāpā ghṛṣyante candanaṃ yathā
śarīrābhyantaragatai prabhūtaiḥ kṛmibhirnarāḥ
bhakṣyante tīkṣṇavadanair ādehaprakṣayād bhṛśam
kṛmināmni ca prakṣiptāḥ pūtimān sasyarāśiṣu
tiṣṭhantyudvignahṛdayāḥ parvatābhyāñca pīḍitāḥ
adhomukhordhvapādāśca dhṛtāstāpyanti vahninā
khādantīmaparādhīnā hanyamānāśca mudgaraiḥ
ye śivāyatanārāmavāpīkūpamathāṅgaṇaḥ
abhidravānta pāpiṣṭhā nṛpāstatra pasanti ca
vyāyāmodvartanābhyaṅgaṃ snānapānānnabhojanam
krīḍanaṃ maithunaṃ dyūtaṃ sādharatni ramanti ca
ye śṛṇvanti gurornindāṃ teṣāṃ karṇau prapūryataḥ
trapusīsārakūṭādyaiḥ kṣāreṇa jatunā punaḥ
sutaptatīkṣṇatailena vajralepena cābhṛśan
kramādāpūryaryataḥ karṇau narakeṣu ca yātanāḥ
aṣṭamo'dhyāyaḥ
oṃ namaḥ śivāya
atha nārakiṇāṃ puṃsām adharmād eva kevalāt
kṣaṇamātreṇa bhūtebhyaḥ śarīram upajāyate
sadyaḥ prajāyate divyaṃ śarīraṃ bhūtasārataḥ
karmaṇā vyatimiśreṇa yaccharīram ihātmanaḥ
tadbhūtapariṇāmena vijñeyaṃ hi caturvidhaṃ
udbhijjāḥ sthāvarā jñeyās tṛṇagulmādirūpiṇaḥ
kṛmikīṭapataṅgādyāḥ svedajā nāma dehinaḥ
aṇḍajāḥ pakṣiṇaḥ sarpā nakrā matsyāḥ sakacchapāḥ
jarāyujāś ca vijñeyā mānuṣāḥ sa catuṣpadāḥ
vāyunā vyūhyamānā ca khe bījatvaṃ prapadyate
yathartukāni bījāni saṃsiktāny ambhasā punaḥ
ucchūnatāṃ mṛdutvañ ca mūlabhāva vrajanti ca
parṇān nālan tataḥ kāṇḍaṃ kāṇḍāc ca prasavaḥ punaḥ
prasavāc ca tuṣaṃ kṣīraṃ kṣīrāt taṇḍulasambhavaḥ
taṇḍulāś ca yadā pakvā mriyantyoṣadhayas tadā
yavādyāḥ ṣaṇaparyantāḥ śreṣṭhāḥ saptadaśa smṛtāḥ
oṣadhyāḥ phalapākāntāḥ śeṣāḥ kṣudrāḥ prakīrtitāḥ
etā lūnā marditāś ca śanai ruddhṛtya saṃskṛtāḥ
sūrpolūkhalayantrādyaiḥ sthālyām udakavanhibhiḥ
ṣaḍvidhāhārabhedena pariṇāmyanti tadvidhaiḥ
iti bhedāḥ ṣaḍaṅgasya madhurādyāś ca ṣaḍguṇāḥ
antaḥ sthūlāśaye pūrvaṃ prāṇaḥ sthāpayati kramāt
ātiṣṭhed bhukta āhāras taṃ vāyuḥ kurute dvidhā
vāyunā dhmāyamāno 'gnir atyuṣṇaṃ kurute jalaṃ
dvidhā bhavati tat pakvaṃ pṛthakkīṭaṃ pṛthag rasaḥ
malair dvādaśabhiḥ kīṭaṃ bhinnaṃ dehād vahir vrajet
karṇākṣināsikājivhād antāḥ śiśnaṃ gudaṃ nakhāḥ
malāśrayāḥ kaphaḥ cāḥ svedo viṇmūtre dvādaśa smṛtāḥ
hṛtpadme pratibaddhāś ca sarvā nāḍyaḥ samantataḥ
tāsāṃ mukheṣu taṃ sūkṣmaṃ prāṇaḥ sthāpayate rasaṃ
rasena tena tā nāḍīḥ prāṇaḥ pūrayate punaḥ
pratarppayanti sampūrṇās tāś ca dehaṃ samantataḥ
tataḥ sa nāḍīmadhyasthaḥ śārīreṇoṣmaṇā rasaḥ
raktāl lomāni māṃsaṃ ca keśāḥ snāyuś ca māṃsataḥ
snāyoḥ śirās tathā'sthīni nakhā majjāsthiṃsambhavāḥ
majjātaḥ karaṇaṃ vaikalyaṃ śukraṃ ca prasavātmakaṃ
iti dvādaśadhānnasya pariṇāmaḥ prakīrtitaḥ
śukrāntaś ca tataḥ śukrādasya dehasya sambhavaḥ
ṛtukāle sadā śukraṃ nirdoṣaṃ yonisaṃsthitaṃ
tadā tadvāyunā spṛṣṭaṃ strīraktenaikatāṃ vrajet
visargakāle śukrasya jīvaḥ kāraṇasaṃyutaḥ
pañcarātreṇa kalalama
budbudaṃ saptarātreṇa māṃsapeśī bhavet tataḥ
dvisaptāhād bhavet peśā raktamāṃsācitādṛḍhā
bījasyevāṅkuraḥ peśyāṃ pañcaviṃśatirātritaḥ
bhavanti māsamātreṇa pañcadhā jāyate punaḥ
grīvā śiraś ca skandhaś ca pṛṣṭhavaṃśastathodaraṃ
māsaiś caturbhir aṅgulyaḥ prajāyante yathākramaṃ
mukhaṃ nāsā ca karṇau ca māsair jāyanti pañcabhiḥ
vibhaktāvayavaḥ spaṣṭaḥ punar māsāṣṭakena ca
pañcātmakasamāyuktaḥ paripakva satiṣṭhati
māturāhāravīryeṇa ṣaḍvidhena rasenaca
tataḥ smṛtiṃ labhej jīvaḥ sampūrṇe'smin śarīrake
sukhaduḥkhaṃ vijānāti nidrāsvapnaṃ purā kṛtaṃ
mṛtaś cāhaṃ punarjāto jātaś cāhaṃ punarmṛtaḥ
nānāyonisahasrāṇi mayā dṛṣṭāni jāyate
adhunā jātamātro'haṃ prāpte saṃskāra eva vā
adhyeṣyāmi śivajñānaṃ saṃsāravinivartakaṃ
evaṃ sa garbhaduḥkhena mahatā paripīḍitaḥ
jīvaḥ karmavaśād āste mokṣopāyaṃ vicintayan
yathā girivarākrāntaḥ kaś cid duḥkhena tiṣṭhati
tathā jarāyuṇā dehī duḥkhan tiṣṭhati veṣṭitaḥ
patitaḥ sāgare yadvad duḥkham āste samākulaḥ
garbhavāsāt paro vāsaḥ kaṣṭenaivātra kutracit
dehinā duḥkhabahulaṃ sughoram atisaṅkaṭaṃ
ityetad garbhaduḥkhaṃ hi prāṇināṃ parikīrtitaṃ
calāsthirāṇāṃ sarveṣām ātmagarbhānurūpataḥ
garbhāt koṭiguṇaṃ duḥkhaṃ yoniyantraprapīḍanāt
śarīraṃ pīḍyamānasya yantreṇaiva samantataḥ
śirasā tāḍyamānasya pāpam udgarakeṇa ca
garbhāt koṭiguṇaṃ duḥkhaṃ paritrāṇam avindataḥ
yantreṇa pīḍitā yadvan niḥsārāḥ syustilekṣavaḥ
tathā śarīraṃ niḥsāraṃ yoniyantraprapīḍitaṃ
raktamāṃsamṛdāliptaṃ viṇmūtradravyabhājanaṃ
keśaromatṛṇācchannaṃ rogāyatanamāturaṃ
badanaikamahādvāraṃ gavākṣāṣṭaka bhūṣitaṃ
oṣṭhadvayakapāṭaṃ ca dantajihvārggalānvitaṃ
jarāśokasamāviṣṭakālavaktrānalombhitaṃ
bhogatṛṣṇāturaṃ mūḍhaṃ rāgadveṣavaśānugaṃ
saṃvartitāṅgapratyaṅgajarāyupariveṣṭitaṃ
viṇmūtraraktasiktāṅgaṃ ṣaṭkośāṃśasamudbhavaṃ
śatatrayaṃ ṣaṣṭyadhikaṃ pañca peśīśatāni ca
sārddhābhis tisṛbhiś channaṃ samantād romakoṭibhiḥ
śarīraṃ sthūlasūkṣmābhir dṛśāś cayāvatāḥ sthitāḥ
tāvat ībhiś ca nāḍīnāṃ koṭibhis tatsamanvitaṃ
dvātriṃśad daśamāḥ proktāḥ viṃśatiś ca nakhāḥ smṛtāṃ
pittasya kuḍavaṃ jñeyaṃ kaphasyārddhāḍhakaṃ smṛtaṃ
pañcābrade palājñeyāḥ palāni daśa medasaḥ
palatrayaṃ mahāraktamajjāyās tu caturguṇaṃ
śukrārddhaṃkuḍavaṃ jñeyaṃ tadvīryan dehināṃ varaṃ
māṃsāsthnām ekapiṇḍena palasāhasratram ucyate
raktaṃ palaśataṃ jñeyaṃ viṇmūtrañ cāpramāṇataḥ
śukraśoṇitasaṃyogād dehaḥ sañjāyate yataḥ
nityaṃ viṇmūtrapūrṇaś ca tenāśuddhaḥ prakīrtitaḥ
yathāntarviṣṭhayā pūrṇaḥ śuciḥ syān na bahirghaṭaḥ
yaṃ prāpyāti pavitrāṇi pañcagavyaṃ havīṃṣi ca
aśucitvaṃ kṣaṇādyānti kim anyad aśucis tataḥ
hṛdy ānyapy annapānāni yaṃ prāpya surabhīṇi ca
aśucitvaṃ prayāntyāśu kim anyad aśucis tataḥ
svadehāt kaśmalaṃ pūtis tad ādhāraḥ kathaṃ śuciḥ
dehaḥ saṃśodhyamāno'pi pañcagavyakuśāmbubhiḥ
ghṛṣyamāṇam ivāṅgāraṃ nirmalatvaṃ na gacchati
śucir ekaḥ pradeśo' pi viṇmūtrasya dṛter iva
smṛtvātmadehasrotāṃsi mṛttoyair dhāvataḥ karau
tathāpyaśuci bhāṇḍasya na virajyanti kiñjanāḥ
kāyaḥ sraggandhapuṣpādyair yatnenāpi susaṃskṛtaḥ
na jahāti svabhāvaṃ hi śvapuccham iva nāmitaṃ
yathā jātyaiva kṛṣṇārṇī na śuklāsyādupāyataḥ
tathā saṃśodhyamānāpi bhaven mūrtir anirmalā
na virajyati loko'yaṃ pīḍayann api nāsikāṃ
sarvam etaj jagatpūtaṃ deha evāśuciḥ paraḥ
gandhalepāpanodārthaṃ śaucaṃ dehasya kīrtitaṃ
dvayasyāpagamāt paścād bhāvaśuddhyā viśuddhyati
gaṇgātoyena sarveṇa mṛdbhāraiś ca na gopamaiḥ
āmṛtyorācaracchaicaṃ bhāvaduṣṭo na śuddhyati
tīrthasnānais tapobhir vā bhāvaduṣṭo na śuddhyati
adṛtiḥ kṣālitastīrthe kiṃ śuddhim adhigacchati
antarbhāvapraduṣṭasya viśato'pi hutāśane
bhāvaśuddhiḥ paraṃ śaucaṃ pramāṇaṃ sarvakarmasu
anyathāliṅgyate kāntā bhāvena duhitānyathā
paśya paśya ca bhāvasya mahābhāgyam aśoṣataḥ
pariṣvakto'pi yannāryā bhāvahīno na kāmayet
bhāvanā dvividhānasyambhakṣyāṇi surabhīṇi ca
abhāvena narastasmād bhāvaḥ sarvatra kāraṇaṃ
bhāvataḥ suviśuddhātmā svargaṃ mokṣaṃ ca vindati
jñanām alāmbhasā puṃsaḥ sadvairāgyamṛdā punaḥ
avidyā rāgaviṇmūtralepagandhaviśodhanaṃ
evam etac charīraṃ hi nisargād aśuci smṛtaṃ
tvaṅmātrasāraṃ nissāraṃ kadalīsārasannibhaṃ
jñātvaivaṃ doṣavad dehaṃ yaḥ prājñaḥ śithilībhavet
so'tikrāmati saṃsāraṃ dṛḍhagrāhī ca tiṣṭhati
evam etan mahākaṣṭaṃ janmaduḥkhaṃ prarkīrtitaṃ
puṃsām ajñānadoṣeṇa nānākarmavaśena ca
garbhasthasya smṛtir yāsīt sā jātasya praṇaśyati
bāhyena vāyunā tasya mohājñānena dehinaḥ
spṛṣṭamātrasya ghoreṇa jvaraḥ samupajāyate
sammūḍhasya smṛtibhraṃśaḥ śīghraṃ sañjāyate punaḥ
nacātmānaṃ vijānāti na paraṃ naca daivataṃ
na śṛṇoti paraṃ bhūyaḥ sati cakṣuṣi nekṣate
saṃsāre kṣipyate tena rāgalobhavaśānugaḥ
gaṃgāsmṛter abhāvena śāstramuktaṃ śivena tu
ye satyasmin śivajñāne sarvakāmārthasādhake
bālarogaiś ca vividhaiḥ pīḍābālagrahair api
tṛḍbubhukṣāparītāṅgaḥ kvacit tiṣṭhati cāraṭan
viṇmūtrabhakṣaṇādyaṃ ca mohād bālaḥ karoti vai
kaumāre karṇavedhe ca tanmātā pitṛtāḍanaṃ
akṣarādhyayanādyaiś ca duḥkhaṃ syād guruśāsanāt
pramattendriya bṛtteś ca kāmarāgaprapīḍanāt
rāgābṛtasya satataṃ kutaḥ saukhyaṃ hi yauvane
īrṣyayā ca mahad duḥkhaṃ mohādraktasya jāyate

hṛdaye tu sadā gāvo gavāṃ madhye basāmy ahaṃ
gavāṃ mantraṃ samuccārya japettāsāṃ puraḥ sthitaḥ
gandhapuṣpākṣatonmiśrāṃ gṛhītvā tāmrakarkkarīṃ
śṛṅge pucchaṃ pradhānāyā goś ca snāpyatadambhasā
ānayed dhūpapuṣpādyam ekaikaṃ śrāvakaḥ kramāt
sarvasādhujanārthāya jñānamatra prado'pi vā
ācāryebhyaḥ kare dadyād vācakaḥ kusumatrayaṃ
iti śaktayā ca bhaktayā ca kṛtvā pūjāṃ pradakṣiṇaṃ
tasya puṇyaphalaṃ vakṣye śrotṛṇāṃ vācakasya ca
dhanamāyuḥ prajāḥ kīrtiṃ prajñāṃ buddhiṃ śriyaṃ sukhaṃ
iha samprāpya vipulaṃ dehānte śāntimāpnuyāt
asampūjya śivajñānaṃ pradeśe cāpyasaṃskṛte
vācayannarakaṃ yāti tasmātsatkṛtya vācayet
gaccheyuḥ kuśapuṣpārthaṃ śivārccādhyānatatparāḥ
tataḥ śāstrasamāptyante pūjāṃ kṛtvā viśeṣataḥ
śivavidyāgurūṇāṃ ca bhatyā ca śivayoginām
bhojanaṃ kalpayec caiṣāndīnāndhānāṃ ca sarvataḥ
gurave dakṣiṇāndadyācchavetaṅgomithunaṃ śubham
bastrayugmāṅgulīyaṃ ca ghṛpūrṇaṃ ca bhājanaṃ
vācakāya pradātavyā dakṣiṇā pūrvabhāṣitā
abhāṣite'sya dātavyā guror ardhena dakṣiṇā
śeṣāṇāṃ ca yathāśakti dakṣiṇā śivayogināṃ
dadyād vibodhayetpaś cātpradīpāṣṭaśataṃ budhaḥ
nivedayecchivāyaitadaśeṣaṃ puṣpavāriṇā
jñānapuṇyaṃ mahāśāntaṃ tattasmādakṣayaṃ phalaṃ
evamudyāpa kṛtvā śivajñānasya bhaktitaḥ
aśeṣapāpanirmuktaḥ śṛṇuyatphalamāptuyāt
kulatriṃśakamuddhṛtya bhāryāputrādisaṃyutaḥ
bandubhiḥ svajanaiḥ snigdhair bhṛtyaiḥ dāsaiḥ samāśritaiḥ
mahāvimānairārūḍhaḥ sarvakāmasamanvitaḥ
tatra bhuṅkte mahābhogānicchayā śivavadvaśī
dātā hartā ca kartā ca yāvatsamplāva mahat
pralayānte tataḥ prāpya jñānayogamanuttamaṃ
parameśaprasādena mucyate nātrasaṃśayaḥ
na mārī na cadurbhikṣaṃ na rakṣāṃsi na cetayaḥ
nākālomriyate rājā pīḍyate na ca śatrubhiḥ
śṛṇoti yatra satataṃ śivadharmannarādhipaḥ
tatra deśe bhaven nityaṃ sarveṣaṃ dehināṃ śivaṃ
iti śrīśivadharmottare dvādaśo'dhyāyaḥ
śivāya namaḥ
