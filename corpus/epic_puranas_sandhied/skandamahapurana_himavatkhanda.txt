gṛhe tvekaguṇaṃ kṛtyaṃ nadyāṃ daśaguṇaṃ smṛtam
gavāṃ goṣṭhe śataguṇaṃ magnyāgāre śatādhikam
siddhakṣetreṣu tīrtheṣu devatānāṃ ca saṃnidhau
sahasraśatakoṭīnām anantaṃ śivasaṃnidhau
girikūṭe mahātuṅge kiṃśuke ca tathāvidham
koṭikoṭiguṇaṃ puṇyaṃ tatra kṛtyaṃ pralabhyate
sānuṣu himaśailasya tapasām arbudaṃ guṇam
tuṣāradarśane kūṭe kṛtaṃ daśaguṇaṃ phalam
himakaṇṭāṅkite kūṭe kharvaguṇaṃ phalaṃ labhet
upatyakāsu śītādrernikharvaṃ madhyataḥ punaḥ
himasaṃhatikūṭeṣu mahāpadmaguṇaṃ kramāt
āsanaṃ ca tato dhīmān prakalpayedvidhānataḥ
kauśeyaṃ pauṣṭikaṃ jñeyaṃ kāmbalaṃ duḥkhamocakam
yathokte cāsane dhīmānṛjukāyaḥ praveśayet
svastike caiva padmākhyaṃ saṃvidhāya vicakṣaṇaḥ
jānūrvorantare samyak kṛtvā pādatale śubhe
ṛjukāyaḥ samāsīnaḥ svastikaṃ tu mahāsanam
vidhāyetthaṃ mahāprājñaḥ prāṇāyāmatrayaṃ tataḥ
ṛṣīn ādau ca saṃsmṛtya maunī nimilitekṣaṇaḥ
vidhāya pūrakaṃ kumbhaṃ recakaṃ ca tataḥ param
triṣu sthāneṣu dhyeyaṃ vai śyāmaṃ raktaṃ tathā sitam
nābhau hṛdi lalāṭe ca pūrake kumbhake tathā
recake ca mahāprājño jñeyaṃ kramāt kramaṃ dvija
aṅgeṣu sparśanaṃ kuryāt ṣaṭsu sthāneṣu mantravit
hṛllalāte śiraskandhacakṣuṣpāṇiṣu saṃnyaset
arghyaṃ dadyāt sudhīḥ pūrvaṃ sūryāya puṣpasaṃyutam
āpohiṣṭheti mantreṇa manuṃ smaraṃstu cetasā
tato mālāṃ samādāya yathoktamaṇisambhavām
japaṃ kuryāddhiyā mantrī dhyānasthaḥ sujitendriyaḥ
rudrākṣairnirmitā mālā sarvakāmaprasādhanī
sphāṭikairnirmitā mālā sarvasiddhipradāyinī
mauktikaiḥ sambhavā mālā saubhāgyaśrīprasādhanī
mantrapratyakṣadā cānyā padmarāgodbhavā tathā
sauvarṇī rājatī mālā sarvān kāmān prayacchati
saṃkhyāṃ kuryāt tato bāhye yajjaptaṃ saphalaṃ bhavet
asaṃkhyātaṃ ca yajjaptaṃ tat sarvaṃ niḥphalaṃ bhavet
gṛhasthānām ayaṃ dharmo yoginām anya īritaḥ
gṛheṣu yaddharmakṛtaṃ sudhīraiḥ samīritaṃ taddhi gṛhasthadharmam
iti śrīskandapurāṇe himavatkhaṇḍe gṛhasthadharmaṃ nāma daśamo adhyāyaḥ
atha dharmaṃ pravakṣyāmi yogināṃ ca viśeṣataḥ
brāhme cotthāya yogīśaḥ smaran vai gurum īśvaram
śaucaṃ kṛtvā tato yogī siddhakṣetre samāsane
ṛjukāyaḥ samāsīnaḥ prāṅmukho vā udaṅmukhaḥ
brahmarandhre sadā pūrvaṃ lalāṭe ca tripuṇḍrakam
nābhau pṛṣṭhe tathāgastya kūrparayostathāpare
ābrahmarandhrapādāntaṃ yoginām āplavaṃ matam
nābherūrdhvaṃ gṛhasthānāṃ yatīnāṃ ca viśeṣaṇam
evaṃ snātvā mahāyogī rudrākṣān dhārayet tataḥ
ātmānam ātmanā yogī dhyāyet tam īśvaraṃ param
praṇavarūpam ādyantam aṅguṣṭhamātram īśvaram
prāṇāyāmatrayaṃ kuryāt tato yogī suniścalaḥ
pūrayediḍayā vāyuṃ piṅgalayā ca kumbhayet
recayecca tayā nūnaṃ brahmarandhraṃ suṣumṇayā
nirālambastadā yogī paraṃ yogam avāpnuyāt
snāpayenmānaseneśaṃ mānasena prapūjayet
kṣaumaiśca mānasodbhūtairmānasādyaiśca candanaiḥ
mānasotphullapuṣpaistaṃ dhūpairdīpaiśca mānasaiḥ
ṣaṇṇāṃ sthānodbhavairvarṇai rakṣagaṇaiśca mantravit
tārapūrvo namaścāntaḥ śivāyeti tataḥ param
mumukṣuḥ prajapenmantraṃ duḥkhasaṃsāratārakam
śivaśaktyā samāyuktaṃ mantraṃ ṣaḍakṣaraṃ param
ṣaṭsvaraiḥ saṃyutaṃ bījaṃ satārakaṃ japedbudhaḥ
iti yogaṃ paraṃ viddhi yogināṃ paramātmanām
evaṃ yaḥ kurute yogī jīvanmukto na saṃśayaḥ
ṣaḍṛtūnāṃ yathāyogyaṃ mānasasambhavairmune
pūjayedupacāraistaṃ kāle kāle yathodbhavaiḥ
adbhutaṃ bhavatoktaṃ me sarveṣāṃ jīvināṃ tathā
yogināṃ śaktidhṛk snānaṃ praśastaṃ ca vibhūtibhiḥ
kasmācca tat samudbhūtaṃ phalaṃ ca kīdṛśaṃ bhavet
śṛṇu kumbhaja vakṣyāmi pṛṣṭaṃ yadbhavatā hi mām
trailokyaṃ bhīmarudreṇa prakṣānalena cakṣuṣā
tadbhasma ca samādāya rudreṇāṅgaṃ pralepitam
dūrīkṛtaṃ jalasnānaṃ tasmādbhasma praśasyate
prāpnuvanti paraṃ kṣipraṃ pūtai rudrekṣaṇodbhavaiḥ
nāsti bhasmāt paraṃ snānaṃ mokṣaṃ ca bhasmanaḥ param
nāsti bhasmāsanāt stheyaṃ nāsti bhasmārcanāt param
na pāpairlipyate dhīro bhasmasnāyī śivātmakaḥ
pātakaiḥ pañcabhiścāpi brahmahatyādibhistathā
āsīt kaściddvijaśreṣṭhaḥ sarvavidyāsu pāragaḥ
satyaśīlastapaḥkliṣṭo dāntaśca sarvajantuṣu
havyakavyakriyācārāt kṛtavānarcane rataḥ
dānaśīlo 'abhavacchaśvadatithīṃśca priyaṃkaraḥ
tasya putro dvijaśreṣṭho viṣṇudāsa iti śrutaḥ
vidyāhīno mahatkaṣṭī pañcatve ca pitaryabhūt
sarvācāraḥ sadā lokaiḥ sarvabhakṣī viḍambanaḥ
niśācāro divāsupto veśyāgamanatatparaḥ
mlecchaḥ śaṭho mṛtaḥ pūrvaṃ vāgmatīsutaṭe mune
ekadā divase vipro bahukṣudhāprapīḍitaḥ
yācitavāṃstadā rātrau bhāryāṃ pratyaśanaṃ dvijaḥ
paribhramya niśāyāṃ sa deśaṃ grāmaṃ gṛhaṃ prati
viṣṇudāsa uvāca
tīkṣṇakṣudhānalārto 'ahaṃ yena jīvaṃ pradhāraye
vātyābhyantaram āgāraṃ bhramitaṃ bahu rāṣṭrakam
satyam uktaṃ priyenādya pīḍito 'asi kṣudhānalaiḥ
bhraman pariśramādduḥkhī prabho tvaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ
mayā sahodayāpyadya nānnaṃ bhuktaṃ śape priya
nāsti kim annam āgāre kiṃ dāsye te kṣamasva me
bhāryayā madhuroktāyāḥ krodhena mūrchito bhṛśam
kṣudhā prapīḍitaḥ krodhī bhāryāṃ madhurabhāṣiṇīm
kukṣim uddiśya pādena bhartsayaṃśca jaghāna tām
mṛtāṃ bhāryāṃ sagarbhāṃ tāṃ gṛhe tyaktvā ca sāmpratam
niryayau sa puradvārāt pāpiṣṭhaśca tato bahiḥ
bhramati sma tato bhraṣṭo deśāddeśāntaraṃ bahu
taskaravṛttim āsthāya kaṃcidabdaṃ ca nītavān
dhṛtvā lokāḥ prabandhyāśu tāḍayanti sma taṃ bahu
tato rātrau pramucyāśu daśanai rajjukaṃ rahaḥ
pradudrāva bahiḥ pāpī sthānaṃ cintitavān vane
nihatya pathikāṃstatra kāṃścinmāsān nināya saḥ
anivedya ca paitre tanmāṃsaṃ jaghāsa pāpavān
kṣudhānalakṛśāṅgaśca tṛṣāturaśca kuṣṭhavān
tata ekākinaṃ vipraṃ pathi yāntaṃ dadarśa saḥ
bhikṣārthinaṃ mahādīnaṃ niḥsvaṃ tathaikavāsasam
dūrācchareṇa pāpiṣṭho jaghāna pathikaṃ vane
hatvā tatastadaṃṣuṅge anveṣayan vasūni ca
bhraṣṭaḥ kṛtvā kim astīti kim apyadarśitaṃ vasu
tato dadarśa rudrākṣaṃ mūrdhanyekādaśānanam
taṃ samādāya rudrākṣaṃ svamūrdhni ca babandha saḥ
tatra kuśeśvaro liṅgaḥ svayambhūstejasāṃ paraḥ
saṃgame indravatyāśca ādiliṅgo virājate
pañcapāpairniṣedhātmā kuṣṭhavraṇī savepathuḥ
taddadarśa mahāliṅgaṃ pūjitaṃ muninā mudā
phalamūlopahāraiśca saha pañcopacārakaiḥ
tapo 'asite mune pakṣe trayodaśyāṃ mahāniśi
ṛṣiṇā kuśanāmnā vai vinidreṇa ca pūjitam
kathaṃ gṛhṇāmi bhakṣyāṃstāṃstatrāhaṃ bhojanāya ca
ekākī snātakastatra caikākinaṃ hi māṃ tathā
tāḍiṣyati sakopaṃ cet sahyo bhokṣyāmi tān balāt
kṣudhayā pīḍitaṃ māṃ ca tāḍiṣyati bhṛśaṃ yadi
dīnaṃ prekṣyātha vā nāsau na mokṣyāmi tathā ca tān
evaṃ vimṛṣyamāṇo 'asau bhraṣṭaḥ svāntena kumbhaja
vepamānaḥ kṛśāṅgaśca abhyagamat tadantikam
liṅgāntikaṃ śanairgatvā sa jahāra balīn balāt
liṅgopari lalāṭaṃ tadgharṣitavāṃstadā dvijaḥ
śanairutthāya kampātmā bhayākulaḥ suvihvalaḥ
saṃsparśya maṇibandhābhyāṃ liṅgādbhasmāṅkitau karau
balīn ānīya pāpiṣṭho bahirāgatya bhuktavān
śivakṣetraparoddeśe saṃsthitāḥ pañcapātakāḥ
samprekṣya tādṛśaṃ taṃ ca vañcayituṃ samāvrajan
tataste māyayā divyaṃ cānnaṃ bahurasaṃ ca tam
lobhayituṃ ca saṃdarśya tam ārūḍhā yayatnire
pāpā ūcuḥ
samānītam ihāsmābhistvatsnehācca prapaśyatām
kṣudhayā pīḍito vipra kṛśāṅgī ca vraṇī tathā
te sahāyā bhavāmo vai jñātvānītam iti dhruvam
tvacchiraḥsthaṃ ca rudrākṣaṃ prakṣipyatāṃ jale 'adhunā
kṛtvaivaṃ vipra śaucaṃ tvaṃ bhasmarudrākṣavarjitaḥ
bhuṅkṣva yathepsitaṃ cānnaṃ priyasakhe gṛhāṇa vai
bhraṣṭa uvāca
nāhaṃ bhuñjāmi cādyānnaṃ yadi caturvidhaṃ varam
samānītaṃ ca yuṣmābhirmatsnehādapi bhoḥ sakhāḥ
phalāsanaṃ mayānītaṃ nānāvidharasottamam
bhūteśvaraprasādaṃ tadṛṣeranugrahāt tathā
vraṇī kampī kṛśāṅgī ca nāhaṃ snātuṃ ca śaktavān
na tyajāmi ca rudrākṣaṃ bhasma dhautaṃ na śaktavān
rātrau bhuktaṃ sukhaṃ pūrṇam atastṛptaḥ phalāni tat
śvo yaddadāti bhūteśo bhokṣyāmi tanna saṃśayaḥ
bhasmarudrākṣasaṃsparśācchivaliṅgaṃ tathā hi saḥ
niṣpāpo jñānavān bhraṣṭaḥ śaṃkarātmā babhūva hi
tasmin sthātuṃ mune bhraṣṭe na śekuḥ sparśituṃ ca tam
bhasmeśākṣakam aṅgeṣu prekṣya bhraṣṭasya pātakāḥ
tatraiva bhuvi saṃlīnā babhūvuste bhayākulāḥ
tataḥ so 'api dvijo bhraṣṭaḥ pañcatvaṃ prāptavān niśi
kauśikītīram āsādya bahuśītaiḥ prapīḍitaḥ
tatkṣaṇe yamadūtāste himālayādrigahvare
tatraiva śivadūtāśca samāyayuśca tatkṣaṇe
sūryāyutapratīkāśe vimāne samadhiṣṭhitāḥ
āropitaṃ vimāne taṃ śivadūtaiḥ suniścitam
gaṇā ninyurhi taṃ śaivaṃ yānena sūryavarcasā
saṃtyajya vigrahaṃ sadyaḥ paraṃ mokṣaṃ sa labdhavān
prekṣya teṣāṃ ca ceṣṭāṃ te cintayanti sma duḥkhitāḥ
vimarṣyādau mitho vipra jñātvā śivabalānvitān
kāla uvāca
bho bhoḥ śivaparā bhaktā enaṃ nārakiṇaṃ dvijam
pātakaiḥ pañcabhirgrastaṃ bhraṣṭaṃ svācārajātitaḥ
goghnaṃ brahmahaṇaṃ strīghnaṃ bālaghnaṃ narahaṃ tathā
anena na kṛtaṃ snānaṃ japapūjādikaṃ tathā
dānaṃ śaucaṃ tathā dhyānaṃ satkarma ca kriyā vibho
itthaṃ pāpāṅkitaṃ bhraṣṭaṃ yāne svāropitaṃ śubhe
vihāya nārakaṃ tasmai yātanā duḥsahaṃ vibho
ādiṣṭāścitraguptena vayam atra samāgatāḥ
ānīya taṃ dvijaṃ bhraṣṭaṃ kṣipyatāṃ narakeṣu ca
iti ādeśaṃ samādāya prabhuṇā preritā vayam
atrāgatāḥ śivātmaṃste kiṃ kurvāmaśca kiṃkarāḥ
śivadūtā ūcuḥ
dūrīkṛtāśca tatpāpā īśvareṇa hi sarvataḥ
niṣpāpo 'asau bhavadyāmyāḥ śṛṇudhvaṃ tasya hetukam
anena sparśitaṃ liṅgam enaṃ kuśeśvaraṃ param
hastābhyāṃ śirasā vatsā ajñānato 'api pūyate
lalāṭe hi vibhūtiṃ ca tasyāṅge taddhi paśyatām
ājñā dattā śivenāśu mahyaṃ loke mama dhruvam
bhraṣṭaścānīyatāṃ ceti vayam atrāgatā ataḥ
idānīṃ nīyate 'asmābhiḥ śivalokam ayaṃ dvijaḥ
yūyaṃ gacchata yāmyaṃ ca mā dūyantāṃ yathāgatāḥ
ityuktvā śivadūtāste yāmyebhyaḥ khalu sāmpratam
tam āsthāya vimāne hi codayadyānavāhanān
sūryāyutapratīkāśe kiṅkiṇījālamaṇḍite
gītavādyaravopete bhraṣṭo yāne virājate
gandharvaiḥ sevakairbhraṣṭa upahāraiḥ prapūjitaḥ
tato 'agamadvimānena bhraṣṭaścoddhṛtaduṣkṛtaḥ
sarvalokān samālokya devānāṃ śivalokakam
sarvalokoṣitān devān sādhu sādhviti vādayan
mudā yukto 'agamacchaivaṃ dvitīya iva śaṃkaraḥ
yugakoṭisahasrāṇi bhraṣṭo gaṇairmahīyate
tato bhraṣṭaśca medinyāṃ janitaḥ sa mahīpatiḥ
jambūdvīpe dvijaśreṣṭha sārvabhaumo hyabhūdasau
tato bhraṣṭaḥ sa medinyāṃ bhuktvā bhogān yathepsitān
ityetat kathitaṃ dhīman viṣṇudāsena yat kṛtam
bhūyo vakṣye vibhūtīnāṃ phalottamaṃ viśeṣataḥ
bhasmasnāyī ca rudrākṣī sa rudro nātra saṃśayaḥ
bhasmasnāyī ca rudrākṣī rudrajāpī viśeṣataḥ
na paśyati tathā yāmyaṃ paraṃ prāpnoti bhāvataḥ
bhasmasnānācca rudrākṣī śivamantrasamāyutaḥ
tat phalaṃ labhate amntrī rudrākṣadhāraṇāt kila
bhāle tripuṇḍrakaṃ kṛtvā śivadhyānasamāhitaḥ
rudrākṣān dhārayeddhīmān sthāne sthāne vicakṣaṇaḥ
śikhāyāṃ keśavṛnde ca grīvāyāṃ caiva hastayoḥ
vakṣasi karṇayordhīmān sa rudras nātra saṃśayaḥ
agamyaḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ draṣṭuṃ ca hiṃsituṃ kutaḥ
te varṇitaṃ bhasmavidhāraṇasya rudrākṣasaṃdhāraṇataśca yat phalam
te varṇitaṃ bhasmavidhāraṇasya ajñānato yaccaritaṃ mayā vai
stokaṃ namerośca phalottamānāṃ śrotuṃ punarvāñchasi kiṃ dvije 'adya
rudrākṣāṇāṃ mahat puṇyaṃ bhavatoktaṃ ca me vibho
tuṣṭo 'ahaṃ śravaṇāt tasya atyadbhutaṃ paraṃtapa
ko devaḥ kīdṛśaṃ rūpaṃ kati phalaṃ ca dhāraṇāt
kathaṃ saṃtuṣṭavān rudro rudrākṣeṇa vada prabho
yaduktaṃ me purāgastya śaṃkareṇācale hi me
rudrākṣāṇāṃ phalaṃ caiva rūpaṃ teṣāṃ yathāvidhi
manmūrtayo hi rudrākṣāścaturvargaphalapradāḥ
pādapānāṃ ca sarveṣāṃ nameruruttamo bhavet
tatphalāni ca jānīhi rudrākṣān rudravigrahān
saṃsārapādapeśānaḥ saṃphalati ca bhūrasān
lokoddhāraṇahetūṃstāṃstathaiva tanmayaṃ jagat
devādayo narāḥ sarve dhāraṇādrudravigrahāḥ
rudrākṣaṃ mokṣadaṃ viddhi śrīsādhanaṃ ca kāmadam
rudrākṣaṃ pāpadāhaṃ ca duḥkhadāridyaghātakam
haste pramāṇabandhena rudrākṣān mokṣam āpnuyāt
bhuktimuktipradaṃ sākṣāt sa rudro nātra saṃśayaḥ
śirasā dhāraṇāddhīmān koṭiguṇaṃ phalaṃ labhet
karṇayordaśakoṭiḥ syāt kaṇṭhe koṭisahasraśaḥ
anantaṃ syācchikhāyāṃ ca tathaiva bāhumūlayoḥ
vakṣasi daśalakṣaṃ syādaṅgeṣu dhāraṇāt phalam
japadhyānavihīno 'api rudrākṣaṃ dhārayan dvijaḥ
devānām asurāṇāṃ ca vandanīyo yatheśvaraḥ
anyāsāṃ caiva mālānāṃ daśaguṇaṃ japāllabhet
lakṣajaptena koṭiḥ syāt koṭijaptena sarvadṛk
koṭikoṭiguṇaṃ puṇyaṃ rudrākṣamālayā tataḥ
vācikena tathā caivam upāṃśunā punardvija
cetasā ca mahāprājña koṭikoṭiguṇaṃ labhet
vaktrāṇāṃ ca phalaṃ vacmi rudrākṣāṇāṃ pṛthak pṛthak
ekavaktraḥ śivaḥ sākṣādbrahmahatyāṃ vyapohati
trivaktraścāgnirūpaḥ syāt strīhatyāṃ harate kṣaṇāt
pañcavaktrastathā rudraḥ kālāgnirnāma bhīṣaṇaḥ
pañcavaktrasya māhātmyaṃ śṛṇu mune samāhitaḥ
agamyagamanāccaiva abhakṣyabhakṣaṇāt tathā
adṛśyadarśanāccaiva avācyabhāṣaṇāt tataḥ
ṣaḍvaktro mama mūrtiḥ syāddhāraṇāt sarvadā mune
ṣaḍvaktraḥ karayoḥ kaṇṭhe dhāraṇācca phalaṃ śṛṇu
saptavaktraśca rudrākṣo hyananto nāma nāgarāṭ
taddhāraṇena saubhāgyaḥ suvarṇāḍhyaḥ sukhī bhavet
ripunāśastathārogī balavān vigatajvaraḥ
rudrākṣo 'aṣṭānano devaḥ śūlapāṇiśca nāmataḥ
tasyāṣṭāsyasya māhātmyam avadhāraya kumbhaja
jīvedvarṣaśataṃ sāgraṃ dhāraṇācca svayaṃ samaḥ
navagrahaistathā bhūtaiḥ piśācairḍākinīgaṇaiḥ
lakṣakoṭisahasrāṇi pañcahatyāṃ karoti yaḥ

saṃdarśya māyayāviṣṭaṃ tvaṣṭāraṃ vavarṣāmṛtam
tatastvaṣṭāmṛtāsāraiḥ paripluto ghaṭodbhava
iti śrīskandapurāṇe himavatkhaṇḍe devāsurasaṃgrāme viśvakarmāpayānaṃ nāmonatriṃśo adhyāyaḥ
