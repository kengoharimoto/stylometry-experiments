gomukhāḍambarāṇāṃ ca bherīṇāṃ murajaiḥ saha
jharjharīḍiṇḍimāṇāṃ ca vyaśrūyanta mahāsvanāḥ
raṇabhūmau mahānādaḥ svargīyaḥ śūrasaṃmataḥ
mantrā daityāḥ samākhyātā yajñakarmaṇi tatra vai
yaṣṭā tatrābhavad bāṇaḥ saṃyuge copatiṣṭhati
aindraṃ pāśupataṃ brāhmaṃ sthūṇākarṇaṃ sudurjayam
udgātā ca mayaḥ śrīmān sthitaḥ sarvabhayaṃkaraḥ
vinadan ditijaśreṣṭho devānīkaṃ vyadārayat
balis tu rājā dyutimāñ śaśvat tatra mahāsuraḥ
devo viṣṇuḥ surais tatra rathavedyāṃ susaṃskṛtaḥ
śaṅkhaśabdaiḥ sutumulair bherīṇāṃ ca mahāsvanaiḥ
praśāntarūpaṃ kṛtvā tu mantraṃ samyak pravartayan
kalmāṣadaṇḍā vimalā vipulā rathapaṅktayaḥ
karṇinālīkanārācavatsadantopabṛṃhakāḥ
tomarāḥ somakalaśā vicitrāṇi dhanūṃṣi ca
asthīny atra kapālāni puroḍāśāḥ śirāṃsi ca
idhmāḥ paridhayas tatra prastārā vipulā gadāḥ
hayagrīvo 'silomā ca rāhuḥ keśī ca dānavāḥ
iṣavas tu sruvās tatra rathākṣasadṛśāḥ śubhāḥ
pratiprasthānikaṃ karma vṛṣaparvākarot tadā
dīkṣitas tatra tu balis tasya patnī mahācamūḥ
dakṣiṇās tasya yajñasya kālanemir mahāsuraḥ
vaitāne karmaṇi vibho yaḥ khyāto havyavāḍ iva
tridaśānāṃ tu sainyasya śarīrair gatajīvitaiḥ
devānāṃ rudhiraṃ saṃkhye papur ugrā diteḥ sutāḥ
nardamānāḥ pramuditāḥ somapānaṃ raṇādhvare
tadā hy avabhṛthaṃ yajñe bhaviṣyati na saṃśayaḥ
mahāsurendrapatayo yajvāno bhūridakṣiṇāḥ
vedavanto vṛttavantaḥ śūrāḥ sarve tanutyajaḥ
trailokyaharaṇe sṛṣṭā yuddhayajñāya dīkṣitāḥ
baddhakṛṣṇājināḥ sarve vratino muñjadhāriṇaḥ
ekaniścayakāryāś ca trailokyajayakāṅkṣiṇaḥ
suradānavasainyānāṃ śabdaḥ samabhavan mahān
nānāyudhanihastānāṃ tvaritānāṃ pradhāvatām
kṣveḍitotkruṣṭaninadair gajabṛṃhitanisvanaiḥ
hayānāṃ heṣamāṇānāṃ dānavānāṃ ca garjatām
kṣveḍitotkruṣṭaninadaiḥ pāṇipādaravais tathā
dānavānāṃ pareṣāṃ ca śastravanti mahānti ca
samare bhīmakarmāṇi sainyāni pracakāśire
tato nāgā rathāś caiva jāmbūnadavibhūṣitāḥ
bhrājamānā hy adṛśyanta meghā iva savidyutaḥ
ṛṣṭikhaḍgagadās tīkṣṇāḥ śūlaśaktiparaśvadhāḥ
cāru bibhrājire tatra sveṣv anīkeṣu bhāgaśaḥ
rathā bahuvidhākārāḥ śataśo 'tha sahasraśaḥ
hemasaṃchannaśikharā jvalanta iva pāvakāḥ
dānavānāṃ surāṇāṃ ca samālokyanta sainikāḥ
saṃnaddhāḥ samadṛśyanta jyotīṃṣi gagane yathā
ṛṣabhākṣāḥ suragaṇāś camūmukhagatā babhuḥ
nānāvarṇāḥ patākāś ca dhvajamālāś ca saṃyuge
yudhyatāṃ raṇaśauṇḍānām īrayām āsa mārutaḥ
dhvajālaṃkāravastrāṇi varmāṇi kavacāni ca
raśmibhir bhāsayām āsa raśmivarṇāni raśmimān
ubhābhyām aprameyābhyāṃ balābhyāṃ pādacāriṇām
rajaḥ pracchādayām āsa patrorṇāḥ pāṇḍurā diśaḥ
divyāyudhadharāḥ sarve dīptāyudhaparicchadāḥ
girikūṭocchrayāḥ sarve tadā te devadānavāḥ
bāṇaiḥ surucirais tīkṣṇaiḥ patravājair durāsadaiḥ
mudgarair musalaiḥ śūlair ayastuṇḍair ulūkhalaiḥ
vajrair aśanikalpaiś ca khaḍgavṛkṣādibhis tathā
sāgarābhyāṃ mahāsenāṃ devānāṃ daityapuṃgavaḥ
saṃśoṣayati bāṇaughair arkāṃśubhir ivārṇavam
mārutaḥ sa mahāvegaḥ sāvitraḥ śaktim uttamām
āpatantī ca sā śaktir maholkā jvalitā iva
dvidhā chinnā kṣurapreṇa bāṇenādbhutakarmaṇā
hatāyām atha śaktyāṃ tu sāvitro devasattamaḥ
viśvakarmakṛtaṃ divyaṃ sutīkṣṇaṃ dānavārdanam
agṛhṇān niśitaṃ khaḍgam āśīviṣam ivoragam
taṃ gṛhītvā raṇamukhe prajvalantaṃ mahāprabham
bāṇābhyāśe mahātejāḥ khaḍgapāṇir avasthitaḥ
lohitākṣaṃ mahākāyaṃ cikṣepa ca nanāda ca
tato 'rkakiraṇākārān aśanipratimāñ śitān
saṃdadhe cāśu bāṇaughān āśīviṣasamān balī
rukmapuṅkhān pradīptāgrān ugravegān alaṃkṛtān
ākarṇapūrṇāṃś cikṣepa śarān ugrān samantataḥ
dṛḍhacāpapramuktās te śarā vaiśvānaraprabhāḥ
sāvitraṃ chādayām āsuḥ kailāsam iva toyadāḥ
saṃchādyamānaḥ śastraughair bāṇena balisūnunā
parāṅmukhaḥ suravaraḥ prayātaḥ sarathadhvajaḥ
parājitya ca sāvitraṃ bāṇaḥ paramaharṣitaḥ
pragṛhya kārmukaṃ ghoraṃ gataḥ śakrarathaṃ prati
balaś cāpy asuraśreṣṭhaḥ pragṛhya mahatīṃ gadām
dhruvāya vasave mūrdhni raudrāṃ cikṣepa dānavaḥ
śeṣaś ca vasavaḥ sarve divyāstrair ghoradarśanaiḥ
prācchādayan raṇe daityam ādityam iva toyadāḥ
tataḥ so 'marṣito bāṇair balo dānavasattamaḥ
pātayām āsa śatrūṇāṃ samāvidhya mahāsuraḥ
diśaḥ prādrāvayat sarvāṃs stridaśān sā mahāgadā
indrāśanir ivendreṇa pravṛddhāḥ sumahāsvanāḥ
tasyāḥ suvidyudghoṣāyās tena śabdena vejitāḥ
tadudīrṇaṃ rathānīkaṃ sūryābhaṃ meghanisvanam
devānāṃ śaradhārābhiḥ samantād abhyavarṣata
balas tu sa gadāpāṇir vyāditāsya ivāntakaḥ
taḍidguṇārkasadṛśo vaiśvānara ivāparaḥ
avasphūrjan diśaḥ sarvāḥ svena vīryeṇa dānavaḥ
samṛddhas tarasā devān vāyur vṛkṣān ivaujasā
śamayaṃś ca maheṣvāsāṇ vasubhyāṃ samasajjata
āpaś caivānilaś caiva vavarṣatur ariṃdamau
śaravarṣāṇi dīptāni meghāv iva paraṃtapau
kṣiptāṃs tān viśikhān dīptān antarikṣe sa cicchide
rathaśaktibhir anyonyaṃ viśikhaiś cābhyakṛntatām
carantau vividhān mārgān maṇḍalāni ca bhāgaśaḥ
asibhyāṃ carmaṇī divye vipule ca śarāsane
nikṛtyācalasaṃkāśau bāhuyuddhaṃ pracakratuḥ
dvipāv iva viṣāṇāgraiḥ śṛṅgair iva mahāvṛṣau
rathaṃ tyaktvā bhayāt tasya pranaṣṭaḥ prāṅmukho vasuḥ
saṃrabdhau ca mahābāhū maheṣvāsāv ariṃdamau
parasparam udīkṣetāṃ dahantāv iva locanaiḥ
visphārya ca mahac cāpaṃ hemapṛṣṭhaṃ dūrāsadam
saṃrambhāt sa vasuśreṣṭhas tyaktvā prāṇān ayudhyata
bhānumadbhiḥ śilādhautair bhānoḥ pracchādayat prabhām
asṛjat sāyakān dīptān bhīmavegān durāsadān
mahātejā mahābāhur mahāvego mahārathaḥ
sa totrair iva mātaṅgo dāryamāṇaḥ patatribhiḥ
tam āpatantaṃ vegena saṃrambhān namucī raṇe
tataḥ prādhmāpayac chaṅkhaṃ bherīśatanināditam
vikṣobhya tad balaṃ harṣād uddhūtārṇavasaprabham
aśvān ṛkṣasavarṇābhān haṃsavarṇaiḥ suvājibhiḥ
samāśliṣṭāv athānyonyaṃ vasudānavayo rathau
dṛṣṭvā prākampata muhus tridaśānāṃ mahad balam
krodhasaṃraktatāmrākṣau prekṣamāṇau muhur muhuḥ
garjantāv iva śārdūlau prabhinnāv iva vāraṇau
mahāmeghopamaṃ raudram āsīd āyodhanaṃ tayoḥ
rathāśvanarasaṃbādhaṃ mattavāraṇasaṃkulam
samājam iva taṃ dṛṣṭvā prekṣamānau mahārathau
āśaṃsante jayaṃ tābhyāṃ yodhās te gaṇaśaḥ sthitāḥ
tayoḥ praikṣanta samaraṃ saṃnikṛṣṭaṃ mahāstrayoḥ
tau chādayantāv anyonyaṃ samare niśitaiḥ śaraiḥ
śarajālāvṛtaṃ vyoma cakratus tau mahābalau
tāv anyonyaṃ jighāṃsantau śarais tīkṣnair mahārathau
prekṣaṇiyatarāv āstāṃ vṛṣṭimantāv ivāmbudau
suvarṇavikṛtān bāṇān pramuñcantāv ariṃdamau
bhāskarābhaṃ tadākāśam ulkābhir iva cakratuḥ
tayoḥ śarāḥ prakāśante devadānavayos tadā
paṅktyaḥ śaradi mattānāṃ sārasānām ivāmbare
tridaśāśvagajānāṃ hi śarīrair gatajīvitaiḥ
tataḥ sudhāraṃ jvalitaṃ sūryamaṇḍalasaṃnibham
pravarau viśvakarmāṇau māyāśataviśāradau
ghoras tayoḥ saṃprahāraḥ prāvartata sudāruṇaḥ
tvaṣṭā tu niśitair bāṇair daityaṃ tu baladarpitam
parākrāntaṃ parākramya vivyādha triṃśatā śaraiḥ
mayas tu prativivyādha tvaṣṭāraṃ niśitaiḥ śaraiḥ
sudhautaiḥ suprasannāgraiḥ śātakumbhavibhūṣitaiḥ
nanāda ditijaśreṣṭho hatas tvaṣṭuḥ śarair mayaḥ
śaktiṃ kanakacitrākṣāṃ citradaṇḍāṃ mahāprabhām
bhīmāṃ sarvāyasīṃ tvaṣṭā puraṃdara ivāśanim
tāṃ tvaṣṭur bhujanirmuktām arkavaiśvānaraprabhām
mayaś ciccheda tīkṣṇāgrais tūrṇaṃ saptabhir āśugaiḥ
tataḥ kṣiṇvann iva prāṇāṃs tvaṣṭuḥ kopān mahāsuraḥ
preṣayām āsa saṃrabdhaḥ śarān barhiṇavāsasaḥ
ciccheda bāṇāṃs tvaṣṭā tāñ jvalitair nataparvabhiḥ
daityasya sumahāvegaiḥ suvarṇavikṛtaiḥ śaraiḥ
tau vṛṣāv iva nardantau balinau vāśitāntare
śārdūlāv iva cānyonyaṃ prasaktāv abhijaghnatuḥ
anyonyam abhivīkṣantau kruddhāv āśīviṣāv iva
mahāgajāv ivāsādya viṣāṇāgraiḥ parasparam
śaraiḥ pūrṇāyatotsṛṣṭair anyonyam abhijaghnatuḥ
tataḥ sa vipulāṃ dīptāṃ mayo rukmāṅgadāṃ gadām
tvaṣṭuḥ saṃprāhiṇotkruddhaḥ sarvaprāṇaharāṃ raṇe
tayā jaghānātirathas tvaṣṭur uttamavājinaḥ
gadayā dānavaḥ kruddho vajreṇendra ivācalān
tataḥ kruddho mahādaityaḥ kṣurābhyāṃ tasya saṃyuge
dhvajaṃ tvaṣṭur atha cchittvā sūtaṃ ninye yamakṣayam
dṛṣṭvā tvaṣṭā hataṃ sūtaṃ kṣurābhyāṃ vinipātitam
hatāśvaṃ ratham utsṛjya sūtaṃ ca patitaṃ bhuvi
visphārayan mahācāpaṃ sthito bhūmau mahābalaḥ
hatāśvasūtaṃ virathaṃ dṛṣṭvā ripum avasthitam
jayaśriyā sevyamāno dīpyamāna ivācalaḥ
mayaḥ kālāntakaprakhyaś cāpapāṇir ivāntakaḥ
svaṣṭuḥ so 'kṣipatātyugrān nārācāṃs tigmatejasaḥ
caturdaśa śilādhautān sāyakān vividhākṛtīn
te papus tasya devasya śoṇitaṃ rukmabhūṣaṇāḥ
āśīviṣā iva kruddhā bhujagāḥ kālacoditāḥ
ardhapraviṣṭāḥ saṃrabdhā bilānīva mahoragāḥ
te prakāśanta nārācāḥ praviśanto vasuṃdharām
astaṃ gacchantam ādityaṃ praviśanta ivāṃśavaḥ
taṃ pratyavidhyat tvaṣṭā tu jāmbūnadavibhūṣitaiḥ
vidārya viviśur bhūmiṃ bhujagā iva vegitāḥ
sapatravegair vikṛtair jvaladbhiḥ prāṇanāśanaiḥ
tvaṣṭātha mayanirmuktaiḥ sāyakair arditaḥ prabhuḥ
apayāto raṇaṃ hitvā vrīḍayābhisamanvitaḥ
taṃ tatra hatasūtāśvaṃ bhujaṃgam iva nirviṣam
tvaṣṭāraṃ virathaṃ dṛṣṭvā muditaḥ sa tu dānavaḥ
visphārayan suruciraṃ cāpaṃ rukmāṅgadaṃ dṛḍham
pulomā tu balaślāghī dṛpto dānavasattamaḥ
raṇe śvetāśvayuktena rathena pratyadṛśyata
sarveṣām eva bhūtānāṃ yaḥ prāṇaḥ kathyate dvijaiḥ
balinā kālakalpena vāyunā saha saṃgataḥ
daityacāpacyutair bāṇaiḥ prācchādyanta diśo daśa
raśmijālair ivārkasya vitataṃ sāmbaraṃ jagat
vṛto daityaśatair vāyū raśmivān iva bhāskaraḥ
daityacāpabhujotsṛṣṭāḥ śarā barhiṇavāsasaḥ
rukmapuṅkhā vyarājanta haṃsāḥ śreṇīkṛtā iva
cāpadhvajapatākābhyaḥ śastrā dīptamukhāś cyutāḥ
prapatantaḥ sma dṛśyante daityasyāyutaśaḥ śarāḥ
evaṃ sutīkṣṇān khacarāñ śalabhān iva pāvake
suvarṇavikṛtāṃś citrān mumoca ditijaḥ śarān
tyaktvā prāṇān atikramya vivyādha navabhiḥ śaraiḥ
tasya vegam asaṃhāryaṃ dṛṣṭvā vāyuḥ sanātanaḥ
tato vidhamya balavāñ śarajālāni mārutaḥ
vivyādha daityaṃ viṃśatyā viśikhair nataparvabhiḥ
mārudgaṇānāṃ pravarā daśa divyā mahaujasaḥ
abhyadhāvanta ditijāḥ paulomāḥ krodhamūrchitāḥ
te samāsādya pavanaṃ samāvṛṇvañ śarottamaiḥ
parvataṃ vāridhārābhiḥ prāvṛṣīva balāhakāḥ
te pīḍayantaḥ pavanaṃ kruddhāḥ sapta mahārathāḥ
prajāsaṃharaṇe ghorāḥ somaṃ sapta grahā iva
tato dakṣiṇam akṣobhyaṃ nānāratnavibhūṣitam
teṣāṃ mūrdhasu daityānāṃ pātayām āsa vīryavān
tyaktvā prāṇān pulomā tu vivyādha navabhiḥ śaraiḥ
tasyāpi tam asaṃhāryaṃ dṛṣtvā vāyuḥ sanātanaḥ
asaṃcintya śaraughāṃs tāñ jvalitān vai pulomataḥ
teṣāṃ vidārya tejāṃsi dānavānāṃ mahātmanām
śoṇitaklinnamukuṭā gairikārdrā ivādrayaḥ
te bhinnavarmāsthibhujāḥ patanto bhānti dānavāḥ
mātaṅgayūthasaṃrugṇāḥ puṣpitā iva pādapāḥ
teṣāṃ vidāritair dehair dānavānāṃ mahātmanām
tataḥ prāvartata nadī raudrarūpā bhayāvahā
prasravantī raṇe raktaṃ bhīrūṇāṃ bhayavardhinī
saṃbhṛtā gatasattvaiś ca yakṣarākṣasakhecaraiḥ
sānukarṣapatākaiś ca sopāsaṅgair dhvajai rathaiḥ
śīrṇakumbhais tathā nāgair ghaṇṭābhiḥ suvibhūṣitaiḥ
prāsatomaranārācaiḥ śaktikhaḍgaparaśvadhaiḥ
suvarṇavikṛtaiś cāpair gadāmusalapaṭṭisaiḥ
kanakāṅgadakeyūrair maṇibhiś ca sakuṇḍalaiḥ
tanutraiḥ satalatraiś ca hārair niṣkaiś ca śobhanaiḥ
vimardo devasainyānāṃ sadṛśaḥ karmaṇāṃ babhau
saṃvṛtaḥ pavanaḥ śrīmān gadāmusalapāṇinā
te jaghnuḥ śatasāhasrāḥ pavanaṃ dānavottamāḥ
hatvāṣṭau tatra yodhānāṃ śatāni pavanaḥ prabhuḥ
kṛtvā mārgaṃ suraśreṣṭhaḥ prayātaḥ sa mahārathaḥ
adyāpi ca suvistīrṇaḥ panthāḥ saṃdṛśyate divi
nāmnā vāyupatho nāma siddhāḥ paśyanti taṃ divi
hayagrīvas tu ditijaḥ pūṣāṇaṃ prati vīryavān
nanāda sumahānādaṃ siṃhanādaṃ mahārathaḥ
visphārya sumahac cāpaṃ hemajālavibhūṣitam
pūṣāṇaṃ ditijo 'paśyat kruddho ghoreṇa cakṣuṣā
bhujābhyām ādadānasya saṃdadhānasya vai śarān
muñcataḥ karṣato vāpi dadṛśus tatra nāntaram
agnicakropamaṃ dīptaṃ maṇḍalīkṛtakārmukam
tadāsīd dānavendrasya savyadakṣiṇam agrataḥ
rukmapuṅkhais tatas tasya cāpamuktaiḥ śitaiḥ śaraiḥ
prācchādyanta śilādhautair diśaḥ sūryasya ca prabhāḥ
tataḥ kanakapuṅkhānāṃ śarāṇāṃ nataparvaṇām
nabhaścarāṇāṃ nabhasi dṛśyante bahavo vrajāḥ
girikūṭanibhāc cāpāt prabhavantaḥ śarottamāḥ
śreṇībhūtāḥ prakāśante yāntaḥ śyenā ivāmbare
gṛdhrapatrāñ śilādhautān kārtasvaravibhūṣitān
mahāvegān prasannāgrān mumoca ditijaḥ śarān
tataś cāpabaloddhūtāḥ śātakumbhavibhūṣitāḥ
dehe samavakīryanta pūṣṇaḥ suniśitāḥ śarāḥ
te vyomni vikṛtākārāḥ saṃprakāśanta sarvaśaḥ
śilādhautāḥ prasannāgrāḥ pūṣaṇaṃ vikṛtāḥ śarāḥ
parvataṃ vāridhārābhir yathā prāvṛṣi toyadāḥ
tatas tu pūṣṇo devasya balaṃ vīryaṃ parākramam
tāṃ samudrād ivodbhūtāṃ śaravṛṣṭiṃ samutthitām
nācintayat tadā pūṣā daityāṃś cābhyadravad raṇe
hemapṛṣṭhaṃ mahānādaṃ pūṣṇa āsīn mahad dhanuḥ
vikṛtaṃ maṇḍalībhūtaṃ śakrāśanim ivāparam
tataḥ śarāḥ prādur āsan pūrayanta ivāmbaram
suvarṇapuṅkhāḥ pūṣṇas te prabhavantaḥ śarāsanāt
māleva rukmapuṅkhānāṃ vitatā vyomni patriṇām
prādur āsīn mahāghorā bṛhataḥ pūṣakārmukāt
tato vyomni vibhaktāni śarajālāni sarvaśaḥ
āhatāni vyaśīryanta śaraiḥ saṃnataparvabhiḥ
tataḥ kanakapuṅkhānāṃ chinnānāṃ kaṅkavāsasām
patatāṃ pātyamānānāṃ kham āsīt saṃvṛtaṃ vrajaiḥ
pūṣā prāpūrayad bāṇair hayagrīvaṃ śilāśitaiḥ
nāmāṅkair arkasadṛśair divyahemapariṣkṛtaiḥ
tato vyasṛjad ugrāṇi śarajālāni dānavaḥ
amarṣī balavān kruddho didhakṣann iva pāvakaḥ
