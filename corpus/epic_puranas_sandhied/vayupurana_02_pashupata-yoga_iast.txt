vāyuruvāca
ekaṃ mahāntaṃ divasamahorātramathāpi vā
arddhamāsaṃ tathā māsamayanābdayugāni ca
mahāyugasahasrāṇi ṛṣayastapasi sthitāḥ
upāsate mahātmānaḥ prāṇaṃ divyena cakṣuṣā
atūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi prāṇāyāmaprayojanam
phalañcaiva viśeṣeṇa yathāha bhagavān prabhuḥ
prayojanāni catvāri prāṇāyāmasya viddhivai
śāntiḥ praśāntirdīptiśca prasādaśca catuṣṭayam
ghorākāraśivānāntu karmaṇāṃ phalasambhavam
svayaṃkṛtāni kālena ihāmutra ca dehinām
pitṛmātṛ praduṣṭānāṃ jñātisambandhisaṅkaraiḥ
kṣapaṇaṃ hi kaṣāyāṇāṃ pāpānāṃ śāntirucyate
lobhamānātmakānāṃ hi pāpānāmapi saṃyamaḥ
sūryendugrahatārāṇāṃ tulyastu viṣayo bhavet
ṛṣīṇāñca prasiddhānāṃ jñānavijñānasampadām
atītānāgatānāñca darśanaṃ sāmpratasya ca
indriyāṇīndriyārthāṃśca manaḥ pañca ca mārutān
prasādayati yenāsau prasāda iti saṃjñitaḥ
ityeṣa dharmaḥ prathamaḥ prāṇāyāmaścaturvidhaḥ
sannikṛṣṭaphalo jñeyaḥ sadyaḥkālaprasādajaḥ
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi prāṇāyāmasya lakṣaṇam
oṅkāraṃ prathamaṃ kṛtvā candrasūyauṃ praṇamya ca
āsanaṃ svastikaṃ kṛtvā pajhamarddhāsanantathā
samo dṛḍhāsano bhūtvā saṃhṛtya caraṇāvubhau
saṃvṛtāsyo'vabaddhākṣa uro viṣṭabhya cāgrataḥ
pārṣṇibhyāṃ vṛṣaṇe chādya tathā prajananaṃ tataḥ
kiñcidunnāmitaśirāḥ śiro grīvāṃ tathaiva ca
samprekṣya nāsikāgraṃ svaṃ diśaścānavalokayan
tataḥ sattvasthito bhūtvā yogaṃ yuñjan samāhitaḥ
indriyāṇīndriyārthāṃśca manaḥ pañca sa mārutān
yastu pratyāharet kāmān kūrmo'ṅgānīva sarvataḥ
pūrayitvā śarīrantu sa bāhyābhyantaraṃ śuciḥ
ākaṇṭhanābhiyogena pratyāhāramupakramet
tathā dvādaśamātrastu prāṇāyāmo vidhīyate
dhāraṇā dvādaśāyāmo yogo vai dhāraṇādvayam
vīkṣate paramātmānaṃ dīpyamānaṃ svatejasā
prāṇāyāmena yuktasya viprasya niyatātmanaḥ
sarve doṣāḥ praṇaśyanti sattvasthaścaiva jāyate
evaṃ vai niyatāhāraḥ prāṇāyāmaparāyaṇaḥ
jitvā jitvā sadā bhūmimārohettu sadā muniḥ
ajitā hi mahābhūmirdoṣānutpādayedbahūn
nālena tu yathā toyaṃ yantreṇaiva balānvitaḥ
āpibeta prayatnena tathā vāyuñjitaśramaḥ
nābhyāṃ ca hṛdaye caiva kaṇṭe urasi cānane
kiñcidūrddhvaṃ parasmiṃśca dhāraṇā paramā smṛtā
prāṇāpānasamārodhāt prāṇāyāmaḥ sa kathyate
manaso dhāraṇā caiva dhāraṇeti prakīrtitā
nivṛtti rviṣayāṇāntu pratyāhārastu saṃjñitaḥ
sarveṣāṃ samavāye tu siddhiḥ syādyogalakṣaṇā
dhyānayuktaḥ sadā paśyedātmānaṃ sūryacandravat
agnyabhyāśe vane vāpi śuṣkaparṇacaye tathā
jantuvyāpte śmaśāne vā jīrṇagoṣṭe catuṣpathe
udapāne tathā nadyānna bādhātaḥ kadācana
kṣudhāviṣstathā'prito na ca vyākulacetanaḥ
yuñjīta paramaṃ dhyānaṃ yogī dhyānaparaḥ sadā
etān doṣān viniścitya pramādādyo yunakti vai
tasya doṣāḥ prakupyanti śarīre vighnakārakāḥ
tasya doṣāḥ prakupyanti ajñānādyo yunakti vai
tasmājjñānena śuddhena yogī yuñjetsamāhitaḥ
teṣāṃ cikitsāṃ vakṣyāmi doṣāṇāṃ ca yathākramam
yathā gacchanti te doṣāḥ prāṇāyāmasamutthitāḥ
snigdhāṃ yavāgūmatyuṣṇāṃ bhuktvāṃ tatrāvadhārayet
bhuktvā dadhi yavāgūrvā vāyurūrddhvaṃ tato vrajeta
tathāpi na viśeṣaḥ syāddhāraṇāṃ mūrdhni dhārayet
imāṃ cikitsāṃ kurvvīta tayā saṃpadyate sukhī
manasā yadvrataṃ kiñcidviṣṭambhīkṛtya dhārayet
uroddhāte uraḥsthānaṃ kaṇṭhadeśe ca dhārayet
tvaco'vadhāte tāṃ vāci bādhirye śrotrayostathā
jihvāsthāne tṛṣārttastu agre snehāṃśca tantubhiḥ
kṣaye kuṣṭe sakīlāse dhārayetsarvasātvikīm
vaṃśakīlena mūrddhānaṃ dhārayāṇasya tāḍayet
mūrdhni kīlaṃ pratiṣṭāpya kāṣṭhaṃ kāṣṭena tāḍayet
bhayabhītasya sā saṃjñā tataḥ pratyāgamiṣyati
atha vā luptasaṃjñasya hastābhyāṃ tatra dhārayet
pratilabhya tataḥ saṃjñāṃ dhāraṇāṃ mūrdhni dhārayet
divaṃ ca pṛthivīñcaiva vāyumagniṃ ca dhārayet
prāṇāyāmena tatsarvaṃ dahyamānaṃ vaśībhavet
prāṇāyāmāgninā dagdhaṃ tatsarvaṃ vilayaṃ vrajet
kṛṣṇasarpāparādhaṃ tu dhārayeddhṛdayodare
maharjanastapaḥ satyaṃ hṛdi kṛtvā tu dhārayet
viṣasya tu phalaṃ pītvā viśalyāṃ dhārayettataḥ
sarvataḥ sanagāṃ pṛthvīṃ kṛtvā manasi dhārayet
hṛdi kṛtvā samudrāṃśca tathā sarvāśca devatāḥ
sahasreṇa ghaṭānāñca yuktaḥ snāyīta yogavit
udake kaṇṭhamātre tu dhāraṇāṃ mūdhni dhārayet
pratisrotoviṣāviṣṭo dhārayet sarvagātrikīm
śīrṇo'rkapatrapuṭakaiḥ pibedvalmīkamṛttikām
vyākhyātastu samāsena yogadṛṣṭena hetunā
athāpi kathayenmohāttadvijñānaṃ pralīyate
gandhaḥ śubho mūtrapurīṣa malpaṃ yogapravṛttiḥ prathamā śarīre
ātmānaṃ pṛthivīñcaiva jvalantīṃ yadi paśyati
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte pāśupatayogo nāmaikādaśo'dhyāyaḥ
sūta uvāca
ata ūrddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi upasargā yathā tathā
prādurbhavanti ye doṣā dṛṣṭatattvasya dehinaḥ
mānuṣyān vividhān kāmān kāmayeta ṛtuṃ striyaḥ
vidyādānaphalañcaiva upasṛṣṭastu yogavit
māyākarma dhanaṃ svargamupasṛṣṭastu kāṃkṣati
upasṛṣṭantu jānīyādvuddhyā caiva visarjjayet
upasargāḥ pravarttante sāttvarājasatāmasāḥ
pratibhāśravaṇe caiva na devānāñcaiva darśanam
bhramāvartaśca ityete siddhilakṣaṇasaṃjñitāḥ
vidyā kāvyaṃ tathā śilpaṃ sarvaṃ vācāvṛtāni tu
vidyārthāścopatiṣṭhanti prabhāvasyaiva lakṣaṇam
śrṛṇoti śabdān śrotavyān yojanānāṃ śatādapi
yakṣarākṣasagandharvvān vīkṣate divyamānuṣān
vetti tāṃśca mahāyogī upasargasya lakṣaṇam
devadānavagandharvān ṛṣīṃścāpi tathā pitṝn
bhrameṇa bhrāmyate yogī codyamāno'ntarātmanā
bhrameṇa bhrāntabuddhestu jñānaṃ sarvaṃ praṇaśyati
vārttā nāśayate cittaṃ codyamāno'ntarātmanā
varttanākrāntabuddhestu sarvaṃ jñānaṃ praṇaśyati
āvṛtya manasā śuklaṃ paṭaṃ vā kambalaṃ tathā
tasmāccaivātmano doṣāṃstūpasargānupasthitān
ṛṣayo devagandharvā yakṣoragamahāsurāḥ
upasargeṣu saṃyuktā āvarttante punaḥ punaḥ
tasmādyuktaḥ sadā yogī laghvāhārī jitendriyaḥ
tathā suptaḥ susūkṣmeṣu dhāraṇāṃ mūrdhni dhārayet
upasargāḥ punaścānte jāyante prāṇasaṃjñakāḥ
pṛthivīṃ dhārayetsarvāṃ tataścāpo hyanantaram
tato'gniñcaiva sarveṣāmākāśaṃ mana eva ca
kiddhīnāñcaiva liṅgāni dṛṣṭvā dṛṣṭvā parityajet
pṛthvīṃ dhārayamāṇasya mahī sūkṣmā pravartate
apo dhārayamāṇasya āpaḥ sūkṣmā bhavanti hi
śītā rasāḥ pravarttante sūkṣmā hyamṛtasannibhāḥ
tejo dhārayamāṇasya tejaḥ sūkṣmā pravarttate
ātmānaṃ manyate vāyuṃ vāyuvanmaṇḍalaṃ prabho
ākāśaṃ dhārayāṇasya vyoma sūkṣmaṃ pravarttate
paśyate maṇḍalaṃ sūkṣmaṃ ghoṣaścāsya pravarttate
ātmānaṃ manyate nityaṃ vāyuḥ sūkṣmaḥ pravarttate
manasā sarvabhūtānāṃ manastu viśate hi saḥ
buddhyā buddhiṃ yadā yuñjettadā vijñāya buddhayate
etāni sapta sūkṣmāṇi viditvā yastu yogavit
yasmin yasmiṃśca saṃyukto bhūta aiśvaryalakṣaṇe
tasmādviditvā sūkṣmāṇi saṃsaktāni parasparam
dṛśyante hi mahātmāna ṛṣayo divyacakṣuṣaḥ
saṃsaktāḥ sūkṣmabhāveṣu te doṣāsteṣu saṃjñitāḥ
tasmānna niścayaḥ kāryaḥ sūkṣmeṣviha kadācana
aiśvaryājjāyate rāgo virāgaṃ brahma cocyate
viditvā sapta sūkṣmāṇi ṣaḍaṅgañca maheśvaram
anantaśaktiśca vibhorvidhijñāḥ ṣaḍāhuraṅgāni maheśvarasya
ekā bahiḥśarīre'smin dhāraṇā sarvakāmikī
bhūtānyāviśate vāpi trailokyañcāpi kampayet
manodvāraṃ hi yogānāmādityañca vinirddiśet
ādānādikriyāṇāntu āditya iti cocyate
etena vidhinā yogī viraktaḥ sūkṣmavarjjitaḥ
paiśācāṃśacava piśācāṃśca rākṣasena ca rākṣasān
prajāpatiṃ tathā caiva prājāpatyena sādhayet
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte yogopasarganirūpaṇaṃ nāma dvādaśo'dhyāyaḥ
vāyuruvāca
tatrāṣṭaguṇamaiśvaryaṃ yogināṃ samudāhṛtam
sāvadyaṃ nāma tattattvaṃ pañcabhūtātmakaṃ smṛtam
niravadyaṃ tathā nāma pañcabhūtātmakaṃ smṛtam
indriyāṇi manaścaiva ahahkāraśca vai smṛtam
tatra sūkṣmapravṛttantu pañcabhūtātmakaṃ punaḥ
indriyāṇi manaścaiva buddhyahaṅkāra saṃjñitam
punaraṣṭaguṇasyāpi teṣvevātha pravarttate
tasya rūpaṃ pravakṣyāmi yathāha bhagavān prabhuḥ
trailokye sarvabūteṣu jīvasyāniyataḥ smṛtaḥ
aṇimā ca yathāvyaktaṃ sarvaṃ yatra pratiṣṭitam
trailokye sarvabhūtānāṃ duṣprāpyaṃ samudāhṛtam
taccāpi bhavati prāpyaṃ prathamaṃ yogināṃ balāt
śīghragaṃ sarvabhūteṣu dvitīyaṃ tatpadaṃ smṛtam
trailokye sarvabhūtānāṃ prāptiḥ prākāmyameva ca
jñātvā tu paramaṃ sūkṣmaṃ sūkṣmatvāccāpavargakaḥ
vyāpakastvapavargācca vyāpitvātpuruṣaḥ smṛtaḥ
puruṣaḥ sūkṣmabhāvāttu aiśvarye parataḥ sthitaḥ
guṇāntarantu aiśvarye sarvataḥ sūkṣma ucyate
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprākte yogaiśvaryāṇi nāma trayodaśodhyāyaḥ
vāyuruvāca
na caivamāgato jñānādrāgāt karmma samācaret
rājasaṃ tāmasaṃ vāpi bhuktvā tatraiva yujyate
tasmāt sthānāt punarbhraṣṭo mānuṣyamanupadyate
tasmādbrahma paraṃ sūkṣmaṃ brahma śāśvatamucyate
pariśramastu yajñānāṃ mahatārthena varttate
bhūyo mṛtyuvaśaṃ yāti tasmānmokṣaḥ paraṃ sukham
dṛṣṭvā tu puruṣaṃ divyaṃ viśvākhyaṃ viśvarūpiṇam
viśvapāda śirogrīvaṃ viśveśaṃ viśvabhāvanam
viśvagandhaṃ viśvamālyaṃ viśvāmbaradharaṃ prabhum
gobhirmahī saṃyatate patatriṇaṃ mahātmānaṃ paramamatiṃ vareṇyam
kaviṃ purāṇamanuśāsitāraṃ sūkṣmācca sūkṣmaṃ mahato mahāntam
yogena paśyanti na cakṣuṣā taṃ nirindriyaṃ puruṣaṃ rukmavarṇam
aliṅginaṃ puruṣaṃ rukmavarṇaṃ saliṅginaṃ nirguṇaṃ cetanaṃ ca
nityaṃ sadā sarvagatantu śaucaṃ paśyanti yuktyā hyacalaṃ prakāśam
tadbhāvitastejasā dīpyamānaḥ apāṇipādodarapārśvajihvaḥ
atīndriyo'dyāpi susūkṣma ekaḥ paśyatyacakṣuḥ sa śrṛṇotyakarṇaḥ
tamāhuragryaṃ puruṣaṃ mahāntaṃ sacetanaṃ sarvvagataṃ susūkṣmam
prakṛtiṃ sarvvabhūtānāṃ yuktāḥ paśyanti cetasā
sarvvataḥ pāṇipādāntaṃ sarvvato'kṣiśiromukham
sarvvataḥ śruti ma mālloke sarvvamāvṛtya tiṣṭhati
yuktā yogena ceśānaṃ sarvvataśca sanātanam
puruṣaṃ sarvvabhūtānāṃ tasmāddhyātā na muhyate
bhūtātmānaṃ mahātmānaṃ paramātmānamavyayam
strīpumāṃsaprayogeṇa jāyate hi punaḥ punaḥ
mṛtpiṇḍastu yathā cakre cakravātena pīḍitaḥ
evamātmāsthisaṃyukto vāyunā samudīritaḥ
jāyate mānuṣastatra yathā rūpaṃ tathā manaḥ
vāyuḥ sambhavate teṣāṃ vātāt sañjāyate jalam
jalātsaṃbhavati prāṇaḥ prāṇācchukraṃ vivarddhate
raktabhāgāstrayastriṃśacchukra bhāgāścaturddaśa
pituḥ śarīrāt pratyaṅgarūpamasyopajāyate
tato'sya māturāhārāt pītalīḍhapraveśitam
nābhi srotaḥpraveśena prāṇādhāro hi dehinām
navamāsān parikliṣṭaḥ saṃveṣṭitaśirodharaḥ
veṣṭitaḥ sarvvagātraiśca aparyyāyakramāgataḥ
navamāsoṣitaścaiva yonicchidrādavāṅmukhaḥ
etāstu yātan ghorāḥ kumbhīpākasuduḥsahāḥ
tasmācchinnāśca bhinnāśca yātanāsthānamāgataḥ
evaṃ jīvastu taiḥ pāpaistapyamānaḥ svayaṃ kṛtaiḥ
prāpnuyāt karmmabhirduḥkhaṃ śeṣaṃ vā yādi cetaram
te nityaṃ yamaviṣaye vibhinnadehāḥ krośantaḥ satatamaniṣṭasaṃprayogaiḥ
śuṣyante parigatavedanāśarīrāḥ bahvībhiḥ subhṛśamadharmmayātanābhiḥ
karmaṇāḥ manasā vācā yadabhīṣṭaṃ niṣevyate
yādṛg jātāni pāpāni pūrvvaṃ karmmāṇi dehinaḥ
saṃsāraṃ tāmasaṃ tādṛk ṣahvidhaṃ pratipadyate
mānuṣyaṃ paśubhāvañca paśubhāvānmṛgo bhavet
mṛgatvāt pakṣibhāvantu tasmāccaiva sarīsṛpaḥ
sthāvaratvanna saṃśayaḥ
sthāvaratvaṃ punaḥ prāpto
yāvadunmiṣate naraḥ
ityevaṃ hi manuṣyādiḥ saṃsāre sthāvarāntake
sāttvikaścāpi saṃsāro brahmādiḥ parikīrttitaḥ
piśācāntaḥ savijñeyaḥ svargasthāneṣu dehinām
caturddaśānāṃ sthānānāṃ madhye viṣṭambhakaṃ rajaḥ
saṃskārāt pūrvadharmasya bhāvanāyāṃ praṇoditaḥ
mānuṣyaṃ bhajate nityaṃ tasmānnityaṃ samādadhet
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyuprokte pāśupatayogo nāma caturddaśo'dhyāyaḥ
vāyuruvāca
caturddaśavidhaṃ hyetadbuddhvā saṃsāramaṇḍalam
tathā samārabhet karmma saṃsārabhayapīḍitaḥ
tathā samārabhedyogaṃ yathātmānaṃ sa paśyati
eṣa ādyaḥ paraṃ jyotireṣa seturanuttamaḥ
vivṛddho hyeṣa bhūtānāṃ na sambhedaśca śāśvataḥ
tadenaṃ setumātmānaṃ agniṃ vai viśvatomukham
hṛdisthaṃ sarvabhūtānāmupāsīta vidhānavit
hutvāṣṭāvāhutīḥ samyak śucistadgatamānasaḥ
vaiśvānaraṃ hṛdisthantu yathāvadanupūrvaśaḥ
apaḥ pūrva sakṛt prāśya tuṣṇīṃ bhūtvā upāsate
prāṇāyeti tatastasya prathamā hyāhutiḥ smṛtā
apānāya dvitīyā tu samānāyeti cāparā
udānāya caturthīti vyānāyeti ca pañcamī
svāhā kāraiḥ paraṃ hutvā śeṣaṃ bhuñjīta kāmataḥ
apaḥ punaḥ sakṛt prāśya tryācamya hṛdayaṃ spṛśet
ॐprāṇānāṃ granthirasyātmā rudro hyātmā viśāntakaḥ
sa rudro hyātmanaḥ prāṇā evamāpyāyayet svayam
tvaṃ devānāmapi jyeṣṭha ugrastvaṃ caturo vṛṣā
evaṃ hṛdayamālabhya pādāṅguṣṭe tu dakṣiṇe
viśrāvya dakṣiṇaṃ pāṇiṃ nābhiṃ vai pāṇinā spṛśet
tataḥ punarupaspṛśya cātmānamabhisaṃspṛśet
akṣiṇī nāsikā śrotre hṛdayaṃ śira eva ca
dvāvātmānāvubhāvetau prāṇāpānāvudāhṛtau
tayoḥ prāṇo'ntarātmāsya bāhyo'pāno'ta ucyate
annaṃ prāṇastathāpānaṃ mṛtyurjjīvitameva ca
annaṃ brahma ca vijñeyaṃ prajānāṃ prasavastathā
tadevāgnau hutaṃ hyannaṃ bhuñjate devadānavāḥ
gandharvayakṣarakṣāṃsi piśācāścānnameva hi
iti śrīmahāpurāṇe vāyu prokte pāśupatayogo nāma pañcadaśo'dhyāyaḥ
vāyuruvāca
ata ūddhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi śaucācārasya lakṣaṇam
yadanuṣṭāya śuddhātmā pretya svargaṃ hi cāpnuyāt
udakārthī tu śaucānāṃ munīnāmuttamaṃ padam
mānāvamānau dvovetau tāvevāhurviṣāmṛte
avamānaṃ viṣaṃ tatra mānantvamṛtamucyate
prāpyānujñāntataścaiva jñānāgamanamuttamam
cakṣuḥ pūtaṃ vrajenmārgaṃ vastrapūtaṃ jalaṃ pibet
satyapūtāṃ vadedvāṇīmiti dharmānuśāsanam
evaṃ hyahiṃsako yogī bhavediti vicāraṇā
yuktastathā caredbhaikṣaṃ satāṃ dharmamadūṣayan
bhaikṣaṃ caredgṛhastheṣu yathācāragṛheṣu ca
