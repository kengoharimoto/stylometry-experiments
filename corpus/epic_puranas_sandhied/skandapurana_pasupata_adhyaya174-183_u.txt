puruṣaḥ pañcaviṃśaśca sarvārambhavivarjitaḥ
nityaḥ sarvagataḥ sūkṣmaścetāvāṃstriguṇojjhitaḥ

viditvā na viśantyenaṃ bhūyaḥ saṃsārapañjaram

sūkṣmā prakṛtiravyaktā bhojyā sarvaratā calā

tattejaścakṣuṣā hṛtvā sarvanāḍīsamāśritam
vāyurdhanaṃjayo nāmnā yo hṛdi sphurate sadā
trīṇi tasya mukhāni āhuḥ śiro nābhi hṛdeva ca
ye 'anye prāṇā navākhyātā vāyavaḥ kīrtitā mayā
tadvaśādutkramantyete tasmiṃstiṣṭhanti saṃsthitāḥ
nābhībandhanam āsādya nāḍīnāṃ vaktram āsthitam
dvitīyaṃ karṇikāyāṃ tu tṛtīyaṃ tālusaṃsthitam
kṣurikā paraśuḥ śūlaṃ trividhotkrāntirucyate
śūlaṃ hṛdi kṣuraṃ nābhyāṃ tālau paraśurāsthitaḥ
tāluke hṛdi vā baddhaṃ trayaṃ nābhyāṃ pratiṣṭhitam
tālau viyojitaṃ sarvaṃ prāṇam eva vimuñcati
ākṣiptaṃ tritayaṃ nābhyāṃ karṇikāyāṃ viyojitam
yathā jīvo na baddhyeta yoginastadidaṃ śṛṇu
bāhyaḥ śarīrajaścaiva mṛtyurdvividha ucyate
viṣaśastrāgniyogaistu sarpadaṃṣṭrimarujjalaiḥ
bāhyo mṛtyuriti khyātaḥ śarīraṃ tu nibodha me
yogopāyakṛto yaśca yaśca kālakṣayāgataḥ
tayorbhedavikāre tu śṛṇu vyāsa samāsataḥ
yo 'ayam āghoṣajaḥ śabdaḥ śrūyate śrotrasandhitaḥ
śabdasparśaguṇātīto rūpayogaṃ vimuñcati
evam agnau praṇaṣṭe tu sarva evānilāstataḥ
udānenāśu saṃyujya karṇikāyāṃ viśanti hi
indriyāṇi mano yānti manaḥ prāṇavinirgamam
teṣu sarveṣu līneṣu prāṇān vāyurnipīḍayet
pīḍyamānāstataḥ sarve te bhavantyekato 'anilāḥ
śeṣastatra hi mūrmāro jīvaṃ tyaktvā sa tiṣṭhati
niṣkramanti tato dehāt tvaritaṃ vāyuneritāḥ
prāgupāttastato 'asyānyo dehaḥ sūkṣmo vijāyate
sa tatra sandhito jīvo bhūyo mūḍhaśca tiṣṭhati
evaṃ kālakṛtā hyeṣā sarvabhūtagaṇasthitiḥ
vinā yogaṃ na jīyeta sūkṣmaṃ māheśvaraṃ padam
upaviṣṭaḥ prasupto vā utkrāntyabhyastasaṃyamaḥ
svecchayā mucyate vyāsa jñātvā taṃ tu maheśvaram
kṣurikāṃ paraśuṃ vātha śūlaṃ vā dhārayenmuniḥ
tataḥ sarve 'asya līyante vikārāḥ karaṇaiḥ saha
vīkṣate ceśvaraṃ brahma ṣaḍviṃśaṃ svatanusthitam
sukham ārohati brahma harmyapṛṣṭhaṃ talādiva
padārthaṃ pañca vijñāya duḥkhāntapariniṣṭhitaḥ
pañcabhirbrahmabhiḥ pūto bhasmanā dīkṣito dvijaḥ
śaṃkaraikamanā yogī jñānam etadavāpnute
māheśvaram imaṃ yogaṃ niṣkalaṃ muktikāraṇam
mahādevaparo bhūtvā jñānam etadavāpnuhi
mahādevaṃ prapannā ye tān muktvā śivayoginaḥ
evaṃ pāśupatā viprā niṣkalaṃ taṃ maheśvaram
bhavyabhūtabhavānāṃ ca saptānām ūrdhvataḥ sthitaḥ
nāsau tarkavinirdeśyo lokāstasmin pratiṣṭhitāḥ
brahmāṇḍaṃ sakalaṃ tasmiñchivaloke pratiṣṭhitam
nāsau naśyati saṃhāre dhruvaḥ sthāṇuḥ surālayaḥ
yatrāhaṃ sanakādyāśca brahmā caiva pitāmahaḥ
bālakhilyapurogāśca siddhāśca kapilādayaḥ
śivayogapariprāptyā sattvādiguṇavarjitāḥ
tiṣṭhāmo nirvṛtā vyāsa sevamānāstam īśvaram
paramāṇurasau īśo bhāvagamyo manīṣiṇām
kṣamā satyaṃ dhṛtiścaiva tapo vairāgyam eva ca
sraṣṭṛtvaṃ jñānavairāgyādātmabodho maheśatā
aṣṭamaṃ dhruvam adhyeyam akṣaraṃ muktikāraṇam
māyādhāma tadīśasya jagatsaṃhārahetumat
ūrdhvato brahmalokasya yojanānāṃ mahātmanaḥ
viṃśatīnāṃ tu koṭīnāṃ puraṃ sthāṇoḥ samāsthitam
etāvān ūrdhvasaṃcāro gatyantaśca tataḥ smṛtaḥ
asaṃkhyātāḥ pure tasmin nityāḥ sūkṣmāstathāvyayāḥ
jñānādhikāradharmiṇyaḥ śaivīprakṛtayaḥ sthitāḥ
yābhyo jajñe maheśānau brahmaviṣṇū prajāpatī
tābhyāṃ prakṛtibhūtābhyāṃ sraṣṭṛtvaṃ paramaṃ dadau
akṣayo vimalaḥ sūkṣmo niṣkalaḥ sakalo 'api ca
yadāsīddhaimam aṇḍaṃ tu laukikaṃ tu āpavargikam
tasmādbījasya nirbhedaḥ kṣetrajñaḥ sa vibhuḥ smṛtaḥ
prakṛtirvaiṣṇavī proktā sā ca tasmādvinirgatā
surāsurādisṛṣṭyarthaṃ brahmatvaṃ punarāsthitā
aṇḍasya bhedataḥ padma brahmaṇaḥ śaṃkarātmanaḥ
yat sthānaṃ vigraheśasya īśvarasya parātmanaḥ
nāmnā śivapuraṃ vyāsa gatirīśvarayoginām
śataṃ śatasahasrāṇāṃ yojanānāṃ taducyate
mahīmaṇḍalasaṃsthānaṃ tanmadhye vistṛtaṃ smṛtam
mahatā haimajātena prākāreṇa suveṣṭitam
jarāmṛtyuśramavyādhi kopamohamadādayaḥ
śataṃ śatasahasrāṇāṃ yojanānāṃ yathādiśam
tat puraṃ vṛṣabhāṅkasya sarvavyāpi samṛddhimat
divyā mārakatī bhūmiḥ sparśamātrasukhāvahā
vaidūryamaṇikalpāni bālārkasadṛśāni ca
mahāhradapramāṇāni nālairvaidūryasannibhaiḥ
indranīlanikāśāni mahāmaṇinibhāni ca
pure bhagavataḥ śambhoḥ pārāśarya nibodha tāḥ
varadā ca vareṇyā ca vareśā varavarṇinī
vārāhī vāribhadrā ca varā caiva mahāpagāḥ
nānākusumasaṃmiśraṃ tā vahanti jalāmṛtam
na devā na munīndrāśca nāsurāḥ pitaro na ca
meruśṛṅgapratīkāśo digbhadrastapanaprabhaḥ
prāsādo lakṣapādastu kāmagaḥ kanakāmayaḥ
vaidūryairvyomasaṃkāśaiḥ sphāṭikaiścandrasaprabhaiḥ
prabālairarkasaṃkāśairindranīlamayaiḥ paraiḥ
śaśiraśmiprakāśābhiḥ kvacidarkāṃśurūpibhiḥ
śobhitaṃ tat patākābhirvirarāja sukhānilam
śuśubhe tat puraṃ kīrṇaṃ pādapairgirisannibhaiḥ
mahīdhrāṇāṃ yathā meruḥ sarveṣāṃ pravaro mahān
tatra viśveśvaraḥ sākṣānmahābhūtapatirharaḥ
sadāste sahitaḥ patnyā ṣaḍviṃśaḥ parameśvaraḥ
lakṣmīrmeghā dhṛtiścaiva hrīḥ śrīḥ kīrtiḥ sarasvatī
śivabhāvārpitātmāno ye narā rudratatparāḥ
yatra tatra mṛtā vatsa teṣāṃ tatrāspadaṃ smṛtam
ye ca māheśvarā vyāsa tiṣṭhante śaṃkarālaye
munayaḥ śaṃkaraṃ bhaktāstāñchṛṇuṣva samāhitaḥ
ṛbhurbrahmā surajyeṣṭho naranārāyaṇāvapi
śiṣyaḥ siddhaḥ pañcaśikhastathā
munirhayaśirā yaśca yajñavalkādayastathā
tryakṣāḥ śaṃkaravarṣmāṇaḥ paramaiśvaryasaṃyutāḥ
tiṣṭhantīśvaramūlasthā guṇātītā mumukṣavaḥ
īśvaraṃ te samāviśya prāpnuvantyapavargikam
yaiśca dṛṣṭāni viprendra śivaliṅgāni tāni tu
kedāro madhyamaścaiva girīśaśca mahālaye
catuḥśṛṅgeśvaro devaḥ śrīgirau ca maheśvaraḥ
kārohaṇaśca deveśo gaṅgādvāre ca śaṃkaraḥ
bhadreśaśca mahādevaḥ śaṅkukarṇeśvarastathā
mahākāleśvaraścaiva gokarṇe ca tathā hyubhau
avimukteśvaraścaiva yāni cānyāni bhūtale
te 'api yānti mṛtā vyāsa tat puraṃ girijāpateḥ
yogaḥ pāśupato yaśca mayā te parikīrtitaḥ
bhaktiryeṣāṃ bhave nityaṃ kṛtārthāste narottamāḥ
mṛtāḥ śivapure vyāsa tryakṣāḥ paṭṭiśapāṇayaḥ
nandīśaskandasaṃkāśā yogaiśvaryabalānvitāḥ
rudrā bhavanti te vyāsa sadā śaṃkarapārśvagāḥ
eṣa te kathitaḥ sarvo yogo māheśvaraḥ paraḥ
sakalaṃ niṣkalaṃ caiva jñātvaivaṃ param īśvaram
bhaviṣyasi ca yogātmā jñānasyāsya prabhāvataḥ
jñānaśaktyā smṛ brahmāṇḍodbhavakāraṇe
yajñadharmapracārārthaṃ vedaṃ svāyambhuvasmṛtam
vijñānāt tvam anusmṛtya caturdhā vikariṣyasi
tvatkṛtāḥ pracariṣyanti tīrthakṛt tvaṃ bhaviṣyasi
śāṃkarajñānasampanna īśvare layam āpsyasi
evam uktaḥ sa viprendro hṛṣṭasarvatanūruhaḥ
upasadya munīndraṃ taṃ bhasmasaṃskāram āptavān
tatkṣaṇāccāsya yogo 'asau prādurbhūto mahāmuneḥ
śarvāyatanavīkṣārthaṃ vicacāra mahītale
evaṃ sanatkumārastu pṛṣṭo vyāsena dhīmatā
munīndraḥ kathayāmāsa purāṇaṃ skandasambhavam
śivayogodbhavaṃ dhyānaṃ sarvajñānārṇvamvaṃ mahat
tasmādyūyam api jñātvā vrataṃ pāśupataṃ dvijāḥ
abhyasya śāṃkaraṃ yogam īśvare layam āpnuta
vedārthasmṛtisambaddhā vidyāścakre mahāmuniḥ
purāṇaṃ prathamaṃ prāhurādyaṃ nandīśvaro dvijāḥ
kṛtsnasya jagato viprā ādikartā yathā haraḥ
tathā sarvapurāṇānāṃ purāṇo 'ayaṃ paraḥ smṛtaḥ
ṣya sāyujyaṃ śṛṇuyādvibhoḥ
ādyaṃ puṇyam umāpaterdvijavṛṣāḥ stotrānuvādaṃ paraṃ
pnuyuḥ
sarve te jvaladarkavahnivapuṣastryakṣāḥ śaśāṅkatviṣaḥ
